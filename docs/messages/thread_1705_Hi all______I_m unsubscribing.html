From: June (nfmoon@mindspring.com) | Date: 2006-02-19 20:33:17 UTC
I had already unsubscribed and then decided it wasn't right to just
go and not say anything, and I see it hasn't taken effect yet so
I'll post one more time.

In all honesty, the times I *do* post, which I know aren't that
often, I feel like I'm kind of talking to myself. That's probably
because I *don't* post that often. I don't have any boxes in Texas
anynore and probably won't be planting anymore until I retire and
hopefully move back there, so I really don't feel like I'm part of
Texas boxing.

Some of you might remember I work for the transit authority here in
nyc and we went on strike recently, as you may have read or heard. I
just found out that next month they will be taking the fines out of
both our paychecks next month. I don't think this is the time for me
to be spending money on plane tickets, car rentals and hotels and I
don't want to just charge them, so once again I won't be coming to
the gathering. I had been questioning whether or not I should come
anyway, for the same reasons I am leaving the list.

Hopefully I'll be living back out there in the not too distant
future and can become a real part of the Texas boxing community.
Right now I just don't feel that I am.

I know you all will have a wonderful time at the gathering.

MissMoon
