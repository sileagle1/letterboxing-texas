Just had an email that Wanda and Pete have signed up to come to the
Great Book Adventure. Those of you who have followed Wanda's
adventures know that she is a prolific letterboxer--with more than
10,000 finds. More than her F count though, she is an avid hiker, an
enthusiastic participant, an encourager of newbies and more. No
matter how many boxes Wanda hits in a day, she takes the time to write
a little note in each one and carefully rehide. I am still happy to
see her stamps and notes in the boxes she hit when here for the Brazos
Bend Texas letterboxing event. Looking forward to meeting Pete as
well. . .

So watch out! No letterbox in Texas is safe!
If you want to read Wanda's last adventures in Texas, check this out:

A few weeks ago, we were boxing in Arizona and saw they had stamped in one of the boxes there.  We were so excited to see someones name we recognized!!

Boxing there is quite different than here - mainly due to the rattlesnakes but we saw all kinds of containers - peanut butter jars, mayo jars, etc.  I did find it neat that several were covered with a 12" square  of sand colored vinyl material.  Worked very well in disguising the boxes. 

Out of the 4 or 5 people that I emailed about finds or missing boxes only 1 responded so that makes me think that Texas letterboxers are the absolute BEST!!!

Frye Team
