Bad News about the Circus

From: Lou (lou.catozzi@att.net) | Date: 2012-04-29 23:43:07 UTC
I'm afraid I have some bad news on the Circus. Yes. I am the one who took it from the White Rock Cemetery in Bee Caves last month. I've had it and I've carved a new stamp to add to it and had it all ready to plant back in the wild for about a week now.

Well, last night while my son was at the Round Rock Senior Prom at the Arboretum (in Austin) the Circus was taken from my Jeep. If any of you own a Jeep you know that you really can't secure it from people who want to get into it. For that reason I don't keep anything of any real value in it but I thought the Circus would be safe enough. Apparently not.

Funny thing. I think another letterboxer might have taken it! When I was going looking at Jeep today I found what I can only describe as a calling card or LTC or something similar. It was a white stock card with the stamped image of a pirate on it and signed "The Jackal".

I guess we will just have to wait and see if the Circus shows up somewhere. I hope it's safe.


PI Joe
