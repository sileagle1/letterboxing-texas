Public Welcome to New Members

From: (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-04 13:05:23 UTC
I've approved several new members in the last couple of weeks, at
least a few of whom came to us with an interest in letterboxing as a
result of the Time magazine article!

I would like to take a moment to publicly welcome those of you who
are new to us and encourage you to jump in and to ask any questions
you may have. Please know that your questions will be handled by
this group in a helpful, friendly manner - we live by the adage "No
question is a dumb question!". In fact, if anything, I think you'll
be overwhelmed by the willingness to help that you will find in our
group.

You are welcome to lurk if you prefer, but we'd love to read an intro
from each of you before you settle in to lurkerdom! Texas still has
very few letterboxers and relatively few boxes, so we like to meet
new boxers in our great state!

As you begin boxing, don't be discouraged if there aren't many boxes
in your area. For one thing, we have several members who are
committed to getting more boxes out there. And for another, planting
them can be as fun as finding them. I am amazed at how many of our
members have begun boxing since this list began in December and how
many of them are actively committed to this pasttime and have already
boosted their P (Placed) and F (Found) counts in that short time. In
short I'm saying don't despair because "Coming soon to a park near
you...!"

Welcome and have fun!
Lucy
