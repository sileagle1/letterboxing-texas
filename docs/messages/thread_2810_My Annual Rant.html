From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-06-07 22:06:06 UTC-05:00
Ya know, I've lived in Texas MOST of my life and spent more summers than I care to count here, and yet every year, somewhere around this time, I wake up to the rude realization that it is just stinking hot in Texas!
 
I left a scout meeting a few minutes ago.  As I was walking toward the door, I saw that it was already dark and so I expected a little relief from the heat as I opened the door.  Instead I was greeted by an ovenous wind (yes I know, there is no such word as ovenous, but there is NOW and don't try to argue with me because I am RANTING and when I am ranting is NOT a good time to argue with me!) and a choking humidity.  I felt like I was in a - a - crawfish pot!  YUK - YUK - YUK!
 
Heat stinks and I am ready for the fall.  Please, please tell me it will be early this year.  Lie to me even - but please, tell me it will be early!
--
Barefoot Lucy
