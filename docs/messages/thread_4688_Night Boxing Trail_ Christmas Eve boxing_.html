:) You know I have no patience... (my brother use to call me grasshopper as well) lol I was going to pester you Pi Joe and ask you how your new carves are coming along. But I know the holidays slows us all down. But you teased with the mention of carves, and scouting out trails, and now night boxing from you!?! I only have a week left off work. Please let me have something to find!!!
 
I did go boxing on Christmas Eve and found all 8 boxes in Pedernalas State Park.  I recomend listening to the weather forcast when it says 80% chance of rain at 2pm. I should have done the creek crossing box first, not last. It was raining almost the whole time I was boxing but I was under trees and it was only a few minutes of hard rain at first, then sprinkling the rest of the time untill the last half a mile. On the last box I had to cross a creek and I left my shoes on the other side thinking just up the hill was the box. It was a longer walk than I thought and I ended up walking barefeet over broken asphault, stickers, and cacti before getting to the box. Then the skys opened up on me and I was half a mile from the car. I hobbled back barefoot along the trail, enjoying the river of water that was cooling my feet as I walked downhill tword the creek crossing. The creek had rissen by the time I got back to it and Dreamwalker was standing on the other side waiting for me, worried I wouldnt make it back across and I'de get stuck in the bad rain with no shelter. He was soaked through.  By the time we got back to the car my 3 layers of clothes were soaked through and I was dripping wet. I striped off all but my last layer and we headed home, cold, wet and I was so freaking happy I got all 8!
 
~Dawns Glory~
