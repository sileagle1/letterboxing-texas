Texas Tour Bus 2 - Lake Tawakoni State Park

From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-03-22 19:49:38 UTC
Mr. Thomas Lea and Bobby Hill have gotten on the bus and have
travelled to Lake Tawakoni State Park.

Make your way to Wills Point, which is in Van Zandt County. At the
intersection of US 80 and FM 45, follow 47 across the RR tracks and
through downtown. You should see signs to Lake Tawakoni SP. You
will eventually turn left onto FM 2475 North and it will take you
into the park.

As you enter the park, follow the signs to the boat ramp/swimming
area. After you pass the rest rooms on your left, pull into the
first parking lot on the right.

Look for the NATURE TRAIL sign a few yards on the other side of the
WHEEL CHAIR ACCESSIBLE parking spaces.

To the Bus:

At the NATURE TRAIL sign, follow the trail to the right.

As you follow the trail through the woods, you will come to a large
clearing/group camping area. Go through the clearing. The trail
will split. Follow the trail to the right.

Taking this trail, you will come to an intersection. From here, go
LEFT for 70 steps. Look to your right. There is a tree that splits
about 4.5-5 feet up. (Some of the tree's branches come all the way
out to the trail.) The tree is about 16 steps off the trail. The
bus is parked behind this tree.
