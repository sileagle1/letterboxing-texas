I'm always glad to have visitors in the area to find
boxes and leave a few behind... :)

I was in Florida this past week and did some boxing. I
only found one box. The ones that were missing were
probably taken out by the hurricanes. I placed one box
(watch for clues to "Texas Tourist") and left a HH
with the box I found. After all the exploring, I'm

be back in the area again next year, so I will be able
to plant more boxes and (hopefully) find even more!
