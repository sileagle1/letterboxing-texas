Last Minute information on Rusk

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-16 19:03:05 UTC-08:00
Apparently Parks & Wildlife recently reduced staffing
at Rusk State Park so that the park is only staffed
Friday-Monday. The result is that with no one at the
gate to tell them the park is full, folks are pouring
in tonight and setting up camp without reservations,
despite the fact the park has been booked for quite a
long time.

Silver Eagle and Baby Bear are already here and are
going to attack the problem early on (they themselves
are having to share a site with someone else for the
night); however, if you have reservations at the park
for tomorrow, I suggest you get here as early as
possible so you can make sure to press the point that
you have a reservation. If you can't be here early,
send me your reservation information and cell phone
number and I'll try to check you in at check-in time.
At the very least, I'll pressure them that you are on
your way and expect to have a site waiting for you.

It'll get sorted out and we'll have a great weekend.
Looking forward to seeing all of you!

Lucy

P.S. I ran across this map today of Rusk and its
highlights. For those of you planning on planting
boxes while you're here, this might give you some
ideas about where to plant:

http://www.rusktexascoc.org/rusk_fun_map.pdf
