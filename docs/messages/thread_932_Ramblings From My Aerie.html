Sorry I have not participated in a while, but work
and vacations have put me behind. I have finally
browsed most of the posts over the last few weeks
and will try to address several things in this one
long post.

Introduction: Since there are so many new members
on this list (welcome!), I guess I should introduce
myself to them. I live in Sugar Land and have been
letterboxing almost 3 years now. I have a wife and
2 children, but they are nonboxers for the most part
(though they will indulge me occasionally). I have
a website that includes all my LB clues, along with
my Hitchhikers, Events, Series (groups of LB with
the same theme, like Beer) and other LB links. It
can be found at: http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/

Events & Gatherings: So far we have had 2 Texas Events,
March, 2004 and March, 2005. I plan to continue organizing
these annual Texas-wide Events in the Spring of each year,
but I'm starting to see interest in more frequent, smaller
gatherings. I don't have the time to organize them, but
recommend anyone interested go ahead and do it. Maybe
each region of Texas could have its own Gathering in the
fall so people would not have to drive as far to meet others.

State Of TX Letterboxing: I am very pleased to see how
letterboxing continues to grow in our great state. Somebody
said in a post that Baby Bear and I must feel amazed how
this hobby has taken off since we started so long ago, and
they are correct. In fact, I no longer feel like I must
nurture TX Letterboxing to keep it going, it has grown up
and become self-sufficient. Thanks to all for making this
happen and continue to be supportive of each other!

Silver Eagle
