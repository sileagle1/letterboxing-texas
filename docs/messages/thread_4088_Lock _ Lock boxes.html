From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2009-05-14 20:28:32 UTC
I think it kind of depends on where you are. I use to buy them at Target, but the one where we shop doesn't carry them anymore. You can get certain sizes at Wal-Mart, but I find they come in a pack of 3 different sizes, and one is a very small round box. The best bet is online, and that's usually where I get them. I have bought them at www.heritagemint.com and I've heard you can get them at qvc. They come in a lot of sizes, so the trick is finding the right size for a letterbox. The one I like best is about 4 1/2" square and 1 1/2" deep, but I haven't been able to get that size lately. I found one place where you can buy them already painted with camoflage paint, see this link:

http://www.dixiegraphix.com/shop/letterbox/containers/main.html

The price for the boxes aren't bad, but the shipping may be prohibitive. I'm not sure where they are shipping from.

Boots Tex
