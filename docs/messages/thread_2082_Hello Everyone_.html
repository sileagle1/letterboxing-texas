From: Sandi (sandibox@msn.com) | Date: 2006-08-17 18:40:30 UTC
Hi Janet,

Welcome! What part of the state are you in? I think you'll find
letterboxing an wonderful obsesssion (I mean hobby) for the entire
family. When my daughter tells her friends about letterboxing, they
are jealous that we have such a fun activity to share with each
other.

You can find great information on how to get started by visiting
www.letterboxing.org and viewing the getting started link. Also be
sure to check out www.atlasquest.com and read the tutorials in the
toolbox section. Also, the good folks on this list are a great
source of information and encouragement. Just ask!

Happy trails!
SandiBox
