I hope I get to meet all of you at the Event that I have
just missed on the trail.
Baby Bear and I bumped into 3 new boxers in a small park
in the middle of Idaho last summer. We had taken the box
to a picnic table and watched as this trio pulled up and
proceded to follow the clues to the box. They looked
disappointed at not finding it until I walked up with it
in my hand. They had no idea how rare this encounter was
and I had to explain about exchanging before they would
stamp into my logbook. This encounter is even more
amazing when you realize there were only a handful of
letterboxers in the entire state at that time!

Silver Eagle
