From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-02-22 08:36:38 UTC-06:00
I know you're gonna be surprised, but my real name is NOT Barefoot Lucy.  Don't pass out - please!  In letterboxing though, I prefer to go by Lucy and here's why:
 
My given name is hard for folks to remember and it is almost invariably mispronounced the first several times someone calls me by name (but I DO feel strongly enough about it to correct folks - nicely).  At the same time, I identify very closely with my trailname- I've used it as a screen name since long before I ever letterboxed.  I'm very comfortable with going by it and I like it very much (I like my given name too).  It's also the name I go by that is purely fun - it doesn't tie me to any responsibilities!
 
SO - when I put my name on my name tag, it'll be Barefoot Lucy.  You can call me all of that, or you can call me Lucy, or if you really just want to call me by my given name, you can ask me it and I'll tell you and then I'll correct you until you get it right (no hard feelings)!
 
As far as what to call other boxers, I would say go with what they put on their name tag.  As far as what you put on your name tag, you can put whatever you are most comfortable going by.  If you really like your trail name but you feel a little silly going by it, just jump out there and give it a try!  I'd say most folks go by trail names.

I know you're gonna be surprised, but my real name is NOT Barefoot Lucy.  Don't pass out - please!  In letterboxing though, I prefer to go by Lucy and here's why:

 

My given name is hard for folks to remember and it is almost invariably mispronounced the first several times someone calls me by name (but I DO feel strongly enough about it to correct folks - nicely).  At the same time, I identify very closely with my trailname- I've used it as a screen name since long before I ever letterboxed.  I'm very comfortable with going by it and I like it very much (I like my given name too).  It's also the name I go by that is purely fun - it doesn't tie me to any responsibilities!

 

SO - when I put my name on my name tag, it'll be Barefoot Lucy.  You can call me all of that, or you can call me Lucy, or if you really just want to call me by my given name, you can ask me it and I'll tell you and then I'll correct you until you get it right (no hard feelings)!

 

As far as what to call other boxers, I would say go with what they put on their name tag.  As far as what you put on your name tag, you can put whatever you are most comfortable going by.  If you really like your trail name but you feel a little silly going by it, just jump out there and give it a try!  I'd say most folks go by trail names.
