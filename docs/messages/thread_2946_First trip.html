First trip

From: Brandy (brandy1975@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-07-22 01:31:41 UTC
Hi. My family and I are new to LBing. I have been talking about it
for months and reading everything I could find on the subject. I
fractured my knee Easter weekend and had to put off alot of summer
outings. This past week we went camping in East Texas (me on one leg)
and set out to find our first boxes. Kids were thrilled. We attempted
4 boxes and actually found 2.

I managed to hike a little over a mile (RT) one crutches...but I could
not be left out of the first find. I let the rest of the family tackle
the rest on their own...with a camera in hand.

It was great to get out...and LBing was a great motivator for me to at
least try to walk. Thank all of you for all of your great stories and
information.

The Boehme Gang
Orange, TX
