Texas Trash

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2012-11-26 15:46:09 UTC
I thought I'd share a comment with you that I got from a family of letterboxers called "The Herd". I guess they are visiting from out of state. They asked for clues to my box in Huntsville State Park called "Texas Trash", in which I ask them to pick up any trash they might find along the trail while walking to the box. Here's the comment:

"Thanks- great box (and it's in great condition still). If I'd known how much fun my six children would have picking up trash I would have made a game of it years ago. All together they managed to find over 120 tiny pieces of trash- combing the trails on both side and up and down each junction. I'm sure other hikers thought we were crazy yelling, "here's another piece of trash!" and "I've picked up more trash than you!""

I guess I need to send this to the Ranger at the park.

Boots Tex
