Wild West Rodeo and Show

From: Cheryl Pickenpaugh (pickenpaughc@yahoo.com) | Date: 2017-01-09 00:21:51 UTC
This wild bunch has been camping out here on our ranch for a few months, and finally was able to convince them to move on down the road.  Saw them head thru Gladewater, stop in at the Round-Up Rodeo grounds and look around, then head out west a couple miles but then stopped in at the Gladewater Memorial Cemetery.  They went left, then left and then took a sharp right, went north until they saw a three foot angel on the left and went to check it out.  Then saw a nice place to bed down in the thicket behind a nice heart shaped tombstone with Sandra Kay Hale on it.
