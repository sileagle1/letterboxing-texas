Huzzah! Huzzah!  

 

Her Royal Majesty Queen Beeloved and His Gentle Lordship Beneficus Rex hereby extend to all within hearing an invitation for a gathering of fun, food, and frolicking (for letterboxes)!

 

Those who care, bring potluck fare for friends to share. For artisans who would place a treasure there, a Camelot theme would seem most fair.  

 

Journey a short distance north of Lake Lewisville and join us at South Lakes Park in Denton, Texas. The park is located on Hobson Road, about one mile south of the corner of I-35E and Teasley lane.   

 

Until then, Fare Thee Well! 

 

Please go to AtlasQuest to sign up:

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=662

 

 

 

***There will be carving and book-binding classes - presented informally. Please bring carving material and any tools you would like to have for the carving. Also, if you are interested in bookbinding, bring decorative paper for the cover. If you'd like to attend a class - just show up. Classes will most likely be in late morning or early afternoon. ***
