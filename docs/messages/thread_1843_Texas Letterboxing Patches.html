Hey Mike, your logic there made me think of New Math.  Remember that?  One is about what you would get when you add 1 seven times!  LOL  
 
I was the victim of New Math for one horrendous year of school when I already didn't get math and then they tried to "help" me by removing any sort of logic or method from math.  I vividly remember my dad, a patient man who valued education and expected that I respect my teachers and my lessons, when he finally had all the New Math he could stand and threw the book on the floor beside the table and started showing me how to cipher by hand and good sense.  He told me he was going to show me math the right way and if anyone had a problem with it, to talk to him.  I seriously think he considered burning my math book that night!   I even remember the look on my teacher's face when I (very frightened) told her my dad didn't want me using the textbook anymore - I think she was secretly relieved!  This is the man who taught me to measure rise and run in large areas without equipment of any sort and how to use a slide rule...bless his soul...
 
So here's my stab at New Math - I already have a patch, and so does my LSS, and so does my daughter and her boyfriend, and my son, and my backpack, so ummm...put me down for 20.  But then, I'm not stuck by my LB lonesome out in El Paso and I like to leave 'em behind in boxes every now and then, especially when I know there's a good chance some newbies are following behind me. 
 
Lucy
