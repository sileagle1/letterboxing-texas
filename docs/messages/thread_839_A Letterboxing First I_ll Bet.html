A Letterboxing First I'll Bet

From: JuneMcAllister (nfmoon@mindspring.com) | Date: 2005-05-15 11:51:21 UTC-04:00

Last week I mailed the Texas Birthday Box back to celticlions since the next birthday on the list is not until the last of June. I mailed it from the little mini post office located in the Federal building in lower Manhattan, across from my office. As you can guess, security is always high there. I've mailed large brown envelopes before, the padded kind, and regular mail, but never a box that size.
 
When I went through the metal detector and handed the guard the box for him to put through the x-ray machine, he told me I couldn't mail a box like that unless I went outside to the van where the canine unit is posted.
 
I had to go back outside and get the box sniffed by the bomb sniffing dogs!! I'd be willing to bet that NO letterbox has EVER had to be sniffed for a bomb.
 
A letterboxing first, and it happened here folks!! 
 
MissMoon
