“Officials have confirmed that tar balls discovered over the holiday weekend on Crystal Beach in the Bolivar Peninsula are from the Deepwater Horizon oil spill, marking the first time oil from the spill has been found on Texas beaches. About 10-12 tar balls washed up on Crystal Beach on Saturday. On Sunday, 5 gallons of oil were also found on the same beach….

Some members of the Texas Traveling Circus, having rested and relaxed a bit in Wilson County, have become increasingly concerned about the current situation in the Gulf of Mexico.  Fausto the Great and Leopold the Strong Man, who believe they can do anything, want to go to the coast and help with the cleanup effort. They are rallying the others to join in on this mission.  At this time, support for this endeavor is more or less limited.  Flixxy the Dancing Pony, being pure white, and the Fire Breather, not wishing to cause the oil to accidentally ignite, are leading the oppostion. They are of the mind that they should continue on their circus tour, traveling perhaps to West Texas, where they understand circus visits are rare.  At this point all of their discussions are civil, but you never know how things will go when the Bearded Lady gets involved….

What will they do?  Friends, I’ll let you be the first to know when I find out.

Texas Traveling Circus

From: boxhounds08 (boxhounds08@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-07-12 02:35:35 UTC
Saturday morning we were headed to Hunt to do a favor for a friend and hopefully get in some much needed letterboxing time. Our cell phone rang just outside of San Antonio. It was an emergency call from Corazon. It seems she had stumbled upon several memebers of the TTC outside of Starbucks who needed to get to the bearded lady immediately! Corazon had graciously agreed to give them a ride, but with the members scattered all about San Antonio, she needed help finding them all and getting them to a central meeting point. We spent several hours making phone calls and checking out Home Depot, Lowes, bowling alleys, etc. It seems that everyone but Fred Jewell was ready to get back on the road, apparently Snoga's Barbecue was closed and Fred would not leave without some good Texas barbecue. After much mediation, Fred agreed to be taken to Bill Miller's BBQ instead; where we met up with Corazon, the bearded lady(who had decided against waxing) and the rest of the troupe. After everyone had their fill of barbecue and sweet tea, they decided that since the circus always makes people laugh and feel happy, perhaps they were doing a little of both community service and work. They happily made up and decided to head back out on the road. They were last seen heading toward West Texas on 1-10, I think I heard them mention something about Midland?
