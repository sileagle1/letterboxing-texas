Hmmmm....  well - on this note let me make an open offer to the Texas group.
 
The folks I hang out with are a neat bunch.  There is a core group of about 10 people, with perhaps 8 others who drift in and out.  Of the core 10, 8 are ham radio operators, 7 own guns, 5 are licensed to carry, 6 are letterboxers/geocachers, 5 own/work/train dogs, 5 have off-road SUVs, 7 are hikers, 3 work in electronics, one is a retired scientist.....  many are x-military.... talk about a diverse group!
 
We have a Friday night gathering, called the Friday Night "DO".  The talk ranges from work to guns to radio to camping, letterboxing/geocaching, hiking, jeeps, and on and on....  and then there is food.  It is usually a semi-planned pot-luck.  Someone suggests a "theme" for the "DO". and everyone tries to bring something to fit the theme.  There is always way more food than we can eat.  We try valiantly, but there is always a large amount of left-overs.
 
Should any of you find yourselves stuck in El Paso on a Friday evening, consider joining us.  Don't worry about bringing anything but an appetite.  Some warning might be good as we sometimes skip a week when life gets too busy......  but most Friday nights there is a "DO"
 
Y-all come for a visit.
 
Mike.
