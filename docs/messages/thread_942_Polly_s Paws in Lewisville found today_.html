I've been driving around all week with the clues for Polly's Paws and
finally, today, made it to Lewisville to find this LB in a very pretty
little park, nice short walk in the SHADE - hooray! This is one that
would be great to take kids along because the park has a wonderful
playground, the walk isn't long or hot, and the LB is pretty
accessible. Cute paws stamp, too. Thanks very much, Red Berry, for
planting it. I was delighted to be FF AND to refind the 1st
Anniversary HH, which had been sent to me from Puddle-Splasher in CT
(she'd been FF for that one)and which had been released in the
CelticLions' LB in Denton previously. Now, it can continue its travels
around TX after I release it again in the next couple of weeks.
