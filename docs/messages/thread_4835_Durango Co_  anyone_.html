From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2011-06-29 02:24:24 UTC
I was there 2 years ago and it was worth the visit. The only 2 I didn't find were Animas Overlook by Wanda & Pete, and Where Eagles Fly by Front Range Hiker.

Another nice hike not too far away is the Haflin Creek Trail, which also has a nice view and a few letterboxes, including one I placed (The Sacketts).

Depending on how much time you have, you should also take the train to Silverton or drive the Million Dollar Highway to Ouray. You can also do an easy raft trip with the family right in town. Have fun!

Silver Eagle
