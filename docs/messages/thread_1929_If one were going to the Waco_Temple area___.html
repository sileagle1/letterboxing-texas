From: janetsredbarn (janetsredbarn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-16 04:27:37 UTC
If you don't mind a fairly long hike, check out the box called "4ID".
It's located in Chalk Ridge FAlls Park out of Belton near Stillhouse
Creek Lake. The hike along and above the creek is worth every step,
featuring a waterfall and a suspension footbridge. This place is
begging for more letterboxes.
