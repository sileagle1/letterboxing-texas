3 new letterboxes

From: tx2chic (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-05-06 22:08:06 UTC
I planted 3 new letterboxes this afternoon in two very pretty parks in
NE Tarrant Co. One is at the Bedford Boys Ranch in Bedford, near the
little lake, a favorite place to feed the ducks and so the perfect
place for "At the Pond: Allie's Duck." The other two are in Grapevine
at the lovely Heritage Park - "In the Creek: Lauren's Turtle" and "In
the Tree: Meghan's Bird." These are really pretty parks, especially
right now when the flowers and plants are in bloom (the oakleaf
hydrangea at Heritage Park is really gorgeous right now!). I never
realized how pretty that park was until I scouted locations for these
letterboxes, even though I've driven past them for years. I hope you
enjoy them as much as I did! These 3 letterboxes were placed for my
granddaughters who dictated the choice of stamps. Clues are on LBNA.
