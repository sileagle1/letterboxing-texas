From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-13 21:57:20 UTC

We took a friend of Batrick's (Ace) on his first letterboxing
adventure today in Houston. The boys really enjoyed finding some of
the boxes in the memorial park area (and goofing around). We hid a
small, simple box, Orange You Glad? in celebration of the adventure.

Did I say goofing around? As they are 7 & 8 year old boys, they were
tossing the box in the air and catching it. One of them dropped it,
so the corner of the lid is cracked. We went ahead and hid it
anyway. I'm going to replace the box in the next few days before it
rains, but wanted to let you know if you find a box with a cracked
corner, that would be the boys'. Pretty typical--at least it was the
box and not their heads.

Dewberry
