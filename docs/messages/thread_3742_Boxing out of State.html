Boxing out of State

From: Pastry Princess (hookem77532@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-08-19 13:30:29 UTC
I just wanted to share a grand adventure we had this weekend. I'll
try not to put any spoilers. My mom aka: boonesburrow, monkey biz,
and myself went boxing in Louisiana and Mississippi. We started off
going to Natchez Mississippi Sat. morning. Monkey Biz learned lots
of interesting facts at the Natchez Cemetary. This is one of the
most beautiful and history inriched cemetaries I have ever been to.
If you ever go find out the story about the turning Angel.
Then we went to Emerald Mound. It is only 1 of 2 Indian Mounds of
it sorts in the US. The other one being up north. We were able to
climb
to the top and it was amazing.

Next we were off to boxing in North Louisiana. The kids ended up
getting to walk across a suspended bridge in the middle of the
woods. They were scared at 1st but then decided it was pretty cool
and wanted to go back that way. Next we went to meet up with a lady
named Zinni. She is sooo very nice and was lonely to get some boxes
put in her area. What she didn't know is I had put some together
before I ever left Texas and then had carved 2 more while I was there.

Long story short: If you need some kid friendly, fun adventure, and
want to learn alot of history while in North Louisiana or Natchez
Mississippi. Check out the boxes placed there.
In all planted 1 in Mississippi and 5 in Lousiana. Found 8 in all.

Where have you gone that has been a grand adventure out of state?
