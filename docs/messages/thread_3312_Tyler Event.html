From: bluebutterflytexas (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-01-26 15:20:13 UTC

Thanks to everyone who braved the cold , rainy weather to attend the event. We had a GREAT time!! I did not get a complete total but, I think we had between 30-35 people . It was a success. A special thank-you goes to Lucy Locket who did a great job on the event stamp. Also, thanks Corazon for the door prizes. Batty Girl had a hard time getting there on I-20, we are glad you made it! Thanks Grizzled Gryphon for making it a memorable evening for all of us.

     Lucy, we missed you! Everyone was looking forward to meeting you. My thoughts and prayers are with you. We will have to do this again when the weather gets warmer!

Happy Boxing,
