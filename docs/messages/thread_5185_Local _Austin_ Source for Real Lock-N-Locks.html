I was out at lunch time today and checked at a few asian markets in the area (North Austin) and came up with Lock'N'Lock boxes at the Hana World Market on Parmer. I bought some of the 15oz rectangular boxes for $2.29.

If we get enough local interest perhaps we can put together a group order for some of these little plastic gems.
