Don't Forget Mystery Letterboxes

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2010-01-24 17:46:56 UTC
Lately it has become apparent to me that many new letterboxers are not aware of some Mystery boxes located in parks where they recently found other boxes. Many boxes are listed as Mystery simply to keep them off the radar of Park Staff, not to make them harder to find by letterboxers, so check them out before going on your next trip.

Silver Eagle
