New Box In Northeast Tx

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-04-25 18:45:31 UTC

My wife and I went to Lufkin this weekend to visit a friend
and then spent the night in a B&B outside of Nacogdoches. The
weather was great and we enjoyed walking around town, especially
at the SFA Arboretum. I managed to place a box (Sam I Am)
in the SFA Experimental Forest outside of Nacogdoches, another
great place to visit. I will post clues as soon as LBNA site
is working again. I only had time to find one box in the area,
Barefoot Lucy's Pack 403 box in Ratcliff Rec Area. It was
doing well with a few visits and the park looked like a great
place to camp. I highly recommend visiting this part of TX
and I hope to get farther north when I have more time. The
wildflowers were great, even if they were not Bluebonnets.

Silver Eagle.
