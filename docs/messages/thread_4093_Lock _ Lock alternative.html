From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2009-05-21 04:25:47 UTC
Rubbermaid is making boxes now with 4 locking tabs like Lock and Lock. They're called Lock-Its. I picked up a couple at Kroger, but haven't seen them anywhere else yet, but they look great. I bought some small boxes from Dixie Graphics online and the service was very good. Dixie is a letterboxer and has other letterbox-related items. She sent an LTC with the order that was fantastic. Just a little plug. I'd rather buy letterboxing supplies from letterboxers when I can.
