Memorial Park 2012

From: Blue Letterboxer (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-10-18 20:40:47 UTC
I know others have already reported what is going on at the park, so if I am repetitive, please forgive me.


I cannot tell you how sad I was today, as I drove up to Memorial Park, and looked at the scene before me. They are cutting the timber in the park, and ALL the Multi-use Trails are still closed,  but there are still people using them. I looked behind the baseball field, and there are still lots of "logs" piled back there. I chose to go over to the Rugby field, and walk that trail since the one by the BB field was so muddy. I am sure it is just as bad as the purple and yellow trail I was on. I stepped over the barriers, and made my way down the purple trail, looking for "Foghorn Leghorn" just in case it was still there. (Note to Baby Bear, The pine tree and the box are gone.) They have cut down all the big pine trees and left very little. I saw a loaded log-truck exiting the park, so they are still in the process of cutting the timber. I called the number on the sign for info, and got this message: "Memorial Park: All multi-use trails are closed" that was all I got. They are not doing anything to the areas they have already cut, and who knows when they will. I am sure a high percentage of the boxes there are missing. So very sad!
One of my all time favorite boxes "Llawnmower Llookout",  has probably been destroyed. I guess once they finish and get the trails open again, we can have a gathering and plant more boxes.
Boots, I am sure it will be a while before you are able to safely hide your box again.

Blue Butterfly
