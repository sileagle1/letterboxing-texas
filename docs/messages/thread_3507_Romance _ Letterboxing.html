Romance & Letterboxing

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-04-21 15:23:35 UTC
This last weekend I celebrated my anniversary by taking Mrs. Eagle to
a B&B in Fredericksburg for a romantic get-away without kids. We
have been to this area many times and it is a great place to visit
with many things to do, including shopping, hiking, eating &
drinking, and thanks to all of you, letterboxing. Since many boxes
are placed in areas we both like to visit, especially the wine series
by Baby Bear, I was able to please the LSS and letterbox! In fact,
at one point she commented that it was a great trip because we both
got to do what we each liked at the same time. For instance, when we
went shopping in town, which I hate, I still had fun by going to a
store that had over 100 free samples of food. Then when we went to
Wildseed Farms (a place like the Antique Rose Emporium only bigger),
I sat outside and listened to a live band while she browsed. Then at
the wineries, we both tasted, then I found the box. Even hiking at
the parks worked for both of us since she could look for birds while
I stamped into the boxes. I highly recommend this area to everyone!

Silver Eagle

P.S. I placed 2 new boxes to further entice you: Horton 2 and Turkey
Peak.
