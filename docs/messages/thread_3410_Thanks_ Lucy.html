Thanks, Barefoot Lucy, for taking care of my one and only East Texas box, Boxcar Willie.  The stamp was broken and the logbook was full, so I recarved the stamp and made a new logbook and Lucy replaced both for me, plus doing a little "box cleanup" on a box that's been in place since March 17, 2006.  Isn't it wonderful how letterboxers look after each other's little treasures in the woods? 
    
Last week I replaced Bach's Box on the SMU Campus in Dallas, in the same place where it had been for just over two years.   Today I replaced logbooks for The Blues Brothers and Ornette Coleman, my two boxes in the Ft Worth Botanic Gardens, and noted that both are still happily well-hidden where they've been since Oct., 2006.    
 
It really is satisfying to have a box you like, a stamp you're pleased with, and a location that visitors enjoy -- and have that box survives a long time with lots of visitors, because other letterboxers are taking such care!   
 
Thanks, all!  Happy letterboxing!
