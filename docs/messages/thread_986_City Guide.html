City Guide

From: monkeytoes108 (cruschhaupt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-03 13:01:49 UTC
While I was adding the subterfuge clause to one of my boxes, a
thought occurred. Any of you coming to find these boxes would be
strangers to Victoria, coming from many miles away. You may want to
know a little about my town. You may wonder what else there is to
do. You may be hungry. What to do?

To make your letterboxing excursions to my little town as pleasant
as possible, I added a guide to Victoria to my webpage and linked it
to all my boxes. It has my favorite local resturaunts that are in
close proximity to each box. That way, you won't have to suffer a
pre-cooked McMeal.

It also has a very brief history of Victoria, and I will add other
places of interest that do not have a box nearby, in case you want
to hang out around town (and maybe place your own box). Please
consult at your leasure before any Victoria day trips you may have.


Anyway, I thought this would be a good idea to share with everyone.
Most of us not in Houston, (Lucky B***ards!) are not familiar with
the areas we box in and clues to things other than area boxes would
make a welcome addition.

-MonkeyToes


ps- Here's the link:

http://www.geocities.com/cruschhaupt/Letterboxing/Guide.html
