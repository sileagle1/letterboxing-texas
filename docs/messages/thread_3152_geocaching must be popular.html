geocaching must be popular

From: Sandy Milfeld (pugapalooza@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-11-02 00:18:44 UTC-05:00
I don't know much at all about how widespread geocaching is these
days, but it must be everywhere. We were out for a walk today and my
son found some rocks stuffed in the hollow of where a tree branch had
recently been cut off a large oak. It was the oddest thing to see
these very white rocks sitting in this hole. He asked if he could
pull the rocks out to see if something was hidden there. We weren't
letterboxing at the time, and the hollow was obviously too small to
hide a box of any kind, so I said "sure." I was kind of curious,
myself. Well, just as we are fishing out the last rock, a man comes
jogging by and says, "You must be geocaching!" I was speechless! If
he had said, "You must be letterboxing", I would have fainted dead
away. Geocaching must be popular!
