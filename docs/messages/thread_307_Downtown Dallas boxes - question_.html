Tiger wrote:
< some combination of activities with letterboxing.>>

Gosh, Tiger, I guess your response makes me realize that my questions
sounded pretty dumb;-)!

Let me explain: We're trying to decide how to set up the clues and
locations for 3 urban letterboxes. We'd like to link all 3 because they
have a common theme, but they are scattered over quite a large area. We
were considering including helpful directions for driving/walking/public
transportation from one box to another, but the clues began gaining epic
proportions.

It occurred to us that one way to decide how much info to include was to
try to gauge whether most visitors to the letterboxes would be from
around the DFW Metroplex, or whether a series of 3 might attract folks
from further away in the state ( and out-of-state).

Thank you to those who responded enthusiastically - we'll send you the
clues privately to beta test and give us some feedback! It will be a few
weeks before they're all ready, but I'll let you know!
