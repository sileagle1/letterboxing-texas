Huzzah! Huzzah!

Her Royal Majesty Queen Beeloved and His Gentle Lordship Beneficus Rex hereby extend to all within hearing an invitation for a gathering of fun, food, and frolicking (for letterboxes)! Those who care, bring potluck fare for friends to share. For artisans who would place a treasure there, a Camelot theme would seem most fair.

Journey a short distance north of Lake Lewisville and join us at South Lakes Park in Denton, Texas. The park is located on Hobson Road, about one mile south of the corner of I-35E and Teasley lane.

Until then, Fare Thee Well!
