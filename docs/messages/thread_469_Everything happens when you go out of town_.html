Wow,
I missed a lot of news while I was up in the Sunflower State!

Carie, I am so sorry that two-legged reptile did such a horrible
thing. He's probably not a very happy person and takes it out on
others.

Congrats TeamKing! Wow, I am only at F28 but I'll keep plugging
away.

I placed boxes 3 and 4 of my Lost Texan series in Eastern KS this
weekend. The first two are in OK. My goal is to put two in every
state I visit. There are some great boxes up there to find. We had
fun finding some by Wes and Jess and Ryan and Allison.

The other cool thing is I FINALLY converted a non-boxer to becoming
a full-blown boxer. Look for Meatwad's stamp in KS boxes. After
getting blank looks from everyone else I finally convinced someone
how fun LB is! Yippee!
Glad to be back in the Lone Star State
Kantexan
