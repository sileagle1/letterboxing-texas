From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-02-04 19:19:09 UTC-06:00
When I have a full logbook in my letterboxes, I generally like to leave it and just add another one if space permits (personal preference), because many boxers enjoy taking the time to look through the old logbooks to see who has been there before them. Basically, unless the owner of the box has asked you to remove it, it's probably better to just leave it and try to notify the owner. If they don't respond, at least you've tried. One thing some boxers will do is carry a couple of extra logbooks with them in their backpack and leave the extra one in the box they've found in addition to the full logbook. Of course, many boxers do want to remove their full logbooks, but it's best to check with them before removing it.
 
Celticlions are among the original letterboxers in the state and are definitely one of the nicest families you will ever meet. I believe their family is on a little break from letterboxing right now, but hopefully they will be back full force before long. They certainly add a lot to this sport. My suggestion would be to just return the logbook to the box next time you find yourself down that way.
 
Puddle Splasher
