Stargazer's Obituary

From: Sandy Milfeld (pugapalooza@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2008-01-14 21:51:20 UTC-06:00

Fort Worth resident and letterboxer Mrs. Star Gazer died Jan. 13, 2008, after being bitten by a spider while letterboxing in the area.

A service was held Monday at her home in Fort Worth. She was interred in a box in her closet.

Star Gazer was born in December 2004 to parents, Atlas Quest and LbNA. She was a proud member of the P-50 Club and the F-100 Club. Her family wrote: We always thought it would be the poison ivy that got her. She will be missed. She is survived by her daughters, Hottopic and Skittles, and her son, Sea Dragon.

Her letterboxing arrangements have been entrusted to Ms. Black Widow.
