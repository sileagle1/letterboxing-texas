From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2012-05-09 16:48:14 UTC
Our daughter and 15 year old grandson did some volunteering at a local park. Here's what she found:

"Some of the things Colby and I found while picking up trash on a creek path at Jesse Jones County Park and Nature Center. (Colby had to do a volunteer service for a project in school).
- A small soccer ball
- A TV remote
- Two plastic balls from a McDonalds ball pit (in completely different areas)
- A baseball
- A little green plastic hand
- Three newspapers still rolled and in their plastic sleeves
- A ping pong ball with a shamrock on it
- A bottle of Visine
- Two hospital gloves again in two different areas (I'd hate to know what those were used for)
and
- A thousand and one plastic water bottles (like people hike here and just throw their bottels in the bushes)."

At least she didn't mention a letterbox.
