Almost there, and here comes the cold and the rain.  Hopefully Saturday will be clear.  I think all is in place.  Some last minute items:


**** Time on Saturday.  Officially we don't get hall until 10am, so if you come earlier than that and it is still locked up, then plan on coming after 10:15am (as they would not let us get it early).


1) Creek crossing - As Bell Motel stated, may be deeper water by Event time.   I do NOT suggest hopping across rocks at edge of falls.  Slippery and dangerous.   Silver Eagle and I are going to check on Friday to see if easier way to cross creek downstream.  Also, there is a way to access the trails on the other side via businesses or State park building on main road outside the park.  We will have a map of this at the event.


2) Remember to bring your own drinks.  Food is shared with all, but each group is responsible for own drinks. 


3) Weather - I would bring rain gear, and layers of clothing.


4) Gear - hiking stick is good for flushing out snakes.   Flashlight or headlight for night boxing.  Hiking shoes.


5) HAVE FUN - Do what you want to do.  Find boxes, exchange with other boxers, or just sit and talk to some good people (this have become my favorite part). 


See some of you on Friday, and the rest of you on Saturday.  Be Careful driving up and on the trails.
