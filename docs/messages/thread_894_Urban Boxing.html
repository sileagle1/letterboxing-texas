Urban Boxing

From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2005-06-05 22:20:23 UTC-05:00
Had an interesting urban box last weekend. . .

We were in Cleveland (Ohio) visiting good friends and I decided I
wanted to plant a box at the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame downtown. So, I
checked LBNA and Atlas Quest but didn't see that any were there. Then
I checked with a Cleveland area boxer to confirm and they mentioned
something to the effect of 'it might be hard to put one there, it is
a very urban location'. Well, this made me laugh. When I think of the
urban boxes in Houston and Dallas alone, I figure we can urban box
with the best of them! LOL

I ended up placing the box right out in front of the entrance in the
ever-present flower bed.

It had a first finder within a week. Yeah! Success!

TeamKing
