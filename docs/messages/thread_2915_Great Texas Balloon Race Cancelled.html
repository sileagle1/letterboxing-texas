Great Texas Balloon Race Cancelled

From: bluebutterflytexas (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-08 13:13:48 UTC
Just wanted to let anybody know that may be headed to Longview for the
Balloon Race/activities this week-end, it has been cancelled. Our
family looks forward to this event each year and are disappointed by
the news. The grounds where the event is held, is too soggy to bear the
weight of vehicles and ballons. The heavy rains here in East Texas has
closed roads and flooded homes.

For more info: http://www.ktbb.com/news/View.php?ArticleID=13831

Just wanted to give a heads up.

My grandson spent the night with me last night, and we took a walk around eight p.m. As we returned home, we were surprised to look up and see a beautiful hot air balloon passing over our house. Then this morning at around 6:45 a.m. (he is FOUR), as we were going into McDonalds for our breakfast, we saw two more hot air balloons take off, and they gently floated right over us. He was thrilled (as was I) to be able to look right inside of them and see the flames. I'm not sure where they were going, but I certainly wished I were in one.
