Are there souls who yearn to meet some woodsy spirits
in a spooky forest in the towne of Houston?
If so, ye must watch and wait and gather ye wits
Your reward shall be boxes, company and fun.

The month should be the eighth; its name is off by two.
All Hallow's Eve comes, but not for a dozen more
The moon's full opalescence is five days past view,
But, daytime woods host friendly sprites & creatures more.
