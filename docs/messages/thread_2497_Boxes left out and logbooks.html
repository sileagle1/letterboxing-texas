Re:  Boxes left out and logbooks Thank you so much.  It turns out the Raf’s Bat isn’t missing after all!   Yay!  

I’m not upset about the moons.  Mainly I hate to lose the logbooks.  Any stamp can be re-carved, but the logbooks are irreplaceable.  All of that history...just gone.  I’ve always fought the urge to frequently replace logbooks, so that if one goes missing, I only lose the most recent additions...but I deep down feel that the logbooks, as well as the stamps, are there for other boxers, not me.  One of my favorite parts of finding a box is looking through the logbook to see all of the previous finders.  And it is especially exciting to find one that’s been around for a long, long time.  So, instead of replacing, I try to visit my boxes frequently to check in with the logbooks, see who all’s come by.  It’s surprising how many people stamp in but don’t log finds on the web.  

Anyway, I’m cool with it.  I’ll won’t replant the moons, but I have several new boxes ready to go out closer to spring.

Stargazer
