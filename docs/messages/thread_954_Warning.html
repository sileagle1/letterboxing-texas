From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-06-26 03:53:09 UTC
After a fun day of picking blueberries in Montgomery
County, I talked the family into letterboxing on
the way home. Our last stop was Terry Hershey Park
to get the Star Wars boxes. Couldn't find the first
one but got the other 2. However, when we returned
to the car it had been broken into and my wife's purse
stolen with everything in it. I just wanted to warn
everyone not to leave valuables in the car even if
only gone for 15 minutes like we were. It really
galls me that somebody would take advantage of others
trying to enjoy nature. Nothing is sacred anymore.
I have to admit I'm pretty down right now. Between this
breakin and the idiot stealing boxes I have a very low
opinion of the Human Race right now (letterboxers excluded).

Silver Eagle
