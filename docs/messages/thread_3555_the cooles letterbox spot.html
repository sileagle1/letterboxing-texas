What a great topic!  I have seen so many amazing spots because of letterboxing, it is impossible to choose just one.  Some of my very favorites have included the bridge in Waco, the most astounding view from the 360 bridge in Austin, the Sea Center Texas (this has become one of my kids all time favorite places), the giant president heads in Houston (I almost crashed my car when I rounded the corner and saw a 50 foot tall bust of George Bush, I just wasn't quite prepared I guess.)  I guess if I had to choose just one it would be Stonehenge 2 near Kerrville...the most astounding thing I have ever seen.  This box really gives meaning to the phrase "only in Texas"!  MO5
