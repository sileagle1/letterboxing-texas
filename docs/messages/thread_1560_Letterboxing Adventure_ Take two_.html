From: (kacmbc@aol.com) | Date: 2005-12-31 22:37:07 UTC-05:00
I managed to drag my husband (not through any weeds or closed trails!) to Austin today in search of letterboxes.  As a reward, he was "allowed" to go to his favorite bike shop, Jack and Adam's after lunch.  I also planted a letterbox during our outing and will be posting clues soon!
 
Boxes we found:
Lightning Sword
Spider Sword
Keep Austin Weird
Mexican Bat
Texas in the USA
Mad Dog Margarita - I so badly wanted a margarita but still had to go running!
Oakwood Cemetery- awesome hiding place!!
Bevo- I didn't have burnt orange ink so I stamped in Aggie Maroon!  :)
 
Boxes we attempted:
Longhorn
Texas Sized Letterbox
 
We had an awesome day and the weather was perfect.  We even managed to get a four mile run in around Zilker Park!  Thanks to all who planted the boxes that we found today!
 
Runs for Chocolate  :)
