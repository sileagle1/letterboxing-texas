4th Annual TX LB Event - Yet Another Venue

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-11-13 20:15:17 UTC
I'm having trouble finding a place in the Central Region that meets all
the criteria for our Event and is available, so I cheated and looked
outside the Central Region. Lake Somervill State Park Birch Creek Unit
is in Burleson County, which is just barely out of the Central Region,
but it has over 100 campsites, 3 covered picnic pavilions and several
miles of trails. Even though the Event will be too soon for
wildflowers, this area is a great place for viewing them and might be a
good theme for the Event. I plan to try booking a pavilion tomorrow so
hopefully this spot will work for everyone.

Silver Eagle
