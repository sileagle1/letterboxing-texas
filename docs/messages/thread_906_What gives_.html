We've gotten about 10 new members in the last ten days
- now don't think I'm complaining, but what's up with
that?

You new folks, what brought you here? Did you see an
article? Planning on traveling to Texas soon? Heard
that we are such a bright and articulate bunch that
you just HAD to be a part of our group? Maybe you had
a coupon? Hmmm?

Whatever brings you here, we welcome each and every
one of you with our big ol' Texas arms wide open! If
you haven't already done so, speak up and tell us a
little about yourselves - how long you've been
letterboxing, what you like about the hobby, what you
hope to get from or share with us, what part of the
state you are from? Anything you think would be of
interest to us!

This might also be a good time for old members to
update the group about themselves so the new folks can
get to know us too!
