The Epic Visit to Momma Letterboxing Odyssey

From: tonialmo (tonialmo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-08-06 02:21:02 UTC
Big Red Coyote (who hasn't joined the group yet) and I just concluded
the "Epic Visit to Momma Letterboxing Odyssey". My mother lives in Big
Spring and we live in Uvalde. Starting July 30th with one in
Rocksprings and concluding today with one in Leakey, we found a total
of 26 boxes. All of them were great! Only one box that we sought was
missing and there was only one box on our "agenda" that we decided not
to search for because it required a hike. We had the Jeep overheat and
have to be worked on in Abilene and it was after 2 pm when we got back
on the road. Another reason for skipping it is that another attraction
that the clue to the box references is closed on Monday. We want to
visit the first site and then move on to find the box (when it's
cooler). Letterboxing made a long, boring drive so much fun. We get
to see places/things that we normally wouldn't see. We went one route
there and another back. We scoped out a few places along the route
that we want to place our boxes. Now to get carving until we can
afford another expedition.
