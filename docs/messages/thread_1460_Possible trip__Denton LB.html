Since Lucy mentioned this infamous Denton box (DAY of the DEAD):

 

<< I plan on coming up through Greenville and possibly heading up to Denton briefly (to get that infamous box that requires a Miata among other things!), >>

 

I just wanted to announce as publicly as possible that The Gentleman pulled that box a few weeks ago. At the time he created it, he was going to school in Denton, and was able to keep an eye on that box – but is now working and living in Dallas. We were always a little concerned about the location of that box – he had placed it about 6 feet off the ground, forgetting letterboxers like his 5 foot high mother and others who cannot reach that far. Which is why Viewfinder had to utilize her creativity and her Miata to get it! We were also concerned because the box was in a location which made it very hard to be subtle in retrieving it. The stamp is very detailed and is part of a series of boxes based on old cult Zombie movies. The second box is all ready to go, so if anyone out there is interested, keep an eye on CelticLions clues for both boxes coming soon!
