Re: Austin Article

From: Dee Pirate (dezwrestles@mail.utexas.edu) | Date: 2009-04-13 00:13:10 UTC
I thought the article was good in covering the bases of letterboxing. It was very enthused, excited and portrayed the hobby in a fun light which is great. Hopefully we get some cool new boxers out of it.
But as an Austin boxes I feel that the reporter just spilt some of the better Austin boxing secrets. I know some of the boxes she mentioned she didn't contact the placers before placing them on the pedal-stool for noxers. "The overturned purple bucket in that garden? Definitely." That box has already been stolen and is missing. The haiku quotes being published with out author permission or even without their author's trail name is sub par ethical in my opinion as well. I am glad none of my boxes were mentioned and I feel sorry for those who were blurted about with out the owners consent.
In all she stressed it was all very secretive, with out keeping it of much a secret.

Dee Pirate
