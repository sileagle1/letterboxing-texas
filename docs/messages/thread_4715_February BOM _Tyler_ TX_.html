Here are the cluesto the February Box of the Month in Tyler, TX:

Park in the Robert E. Lee parking lot on Donnybrook. Find the Rose Rudman trail across from the Agricultural Sciences building. Cross the bridge and keep on that trail. Take a right at the Bell Gazebo. Go to the third bench. Stand directly in front of the light post marked C15. Directly behind the light post is a medium sized tree. The box is behind the tree.

Please rehide and cover well.

If you'd like I can add you to a list of people that get an AQ mail when something changes with box. For example location, or stamps. Just AQ mail me askinig to be on that list.
