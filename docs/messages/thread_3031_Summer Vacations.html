So, now we know where SE and BB went, and that they splurge when the kids aren't along :).  My goal is to someday have all 50 states under my belt, I don't have that many more to go, but Hawaii is a LONG way off.
 
So I wonder, where did the rest of us go for summer vacation?  We started off the summer in the pristine Upper Peninsula of Michigan, a trip that I would highly recommend to anyone who likes to be outdoors.  It is one of the most beautiful places on earth.  (And MUCH cooler than Houston in June!)  Then we went to South America (Chile) to visit my Mother in Law during July.  It was an amazing trip, and another beautiful place.  Look up "The Valley of the Moon" sometime...it is a landscape that is like no other.  My favorite week of the whole summer was a week at Crystal Beach, we are lucky to have neighbors who loan us their beach house from time to time.  Just me, the kids, and a near miss of a tropical storm.  I wish summer could last forever.  MO5
