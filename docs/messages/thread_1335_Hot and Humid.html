From: (kacmbc@aol.com) | Date: 2005-09-27 16:16:26 UTC-04:00
They DO help!!!  My husband uses them for adventure racing where they dip the bandana into water and put them around their necks.  The coolant stuff swells up and then once it dries up, the bandana returns to normal size.  My husband LOVES it!!  We also dip it into cold water/ice water to make it even cooler!  Another idea if you need to be cooled off quickly ( another adventure racing tip) use the instant ice packs.  He carries a couple of them in his pack when they go out on long treks or bike legs or paddle legs and even if it is cool for several minutes, it is better than nothing!!
