Moved one box, planted another

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2013-02-27 02:53:03 UTC
We went to check on my box, The TALE Brothers, at Stephen F. Austin SP today, because it hasn't been reported found in a year. The old entrance to the Copperhead Trail has been blocked by a gate and moved, the first part of the trail rerouted, which made the clues to my box completely wrong and the box unfindable. Rather than leave the box there and try to change the clues, I picked it up and will rehide it in Bastrop or Buescher, a place more appropriate for the box. I placed a new box in Colorado County to make up for it. We also found Silver Eagle's new Fayette County boxes today, and we highly recommend them. See Y'all soon!

Boots Tex, with Lone Star Quilter
