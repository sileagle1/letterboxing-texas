Hello Silver Eagle. We recently returned from our vist to Durango Co.
and found your box Treasure of the San Juans...it was quite an exciting
find as i had my Mom and Husband with me, neither of them box but i
think they are a little more interested in it since we had such a great
time finding your box. The scenery was absolutely amazing, the
"treasure" is still there and you have quite a few treasures left. My
only compliant was the mosquitoes...LOL they were big and thirsty! We
really didn't notice the mosquitoes until we got off the main trail and
boy did they come out! I was glad i brought my hoodie with me to cover
up during the stamping and re-hiding...

Thanks Silver Eagle for such a great box, great hike, and an
introduction to a new, beautiful place to visit while in Durango.

Carol

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2011-07-09 14:03:38 UTC
I'm glad you enjoyed it (except for mosquitoes) and that there was still some treasure for you. The area around Durango is beautiful and Andrews Lake, where this box is located, is a great place to experience it. It is the site of my very first letterbox find in Colorado way back in 2003, and I think that box (Andrews Lake) is still there, though tricky to find. I had been to Colorado several times before, but letterboxing lead me to several new places that year and it started a trend of incorporating boxing with vacations to see what the locals like. It is still one of the best aspects of letterboxing for me.

Silver Eagle
