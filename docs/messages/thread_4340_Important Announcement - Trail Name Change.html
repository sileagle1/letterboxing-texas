Important Announcement - Trail Name Change

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-01-03 18:41:09 UTC-06:00

I have been asked by the letterboxer formerly known as Lightning to make this very important announcement.

In light of the fact that he has moved along in scouting and is now a member of the Dragon patrol, and that he has chosen to carve his own signature stamp in the image of a dragon, and to stamp said dragon in a lovely emerald green shade, and further, because he has developed an interest in Latin and has long had an interest in astronomy, the letterboxer formerly known as Lightning has put much thought and consideration into a new trail name and has arrived at this: Draconis viridis.

As often happens, a familiar version of the name will work itself out; in the meantime, I will be proud to have Draconis viridis accompany me to the gathering in March, where his new stamp will make its formal debut.

Thank you for your attention to this announcement.
--
Barefoot Lucy
