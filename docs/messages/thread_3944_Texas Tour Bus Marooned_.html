After capturing the TTB2 I put them scallywags to work. Swabbing ye
old' the poop-deck and mending the canvas. Then a steady breeze came
up over the South Seas, so I took the helm of the xebec and shoved
off. It was steady sailing `til I let shipmate Anna Nicole talk the
wheel. A'vast, we landed shipwreck on lake Grapevine. Furious, I
decided to make the scurvy lot walk the Rockledge! Almost a mile they
trudged along the left lake trail `til in sight of a plank bridge.
Looking rather shaky. I veered them right at the Y, 40 paces to power
mast. Following the bowline NE into the woods the landlubbers took
cover between two pine saplings 15 steps north, just on the south side
