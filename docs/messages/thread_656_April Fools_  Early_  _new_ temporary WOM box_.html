From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-03-08 18:23:30 UTC

This is no joke!

North of where two Jesters meet
A Fool lies in wait. . .

Happy April Fools

We have created a temporary WOM box (thru April 6th) to celebrate
that deliciously silly holiday April Fools (a particular favorite
when you're a 7 year old boy). Clues for this box are hidden inside
our Rabbit House box. Because some of you have already visited that
box and would have no reason to go back, we're just letting you know
about it. We won't be posting this new tricky (the trick is that
there's no trick) box on LBNA, we're leaving it "unpublished", but
you are more than welcome to share the location of the clues with
any letterboxing friends or email buddies.

We've included a different kind of first finder prize than our usual
bookmark, more of a treat than a trick. Would love to hear what the
First Finder thinks!

Isn't it funny? The Rabbit for Rabbit House was my first carving.
I was so proud of it, and now it looks very crude, especially
compared to the intricate work of say the April Fools Carving. I
considered re-carving Rabbit, but decided to leave it alone. I
wonder how others feel about their first carvings?

If all goes well, we'll be placing Back in the Saddle later today as
well.

Dewberry of
--Gramatrick
