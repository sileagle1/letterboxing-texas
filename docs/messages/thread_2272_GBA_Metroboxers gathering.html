Hello everyone!

I'm sorry to be so late in posting this message but I
just wanted to let everyone know that the girls and I
had a a blast at the Great Book Adventure Saturday!!
It was wonderful getting to meet everyone and putting
names to faces..or rather stamps to faces. A great
big Thank You!! to Dewberry and TeamKing for all their
hard work in getting everything ready.

We are sorry we missed out on the fun at Memorial
Park. We got lost (imagine that, coming from a small
town in the Piney Woods of East Texas and getting lost
in Houston) and by the time we found where ya'll were
going to be at it was around 3pm. We did plant a box
in the butterfly garden so it wasn't a wasted trip.
Monday we went to Galveston and planted one at the
girls' favorite resturant on the island, Rain Forest
Cafe.

We can't wait to go to the next one. We will be in
Arlington this weekend for FrightFest at Six Flags,
planning to plant one somewhere there and four or five
more along the way. The weekend of November 12th we
will be in Dallas again for SnowFest at Market Hall.
The weekend of November 18th is already booked solid
there is a homeschool convention in Shreveport that I
want to go to so we will already be missing the
Dickens on the Strand parade but I will discuss it
with the girls and see which they would prefer to go,
if all the chatter on the way home from Houston Monday
evening is any indication...it looks like we will be
seeing all of you again the 18th :-)

Sandy
mom of DragonFly Beauties aka Atoy Adventurers
