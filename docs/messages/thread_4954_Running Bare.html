From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2012-02-17 01:27:17 UTC
I went to Memorial Park today to find some boxes and do some maintenance on my NADA CLUE boxes, and I saw something that made me think of IRA Boxer's event box at the TALE8 event last year. It was called "Running Bare". I was on the purple trail down near where it goes down to Buffalo Bayou and a man ran by me, literally running bare. As in, nothing on but running shoes. So, wouldn't that be considered illegal? Should I have called the cops? He ran on down the trail until he was out of sight (thank goodness) and I never saw him again. The trails are marked as "closed", but I ignored that and had already seen a lady walking her dog on the red trail. I don't know if this guy had a plan in case he saw someone, but he basically ignored me and kept on running. I just thought you should be warned.

Boots Tex
