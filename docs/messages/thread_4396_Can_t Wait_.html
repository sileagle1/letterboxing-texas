From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-02-28 18:40:52 UTC-06:00

I would so LOVE to ride in the backseat with y'all one of these days!

In another life, when I worked at a job that called for lots of travel, I had a variety of travel companions. My favorite was Betzy, who could "explore" with the best of 'em. We went to Austin together numerous times, but we always managed to get lost along the way because we had to detour to explore something that caught our attention. I really liked traveling with her because she had no problem whatsoever with my making a left when I should've made a right (mainly because she was just as guilty of the same thing), and getting so far off the beaten path that we had to get out the map and HOPE we could figure out where we were. We found so many cool things that way and had a great time. We traveled very well together, but we made other folks CRAZY when they had to ride with us. We made a pact between ourselves not to ever let anyone else drive because it wouldn't be nearly as much fun!

Again, I wish I had known about letterboxing in those days, and I'll bet Betzy would've been a letterboxer too...sadly, I've lost track of her.

Lucy
