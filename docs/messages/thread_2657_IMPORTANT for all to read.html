Tomorrow is JBK's birthday and I happen to know this because she
mentions it in the clues of her March Holiday Series.

Besides becoming quite a prolific Texas planter, she returned a few
days ago from a trip on which she placed 30 boxes across the South.
30 boxes...In a week! She has also begun the monthly holiday series
which I and others have been enjoying. Obviously, she is really
contributing to our letterboxing enjoyment.

We all also know she's had a tough road this past year. Her good
friend Joan passed away, someone she shared several of her favorite
hobbies with, including letterboxing. However, even though you can
tell she feels it deeply, she never complains, never looks for
sympathy - she faces it matter-of-factly and deals with it. I really
admire her for that and I hope that when I lose someone close to me I
can do the same. I feel very fortunate to count her among the many
friends I've made while letterboxing.

ANYWAY- would you each take the time to send her a birthday message
tomorrow? Her email address is tigger32167@yahoo.com. If you really
wanted to do something special for her, you could plant a box in her
honor. Or do something else kind for her. And if you aren't so
inclined - that's cool too. Just thought I'd mention the
opportunity...

Back to your regularly scheduled letterboxing.
