Fall is the very best time to be out here. From now to Mid-November it is
just beautiful. The days are already cooling. After the hot summer - the
mornings now are a bit chilly - at 70. There are places in this country where
70 is a nice summer temperature. Those places also have something called
SNOW! We can suffer through June-July-August, knowing that December
through March will see days where the temperature might reach 70.
 
Today is going to be a busy one for us.....

it - Meet The Breed. We show up with dogs and printed information. The
object is to be sure people understand the size of the dogs, and the breed
specific problems etc. The worst thing we see is that people buy a cute
Great Dane puppy, then 8 months later it is "too big" and they dump it in
the dog pound.

El Paso. I guess you didn't know this. But according to our present government,
