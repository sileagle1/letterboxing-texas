From: Wouldnt you like to know! (love_bug24@msn.com) | Date: 2007-06-20 04:12:29 UTC
Our group is pretty laid-back and open to talk about just about
anything pertaining to letterboxing, and sometimes NOT pertaining to
letterboxing. Feel free to jump in and begin posting as soon as you
get this message back. I do ask, though, that you post an
introduction of yourself so the other members know that you are out
there, even if you just intend on lurking (and that's okay!). Just
let us know a little something about yourself, how you got involved
in letterboxing, and what your interest is in Texas letterboxing,
what your favorite color is - whatever you think is important!

I recieve and email asking me to introduce myself! So my name is
Kelli Smith I was born and raised in a little town out of Beaumont,
Texas. I evacauted to Marshall, Texas in Sept of 2005 from Hurricane
Rita! I am a single mom of two and just landed my first steady job 45
days ago and Marshall Regional Medical Center as a Unit Clerk. I am
my daughter's softball coach and son's cub scout leader (Webelos). I
meet Barefoot Lucy in June of 2007 at Dad and Lad (cub scout camp),
where my son, x-hubby, and I got introduced to letterboxing. I was
listening to Barefoot Lucy talk about the history of letterboxing and
had got takin in by how neat it all sounded and sat myself down and
listened to everything she had to say and would share with me! I felt
kinda like I was asking to many questions but she just kept answering
them like ti was nothing! Thank you for taking my under wing and
talking and sharing with me about it! Since then my son and I have
talked on several occasional aboput startign and have been trying to
think of names and what we would like our stamps to be. Also since I
have meet a wonderful new man and we have got him intrested in it to,
so as a family we plan on this being our little adventure! It has
been hard to find anything to get my son intrested in since we
evacauted he has not made the transition to the move and think seem
to have his attention! Anyway I can't wait to get my feet wet and get
started. My favorite color is red and favorite animal is panda bears
so more than likely my stamp and name might have something to do with
Panda's. I am workign on getting my whole cub den into this as well
so maybe we can plant some more boxes in the Marshall area! Thanks
again for welcoming me into the group and Barefoot Lucky for doing
the cub scout camp in June! It have opened up new door for some boys
and they had a great time!
