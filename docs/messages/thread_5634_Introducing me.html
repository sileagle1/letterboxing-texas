Hello fellow Texas letterboxers,
My name is Marisa. Trail name is LadyRisa. I’m on the far east side of the DFW metroplex. So far all my letterboxing has been in Dallas area. I participate in a lot of crafting swaps on the swap-bot website. Witch Kittin was a swap partner & mentioned letterboxing as one of her hobbies. I look forward to meeting other boxers. What else about me? I’m a city worker; I have a husband, 2 small kids, & a cat. So I stay pretty busy. Anyway, nice to meet you.

~LadyRisa
