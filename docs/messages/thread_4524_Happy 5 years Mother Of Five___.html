Re: Date of First Box ( was Re: [letterboxingtexas] Happy 5 years Mother Of Five!!!)

From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2010-04-28 19:32:31 UTC-05:00
My first day of letterboxing was Leap Day 2008 -- February 29, the day before TALE5.  My college roommate, Astro D, and her friend, Lionsmane, called to say they were driving in from Santa Fe, New Mexico to attend a Letterboxing Event, and asked if I wanted to tag along.  Sounded like fun to me.  They showed up on Thursday night with gifts for me -- a hand-made logbook, and a hand-carved stamp reading "Bell Motel".  We went out the next morning and found 15 boxes, the first being "Great Hills Park", the one closest to my house.  I couldn't believe how much fun I was having.  The next day we drove to Bastrop for TALE5, and met a crowd of great, friendly, artistic, fun-loving people.  I had even more fun!  I can't explain why this hobby brings me so much joy, but it really does.  I owe a huge debt of gratitude to Astro D, and everyone else I've met (or who has planted a box I've found) in letterboxing, for all these good times.
-- Bell Motel
