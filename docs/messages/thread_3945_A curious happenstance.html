A curious happenstance

From: Gryzzled Gryphon of Coryell County (gryzzgryph@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-08 03:58:06 UTC
I happened to be in the Metroplex today, paying my respects to the late
Greer Garson Fogelson, when it occurred to me that since I was in the
neighborhood (more or less) I should stop by Grapevine Lake to check on
certain property of mine. And so I did, and as I was approaching the
bridge that leads to said property I heard the most horrendous
cacophony imaginable (outside a fraternity house). Detouring from my
quest, I came upon a busload of rum-besotted pseudo-pirates "singing"
sea chanties. In the interest of public decency I left off my errand
and bundled the lot into the Crystal Cruiser and took them home. It
would be difficult to say when they will be fit to travel again as I
anticipate that a massive hangover is going to result in a massive
layover. I hope they have some spare booty stowed in that chest of
theirs.
Best regards, Gryzzled Gryphon of Coryell County
