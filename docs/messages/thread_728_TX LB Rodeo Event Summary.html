TX LB Rodeo Event Summary

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-03-21 18:22:45 UTC

A combination of good weather (75 degrees, partly cloudy), great
food, plentiful letterboxes, and some of the best people you will
ever meet made our Second TX LB Event a huge success. Over 50 people
attended and there was non-stop activity the entire day. My only
regret is that I did not get a chance to talk longer with more
people. The Wild West Clue game was a big hit and I tip my hat to
Celtic Lions for organizing it. I also want to give special thanks
to Wanda for driving all the way from Rhode Island, to the Louisiana
boxers who made the Event memorable, and to all Texas Letterboxers
that came and shared food, stamps and stories. Ya'll are the
greatest!

Silver Eagle

P.S. Some pictures I took have been posted in the Photos section
under album TLRE. I hope to add them to the Event website, too.
