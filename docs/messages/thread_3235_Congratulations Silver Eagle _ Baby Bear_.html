Congratulations Silver Eagle & Baby Bear!

From: SandiBox (sandibox@msn.com) | Date: 2007-12-25 04:42:19 UTC

Congratulations to Silver Eagle and Baby Bear for being honored with a Lifetime Achievement Letterboxing Award for their many contributions to letterboxing, not only in Texas, but throughout the US.  This recognition confirms what we already knew - that you are pioneers of the sport who exemplify the spirit of letterboxing.  Well deserved kudos to you both!

If you missed Wanda and Pete's post on the main list you can read it here:  http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa/message/61881.   The message has a link to Wanda and Pete's website where you can read the details of the award. 

This to me encompasses the sense of tradition and community I referred to in an earlier post.  I would encourage everyone, especially newer letterboxes to read this article.
