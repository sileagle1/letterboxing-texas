From: the PEAS (bowerspeas@mailaka.net) | Date: 2007-04-09 21:15:45 UTC-06:00
Below is the update I posted to the event listing on AQ today.  Again, all are welcome to this 4/21/07 gathering just south of Dallas!!  I also welcome your suggestions and thoughts. Looking forward to seeing many of you "at the park!"
S of the PEAS

**UPDATE 4/9/07**
OK ... we've narrowed it down. Since we are going to the Joe Pool/Cedar Hill area, let's start in Duncanville. Since this started out as "A Day at the Park," let's choose a city park where we can meet (~10:30 AM?), exchange, get the event stamp, chat, and eat lunch, and let the kids play.

The two city parks in Duncanville that we are considering are Lakeside Park (home of The Duncanville Dragonfly letterbox) and Lions Park (formerly Southside Park, home of Aslan's Letterbox). I have been to Lakeside and it would be fine. Has anyone been to the other park? The placer of Aslan's Letterbox mentions in her clues that it is a wonderful park, playground, picnic tables, etc.

Then after that, there are a few options. There are a few letterboxes at Cedar Hill Nature Preserve which is just a few blocks away. CHNP is free to enter and has nice hiking trails. Also, just beyond that is Cedar Hill State Park. This park has an entrance fee, but houses a couple of other letterboxes. Some may want to go box at one of these places (or somewhere else!) and then meet up again for dinner in town. Others may just want to hang out in town and have a relaxing day. This area could use a few more letterboxes, too, so maybe some of us can plant some before (or during) the event.

Thoughts?
