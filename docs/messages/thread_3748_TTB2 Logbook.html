From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-08-23 03:44:57 UTC
Now that TTB2 is full and won't be taking on any new passengers, I
thought it would be prudent to place a copy of the logbook on this
List, just in case it gets lost like the first bus. Baby Bear managed
to scan the pages before LB Bozo hijacked the bus and I just copied
them to the Files section under the TTB2 folder. If you haven't gotten
the bus yet I recommend not viewing the images so you will be surprised
when you finally get it, but it's up to you. I realize this is like
telling you all your Christmas presents are in the closet, but don't
peek. Enjoy!

Silver Eagle
