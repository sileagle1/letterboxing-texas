From: hancockkim (purpleintex@aol.com) | Date: 2007-01-01 03:34:57 UTC
Threw the coarse of the past few days I have came to the conclusion
that we all act out sometimes because each and everyone of us love
this Hobby/Sport so very much. I'm not the first to get upset and
surly will not be the last. All I can do is say I'm sorry and once and
for all say that I was only trying to do my best in a even worse
situation. I know what to do now and will always be prepared for the
outcome. I will always now travel with a extra carved stamp along with
all the other things to carry and simply replace a sub standard box
until the owner can return or tell me what to do for him/her with a
note in the box explaining why that persons stamp was not there
anymore. To this I would only do that if the entire box was in fact
destroyed as in these cases. I'm very sorry to Viewfinder for not
reading her letter as it was intended to be read. I took it wrong and
got my feelings hurt and reacted maybe not as I should have. I have
only love in my heart for all who love LBing as I so dearly do. I
never ment to cause harm or ill will against something I love to do.

Thank You All,
Your Friend in LBing,
Kim
