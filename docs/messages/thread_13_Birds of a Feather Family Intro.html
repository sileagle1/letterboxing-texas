Birds of a Feather Family Intro

From: Birds of a Feather (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-23 05:22:53 UTC

I am Sea Dove, my wife is Peacekeeper (Dove), my daughter is Splish
Splash (Duck), and my son is Liberty Eagle. We make up the Birds of
a Feather Family.

My wife was introduced to letterboxing in September 2003 via another
mom in Girl Scouts who had seen the Family Fun magazine article. She
decided to approach my daughter's Girl Scout troop about
letterboxing.

The girls liked the idea and my wife began to plan which letterboxes
they would attempt. She was looking over the Hodges Bend letterbox
and the Sugar Land letterbox as they were the closest ones to us.
She had been reviewing Silver Eagle's website for information and
had shown it to me.

The next day I was visiting with a co-worker and mentioned
letterboxing. Come to find out, he was Silver Eagle.

A quick gathering of the Girl Scout Troop (plus my son) was held on
the first Saturday in October and Silver Eagle came to talk about
letterboxing to the girls. Afterwards, they exchanged stamps and
went to find the Sugar Land Letterbox.

Our website for letterboxing is
http://www.geocities.com/birdsofafeather_lbox

We have placed two letterboxes on at trip to the Rio Grande Valley
while visiting my parents and grandparents who are Winter Texans.
The website has the clues for the boxes.

The boys are ahead on the finds as we went letterboxing while the
girls were on a ski trip in Colorado and we had a trip to the Corpus
Christi area.

I am hoping to make it out to Bastrop with the kids for the Texas
Event.

Sea Dove
