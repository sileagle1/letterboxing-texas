Tomorrow night is Earth Hour all over the world. Turn off your lights for one hour at 8:30 pm and take a little time to remind yourself about being a good steward of our planet, being conservation-minded, and striving to be energy-conscious.

Have you ever seen one of those photos from the space shuttle or the space station that shows the earth lit up? Have you ever noticed that North America looks like a big neon floodlight compared to Africa?

For all we have and all we have yet to receive, we can give a thank you to our planet by laying off the lights for an hour!
