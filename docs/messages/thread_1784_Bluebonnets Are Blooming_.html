Bluebonnets Are Blooming!

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-03-27 01:34:08 UTC
I just got back from a Scout campout at Lake Somerville
State Park and saw quite a few bluebonnets blooming. It's
not as good as previous years, but still worth seeing. I
managed to do some letterboxing in the area and highly
recommend visiting all the boxes in Washington County.
Also, I noticed that the picnic pavilion at the Park was
nice with a great view, and there was plenty of camping
spots. Next time the Event is held this way we will have
to consider Lake Somerville, especially if the wildflowers
are blooming.

Silver Eagle.
