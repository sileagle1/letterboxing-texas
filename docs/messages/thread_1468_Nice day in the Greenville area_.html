From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-11-11 17:33:28 UTC-05:00
Puddle-Splasher and I took advantage of that crisp one-day-of-fall we had this week on Thursday and went to Greenville to find the 4 letterboxes planted by Catpawz, and then to Sister Grove Park near McKinney to find those 2 boxes, and all 6 were alive and in good condition.  We visited 2 pretty parks in Greenville, a beautiful sculpture in a Meditation Garden in a cemetery, and a memorial to Audie Murphy that has taunted me every time I've driven past on I 30 because I knew there was a letterbox there somewhere - and now we've found it!  And then we discovered this pretty wooded park on an island at Lake Lavon east of McKinney, where the leaves were beginning to show red and gold and the hiking trails wound through the woods toward the lake, and 2 letterboxes!  And all of these boxes have good stamps, really good stamps!  Thanks, Catpawz!  We know Barefoot Lucy will enjoy all of these if she comes that way!  -- Viewfinder
