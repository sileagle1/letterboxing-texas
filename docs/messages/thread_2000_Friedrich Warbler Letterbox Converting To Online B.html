Friedrich Warbler Letterbox Converting To Online Box

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-07-19 18:28:51 UTC
I just received an email from a ranger at the Friedrich
Wilderness Park in San Antonio requesting me to remove
this box and the clues from LBNA. They were nice about
it and their reasons were sound (off-trail paths to boxes
cause damage and disturb wildlife) so I will comply.
Instead of removing the clues, I will probably convert it
to an Online Box, where you can use my modified clues to
still enjoy the park (though not off-trail), but instead
of a physical box, you can print the stamp image and log
your find on my webpage. I have done this before with
some of my other boxes, and even though it is not quite the
same as finding a real box, at least it introduces people
to some great places while letting them log a "Find" of sorts.
I call it Online since Virtual has already been used and
means something else.

As far as the box goes, I will not be able to remove it any
time soon, so if you want to get it, be sure to print the
clues now before I change them, and go in the next month or so.
