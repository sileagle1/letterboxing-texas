From: dasprite222 (dasprite222@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-28 15:27:39 UTC

This is the Sprite and the Highlander from upstate NY! We've been
down to Dallas for our Thanksgiving family getogether and were
thrilled to find this hobby has taken off down here! We moved to NY
in 2000 and didn't find out about letterboxing until just this year,
but we couldn't have picked a better time to start...there's boxes
EVERYWHERE!

We added two new boxes to the North Texas bunch, Coming Home and Once
a Year (Uncle Julie). The second one is gettint the camo treatment
from a local friend before they place it in the spot we chose, but
the other is out and about already (posted on both LBNA and Atlas
Quest). Also put an HH out there and picked up one of our own, so
you've got new travelling blood out there!

We'd love to get to know you guys...even though we'll only be around
once a year, email and groups like this last forever (or until the
systems crash)! Could we prevail upon any of you to give us a tiny
bit of info on yourselves (like names and where you are in TX so
we'll know who we've met?). So far, our only connection is the Celtic
Lions (who placed some GREAT boxes out our way!), so anyone who
wouldn't mind a brief repeat of what I'm sure you all did years ago,
please do! Feel free to contact us offlist, too if you want to visit
the Buffalo Niagara region...we will GLADLY zip you around to the
overabundance of local boxes. We're also able to place boxes up here
for you, so if you'd just like to see your HH move a bit (or just get
your name in a different corner of the US) write us!

Thanks again to the Celtic Lions for a GREAT Thanksgiving treat...our
other Texas friends liked it so much that they're getting started
boxing, too!

The Sprite & The Highlander
