Brand new and loving it!

From: Amy (kevamy1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-21 13:04:00 UTC
Hello all! I am brand new to letterboxing after reading about it in a
magazine. I'm in Houston and I was happy to find many boxes near my
home. I've taken my kids out a couple of times and so far we've found
3 boxes. We love the outdoors and this is a great alternative to the
frog-catching my hubby does with them! Our trail name is Treasure Bugs
as we seem to always be together. I am originally from Chicago, but
transplanted here 11 years ago for a teaching job. It was very
appropriate that the first box I found was called "An Apple for my
Teacher."
I look forward to finding more boxes and to eventually one day actually
hiding one.
Can someone please explain the tour bus to me...

Thanks!
Amy
