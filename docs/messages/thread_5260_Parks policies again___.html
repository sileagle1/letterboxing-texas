Message to Webmasters,

From: Bastrop SP:

Message:
Good day...

My name is Robert (Park Ranger- Resource Specialist) and I am trying to reach
out to the letterboxing/geocaching communities. It has been brought to my
attention that articles have been placed on state property without
authorization. According to and in persuance of Section 13.101-13.112 of the
Texas Parks and Wildlife Code and Chapter 59.134-135 of the Texas Administrative
Code, ALL geocaching and letterboxing on state property needs to have an
associated permit submitted to the Superintendent/Manager of that station. The
permit is free.

I would appreciate your assistance in notifying anyone that has placed an item
on Bastrop and Buescher State Parks to call or stop by the respective park and
either complete the free permit form or to pull their item.

Texas Parks and Wildlife respect the geocaching and letterboxing programs and
hope that more people will get involved in these activities. In part, by
filling out the permits, it allows us to maintain a database to monitor the
statistics of the geocaching/letter boxing guests here at the parks.

Thank you for your assistance,

Robert T.
Resource Specialist
Lost Pines Complex
Bastrop, Texas
