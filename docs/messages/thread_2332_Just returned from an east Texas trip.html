Just returned from an east Texas trip

From: Rhonda (krmosborn@aol.com) | Date: 2006-11-28 14:17:22 UTC
We just got back from a quick day trip to east Texas - the first time
in quite a while that we've had a chance to hunt for boxes. What a
day!! We had planned on making a round trip between Tatum, Henderson,
and Kilgore, but loved the Martin Creek State Park so much that we ran
out of daylight. By the way -- a word of warning to others venturing
to Martin Creek - beware of the attacking ducks!! LOL My daughter got
chased all over the picnic area - I guess the little guys were hungry
and expected to be fed.

We found all that we searched for, except the daffodil stamp in the
Spring Flower Trail series. According to the log book at the last
stamp, the daffodil stamp had eluded several people.

Thanks to all of you who placed those boxes -- my family had a great
day searching!

The Munchkins
Shreveport, LA
