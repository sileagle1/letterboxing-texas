From: josephine_tx (josephine_tx@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-23 23:20:29 UTC
Just wanted to drop a line, folks, and let you know about our
letterboxing fun this Sunday!

My newest letterboxing cohort "Crazy Daisy" and I went letterboxing
this weekend! First we went to check out Xi-Ca-Yo in Nac which alas
was TOO thrilling for either one of us, as it included wading
through waist high water mocassin infested waters! We only saw two
of the snakes, but that was two too many! We have decided to come
back in the winter when hopefully the water will be down more and
the snakes are in hibernation.

This was her first time letterboxing, so we had quite an eventful
day, we searched for six different boxes--not including Xi-Ca-
Yo...AND she found not one but THREE hitchikers! Alas, one was a
hitchhiker I had placed, and another one that my other cohort
Bangwell Putt had hidden. But the third one was the August Hello
Kitty by Smart Cookie.

So! Not only was she overly jumpy after the snake experiences, but
then she was tired from all the hitchhikers. Quote: "Boy this is a
lot of work!" She was tired, exhausted from our romp, BUT! she
still thinks it is a fun idea and her husband is even thinking of
joining the fun!

Love you all!

Josephine
