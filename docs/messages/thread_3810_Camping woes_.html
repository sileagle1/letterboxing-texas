From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-10-14 05:40:02 UTC-06:00
Hmmmm....  little dogs, right?  I really dislike little hyper yappy dogs.  Give me a big calm regal animal every time.  And then train train train until the dog is a very polite member of society. 
 
I find that trained dogs are much better behaved, and that most dogs love the training.  I never intended to show Parker.  That was not my hobby.  But he loved it so, and since we were going to the shows anyway as the support crew, why not show a little too.  Parker really enjoyed the ring.  Wish you could have seen him.  We really didn't care if we won or not, and we would have quit the moment it wasn't fun anymore.
 
As to skunks....  if you really want to get rid of them a good air rifle is the ticket.  I use a Benjamin - Sheridan in .22 caliber.  Yes - a .22 caliber air rifle.  Gets it done just right and QUIET too.  It will not leave a dent in an Armadillo though.
 
Well - 0540, gotta run to work.  Later.
