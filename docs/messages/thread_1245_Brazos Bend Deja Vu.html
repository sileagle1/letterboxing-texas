Brazos Bend Deja Vu

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-08-28 21:59:17 UTC
I just got back from a Boy Scout campout at Brazos Bend SP
and it brought back fond memories of our Event last March.
Even though the weather was much hotter, it did provide us
with another Texas-size storm last night, just like we had
at our Event. I was too busy with Scout activities to even
check on my box in the park, but my memories of last March
kept my letterboxing spirit alive while swatting mosquitoes
and sweating in my tent. Thanks to all TX Letterboxers for
helping me survive the weekend!

Silver Eagle

Re: Brazos Bend Deja Vu

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-08-29 13:12:43 UTC
Your'e right, it is a Scout mecca, and that is why it
is so hard to get a campsite in Fall-Spring. I was
amazed how easy it was to get any campsite we wanted
in the summer. Of course, no sane person would WANT
a campsite in the summer!
We actually did do a night hike of sorts, but not a
BOXDN hike. We had to walk from the Observatory to
the campsite (about 1 mile) at 10:30 pm and it was
reminiscent of the pre-BOXDN hike that a few of us
TX Letterboxers took with him and Wanda to stamp
into Kissin Fishies Mr. Howdy box. There was lighting,
thunder and wind, just like that memorable night in March.

Silver Eagle

Re: [letterboxingtexas] Re: Brazos Bend Deja Vu

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-29 08:33:59 UTC-07:00
Lightning and I didn't do the night hikes at the
gathering, but we did go to the observatory Saturday
evening. The storm prevented us from being able to
see anything in the sky, but it was beautiful to watch
from the top of the observatory.

I don't think he or I will ever forget that gathering
- the wind actually pulled our tent stakes up, which
wouldn't have been so bad except that we also had a
very small slit in the side of our tent that has never
mattered in three or four years of camping with it -
until that night! We wound up spending the last half
of the night in the car. It was a beautiful storm
though!

It looks like it may be him and me on our own again
for the JEN gathering....

Lucy
