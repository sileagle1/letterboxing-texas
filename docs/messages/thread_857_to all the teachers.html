From: JOY (TeamTexUS@houston.rr.com) | Date: 2005-05-26 19:10:04 UTC-05:00
Thank you!!! Packed off the students today,and will close-out tomorrow !  A lot of people don't realize how creative teachers have to be. I teach 7/8 grade students who are mentally retarded and autistic. I do not receive textbooks for my students. Each student is taught according to an Individual Educational Plan (IEP), which sets forth goals for the student. It is up to me, however, to figure out how to teach that goal to the student and to beg, borrow, steal or otherwise find or create the materials needed to do it. Then, I have to figure out how to accurately assess the learning of a non-verbal student who does not read or write and uses sign language only for the most basic of needs (drink, restroom, etc.). I love what I do because I am constantly challenged and I have the opportunity to make a difference in a child's life! And, I have the whole summer to box, box, box!!
 
JOY
