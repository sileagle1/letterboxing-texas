p_quest2005 <p_quest2005@yahoo.com> wrote:

whew.  i've had a crazy schedule lately.  it took me ~a week just to
place the bus, and after having planted it on monday morning, here i
am finally letting y'all know where it is:

basically, it's at the NE corner of 635 & the Bush, where
carrollton, farmer's branch, and irving meet.  the easiest way might
be to take Luna Rd. north from 635, past Valley View Ln, then turn
right (east) onto Keenan Bridge.  go past the small building(s)
until you get to a gravel driveway on the left.  turn onto the
driveway.
on the driveway, facing north, you will see three "trees" on your
immediate left.  going just on the north side of these, go west
until you run into a perimeter fence.  look for the conspicuously
large mound of dead leaves.

if memory serves correctly, the bus should be no more than ~50 feet
from the road.
landmarks to look for: FB water tower relatively close by (on davis
road).  a three-story building that looks like a fireman's training
house inside the perimeter fence that you'll be parking outside of.

let me know if there are any problems

p ?uest
