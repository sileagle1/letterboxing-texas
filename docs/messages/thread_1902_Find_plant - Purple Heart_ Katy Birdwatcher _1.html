Hi,

The Purple Heart letterbox (Katy, TX) is easy to find if you follow
the directions. It's easiest to find the box from the side of the
fence just outside the park. (You'll see.) I recommend poking around
with a stick in the hiding area, but there are plenty of them on the
ground, right near the box.

I've planted a box at that same park, too:

http://www.aisling.net/hobbies/letterboxing/tx-katybirdwatcher.htm

It's an easy find--perhaps too easy--but it could be tricky to be
discreet if the playground is busy.

The Katy VFW Memorial Park is a lovely place for a picnic, and it's
ideal for the family, since you can let the little ones play and
they're likely to remain in easy view throughout the park.
