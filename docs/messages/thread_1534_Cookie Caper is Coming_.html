Cookie Caper is Coming!

From: busybeesfamily (bbbndrider@msn.com) | Date: 2005-12-20 01:57:08 UTC
Hi everyone,

Wow! I haven't been on the group in such a long time. With hubby
in Afghanistan...things are almost always chaotic around the
Busybeesfamily's house.

But..with Girl Scout Cookie time fast approaching, we decided it was
time for a series of 8 boxes called "A Cookie Caper"! This will be
a series of boxes, with two having cryptic clues, one being a
microbox and one being a "piggy-back" box. I am thinking about also
including a hitchhiker that can only be moved from one Cookie box to
another Cookie box in the series. We will also offer a bonus box
for all those who find all eight boxes.

Anyhoo...at the end of the series, each person who successfully
completes and can show proof of all 8 Cookie box stamps will earn a
box of Girl Scout Cookies (of their choice). I will have a contact
number set up so that IF (a big "if") I am available, I can meet you
to give you the box of Cookies OR...you can email/snail-mail me
proof and I will mail you a box of GS Cookies (your choice, again).

As another alternative, I can bring everyone's cookies to the 3rd
Annual Texas LB Event OR I can send them all to one person who will
bring them to the event.

All stamps/boxes/clues pages are already under construction and I
will attempt to plant all boxes around (or before) January 7th. The
boxes will remain in place until February 25...the end of the Lone
Star Girl Scout Council's Cookie Sale.

I will NOT post these clues on either AtlasQuest or LBNA until AFTER
the series is completed and the boxes are pulled (so you will still
be able to log your finds). I don't want just TONS of people doing
the series and perhaps having the boxes go missing or being
vandalized. I WILL post the link to the Clues Pages on January 5
(or thereabouts) to THIS site so that all Texas Letterboxers may
attempt the series if they wish. You may also feel free to share
the clues with those letterboxers that you know to be responsible
letterboxers. (I will not have lots of time to check these boxes or
perform maintenance since I will be in the midst of Cookie Sales for
my troop of 12 girls!)

Most boxes will be hid in and around Georgetown (with the exception
of one), but you will be able to do the entire series in ONE DAY if
you don't piddle around.

I hope you will all come to our lovely area of the Hill Country to
do the Cookie Caper series and collect a box of your favorite Girl
Scout Cookies! (While you're here...consider planting a box of your
own!)

Please feel free to email me if you have any questions or
suggestions.

Thanks, Brandi
(Batty B of Busybeesfamily)
