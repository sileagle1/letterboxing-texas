New Boxes & Old One Relocated

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2008-12-31 03:29:41 UTC
I went camping with my daughter yesterday and managed to place a few
boxes along the way. First, I placed a new Mystery box called Jesse's
Jewels 2 to replace the one that went missing. The first several
finders will get some jewels to keep.

Next, I finally placed a box that was long overdue. Quilted Boots is a
box in tribute to Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter. I think most of you
will agree that they are fine representatives of TX Letterboxers, and
their boxes are worth the drive!

Then I placed another Mystery box called Jonathan Livingston Seagull,
which is part of my Literature Series. It won't be hard to find and I
hope you read the book if you haven't already.

Finally, I relocated my Livingston Largemouth microbox and replaced the
log, so reprint the clues before you go for it.

Thanks,
Silver Eagle
