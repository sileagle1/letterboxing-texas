Now I'm not making fun of east tx crew OR their clue writing, but I had a good laugh over this clue just now:It's a throwback to the old "recover well" joke of a few years ago.

I was reading the above clue the other day, and just glanced through it quickly. As I came to the last line, I was shocked - even horrified. How could such a horrendous event be mentioned so casually in a clue? Was it a letterboxer? Was it a historical event? Was it just a news item? I didn't quite get it, butI was in a hurry, so I moved on.
As I was re-reading clues, I read this same clue again a little slower and a little better, and then I realized what they were really saying was not how I read it initially....
