I’m still just constantly amazed at the amount... and quality... of information that is available on the Internet.  If you were born before the 80s, you probably have a better idea about what I’m talking about.  Before then, I would have had to go to the library, and then a couple more, and MAYBE someone would have written a book about Drive-in Theatres.  And then, to tell you about it, I would have typed it up, made copies, and mailed it to all of you.  AND, it’s very possible that without the Internet, this book on Drive-ins would have been close to impossible... they relied a lot on people sending in information to them... over the Internet.  It still amazes me.  Think for a second what Letterboxing would be like without the Internet...  

Next, I think I’ll spend some time being thankful for the wheel.

Busy appreciating the obvious,
