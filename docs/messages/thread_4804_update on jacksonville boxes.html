From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-06-04 21:00:28 UTC-05:00
Like JBK, I've ventured out to check my last two active boxes here in Jacksonville. As I told y'all last weekend, my Flying Again box is still going strong, but I did bring it home to clean it up and freshen the logbook. It will go back out tomorrow to the same place.

Spring Hope appears to be missing, although it may have been sucked in by the undergrowth. This is one box I have committed to replacing as needed because of Ruby Tuesday's and Stillwater's experience with it. I have carved a new stamp, better than the first if I do say so myself. I think I will rehide it slightly differently so that anyone who has previously claimed it can refind and reclaim it. It should also go out again tomorrow.

The quest boxes for next Saturday will be placed permanently after the event is over, but they will be placed in different locations and the clues will definitely be - different. JBK has the vast majority of our local boxes anymore and I think I need to add my own style back into the Jacksonville scene to offer a little more variety (no offense JBK).
