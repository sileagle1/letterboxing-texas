From: Tiger Moses (tigermoses@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-14 14:08:11 UTC-05:00
print it on a laser printer or photocopy to copier that uses toner!

Then you can iron onto eraser or other stamping material usually.

No need to mirror/reverse image

Not HOT but pretty warm on iron!
