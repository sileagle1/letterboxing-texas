Some other suggestions for boxes would be plastic pill bottles or plastic jars with screw-top lids (e.g. pickle relish, banana peppers, etc.).  I have had pretty good success with them and they are free after using the original contents.  Just be sure to thoroughly clean them out, maybe using bleach, to get rid of food smell.
