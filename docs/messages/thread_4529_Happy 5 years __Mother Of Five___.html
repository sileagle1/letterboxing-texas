On Wed, Apr 28, 2010 at 7:32 PM, Vicki Bell <vicki@bellmotel.com> wrote:

My firstday of letterboxingwas Leap Day 2008 -- February 29, the day before TALE5. My college roommate, Astro D, and her friend, Lionsmane,called to say they were drivingin from Santa Fe, New Mexico to attend a Letterboxing Event, and asked if I wanted to tag along. Sounded like fun to me. They showed up on Thursday night with gifts for me --a hand-made logbook, and a hand-carved stamp reading "Bell Motel". We went out the next morning and found 15 boxes, the first being "Great Hills Park", the one closest to my house. I couldn't believe how much fun I was having. The next day we drove to Bastrop for TALE5, and meta crowd ofgreat, friendly, artistic, fun-lovingpeople. I had even more fun! I can't explain why this hobby brings me so much joy, but it really does. I owe a huge debt of gratitude to Astro D, and everyone else I've met(or who has planted a box I've found)in letterboxing, for all these good times.
-- Bell Motel
