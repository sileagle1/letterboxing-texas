Event Report

From: Ashby (ashby@ic.org) | Date: 2010-08-13 12:33:43 UTC-07:00
On Wednesday, we attended the Dick and Jenny's Dessert Party event, hosted by Blackberry Patch, in New Orleans, LA.  A great time was had by all.

We had people from three states (TX, LA, NC) get together at world famous Dick & Jenny's restaurant, for dessert and visiting and exchanges.  We welcomed Good Ol' Gal, who is new to letterboxing, and traded stories and local boxes and I invited them all to TALE, too.

Have a great day, everyone!

ashby
