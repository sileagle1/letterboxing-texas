I just wanted to say that "Sylvester" by baby bear is indead still active as I found him this morning stalking a bird. He had moved from his original hidding spot.
Sylvester is watching for birds to nab 12 steps behing the "Live Oak" sign between Oak tree 2 and 3, under some bark.
 
Now if you ask me no bird would get caught with a cat hidding in such a way.
 
I added some new log paper because everything in the box was soaking wet! Nothing was salvageable but the slipery stamp. So if anyone else wants to go find him please bring a new log to replace the few sheets I added and a new ziplock baggy.
