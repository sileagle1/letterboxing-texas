Yoda Says Thanks!

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-12-08 01:40:22 UTC
This morning while looking at newly posted letterboxes,
image my delight to see one (If You Plant Them) in my
hometown of Sugar Land at a park I visit all the time
for my daughter's soccer practices. Then imagine my
surprise to realize it is a box dedicated to me! It
evidently was the brainchild of Barefoot Lucy and was
placed by her accomplice Dewberry. All I can say is I
am deeply touched and honored by this ultimate recognition,
and I thank you very much. I was so anxious to find it
that I was late to work after going for it this morning.
Great stamp and logbook, with a nice sentiment written
within. I guess it was appropriate for me to be first finder!
A little over 3 years ago when I first started this crazy
pasttime of ours, I never could have imagined something like
this would someday happen to me. Shucks, I would have been
amazed and pleased just to have a tenth of the great TX
letterboxers we have today looking for my boxes and placing
their own. Back then, finding boxes and having others find
mine was the greatest feeling. However, as time wore on,
that feeling grew less and less intense, just as most new
things do. But happily, the warm feelings I get for being
a part of this group and the obvious appreciation I sense from
all of you just continues to get better and better. Thanks
to everyone, and may the Letterboxing Force be with you!

Silver Eagle
