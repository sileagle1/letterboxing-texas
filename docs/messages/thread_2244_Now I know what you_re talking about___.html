Now I know what you're talking about...

From: jayceeinbedford (jayceeinbedford@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-10-18 00:24:49 UTC
My two youngest daughters and I headed out to Waxahachie this
afternoon. We made it to SCC at 5:00. It was pretty creepy. The
parking lot should've been full of cars vying to get to the exit
first to go home.

Carrie, 21 now as of the 8th, pointed to a place that in her
interpretation would've been where the clues led us. I said, "No way.
It's over there somewhere." Man I hate when the kids are right. We
walked and drove around for 15 minutes before I gave in to show her
how wrong she was. Okay, so she was right this time.

Cassie, still 5, slept from downtown, through the entire find, all
the way back to Bedford.

As we were leaving the parking lot, a Sheriff drove by, then another
one came in behind us and followed us the entire 5 miles back to the
freeway. He was less than two car lengths behind us as we did from 45
to 55 the whole way. I think someone must have called in. He was
probably looking at all the crazy things that have involved my car in
the last year. It was pretty nervewracking.

We made it safely home. Gary, who didn't get off in time to go, is
making chicken strips and mashed potatoes. I don't have the heart to
tell him that we got lost on the way home (287) and stopped at
Braum's for an ice cream.

Janet
