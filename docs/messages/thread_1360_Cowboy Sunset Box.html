Re: [letterboxingtexas] Re: Cowboy Sunset Box

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-10-05 07:41:34 UTC-07:00
BEA=Beignet-Eating Alien.

That's who we in Texas accuse of stealing our
letterboxes when they go missing.

It all goes back to several months ago, as we were
getting ready for the March gathering, and there were
a few boxes that went missing at about the same time.
At the same time as that was happening, there was some
discussion about great places to eat beignets, and we
decided that not only was it aliens stealing the
boxes, but beignet-eating aliens. At the same time,
we also had a New Orleans boxer who participated on
the list quite a lot. He graciously hosted breakfast
at his campsite at the gathering, and he fixed and
served beignets. He also brought an "emissary" from
the aliens and we were going to sit down with him to
negotiate the terms of the return of our missing
letterboxing. All was going well until a box actually
came up missing AT THE GATHERING! At that point,
negotiations were over and we opted instead to hang
the alien emissary in effigy.

Lucy
