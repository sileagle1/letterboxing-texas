Hello Fellow Letterboxers,
 
Since beginning letterboxing in November 2005, I have been receiving news about the Texas letterboxing group from my dear husband, El Lobo. Lately though, getting news watered down is not sufficient to nuture my spirit and, at the encouragement of my famous family members, Boots Texas and Lone Star Quilter, I joined the group. This afternoon, after a few days of letterboxing with said family near Austin, I have been reading through the posts of the last few days. What a delight! In a sense, knowing all of you from this group will make finding your boxes even more special.
 
Living in West Texas with El Lobo means we travel for hours before we can letterbox. Our fervent hope is that other people will take up the sport and boxes will spread throughout Texas and into the far regions of our great state. May I see you on the trail...  Star Seeker.

From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-04-08 16:51:41 UTC-05:00
Welcome! There were precious few letterboxes in the DFW area a few years ago and now there are many! I guess: "if you plant them, they will come"! I think when new people hear about letterboxing and check it out only to find a few, if any, boxes in their area, they give up; but if there are plenty, they jump in and before long they are also enjoying the fun of planting boxes too.... and so on. I think many Texas letterboxers are more aware of planting boxes when they visit less populated areas of Texas (letterbox-wise) now, so that we will all have boxes to search for when we travel. Again welcome to the list.
