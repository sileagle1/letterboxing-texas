Rusk about using their group tent site. It holds
about 15 tents, is primitive, and is $7 per tent. It
would also have to be reserved as a group, so I would
be glad to handle that here. No deposit required,
just a handshake commitment. This park has 8 boxes in
it.

access - water is available not far from the site
though.

If you are interested, let me know fairly soon so I
can speak for the site. I would like to be able to
tell them how many tents to expect.

RE: [letterboxingtexas] Additional Camping in Rusk

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-05 05:29:21 UTC-08:00
If I had to guess, I would say the burn ban is lifted
or about to be. Obviously though, that's subject to
change on any given day!

My in-laws have property at Lake Jacksonville and the
lake got so low that an old abandoned road that was
flooded when the lake was built in the 50's has
resurfaced, meaning we could literally walk in 3-4
minutes what took 7-8 minutes to drive to. We took
the boat out at Christmas and had to step down 5 feet
from the pier to their floating boathouse (which was
barely still floating instead of resting on the
bottom).

However, I've been out rambling the last two days and
the ground feels really good to walk on now - there is
actually some moisture remaining in the ground so the
endless absorption has slowed down considerably up
here. Lightning and I were at Lake Palestine's dam
yesterday and there was actually some water flowing,
not over the spillway, but through the tube mounted at
the top of it, and the water flow away from the dam
was healthy. We felt kind of silly when we parked and
got out of the car to just look at it, but we didn't
feel so bad when we realized there were about 6 cars
also stopped to admire the presence of WATER!

In other words, I think the only way we would be
prevented from having fires at gathering time is if we
get absolutely no rain between now and then, AND we
get lots of high winds and heat. The way this year
has gone, it's possible that will happen, but
hopefully not too likely!

Lucy
