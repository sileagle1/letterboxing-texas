Barefoot Lucy not only checked on the Fish Fry place, but
also located the elusive Picnic Pavilion where the Event
will take place. It seems the map on the website is wrong
and the pavilion is not as described. The bad news is that
it is smaller than advertised, though it should work. The
good news is that it is a nice stone pavilion with a fireplace
right by the lake. To find it, park in the lot by the train
depot and walk toward the lake. We will be right in the most
crowded part of the park, so we might get some curious looks.
BL suggested we make a sign saying "Alden Family Reunion"
to really confuse people.
Baby Bear and I will be at the park Friday night and plan
on going to the Maydelle winery around 6:00 if it is still open,
then going to Bertha's. Maybe if others go to the winery a
little earlier we can keep them open and not overwhelm them
all at once. Looking forward to seeing everyone either Friday
night or Saturday!
