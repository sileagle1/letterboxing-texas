We've all heard of the Bermuda Triangle, we know about the Urban
Triangle here in TX, but I dub my LB adventure today the RURAL
TRIANGLE. I left my home in Nacogdoches County and went to Mt.
Enterprise to discover THE LOST ROSE. I then headed west to find
TRIBUTE TO THE IRON ORE MINES in Reklaw. I also picked up a hitch
hiker along the way. I made it back to my home and am now admiring my
stamps and just a few bug bites.
