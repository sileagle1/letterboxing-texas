Stinkin' counts

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-07 19:51:03 UTC-07:00
Every now and then I sit down and try to figure out my
PFX counts. And once I start trying to figure it out,
I remember why I don't keep up with it.

Now I have a few situations that came up mainly at the
gathering because everyone was so darned creative.

First of all, I'll start with the easy one. What is
the designation for a Postal LB? I think I've used
"U" (as in USPS) before, but I don't recall if that's
correct. I know I could just include them in my F
count, but I don't really want to include them there
because I didn't find them, they found me.

Secondly, there were a couple of Hitchhiker Hostels at
the gathering. Do the hostels themselves also count
as H's, or do they count as F's? They weren't really
finds because they were brought to the gathering, and
they aren't really hitchhikers because they usually
stay in one place. I thought about counting them as
B's for brung's-heck, I don't know.

Now - travellers. Do travellers count as exchanges or
WHAT? So if, say, I got Catpawz' stamp AND her
traveller, do I count her stamp as an X and her
traveller as a F? Never mind...just looking on Silent
Doug's site and that seems to be what he is saying.

And as for that crazy Clue game - what the heck are
all THOSE stamps??? LOL

Oh yes, the Happy Birthday Texas LB. Now, technically
it is a Postal, but couldn't it also be an Event box?
Okay, just kidding on that one - I'm counting it as a
Postal, but I had to throw that thought out there.

If going to gatherings makes my brain hurt this much,
I'm gonna have to quit going to 'em - NOT!

Lucy
