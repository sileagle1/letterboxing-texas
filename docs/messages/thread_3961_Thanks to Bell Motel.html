From: Shirley A (gramwalks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-18 21:55:43 UTC
Today I received my signature stamp back by snail mail. I REALLY
appreciate the trouble she went to to return my first stamp. I was
attached to it, (but not enough to travel 70 miles lol). In Oct I was
in Waco letterboxing and somehow managed to lay the stamp in the leaves
instead of in my pack as I usually do. Needless to say when I went to
Georgetown a few weekends later I had no signature stamp. I figured
it was a goner but last week it was found and today it has returned
home. Many thanks to Bell Motel. zturtle
