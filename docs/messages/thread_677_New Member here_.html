Hi all!
I am a new member, and wanted to introduce myself.
My name is Semalee, and when letterboxing I would sign it with Mags, or Bilbrey family.  We have been letterboxing for almost a year, but have not made any stamps or boxes yet, just having a great time finding all of the wonderful boxes that are out there.  I heard about letterboxing through a magazine article on how to walk more.  I knew this would be something that would quickly become addictive for me, and I was right!!  I have now gotten a couple of friends hooked and my sister!!  We search for boxes whenever we are going anywhere else, because I think it is a great way to bring something back from a trip.  While in Colorado last summer we took my dad letterboxing and he loved it!!  Anyway, I am glad to be here.   Thank you so much to Jennifer of TeamKing for letting me know about this list.  I understand there is a gathering next Saturday, I am wondering if it is too late to RSVP??
 
It is good to get a chance to be on this list.  Thank you!
Semalee
