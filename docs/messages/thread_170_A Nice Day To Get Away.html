A Nice Day To Get Away

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-06-12 02:16:00 UTC
The family and I headed north out of town to pick blueberries
at Moorhead's Blueberry Farm:
http://www.moorheadsblueberryfarm.com/
It was so fun and tasty (we ate as much as we kept) I talked
them into going after a few letterboxes on the way home. After
a stop at Old Town Spring (to appease the wife), we headed to
Cullen Park where I found Cullen Cycle and Farmer's Lair doing
fine. We then went to Terry Hershey Park and found Blessing
Box. I looked for Hershey Kiss, but as Baby Bear already
said, it appears to be missing. We then went to nearby Lupe
Tortillas restraurant for some great Mexican Food before
heading home. Now I'm going to have blueberries on ice cream!

Silver Eagle
