Hope everyone had a great Memorial Day weekend. My family
spent most of the time at Galveston, but I did manage to
squeeze in a little letterboxing. I checked on my Ryan
Express box in Alvin and it is alive & well, waiting for
more visitors. I also place a new box in Quintana, which
is south of Lake Jackson, where my Gordon Grouper box is.
With these and the ones at Brazos Bend SP & Varner Hogg SP,
a day trip south of Houston might be worth your while.
By the way, Galveston is crying for more boxes, since my
Brown Bomber is the only one currently on the Island.
