Laundered logbook

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-06-05 16:02:32 UTC-05:00

Well I have done something I've never done before. I ran a personal logbook through the washing machine. Stupid how it happened..Was entirely preventable, but it happened all the same. The worst part was that it was our very first logbook. First letterbox found. First letterbox placed. First event. Exchanges with people who have been out of the game for five years or more. Some of the boxes have been gone a few years too.

When I saw it on the bottom of the washer, I nearly cried. I set it aside to build up the resolve to look at it. I finally realized time was probably critical, so I started going through it page by page. Surprisingly very few of the images were ruined and only a few were even damaged!

I tore the book apart and laid the pages out on my workbench and within hours they were dry. I had settled in to do some work and sweet little Trail Whale came outside and leaned up on the corner of my bench and started asking about each image. I told him what they each were and he listened attentively, finally saying "Grammy, you should not have washed these. These are very important papers." He IS a letterboxer!

What started out as a horrifying discovery turned out to be a special few moments with my precious grandson.
