A trip down the flower trail

From: bluebutterflytexas (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-12 19:48:18 UTC
PsGuru and I headed to Tatum today so I could re-place some logbooks in
my series at Martin Creek Lake SP. What a beautiful day it was!!! All
the boxes are dried out and logbooks are in place. I re-placed the
daffodil, which seems to have had a hard time keeping roots down on the
trail. There is a LOT of trail damage there and several BIG trees which
you have to step or climb over to continue on the trail. Also, had to
pull Catch a Falling Star at Tatum because they are clearing out the
tree line.
I finally was able to find a new hiding spot for my Great Texas
Balloon Race Box!!! YIPPEE!! I have been to Longview several times
trying to find the right spot. The trail is a new one and I really like
it. The clue has been updated.
Happy boxing!
Blue Butterfly

BarefootLucy....your box is dry and still well hid at Martin Creek. The
logbook was a little damp, but i re-sealed the bag.

Hi, I guess we were right behind you at Martin Creek Lake SP. We
noticed that the logbooks were replaced on 4/12 and that was the date
we were finding your boxes there. We also went to Tatum and could not
make heads or tales out of the clues to the box in the park as there
was so much construction going on. I said, at the time, that I would
have to inform the owner of this box of the construction so they could
remove the box until the construction was finished. Glad you were ahead
of us there too.

Barefoot Lucy, your box in Henderson was so soaked and rusty that we
took it to our RV for a few days and I reworked it, dryed it and made a
new logbook and a new box for it. The box lid was damaged so that it
would not close tightly and it let water in. I put the dried logbook
back in the box also. We replaced it 4/13 in the afternoon. You may
want to check on it but I think it will be ok.
