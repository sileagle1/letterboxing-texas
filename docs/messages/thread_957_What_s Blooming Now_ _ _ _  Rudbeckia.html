From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-26 23:47:30 UTC
For those of you who may be new, Gramatrick has a travelling box
called What's Blooming? The box highlights a flower that is blooming
in our garden at that moment. This box is meant to travel all over
the greater Houston area, and letterboxers are welcome to refind it--
because it will be in a new location with a new stamp, at least as
long as we can keep it alive. This box was launched last month with
Foxglove (one of my favorite stamps we've carved) in Memorial Park.
We pulled the stamp and have rehidden it in a park in West Houston
with a new Rudbeckia stamp.

So, if you want to see What's Blooming? come find our box!
