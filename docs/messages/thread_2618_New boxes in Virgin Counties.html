From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-03-05 00:06:29 UTC
Today, on the way home from the TX Wildflower LB event (where we had a
great time, by the way, cuddling by Baby Bear's wonderful fire), we
placed 3 new letterboxes. They are "Who Will Go With Old Ben Milam?"
in Milam County (first one in that county) and "Fiesta de Hogos"
and "Madison Quilt Block" in Madison County, which previously had no
boxes. (Sorry, GG!) We're trying to fill them in, one box at a time.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
