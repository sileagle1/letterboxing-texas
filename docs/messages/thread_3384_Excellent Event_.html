Excellent Event!

From: Puddle Splasher (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-03-03 01:39:41 UTC
I think I enjoy these events more each time I attend one. I still
have the same problem: too much going on, but too little time! There
were so many cool people there, and I missed getting to do exchanges
with everybody, so next time! Silver Eagle and Baby Bear, what can I
say? You're the "hosts superior" (sounds celestial, doesn't it?). The
event was absolutely great and the campfire was so much fun. I wish
everyone had been there. Reliving old stories and hearing new ones
was fantastic. Okay, for those of you who thought that Dewberry,
Mother of 5 and I cheated at the match game, I wanted to confess, BUT
I gave the bottle of "Cranberry Mead" back to Silver Eagle, and we
used it at the campfire to toast the demise of Gryzzled Gryphon's
cranberry cruiser, may it rest in peace. That combined with some
potent "chocolate cake" shots provided by SandiBox and some
incredible cobbler made by Fearless Fox and Lightning just topped off
the event. I'm happy that Chrispy's little letterboxer-to-be, didn't
decide to make an appearance at the event, although I am anxious to
meet him/her. Oh, and Mother of Five, I didn't really consider us to
be LOST on that trail, we only misplaced the original trail for
awhile. How do we do that? (And you promised not to tell anyone!)
Dewberry, I was so excited that you came! It was fun having
that "stealthy" group of eight moving down the trail in mass, lead by
Blue Butterfly and Lucy Locket with SandiBox in the back reading the
clues and shouting them up to those in front, sending SoccerChic into
the sticks and briars to retrieve the boxes, and then sitting in a
huge circle in the middle of the trail stamping in! Luckily, we owned
the trails Saturday and the only other people we passed were
letterboxers! Obviously, I didn't have time to find all the wonderful
boxes hidden during the event, but Viewfinder and I are already
planning our trip back to Bastrop at the end of the month (hereby
known as the "Top-Down Excellent Adventure 2008") to find every last
one of them! I appreciate everyone who came and participated and
especially send a shout-out to Silver Eagle and Baby Bear for all the
planning. It was great.
Also, I've heard a rumor that the Texas Tour Bus Two is on the
move and may be available again pretty soon! All I can say is,
Barefoot Lucy, you'd better sleep in your tennis shoes and have a
full tank in your car at all times!
Puddle Splasher
