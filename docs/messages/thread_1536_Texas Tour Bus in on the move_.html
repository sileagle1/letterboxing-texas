Texas Tour Bus in on the move!

From: Delivery Girl (delivery_girl_@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-12-21 21:41:53 UTC-06:00

The Texas Tour Bus has been fully gassed and has made another stop! You can find the bus at Cleburne SP located in Cleburne, TX.

Clues:

Start with looking at the map http://www.tpwd.state.tx.us/publications/pwdpubs/media/park_maps/pwd_mp_p4503_013a.pdf

~Take the spillway hiking trail by starting where the little man symbol is on the map. There is a little culdisac to park in.

~Follow the trail across a mini dry creek with 3 cement squares and follow the trail steadily up the hill till you come to a wide trail.

~This trail will bring you to the spillway.

~You will see a trail sign that says "to overlook or Lake loop". From the sign turn Left and follow the trail near the cliff. Look for 4 white rocks in a mini trail and take the trail.

~Follow the trail into the trees and go to the left tree. The box is in the back of the tree covered in White rocks and sticks.

Have fun and enjoy the park. I planed 2 new boxes today also!
