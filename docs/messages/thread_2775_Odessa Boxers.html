Re: Odessa Boxers

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-05-16 20:43:25 UTC
There are 6 boxes listed in Odessa and 10 in Midland.
LSQ's "Guardians of the Record" and Will's World's "The Great Coin
Flip Fiasco", both in Midland, were reported missing last week by
Gryzzled Gryphon and El Lobo's box, "No Pit to Hiss In" in Odessa
has been missing for a while. El Lobo also has a nice box in
Stanton, Martin County, which is only 20 miles east of Midland, but
is listed in the Texas Panhandle District. Star Seeker has a
beautiful box at the Meteor Crater west of Odessa, part of
her "Celestial" series. The one near the airport "In Memory of 5
Guardian Angels" by texasangels doesn't appear to have ever been
found, so I don't know its status. I believe all the rest are alive
and well. You could contact El Lobo, who lives in Midland, and he
would be glad to help, but he doesn't always read his email (sorry,
brother-in-law):).. She could always contact me (redbarn@eastex.net)
and I could get in touch with El Lobo and Star Seeker if necessary.

Boots Tex

called "Nodding Donkeys" (pump jacks). There's a lot of old rusty

old equipment, but none of it is hard to get to, and there are
probably endless places to stash a box of any size. There is a fee

careful. Of course, that's true just about anywhere outside in the
desert, around mesquite bushes, etc.
