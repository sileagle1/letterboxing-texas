Just joined today and wanted to introduce ourselves. We've been letterboxing for just under a year and were introduced to it by some friends of ours who read an article about it and thought it sounded like something we'd enjoy. We went out that very night and found our first box and have been hooked ever since! We're live in Waco and have planted about a dozen boxes so far, mostly in Waco. This past summer we took a road trip to Colorado and did a little letterboxing there, but spend several days boxing in Santa Fe which was a BLAST!

We're looking forward to meeting many of you this weekend in Bastrop!
