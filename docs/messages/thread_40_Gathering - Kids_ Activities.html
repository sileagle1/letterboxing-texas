My thoughts on kids' activities is to have an
icebreaker or two, then let them do their thing as
they want to do for awhile, then gather up those who
want to do something else. I'll have a few things
available for those moments when things seem to going
the wrong direction, and I'll bring some coloring
sheets or quiet activities for the kids who need a
little down time.

I don't much care for gestapo-style activity planning,
more of a laid back approach, but it sure is nice to
have plans and back up plans when it comes to kids,
then use what of them we need.

Nice to hear there are some other older girls - a crew
of three or four is nice for keeping a group of kids
busy!
