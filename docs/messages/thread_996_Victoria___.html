I've done my time in Victoria too. Lived there for 4 years, just long
enough to get through high school and get the heck out of there.
Seriously, it's a nice town, just not much to do. My step-mother lives
there and we visit her on holidays. My most recent trip was over Easter
where the most exciting part of my trip was letterboxing. Found Lost
Moi and Duck pond, even managed to drop off a hitchhiker I'd brought
with me from a recent trip to Colorado. NEVER even came close to finding
Box Kite, and after traipsing through the park carrying my 1yo and
having my 5yo drill me with about 100 questions in 100 degree heat...I
was really upset....but, the park was really neat it wasn't there when I
was in HS wish it was though, seems like a great place to hang out.
