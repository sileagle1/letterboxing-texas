Hi Turtlebug! The annual statewide event (TALE - 8) will probably be in early March, 2011, and we are hoping to do it at Fort Parker State Park (so hopefully that is "central" enough). Once we nail down, it will be posted to this talk list, and to Atlas Quest Events. Event is usually all day on a Saturday, with a campfire gathering at the end of the day (thus soome of us camp, and other stay nearby in hotels). The last 3 events we have also gathered on Friday night somewhere for dinner. We hope you can make it!!! Welcome to Texas Letterboxing.

Hi Stacey,
 
Welcome to letterboxing!  I live in the Dallas area, but make frequent trips to Central Texas.  I highly recommend the boxes in Belton along I-35.  That said, Belton had severe flooding last week in that area, and I am curious as to whether the boxes there survived.  If you enjoy hiking, there are a few boxes near Chalk Ridge Falls and the nearby dam that I have enjoyed as well (also in Belton).  Temple has a few boxes, and Waco has a great number of boxes.  I spent about 5 hours at Baylor University recently looking for boxes, and barely scratched the surface.  They had some very creative clues, and I think you will thoroughly enjoy them!
 
God bless,
Elwood Fan (Rachel)
