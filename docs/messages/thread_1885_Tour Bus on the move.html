"You may all go to hell, and I will go to Texas."
 
That is what Davy Crockett said before he left Tennessee to come to Texas, and he said it again just before he hopped on the Texas Tour Bus (I heard him!)  I know Davy is not a Native Texan, but neither am I, and I thought he should join the trip to represent those Texans who were not born here, but love it just the same.
 
The Bus is stopped at Burroughs park in Tomball.   To reach the park go east on 2920 from Tomball to Huffsmith-Kohrville road and turn left. Go 1.7 miles to West Huffsmith road and turn right. Go 1.3 miles to the park entrance on the left. Turn in and stay on the main road to the back of the park (passing the lake). At the end, circle around the loop and park near the trail sign on the right (opposite the playground area.)
TO THE BOX:
Start at the trail sign.  With the sign to your back start your journey on the crushed granite path into the woods, passing the volleyball court on your right.  After about 310 steps you will arrive at a sandy trail on your right (it is the first trail off the main trail.)  Take it for 45 steps.  Stop and look to your left, you should see 2 large pines side by side 22 steps off the trail.  Walk to the pine on the right, the bus is hidden behind it under sticks and pine needles.  Have Fun!  Oh, and bring a BIG backpack...it is a very large box!  MO5
