Good morning, guys!

As of this morning, we have 9 Texas Letterboxing T-shirts on order. When we
reach 20, I'll submit the order and then it's about 3 weeks before they're
ready.

The Texas T-shirts are 100% cotton, "beefy T's", in desert-washed pigment
dyed colors of dusty blue or dusty green. On the upper left chest area is
the orange Texas state shape letterboxing logo (see the files). In the past,
these shirts have cost about $9.00 each. Sizes are YOUTH (S,M,L) or ADULT
(S,M,L,XL,XXL,XXXL) with the larger sizes costing a couple of dollars more.
If you would like to order T-shirts, please include the following
information in an email to us at:

celticlions@comcast.net

1. Your trailname

2. Your "real name"!

3. Your shipping address

4. The number & sizes of shirts

5. The color (Blue or Green)

We MetroBoxers are talking about a gathering or mini-gathering this fall,
but nothing is beyond "chatting" stage yet. If we are able to host
something, then of course everyone is welcome to pick up shirts at that
event. Barefoot Lucy will be hosting a "barn-raising" possibly in October,
so that may be another pick-up opportunity.
However, when you are estimating your cost, it would be better to assume you
may need to pay shipping (about $3-$4.00). Checks payable to me will be due
only when I can confirm to you the exact cost, which will happen when we
have 20 or so shirts ordered.
