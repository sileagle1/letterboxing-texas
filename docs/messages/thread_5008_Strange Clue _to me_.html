You can enter the coordinates into google earth and with the aerial photo zoomed in all the way you can usually see right where the coordinates are leading you to. I know some geocachers who can look at these views and go "Yup, it's right inside the end of that guardrail." or "right under the skirt of that light pole." If you do this on a smart phone in the field with you you can really cut down the search time.
