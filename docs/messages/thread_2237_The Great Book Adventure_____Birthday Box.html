 We CelticLions never put folks in the dungeon for being green…or silly. After all, we know it’s not easy being green. However, we have no patience with people who are fuchsia…just an idiosyncrasy of ours, I fear.

Happy Birthday to Cassie in advance!                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                If Miss Cassie has her own stamp and is an especially careful stamper, she would be welcome to stamp into the Birthday calendar! When you see the size of the little squares you have to aim for, you’ll understand my caveat!!! In general, six-year olds might be a tad young to stamp in to a letterbox without very heavy-duty parental supervision – I have a letterbox log on a shelf in the dungeon where a young ( I assume!) letterboxer joyfully stamped ( or half-stamped) every page in the book. There is a half-hearted apology from the parent written on the last page about “how much _ _ _ _ _enjoys her new hobby”…..this IS the kind of thing that makes normally well-behaved and pleasant CelticLions go bonkers. Even long-suffering Stillwater.

But I do hope you’ll bring Miss Cassie over to give it a go! (There are some fun little items that traveled with the box she might enjoy!)
