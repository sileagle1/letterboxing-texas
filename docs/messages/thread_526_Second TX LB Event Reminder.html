Since many may not be aware, I thought it timely to remind
everyone of the TX LB Rodeo Event to be held at Brazos Bend
State Park on Saturday, March 19, 2005. You can find
information about it in the Links section under TX Events,
or go directly to it from this link:

http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/TLRE/

coming and want to bring something, just email me and I
will add you to the list. I just looked and there are
still some camping sites available, but you need to act
fast. Looking forward to seeing everyone!
