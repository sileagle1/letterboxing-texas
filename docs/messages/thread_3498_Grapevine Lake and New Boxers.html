My in-laws have been out of town, so we've been feeding their goats and dogs and cats and donkeys these past few days.  We went out there last night in Fearless Fox's current project, a Toyota Selica convertible, and it was glorious!  The trees and grass were all so green and the air smelled so rich, I couldn't decide if my eyes were going to burst before my nose pulled itself right off my face as they all tried to take everything in.
 
 Top that off, and we got there to find two newborn goats and had to go looking for a third, which we found in a luscious little shady meadow near the pens - how much more can a Texan ask for than a beautiful spring-time day, except maybe not to have to go back indoors?
