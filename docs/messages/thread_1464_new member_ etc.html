hello all,

i'm a native who is back in the DFW area, though letterboxing is a
very new hobby for me. i started this past summer in north
carolina, lost my log book after ~15 finds, and have since started
over. as some of you know, i indicate aubrey as my home, though
technically the house is located in oak point. these are areas ~10
miles east of denton (basically, take 380 east until you see the new
communities). so, since it's a bit of a drive to get down to the
LB's in the DFW area, i try to make the most of my time while i'm
there.
anyway, i wanted to share a couple pieces of info, for what they're
worth:
- if y'all need someone to adopt the Day of the Dead stamp, it's a
rather quick and painless drive for me to make (though pulling my
own car up to the tree to look in was not so painless).
- Barefoot Lucy (and everyone else): i have carved and ready to go
5 stamps to be placed up in this area, to include one in denton
proper. i haven't planted them yet, because i wanted to round out a
series i'm making, which is 2 stamps away from being complete. AND,
i didn't want y'all to come all the way up here for just one or two
stamps at a time, as opposed to the total of 7 i'll have ready
relatively soon. all that to say, if you can't get your daughter's
boyfriend out to a LB somewhere else, i'll be bringing the LB's to
him.

cheers,

p ?uest
