Washington DC

From: Pastry Princess (hookem77532@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-06-12 01:14:22 UTC
Well, I have been out of pocket the last couple of days a few of us
from our GS troop took a trip to Washington DC for the 95th anniv. of
GS. (sing along) We had a blast. While there we got a fellow TEXAN
involved in letterboxing. We had her pick one she might want to find
and what does she pick... The one that turns out to be appox. a 5 mile
hike round trip. OH MY GOSH was I tired. She didn't relize how far it
would take us. And guess what the box wasn't there.
Then on Sat. while at the sing along we found a lady with a
letterbox at the event. Then we found someone that You Guys with more
experience might know...Scarab!!! That's right the one and only!! I
snatched him right up before he could ride away.
He was great with us newbies and even gave us a little hint on
what to ask for with his travler. Then up walks Sam I am of the FD.
We must have been a sight. Stamps everwhere, books laid out, and
having a Blast!!!!
There was even a troop leader next to us that said it all sounded so
great she was going to check it all out when she got home.
Pastry Princes
