Hi all,
 
I'm still around lurking and reading the digest.  I enjoy reading about everyone's letterboxing adventures.  Silver Eagle, your spring break trip sounded great.  You covered a lot of ground in those few days.  I was exhausted just reading about it.  I visited most of the places you listed when I was growing up.  Balmorhea State Park and the swimming pool and Ft. Davis were among my favorite places to visit.  My dad was in the CCC and helped build a lot of the structures at the state park.
 
Anyway, I received my May/June edition of "Quilter's Home" in the mail yesterday.  Imagine my surprise when I found letterboxing listed as #14 in an article titled, "Tips for a sensational quilting summer.  Mood enhancers for summer fun: Some sewing, planning, and a sense of play..."  It is a nice little paragraph about the joys of letterboxing with your family and gives the website link.
 
You just might be finding quilt themed letterboxes soon...right, Lonestar Quilter?
 
Have a great weekend,
