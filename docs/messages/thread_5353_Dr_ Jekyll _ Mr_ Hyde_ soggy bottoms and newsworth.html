Dr. Jekyll & Mr. Hyde, soggy bottoms and newsworthy clues

From: (wry_me@yahoo.com) | Date: 2014-03-15 19:33:03 UTC-07:00


         For anyone going back out to Huntsville to finish up, I've moved Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde to spread things out a bit and updated the clues accordingly, so be sure to check iffen you want it. 

         We managed to do a little boxing yesterday and pull a temp box before it started to rain for real today and then had another BBQ lunch at McKenzie's by unanimous votes this afternoon.  MB, where do you find all these wonderful places to eat?  We are addicted to the fries! 

        It was fun running into Stepping by Faith's clan, even though my moving the box sent him on a wild goose chase!  At least they were close when we ran into them.  Sorry about that! 

        As Open Space and I were encumbered -- er, blessed, I mean -- by kids with divergent interests, we still didn't get all the boxes, but we did get to paddleboat and eat copious amounts of roasted marshmallows, thus hopefully generating a store of kidly goodwill we can draw upon for later forced boxing marathons.  We were feeling lazy anyway. 

          The Renegades happily wore us out and the campgrounds were packed!  Looks like another trip to Huntsville is in my future.  There are worse "problems" to have, say, spring break being OVER. Wahhhhh!  Or maybe having to study for a mid-term which you should be doing instead of noodling around online or logging finds or updating your logbook or coloring in some images, or whatever else I can think of? Except offloading the damp camping gear piled in the back of my van.  Or maybe thinking you can grab just one more on the way home, "reversed engineered," during a lull in the rain.  Riiiiiiight. I can hear Open Space laughing from here.  Dammit!  We had a damp ride home! 

          Also, I've decided you need to imbibe ungodly amounts of hooch in order to properly stumble upon Renegade Kool-Aid.  Perhaps seeing double and tripping over it?  Perhaps residual morphine use? (ha!)  The letterboxing gods are mocking my earnest sobriety.

        I'm thrilled with Martini Man's and Wisconsin's Hikers brilliant clues and can't wait to get out there.  Thanks for gifting us with boxes on your visit.
