Addendum to Jim Hogg Park:

I forgot to mention that if anyone has mentioned in their letterbox
clues to look for the "Iron Ore Exhibit Sign", it no longer exist. A
very large tree (50 foot + and 18 to 24 inches in diameter) fell
between the posts that held up the sign and smashed it smitherins.
The tree fell all on its' own from its' base between 2 and 3 months
ago according to the park caretaker. It wasn't the fault of the
city. :)
