Welcome.
 
    Nothing unusual with finding a Geocache near a letterbox.  Both hobbies have a lot in common.  One is the selection of the "perfect" hiding spot.  All of my letterboxes are "LETTERBOX-HYBRID Geocaches", meaning that they are a Letterbox AND a Geocache co-located in the same container. 
 
    Unfortunately, out here in REAL West Texas, there are very few letterboxers.  And very few letterboxes.  There are 7 in the area.  I own 5 of them.  There are 5 letterboxers, myself and 4 others.  And I got all of them started.  Even helped design, then paid for their stamps.  I am hoping they start putting out boxes soon.  All are also Geocachers.  Be thankful if your area has more.
 
    I only have 20 Letterbox finds, but nearly 200 Geocaches found.  Obviously most of my letterboxing is done when we travel to dog shows.
 
    Again, welcome.
