Thanks to Joy and Michelle and Dewberry and
CelticLions for speaking up so quickly about wanting
to help with my idea!

I've been thinking about it today and I'm going to go
ahead and start working on organizing a planting event
(hate to call it a gathering because it isn't exactly
like a regular gathering, more of a working gathering)
in the Johnson-Ellis-Navarro (JEN) area this fall. If
you've looked at the map, you see that these three
counties are all together, just south of Dallas/Fort
Worth and a little toward East Texas, and yet they
have not a single box! I had first noticed this when
my sis-in-law who lives near Corsicana expressed
interest in boxing, but there were no boxes I could
refer her to!

Here's the concept again: boxers converge on a
specific area to seed it with boxes for the
eventuality that someone in that area will be
interested. We take the time in the morning to do it,
then we get together for a potluck lunch and exchanges
and all that stuff that goes with letterboxing, then
we head out to find each other's boxes before heading
home.

I'm looking toward October. Does anyone who thinks
they may want to participate have a preference or a
specific date preference? At this moment, I am wide
open date-wise, but I know that in the next month or
so virtually every weekend will fill up with scout
stuff, so I need to put a date on the calendar soon.

I thought I would also try to start doing some
research for parks and trails and sites of interest in
the counties.

Other thoughts about making this work?

Lucy
