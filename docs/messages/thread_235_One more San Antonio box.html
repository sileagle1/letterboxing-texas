One more San Antonio box

From: remymom (remymom@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-26 21:08:19 UTC
Thanks Silver Eagle, for letting me know I overlooked one!

The one I found today was the Lone Star Beer LB... it's just a short
distance from the Peanuts one- but for some reason the name of the
box made me think it might be downtown or something, so I never even
looked at it before I printed the others out and came down here.
Anyway, I got the directions for that one and went back out to the
park and found it. And since it was such a short hike, and it wasn't
hot here yet- I decided to follow the trail around some more and
explore the park a bit. I did the "CITO thing" of geocachers and
picked up trash along the way. I didn't have a garbage bag with me
(thought I did, but didn't) so I was crumpling up any bits of paper I
found into the cups and bottles. I picked up a beer bottle that kind
of 'sloshed' a bit. And then when I put some paper down into it, it
sloshed a bit more... and when I took a closer look at the bottle- I
realized that the 'beer' wasn't beer, but a little frog! I set the
bottle down and tapped the bottom end to scare him out and set him
free. Good thing he kept "sloshing" otherwise he would've been thrown
away with the garbage!

Anyway, I had an interesting hike and sending my thanks to Silver
Eagle for filling me in on the fact that I'd missed a box planted in
the area I was hunting in the other day! :-)

Bunnybee from Amarillo- visiting in San Antonio
