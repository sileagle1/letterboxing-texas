I am so glad to see that Maggies Meat Market is still in business.  I had been wondering about it.  They have absolutely THE BEST banana pudding I have ever eaten!

How do you know so much about Victoria? Maggie's actually had a
fire a few months ago. They were closed for repairs until just
recently. They did a little remodelling while they were at it and
now everything is back to normal.

as a breakfast option. I have tried Ventura's, but La Hacienda puts
them to shame.

For Oriental cuisine, we go to Dragon Palace in the HEB shopping
center on Navarro. I'll have to add that too for more variety.

If you like the old homes, you should take the historical driving
tour. I'm not sure where the actuall starting point is, but you
can't miss the signs driving through Old Victoria. I'll find out
where to stop and list that, too.
