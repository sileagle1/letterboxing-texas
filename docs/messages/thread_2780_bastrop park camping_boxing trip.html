Re: bastrop park camping/boxing trip

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-05-22 17:39:35 UTC
We were there in early August with El Lobo and Star Seeker. We
checked out Lost Loblolly and Poison Ivy and they are both alive and
well. Trail Rider was wet and, although the logbook and stamp were
there, in bags full of water, the box was missing. We tried to
clean it up, put in a new logbook, put everything in new heavy duty
ziplock freezer bags, but unfortunately didn't have a new box with
us. You might take a box when you go if you can. Also, look for
Cool Cat. It's on the same trail and in good condition. We didn't
make it by Houston Toad or o-knot. McKinney Roughs Nature Park is
about 8 miles west of Bastrop and it's a very cool place with 4
letterboxes (nearest town listed as Cedar Creek). There is a fee,
so you might want to check it out online.

Boots Tex
