Well, the River Legacy Haunts boxes are in place. I'll warn y'all
ahead of time, they are in high traffic areas, literally! Two are
right off very busy roads. One is off a very busy trail. I'll be
surprised if they last until after Halloween, but if they do, I plan
to move the Screaming Bridge and Hell's Gate to the actual (very
secluded) locations of the haunts at a later date. I just couldn't
do it with my kids.

Oh, one more warning: if you go for Hobo when the wind is out of the
south, beware the smell. You'll be downwind of the city dump.
You'll likely want to get out of there as quickly as possible...and
not just because of the smell! (Evil laugh!!!)

Another hint: find these at night if at all possible. Your chances
of encountering something "out of the ordinary" are much greater.
Spooooooky! The park is open until 10:00pm.
