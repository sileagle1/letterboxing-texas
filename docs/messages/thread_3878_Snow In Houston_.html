From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-12-11 14:32:34 UTC
We had snow in the Houston area yesterday! It wasn't quite cold enough
to stick on the ground here at my house, but big, fat flakes were
coming down enough to bring out the kid in everyone. I know snow is no
big deal to some parts of TX and neighbors to the north, but it is
pretty rare for us. The last time it snowed was the miracle Christmas
Eve snow of 2004, but the last time it snowed this early was in 1944.
I hope this helps get everyone into the Holiday Spirit!
