Many of the boxes in SE MO seem to be placed by the same person.  We looked for two of them near Cape Girardeau, MO without success.  They were Little Whitewater CA and Old Plantation. I had used the “contact the placer” function on the LbNA website and did not get a response, so we didn’t go after any others.  However, this was during the time that some of the “contact the placer” messages were not being delivered. You might want to work on getting a response from the placer before going too far off of your path.
