If you can guess who's hit song this is then you have a clue as to who the newest passenger is!
 
Directions: La Grange is west of Houston and can be reached by travelling I-10 to the La Grange exit, Hwy 77 and turning north.

Once in La Grange you will want to get on Business 71 aka Travis street. You will take this through downtown, passing the courthouse (on the corner of Main and Travis). Just past Main, but before the bridge you will see Boss' House of Steaks on the right and River Park Car Wash on the left. Also on the Left there is a sign for river access. Take the road towards the river.
 
Park in the parking lot (do not go down towards the boat ramp).  Go away from the river to the cement support beam for the bridge.  Facing the beam look to your left and you will see a row of wooden posts.  Follow the posts counting 19 posts.  Now continue around to the left and count 11 more.  You should be in fromt of a large tree with vines wrapped around it.  Box is wedge up in the hole in the base of the tree.  It is wedged up and to the left.
 
Good luck!!
 
P.S.  You can also find the first 2 boxes of  our new series on weather, You Are My Sunshine and Raindrops Keep Falling on My Head while you are there.
