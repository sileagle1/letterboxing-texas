New LB Theme Park

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2016-09-03 18:29:30 UTC-07:00

Now that I have moved to the Hill Country I am finally exploring some of the areas around Austin and have discovered several parks I never knew about.  Some already have boxes in them that I need to find, but others are virgin territory that I plan to populate.  Today I placed boxes in 2 of these virgin parks:  Dripping With Taste, in Ranch Park in Dripping Springs, and Falconhead in Falconhead Greenway in Bee Cave.  Ranch Park only has one small trail that probably can't handle too many boxes, but the Falconhead Greenway has several trails of some length that can handle many.  In fact, since the name of the park and my box are birds, I suggest we make this a bird-themed park, similar to Telge Park being a Halloween theme, and Gus Fruh being a pirate theme.  What do you think?
