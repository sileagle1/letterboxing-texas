Saturday Gathering

From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-02-19 07:39:54 UTC-06:00

I am looking forward to seeing many of you this Saturday at the Ta-Da gathering at Cedar Ridge Nature Preserve. If you haven't signed up yet, the details are at http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=643.
 
I am bringing my "first attempt" of my signature LTC (Letterbox Trading Cards), so if anyone has one they'd like to trade, bring those too and we'll trade!
 
This is a very informal event - just bring a picnic, clues to surrounding boxes (there are three presently hidden in the nature preserve and many more in the surrounding area), your stamp, logbook and letterboxing supplies and look for a small group of boxers at the picnic tables just inside the preserve across from the visitors center by the main parking lot. If you plan to plant a box, just bring the clues to the event to share with everyone who is there.
 
See you Saturday!
