Austin trip

From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-31 16:14:28 UTC
Hey all,
I just got back from a weekend trip to Austin and had a blast. I
had some business down there on Sat., so went a day early to box my
butt off. Baby Bear's Waco box is in a beautiful location right by
the river. Then I found Silver Eagles Salado College. If you
haven't been there -it is very interesting that the town has just
left these old ruins up on the hill there. Had a couple of misses
in Georgetown and Round Rock with missing boxes so went on to
Austin. Ginger Cat has done a great job of planting boxes by great
places to throw back a pint or two! :) Austin is such an
interesting place and I used to just explore the campus and the
capitol before I got into LB. Now I discovered a "mountain" (Mt.
Bonnell) that you can climb the 99 steps to the top for a stunning
view of the river! Silver Eagle's USA box also showed me a great
natural spring pool I am going to have to take my kids swimming in
next time at Barton Springs Pool. Thanks to all who planted on the
way to and in Austin for a great weekend.

on a second note: Welcome, Welcome, Welcome, to all you new
boxers! You have found a great "home" on this list for Texas
boxers. Unlike the big USA talklist, everyone here is encouraging,
and very patient with new boxers (I was one myself back in August).
Enjoy your new hobby, and if you come to Arlington, feel free to
plant until you drop! :)
-Kantexan
