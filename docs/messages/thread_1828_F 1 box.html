From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-04-21 13:08:13 UTC
I have to admit I was a little mad that he deleted the
box without even asking first. What if it was a real
box with a "Dewberry" twist?
Anyway, I kept my response to him civil and explained
the reason behind the box. And even though we still
disagree on a few things, he is working on a way for
those coming in from LBNA to be able to see F-1 clues
without a logged find. Not sure if it can be done, though.
Right now the only way you can use the F-1 feature in AQ
but still have the clue visible to LBNA users without logged
finds in AQ is to post the clue separately in LBNA (don't
use the box in AQ). This is extra work, but will ensure
everyone can see your clue. Of course, this kind of
defeats the purpose of using F-1 in the first place since
search engines can now find your clue on LBNA. But at
least the one on AQ would be safe. I like to think of
LBNA as the main clue repository and AQ as the backup,
but right now, many are doing it the other way. Nothing
wrong with that, but you may not be aware of problems
like the F-1 affair in the future.
I've already spent way more time on this whole thing than
I ever wanted to, but at least most of you know the
situation now and will hopefully be more aware in the future.
As for future boxers ...?

Silver Eagle
(Who me spike the punch :>)
