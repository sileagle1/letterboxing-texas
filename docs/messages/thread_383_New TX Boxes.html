Yippeeee! A reason to visit Amarillo! That is the one area of Texas we've never visited and I'd love an excuse!

And as far as hiding boxes...this started me grinning because that's exactly what our family has been obsessing about lately. My husband decided to try making a fake rock out of mortar to hide a box in an urban park. My son & I pictured something just slightly larger than the Tupperware container...man, were we wrong!

The finished "rock" weighed at least 8 pounds (it felt like 20) and was about a foot in diameter. I think it was about 6 inches high, and that was BEFORE we hit on the idea of adding pebbles & rocks to the top...;-)

The trick was to keep the “rock” moist as it was drying, so it would harden evenly.

When it came to lugging this monstrosity to the location, I couldn't do it. My husband had added his favorite camping tool to the backpack to excavate a slight recess in which to place the rock.(Of course part of that was because I was laughing too hard at our attempt to be "stealthy")Raindear and Kantexan of the Samuraistampers have found this box, and they've both been very polite about it, but I wish I could have seen their faces... ;-)
