Yep, Pardner, the show is on the hoof again. This time they're setting up in the state park just southwest of where Frost Maiden works (hint: Johnson County Courthouse) and which bears the same name as the town she works in. To find 'em, go to the park and pay your toll; then drive down the park road to a small parking area on your right .3 miles beyond the little bridge. Take the right trail down to the creek and, after fording the creek, continue up the trail. At a fork in the trail continue straight, following the "To Spillway" sign, until you reach a T marked by a sign advising you that the lake loop and spillway lie to your left. Looking right you will notice a sign advising that that road is closed. Disregard that sign and boldly go down the road to the right. Soon you will reach another sign warning you to "Go Back". Engage in civil disobedience to the extent of six more steps, then turn to your right to see a gully trail. Follow it for twenty steps. To your left, nestled within the branches of a cedar, the stage is set.
 
By the way, and just as a point of curiosity, how many of you can identify the opening sentence of this report?
