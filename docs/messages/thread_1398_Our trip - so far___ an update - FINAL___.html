I guess this is the final report. Justin didn't even make the first
cut. He was eliminated in the first round. Hey - these are the top
Great Danes in the nation. Just being here was honor enough.

So our total is:

1 trophy for 1st place (Justin in Rally Obedience)
1 trophy for 3rd place (Sammy in puppy class)
1 gold ribbon (Parker for new title - RN)
1 blue ribbon (Justin in Rally Obedience)
1 yellow ribbon (Sammy in puppy class)
2 green ribbons (Parker and Justin for qualitying scores in Rally
Obedience)

The good news is that the dog who won the points from Justin is the
son of my dog's brother. He belongs to my wife's best friend. It was
the last points he needed to earn his championship. How fitting that
he is finished at the national finals. He will compete for Best Of
Breed tomorrow. This lady is on her 4th round with cancer, so I am
glad she won. Somehow we don't mind losing to her. And since my wife
had a lot to do with that breding, she gets some of the credit too.

Me and Parker - we are going out after lunch.... seems there are about
4 geocaches within walking distance of the show grounds.

We depart for Goodyear AZ tomorrow right after Best Of Breed judging
here. Two more days of showing, then we are on our way back home. So
far we are quite pleased. Many will leave here with nothing. We have
some fine dogs. Mighty fine. Of course, Parker is the finest!
He is probably dreaming of lunch right now....

I will be disconnecting at noon here, may not be back up till we are
home. Unless the Motel-8 in Goodyear has internet connectivity in the
rooms.

Y-all have a GREAT weekend. I hope the weather holds well for the
gathering. Perhaps some day we will make it out that way too.

Mike. With Parker snoring away on the bed.
