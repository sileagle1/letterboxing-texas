Texas Tour Bus - Stephen F Austin State Park

Placed by: TeamKing
Placement date: May 13, 2005
County: Austin
Nearest city: San Felipe
Number of boxes: 1
Number of stamps: 2 plus 1 passenger


The Texas Tour Bus has left the station!

The first stop acknowledges the Father of Texas - Stephen F Austin - at
the state park in his name.

To get to the park: Take I-10 to exit #723 San Felipe (19 miles west of
Katy, 3 east miles of Sealy, approx. 150 miles east of San Antonio) and
follow the signs to the park - about 3 miles off of I-10.

To find the box:
As this is a state park, there is a $3 entrance fee for adults. Pay
your fee and get a map. Either follow the signs or look on the map for
the amphitheatre and the group camp area. Between these is the
Cottonwood Trail. Follow the trail until you come to the Raccoon Bend
trail going off to the right. Take this trail. At the top of the
stairs, stop and look left. About 17 paces off is a 4-5 ft tree stump.
Box is located about 1 ft from stump on the side closest to you and
under some brush.

As the first finder, your job is to take the box with you, add a
passenger (stamp), and place it in a new location for someone else to
find. Please let this list know when box is found so no one makes an
unnecessary trip. Then after you re-place it, please place your clue on
this list. That's it!

Box is not listed on LBNA since this is a 'list only' traveling box.
However, it is on Atlas Quest if you want log your find.

Also, while in the park don't pass up the 'Father of Texas' stamp
behind the amphitheatre or the 'Brazos Fish' stamp, which is off of the
Sycamore Trail just down the road from the Cottonwood Trail.

Thanks! Hope you enjoy it!
