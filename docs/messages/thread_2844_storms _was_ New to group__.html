I lived in the San Fernando Valley for the first 15 yrs of my life. So, I was there during the Sylmar earthquake. My Nany actually lived IN Sylmar. At least with an earthquake you can pretty safely go outside and not have to worry about being sucked up into the air. If the ground starts opening up where you are standing you can move and stay away from buildings and trees that may fall on you. (But, truthfully, it was still scary) Tornados, they horrify me, they just scare the poop out of me. It's really hard trying to not terrify Cassandra when there's one around. I try to make a game out of it, when in truth, I'm praying for our lives.
 
Janet, BF
