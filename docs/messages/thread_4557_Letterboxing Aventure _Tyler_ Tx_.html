Letterboxing Aventure (Tyler, Tx)

From: J. G. Bartley (crazydaisycat@hotmail.com) | Date: 2010-05-31 18:37:10 UTC-05:00
Today my family and a family friend who has been to a Letterboxing Workshop but doesn't Letterbox regularly had an adventure!
 
First, we went to Rose Rudman Park and found GAQLB08: Buffalo Wings. Then off to How Hot is it? East Texas. We got lost even though I know that park like the back of my hand. While on our way back to the car we past the spot where I Lost My Car is and my friend (Lissy, we call her but her stamp says Rose City on it), Well, I gave her some clues that I memorized for it and she found that box. So, Mother's Love Lissy found it. Just to let you know.
 
Then, we decided to eat lunch since it was 1:30. So we drove to Chick-fil-a and found MMM!! Sweet Tea! At lunch we all had sweet tea.
 
After that, we went to the Red Barn area and attempted the THE CORSAGE.
 
Then, we went to Faulkner Park and we wander about a half a mile in looking for A Sign: I Love You. The we decided to go back to look at the map at the entrance to the trail. We decided to come back to that box and look for 15 Minutes we read the clue and it said go to the bench and trash can. We were just there! So we walked back and found that box. While sitting on the bench stamping (this is the funny part) there, two dogs run by, one a German Shepherd and the another a small black dog. Then a couple seconds later we here this yelling, we ignored it. Then Lissy said is he saying help?  So we scream back are you saying help? No answer, then we here it again. Then, there was a rustling in the woods. A man comes running out asking have you seen two dogs. We answer yes and point to the left. Now this man looks like James the bad vampire on Twilight. Since we were finding 15 Minutes we thought it was right to say (just to go with the box) he was the GEICO Caveman. We don't know if he found the dogs but there was a sign a the trail head saying all dogs must be on leases. Now I see why.
 
This was our adventure for the day.
 
MegaFamily
