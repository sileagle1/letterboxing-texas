As far as I know Choi does not fit into the Texas story.  He lives in MA and became the webmaster of LBNA after Wes Garrison left in 2004 or 2005.  Eoghan on the other hand was one of the early TX letterboxers, though not the first, and I would love to hear more about his history.  I have surmised that he was a University of TX student in 2000 when he started placing boxes and he was dating his future wife, Pumpkin Princess, who also letterboxed.  Both did Geocaching, too, along with TX Hipower, now known as PI Joe.  Perhaps he could tell us more?

FYI, the first TX letterboxers were a group of ladies in the Dallas area and the first TX letterbox was Duck Creek #1 placed by Julie on 8/16/1998.  When I first started letterboxing I tried to find this box (and a few others in the area) but they were already missing.  I think Eoghan was able to find it, though.  Here is a link to Julie's old website, which includes a link to a picture:  http://anniespark1e.tripod.com/letterboxing.html
