First letterbox attempt

From: monkeytoes108 (cruschhaupt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-03-04 03:05:42 UTC
I work in Seadrift and got off early today so I thought I'd head on
down to Magnolia Beach to find Silver Eagle's "French Flag"
letterbox. Exellent hiding place Mr. Eagle, but alas no box was in
it. I looked around for a while and thought I'd retrace my steps.
This time when I approached the cement block named in your clue, I
noticed the biggest rattlesnake I've ever seen. If I had put my
hand on top of the block when I firstI looked under it, I would have
felt snakeskin. This rattled (pun intended) me all the way home...
empty handed.

I'm writing this to inform Silver Eagle of the unfound box and to
remind everyone else of the dangers involved in our little hobby
here. I've been outdoorsee all my life and know about snakes. For
some reason I didn't think of them being at the shore. Never let
your guard down. I shudder to think what would have happened if the
snake would have been in the whole i stuck my face in.

Treading carefully,
MonkeyToes
