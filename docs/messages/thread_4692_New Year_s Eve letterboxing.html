New Year's Eve letterboxing

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-12-31 22:02:29 UTC-06:00

Trail Whale and I took Fearless Fox and Draconis viridis down to the 4C to do a little overnight mud-slogging, so on our way home we stopped off in Alto to find a couple of JBK's boxes. We were able to find POW's at La Pitit Mont Rouge and The Sunflower. One was pretty water-logged, but we were able to get it cleaned up enough to make do until JBK gets a chance to check in on it - the other was in good shape but the logbook was full so we added some pages.

Trail Whale was decidedly curious about the whole thing, but he was unable to get out and walk with me due to his shoes being swamped by some enthusiastic puddle-hopping that occurred during our stop at Mission Tejas. He did declare that his whale stamp is to be done in orange, so any time you see a cute little whale stamped in orange, you know who that was (and that reminds me - he took possession of his stamp in the van so I'd better find it before it disappears forever!)

Happy New Year to you all!
