Newbie saying Howdy!

From: Jet Phoenix (jet_phoenix@email.com) | Date: 2005-01-19 16:35:26 UTC-06:00
Howdy from Wichita Falls, Texas!

I just learned of letter boxing this past November, quite by accident. My sister-in-law and I decided to go on a camping trip to Lake Arrowhead (Clay county) which is very close to where we live (we leave the men at home, they're no fun)! While I love to camp, I thought I would see what else one could do at Lake Arrowhead, and stumbled upon clues left by SilverEagle. I thought, now that sounds like FUN!!! We both just happened to have some blank books we picked up, and hadn't used for anything, and I happened to have stamp pad....so off we went, spirits high! We took picturs of stuff we saw along the way (butterflies, yep! even in November). But alas, there was no box to be found. But we talked about what fun it was just looking all the way back to camp.

So, while I haven't actually found a box yet, I am already hooked! LOL I am really looking foreward to hitting the local areas and finding those boxes! I see that SilverEagle has been very thorough up in here in North Texas, so I will just thank you now. *grin*


Jet Phoenix
