Question 3. Are there any CAN'T MISS boxes at the River Walk? Alamo?
Near-by area? Are there any ABSOLUTELY CAN'T MISS boxes in San Antonio?

There are no boxes at the River Walk or Alamo that I know of.  There are 3 boxes on the south side of SA at the one of the missions and on a hike/bike trail near there. (The missions are NPS territory, so I can’t confirm that they are still there).  Closer to downtown there are 2 boxes at Brackenridge Park.  The park has just had extensive renovations, and I can’t confirm that they are still there.  Your best bet would be McAllister Park, on the north side of SA, very near the airport.  There are 4, maybe 5 boxes in that park, and there’s no real reason to think they might have been compromised.
