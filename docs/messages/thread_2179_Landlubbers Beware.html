That is so cool about the coyotes!  I had lived in TX for a long time, been camping in many many parks (yes, even in west Texas) and never seen one, much to my dismay.  The first one I saw was on a trail across from my Grandmother's farmhouse in Michigan where I had spent most of my childhood.  It was very Very close, right in the middle of the trail.  When my brother and I reported it to the ranger she told us it was a lost black lab.  The black labs I have seen don't much resemble a coyote, makes me sort of wonder about the training Michigan DPWS get.  Congrats on a great morning surprise!  MO5
