Trip to Palestine

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-06-14 13:49:15 UTC
We took a little day trip yesterday up to Palestine to plant a new
Texas Governors Series box called The Railroad Man. We stopped off
at Moscow on the way to place The Post Man, another Governor. While
in Palestine, we found Barefoot Lucy's box Columbia of the
Pineywoods and enjoyed a fine lunch at the Ranch House. Then we
went to Davey Dogwood Park for BFL's Signs of Spring, Baby Bear's
Are You Thinking What I'm Thinking? (hilarious box) and Silver
Eagles's Go Dog Go. Then, to Elmwood for Blue Butterfly's Elmwood
Gardens and Cuney for Oscar's Lunch (we had already eaten, thank
you). Came through Jacksonville and picked up JBKokopelli's The
Magnolia and the Cowboy, detoured to Maydelle and found Silver
Eagle's TX RR3. Then, 2 boxes placed by Dewberry, Shenandoah Shadow
and Still Dreamin', both of which have awsome stamps. Too many
boxes, not enough time, as usual, but we did what we could. We
found all the boxes we attempted and all were in great condition. I
also went out on the Lone Star Trail with Fish Tree recently and he
placed a box called Wishin' I Was Fishin' and I placed one
called "The Lady" was a Tram. These are both near his box Saint
Hyacinth. I hope you go sometime and find his boxes. They're all
fun and his stamps are unique and outstanding.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
