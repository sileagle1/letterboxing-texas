I gritted my teeth. Still am. I am a very opinionated person. After being online since 1994 or thereabouts and getting in some pretty stressful bouts years ago, I promised myself that I would never EVER again get involved with stressful, back and forth crap with a bunch of people that I didn't *really* know and for the most part never would. It never ceases to amaze me how people never learn that things are easier said when sitting here at the keyboard all alone and not facing people and can NEVER be taken back. Once you hit that send button, that's all she wrote. AND they never learn that the written word can be the poorest of communicators and widely and often wrongly interpreted. That whole thing started with the post of ONE person who expressed an opinion with no evidence of any kind and they were off and running.
 
Not to mention the fact that I have zero knowledge of what did or didn't go on before. I have to say though, that I don't think an online group, bb, or forum has ever existed that didn't have some arguements. It's human nature. If it was a group that met weekly in person, the same thing would eventually happen.
