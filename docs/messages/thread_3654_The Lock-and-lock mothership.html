For those of you in or near Houston, I discovered yesterday that the
"Super H Mart", a Korean grocery store on Blalock, west of loop 610
and just north of I-10, has several aisles of every size of lock and
lock box that you can imagine!

very tasty fried chicken, sold at a booth labeled "chicken and joy".
