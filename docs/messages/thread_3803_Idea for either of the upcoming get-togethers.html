From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-10-07 20:55:44 UTC-05:00
I love your carving, Corazon!
 
Boots Tex and Baby Bear did carving demos at the last annual gathering and it was very helpful, especially since they each have very different carving styles and use different tools.  It seems like this is a perennial request at every gathering, so maybe it should just be a standing offering? 
 
Otherwise, I think most folks who carve bring carving stuff with them, and I would say that if a new (or not new) carver wants to see how to carve, they should look at the list of attendees and see if someone whose stamps they are especially fond of is attending, and just ask them to show you!  What's the worst they can say?

My only box in New Braunfels is “Ein Prosit!”  It’s an urban box, but unlike most urban boxes, you won’t have to be stealthy at all. You can leisurely stamp away in broad daylight and the hundreds of people that pass by you will pay no attention to you at all.

 

There are several good boxes in NB by several placers, but it’s my experience that NB boxes go missing far more often than others. I hope you are successful and find all that you are looking for!
