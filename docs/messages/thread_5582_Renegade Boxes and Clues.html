Renegade Boxes and Clues

From: Ann Grimes (grimesae@yahoo.com) | Date: 2017-01-06 02:25:16 UTC
I have a question for the group about the Renegade boxes. I am willing to play!

How do you get the clues? I sent for clues several month back to a box before the event in October and no one responded.

There are 2 boxes near the coming TALE and I would like to go find them. But somebody has to send out the clues. 

Boxes: Super Genius and Beep Beep!

This from the clues on atlasquest site:

If you would like the clues to this box, please submit a email request to: renegades_4@yahoo.com. 

Please include in request the full name of the Renegade Box. Your request will be considered, and if approved by the Renegades the clues will be emailed to you. Clues are not to be shared with anyone else. If box comes to harm due to your actions you will be black listed from future Renegade clues. If you think you have what it takes to play with The Letterboxing Renegades give it a try!

This is what it says to do and I followed the rules and nothing. Can you tell that I am frustrated with this group (Renegades) whom ever they are. Can't play your game if the clues aren't sent out upon request and who decides if you get the approval. 
We need to have an inviting group for others to join and for those of us who have been LB for a while to feel like we are included in the fun. 

Echo
