I have done a couple of series, and I have another ready to be planted.  It is interesting you should ask this today, because I just thought of and idea, and I'd like to see if there is any interest.  If you box with kids (and I almost always do!) series are good because there is more "bang for the buck".  I was thinking of the theme of "Mythical Creatures" for a series, and there are so many to chose from!  So then I thought what if anyone who was interested could sign up to carve a stamp and make a logbook and send them to me.  Then I will plant and maintain the boxes, all along one trail.  Each contributor can take credit for their own box, I'll just send on the clues and you can each post them.  I will also stamp all the stamps and send them to the contributors, so that you can have the stamps in case you are not from the Houston area.  Does this make sense?  If not it is most likely because I am so distraught over sending my little boot wearing boy off to kindergarten tomorrow.  But if it does and you are interested let me know...I'll only have 2 kids at home all day this school year, and I need something to do with all this extra free time!  MO5
