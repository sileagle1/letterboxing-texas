From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-10-25 21:26:57 UTC
Yes, I am a member of the box of the month club.

12 of us, from all across the US are banding together for a unique
project. Each of 12 boxes will spend (a little less than) one month
in each destination.

I will hide the first box soon (probably Memorial Park for easy
access). Then, on the 25th of the month, it will be pulled and mailed
to its next destination. As soon as I receive the next month's box
I'll post it. I probably will announce here, or on GulfCoast Boxers
as well as on AQ.

November's Box is provided by Speedsquare, from Indiana, and is titled
I never promised you a Rose Garden. Look for clues around November 1st.

I may have a special prize for those who get all 12 boxes. Still
thinking of that.

dewberry

Re: xpost Box of the Month Club

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-10-26 12:44:53 UTC
It'll be interesting to see how it goes.

I warned the group not to expect too many finders when their boxes
were down here. We just don't seem to be the rush-out-and-find-it
type of crowd. Not like some of the other stops the boxes are going
to make.

I had done a similar project where I was carving stamps based on what
was blooming in my garden. I would leave the box out for 3 months,
then pull it and hide it in a new spot for the new season with a new
stamp (it had the same logbook, which I thought would be neat). There
really was very little interest (1-2 finders maybe per stop), so I
ceased that project. Generally, I don't care how many finders my
boxes get, as long as the people who do find it seem to enjoy it, but
this seemed like wasted effort that could go into other boxes. These
were some of my most intricate stamps to date and I was putting a lot
of time into them.

I had a box this summer that was out for two months and had two finders.

Now my holiday lights series last year got a lot of finders (I thought
it was fun and people seemed to enjoy it). I have an idea for a new
holiday series for this year that I think might be well received as
well.

dewberry

Thanks to Bailey's Bunch for being FFs on Hefe-Bison. Each recently
planted box is my favorite, but I have to say, these clues will
probably always make me smile!
