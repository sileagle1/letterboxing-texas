Lone Star Trail

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-02-09 15:56:14 UTC-06:00
Oh, darn, we're a little disappointed. We thought our creatures
would find a home up here in our back yard and encourage visitors,
who might then find our local boxes. There are 128 miles of Lone
Star Trail that could use more boxes (hint, hint) :-)
 
I know that the Lone Star Hiking Trail would be a better home, and I agree it needs more boxes.  But when I started thinking about being responsible for so many boxes created by other people, I began to think about whether or not that was a realistic place for me to put the series.   Almost every time I go letterboxing I am doing so with at least two of the five, usually the littlest two, which can sometimes make longer trips from home difficult, especially when they have to be completed before time to pick the big kids up from school!  So I thought it would be better to put them near my house so that I could easily keep an eye on them.  I know this is sort of like changing the rules in the middle of the game, and I hope it hasn't upset any of you.  I promise this Spring I'm going to have some boxes ready to go on the Lone Star Trail...some of my own, so if  something happens to them I will have no one to blame but myself :)  MO5
