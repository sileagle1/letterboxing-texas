Y'all are so funny. 

Just recently, we were across the river in Indiana.  The parking space we were in was directly across from one of those cart corrals in the middle of the parking lot.  Not a single card in there and the darned thing was fairly wide.  We were in the Durango, with only one F350 pulling into a space near us.
 
"I wonder if this car could fit into that," I told Teepsman, as we were "buckle up buttercup"ing.

Teeps starts inching forward until the Durango was totally in the cart corral.  Now, we couldn't open the doors or anything, but darned if we didn't laugh for a while.
