New Gordon Grouper Microbox

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-01-03 15:49:05 UTC
Yesterday I finally made it back to the Lake Jackson area to find a few
of the boxes placed there recently and to place a new one at Sea Center
TX (Gordon Grouper 2). My first Gordon Grouper had been there since
2003 but recently went missing, so I carved a new stamp and put it in a
new hiding place. For those that stamped my first box, this gives you
an excuse to visit this great facility again, and for those that have
never been, it is a great place for families to visit. Enjoy!

Silver Eagle

P.S. I also posted a new TX counties map with the first colored county
of 2008 (Garza), placed by Baby Bear.
