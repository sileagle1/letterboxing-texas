A late-coming introduction

From: Lauren (laurenlafleur@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-08 05:12:09 UTC
Hi all,

Finally I am making some time to introduce myself to the group. Sorry
for taking so long. (I'm the friend Barefoot Lucy mentioned in a post
earlier last week.)

I live in Fort Worth (moved here from East Texas about 8 months ago),
and I have been fascinated by letterboxing for a long time -- even
before I wrote the article about BL's family and their letterboxing.
Finally, I'm working a job that allows me some free time in an area
full of boxes -- and I've been looking for a new hobby that will get
me out and about, and is something I can occasionally take my dog,
Molly, with me. (She and I could both use the fresh air and some walks.)

So, I've stocked up on some stamp-carving supplies (gotta love Hobby
Lobby) and I have a personal stamp design in mind -- I just need to
make it a reality now.

I'm looking forward to my first LB adventure, and I will be sure to
let everyone know when it finally happens =) (I have yet to go hunting
for boxes, but I was very fortunate to share the experience of
planting a box with BL and her family.) I'm also looking forward to
planting some in my area as well as areas where family and friends
live. (I'm fortunate to have my mom in East Texas, my dad on the Gulf
Coast near Beaumont, and my best lifelong friend in Brazoria County --
I'm going to LB all over Texas!)

So, hello from Cowtown, and I not only look forward to sharing
experiences with you all, but I also look forward to learning from
you, too.

Have a great one,
News Girl =)
