while the letterboxes are probably the least important concern in galveston (and the houston
area), we've still wondered how they've faired. our five year old son was watching some of the
footage from galveston and we saw very close to where our two recently planted boxes were.

images we were seeing were right by where we planted the boxes, our five year old said,
"darnit! i guess we're going to have to plant some more...but that's ok." :-)
