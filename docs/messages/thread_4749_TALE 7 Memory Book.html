Re: TALE 7 Memory Book

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2011-03-11 15:23:27 UTC
Well, friends, I fear I have gotten in over my head. Here's the response I got from Shutterfly and if you can understand it, you're way ahead of me:

"Dear Shutterfly Customer,

Thank you for contacting Shutterfly.

I have given the steps to share the photo book project from one account to the other. Please follow the steps:

1) Click on "view all projects" in "My projects" section in "My Shutterfly" page.
2) Select the project in "My projects".
3) Click on "share project" on the right hand side of the page.
4) Please select the "email" option.
5) Enter the email address
6) click on "send"
7) You will receive an email with the "share link"
8) Click on the "link" and "save" the project in your new account.

Once you receive our share email, you can click on the link inside the email to access the book. You can save the book from the email to your account by clicking the box under the project thumbnail and then clicking on the "save to my account" link on the right hand side. It will then prompt you to log into your account, and then the book will be saved automatically.

The shared book can be saved in the account. You can make changes to the book and also order the same.

If you require further assistance, please feel free to contact us.

Sincerely,

Hema P.
Shutterfly Customer Service"

Boots (Confused) Tex
