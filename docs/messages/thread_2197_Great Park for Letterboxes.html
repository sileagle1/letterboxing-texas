Great Park for Letterboxes

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-10-01 16:20:13 UTC-05:00
I know lots of people are coming from out of Town for the gathering in October, and I know the number of boxes and size of Houston can be pretty overwhelming.  Dewberry is going to "show off" Memorial park on Sunday, a great place with lots of fantastic boxes.  Another great place to letterbox in Houston is Telge Park in Cypress, where the Terrors on Telge was held last October.  The park has many letterboxes, most with a Halloween theme.  This week I added "The Haunted Hotel", a five box series.  Dewberry has 2 new ones there, and Teamking has re hidden her Halloween box.  This is a great park to go with kids, and most of the boxes are right up kids alleys.  I just thought I'd point it out to anyone visiting and not sure where to go, and also to all the new Houston area boxers who may not be aware that Telge is "Haunted" with letterboxes!  When I don't have a mad baby trying to help me type I'll sit down and post a list of all the boxes that can be found in this park.  Happy Hauntings...only 30 days until Halloween!!!!!MO5
