% of finds logged

From: Sandy Milfeld (pugapalooza@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-11-06 23:20:40 UTC-06:00
I've always been curious about how many folks actually log their
finds. I collected two logbooks and did some math (yuck!) to find
out what percentage of boxers are logging finds. The average for the
two boxes: 48% of finders logged on AQ or LbNA.

So now I know that when I've gotten enough reported finds to fill
half a logbook, the logbook is probably close to full. :-)
