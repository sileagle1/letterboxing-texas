DH = dancing hamster, yes.  Smart you for figuring that out!
DD = doodley-diddley.  It's a nonsense term to fill up space when you can't think of the right word.  Can be used as a noun or as an adjective.
DS = these are my uncle's initials.  He is a rather famous man, and people like to name-drop in order to feel more important.  It's surprising to me how often he gets mentioned.
