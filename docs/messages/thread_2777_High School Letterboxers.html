High School Letterboxers

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-05-18 15:30:50 UTC
Our son (trail name: fishtree) teaches high school art. He has a
small sculpure class this semester and one of his projects was
letterboxing. Each student had to come up with a trail name and make
a signature stamp. Then, they had to make a letterbox and carve a
stamp, then hide the box somewhere. Also, they had to register on
LbNA and post their letterbox. I notice that a couple of them (snake
eyes and Tacosaurus) have posted boxes in the Atascosita area, so if
y'all are over that way, you might want to look for them.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter

We have three (none of them are kids anymore :) ). One teaches high
school art, one is a paralegal for the Texas CPS and one is a Navy
wife living in Naples, Italy. They have all done some
letterboxing. They are fishtree (he has a box on the Lone Star
Trail), Will's World (he has several placed boxes) and Flash Dreamer

granddaughter, Princess Aurora, also has a placed box. We're a
letterboxing family!
