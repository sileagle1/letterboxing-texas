I'm glad everyone has their different styles in boxing - whether in
placing or finding. The more and more boxes I find, the more you
really get to know everyone from a long distance kind of thing. I
used to really hate urban boxes that were really public. I was
convinced that EVERYONE could see me getting it and I would ruin (or
cause ruin) to someone's box. But as I have gotten more boxes, I have
found no one is really looking at you. Just be casual and re-hide as
well or better than you found it. Seems to work for me. I am
sometimes surprised at the choices for hiding places I come across,
but then I think about my own boxes and figure someone out there
probably thinks the same about them. As time goes on, I think people
will improve their hiding spots. It may take boxes to come up missing
for someone before they improve on their hiding places, but to each
his own.

I still can't believe that Old Town Spring box is still there! It was
so urban and barely hid (in my opinion) that I figured it for a short
timer (but I wanted that location, so I just went with it). Even so,
it has obviously stuck in your head as one you remember.

Those are the boxes I like best.
