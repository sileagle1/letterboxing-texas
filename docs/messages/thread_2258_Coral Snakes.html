I've got to add my 2 cents worth here.  Coral snakes are pretty.  So pretty, in fact, that they can make us do crazy things.  A man walked into our ER with a snake bite on one hand and a (dead) coral snake in the other hand.  He wanted to know if it was poisonous.  He said it was such a pretty snake and he'd never seen one before.  So, he picked up the pretty snake to look at it closer.  He was airlifted to a trauma center in San Antonio where he ended up on a ventilator.  He did fine and is now home, but I bet he now knows the difference between the Coral and King snakes.
