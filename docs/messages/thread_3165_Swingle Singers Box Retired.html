Swingle Singers Box Retired

From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-11-07 11:57:18 UTC
I'd gotten a report that my Swingle Singer's stamp in San Antonio was
falling apart. I knew after I'd planted it that my carving skills at
that time didn't exactly lend themselves to long-life stamps. So,
when Blue Butterfly made a trip to San Antonio this weekend, I asked
her to pick up the LB so I could retire it. This box was hidden for
about 20 months and has seen visitors from all over the US. I can't
wait to see the logbook....thanks to all who found the Swingle Singers
LB.

Lucy Locket
