Thanks dfwnurse for asking what this meant.

With timeoneous off in Deer Park for a debate tournament I ventured to
Georgetown Saturday.

Explored Inner Space Cavern and Berry Springs Park. Found several nice
stamps.. 2 nicely carved by Quatzel. Berry Springs Park was a
particularly nice setting on an historic farm compound with an old pecan
grove. One lovely Lone Star Quilter stamp was particularly colorful and
the Berry Buzy Beaver Letterbox path has been "connected," this was a
nice short hike on a fairly warm day.

Unfortunately I could not get near Blue Hole Park. It was closed up
tight due to flooding according to a posting on the Internet on July
10th. There are at least 5 boxes being held captive in there!!! If
anyone knows when it is scheduled to reopen I would appreciate it if you
would let me know.

I also went to Florence for the second time to find the passport series
box. I found the Florence Cemetery but not on Second and Preslar and I
did not find the stump clues talked about. Found a Maggie Gardner, but
not a Margaret. Found the very nice Holland box earlier this year and
thought I would pursue the Athens at the Fishin 4 Boxes Event on Sept
22. I want the special stamp for finding them all, so I am not giving up.
