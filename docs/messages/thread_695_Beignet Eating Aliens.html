We met another couple with their little boy and girl on the trail a
month or so ago, looking for a couple of LBs at Challenger 7 Park.
They were stamping their books and they saw us coming with our little
stamping "kit" They were new at LBing and merely said, "Hey, we bet
we know what your looking for." We answered back, "And we bet we
know what you're doing too!" It made for a nice afternoon. We
introduced ourselves and spent the rest of the afternoon searching
for the remainder of the boxes together. NEW FRIENDS ARE ALWAYS
GREAT!
