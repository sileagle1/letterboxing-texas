From: Lois (lois_adams37@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-18 01:02:25 UTC
Another disaster. Well, not really. I hid my Little Bunny FuFu
Yesterday (Mon 4/16) and went home and typed up the clues. Put them
on the net. Today my granddaughter came over and asked if she could
have the clues to the box as she wanted to find it. I gave them to
her. She looked them over and said, "Grandma, where does 331 and 198
cross?" I said, "At the light in Malakoff." But I thought that was
hwy 31 and 198. 1 mistake to be corrected. Then she said, " I thought
the school was north of that light." Gee, will I ever get my
directions right! Well with her help, I rewrote the clues. I believe
they are correct now. Hope no one was trying to find the box today as
they might have wound up in Ten Buck Two with no box. Sorry. It is
corrected now. Adams Trails
