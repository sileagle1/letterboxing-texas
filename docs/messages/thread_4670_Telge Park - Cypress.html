From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-11-18 22:05:09 UTC
We were in the park Tuesday. The only place we saw any work was in the corner formed by Telge Road and Cypress Creek. There's a couple of green benches there with a marker of some kind (Pastry Princess had a box there, but it's missing, I believe). It appears that they are clearing out a trail from the park to that spot. The stump where IRA Boxer's letterbox "NOW THAT'S SCARY!" was placed is completely gone, as is the box. The 3 bricks are still lying there. We couldn't check all the boxes, so hope you can make time to do so. Thanks.
