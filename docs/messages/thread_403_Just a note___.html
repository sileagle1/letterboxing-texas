Re: [letterboxingtexas] Re: Just a note... Hey Samuraistampers: Kantexan, Pikachu Ponderer, and Cupcake Kid,

Just wanted to let you know I stamped “Haunted by Waters,” “Green Oaks,” and “Donald” today and all were in good shape.  Did want to warn you that I noticed there is something near “Donald” that will tend to push away anything placed around the edges of the rock as covering (don’t want to say what, so I don’t give away the location.)  The camouflage tape was an excellent idea, by the way.  You may want to keep an eye on it, or try to get it to sit just a little deeper, or further under #78.  I think I was also just about to get “Kelley Park” but didn’t have a compass with me... if I was more sure of myself I would have braved the thorns, ragweed and spiderwebs, but since I wasn’t sure, I decided to wait.  (Also decided to be wearing jeans instead of shorts!)

I wanted to compliment you on finding some excellent hiding places!  

I’m working on some ideas for some boxes but I plan on finding a few more to find out how different people put their boxes together.
