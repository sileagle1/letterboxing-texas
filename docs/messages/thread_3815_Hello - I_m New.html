Hello - I'm New

From: Elizabeth Waltz (liz.waltz@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-10-23 19:53:06 UTC
Hi everyone,

I am new to the area. I am up in Ardmore, OK, but plan to be down to
Texas for boxing quite often. I've been a letterboxer for about 6
years now. My trail name is Penguin Patrol. I have met a few of you
over the years so it is nice to see some familiar faces on the list.
Others I can't wait to meet. I'll be at the Plano event this weekend
and am looking forward to it.

I'm not a real chatty type and mostly I lurk in these large groups. If
anyone has any advice for me about boxing in Texas, I'd love to hear.
Things like watch out for rattle snakes and hey - that's a scorpion
are probably things I need to hear. I've lived in 7 states now, but
this is the farthest west I've been. New terrain for me. Also good to
know are things like don't go near so-and-so park. Or here's a WOM for
you.

Anyway, I'm rambling now. Glad to be in an area with active boxers and
I look forward to meeting y'all in person one day and seeking your
boxes. Maybe I'll even plant a few during one of my trips.

Liz
(Penguin Patrol)
