ALERT! Great News......

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2009-01-15 05:36:27 UTC-07:00

James woke us at midnight.

He was calling from Kuwait. He is on the way back to Ft. Hood. This ends his
last combat tour as a Scout/Sniper/Paramedic in K-Troop, 3d Squadron, 3d Armored
Cavalry Regiment. The Brave Rifles. 4 years of combat - he did his time.

James will be at Ft. Hood until he finishes this enlistment. The plan is for
him to get out of the Army somewhere from July to August. He will be taking a
job as an apprentice electrician in El Paso, and going to school at night.
Someone made him an offer he couldn't refuse. They wanted his leadership
skills, they said they would teach him the electrical skills. A great
opportunity.

Armadillo/Cactus folks may hear KC0WKY on the system calling us in El Paso when
he arrives. Please welcome this fine young trooper home.
