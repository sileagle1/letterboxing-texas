I'm more into "the place" instead of "the box", so I recommend Kleb
Woods in northwest Harris county, Mercer Arboretum near Spring just
north of Houston, and Memorial Park right in the heart of Houston.
On your way to Houston you can detour through Brenham/Navasota area
for beautiful wildflowers and many boxes, especially in Washington-On-
The-Brazos State Park. All of these parks are nice, free and full of
boxes. On LBNA you can search Texas boxes with the park name in the
clues to find many of them. Have fun!

Silver Eagle
