dino valley birthday

From: studiousmom (studiousmom@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-03-13 11:33:18 UTC-06:00

I have to tell y’all about my letterboxing day yesterday.  It was my birthday and I had a star party to do at Dinosaur Valley State Park in Glen Rose.   I went way early so I could do some boxing and hiking.   My 5 year old son almost drowned in the Pauluxy River, his shoe went floating downstream.  We found out my dog can’t swim and we had to carry her across the river.  My car battery went dead.  I hit a curb and lost a hubcap.  And finally, on the way home, I had a blowout on the highway.  I called our insurance company and the rep said, “Happy Birthday, you don’t have roadside assistance.”  So I had to change a tire for the first time in my life.  I broke a nail and the van fell off the jack.   In spite of everything, the weather was beautiful and I found two great boxes.  So in retrospect, it was a good day!

 

Stargazer

Well, happy Belated Birthday, Stargazer – and I’m so sorry it did not turn out perfectly for you! Looks as though you & Hawk share a birthdate, so you are in good company!  Next year we’ll have to make sure your day is a very happy one!
