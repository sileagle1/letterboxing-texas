Well, some would say that I struck out today while letterboxing on my "lunch hour". First I checked out "360 Dog's Eye View" by the Loop 360 bridge over the Colorado but that was missing as Dawn's Glory had reported recently. Next I headed over to the Wild Basin Nature Preserve to look for "Five". I hiked the long way around, checking out every trail in the park just because it was such a nice day and then I got to the box location and it was missing in action also. Zero for two tries today but I had a great time. This is the first time I have had both the time and (somewhat) cooler weather at lunch for letterboxing in many months.
