I want to take this opportunity to publically thank
Mike for his efforts in the El Paso area. At my
suggestion, he is trying to find three of the oldest
boxes in TX, and if found, make sure they stay safe.
Unfortunately, it looks like one of them is already
missing, but at least we know its status. The other
two in Hueco Tanks SP will be tougher to find since they
may be in areas no longer accessible to the public
without a guide. Thanks to Mike's association with
the rangers there he may be able to salvage them for
all of us to enjoy in the future. I hate delegating
all this responsiblity to somebody else, especially
a new letterboxer, but Mike is the only letterboxer that
lives in the area and he has responded in true TX Letterboxer
manner! Once I explained how special these boxes were he
volunteered to do his best. Mike, I appreciate your
efforts and I'm looking forward to hearing more about your
future letterboxing adventures in West TX.
