4 new letterboxes in Keller

From: tx2chic (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-03-28 22:51:43 UTC

I've just planted our first letterboxes - 4 of them so far and at least
a couple more to come, along Big Bear Creek Greenbelt in Keller. They
are: Creek Crossing, Wildflower Meadow, Autumn Gift, and Pecan-
Gatherer (In the Creek and Old Native are still being scouted for
better locations). Now to get the clues posted. These are our first
letterboxes and my first attempts at carving stamps, and they aren't
too bad for a beginner. The walking trail is lovely: a creek, native
trees, redbuds and wildflower meadows (not quite in bloom yet, but will
be within a month), squirrels, ducks, and turtles and a small herd of
longhorns just across the fence from the wildflower meadows.
-- Benny (Viewfinder of tx2chic)
