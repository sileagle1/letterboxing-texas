Circle Tour 2008 is over

Hi Gang!
JBKokopelli and I just got back from our second annual spring break Circle Tour.  She picked me up in DFW last Saturday and we set out for the hill country.  We were on the go constantly, had a blast and saw some great stuff, but are exhausted and ready to be in our respective homes and sleep in our respective beds with our respective four-legged kids.  JBK dropped me off in DFW a little while ago and is on the road headed back to ETX now.  More details to follow after we each recuperate a little, but this will have to suffice for now:

7 Days
2,150 Miles Driven
16 Boxes Planted
106 Boxed Found

I've missed you guys!
Wag Time
PS - We found a wonderful long walking stick at a box site in Georgetown.  We think we know who it belongs to, but we're holding it for ransom because it's really nice!   :-)

PPS - Cross Mountain has an elevation of 1,923 feet and the trail is...long…all uphill...on rock.

PPSS - Today is JBKokopelli's Birthday!!!!!
