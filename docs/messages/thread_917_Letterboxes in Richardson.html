I've been waiting for the chance to go back to Richardson near all
those Pink Flamingos and look for that Harper Valley PTA letterbox and
the Dallas Cowboys letterbox that the Celtic Lions had placed or
adopted for Sprite and Highlander and Wanda and Pete, and this morning
was my first chance! I found the parks easily (nice parks), but there

placement was pretty clever! Thanks, Celtic Lions, for taking care of
these two. Either this fall when the weeds are down, or whenever I'm
covered head to toe in bug spray or clothing, I'll try again for the
Harper Valley LB.
