Heather,
An "Event Box" is a box that is found only at the event, except that some event boxes travel around to different events (I have a Polar Bear event box for cold weather gatherings). There are also boxes that are planted FOR the event and they may or may not stay after the event is over - it sounds like these are the ones PN refers to in her event description. PurpleNic could tell you what her intent is. Sometimes, if you talk really nice to the organizer and it's not a difficulty for them to return later to retrieve the boxes, they will be happy to leave the boxes out for a few days if you tell them you want to find them but can't make the event. Sometimes the boxes are specifically intended for the few hours of the event though, and once they're gone - they're gone.

Lucy
