The LBNA web site at: http://www.letterboxing.org/
has clues to most boxes in North America. Just click
on Clues, then click on the state you want to look at.
In the case of ZZ Top & Black Beans, click on Texas, then
the North Prairie Region and you will see them. You can
also search for boxes by clicking Search For Boxes on the
main page, then pick the state and/or other search criteria,
then click Search Boxes and it will bring up a list.
Since you live in Pasadena, you might want to click on the
Coastal Region of Texas to find some near you. Hope this
helps.
