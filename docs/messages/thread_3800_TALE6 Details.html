TALE6 Details

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-10-04 15:44:57 UTC
Since Mission Tejas SP has several examples of our rich and diverse
history, Baby Bear and I decided to make the theme for the Event be
TX Heritage. This can include people, places and objects that make
up the history, culture and lore of our great state. I have created
the Event website with more details:
http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/TALE06/

I set up the Event on AQ where you can sign-up and specify the
potluck items you want to bring:
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=985

I also created a table in the Database of this Talklist called TALE06
Potluck where you can specify potluck items:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterboxingtexas/database?
method=reportRows&tbl=8

And finally, I managed to reserve the Group Camping Area at Mission
Tejas SP. It holds up to 32 people but is for tent camping only,
with no electricity and just 3 tables. If you want to camp there,
email me off-list and I will let you know if there is still
availability. Even if you don't camp there it will be a great place
to gather for the post-Event campfire Saturday night.

If anyone wants to have any other activities (e.g. demos) at the
Event, mention it here and we can discuss. Looking forward to
another great Event and seeing y'all again!

Silver Eagle

There are two things I would suggest as activities, although they aren't letterboxing activities: 
 
There is a behind the fence hike there that is very interesting and if there is enough interest we should probably get it lined up with the rangers.  One of the things it includes is a glimpse of El Camino Real as it was over 100 years ago.
 
The other thing - if anyone is inclined to take a walk on the dark side literally and figuratively - is a night geocache that is near to Mission Tejas (less than 10 minutes away).  The one time I've gone after it was a rainy night and there was too much reflection to find the markers.
