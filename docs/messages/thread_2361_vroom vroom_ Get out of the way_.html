From: queenbeelovedx3 (queenbeelovedx3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-06 01:47:09 UTC
I just dodged a crazy bus at South Lakes Park in Denton! This driver
was nuts! Jumped the curb at the basketball courts -Some poor lady dove
into a bathroom stall from fear! You should have SEEN her face! It took
out the nets at the tennis courts...just kept on going with 'em stuck
on the windshield! (I think the driver turned on the wipers -to no
avail!) At any rate... I saw him swerve left at the first nature trail,
skidding on loose gravel... almost took out the second nature trail
sign with the map! It banked a sharp right after side swiping the green
post on a bearing of 320 degrees, ricocheted off the evergreen and
landed with a screetch behind a short tree bearing 30 degrees. Out the
rear window I saw Janis flash the peace sign and smile cheerfully, so
I'm sure all is well. A little shaken but not stirred! They're gonna
have a frightful time pulling that poor bus out of the thorns around
there! I'm glad no one was hurt. -Didn't get his plates but I did
notice they were from Houston... boy, I've heard about those Houston
drivers... but I NEVER would've believed it if it weren't for this! ;-)

-Queen Beeloved
*who drives like a granny!*
