Northeast Texas Planting Day

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-04-18 10:45:56 UTC-05:00
We've talked a couple of times about having a planting day in Northeast Texas.  Well let's quit talking and DO IT!
 
This is open for discussion, but here's how I see the format:  Let's set a broad area to plant boxes in (Say, East of Dallas and North of I-20) and set the day to go plant a few and find a few, then meet up in a central location to sit down and eat together.  Maybe somewhere in Pittsburg?  I think it might be a good idea for folks to RSVP with where they are thinking to place boxes, which of course doesn't mean you can't plant wherever you want to, it just might help some folks decide where they will plant.
 
Here's the date that Blue Butterfly and JB Kokopelli and I like - May 12th.  How does that sound to other folks?
 
We might also include our Northwest Louisiana friends in this, since there is now a very small number of them and they would probably like to see more boxes in that area, or may want to do a similar initiative to spread toward our border.
 
Once we have a few details sort of ironed out, I'll post it to AQ (unless someone else wants to do the honors).
So whatdya say?  Y'all with me?
--
Barefoot Lucy
"It's not about footwear, it's about philosophy"
