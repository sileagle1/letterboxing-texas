Village Creek State Park

From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-06 00:46:54 UTC
I was out and about today and visited Village Creek SP in Lumberton.
This park has recently re-opened after Katrina, but only one main
trail and one short trail is open for hikes.

I was lucky enough for find SQUIRE SQUIRREL in good shape. The box
was in it's place, although it was exposed. I also found PEPE LE
TEXAN. The landscape has changed a bit due to the storm and
subsequent clean up, but I found the box laying a few feet away from
the appointed place. I wasn't able to look for DEER FRIEND, as that
part of the park is still not open to visitors.

Happy Trails!
