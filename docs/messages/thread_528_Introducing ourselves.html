Introducing ourselves

From: laura dew (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-01-03 09:02:51 UTC-06:00
Hello,

We've recently joined the list, so thought we'd introduce ourselves
and our family. The parents (Dewberry, Asterix) and boys (Whoopster,
age 7 and Gramapillar, age 3) have had a fun time looking for clues on
our Glitterboxing adventures, as Gramapillar calls it.

We've recently placed our first box, rabbit house, under the moniker
Gramatrick (a combination of the boys' names). Look for others in the
greater Houston area soon from us, including Pumpkin Pi and Mouse
House. They won't all be math puzzles, it just happens that the first
three are (Whoopster is very much into Math).

We started letterboxing last spring, and had a great trip to Colorado
over the summer letterboxing all the way there and back. Had to take
a break due to some major commitments this fall that took up way too
much of our time. We were amazed when we returned to the LBNA website
to find all these boxes planted all over Houston. The boys have had a
ball (and really, so have the adults). Thanks to everyone!

We'll look forward to meeting more of you at Brazos Bend, but we
certainly have enjoyed running into other boxers on the trails and the
email exchanges we've had.

--Dewberry
