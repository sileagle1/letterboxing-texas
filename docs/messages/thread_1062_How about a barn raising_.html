How about a barn raising?

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-15 21:02:54 UTC-07:00
Some of the ideas that have been going around have
made me wonder who might participate in a box planting
along the same concept as a "barn raising"?

West Texas would be my ambitious goal, but there are
problems with that. Still, there are several counties
in the Eastern half of the state with no boxes.
Wouldn't it be fun to all get together on one day and
plant a bunch of boxes in that type of an area? Folks
who couldn't attend could still send boxes. We could
plant in the morning then have potluck for lunch and
go out and find each other's boxes in the afternoon?

Then leave all those boxes for the time when someone
comes along who is interested in LB'ing, so they would
have something for us to do, kind of like Silver Eagle
and Baby Bear did for quite a few of us.

I think we would all have to agree to do whatever
maintenance we could on them as we cross through that
area, given that they are out of our normal range or
there would already be boxes there.

Anyway, this list has several projects going right
now, but I thought that was another one to throw out
as an idea if someone is really interested. I will
likely shoot toward hosting one of these myself in
October/November, possibly in the
Johnson-Ellis-Navarro counties area and if there is
any interest.

Lucy
