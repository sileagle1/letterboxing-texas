From: Amanda Nuernberg (asnuernberg@gmail.com) | Date: 2013-03-24 11:48:58 UTC-05:00
I am so excited to be a part of Letterboxing, especially Letterboxing in TX. I live in Bryan, TX and got introduced into Letterboxing through Girl Scouts. I went to a Leader retreat and there was another Leader there that letterboxed. We attended a workshop together and that's how I met her!

After I got home, I investigated what it was. I've always enjoyed scavenger hunts and treasure hunts......so, letterboxing fit in with that for me! I decided to base my trail name on my last name. It's a play on my last name, animals I collect, and the year I got married. I introduced it to my Girl Scout troop and my family and it was a hit with them also. We've enjoyed small trips in our community with our troop; and I've enjoyed trips with my family to state parks, other communities, and to cemeteries!

I'm happy to be a part of this community and excited to find more letterboxes in TX!

-Nerdbird '09
