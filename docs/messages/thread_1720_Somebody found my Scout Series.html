Somebody found my Scout Series

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-03 06:13:18 UTC-08:00
The coolest thing happened last night! We had our
monthly district-wide leader training meeting and one
of the topics of conversation is getting the boys
outside and doing outdoor requirements and so forth.
As we were closing the topics, one lady mentioned an
event coming up at Texas State Railroad (TSR) in June
that will feature geocaching. I started suggesting
that she go take her Webelos to find my scout series,
and she told me she had already been to them with her
grandkils. I had no idea anyone in the local scouting
community had shown an interest yet! I had
distributed clues on our talk list, but no one ever
said a thing about them.

She went on to tell me that she told the grandkids
they were going to hunt leprechauns and she proved to
them the existence of leprechauns by showing them the
"leprechaun mailboxes". I can only imagine...

Anyway, she got interested and went and found one of
them at TSR, but was too tired after the 2.5 mile hike
at Jim Hogg to look for any but the closest...and she
said she didn't sign in because she didn't have a
stamp.

I was just so stoked when I left the meeting, and I
invited her to the gathering - who knows, maybe she'll
show up.

Lucy
