Yet Another Brazos Box

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-03-14 02:46:05 UTC

After a bad month that included a car accident (with accompanying
month-long fight with the insurance company), four cases of the flu,
one blown-out knee with impending surgery, and assorted other
craziness, we decided that we needed to turn our luck around. So,
we created the Good Luck box, which we hid today at Brazos Bend. It
is fitting that it go there, as once long ago when we were dating, I
found two four-leaf clovers there. To make it fit the theme, we hid
this box on the Horseshoe Lake Trail.

As luck would have it, I never really loved any of the stamps that I
carved. So, although we hid one, there are several other versions
carved. So, just for this event, they will be Asterix' and my
personal travelers (I'm not sure what you call that). Asterix will
be easy to recognize, if he's feeling well enough to come, as he
will be the one on crutches. If not, I'll have the assorted good
luck stamps with me. To get our travelers, just tell us why you
deserve some good luck.

The boys will hopefully each have their own travelers. You can
probably just ask them for theirs--plans are for them to each have a
stamp of something they really like.

We had a great day. I am sorry we just missed Droop. I can confirm
that the park was absolutely crawling with people (and gators), but
with gorgeous weather like this, people wanted to be OUTSIDE.

Dewberry
