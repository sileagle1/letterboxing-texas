From: StarSaels (steves_1701@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-02 04:52:15 UTC
While hunting for the Mother of Texas box yesterday, I was forced to
evacuate the location rather quickly due to a groundskeeper who wanted
to go home 10 minutes early. When I found the hiding spot (I think),
there was a totally unhidden box sitting in there. I grabbed it and ran
back to the car so I wouldn't get trapped in the cemetery, knowing that
I would see SilverEagle today...

When I got to the next stop, I went to stamp in and discovered that
what I had found was not what I was expecting. I have a Ziploc
container that has in it a green ink pad and a store-bought stamp.
Perhaps the stamp has something to do with the name written in faded
Sharpie on the lid: Garcia Middle School.

Does anyone know about this "mystery" box? Silver Eagle's still
scratching his head... ;-) I still have it in my possession...

StarSaels
