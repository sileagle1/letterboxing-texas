I have an AQ question that you might be able to answer, and which might have some relevance to others here.
 
I pulled a search of boxes within 25 miles of Denton.  I've identified the ones I really want to find.  I see the little check boxes next to the box names and I click the ones I chose.  Now I clicked the "Add tags" button and my selections have a star beside them.  What the heck do  those little stars do for me?

But I still have a question - what does the tag do for me?

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-08-15 01:52:08 UTC
Tagging was very helpful to me when I was planning our last roadtrip.

Say you have identified about 15 boxes (that's just a random number,
it can be anything) you want to find. You can do a search just for
your tagged boxes and then they'll pop up. From there, you can click
those boxes and print whichever you want to print.
