Texas Gatherings, Past and Upcoming

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-13 07:00:38 UTC-07:00
Silver Eagle has hosted the past two gatherings in
March, and I believe he is planning to host another
one in the Spring. These have turned out to be full
gatherings for everyone from all over (including New
England-with Wanda of "Wanda and Pete" distinction
attending the last gathering).

This fall, there is at least one mini-gathering
planned. Possibly others will begin popping up as the
weather begins to cool off!

The next one that I know of is the JEN Barnraising
event, which I'm hosting. This event is intended to
be an intensive planting effort to get three counties
populated with some boxes for future boxers as well as
those of us who want to take a little road trip. The
event will be held in Waxahachie on October 23rd and
the counties to be seeded are Johnson, Ellis, and
Navarro (JEN). Here's a link for it:

http://www.geocities.com/barefootlucy/JENBarnraising

All are invited and even those who aren't able to
attend can participate by sending boxes up for the
rest of us to plant on their behalf.

ALSO, when I get back on the computer later today (I'm
heading out in just a sec to a Raingutter Regatta, and
on from there with hopes of meeting up with a couple
of fellow boxers!), I'm going to tell y'all about an
idea Ruby Tuesday and I have been talking about! Stay
tuned...

Lucy
