Some of you (Mr. Silver Eagle) know I am supposed to be researching some
lost or abandoned letterboxes at Hueco Tanks.

I FINALLY got it set up with the head ranger. I am to be out there Sunday
around 10 or 11 am to search for the two boxes. IF they exist, we will see
what needs to be done. As long as they are not in a restricted area, they
can remain. Under the park rules someone will need to adopt them and take
responsibility for their care. I guess that would be me. If they are in a
restricted area, they can be moved or removed. I guess I will decide what
to do once I see what we have to work with.

I will make every attempt to keep these boxes alive if possible since they
are some of the oldest ones in Texas.

I will report on what I learn upon my return.
