From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-07-10 16:18:15 UTC
We're planning a little weekend road trip to Fort Worth to see the
Star Wars exhibit, and I thought we'd come back through Waco. We
wanted to stop by Lake Whitney SP as that's one we've never been to.

I read on the TPWD website this morning that both Lake Whitney and
Mother Neff are closed due to flooding.

Does anyone else know of additional closures? The flooding has been
awe-inspiring.

I got a note from Boots Tex that my Georgie & The Dragon boxes were
washed from the Montgomery County Nature Preserve. I want to say
thank you to him and LSQ for taking the time to check on those boxes.
You're the best!

dewberry


Re: parks closures & flooding

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-07-11 01:10:57 UTC
We went to Hamilton to visit my sister and checked on "Brushy Bill
aka Billy the Kid" and "Crazy Nine Patch" and there are both doing
well. Also, we went through Waco yesterday and planted 3 new Texas
Governors boxes in Oakwood Cemetery. Look for the clues to be
posted soon. I had planned on putting 2 there and one in Mother Neff
SP (that would be former governor Pat Neff), but found a good spot
in the cemetery, so that's where he ended up. Mother Neff is
closed. All the rivers and creeks that we saw were out of their
banks.
Thanks for the compliment, Dewberry, I'm sorry I had to report the
bad news about Georgie and the Dragon.

Boots Tex
