Letterboxes in Jacksonville

From: Lois (lois_adams37@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-27 03:37:54 UTC
Wow, there are alot of letterboxes in Jacksonville. We went today and
Barefoot Lucy was just a few steps in front of us all the way. I
really thought we might beat her or GG finding the June series first
but no such luck. We could spend a week or two gathering all the boxes
in Jacksonville. Thought about finding a motel and staying at least 2
more days but it is hard on old people to letterbox more than one day
at a time. I will be so sore tomorrow from finding the Love's lookout
boxes I won't be able to get out of bed. I might as well be my very
own bed so we drove home. See you on the trails. Adams Trails


Re: [letterboxingtexas] Letterboxes in Jacksonville

From: Carolyn Ramirez (danielcgarcia@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-05-27 08:08:53 UTC-07:00
You are not kidding about there being so many boxes in that area! My loving family, Frogger 99 and PomPom Bear decided that they would take me out there for a belated Mother's Day gift and we went on the 16th & 17th and spent the night and boxed all the way down there and I do believe we could have stayed at least another week and still not found all of them! We did manage to find about 20 along the way but it was such a nice trip and I really enjoyed Love's Lookout. So much so that I planted a box there! Hope you found it. BTW, BL does that qualify me for the event stamp for the planting day?
Monarch B
