LB Capital Of South (or maybe U.S.)

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-12-04 20:36:13 UTC
On the Big List, someone asked what was the LB Capital of the U.S., and
the leading candidates were Manchester, CN (118 letterboxes) and
Portland, OR (124 letterboxes). I did some searches and found
something quite surprising: Houston appears to be the city with the
most letterboxes with 183! And that does not count Mystery boxes and
ones using suburbs as city names. Also, if you include all the cities
comprising the Dallas Metroplex, it probably has the most anywhere. I
find this amazing considering just a few years ago the entire state did
not have this many boxes. TX Letterboxers, you should feel proud.
