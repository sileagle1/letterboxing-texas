Trip Summary

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-02-02 16:40:55 UTC-08:00

I'm back from my letterboxing trip and finally posted all my clues.  It was great getting to see the famous trees in person after reading about them and their history.  I also had some unexpected joy while in Panna Maria placing my box in the cemetery there.  As I entered, it looked familiar and I suspected I had been there before for a letterbox.  I didn't have the clues but since there weren't that many good places to hide a box, I went to the obvious tree and there it was.  When I looked at the logbook I saw that it was placed by Corazon in 2004 and that I had visited in 2005.  Even more amazing, my daughter Running Rain had been with me and stamped in as well, and she hasn't letterboxed in many years!  It is not very often you get to see an original logbook from that far back, and it brought back great memories of me and my kids letterboxing in the old days.

I have now placed 27 Famous Tree boxes and to help you know where they all are I have created a web page with all of them together with links to their clues:

http://sileagle.webs.com/famoustreesoftx.htm

Also, on each clue page there is a link back to this page.  This isn't a shameless plug for my letterboxes, but I do urge you to go see these trees.

Silver Eagle
