From: Puddle Splasher (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-01-11 02:52:41 UTC
I will be leaving the "Jingle Bells" box out until February 1st, at
which time it will be replaced with a St. Patrick's Day box. Today, I
also placed a Valentine Box, "Be My Valentine", in the same park, Arbor
Hills Nature Center in Plano on Parker Blvd. (just off the tollway
north of Dallas). By the way, "Jingle Bells" was recently reported
as "attempted" however I found it safe and sound right where it should
be.

While hiding the new box today, I checked on another box I have hidden
there, "Welcome to the World Baby Girls". The trail where it was
located, had recently been closed and they had filled the trail with
logs and branches to keep people from using it. Obviously, I had to
work my way through the branches to retrieve my box, but I was able to
hide it on another trail very close to its original location. So if you
already have those clues printed out, you will definitely want to print
out the newly edited clues.

I took the opportunity to hike around many of the trails in the park
today, and it is amazing how pretty and extensive those trails are.
Hopefully others will feel compelled to plant some boxes in that park.
It's just a really nice place to walk and enjoy nature... right in an
urban location!
