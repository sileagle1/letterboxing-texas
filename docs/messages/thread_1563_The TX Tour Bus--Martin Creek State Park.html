The TX Tour Bus--Martin Creek State Park

From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-01 23:42:40 UTC
The TX Tour bus has made a pit stop at Martin Creek Lake State park
in Tatum, TX. The bus driver has picked up two new passengers, but
there's still plenty of empty seats left for more!

After paying your entry fee and picking up a map, make your way to
the Bee Tree camping area and park by the rest rooms. Walk down the
road (clockwise from where you entered the area) to campsite 57.

There is a little trail behind Campsite 57, so enter this trail.
You will see a small stump (6-8 inches high) on your right. Take
the trail to the left.

After about 50 steps, you'll see a sign on your right that says MILE
8 and has a picture of a bike on it. Turn right and stand with the
sign to your left. You will be facing the water.

Take 41 steps down the trail. If you go past a large pine tree on
the edge of the trail (on your right), you've gone too far.

Turn to your right and you will see 2 sets of logs making a T.
(Even farther behind that, you will see a fallen tree lodged into a
triple-trunk tree about 7 feet off the ground).
