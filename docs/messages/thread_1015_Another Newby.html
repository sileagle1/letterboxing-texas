Welcome, welcome!
Whereabouts in Texas are you? We're in the DFW area, and if you need help or

It must have been discouraging to not find that box, but I'm glad you aren't
giving up. If I'm sure I'm going to be hunting specific boxes, I'll
occasionally write to the owner to check on the status. (In fact, your note
reminded me that I need to check on one of our boxes which was reported
missing lately - but I always like to go ascertain that they are missing
myself, before I post it)

actually can't find out of the ones we search for - can't come up with a
good number - but it certainly does happen!

victoriously snatched from under a bush in front of a restaurant, thinking
for sure he'd found a very elusive box...!
