From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-07-06 23:56:07 UTC
I'm back from vaction to the Hill Country and I did manage
to do a little letterboxing in between wine tasting, dude
ranching and setting off fireworks. I ended up finding 18
boxes and placing 4 new ones, and also replaced my Stevie
Ray microbox. The only 2 boxes I did not get that I tried
for was Baby Bear's Park Ranger in Kerrville SP (road was
closed for construction), and Corazon's Bear With Me in New
Berlin (not smart enough to figure it out). I found 2 HH
in 2 different boxes, but I had already found both before
so I left them for somebody else to find. It was a fun
trip and I envy those of you who live in that area of Texas.
Thanks to all who planted boxes to make my trip even better!
