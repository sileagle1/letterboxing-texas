Wow, who knew a simple post about a football game would generate so much emotion on non-football issues!  First of all, for all those that don't feel welcome in this Group, I'm sorry you feel that way.  Many of us have been here a long time and maybe we don't post as much as we used to or we respond in a callous way, but it doesn't mean we are that way in person.  Also, some people that post (e.g. LB Bozo, IRA Boxer) are known by us old-timers as harmless irritants that are just trying to get a rise out of somebody (usually Baby Bear).  So before you judge us, come to an Event and meet some of your peers.  I guarantee you will feel welcome, and that will carry over into this Group.

Second, I realize that many of you like to hear from Mike, even though it is usually on non-letterboxing topics, so I recommend you find out what other Groups he belongs to and join those to keep up with him.  Mike, I have enjoyed finding your boxes and I recommend you keep them out there, even if nobody adopts them right away, and good luck to you in your future endeavors.
