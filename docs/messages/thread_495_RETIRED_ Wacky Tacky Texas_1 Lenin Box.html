Stillwater & I drove over to Goff's Hamburgers last weekend to pull the
WACKY TACKY TEXAS #1 Box - Lenin. We had learned that the Hamburger joint
was going to be moved in January and we wanted to scoop the box before it
was found or trashed in the move.
Today CactusLicker told me that his history teacher announced that the
infamous Lenin statue at Goff's Hamburgers was sold on EBay recently! So it
looks as though this box is permanently retired...( what are the chances
we'll find another Lenin statue;-)???)
Thanks to all of you who found the box in very tricky surroundings, braved
the toxic puddles in the parking lot and left such great notes in the
logbook!
