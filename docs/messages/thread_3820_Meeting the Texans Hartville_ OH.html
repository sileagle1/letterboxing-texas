Meeting the Texans Hartville, OH

From: Sandra (grannybutterfly06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-10-26 13:02:04 UTC
Well Adams Trail led them to meet some OH boxers yesterday and you
can see the photos on AQ.

The restaurant is an Amish one and quite large but through traffic
all day long.

We stamped..had folks looking at us kind of funny...then ate, and
decided that there would be a room at the library where we could
carry on and get to know one another.

This little Meet N Greet...when I counted the feet and divided by 2
turned out to be 22 of us!! Whew..when you throw a party these OH
boxers come to welcome folks.

We enjoyed meeting and even had 2 other newbies who hadn't been to an
event before either! Young and old alike enjoyed the weather, the
food, the hiking and the laughing.

Be sure you check out the pictures on AQ. It will give you some
faces to put with the trailnames.
