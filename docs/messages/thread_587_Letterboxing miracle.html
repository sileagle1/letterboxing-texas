Letterboxing miracle

From: carrascoruth2000 (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-01-25 18:48:49 UTC

Well you all, a letterboxing miracle happened this afternoon. A
neighbor of mine from a couple streets back brought a package to my
door which had been mistakenly delivered to her house...IN NOVEMBER!
I opened it, and it was the Texas PLB from monkeytoes! Seems that
happy homeschoolers had mailed it in November, and somehow it wound
up at this woman's house. She had been in the hospital having hip
replacement surgery, and then at her sister's house to recover until
just last week. While she was gone one of her neighbors collected
her mail, and the PBL! Who knows how many unknowing hands the little
package went through. I am going to sit and go through the logbook,
stamp in, and I will send it on its way. So monkeytoes, if you were
wondering what had happened to your unbelieveable stamp, now you
know! It will be on its way soon! -Mother of four
