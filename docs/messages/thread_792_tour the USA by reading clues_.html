This just struck me as a funny, goofy thing to do today. I took an
armchair tour of the USA by looking at a handful of clues from very
disparate places. I looked at clues from NYC that talk about taking a
subway train to a station, watching for insanely dangerous traffic,
pretending to talk on a cellphone while dropping your hand behind a
street planter, and going during daylight to avoid crime in areas.
Then, for fun I looked at some Montana clues that talk about hiking
rock canyons over boulders where gold prospectors once searched!
Try it, it's kind of fun!
-Kantexan
