From: Abbie Beck (abbiembeck@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2008-05-05 05:22:33 UTC-07:00
Since we are discussing first-time plants, I wanted to chime in.  I DID IT!!  I guess the event on Saturday inspired me!!!  I hid my first box, "To Serve and Protect," in a small park in SA that was named in memory of a fallen SA Police officer.  Carving the stamp, thinking up ideas, plotting out the clues, putting the box together and hiding are all almost as fun as finding other people's boxes.  I am so excited to get my first AQ mail telling me that someone has found the box!!
 
On another note, our church women's group is having a project night on Fri.  I don't usually go b/c, with 5 children, most of my "projects" include the kitchen sink or the washer and dryer LOL!!  BUT, even with my very basic carving skills, some of the other ladies who are getting interested in lb'ing have asked me to come and give some tips!!!  I'm really excited about showing others how easy it is to get started and I am amazed at how much I could tell them already about tools and carving material...thanks to all you gurus!!!!!
