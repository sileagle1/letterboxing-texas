From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-08-02 15:36:21 UTC
I guess this time of year everyone is staying indoors trying to beat the heat. I just wanted to tell you about a new letterboxing couple in the Houston area. They're new to Texas as well. They inquired about some of our boxes around Coldspring and spent the weekend up here camping and finding letterboxes, despite the heat. We invited them over for an exchange and spent an enjoyable hour getting to know them a little bit. They call themselves FlyCraft, so if you get reports on finds from them, make them feel welcome to Texas and to letterboxing.

I got a report that two of my governor boxes at Oakwood Cemetery have been removed by tree trimming workers. They are "The Real Thing" and "The Lawman". I especially hate it because we had replaced them a few weeks ago after they had gone missing, so they were new boxes. I doubt that I will replace those two unless I can find a more appropriate place other than the cemetery. "Parks for Texas" is still there, at least for the time being.

Stay cool.

Boots Tex
