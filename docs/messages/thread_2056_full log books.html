From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-08-10 21:35:46 UTC-05:00

I pull the whole book and place a new one that is marked on the cover "2nd logbook" and the date I placed the box itself and the date I placed the logbook.  As far as what I do with them then, depending on the condition, I either put them in a baggie or I stack them in my already too stacked up study.  Every now and then I enjoy taking them down and perusing them.
