Re: [letterboxingtexas] Lbna question and help please

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-31 19:01:02 UTC-05:00
Hi Tara,
 
When you log in to your LBNA account, you will see a button across the top for Member Services.  Click that button, then go down to Your Letterboxes and click.  You will see your box there, and under the title of the box to the left, you will see an option to edit or archive.  Click edit.  Go in and make the changes, then save. 
 
Does that help?  If not, email me at barefootlucy@gmail.com and I'll help you off-list.  I'm one of the troubleshooters for LBNA...
 
Lucy
