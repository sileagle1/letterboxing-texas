New list members and who else is out there?

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-21 16:20:34 UTC-08:00
I've really enjoyed reading everyone's comments about
the gathering. I had a great time myself and was glad
for the opportunity to help everyone have a good time
in my little part of the world.

Although I knew my LSS was really pretty sick, I
suspected he wasn't too sorry to miss the gathering -
until he surprised me by saying more than once how he
really wished he could've been there. Slowly but
surely, he's getting hooked!

I just approved list membership for our new friends
from Alto (she-wolf requested membership, on behalf of
the family, I'm sure) - the folks that someone ran
into in the woods? I just can't imagine how I
would've felt if I had been out there trying out
letterboxing and some odd person figured me out and
sent me off to meet a bunch of other odd characters.
Those folks have guts! I'm convinced they'll be with
us for a long while...and that's yet another story to
add to our collection of Texas Letterboxing Stories.

I keep feeling there are several new folks who have
turned up on the letterboxing scene recently, but
we've had relatively few requests for membership. If
you think of someone who needs to sign up, either
point them toward the link at the bottom or send me or
Silver Eagle their email address and we'll invite
them. Although I believe you can certainly letterbox
without ever talking to another soul, belonging to the
lists helps make for good conscientious letterboxers
and it helps new boxers find their way around.

Well, I can't wait to find out where the next
gathering is going to be - I'll see y'all for sure
next year, but hopefully long before then!

Lucy
