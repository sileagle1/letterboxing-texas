If Fearless Fox is into fishing, they have a really nice fishing pier behind the lodge, as well as some nice boulders and points to fish from around the lake. They also have a golf course and a swimming beach (maybe too cool still for swimming in April). Last time I was there was in early October one year and the water was still warm for swimming. My only fear was that I was sharing the beach with hundreds of turtles and I was afraid one might mistake my toe for his dinner (didn't happen though). I grew up in Wichita Falls, so that was one of our favorite lakes to camp when I was a kid. (Not quite as busy and large as nearby Lake Texoma.) I've exchanged quite a few LTC's with Penguin Patrol, so I'm so anxious to finally meet her. Her carvings are great! It's nice that she's hosting this event.
