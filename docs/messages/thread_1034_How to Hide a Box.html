You've gotten several really good suggestions so far,
but one I've always wanted to try and which would
probably be good in an area with less plant growth
than we have here is to make your own rocks and hide
boxes inside them. Figure out what your local rock
formations look like and work to make rocks to match.

How do you do that? I have no idea, but it was
discussed on the big list about two years ago. I'll
ask on that list and we can see what they come up
with....watch for the answer there, or we'll post it
here after we get some suggestions there.

Lucy
