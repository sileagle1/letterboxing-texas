go to the web page I created:

http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/FTLE/

As far as my personal stamp, it is an eagle that I stamp
with silver ink. I started with a store bought stamp which
I thought was great, but as I started carving stamps and
placing them, I felt it was time to carve my own personal one.
I chose the name Silver Eagle because that is what my name
is for Indian Princesses, which my daughter and I participate in.
I love eagles and since I am showing my age, Silver Eagle
seemed appropriate. At least I'm not a bald eagle!
