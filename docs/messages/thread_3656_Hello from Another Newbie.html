Hello from Another Newbie

From: Frances Stinnett (francesstinnett@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-17 17:44:57 UTC-07:00

Hello Everyone,

 

My husband and I are new boxers who live in Commerce.  We're retired from the medical field and find that this activity interests us both and gets us out of the house.  Retirement is an adjustment and this really helps us do something together.  I registered us as "Jungle Jim and Fran", but want to change that to "Celtic Knotheads".  I'm not sure what will come up when I post!  We have found quite a few boxes by jb kokopelli and Puddle Splasher and they are all good.  We also did some boxing on the way to and from the Grandfather Mt. Highland Games in NC recently. 

 

I learned about this hobby from a friend in Maryland, but no one I've talked to around here seems to know anything about it and there are no boxes in Commerce.  We plan to plant some in the future.  Most in our area were placed by jb kokopelli and we'll be after several more of hers tomorrow.  We've learned to take insect repellant and sun block with us.  We're looking forward to attending a gathering sometime and learning more about this great hobby.

 

Fran

 

The Celtic Knotheads
