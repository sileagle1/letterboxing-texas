As I've mentioned before, until recently I have been
directionally challenged, but I've really started
enjoying map and compass stuff through scouting and
letterboxing. In fact, I was on a backwoods hike with
a bunch of scoutmaster type folks a few months ago and
even had the confidence to argue with them about
orienteering, and I turned out to be right! I did
learn recently (thanks Corazon and Navigator) that
it's really important to make sure your compass floats
properly though...

As I have been scouring online maps for this project,
I find myself fascinated by maps - street maps, road
maps, topo maps, lat/long - maps. I think it may be a
new obsession if I'm not careful. In fact, my Flying
Again box is based on a map of a future lake.

Thanks for the map site!
