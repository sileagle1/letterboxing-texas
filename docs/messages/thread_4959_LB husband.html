I got a chuckle out of this and hoped you would too. My husband is visiting his family in Florida this weekend. They went by a pier to take family photos this morning. He was hanging around waiting between shots and something caught his eye- a camoflauge-printed plastic storage container hidden under the pier. He investigated and realized it was a geocache. I guess four years of letterboxing with me has paid off! They all had a good laugh... I have made them go with me in multiple states to find boxes. They're well trained. He also said that after looking inside, they decided letterboxing is far superior to geocaching because of the art involved in the stamps. I was so proud!
