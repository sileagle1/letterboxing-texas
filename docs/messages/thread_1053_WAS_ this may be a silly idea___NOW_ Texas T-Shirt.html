Good morning, guys!

There are indeed Texas Letterboxing T-shirts!
We have made them in two colors so far: a dusty blue and a dusty green,
which the manufacturer describes as "desert-washed" or "pigment-dyed". The
Texas Letterboxing logo is on the upper left, and reads "Lone Star
Letterboxers".

Last time I ordered them, I think they worked out to about $9.00 each, and
we were able to avoid postage by delivering several at Brazos Bend. Shipping
them is about $4.00.

Sizes come in Youth and Adult (the Adult turned out to be MALE Adult - a
little large for us female-types). They are 100% pre-shrunk cotton.

Right now is NOT the best time for turning an order around quickly, since
the company I worked with has a ton of huge back-to-school orders to fill
and one little order for 20 shirts will not be top of their list!!!

But if anyone would like to start placing orders with me, I'll hold on to
them and put them in when we reach enough of a total. We have so many new
letterboxers that I bet we can get there fast!

Soooo - let me know if there is any interest!

Cheers,
Ruby Tuesday

PS I would need to double-check on whether prices have gone up - I remember
that last time we had to split an "ink disposal" fee - (I think it worked
out to about 50 cents each?)

PPS If the group would like to create a new & different T-shirt, I'm fine
with that!
