Hey East Texas LB's and other Texas Boxers in the Dallas metroplex area,

My name is Echo and I live near Longview, TX and for years I have wanted to have a meet-up of LB's in our area. I know it is a large area and we are spread out, so I would like to establish a place to meet that is central for most in our area. I do not have a specific date but am looking at Saturday April 13th , May 18th or May 25th. 

***Message me letting me know which date/s work best for you and any suggestions of where to meet.
Also if anyone wants to help with this please let me 

I know we have lots of state parks, city parks and wonderful restaurants we could meet at in several cities in East Texas. 

I am just putting this out there to see how many would be interested and where you live. This would help in choosing a place to meet so that everyone would not have to drive along way. Besides there are places in East Texas where I have not boxed yet or planted a box and would love to go visit.

***So if you are interested sign up with your name and the City send me message and in on the event page sign up under the car pool needed. Ex: Echo - White Oak, TX

**I will up date those who have signed up as soon as I have an established date and place. I would like to let people know a month out.

Hope to see you real soon!!
Echo
