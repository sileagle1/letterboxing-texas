ADMIN NOTICE-Yahoo Problems

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-05 19:59:18 UTC-07:00
I am on several yahoo lists, own three active ones,
and am on a couple of moderator lists. Although I
haven't seen any big problems here, except that Yahoo
is generally much slower than usual, I've been hearing
from moderators of other lists that weird stuff is
happening, like lists are disappearing or moderators
and owners are suddenly locked out. If it were just a
few mods or a few lists, I would think it was some
sort of operator error, but it is apparently a
documented problem that Yahoo is working to fix.

As of this evening, I'm backing up as much of our list
as I possibly can, although that probably only means I
can get a membership list and whatever files and links
we have on here. Probably no messages. If you have
messages you want and you don't already have them
downloaded, you might look at copying them to your
hard drive.

IN THE UNLIKELY EVENT something happens to this list,
I will set up a new list and invite everyone who is
currently a member here to join back up. With Yahoo's
daily limits on inviting and adding, it will take a
couple of days to get everyone back on. For your
reference, I am signed up on this list under two yahoo
ID's and Silver Eagle is signed up also, so the
likelihood that all three moderator ID's will get
locked out is virtually nil. I'm not sure if Silver
Eagle is on hand at the moment (he was on vacation
last week), but I still have the two ID's, so we still
shouldn't have a problem.

Anyway, I only tell you this so that IF something odd
happens and you get a bounced message or something, it
is probably a yahoo problem, not a list problem, and
I'm trying to minimize the effect of the problem.
Thankfully, this isn't a life or death problem, but it
might be a little inconvenient!

If you need to email me directly, please feel free to
email me at barefootlucy@yahoo.com or
mgb099@yahoo.com.

Here's hoping this message was a waste of time!
Lucy
