Trip to Caddo lake

From: bluebutterflytexas (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-13 13:46:43 UTC
Yesterday,PsGuru, Raoarin' Lion and I loaded up the van and headed
to Caddo lake and Uncertain. On our way, we made a few stops to get
some new boxes:

A Family Tree
Marshall Pottery
Skritz (Raorin'Lion thought this one was "cool")

After stamping in "Skritz", we made our way to Caddo Lake State
Park. Really nice park and the trails are neat and clean. Camping
spots are nice and they have cabings. This another one of the
projects by the "CCC". It is well maintained and they even give
guided tours.

Roarin' Lion and I hit the trails while PSGuru to photos, We took
our time and enjoyed all the info along the trails and found two
good hiding spots (I hope).

After making our way back to the van, we headed to Uncertain.
Uncertain is a unique little community that thrives on tourist and
week-enders. I had quite a problem finding a spot for this box.
There aren't any parks or other places that hiding a box would be
safe, BUT I was determined to place this box in the city limits of
Uncertain. I finally did find a place. Hopefully it will be there
for a while.

It was a fun day!
Blue butterfly
