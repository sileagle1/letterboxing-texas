Boxes temporarily removed...

From: Wanda (wsthm@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-23 23:37:09 UTC
Hi everyone! It's been so long, y'all probably don't remember me, but I
wanted to drop by and give an update on a few of my letterboxes.

In Evergreen Cemetery, near Skidmore, I have pulled three of my boxes.
The Bottino Family emailed me recently telling me that the books were
full and damaged. When I went to check on them, the boxes on two of
them were cracked too, so they will be replaced as well. 'Watchful
Birds' and 'Rest in Peace #1' will be returned very soon, but 'The Bee
Hive Hitchhiker Hostel' is missing 2 parts of the 3-part box stamp. So
I am going to have to do some recarving there. Apparently someone
mistook those two stamps (a bee and a hexagon(?) shape) for
hitchhikers! There are several nice hitchhikers in the hostel, but I am
taking good care of them until the whole thing is re-placed.

I did get a report that the Lost and Found Leaf outside of a cemetery
in Beeville is alive and well - except for needing a new book. I think
it had been reported as missing before. So, I will get back to that one
with a new booklet as soon as I can get around to it.

Happy boxing,
Busy Bee (Wanda)

Hiya Wanda!
 
I was wondering about you just the other day!  It's so good to see you peek in here...
 
For those who don't know, Wanda (Busy Bee) was one of the letterboxers at the first annual gathering in Bastop.  She was also the second member of this list!  I set this list up and then sat on it for a few weeks getting up the courage to put it out for everyone to join - you know that whole idea of throwing a party and having no one show up? - that was me.  In the meantime, Wanda was doing web searches on letterboxing and found the list on her own and joined.  She and I sat there together very quiet on the list, until she finally (very tactfully) said something to the effect of "Well?  Are you going to tell anybody about this list or not?"  Appreciating the kick in the rear end, I posted its existence on the Big List (because that was the only place for letterboxers to talk way back then) and lo and behold if a few other folks didn't show right up and we've grown steadily ever since - believe it or not, this list is 330 strong now...

Wanda, I'd love to know what you've been up to lately!
 
Lucy
