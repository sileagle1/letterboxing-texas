From: CelticLions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-09-19 17:18:42 UTC-05:00

Thanks to Kantexan and the Samuraistampers, we had a great time boxing
on Saturday! I am so grateful that there are energetic folks like you
guys out there increasing our pleasure!
We started with Green Oaks - another one of those neat little places we
never would have stumbled upon without letterboxing. Cute stamp! Good
job, Pikachu Ponderer!
We ended the day with a truly spectacular walk through River Legacy Park
to look for Haunted by Waters- I can't believe we're lived here 20 years
and never knew that park was there. I can't wait to tell our oldest
about the great biking trail - he's getting pretty tired of training
around White Rock - it'll make a lovely change of scenery for him!
StillWaters & Cactus Licker stumbled upon a really gorgeous long huge
copperhead on the hunt, and they are still talking about its beautiful
coloring. (I have to admit I was taking a time out sitting & bird
watching at "Stonehenge" when they found it).
But the most fun was tracking down Kantexan's mystery box & first Hitch
Hiker!!! Loved it - and Jean Lafitte has the coolest HH box I've ever
seen! I won't give anything away, because it's really good - but I think
I'll make sure we keep it on Texas for a while so we all can enjoy it!
