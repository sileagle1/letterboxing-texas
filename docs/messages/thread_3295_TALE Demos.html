Looks like there is some interest in having a carving demonstration at
the TX Annual LB Event, so Baby Bear has agreed to give one on his
technique. Since he uses an Exacto knife, it would be nice if someone
who uses Speedball tools or something else would also give a demo. Any
volunteers from the good carvers out there?

Also, it was mentioned that it would be nice to talk about what makes a
good hiding place for boxes. I think this is a good idea and will
think about how to approach it. We might just have an open discussion
about it if nothing else.

Thanks for the input.
Silver Eagle


Re: TALE Demos

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-01-16 17:24:31 UTC
I wouldn't mind giving a demonstration, but I also use an Exacto
knife, so it might be a duplication. However, carving, I think, is
sort of like drawing in that you can give two people a pencil and
the same subject and the final drawings could be completely
dissimilar. It seems that every carver develops a style that is
unique to them, so much so that, sometimes, you can see a stamp
image and guess who the carver is.

Boots Tex
