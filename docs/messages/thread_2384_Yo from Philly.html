Howdy Y'all (hope I typed that right!),
 
I'm a PA boxer, my dog Dude and I (and sometimes my husband) make up Hikers & Hounds. I am coming to Houston this weekend to visit my two sons who moved there recently. It's a quick trip, in on Friday and leaving Sunday morning. I am hoping to grab a box or two while I'm there, thanks to BabyBear for all of her help pointing me in the right direction(s)!
 
If anyone is interested in meeting up for an exchange, please email me off-list at hikers_n_hounds@yahoo.com. I am staying at the Holiday Inn Select on the SW Freeway, near the Galleria. Maybe for Saturday AM breakfast before you head out to run errands? OR maybe AT the Galleria while shopping, if you have to go there. This will be my first time in a mall in years. I avoid them like the plague. A nice exchange might be just the ticket to keep me from going nuts!
 
This weekend is about seeing my boys for a holdiay visit, but I'd love to try and squeeze a little boxing/exchanging in as well if possible.
 
Thanks and if any of you ever head up to Philly shoot me an email. I have been known to greet out of town boxers at the airport for an exchange!
 
Box Softly,
 
Hikers & Hounds
