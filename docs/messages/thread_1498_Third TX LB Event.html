Third TX LB Event

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-12-01 20:03:12 UTC
Well the polls are closed and it looks like we will
have our next Event on Saturday, 03/18/06 in the NE
Region. Since Rusk SP is the only place I considered in
that area, I propose we have it there, unless someone
has a better idea. I will try to reserve the pavilion
soon and then everyone can start reserving campsites.
We can discuss details when we get closer to the date.
Sorry to all that voted for other regions and/or dates,
but hopefully you can make it anyway. The good news is
that sooner or later we will have the Event in the other
Regions. Also, next year Daylight Savings will occur
earlier in March, so we will get more daylight without
moving the Event into April. Thanks for your participation.

Silver Eagle
