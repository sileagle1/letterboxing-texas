From: autumn.wind (ashby@ic.org) | Date: 2013-08-28 04:03:27 UTC
I often leave a 'first finder" gift when I place a new box. not always, but often. I think it's quite fun to do, just a little something extra. It's certainly not required, or even expected by anyone, so don't feel bad if you have not done it.

I recently had an email through LBNA from someone who had found one of my boxes and they were first finder and got the little necklace I had left. It was just a necklace made from a wine cork and put onto some help string, that's all, but it matched the stamp, so that's why I had made it. or, more accurately, the stamp matched the wine cork! anyway, it was fun to hear they got it and enjoyed it - they were the first finders after the box had been in place two full years, wow!

welcome to letterboxing!

I like your trail name a lot, too. Great!
