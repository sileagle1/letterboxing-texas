Arhhh Me Maties

From: Jenifer Barbee (jaydawnlove@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-08-16 10:26:32 UTC-07:00

I just got back from plundering the hills and finding treasures galore. I'm 10 for 11 boxes and planted one. My hubby told me not to over do it, (I've been super sick the past few weeks) and I defiantly did. 

A few boxes were visible so I stacked a few more small rocks on them and sprinkled some tree debris on top for cover. 

It was extremely humid for us being in a drought and no water being in the creek despite the rain we received last night.  

As any good pirate knows you must first "Weigh Anchor" before ye go plundering for treasure. But be warned, near by "Here there be Dragons." After slaying a dragon in me way I was at a cross road on me map for treasure. Starboard or port? Port it was first. I ran into some "Pirate Kids" along the way and quickly moved on as not to be seen by other pirates looking to plunder these lands. I yelled "Ahoy" at a wonderful surprise find while searching for the treasure. "Aye" Matey it was finally found further along the trail. Time to cross the dry bed and go finding a "PIrate Joe" who could find any treasure I hear. He was no help to me though, wanting to stay guarding his own treasure, so I quickly went aft to the next treasure to be found. I heard that the "Queen of the Irish Seas" had hidden some booty and I was after that. She told me of "The Royal Fortune" just down the trail and I found a cave to plunder! Turning around having missed an octopus I was greeted nicely by a pretty maiden "Hello Sailor" she said. Finishing up I went back for a "Computer Pirate" But was very confused by the cactus and the crevice. There was a small cacti but no crevice, then a large cacti and a crevice filled with rocks but no treasure. Did I go to far? Not far enough? To high up the ledge? Who knows but it wasn't my day to find the computer pirate treasure. I decided to make the pirate who told me of this unfound treasure to "Walk the Plank" and I sailed away happily. 

It was very fun but humid. Very few muggles were out and I only ran across two the whole 3 hours I was plundering treasures. I think it was Tale 10 the last time I went boxing. I'm exhausted but that is part of me being sick... I can't wait to see everyone in October. :)

~Dawns Glory~
