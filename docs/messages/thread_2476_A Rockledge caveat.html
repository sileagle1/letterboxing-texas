A Rockledge caveat

From: Nick Bottom (gryzzgryph@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-05 21:00:15 UTC-08:00
Today I discovered that the trail at Rockledge Park on Lake Grapevine has recently been altered in that a new, presumptively replacement, trail has been built running roughly parallel to the right of the original trail. This means that unless you ignore the signage directing you to the new trail and take the original one (assuming that you know where the old one is), the clues to "Whooping Crane", "The Lost Moose", "White Sails", "Merlin the Mooterious", and "Three Blind Moose" will be of limited value. I have advised the owners of these boxes of the situation and suggest that anyone wishing to visit these should first contact the owner(s) before venturing forth. Unfortunately, there is no need to contact the owner of "Give Meese a Chance" as that box appears to have been a casualty of the recent heavy rains in the Metroplex. It is likely that a new, and possibly improved, box will be planted within the next month or so to serve as a replacement.                                                                       Best regards,           Gryzzled Gryphon of Coryell County
