ghosts & other sneaky folks

From: wry_me (wry_me@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-08-14 05:08:24 UTC
I was boxing recently in McKinney at Pecan Grove. As I was crossing over to the bench mentioned in Boots' clues, I had just turned when a woman came from behind a bush at my back to ask me if I was looking for "the box in that tree." After I replaced my peewadding, (What exactly IS that, anyway? It's something my grandma used to say; "scared the peewaddin' out of me.") we got to chatting. It seems she's always there, for hours at a time, taking pictures of animals, and not only knows where the boxes are but the geocaches as well. So I was flat-out busted. I had already retrieved and re-hidden Puddle Splasher's without her seeing, since she asked me if I knew about "that one over there, too" so at least I'm a little sneaky. It's the first time someone actually knew already what I was doing, though, so it was pointless to lie. It makes me wonder how many times she's seen people boxing and caching and they didn't know it. If she didn't seem so harmless, I'd be a little creeped out by that.
