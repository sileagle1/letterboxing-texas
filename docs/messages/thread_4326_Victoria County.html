Hi everyone!

Rastaman, Agent X and I just got home from visiting my sister for an early Christmas in Victoria, TX.  I discovered while there for Thanksgiving that there was not a single box in the county (the one box whose clue on LbNA doesn't say it's been pulled is no longer there from what we could tell).  So, before we headed home this morning, we planted three microboxes in downtown Victoria.  They are listed on LbNA and AtlasQuest for anyone who's heading through there during the upcoming holidays.

A happy holiday season to everyone!

Rogue Artist
