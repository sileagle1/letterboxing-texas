North Texas Letterboxing Trip - Report

From: Baby Bear (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-04 17:32:11 UTC
Silver Eagle and I made a trip last week from Cypress, up through
Waco area, to Ft Worth/Dallas, and back down I-45. Here is a
listing of the boxes we found, and other info. Hopefully I got them
all

Found: All alive and well (unless noted)
Letter for a Son, We all Scream for Ice Cream, Wildflower Watch,
Dragonfly Dos (lid almost chewed off), Texas State Symbols #1 -
Small mammal, Texas Passport Series #4- Holland, Salado College,
Armadillo (mother neff), CCC Co 817, Daddy Long Legs, Double
Feature, Harry Potter #2, Drifter, Cameron Park Zoo 1 & 2, Wild and
Crazy Guy, Where have all the Flowers Gone, Chicks Rule, Hot Chicks,
Screechin Owls, Wild Cherry, Grandbury Legends Series - #2-John
Wilkes Booth, Badge of Friendship, T.C. Cherries, Marion's Longhorn,
Movie Star, The Wolverine, The Great Migration, Sidewalk
Astronomers, FWAS, Chicka Boom, 5th Grade Cougars, Jazz Quartet
Series - ALL 4, Pepper, Into the Woods, ULP Letterbox Revival, Red-
Eared Slider, Stop Buggin Me Series - All 4, A League of their Own -
#9,1,6,8,10,11,5,3 Mean Moon, North Park Troll, Whistle Stop Spur,
Old Native, Creek Crossing, Wildflower Meadow, Autumn Gift, Pecan
Gatherer, Labyrinth, Bat, Roadrunner, Lonesome Dove, White Sail, The
Lost Moose, In a Creek- Lauren's Turtle (box was so far under rock
we almost could not get out, even with 2 foot stick at full arm's
length), In a Tree - Meghan's Bird, Neckties and Running Shoes, Give
Peas a Chance, Texas Festivals - Hot Sauce Festival (great stamp),
UFOs #2-Beignet Eating Aliens (had to wait for person to leave that
parked in front of tree), Dallas Cowboys, The Duncanville Dragonfly,
Red Barn for Jen, Celebration of the Texas Countryside - ALL 3, SSC -
A Hole in the Ground in Texas, Bardwell - Where Cotton Called the
Shots, Going Batty, Texas Passport Series #3 - Italy, Petroleum
Park, Wolf Brand, Blind Lemon Jefferson, Ambling Armadillo, Take a
Hike, Lone Star Start.

Could not Find:
Lick Creek, Lena's Ghost(think we had the right tree), A League of
Own - #3 (nothing under bridge), Plano Ballon Festival (Looked at
every tree for 1 hour), The Sleeping Panther (may have had old
clues), Butterfly Hello, KatDog (Could not figure out which entry to
park, thus where to look), Jackpot, Gator, The Pelican Brief (trees
are all cut down and ground cleared), Granbury Legends - Jesse James
(found the tree, but could not find box in or under it), Memories,
Pretty in Purple.

We each hid boxes along the way, and should have them all posted by
tomorrow. Thanks to EVERYONE that hid the above boxes!!!!!!!!
