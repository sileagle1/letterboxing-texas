From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-03-18 03:01:34 UTC
It sounds like there is some interest, so we'll go over to the park next week and check out the facilities. If you've never been there, it's a very nice park with 19 miles of wooded trails and a big lake (Raven Lake). It can also be very crowded due to its proximity to Houston. There are several letterboxes in the park and the staff is aware of most of them. Our boxes there are permitted. The permitting process was slow and frustrating, mainly because they had never heard of letterboxing before, but they eventually approved of all the boxes. More later.

Boots Tex
