McKinney Falls SP report

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-07-14 13:02:06 UTC
I finally heard back from the ranger at McKinney Falls SP. Here is
the policy regarding letterboxes:

"Attached is the policy/procedure regarding this type of activity.
You are welcome to make an application and contact us to schedule an
appointment for a site visit with either myself or Assistant Park
Superintendent Janie Reeh to discuss the provisions of the policy
and to assess site placement. An application will not be approved
without a site visit. Approval of your application will depend upon
many factors including the policy in it's entirity and availability
of other 'caches' or similar activities in the general area. Permits
must be renewed annually and will require scheduled quarterly
maintenance during the permit period.

Sincerely,
David Shirley, Park Superintendent
McKinney Falls State Park
512-243-1643"

So, I guess we won't place any letterboxes in that park.

Boots Tex
