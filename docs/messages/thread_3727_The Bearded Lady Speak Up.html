The Bearded Lady Speak Up

From: SandiBox (sandibox@msn.com) | Date: 2008-08-16 09:48:27 UTC

After entertaining the Tax-Free crowds at the mall with my pet monkey until nearly midnight, I was shocked to hear that Baby Bear was last seen at Telge Park (also known as the Letterboxing Halloween park). I immediately suspected that he was up to no good.  What reason could that scoundrel possibly have for visiting the strange cast of characters that inhabit Telge Park?  Some of my best friends are freaks and outcasts, and I was worried about his motives.  And I was even more concerned about my beloved LSS Bozo.  I know Bozo better than anyone, and he is innocent of the accusations being hurled against him by that devious bear.  Innocent I tell you!  Bozo has been hot on Baby Bear's trail not out of malice, but to rescue the TTB2 passengers.  But would that dastardly bear actually sink so low as to harm Bozo just to salvage his own spotty reputation?  With fear gripping my heart, I gathered up a couple of flashlights and one of the circus' dancing dogs, then the monkey and I set off on a daring rescue mission of our own!

 

We arrived at Telge at 2:00 am.  With the dancing dog in the lead, and a full moon lighting our way, we set out to find Bozo and capture Baby Bear.  The dancing dog immediately picked up the gamey scent of matted bear fur and took off into the woods, with the monkey and I racing along behind.  But that bear is tricky, leaving false trails, and we had to double-back a couple of times.  Just as we were ready to give up in despair, we caught a glimpse of brown fur between the trees near a large vehicle.  Could that be the hijacked TTB2?   We turned the dancing dog loose to give chase, but that bear is fast, and alas, he escaped.

 

We ran up to the vehicle, and to our amazement, we found not the TTB2, but the Traveling Texas Circus!!!  And although he was a little worse for the wear, we found Bozo safe and sound inside.  Needless to say, he is quite traumatized by this entire experience, so we have brought him home to the big top to rest and recuperate for a few days.  But good folks, don't worry, the Traveling Texas Circus should be ready for its next venue very soon.  Ladies and gentlemen, children of all ageslook here to the Texas Talk List for the announcement of our next show.

 

In spite of my great relief at having Bozo and the rest of the circus safe and sound, my heart goes out to those poor passengers of the TTB2.   Baby Bear can't weasel his way out of it this time.  HE is the real hijacker!   `Fess up bear where is that bus?!!!

 

The Bearded Lady
