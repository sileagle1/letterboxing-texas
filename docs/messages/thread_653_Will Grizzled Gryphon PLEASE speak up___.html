Yes – my older guys are doomed with their trailnames!

Our oldest picked “Gryphon” because he sometimes boxes with his girlfriends’ parents – The WildBirds- and sometimes with us CelticLions, and a Gryphon is part Lion, part Bird (very diplomatic choice, right ;-)?) He probably won’t change his name no matter how many Gryphons appear!

 

Our middle son was using “NightHawk”, until I mentioned that there was an unbelievable new boxer in Houston named Hawk who was tearing up the trails planting dozens of boxes each week – he decided ( since he hadn’t at that point really stamped in too much) to use “FriarJon” instead – not likely to be a duplicate!

 

And our youngest – CactusLicker – well, it kind of conveys his “running-with-scissors” attitude towards life! And says a lot about his hair, too!

 

The dogs have been remarkably unimaginative with their names…

 

RT
