Mystery solved

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-07-04 18:34:43 UTC
Back in December, we took a trip to Fredericksburg and I took the opportunity to replace two letterboxes that had gone missing: "Unbroken Promises" in Fredericksburg and "Waylon and Willie" in Luckenbach. Thinking the original locations of the two boxes were part of the reason that they disappeared, I placed the new boxes in slightly different spots. Since that time, the two boxes have presented sort of a mystery to me. I would get reports that the boxes were found, then I would get reports that they were missing or at least couldn't be found. Then, someone would find them. I couldn't figure out why some people seemingly had no problem finding them and others couldn't find them at all. A couple of weeks ago when two respected and experienced letterboxers, Mosaic Butterfly and Corazon reported that they couldn't find either box, I decided to see if there was a problem in the clues. I first went into LbNA and read the clues. They seemed correct and straight-forward. Then, I went into Atlas Quest and the clues for both boxes were for the original locations! I can't believe that when I posted the new clues for both boxes, I didn't update them on AQ! If you recently tried to find these boxes using AQ clues, I sincerely apologize for leading you astray. On the other hand, you can't revisit Fredericksburg or Luckenbach too many times, can you?

Boots Tex
