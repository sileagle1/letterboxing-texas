The Circus is in town

From: pincesss09 (sarahkearly@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2012-04-02 13:49:16 UTC
My daddy always enjoyed taking his grandson's to the circus when it came to town each year. However, this year he was unable to because the lord called him home. With this being the 8 month anniversary of his passing, my 10, 8 year old, and I decided to take the circus to him. Find yourself at White Rock Cemetery in Bee Cave. Walk about half way down the path and look to your right for a grave with a green plant with red flowers on it. Say a small prayer for the beautiful man who rests here. Now look a bit more to your right for a heart shaped stone with the name Allen on it. Behind this is a large bush. Under the bush up against the concrete wall, the circus awaits you for your enjoyment.
