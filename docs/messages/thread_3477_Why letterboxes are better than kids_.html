Yeah it is.  I could give you a million reasons why boxing is better.
 
For one, you expect your boxes to stay out late and never call.
 
Boxes don't talk back.
 
Good boxes bring home good grades!
 
You don't need to feed them much.  NO DIAPERS!
 
Other people think your boxes are cute - no matter how old they are.
 
You can adopt a box without a lot of paperwork.
 
You can orphan a box even easier.
 
And on and on and on.........  <grin>
 
Yup - the kids moved out about 3 years ago!!!  FREEDOM !!!!!!!
