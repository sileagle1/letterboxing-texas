Hey there! I don't normally post too much but I just
had to jump in on the cemetery thread.

I volunteer to catalog cemeteries. I make a list of
all the headstones/markers in a certain cemetery in
row order including the names, dates and anything that
is on the headstones. I type it up and send it to the
local genealogical society and they in turn post it to
their GenWeb website. They post them by alphabetical
order. So if you see a listing on a GenWeb website and
it shows the person who cataloged the cemetery I would
suggest contacting them to see if they recorded it by
rows or not. If they did they should be able to tell
you by looking at their notes exactly where to find
the grave.

Happy Trails!!
