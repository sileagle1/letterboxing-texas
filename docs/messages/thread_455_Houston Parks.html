From: The Carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-11-20 21:10:20 UTC-06:00
There are some equestrian trails away from the soccer fields...you can also walk on them.  Also, off HW 6 across from the Houston Farm and Ranch Club there are some bike trails...I'm not sure if they are actually part of Bear Creek or not. -Mother of Four
PS  Ther is also the Bird Santuary in West Houston which has lots of trails and a small number of visitors (see my clues for the missing "Owl Be Seeing You" box) this is a beautiful park, and I'm sure my box went missing because I didn't pick a very secure hiding place...I think it got washed down into the ravine.  As Baby Bear said, some of us are learning our lessons the hard way.  The park is worth a look.
