You aren't far off - I was working on numbers earlier this morning
and this is what I came up with:

At 12/31/04, we had 72 members on the list and 432 boxes in Texas.
At 12/31/05, we had 157 members on the list and 868 boxes.

That means we gained 86 members and 446 boxes in the last year!
