Our trip - so far... an update.

From: (mike2004@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2005-10-19 15:55:54 UTC-06:00
We have been out nearly a week now. My dog is on the bed in our motel
room - snoring.... at 1400 hours. Shameless....but...This is OK - he
earned it. He has earned his new title. Quannah Parker Von
Bonehenge - CD, RN, CGC. Now we start training for the RA title.

My wife Monika is still in the ring with the pup Sammy as I type
this. Looks like at least a third in class, maybe more. The longer
she is gone, the better it gets. Only losers go home early.

Her dog Justin crashed and burned in the obedience trials Saturday,
but took a first-place trophy in the novice-A rally Sunday.

Yesterday was a day off from the shows, so we took a trip. Found 3
letterboxes and 4 geocaches.

And she got me a new T-shirt. It says "Professional Kennel Help
(unpaid)". Given the choice, I would have picked the one that
says "Drives the van and scoops".

Total count: 1 blue ribbon, 1 gold ribbon, 2 green ribbons, and one
trophy.

Hope you-all are doing as well.

Mike. From El Paso, but now in Chandler AZ.
