From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2011-08-17 18:07:17 UTC-05:00

Hi everyone,

I’m scheduling an event for the fall and wanted to let everyone know as soon as I could.  It will be at Stephen F. Austin park near Sealy TX.  I’ve made a reservation for the recreation hall for Saturday, Oct 22.  For those who may be interested in camping there, this afternoon they had one shelter left, and several campsites and RV sites.  The campsites only have water, but they told me you can tent camp at the RV sites.  Also, the facility I reserved is available overnight (Saturday night) and you can tent camp there. 

 

More details to come and I’ll get this posted on AQ shortly.

GROUP REC HALL INFORMATION

This facility is screened in and located at the Trail's Head. Has BBQ grill, fire ring, 10 picnic tables, fans, kitchen with cook stove, refrigerator, and hot/cold running water, and restrooms.
