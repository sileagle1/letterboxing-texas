Great Book Adventure Gathering update

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-12 00:26:57 UTC
Just a quick update to let you know that TeamKing and I continue to
firm up plans for the Great Book Adventure, Saturday October 21, in
Tomball, just outside Houston.

The main focus will be 5 or 6 "book walks." Each walk will feature
stamps from books of a different genre--children's, mystery, etc.
Plus, there will be plenty of bonus fun & games.

We would encourage everyone who is coming to carve a literary-themed
personal traveler. To add to the fun, as part of your PT clues, don't
come right out and say the character or book, but turn it into a
guessing game.

As we continue to firm up the plans, updates and more information will
be posted on the event page at Atlasquest. You can also RSVP here:

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=173

'Twill be fun!
