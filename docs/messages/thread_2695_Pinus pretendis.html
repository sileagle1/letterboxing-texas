Pinus pretendis

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-04-06 20:11:00 UTC-05:00
Have any of you spotted this species of tree before? I can't say I have never before seen one, but I definitely identified one today. 
 
P pretendis is sort of like a mockingbird, in that it often mimicks other species and can be very difficult to identify because of all the variants one might see.  The form it took today was that of a deciduous tree, with a multi-lobed leaf.  The bark didn't flake the way other pinus species do. 
 
So - anyone else seen one of these lately?  I do, in fact, know that JBK has seen one because she was telling me about it just the other day.  I really sort of doubted her at the moment, but now I have to once again apologize to her for doubting her!
