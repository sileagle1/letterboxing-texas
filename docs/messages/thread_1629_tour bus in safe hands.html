From: p_quest2005 (p_quest2005@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-26 13:54:34 UTC
i tried to post this last night, but the site was down:
i got the bus last night with no problems, except the expected traffic
from dallas to fort worth at around 6 pm. actually, once i got to the
park and was getting ready to head down the trail, someone came riding
up on a bicycle. he probably could have seen the color draining from
my face, as i wondered whether he had already snagged the bus. this
would have put me 0 for 2. but no, fortune smiled down on me last
night.
to everyone who has contributed so far, the stamps are great! i'll be
getting it back on the road soon enough--the exhaust from the bus
can't be healthy for us, being trapped inside the house. but i do
have a question. would anyone have any objections to me throwing
paper towels around the stamps and putting them in new bags? (i'm just
talking about the unmarked bags.)

p ?uest
