Boxes at Rob and Bessie Welder Park (Sinton)

From: Wanda (wsthm@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-07-13 05:14:38 UTC
I finally went on a letterbox maintenance outing this evening to
check on the boxes in my Looping Lassos series. Unfortunately out of
the 5 boxes, only 2 were still there - Purple Cow (but the little
poems were molded) and Come On, Sea Biscuit (but it was wet). So I
removed the poems for now, and took Sea Biscuit home with me for an
overhaul.

While there, I checked on Baby Bear's Hiker's Maze. It was still
there, only the book was wet and most (but not all) of the stamps
were washed out. The names were still there though. I'll be glad to
send you the book, if you email me with your address. I put a sheet
of paper in the micro-box, and when I go back again, in about a
week, I will have another little booklet made to replace the
previous one.

I am not going to replace my missing letterboxes, but I think I will
make some new ones for that location. But I won't be putting them
back in the same areas. I was quite surprised at the three that were
missing - well, two of them anyway. I wouldn't have thought anyone
would be wandering around in the area where they were hidden. And
Sea Biscuit - I was surprised it was still safe and sound.
Especially since it looked like the mowers had been over it. Park
maintenance on the hiking-biking trail is not consistant and what
you might hide dangerously far off the trail, in thick snakey brush
one time, might be mowed and detailed the next time you come. So it
is hard to judge...

I took Shabree and Zachary with me, and in searching for one of the
missing boxes, we found a tree covered in wild grape vines. The
grapes were ripe and we picked a few to eat there, and a good amount
to take home too. A nice bonus. :) Then we played and ate cold
watermelon in the playground until dark. It was a really enjoyable
evening.

Busy Bee
