Plant away, and carve away!
 
The only way to get better at carving is to carve, and it's fun to go back and visit boxes with your own early stamps.  Letterboxers will find your boxes no matter what's in them, and a good earnest effort comes through loud and clear, especially if you've gone to the trouble of finding great places to place your boxes.
 
You'll be great before you know it, but only if you keep on trying - and sharing your efforts with the rest of us!
