From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-01 18:02:45 UTC-07:00
LOL Joy,

What a great idea! Helping out with Waxahachie would
definitely cure someone of the perfectionism bug! By
necessity, JEN (Waxahachie) is going to have to be
down and dirty. Although I hope to distribute enough
information so that we can plan some stamps, I suspect
there will be a fair amount of carving on the spot.
BUT - when you are talking about an area with NO boxes
(or possibly a couple by that time), I think
expectations are more toward "are there really boxes
out there?" and less toward "I'm looking for a
masterpiece!"

At the moment, I'm only aware of ONE boxer in the
whole three county area. I've talked to my
sister-in-law who owns a newspaper in Navarro county
(Kerens Tribune) and she's agreed to either cover it
or publish something we give her. I'm still
struggling with the whole publicity idea, even though
my experience with the press was very positive. I'm
leaning toward giving her an article, unless someone
who will be participating feels strongly otherwise.

Still though, when you are talking about one boxer and
no boxes, it is a fertile field. How exciting for us
to be the farmers! It really wasn't that long ago
that my family was the only known LB'ers north of
Lufkin and east of Dallas and any box was an hour
away, and there weren't many of those! Life is good
around here these days!

Lucy
