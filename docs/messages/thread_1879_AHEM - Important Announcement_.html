From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-05-11 20:52:48 UTC-05:00
Now that I have your attention, I would like to point out that we here in Texas allowed a milestone to pass very quietly in April!
 
On April 17, we passed the 1000 box listing mark on LBNA.  I think it's pretty awesome that we zoomed on by that mile marker without evening pausing for breath!  And then, when you consider WOMs, AQ Boxes not on LBNA, and multi-box listings, we are well over 1000 plants in the state.  Go US!!!
 
Does anybody wonder which box was the 1000th listing?  I could pinpoint it to one of six (never mind the formatting - I couldn't get it to line up OR go away):
 
 
 
Box Name Placed By Num Boxes Nearby City     County    
Mine Ore jb kokopelli 1 Rusk, TX Cherokee
Linus Goes to School jb kokopelli 1 Rusk, TX Cherokee
A Tribute to Soldiers jb kokopelli 1 Rusk, TX Cherokee
The Haunted Trail jb kokopelli 1 Rusk, TX Cherokee
Snoopy Climbs to the Top jb kokopelli 1 Rusk, TX Cherokee
Tombs and Tees Puddle-Splasher 1 Euless, TX Tarrant

 
Way to go JB Kokopelli and PuddleSplasher for getting us over the mark!
 
Well, I just needed to make that observation.  You may now go back to your rat-killing.
