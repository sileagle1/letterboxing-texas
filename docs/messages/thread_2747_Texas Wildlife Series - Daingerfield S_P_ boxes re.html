Texas Wildlife Series - Daingerfield S.P. boxes replaced

From: jkcletterbox (jkcletterbox@msn.com) | Date: 2007-04-30 14:48:40 UTC
I just wanted to let everyone know that the boxes that were destroyed
in the recent controlled burn at Daingerfield S.P. have finally been
replaced. I had placed a series of 8 there - 3 of which were melted
and/or gone. Two other boxes were melted, but the contents intact.
Many thanks go out to tx dragonfly for her sitrep and to grizzled
gryphon for his clue suggestions. In the process of replacing those
boxes that were destroyed, I made some changes in location as well and
then updated the clues accordingly. I hope the park goes awhile before
they do another burn. Being such a big series that trails through the
entire park, please let me know if you happen by there and how those
boxes are doing. Thanks for your patience while I recarved and
reassembled. Kandy (JKC)
