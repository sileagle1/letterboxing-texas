From: Tara Burton (mselly13@juno.com) | Date: 2008-08-18 08:59:10 UTC
Just wanted to share my excitement with y'all. We have been
letterboxing for just over a year now. We have tried to get my
parents interested but with no luck.
Well, mom is out of town right now and Dad calls me everyday. Dad
also travels a lot for his job. He flies or drives probably 4 days
out of 5. Monday he going to Bastrop TX and Tuesday will be in Rio
Rancho NM. He comes home each night usually but travels a lot.
So anyway, I call him to find out where places are and stuff like
that. So I was asking him today about a town in south Texas told him
I was looking into a box in the area.
Well, I finally got him to look on letterboxing.org. He seems
interested. He doesn't think he'll have the time to find any in
Bastrop but is definately going to look for the 2 in Rio Rancho. I
told him if he did find them just to sign his name and stuff and we
can come up with a stamp for him later. He seems excited. A new way
to find interesting stuff on the road since he does travel so much. I
went over some of the basic rules with him, ya know be discreet that
sort of thing. But he seems generally interested. I am so excited
for him. I hope he enjoys this. I hope he gets good finds.
