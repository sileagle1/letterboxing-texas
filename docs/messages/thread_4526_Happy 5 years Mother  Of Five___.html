My first box was on September 30th, 2009 and it was called The Best Little Whorehouse in Texas by jb kokopelli. I loved it and went on for my second box of the day Pflugerville Futbol by Silver Eagle, which had trail changes and I ended up looking behind every tree to find the box but I did! And then I went and found Seeking Enlightenment and then 2 others by Pfetch that were missing but I was hooked. It was a great first day of boxing! I would go almost every other day after that to look for a box. Now I go once a week because there aren't many boxes left in the area.
