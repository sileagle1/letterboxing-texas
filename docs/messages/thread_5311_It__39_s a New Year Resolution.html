It&#39;s a New Year Resolution

From: (sandstorm_62@yahoo.com) | Date: 2014-01-07 08:04:53 UTC-08:00

My New Year started out as a bust with having the flu, although, very sick and unable to get out of bed for a week, it didn't stop me from planning. I created and set the foundation for myself the "The Great Texas Wine Sipping Tour", visual blogging and letterboxing along Texas Wineries and vineyards roads and touring routes via my Pinterest board. I wish to thank and acknowledge Baby Bear and Silver Eagle, two of Texas well known Letterboxers, who are my inspiration for doing this adventure. 


Yes I know everyone's resolution are different, but I do hope this message and the list below will inspire you to push yourself to explore more, see new locations, meet new people, and of course, find and enjoy more letterboxes in 2014.


* Host or attend a Letterboxing event

* Create a creative Letterbox that will earn (many) Favorite Points

* Find as many Letterboxes that you can in a year

* Have one day where you meet and collect at least 24 Letterboxer's signature stamps in 24 hours

* Solve the Puzzle Letterbox that stumped you in 2013

* Log a Find for that DNF's (Did Not Find) in 2013

* Add 5 more states or countries to the list of places where you've found Letterboxes

* Spend more time Letterboxing with family and/or friends

* Forget about the numbers, just have fun Letterboxing

* Reach a milestone in Letterboxing

* Be the First to Find at least once

* Write about your adventure letterboxing in a journal for your family to read as a "keepsake".

* Discover a Letterboxing location that makes you say "wow" once a month
