From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-02-03 18:07:06 UTC-06:00
This is for the upcoming "annual" gathering - the 4th anniversary of the first gathering held in Texas.  Silver Eagle and Baby Bear host this gathering and it moves around.  They each bring their chili and everyone else falls in with other stuff.  It's usually a casual gathering with plenty of opportunity to meet and talk to other boxers.  There are some tutorials on the books for this one - namely, I think, something for new box placers and something for carving.
 
At this point, we have several very nice regional gatherings as well, but this is the one considered to be the "come ye, come ye" event.  This is the opportunity for boxers to cross local lines and meet boxers they might not meet otherwise.
 
The "official" site for the gathering is here:
 
http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/TALE05/
 
Although there is also a listing on AQ:
 
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=508
