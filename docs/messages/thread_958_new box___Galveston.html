From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-27 13:27:55 UTC

The boxes at Challenger 7 park (Neptune, Aduceus-Cay, Space City
Shuttle, STS 51-L) are a great little diversion, and very close to I-
45. Others that are relatively near are our new one Labyrinth,
Topaz Memorial Box, Tweet Things, Java Joy (near Tweet Things),
Giant Leap, Orange Show, Magnolia Crossroads & Howard Hughes.

It will take you about a seven minute detour (each way) to go to
Memorial Park, where there are lots and lots of boxes, including
TeamKing's excellent Trivial Pursuit Series, Foghorn Leghorn, Lone
Star Dilla, some at the Arboretum which require more walking.
Depending on what time of day you come through, some of ours at
Memorial Park are drive-bys, including Rodeo Rest Stop and Pumpkin
Pi. Pop the Top is near Memorial Park and is fun.

If you want to email me off list, I can easily help you set up a
workable itinerary, as you were SO helpful in planning the Dallas
trip we still haven't taken (yet).

DB
