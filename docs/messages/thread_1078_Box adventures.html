Sorry Hawk!
We had a good boxing day. Old Baylor Park, 2 boxes, Brenham Ice Cream and Letter For a Son in Chappell Hill. My husband is a mason and this was a real experience for him.
The Ice Cream box was our first urban box and three is a lot of traffic there. So we got the map out and spread it over the area and just felt around. Due to the lack of rain the bushes were kind of sparse and the box was really in plain view.
When we rehid it we covered it up a little with some mulch.
Hopefully the rains we had this weekend will get everything growing again.
Always love going to Brenham cause we get to eat at Purcell's!
