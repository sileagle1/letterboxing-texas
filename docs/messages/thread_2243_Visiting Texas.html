Greetings Texas Letterboxers!


Can't believe it's almost time for us to fly out to Texas, and we're
hardly prepared at all! Got totally burned out on letterboxing
after the Cape Cod gathering (Wanda pushed herself too hard to reach
F12,000), so now we're just planning a couple of days hiking and
camping in the piney woods of Texas before the Great Book Gathering
in Tomball, then some boxing in Memorial Park, and perhaps whatever
else you folks out there might recommend. Looking forward to seeing
you all. We'll be wearing our honorary "Texas togs" (courtesy of
BoxDN and Celtic Lions) from our last visit to Texas, and we'll be
bringing along our "doggie" from "Monster Mash Days" in memory of
Sherlock Holmes, if anyone cares to make that literary reference to
us in order to see him and give him a pat (just watch your fingers)!

Looks like there's some great fun brewing out there in Texas, so see
you all soon,

Wanda and Pete
Charlestown, RI
