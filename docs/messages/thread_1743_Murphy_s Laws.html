Murphy's Laws

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-03-13 10:37:50 UTC-06:00
A while ago Silver Eagle and Baby Bear were asking about Murphy's Laws of Letterboxing.  After arriving home from a camping trip to the hill country I ask you all which you think is worse...to find the accidentally forgotten letterboxing backpack and all the clues sitting on the kitchen table, or the accidentally forgotten Kelty child carrier sitting in the garage?  I did discover, faced with the problem of how to carry a 22 pound baby up Enchanted Rock with no backpack, that in a pinch I can make a baby sling out of a beach towel and rope (it worked too!)  I was so desperate that I would have considered duct taping her to me, however the cammo duct tape was in the letterboxing pack on my kitchen table.  Despite the fact that we were unable to search for boxes, we had a fabulous trip, and saw that the wildflowers are beginning to bloom.  If anyone would like my pattern for the beach towel sling I will be happy to share it this weekend!  MO5
