interesting finds in rusk

From: tigger32167 (tigger32167@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-07 17:03:05 UTC
We went letterboxing this morning in Rusk and found a couple of the
boxes from the gathering in March. The most interesting site today
was the old Rusk College site. We found a 6 week old kitten roaming
around with no mommy in site. For those of you have never been to
this site, there is not a whole lot close by, so the kitten is now
home with us and has already been named LB for letterboxing. Also I
went to look for a box from the March gathering called "Shenandoah
Shadow" and to check on our 2 boxes there and found that the
location for Shenandoah and "Snoopy Climbs to the Top" had totally
been wiped clean of all growth and the log that both of them was
near was completely removed. After frantically searching, with
little LB circling my legs the whole time, I found the Shenandoah
Shadow box completely in tact and luckily enough we found our Snoopy
log book and stamp in tact but the box was completely destroyed. So
we brought the Snoopy stamp, logbook, the Shenandoah box, and little
LB home with us. I will try to get the Snoopy box back hidden asap,
but I need to know what the people from the March gathering would
like me to do with the Shenandoah Shadow box. I do not mind re-
hiding it, just let me know.
What an adventure!!!
JB Kokopelli
