TX Letterboxing Past

From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-02-02 03:33:21 UTC
Silver Eagle mentioned going back and reading some of the old posts in reference to a debate that has recently resurfaced. It reminded me of when I first began letterboxing. I wanted to find out everything I could about this hobby/sport/addiction, but I specifically wanted to find out about the beginnings of Texas Letterboxing. I went back through the archives and read just about every post on this list. I understand that there weren't as many (obviously) posts back then as there are now, but I still think it's a great idea. I was passionate about letterboxing and wanted to find out everything that I could about it. I think that an understanding of and appreciation for our past can help shape a better future for our letterboxing community.

Lucy Locket
