Cool and Wet Day for Letterboxing

From: Birds of a Feather Family (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-17 16:04:21 UTC-08:00
I took advantage of having the day off and set off to find Dewberry's Railroad Monopoly Game series and boxes in the Tomball area.
 
Found the following boxes:
 
* Baby Bear's "The Hunter" and "Bear Contry" - Bear stamp is broken / previous visitors repaired with bandaid
* Mother of Five's "Harry's Headache" - Box had water in it but log was dry but it is almost completely full.
* Tiger's "Mr. Ladybug" - original log soaked again from more water, replacement pages dry. Put the box in a large bag.
* TeamKing's "The Birds" and "Trivial Box Pursuit - History"
* Dewberry's "Railroad Monopoly Game: Pennsylvania RR", "Railroad Monopoly Game: B&O Railroad", and "Railroad Monopoly Game: Reading Railroad"
* Quetzel's "True North?" and "The Stamp"
* King's "Walking Man"                                                          
 
I enjoyed walking the trails with the cool weather.  Had to use a large umbrella as a cover a couple times while stamping in.
 
Thanks everyone for placing the boxes.
