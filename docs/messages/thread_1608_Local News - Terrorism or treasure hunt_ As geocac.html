You know - I am extremely conscious of what my Letterbox-Hybrid Geocaches look like to an outsider.  I always seek permission before placing one.  Silver Eagle can attest to that.  I have a good working relationship with most of the local rangers, and make it a point to keep them informed.  It saves a LOT of trouble.
 
Clear plastic containers help.  But in a 4000ft ASL desert plateau, the UV and blowing sand will eat up a plastic container in weeks.  I use AMMO cans.  But most of my hides are with permission, and the locations are known.  Some of them have bottled water in the hide, and the rangers appreciate knowing water is out there in an emergency.
 
Well - nuf said.
