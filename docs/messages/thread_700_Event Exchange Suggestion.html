Event Exchange Suggestion

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-03-15 20:04:45 UTC

I think we will plan on doing exchanges the same way as
last year; logbooks laid out on tables and people stamp
as they get a chance. To help this process, you should
bring a sheet of paper with your trail name to place under
your logbook so others will know whose logbook they are
stamping into. Also, if you already have exchanges from
some other letterboxers, either from last year's Event or
other encounters, you might want to list Exchanges already
obtained on your paper if you don't want duplicates.
It might be a good idea for people to sign their trail
name on each sheet after they exchange to keep track of
which logbooks you have already done, since people will
be arriving at different times. Not sure the best way
to do this, but this might work.
Also, I recently found out we may have a surprise mystery
guest coming from a long distance, so this Event is really
shaping up to be a special one!

Silver Eagle
