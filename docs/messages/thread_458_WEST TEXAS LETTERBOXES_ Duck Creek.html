Re: WEST TEXAS LETTERBOXES/ Duck Creek

From: Eoghan (eoghan@sharawadgi.com) | Date: 2004-11-20 23:27:16 UTC

I found that one in March '01 and even then the clues were a little
off and the box itself was well concealed. Benches mentioned in the
clue had been removed and we had to navigate based on where we thought
they might have been. the 'path to the right' wasn't much more than a
slightly eroded drainage. When I found the box it was barely visible.
The low area is flood prone and the white rocks were barely visible
above the surface of the mud. the box was full of water, but the log
book was mostly dry. I rehid it as well as I found it, which might
have been too well. Another heavy flood since then may have buried it
completely. Or washed it away. I remember that one pretty well. I'd
hidden a few before then, but that was my first find.
I'd love to make it to an event sometime, but don't think I'll be back
in the Austin area for awhile. Too bad. I'd really like to find all
the new boxes there!

-Eoghan
