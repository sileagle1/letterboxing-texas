Bastrop Parks updates

From: Bonnie Lind (bonnielind1@hotmail.com) | Date: 2013-01-28 19:06:14 UTC-06:00

I went to Bastrop State Park to check on my Pining for the Lost Pines letterbox.  I was surprised how the trails have changed and had to update my clues accordingly.  Even if you think you know the trails at Bastrop, you may want to get a current map from the office.  I asked about the orienteering markers—the park ranger I spoke with said they were all burned.  It doesn’t sound like they have plans to replace them. 

 

Buescher State Park wasn’t affected by the 2011 fires and they have also been working on their trails.  I am pleased to report they have a new shady trail that runs parallel with the gas line trail.  I scanned and attached my maps of the new trails in both parks.

 

In case you haven’t had a chance yet, I hope you can sign up for TALE 10 that happens in the first weekend in March: http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2469.

 

I hope to see y’all soon!
