I have de autobus de tourista

From: Pancho Juan (ponchojuan@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-11-24 13:27:00 UTC-06:00

I am Pancho Juan of Guadalajara Mexico. Pardon my poor English. I will try to be clear.


Loco Cajun got in my way, but I have won. I now own the autobus tourista and take it to Mexico with me. On the claim of Texas as a part of old Mexico, it is justo that it residir en Guadalajara with me.

I follow the path of Domingo Teran de los Rios, passing by many letterboxes as I go. I am taking refugio in missions along the way, and will soon be in my tierra natal with my tesoro.

Via con Dios Texas and Texans, and to the loco Cajun. I go laughing.

Pancho Juan
