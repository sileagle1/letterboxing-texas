Some good things happened in El Paso Today.....

First - I went out and hunted the letterbox my wife Dane Lady placed. She
did a very nice job of it. It was a nice hike - although getting warm by
the time we got down from the mountain at 1000. It is a letterbox hybrid
geocache. As a nice touch - she stuffed it full of kid toys and is letting
any kids hiking to it pick one toy free for hiking - no trading necessary.
Justin, one of the famous Texas Chihuahuas came along. He is just
recovering from what Dane Lady calls "Brain Surgery", so he enjoyed the run
in the mountains too. The staples didn't seem to bother him too much.

Next - I (and Cherokee Brave) placed (replaced) the missing letterbox for
Sandibox
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=17560

We did not stamp in - saving it for a rainy day. Heh heh heh - last time I
did that - it went missing! I hid it in a better spot this time. Changed
clues sent. I suppose she will update the clues soon.

Last - and the BEST NEWS. The LBNA patches arrived last night. So for our
weekly ham-radio gun shooting Letterboxer geocacher hiking and dog training
meeting and pot-luck dinner last night I passed out LBNA, TX-LBNA, and
Geocaching patches to all the participating members. Two guys don't play.
They didn't get patches either!

Only bad part so far is I was told to clean my room, and get my junk off the
table. Can you imagine that! She wants to serve DINNER on the dining room
table!!!! So where am I supposed to clean my rifle and store my hiking
gear????

Hope she don't read this!

Y-all have a GREAT day.
