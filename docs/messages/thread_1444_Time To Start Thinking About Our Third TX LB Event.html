Time To Start Thinking About Our Third TX LB Event

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-11-06 16:47:54 UTC
In the wake of the two mini-events, JEN & Telge, I have
been thinking about when and where our big event should
be held. Since our first one was held in the Central
region and the second one was in Southeast, I thought
we should have it in one of the other regions. I have
been looking at possible sites, mostly State Parks, with
the following criteria: a covered group area able to
seat at least 50 with electricity and restrooms nearby
that does not cost over $50, and plenty of camping available.
So far I have come up with the following suggestions:

NE Region: Rusk SP - seats 75 and has great train ride
N Region: Dinosaur Valley SP - seats only 25 and no electricity
Cedar Hill SP - seats 100 but costs $85
S Region: Choke Canyon SP - seats 100 and is close to Central TX

I'm sure there are other places that might work, but for
right now I am just throwing these out there to start everyone
thinking about it. As far as date, I was thinking either
Saturday, 3/11 or 3/18/06. I'm not sure if having it during
Spring Break is good or bad for most people, but if we have
it much later, soccer and other sports start up and it becomes
harder to get away. I would like everyone to discuss these
and other options and I will put it to a vote in a week or so.
If you have some new ideas or opinions, speak up. Thanks.

Silver Eagle

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-06 13:39:45 UTC-08:00
Although I kind of enjoy getting to go to gatherings
in parks I've never been to, I think having it in Rusk
would be a lot of fun. There are currently 7 very
good boxes in Rusk and I'm about to start planting my
scout "training" series, which will be a series of 6
boxes intended to teach scouts how to be good boxers
AND how to practice good outdoor skills. It should be
a fun series for anyone.

There aren't currently any boxes on the Palestine end
of the train ride, but hopefully a couple will go in
in the next few nonths. The rates for the train are a
little pricey these days, but since they went up on
their rates, they started a kids ride free program on
the weekends. Train doesn't start running until March
12th

I personally opt for the March 18th date, but at the
moment either date will work for me. I know for my
part I would like to see us settle on a date pretty
soon, since my scout calendar will start filling up
for Spring in the next month or so and I would like to
be able to block out a date for the gathering.

Lucy
