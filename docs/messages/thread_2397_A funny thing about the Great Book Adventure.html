A funny thing about the Great Book Adventure

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-19 00:06:19 UTC
Hibisue's post makes me wonder, did anyone find the Little Women
letterbox hidden at the pavilion during the GBA?

A true, but funny story. I forgot to pick up this particular box
after the event. It was an altoids tin, wrapped in BROWN duct tape
and attached via magnets to one of the pavilion pillars. It was not
immediately obvious, but the box didn't exactly blend into the pillar
either.

So, when I finally got back there, more than 6 weeks later, it was
still there, hanging on the pillar, just waiting for me to find it. I
was curious how many other events had happened at that pavilion. I
was really curious how long it might stay, but I picked it up anyway.

No one had stamped in, so I'm curious if anyone found it or if it went
unfound.

I may have to use this hiding method again--it seemed to work very well!

Dewberry
