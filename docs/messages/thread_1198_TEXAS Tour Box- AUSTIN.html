Ok, Pancho Villa has rescued the bus from the clabosse, and taken
the group to one of his favorite resting spots west of Austin. Pace
Bend Park is off Highway 71 west. After paying the entrance fee and
getting a map, park at the hiking trail head near Mudd Point. Hike
the trail to the first Hitching post at the intersection of another
trail. Belly up to the hitching post, or uncomfortable bench,
whatever way you want to see it, and look into the trees. You see
the dead, but still standing lopped off tree, and it's living
companion beside it? The bus is parked in the rocky den behind them.
Please becareful of the biggest daddy long legs I have ever seen in
there. Enjoy. My offer is to take anyone in on my own park pass if
we can coordinate a time. call me 956-357-8770.
Carie
