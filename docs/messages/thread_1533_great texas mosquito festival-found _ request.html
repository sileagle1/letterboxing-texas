great texas mosquito festival-found & request

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-12-19 23:14:17 UTC
We found Great Texas Mosquito Festival. A lovely stamp, gorgeous
logbook and a nice hiding spot overlooking Buffalo Bayou. Overall, a
fantastic box I highly recommend.

However, due I am sure to the recent deluge, the inside of the box was
sopping wet. The logbook, double bagged, was dry as a bone. But, I
suspect the box itself wasn't water-tight (every now and again you get
a lemon). So, I replaced the container with a lock-n-lock box I had
in my pack. But, my box wasn't camo-taped. So, if someone is going
to find this great box (and I highly recommend you do), please bring
along some camo tape and add it to the container!

It's like a progressive dinner, but for letterboxing. Thanks.

Dewberry
