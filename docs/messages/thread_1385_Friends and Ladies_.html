As I understand it, all the boxes for the Terrors of Telge will be collected at the end of November, and my good friend Dewberry of Gramatrick has kindly offered to collect that dripping Dallas box to return it to us, and it will be planted in the White Rock area soon after!

And speaking of my good buddy Dewberry and boxes to be planted in Dallas, I have had the most wonderful letterbox sitting on my craft table for weeks, and haven’t figured out what to do with it! Dewberry sent me a fantastic letterbox with a gorgeous logbook and great stamp – and I’ve been paralyzed! I could plant it around Dallas, because Heaven knows we NEED more boxes, but I’m worried about damage to the logbook if I plant it outdoors and haven’t thought of an appropriate indoor place yet – could send it out as a PLB, but they sometimes go astray, too…dither, dither.dither…!!!

So then I had a brainstorm…( either that or the coffee finally kicked in this morning) – how about if I bring it to the Barnraising as an EVENT box? That way the maximum number of people get to enjoy it, and it will be safe from weather, BEAs and Bigfoots.

And the theme of the box, “If I Only Had a Friend” really fits the idea of a Barnraising, I think!
