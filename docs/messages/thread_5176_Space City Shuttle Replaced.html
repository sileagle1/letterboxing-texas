Space City Shuttle Replaced

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2013-04-03 20:09:47 UTC
A few weeks ago while finding some of the new boxes at Challenger 7 Park I verified that this old box was indeed missing. Since it is one of my oldest and the first one in the park I decided to replace it today for about the 4th time. It is almost in the same spot but I updated the clues to make it easier to find, so check them out before looking. Thanks to all who have found this box over the years and to those who have added boxes of their own. This park is quite the spaceport now!

Silver Eagle

P.S. Some of the clues to boxes in this park list Galveston as the county, but it really is barely in Harris, so look at both on LBNA.

And I think I forgot to mention that I've adopted, recarved, & replanted Hawk's old STS-51 box in that park.  (It had a non-relevant replacement carve in there.) It has a new image, logbook & container.  I only co-opted the location and part of the name.  There's also a HHH there. 
 
I should also mention that I've updated the clues to my boxes at Stephen F. Austin State Park.  The trailhead change for Copperhead trail and changing landmarks required an update for these newbie plants of mine, so be sure to print the new clues. I also recarved one of the series that went missing right away.  I will say that those cellphone boxes they sell in the camping stuff are fabulous for keeping out moisture, though they are expensive and don't have much room.  I'm pretty sure now that they also float!
