Mine would have to be finding JB's tribute to Joan boxes. The message
behind the finds far out way any that I have found. I to also lost
someone very dear to me and I even named my oldest daughter after this
close friend. We did everything together and 7 months after we left
high school a drunk driver hit her coming home from church one Sunday
morning. Killed her on the spot. It has been 19 years since she died
and there has never been a week go by for the 19 years that I do not
think of her and wonder what she would have been or grew up to be.
This has inspired me to make a series of boxes in her memory in this
coming new year. I hope to inspire someone to relive a fond memory of
somebody they love just as JB did for me. Keep a eye out for the new
boxes.

Your friend in LBing,
Kim
