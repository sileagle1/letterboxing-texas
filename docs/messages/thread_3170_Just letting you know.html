Just letting you know

From: Lois -James (adamstrails@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-11-09 21:03:11 UTC
I have removed The Blue Daisy for a time. That is the box that some
homeschoolers found by mistake and we had such a hard time finding it
and replaceing it a time back. Today I went to the park and sure enough
the homeschoolers were there. Now to make it very clear---I am not
against homeschoolers so please do not take it that way. In fact the
experience was very refreshing.

It seems that every Friday, weather permitting, the area homeschoolers
have an outing in that park. I pulled up and started to check on The
Blue Daisy when I came in contact with 2 girls. I ask them if they had
found my letterbox. One said I didn't. Then the littlest of the 2 said.
I did or rather my brother did. I asked if they found it today. She
said yes, it is still there. I said ok, I am going to check on it now.
When I came back, the brother and sister were sitting on the rail
waiting on me. I just said as I put the box in the van, Thanks for
checking on it for me. The boy said,"Your Welcome" as we parted ways
waving at each other. I will replace the box as one and I don't
remember who, suggested in another location in the same park. Hopefully
where they do not play on Fridays.
