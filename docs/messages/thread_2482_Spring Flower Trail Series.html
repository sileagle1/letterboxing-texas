I got a chance to get out in the fresh air today. I headed
down to Martin Creek Lake SP to replace my Daffodil box. They have
changed a lot in the area of the Daffodil since I planted it,so that
is why no one could find it. The signs are changed and there is a
small clearing there now. I did find the box intact and it is
waiting for the next boxer to stamp in it. I had a replacement box,
but thankfully I did not need it. The clue has been updated. I am
sorry several of you did not get that stamp. If anyone wants it, I
can sen you a picture of it throuhg email. Just let me know.

hard to re-plant the Great Texas Ballon Race Box, but it will have
to wait for another day. The sun went down before I could find a
safe place. Hopefully I will get back to it soon. While in Longview,
we saw several hot air ballons in the sky! I am not sure why they
were up but, I loved seeing them!
I have two more boxes to place so look for new clues soon. One
for Smith County and one for Rusk County. It was a beautiful day and
I loved walking the trails on a cool Saturday morning.
Happy Boxing!
