From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-08-07 21:11:57 UTC-05:00
I would say that any of the boxes in Telge Park (check LBNA, lots of the clues cannot be found on AQ) are pretty easy in terms of terrain, length of walk, etc.  The other nice thing about Telge is that there are boxes from many different placers.  Kids can walk the trails and there are bathrooms.  Make sure to take mosquito repellant, they can be VERY FIERCE there!  If you want suggestions about specific boxes, or which are easier to do with kids, or anything else :), just ask!  It is a great place to go letterboxing...especially if you like Halloween!  MO5
