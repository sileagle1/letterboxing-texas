I have bio kids, adopted kids and foster kids.  They call my mom either Mom Mom or Mama Abuela.  My dad was Pop Pop before he passed. 

 

Mama Abuela – though it has a silly literal translation – is sort of lyrical and pleasing to hear and I expect it will stick long after my Hispanic children have gone on to their forever families.

 

Mom mom and Pop Pop both came about because the first grandchild couldn’t say Grandmom and Grandpop.  I agree with another post that no matter what you want to be called, the child may settle on something else entirely! (and you will probably love it because it will be your first gift from that precious grandbaby).

 

Best wishes all around…
