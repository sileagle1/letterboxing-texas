I think it might be fun to organize a Texas list box exchange.  Is
anyone interested in creating a box that would be mailed to another
member of the exchange (in a different city), who would then plant
it in their area?  They did this on the Great Lakes list, and I am
for blatant copying of a good idea.

(Possibly, if you live in a low-letterbox-populated area, but don't
feel up to carving a stamp, you might be on the receiving end of a
box without having to necessarily create one--our way of helping get
some boxes out there.) 

We have some terrific talent and some terrific talent-in-training. 
Texas is so large that it sometimes is hard to travel around the
state.  I, for one would love the opportunity to see some of the
artistry and imagination of others throughout the state.  I loved
the Clue Exchange at the Gathering because you got to see the
different ways people could interpret the theme.

I think a unifying theme might be best.  However, I am open to
suggestions on what it could be.  Totally Texas?  Texas Wildlife
(open to all interpretations)?  Road Tripping?  Or, something as
simple as Halloween Tricks & Treats?  Lots of creative folks out
there that can help come up with ideas. 

I will volunteer to coordinate, if there's interest.  Maybe a target
exchange date of September 15th or so, to give people plenty of
time. 

So who's in?  And anyone have theme ideas?
