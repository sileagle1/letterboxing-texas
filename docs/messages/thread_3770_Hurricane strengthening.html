Ike is getting stronger. Overnight the wind speed picked up 5 mph, the motion
picked up 1 mph, and the eye pressure fell 3 milibars. If the wind speed goes
above 110 mph it becomes a category 3.

The eye is about 250 miles from Galveston right now.

Virtually all but one model show the eye coming across anywhere from the TX/LA
border to San Antonio. That last model shows it coming toward El Paso. We are
NOT thinking of evacuating at this time. .

a place to hook up a trailer. Pets are welcome here too. Unfortunately, the
bunkhouse it already full of puppies.

In case no one ever gave it a thought - being ham operators my wife and I are
also trained official weather spotters. Don't look for us in the hurricane
spotting job though - they don't need us there.

Our thoughts and prayers are with you-all evacuating. And your pets! Good
luck.
