From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-09 04:59:53 UTC-07:00
As far as white vs pink speedball, I have to ditto
what Silver Eagle said. I tried it but didn't like
the way it carved. I never planted a box I carved
with it, but I did use a sig stamp for a short while
that was made from it. I look back at the stamps I
carved with it, and even not being exposed to the
elements, they don't have the feel of a stamp that
withstands the test of time.

I've been tempted again, when it was all I could find,
to buy it again. I've managed to remind myself I can
always find a pink pearl eraser.

Lucy
