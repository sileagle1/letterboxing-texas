From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2015-03-04 14:38:17 UTC-06:00
I went to McKinney Falls Park yesterday, and the river was running high enough that even I was reluctant to leap from rock to rock across the falls, and more water is coming!  I stayed on the camping side of the falls.
I saw my first coyote in the wild there yesterday, too.  I stopped on the trail, turned around, and saw a large coyote about 50 yards behind me, just looking at me.  After a few moments, he turned, slowly walked about 20 more yards away, and turned back and just looked.  After a few more moments, he turned and kept walking away.  It was pretty cool!
-- Bell Motel
