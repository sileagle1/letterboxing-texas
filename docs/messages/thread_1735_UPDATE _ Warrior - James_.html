From: Michael P. Olbrisch (mike2004@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2006-03-12 02:14:59 UTC

Howdy Y-all.

This will be just a quick update. We are still doing well out her
in El Paso. Wish we could join you-all, but we have been on the road a lot
these last months to dog shows and to welcome James home from Iraq. I am
working 10 to 12 hour days, and I needed a 3 hour nap today just to keep
going. We have a ham-radio related picnic/camping outing in Abilene in May,
so it isn't like we don't have stuff going on.

The real news is that James (still in Ft. Carson/Colorado Springs)
is really taking to Letterboxing. He is making the most of the months he
has left in Colorado. But he is already looking forward to coming to Ft.
Hood/Killeen Texas. And he will be Letterboxing down there too.

What does this mean? Well, it is likely that many of you will meet
him. And it is also likely that next year we ALL will make the gathering.
Wish we could do it this year.

Last - just wanted to wish you-all a VERY GOOD TIME at the up-coming
event. Hope everything goes off without a hitch, and everyone has a really
fantastic time. I am sure I will hear of it here on the list, and regret
all the more not being there.

GOOD LUCK - HAVE FUN!

Oh - Mr Silver Eagle Sir, looks like my son and I will do the peak together
in the next few weeks - will keep you posted. Haven't forgotten about Hueco
Tanks either, still working that.
