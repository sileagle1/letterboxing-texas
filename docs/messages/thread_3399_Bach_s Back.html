Bach's Back

From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2008-03-11 19:12:05 UTC-04:00
Bach's Box is now back in place near the Calatrava Wave fountain/sculpture in front of the Meadows Museum on the SMU campus in University Park (listed as Dallas), with a repaired stamp and new logbook and new box.  As before, this location requires extreme stealth and some patience to actually get the box and replace it again without being detected, as it is in the middle of a very busy location!  Surprisingly, though, the box has remained in place for almost exactly two years, so with care it can be found, stamped, and replaced safely.  I'm hoping Bach enjoys a much longer stay in this lovely location for future boxers to find, too.   Enjoy!
