TALE 14....Angels among us

From: (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2016-04-04 13:15:14 UTC-07:00

As I drove home today, I had a chance to really reflect on the events of the last three days. When I called my husband to tell him that I had a mud bath on the trails, he immediately said "I'm sure your letterboxing friends helped you and took good care of you". Yes they did!  I have to say, that I am so thankful and blessed to be a part of this great community of fellow Boxers. Thanks to Dr. Bee, her Dad (sorry do not know his trail name) , LCAB, and Lucy Locket, I was able to recover from my mishap and return to a warm welcome (cough, cough) at the dining hall. I was a little sore the next day, but had a great time on the trails  boxing with LL, Beach Bum and Pink Cookie.
Once again, I must say, I am so glad that it was a mishap that was easily recovered from, and I learned a lesson, always leave your muddy boots outside in the sun. After all, they are a Bio-Hazard!
To all the new Boxers that attended the event...Thank-you for joining us in this great hobby, and please feel free to ask questions, we are glad to help!
 It was sad to see that little Baby Bear in a jar, and  I am sure The Ghost of Ira had something to do with  that one! But seeing Silver Eagle model that bikini was priceless! Who knows who or what will show up next year, but just seeing everyone is a gift in itself.
See you next year!
Blue Butterfly
