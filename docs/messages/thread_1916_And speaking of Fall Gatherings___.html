From: Michael P. Olbrisch (mike2004@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2006-06-07 03:28:55 UTC
Close.  Midland is 4 hours from east El Paso.  About 4.5 from my house.  The drive is split between I-20 and I-10.  I-20 ends at Kent Texas.
 
Y-all are welcome out here.  There are now 7 letterboxes - soon to be 8.  6 are pretty easy.  Two are strenuous hikes.  One hike 6 miles and <1000 feet elevation.  The other almost 8 miles and > 3000 feet elevation.  There will be an 8th letterbox soon.  Maybe this coming weekend.
 
Mike.
