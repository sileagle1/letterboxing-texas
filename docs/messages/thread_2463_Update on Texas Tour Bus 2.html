Update on Texas Tour Bus 2

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-01-05 00:58:16 UTC
Just a quick update on Texas Tour Bus 2: When the bus left Spring, it
headed up 59, but somewhere made a detour to central Texas and was
delivered to a place called Prairie Chapel Ranch, where it stayed for
a few days. I tracked it down and saw it leave the ranch today,
heading east toward Waco. The funny thing is, there is no bus
driver. This must be a rental, because the rancher is driving and the
only other person aboard is an attractive lady that I assume is his
wife. But, something is odd here, the bus is escorted by a convoy of
black Suburbans, sort of like a motorcade. These must be Very
Important People. Wait, the bus just turned south on Highway 6. I'll
have to keep my distance but I'll keep following and keep you informed
of its progress.
