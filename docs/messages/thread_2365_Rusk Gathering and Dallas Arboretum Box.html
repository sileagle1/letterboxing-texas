Glad to hear there is going to be a gathering close to home and look
forward to meeting these new boxers here in East Texas! You know you

sprinklers hoping it will stay there. It's the same stamp. Lucy
Locket and I are planning a boxing marathon in a couple of weeks so I
may go back and try to find another spot.
