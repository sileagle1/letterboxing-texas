Hi everybody!

Don't know about you guys, but I'm forever having a hard time finding
the stuff that I like for stamping. I just found a new web site,
called stampandgo.com that sells something like 60 different colors
(incl. gold and silver) of the cat eye pigment ink pads for $1.79
each! YEA! Much better than the $2.69 that I have paid for them
previously.

They are really great. Pigment ink, vibrant, archival quality,
raised ink pad, HUGE choice of colors, small enough to carry lots.
The site also carries the replacement ink so you don't have to buy a
new pad everytime the ink runs out!

Even better, I got an email confirming that my order had been shipped
the SAME DAY I placed the order! Talk about fast! And the ink pads
arrived in great condition. I was really impressed.

Not sure if anybody else is nuts about it like I am...but wanted to
share, just in case. :-)
