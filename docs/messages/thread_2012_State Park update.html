I am not trying to start a political debate, I just wanted to keep you up to 
date on the State Park situation.  It looks like this might be an issue in the 
governor election this fall.  When you vote, check your nominee's stance 
on State Parks.  All the candidates are coming up with their reasons for 
saving/not helping the parks.  Again, I don't want to start a political 
war here, just wanted to let you know what is going on.

--------------------
Perry urged to shift funds to flagging parks 
Gov. Rick Perry rejects a call to use part of his job-creation fund to 
address the decline of the state parks system.
