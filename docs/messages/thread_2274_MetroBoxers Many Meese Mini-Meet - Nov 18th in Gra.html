MetroBoxers Many Meese Mini-Meet - Nov 18th in Grapevine!

From: CelticLions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-10-29 16:37:11 UTC-06:00

The MetroBoxers from the Dallas/Fort Worth and surrounding areas are planning a little casual get-together with a tongue-twister of a name:  Many Meese Mini-Meet! We would love to meet & greet and letterbox with all of you!

Since this idea was whipped up rather spur-of-the-moment, please do not expect a gorgeously orchestrated event like Dewberry & TeamKing’s Great Book Adventure! We are still in the planning stages, but have circled in the date of Saturday, November 18th, and the location of Grapevine. AtlasQuest has the event listed, so further info will be available on that page as we figure this thing out. The theme was created around PuddleSplasher & Viewfinder’s MOOSE-themed boxes in the area, and we hope to amoose you with more meese marvels to come!

 

Cheers!
