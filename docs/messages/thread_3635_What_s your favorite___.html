From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-06-27 05:16:18 UTC-06:00
Trail Mix.
 
Mountain Trail Mix is my very favorite, but the Tropical will do also.  The mountain had M&Ms in it, so they don't cause a mess.  The Tropical has little Hershey's chocolate bits, they will melt.  But it works OK for cooler weather.
 
I wouldn't want to live on the stuff, but it is quick and easy on the trail.  If you are hiking several days you might want something more substantial.
