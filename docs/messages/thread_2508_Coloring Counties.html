Ok, since most people would prefer to see a map of our progess, I
have created one and added it to the Files (lb_counties_map.jpg) and
deleted the Word document. It shows counties colored in green that
had boxes as of 12/12/06 (the date the webmasters last updated the TX
clue map with my updates), and counties colored in cyan for boxes
added since then. I will try to keep the map updated and to notify
Choi periodically to have him update the TX clue map. I will need
your help, though. If you place a box in a virgin county or notice
one that should be colored cyan that I missed, please notify me or
post to this list with a Subject of "Color Me Please". I will then
update and upload a new map as soon as I can.

Now for a few things I need to mention. First of all, I will only be
coloring in counties that have boxes with clues in LBNA with the
correct county posted. This means if a box is on AQ but not on LBNA,
it won't count, since it is an LBNA map that we are trying to fill in
and AQ doesn't deal with counties anyway. It also means that mystery
boxes that don't show the county will not be colored. This applies
to any WOM boxes, too. You could post a WOM box clue with the county
and just have people email for clues if you want it to count.
Second, some counties (e.g. Shackelford) have a box that is listed as
missing, so are not colored. Other counties (e.g. Liberty) have a
box that is also missing but is not listed that way, so it is
colored. To keep it simple, counties not currently colored will need
at least one box placed in them before they are colored, and no
counties will be uncolored if a box goes missing in the future.

Remember, this is just for fun and not a contest, so please don't
feel any pressure to color all the counties in your Region before
others. So place when you can and enjoy watching our progress!
