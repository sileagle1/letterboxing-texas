frogkisses72 <mothersinstinct@yahoo.com> wrote:
So I get this excited can barely understand fast paced talker on my
phone. After thinking about the conversation, this is what I hear.

"Stacy..we need to get in our cars NOW and go to Wills Point." OMG! I
say. The bus The bus!"

Turns out Texas Pokey saw the post 4 minutes after it was posted. I
throw the kids in the car. Yes I buckled up Tadpole in his car seat.
Luckily the diaper bag was packed and the car was full of gas. I
head towards Txtatas to get hernot knowing if she is going with us
yet. Hee hee. Second call from TP that TT cannot go this time. Darn
It!!

I make a U-turn and race for the freeway. After hitting the drive
thru for the older boys, I race for Lake Tawakoni SP. I had to put
the cruise control on to control my speed down the I20. Going 70mph
never seemed so slow. Thank goodness I was there not that long ago
and still remember how to get there. If you know meI have no memory
of where places are located or how to get home from them. My
instructions from TP are "if you get there before metackle anyone in
our way to the bus." Hmmm..should be interesting.

Upon arrival to the park I jump from the car, scan the parking lot to
see if I recognize any cars from Bastrop. Nopethank goodness. No
tackling necessary. TP pulls in next to me within minutes of my
arrival. I leave my kids and the baby at the car and the rest of us
just about run down the trail. After walking and sinking in poison
ivy/oak I we make it to the tree.

SO AT 4:12 THE TEXAS TOUR BUS 2 has been busjacked.
