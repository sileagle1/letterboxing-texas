Lucy
-- Yes, it's actually very easy to post clues on both AQ and LBNA simultaneously, if you begin with the AQ site to add the clues AND click the box that says Add to LBNA, and also click the box that says Host Clues on AQ (unless you plan to link to your own or another site to actually list the clues).  I usually miss at least one of those boxes and wind up doing it all two or three times, but that's because I have an attention problem!  If you read the prompts you can easily do it right the first time and list your boxes on both sites, or if you've already listed them on LBNA, then just list it on AQ also so others who use that atlas feature will find your boxes listed there, too!  -- Viewfinder
