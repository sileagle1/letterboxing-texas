I read all the notes today, I don't think anyone answered your question about the Texas Tour Buses. So, being the friendly sort that I am, I'm going to attempt it. Y'all, please, correct me if I am wrong in any part.
 
Somebody carved a bus and driver, they put them in a large Rubbermaid (or other type) of plastic box with camo tape on it. They hid the box and posted the clues only in this Yahoo Group with "Texas Tour Bus" in the Subject Line (This allows a quick search of those posts to follow the bus). Boxer #2 sees the clues on the group and goes and finds the box. Instead of leaving it there, they take it and add a passenger (A Texan). After stamping/logging in and adding their passenger, they take the box out and hide it somewhere else and add the clues to this group so the hunt can continue. The bus is not allowed to leave Texas (something about the driver not having the proper papers and license to cross the state line, j/k).
 
You can search the archives for Subject: Texas Tour Bus and it'll show alot of the clues (I'm not good at that kind of stuff, but I have done it once to read the clues). Some of them are hilarious. I would be entertained just reading them. There are two buses out there now. I haven't heard much about them, but one down South somewhere, just waiting. But, I bet it doesn't last past this weekend before heading out to a new destination.
Happy Hunting,
