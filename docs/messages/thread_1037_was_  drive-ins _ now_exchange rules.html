This is a little different than a usual series. In this format, one
letterboxer carves the stamp and creates the box and another
letterboxer places it in his/her city or town and then serves as
a "foster parent"--doing maintenance as need be. The idea is to
give boxers in one part of the state (or country) the opportunity to
enjoy stamp images from letterboxers whose boxes they may not
otherwise be able to find. The Drive-in theme may not work with
this, but at this point we're just brainstorming.

The point is to have fun with it and to get to know other boxers a
little better.
