I have updated the Atlas to include the newest letterboxes from TeamKing, Hawk, krazykatzen, Kantexan of the Samurai Stampers, and Celtic Lions.
 
Also have added additional links for boxes listed with Ryan Carpenter's AtlasQuest
 
Enhanced to Group Display - Now when you move your mouse over a stickpin for a group or click on it for more details, it will list the names of the letterboxes that make up the group.  The mouseover display acts strange when the group has over 100.  Just click on the pin to view the details on the right side of the window.
