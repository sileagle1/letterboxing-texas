Terors of Telge and other treats!!!

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-10-15 11:36:31 UTC-05:00
LSS and I made a quick stop at the Terrors of Telge yesterday on our way
home to Dallas from Galveston...we felt a little like kids sneaking into
their parents' bedroom and peeking in the closet at the Christmas presents
long before the big event;-)

You Houstonians are in for a real Halloween treat! This is a great park - I
couldn't believe that we were surrounded by suburbia. Beautiful big pines,
interesting trails along a creek, and we lucked out - the weather was
gorgeous...we actually even enjoyed our first encounter with the notorious
banana spiders! (Sorry, TeamKing - we really did think it was cute!!!)

We stopped at the park around noon and found some great boxes: "WANTED: A
Few People.."(GREAT CARVING!), "Park Angel"(CAN'T BELIEVE HOW LONG THIS NEAT
BOX HAS BEEN AROUND), "Puppy Love"( GREAT HIDING SPOT), "True Goblin" ( WOW
- VERY CREATIVE IDEA AND STAMP), "Mr. Bones"(CLEVER, CLEVER SERIES- AND
GREAT CARVING)( 3 out of 4 boxes - couldn't find the 3rd), "Ghost of
John"(LOVED THESE CLUES!), and "I Love Bananas"( VERY COOL & GREAT STAMP -
TEAMKING SCORES AGAIN!).

Also, we left a ghostly guest for your Halloween activities - hope she won't
dampen your spirits...!

Our only frustration was that we ran out of time and had to leave Telge
without hunting the remaining boxes - but we did meet up with TeamKing and
enjoyed a delightful visit with her - actually, we so enjoyed her that we
lost track of time and hit Houston traffic right at rush hour! Oh well - it
was worth it!

We're driving back through Houston in early December, and really looking
forward to hitting the Woodlands boxes by RunsForChocolate!

Thanks to all you Houstonian Haunters for the fun!
Ruby Tuesday
