From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2006-10-23 02:39:17 UTC
LSQ and I went back to Burroughs Park this afternoon and logged into
19 more boxes (and a hitchhiker) that we didn't have time to find
yesterday. I was a little surprised to find that many of the boxes
had only a few entries, Most of the ones on Walk 4 had only one entry
(Daisy and Onslow). That was the "mystery journey" and if you missed
it, you missed some fantastic boxes and great stamps. We loved the
felt-wrapped stamps (some of them even had hand-made felt pouches for
the stamps) and the imaginative log books. The contributers obviously
put in a lot time on their boxes and I'd like to sincerely thank them
for that. The stamp(s) for "A Series of Unfortunate Events" by Camp
Fire Lady from Washington was a work of art. That was probably my
favorite box of the weekend. A Big TEXAS THANKS from us for
generously sharing your talents with us.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
