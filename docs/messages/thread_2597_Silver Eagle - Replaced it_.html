Silver Eagle - deleted your last message so sending in the blind to
the list. Please reply when you get this.

We got her done!

The wonderful Garmin GPS found us a walmart. Inside was lock-n-lock,
a spiral-wound 50 piece 3x5 card book, zip-lock freezer bags, and
mechanical pencils.

With the help of a passing police officer with a flashlight (who loved
our dogs BTW), we ventured into the darkness to replace your water-
logged box. I couldn't stuff the old log-book in the lock-n-lock.
You could squeeze a cup of water from it. So I have it, will carry it
to El Paso with us. There is NOTHING left of the stamps in it. I can
mail it to you if you like. Swamp water and all!

Then we took James to Joe's Crab Shack for dinner, and sent him on his
way to Ft. Hood. We will leave for El Paso at 0500 Monday.

Take care sir.

Mike.
