From: Bonnie Lind (bonnielind1@hotmail.com) | Date: 2013-12-31 17:18:54 UTC-06:00
TALE 11 stands for the 11th Texas Annual Letterboxing Event, an event hosted by Silver Eagle and Baby Bear.  It is held the first weekend in March and has been held in different parks throughout Texas.  This year is will be held in Huntsville State Park, Huntsville, TX—a bit north of Houston.  A number of folks camp/RV/stay in hotels in the area and we try to meet for dinner on Friday night.  It is great fun and I look forward to it all year!  It is a blast to meet up with people who you’ve “met” by seeing sig stamps in logbooks. 
 
Here are links for more information on both the event and the Friday evening dinner—and the chance to sign up.  These links may open up even more questions for you—please ask away!  I hope you can make it to the events—I look forward to meeting you.
 
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?eventId=2840
 
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?eventId=3041
