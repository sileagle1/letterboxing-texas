Looks like our Texas group is growing by leaps and bounds! This is
really exciting - we're looking forward to lots more boxes to find!

We have started up another order for the Lone Star Letterboxing
T-shirts, and only need a total of 12 shirts to submit it to the
silk-screening company.

The current shirt is desert-washed pigment dyed denim blue with the
Texas Letterboxing logo on the left chest. Above and below the logo in a
coordinating bright green is: "Lone Star Letterboxing".

The shirts range in size from Youth Small to Adult XXXL, $9.00 per shirt
up to Adult L. Bigger sizes are a dollar extra per additional size. The
packing & shipping for padded mailers, postage & delivery confirmation
came to $3.50 for one shirt and a dollar extra per additional shirt.

These shirts are also available in other "desert-washed" colors and I
plan to order a couple in dusty forest-green for my family. The logo
would also look good on a dusty royal blue or peach color. It would not
show up on lime green because of the outlining. Other colors would
probably clash a bit with the orange, green and royal blue logo!

Please email me off-list if you would like to place an order:
celticlions@comcast.net

And please bear in mind that these shirts may take a month or more to be
delivered - we need to reach an order of 12, then it should be 10
business days for turnaround on the printing and a few days for me to
package & mail them!
