We've posted the rules for the CLUE game in the files section - it is a
work-in-progress as our family tries to debate the best way to do this!

One note: If you enjoy coloring your stamp image with markers, you may want
to just bring a black ink pad and stamp quickly - save the coloring for
later! From what I hear of Monster Mash, that was a small complaint - folks
who were taking quite a while with artistic touches!
Some of the stamps submitted would lend themselves very nicely to stamping
with black, and using markers or wooden colors later. Some are solid images,
and would lend themselves to stamping with solid colors.
We will provide an ink pad with each stamp, but no guarantees that it will
be your favorite color!
