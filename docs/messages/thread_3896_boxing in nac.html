Hi all,

Today we went out for a walk on this wonderful beautiful sunny warm (winter?) day. We were just moseying around downtown and decided to check on a box that is there and we were so delighted to discover that a whole group of boxers had just found it yesterday! And, these were names we did not recognize, even though they said "Nac". It is really a fun delight to me, to have "cousins" out there in the world, whom I have not met, and may never meet, but still have something in common with.

"having something in common with" complete strangers is a very gives-me-hope-for-the-world sort of feeing, I think.

I love letterboxing! :)

ashby
