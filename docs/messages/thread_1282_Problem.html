From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-06 01:19:20 UTC
I think I can officially declare myself an out of control
letterboxer. I have never been the member of a chat group before I
began letterboxing. I am now a member of five different LB groups and
am awaiting approval to a sixth. I have never thought about
an "online diary" before, but now I have my own blog dedicated solely
to the sport of letterboxing. I carefully calculate how far away
letterboxes are, and how many I can pick up between POINT A and POINT
B as to make the most of my letterboxing time. I secretly wish that
on bus trips that I will make with my students this year, we could
leave an hour or so early so that I could make time to do a little
boxing. I have never had an interest in using sharp tools, but now I
cuddle up on the couch on a rainy day and carve various shapes out of
rubber.

Thanks to all of you who have planted boxes for me to find. If I
haven't found them yet, don't worry...I plan to hunt them down and
stamp in!

Lucy Locket
