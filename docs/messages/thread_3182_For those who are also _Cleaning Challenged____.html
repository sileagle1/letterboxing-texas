For those who are also "Cleaning Challenged"...

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-11-14 23:08:46 UTC-06:00
We've had this talk before, about how all the cool things we do are much more fun than cleaning, and how we have too much clutter...
 
WELL - I cleaned my kitchen counter this evening and all that is on it now is stuff that should be on it.  That does NOT include the glue gun, the multiple pairs of scissors, the large roll of brown paper, the several all-but-burned-up-candles, the bag of carving tools, and the multiple loose pieces out of my backpacks.  (I blame the guys I live with for all the clutter in the kitchen, but in my honest moments I have to admit the cluttered kitchen belongs to me - the cluttered living room belongs to them and their backpacks and camping equipment).  I wiped the countertop down with hot soapy water and then cloroxed it, and then (in true FlyLady style) I shined my sink.  I now am contemplating pushing the piles of junk off my kitchen table, climbing on top of it and stretching out and going to sleep on it, just so I can wake up gazing on my clean countertops!
