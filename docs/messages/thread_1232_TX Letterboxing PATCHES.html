Good morning, guys!

I just uploaded a file to our group which gives the information on the new
TX patches, including prices (please check my math!).
You may wonder why I haven't rounded up the prices to some sane figures!
Well, I really do not want to be making even a few cents profit off our
letterboxing group - but I'd like to toss out an idea for future discussion:
next time we, as a group, decide to produce shirts, patches or whatever, it
might be useful to round up the costs and throw the difference into a "pot"
for reserving the group areas for gatherings. Or to buy prizes for games at
gatherings - or drinks - or firewood - whatever!
But for now, you guys will have to tolerate writing checks for odd numbers
of dollars and cents!!!

Ruby T.
(The contact info for ordering patches is included in the file for future
reference)
