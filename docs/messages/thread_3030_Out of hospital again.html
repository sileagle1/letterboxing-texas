From: Lois (lois_adams37@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-21 16:38:55 UTC
Got out of the hospital Friday Aug. 17th again after staying for a 6
days. I had a bleeding ulcer that they fixed but I fell while I was

to that. It was reported to me that the stamp was broke. We are planing
to go to the Athens Mini Meet. It will be so glad to meet the new
letterboxer here in this area and also to see others that come.
