Thanksgiving in Arkansas - Baby Bear

From: Baby Bear (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-27 17:29:22 UTC
Looks like we were not the only ones in Arkansas this
Thanksgiving. We headed out Thursday morning, plowing through East
Texas to DeKalb, where we visited Hoss and his letterbox, then on to
SE Oklahoma and Beavers Bend S.P. Found 5 boxes, and placed one.
Also had turkey lunch at their facility. What a beautiful park!
Then on to the Talimena Byway, but we went west on it to Talimena
State Park, and found box there. Then we finished the day driving
up to Fort Smith and staying in hotel (weather was so great, we wish
we would have camped this year like we usually do). Friday we went
east to Mt Nebo S.P., and did some hiking. Then south to Petit Jean
S.P., finding Petit Polecat box and hiking down to Cedar Falls. My
wife and I have visited this park about 8 times now, and we still
love it. Fall colors were great. We then headed west to Mount
Magazine S.P., where we checked into the new lodge. It was only
opened this May, and is one of the best lodges we have ever stayed
in. What a view from our 3rd story room!!! Saturday we got up and
hiked to the top of Arkansas, and picked up Silver Eagle's box. We
then explored the rest of the park until noon, when we headed for
Paris and Altus, the Arkansas Wine Country! We visited all 5
wineries (only 6 in state that I know of), and had a great time.
The lady at Post Winery rememebered us from last year (that may not
be a good thing). All the tasting is FREE, and at Post they will
let you taste it ALL (we made it through 12 different wines). The
last winery was Chateux Aux Arc, where again they remembered us from
last year(seems to be a trend here...hope it is because we came so
far). Then we headed back to the lodge to dry out, and had a great
dinner. Sunday we headed home, having breakfast at Queen Wilhemena
S.P., then driving the Talimena byway (finding S.E's 2 boxes) to OK,
then south to Texas, through Tyler, Palestine and on to Fort Boggy
State Park near Centerville. Found box there, and left "Boggy Creek
Monster" - I carved this box 3 years ago, but only recently found
out they finally developed this park. Then back home after dinner
in Magnolia. We had a great trip, found all the boxes we looked
for, and really enjoyed the new lodge at Mount Magazine!!! Arkansas
really is a good escape from the flat land to the "mountains". Take
Care.
