From: JOY (TeamTexUS@houston.rr.com) | Date: 2005-09-25 14:53:04 UTC-05:00
Well, it would depend. If I was going to stay with friends, and figured I was just escaping a bad storm, and might come back to some damage or maybe no damage (I think a lot of people left because they were afraid they would lose power and didn't want to suffer the heat and inconvenience) and knew I 'd have some free time, I'd take it if I could. If I thought I would have to stay in a shelter or I was quite sure I would be coming back to a pile of rubble or a lake where my house used to be, I would leave it.

I've gotten to where I take my LB'ing stuff pretty
much everywhere. I do need to get better organized so
that I have everything I need in one place to grab and
go. Right now everything is piled in a couple of bags
together and I have to dig around to get just my
personal stamp and so forth to go hunting. I've
gotten to where I also keep my library card with my
lb'ing stuff so I can just about always check in at a
library and get privileges based on my good standing
at my home library. My LSS is still a little puzzled
by that logic - he gets it, but he's not convinced
that's the best use of my library card. He'll get it
some day - I have faith in him!
