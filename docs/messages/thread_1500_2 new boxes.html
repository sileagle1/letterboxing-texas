I have 2 new boxes out. Called, Ivy and Humane Society of East Texas.

Ivy - Find a parking spot a long the square. You must pay to park anytime before 5PM. This spot is busy so I suggest going after 6PM. After you park, walk to the historical marker of Tyler behind the fountain. Walk to the right and around the flower bed. At the end of the flower bed face the flag pole. Take about 12 steps torwards it. Now you are at a lamp post. The box is at the base of the lamp post. Color: Green

Humane Society of East Texas - 
 
Hours: Tuesday - Sunday 11 AM - 1 PM and 2PM - 5PM

To the box: From SSE Loop 323 go East on Erwin (2767). Drive 1/2 mile, then turn left on 886 at the yellow sign. After you pass the cemetary there is a small, maybe, four car parking lot for the Adoption Center. Park there and and go inside. Be sure to close the gate behind you, you don't want the dogs to get out. Ask at the counter for the letterbox. Do your stamping and look around. If you would like you could adopt a dog or cat.
