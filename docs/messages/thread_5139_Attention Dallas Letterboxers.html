From: Michael Ridout (michael_ridout@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-01-31 06:04:11 UTC-06:00
I feel like I need to start with my standard qualifying statement that I know I don't have many plants under my belt – at least not enough to qualify me to chime in on this conversation with some of you heavy hitters…

I know that Jerry Rashall, the ranger at Village Creek S.P. checks LBNA and Atlasquest; he contacted me after I had placed a box there and asked me to go through the permitting process. Once I did, he walked the clues and checked out the box and approved it no problem. I appreciate that he was aware that I had planted, and then he reached out to me and helped me get in compliance. I just planted another at V.C.S.P. this weekend, and I went ahead and filled out the permit while I was camping and left it with him.

If I knew that personnel at all parks were that aware and cooperative, I would think I would fill out permits every time. I guess that if I were to head to, say, Enchanted Rock this weekend, I would be inclined to ask folks in this group what their experience was with staff there.

Phoenix8
