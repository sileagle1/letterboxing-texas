Busy Day on the Trail

From: SandiBox (sandibox@msn.com) | Date: 2008-11-16 03:19:38 UTC
SoccerChik and I haven't done much letterboxing since we returned
from Portland, and since I love boxing in cooler weather, we decided
today was too nice to pass up. And the TX Tour Bus was just sitting
at Telge waiting for someone to wander by and grab it. So we called
Pug Momma to see if she was game... she always is... and off we went.

Our first stop was at Telge Park, where we found the TTB stranded on
the side of the road. We didn't want that poor bus full of
passengers to freeze tonight, so we helped them back to our car.
Then we found the seven new boxes that had been planted at Telge
since our visit last year and checked on my boxes. All the finds
were in good shape, except my Vlad to Meet you box had been taken out
of its hidey hole by a critter and was laying out in the open about
10 feet away. Glad I took the time to check on it!

It was lunch-time, so we headed off toward Tomball, and stopped at
Los Cucos for some delicious Mexican food and their fantastic salsa.
(Runs For Chocolate, are you listening?) We were so full when we
left that we all wanted to curl up and take a nap, but we soldiered
on to Burroughs Park. There are quite a few boxes from The Great
Book Adventure that we'd never found. The trails there still have
quite a bit of damage from Ike, and several times we had to climb
over downed trees to reach the letterboxes. Most of the boxes we
found ranged from damp to down-right soggy. It is interesting to note
that lock-in-locks were slighly damp, but any other type of container
was pretty wet. I'll send updates to each of the planters, but if you
have boxes there, you might consider checking on them. There are
still about 10 boxes in the Tomball area we haven't found, so if next
weekend is as nice as today, we might head back over there and do
some maintenance if possible. I also want to plant another letterbox
in my "Thanksgiving in Plymouth" series, and one at a cool little
store we visited in downtown Tomball.

Found:
1 TTB
12 Letterboxes
1 tick at Telge Park - beware!

We had a great time. Thanks to everyone who plants letterboxes for
us to find!

SandiBox
