Re: [letterboxingtexas] Re: Home sweet home/ first finders

From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2005-03-20 23:33:38 UTC-06:00
Ahh. You just have to be REALLY stealthy! I got the Kemah Stingray box
on Labor Day - which was a hot, sunny day and half of Houston was in
Kemah and in the water fountains. People were sitting across from me
and next to me. BUT, I got it!

Sometimes the 'putting back' is the hardest part. Does anyone else
notice that?

Glad you hung in there to find it. I had a lot of that on Saturday.
When I went to get the Horseshoe box, I found a man in the tree digging
around. Another boxer, I thought. But then I noticed he didn't have a
bag or anything with him. Seems like he just spotted the box from the
trail and went to investigate. I wasn't sure what to do. Finally I
decided to just jump in and explain it all before he carted it away! He
seemed pretty interested too. Whew. Then I had to wait for about 3
groups of people to pass on the Baby Brought the Stork - as they were
walking toward the corner where the box was located and all spread out.
I must have sat there for at least 10 mins or so.

All fun though! Was FF on Hawk's Aaalligator box and Birds of a
Feather's LA box.

TeamKing
