From: JuneMcAllister (nfmoon@mindspring.com) | Date: 2005-05-02 19:59:31 UTC-04:00
The one that natty bumpo did in Pennsylvania (and I think they are all like this) was a race. There had to be at least 2 people in each car. I  think that may have been to keep the number of cars down for the area they were in. It was a really big park I think, that they could drive in--there may even have been only so many cars allowed. But if you all do it by going on the highway, you wouldn't have to do that I guess. The Red Hat Boxers had a group stamp. I don't know if each person stamped their personal stamp also. I doubt it.  I don't know what a group of people all with their own stamps did. Maybe each carload had a group stamp? If it's a race, I would think they wanted to go as fast as possible. There was a prize for the carload  of people who finished first, second and third. I couldn't go but everyone said it was a lot of fun.
 
The thing that makes it so much fun is the fact that it's a race!!
 
You could e-mail him. He seems like a nice guy. I don't think he would mind ansering any questions you have. If you do a placer search on LBNA site he'll come up.
