From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-08-22 12:51:08 UTC-05:00
If you feel as though I've been posting Happy Birthday messages every other
day this month - you're very nearly right! We seem to have an abundance of
August babies!
But this is a very special Texas letterboxer, whom we need to really cheer -
BabyBear was one half of the dynamic duo who really seeded our state with
boxes when there was virtually no such thing as Texas letterboxing.
Our family has always looked forward to his boxes - great and often humorous
stamps with such character!

Happy Birthday, BabyBear!
