Hi Sherry!
I buy most of my supplies at Hobby Lobby. The rubberstamp carving
supplies are in the area with the calligraphy supplies. I have the
very small, two nib, Speedball set, but use an exacto knife more. I
did buy an extra nib - the very small point one, and like it too.
For carving medium I have only used the pink stuff and then
Staedtler MasterCarve 'Cuts Like Butter'....it really does. ;) That
is my favorite, but has gotten almost impossible to find in Corpus
Christi.

I have never been to the Kemah Boardwalk, but our son and his family
went last year and really liked it. Good luck with a letterbox
there. It sounds like a great idea.

You wrote, "And once we are ready to plant it, we just register at
the LBNA website and enter the clues, right?". Actually, you should
hide the letterbox first and then post the clues. This allows for
last minute changes in details. Also my experience has been that a
couple of times I have been all ready to plant a box in a certain
place, gone there, and then found that there is absolutely no place
where I can put the box in the area I intended.

Happy Trails!
