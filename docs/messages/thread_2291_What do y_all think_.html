As I just posted, we went to a folk festival this evening. It
actually started last night and ran all day today, but we only got to
go for a few hours.

It was held at the site of a renaissance festival that is held in East
Texas each year in April and May, . There are quite a few permanent
structures relating to the faire, so the location goes idle much of
the year.

As I hung out and relaxed, I realized how much like my letterboxing
friends folk music folks are. Wouldn't it be cool to have a gathering
coinciding with a small folk festival? The site is closer to Tyler,
which is still pretty much box-poor, although there are lots of places
to put boxes. Imagine mixing music and boxing - what do y'all think?
