There was a saying in the Air Force that the "best two assignments were the one you were coming from and the one you are going to." (I guess it's just human nature to never be satisfied with where you are, right?)
 
That saying applies to my dream letterbox: it was the one I just found and the one I am hoping to find next!
 
Yesterday was a perfect letterboxing day and we had a wonderful time scrambling up hills, ducking behind trees, getting ink all over our hands and mud on our knees, then trying to muster up the dignity to walk into Whataburger for lunch.
 
My next dream box is my next find!
 
Happy boxing.
