From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-21 19:47:13 UTC
Alright, I am such a goob. I have the Daily Grind stamp and teeny-
weeny log book ready. The first meeting of the B-HACK (Boxing
Houston-area Coffee Klatch--name suggestions freely taken) can now
be set up.

Just so you'll know, the Daily Grind in on Washington Ave., between
Shepherd & Yale. It is open all day and into the night (see how
firm I am on their hours?). They make a great breakfast (I love
their migas and the cheese jalapeno grits), including a killer eggs
florentine for our new vegetarian member (assuming you eat eggs).
They also serve lunch and I think dinner and sometimes in the
evening have music, not that I have ever been. My kids like the
waffles. On the weekend, service can be slooooow, but the food is
good, so Asterix picked it for his father's day breakfast. They
make an excellent latte, and my kids like the cocoa.

Parking is a bit of a trick. There is a small lot, and if it is
full, you can park on Washington anytime after 9 and all day on
Sunday. There was a falling out with the church next door and
customers can no longer park in the church's lot.

So, who's the organizer? It's a sacrifice, but I can hide the box
tomorrow when I pick up my java fix.
