From: Lighthouse Luvin Momma (pyle.of.adventurers@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-04-14 20:41:13 UTC-05:00
Ok, so I figured we have lurked around enough and its time to introduce ourselves. I'm kind of nervous about this but here goes anyway. I am Lighthouse Luvin Momma and I usually travel with at least part of if not all of our 6 kids, they are: Heartfelt Melody 13, Dolphin Darlin almost 13, Lil Miss Ladybug 10, Happy Trails 9, Hands On 7, and Baby Bee 5. The MR of the family does not share mine and the kids enthusiasm for letterboxing but he is glad we have found something that we all enjoy for the most part. We havent convinced Baby Bee that traipsing thru the woods and getting dirty is a good thing yet but, shes coming around to the joys of dirt and ink stained hands lol.
 
We have been lurking for way to long I admit that, however I just wasnt sure where to jump in if that makes sense. I so wanted to go to the TALE event, but it was to far away for us, and then I would love to go to the Gusher event this weekend and had even planned on going at first till I realized that it was the same weekend that the MR and I had planned a getaway without any kids.... Those kidfree times are few and far between.. Lol
 
We have planted several letterboxes here in Palestine, one in Tyler that has been found several times now, and just this last weekend my friend, and her daughter and my daughter Lil Miss Ladybug planted a box for us down in Spring. I'm afraid that my stamps are nowhere near the same quality as everyone elses but I am sure having fun making them, as much fun as I have finding everyone else's lol.
 
Hope everyone is doing well!

At this point we all just took off and headed back to the car splashing thru the puddles...
