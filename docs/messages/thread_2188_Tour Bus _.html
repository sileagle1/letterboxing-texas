Well, Howard hughes is so darn grumpy. Impossible to please!

My hubby worked on him yesterday until called away to the greens and
worked again today- he is finished but Howard is nothing like what I
had hoped for. His image is not inverse as I had hoped. More inking
and some refining and the image is better but nothing like the
fabulous Joan Crawford or Carol Burnett.Now delima, I can not hurt
hubby's feelings and kick Howard off the bus, as this was quite a
challenge and Howard's hair is amazing- a definate hair club for men
poster child - but after consultation with a trusted source I have
decided it is best if the bus remains parked until another passenger
joins Howard (he never gets out anyway- old hypochondriac) . So we
will add a new rider more representative of Mike's true carving skills.

I will head it on soon to new territories.The driver is confused, he
called "All Aboard" and there is no gas available so everyone hoped
off to enjoy a day in the beautiful East Texas sunshine- not a one of
them has offered to help clean the shed though.

Tracy
