From: Wes (wzub21@aol.com) | Date: 2004-04-12 13:50:53 UTC
Hi Gang. We took a little camping trip out to Bay City and found
Silver Eagle's Bird Superbowl Letterbox. Some "creature" built a
nest where it was hidden and dislodged the microbox. But looking
around in the weeds and tall grass, we did manage to find it.
Evidently, we were the first to find it, as there were no other
stamps in the book. We re-hid it, again and hopefully no other
critters will get to it.

The two ladies at the Kiosk, were very excited about having a
letterbox at the bird sanctuary, and became very enthusiastic about
letterboxing in general. We may have a couple of new converts.

When are we going to have another "event"?

Warm Regardes to all
