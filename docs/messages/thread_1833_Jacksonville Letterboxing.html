Jacksonville Letterboxing

From: josephine_tx (josephine_tx@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-23 14:03:46 UTC
Yesterday, Ashby, myself, Random and Enaj, all of us went
letterboxing before going to the Deaf Festival they were having in
Jacksonville. I have to say it was a delightful little trip. We
did not find all the letterboxes, and we think some of them must be
missing.

POOR POOR Enaj! We split up the search among the four of us,
letting each person have a turn looking for a box, and each time it
was Enaj's turn to find the box...we didn't find it! And that was
after a good long while of looking!

Also, while in Jacksonville, I finally dropped off The Cichlid Guy's
hitchhiker "Traveling Pumpkins," and I also wish that i had waited
and dropped off the "In this Sign" hitchhiker there in honor of the
Deaf Festival...but oh well. AND we found the "H. H. Chic"
hitchhiker by Tx2chick.

So all in all, it was a great outing that ended well, and not only
that Enaj and Random held up surprizingly well despite the long day!

Josephine
