In a message dated 8/2/2007 4:47:43 AM Central Daylight Time, letterboxingtexas@yahoogroups.com writes:
I would probably avoid any of the boxes that are located on the north shore of Lake Grapevine (Flower Mound, etc).
I would amend that to include all the letterboxes on any trail around the shores of Lake Grapevine, whether the north shore trail, the Rockledge Park trail near the dam going north, or the letterboxes at Bob Jones Park on the south shores.  The lake is no respecter of shores and trails currently - while a few of those boxes are probably gone, others are likely still in place but they're pretty much all inaccessible for now.  However, there are lots of boxes listed in Arlington and elsewhere in the DFW area that aren't near that lake!  Happy Hunting.
