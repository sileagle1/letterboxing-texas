From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2013-07-11 12:23:24 UTC

Re: Davis Mountains State park..

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2013-07-11 15:56:12 UTC
The cable box is located by the picnic table slab, which is on the opposite side of the trailer from the electric, water and sewer, so you need a long coax cable, unless you want to put the tv on the picnic table and plug directly into it. You just plug it in and it should work, but it didn't work for me, although it worked in my brother's motorhome. I was okay with that, because we didn't want the kids to be stuck inside watching tv anyway. Only the sites with full hookups have cable. You get whatever cable channels are available and the local channels from the stations in Midland and Odessa.

Boots Tex
