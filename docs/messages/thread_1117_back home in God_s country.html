back home in God's country

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-07-27 21:51:20 UTC-05:00
Hey you all!  We just arrived back in the great state of Texas after a long road trip to Michigan's upper penninsula...a beautiful, breathtaking spot to say the least.  We found a few boxes while there, in some of the most wonderful spots, and with stamps that rival even Baby Bear and Monkeytoes carving.  My little Texans couldn't brave the cold waters of Lake Superior (spoiled as they are by Gulf waters) but they hiked their little feet off...much easier to do in temps. of around 60-70...imagine being cold at night tent camping in July!  We also planted a box called the lost Texans, so if you ever happen to be in Marquette MI you'll have to go hunting!  Since there are over 800 messages in my in box I fear I will never read I'll just tell you all ahead of time if I don't reply to something it is not out of rudeness, but I'll never be able to read all that!  Seems a lot has happened since we left, or maybe it is just so hot everyone is writing instead of boxing.  Either way, we are happy to be back in Texas, that's for sure!  -Mother of Five
