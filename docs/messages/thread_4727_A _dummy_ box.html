A "dummy" box

From: grannybutterfly06 (grannybutterfly06@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-02-28 17:50:04 UTC
Someone from New England I believe sent me a small laminated card that you could put in a dummy box to place in the exact location where you take the box...then move away with the real thing to stamp and return.

It gives someone who is looking for the box a chance to know they found the right spot but that its off being logged by someone. I haven't ever used it but thought it was a great idea. The laminated card can be placed in a pouch or box to carry with you all the time for things like that.

I like the "planning a hike for the grandkids" routine too to explain the notebook, pack, etc.
