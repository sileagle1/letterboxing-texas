From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2007-07-14 21:06:45 UTC-04:00
We've had so many new letterboxers reporting finds, placing boxes, and showing up on this list in the past few months, it's very exciting!  It also occurred to me (and to PuddleSplasher, my sister), that many of you in the greater Dallas-Ft. Worth area might not be aware of the DFW Metroboxers site (http://groups.yahoo.com/group/metroboxers).  It was created by Ruby Tuesday of the CelticLions for our local gathering in January at Lake Grapevine, the ManyMeeseMiniMeet, and is monitored by RT, PS, and me (VF).  It's not as big or as active as the Texas list, but it's a good place to talk to local boxers about local issues or information, and it gets pretty active whenever a new gathering is in the works (one little one might be coming up  in August).  If you live anywhere  in the DFW metroplex-- pretty much anywhere from the Red River to Waco, or maybe even Austin, heck, the Rio Grande, and from Weatherford  to Greenville, or as far east and west and you care to consider-- you may want to join the Metroboxers list, too.   Welcome to check it out!
