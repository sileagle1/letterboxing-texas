Re: [letterboxingtexas] 35 mm film canisters

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-26 06:00:20 UTC-07:00
I am the QUEEN of film canisters! I use them for
everything, especially in scouts. At Walmart, they
actually stop me if they see me walking by without
stopping at the film counter and ask me if I'm gonna
pick up canisters...Of course, with scouts, if I need
one canister, I need forty.

Here's one pretty cool use for them: make a kid's
first aid kit for them to carry. Put inside it a
bandaid and an antiseptic wipe. Poke holes in one
side and run a leather thong through it to make a
necklace. They think they are pretty special, having
their own first aid kit.

Here's a non-letterboxing related one, but pretty
darned fun: Alka Seltzer Rockets. You have to use
the ones with the lid that snaps around the outside,
like the black ones with gray lids. Fill it about
halfway with water, drop a half a tab of alka seltzer
in it and snap the lid on quickly, set it down on its
lid and step away...cool stuff!

Lucy
