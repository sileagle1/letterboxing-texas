Do you guys remember when every week or so it would seem as though the
incredible HAWK had swooped in and planted more boxes in Houston???

Well, East Texas may have its own version of HAWK...the TXLuvBug. A week ago

through Jacksonville, Rusk and the surrounding area.
We couldn't believe how many boxes TxLuvBug has planted in her short career!
Wow!
We especially loved the storyline of "A Family Affair" and the delightful
surprise of "Water from the Rocks" - this is one of those neat spots you'd
probably drive right by without the promise of a letterbox!
Unfortunately, (as she already has posted), a couple of her boxes were
missing, but planted in a park which we really enjoyed anyway.

Thanks for adding to the game!
