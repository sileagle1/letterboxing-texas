Letterboxing report - Fort Worth

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-02-06 11:01:14 UTC-06:00
With our usual impeccable timing, Stillwater, Vandal & I drove to Fort Worth
yesterday, thinking it would be a perfect day to hunt for Kantexan's
Mondrian series...it was a little chilly and we figured the area around the
Kimbell Art Museum and Will Rogers Coliseum would not be very populated.

Have you ever seen Fort Worth on the last weekend of the LiveStock Show???
The good thing was that so many people were milling around, we were barely
noticeable!!!(Although the mounted police are a little intimidating when
you're skulking in the bushes)
This series of 5 boxes by Kantexan & the SamuraiStampers has got to be at
the top of our "most creative idea" list! We loved it. (We were unable to
find the last box in the series, but only Kantexan would be able to confirm
that it is missing)

I'm so grateful for all the Texas letterboxers who plant fantastic boxes for
us to find!
