I buy a 9 x 12" Strathmore Sketchbook of 100 sheets of 60lb. paper from
the art department and cut it down. I can bind about 40 pages into each
logbook without it being bulky, but it has a nice tooth for stamping,
and is thick enough not to bleed through.
