Hot day for boxing!

From: The Carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-06-08 21:56:00 UTC-05:00
We went out to Tomball today with great plans of finding all 3 boxes in Burroughs park.  However the heat and 3 toddlers with their minds on nothing but the swimming pool (plus 7 other kids who didn't complain quite as much as the toddlers!) Limited us to only the True North? box...found alive and well.  Actually, thinking about what a circus my sister in law and I are when we go boxing with all the kids it is a miracle we have never been caught.  I guess people are too scared of that many kids in one place to notice what we are doing!  Anyway, we somehow managed to plant a new box in the park today...Harry's Headache.  So all you Harry Potter fans (and I know you are out there) brush up on your HP trivia, and I will try my best to post the clues tonight.  By the way, for those that searched for the May The Force Be With You box, Teamking found it, so its really there.  We have 2 more in the series to plant, will try to do it Friday afternoon.  -Mother of Five

Actually, the reason we made the Harry box is becaus I am getting sick of my
