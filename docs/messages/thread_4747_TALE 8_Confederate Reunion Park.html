Hello All,

After all the greatness of TALE 8, I’ve had a bit of a disappointment. My Sons of Confederate Veterans box has been unceremoniously ousted from Confederate Reunion Park in Mexia.  This brings up several issues:

 

First, if you are considering putting a box there, you might want to reconsider.

Second, to the best of my knowledge there are two other boxes there—so tread lightly if going after them.

Third, if you happen to know of a fitting home for my Sons of Confederate Veterans box, would you please contact me?

 

Thanks,
