YES!  I just got back in touch with a computer this morning after arriving back home about 1 a.m. this morning!
 
Jonah (how easy is THAT to make a sig stamp???) was born Friday evening at 7:50.  He was 8 lb 8 oz, and 20" long.  Lots of dark hair.  He seems to be very serene, which is not surprising given that both his mama and his daddy are very laid back. 
 
The family went home from the hospital yesterday about Noon.  Fearless Fox and I , along with Uncle Lightning, spent much of the weekend cleaning and washing clothes and making food so they could enjoy their first days at home without worrying about home drudgery.  In the midst of all that, we spent lots of time at the hospital holding Baby Jonah.  Lightning I think was the proudest of us all!
