Scarecrow Festival

From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2005-08-27 11:52:29 UTC-05:00
We've been going to the Scarecrow Festival in Chappell Hill for years
now. It's a really nice drive out there and it's so small town - a
nice break from Houston! All arts & crafts. So if you like
decorations for Halloween or Thanksgiving or Christmas, bring your $
$. Also, the whole town gets into the mood and if you drive around
the residential streets you'll see the houses all decorated - sort of
like driving around looking at Christmas lights only Halloween/Fall
theme. It's sort of an annual tradition for us.

Date is October 8-9 this year.

TeamKing
