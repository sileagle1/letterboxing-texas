Today (a nice COOL day in north Texas!)2 of the granddaughters and I
found the Red-Eared Slider LB in Arlington planted by Urban

last visitors to that LB.) This marks my 100th and now, 101st LB!
Hooray! Break out the confetti! (and guess who had been the FF of

something about turtles! -- ALSO we pulled a LB today for rescue

little boys playing war who were all around it and just hadn't yet
noticed it, although it was lying actually lying out in view. We hope
we were stealthy enough in removing it that they didn't see it, but we
took it with us. I will replace it and possibly move it. So hold off
on finding that one if you haven't already - I'll put it back ASAP. --
