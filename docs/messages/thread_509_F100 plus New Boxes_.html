F100 plus New Boxes!

From: Hawk (omkarmashanti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-26 18:36:18 UTC-08:00
I finally got F100 today!!!!!!!

After four tries, I finally got BabyBear's "Lucky
Dog". The first time it was missing. The second and
third times I tried, the park was under water. This
time, The park was only slightly mushy, but the box
had washed off into a BIG BUNCH of thorny underbrush
-- OUCH! The box's contents were dry (good job with
the baggies, BabyBear!) and has been put back in the
proper place.

Also, I planted some boxes:

1. Sitting Duck
Put on our walking shoes, TeamKing! You're going to
need a long lunch break to get this one. This is my
favorite stamp so far. A bit punny, too!

2. Where in the "H"? (Actually adopted this one)

3. Millie's Treasure
I have tried to plant this box 3 times, and finally
the park dried out enough for me to get it planted. It
is actually a few feet off the ground. I got creative
with a strip of Velcro to hold it onto a tree branch.
Whoever finds this, let me know how it's working.

All three of these require some brain-power to figure
out the clues.

Hawk
F100 P64 X8
