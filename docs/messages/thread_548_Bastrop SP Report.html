Bastrop SP Report

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-01-09 23:08:17 UTC

Hello all,

We're back from a day in Bastrop SP and thought we'd report the
good, bad and the ugly of our trip.

On the good front, we successfully found Cool Cat, Lost Loblolly,
Poison Ivy, Trail Rider, 10th Mountain Division and O-Knot #1.

On the bad front, Skinny Pine was still in place, but its log book
was soaked. We dried off the stamp and rebagged and replaced, but
we didn't have a dry log book with us.

Now, for the ugly. At some point, a fire went through portions of
the park. We found the quite charred remains of These Boots ARE
Made for Walking, with the spiral from the notebook, some charred
pages and a bit of the lid (Paul took a photo if you'd like to see
it). We also couldn't find Houston Toad. The log that we thought
it should be hidden inside was quite charred. The box may still be
there, but we couldn't find it or any remains of it anywhere. It
wouldn't be the first time we've missed a box, but the area was
pretty crispy.

About this point, the kids refused to hike any more, so we couldn't
check on any other boxes in the park. We think the kids did pretty
well for 3 and 7, so we were quite pleased with the day.

We also had a fun time at Mountain Hill State Park yesterday on our
way to Bastrop. Found both ZZTop and Black Beans in place and in
good shape.

That's it. Hope everyone else had as good a weekend.

Dewberry & Co.
