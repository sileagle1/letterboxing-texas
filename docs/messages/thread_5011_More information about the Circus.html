More information about the Circus

From: BarefootLucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-04-30 04:17:13 UTC
We received a membership request earlier today from someone with a jackal-like email address. I won't share the details, but the gist of their reasoning for requesting membership was pretty ominous and it referenced the Circus.

I was at first inclined to deny their request because I think it is generally bad form to give in in any way to someone who chooses to use intimidation to make themselves known, but I decided I have no right to make that decision since the Circus doesn't belong to me, and since the group should have some say in how this goes.

I have approved the membership of this jackal "person", but I would suggest that if they choose to be heard on the list, that we not allow ourselves to be taken in by them. In other words, I think healthy skepticism should be our watchword.

I hope I don't regret the decision to allow them onto the group. Let me also say that I made this decision completely on my own, and if allowing this member onto the list comes to trouble, I take the entire blame on my own shoulders and BootsTex and Silver Eagle had nothing to do with this decision.
