Hi Ruby,
My little Samurai Stampers all have mediums. My four and 8 year old
almost use them as jammie shirts, but they are just the right size
for my ten year old. I would say the mediums correlate to probably
a size 10. I do want to let the new boxers know, however, that the
shirts are awesome! They are great quality. We have washed ours a
bunch and they never fade. I would say, though, that the adult XL
is more like a man's XL.
