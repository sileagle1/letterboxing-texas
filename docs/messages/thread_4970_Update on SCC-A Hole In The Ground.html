Update on SCC-A Hole In The Ground

From: Rachel Stout (prazehm878@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-03-02 11:39:57 UTC-08:00
I visited the site of SCC Hole in the Ground this morning.  Entrances to the parking lots were fenced and locked.  At the risk of a spoil, I didn't want to post too much information here, so I blogged some of the details (with pictures), for those of you who want to hear more information. (The blog doesn't mention the name of the letterbox, or the location.) 

There are Private Property/No Trespassing Signs up.  The location of the lb is outside of the fenced in area, and I felt like it was safe, at least for the time being, so I left it in place-also because I have a fear of being arrested for trespassing.  If those in the group feel like it needs to be removed (even if temporarily), I don't feel comfortable removing it with the signs in place as they are, although, perhaps there are others who wouldn't feel the same way that might want to rescue it.  I don't mind checking up on it from time to time-like I said before, I am only about 10-15 minutes from the location.

For those not worried about the spoil, there are pictures, and a description of where the letterbox is located on the blog:  http://lifeasitistous.blogspot.com/2012/03/no-trespassing.html 
 
God bless,
Elwood Fan
