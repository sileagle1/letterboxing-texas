My family and I follow the space program very closely
and were thrilled when the new shuttle mission lifted
off earlier this week (on my husband's birthday no
less!).

In honor of this event, I planted a box on the site of
what will one day be Lake Columbia (renamed from Lake
Eastex after the Columbia accident made the space
program tragically real to us in our area as it rained
down debris over East Texas).

Sooo...if anyone follows the space program like we do
and wants to take a Sunday afternoon drive, this would
be a fitting weekend to visit my new box.
