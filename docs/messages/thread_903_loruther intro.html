Hello there!  I am new to this group and need to introduce myself.  I am loruther, mother of two up-and-coming letterboxers.  I was lucky in that my second trip letterboxing was out of state--Durango, Co.  I love this activity; I think it is a great way to see places, (especially my beloved Houston where I was born and raised), a great way to expose my kids to the neat things Houston has to offer, and a super way to spend family time.  I hope to give input, but admit that I may lurk at times when things around my house are busy--ask MAGS!  My most exciting thing is that I have introduced my extended family to letterboxing and they are hooked; esp. my 12 yr old niece!  Thanks for all the great boxes out there--the artistry is wonderful and the sites are just as nice!
