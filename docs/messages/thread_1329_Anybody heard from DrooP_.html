RE: [letterboxingtexas] Re: Anybody heard from DrooP?

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-25 13:54:02 UTC-07:00
I was hoping to log on and find DrooP had checked in
myself.

We lost power about 11 a.m. yesterday and expect to be
without it until Tuesday, according to the power
company. I feel much worse today than I did
yesterday, probably because of the heat and the
humidity at home. I'm at my husband's office right
now and will stay and hang out here until fairly late
this evening. I'm going to go take a nap on the couch
after I hit send...

Lots of trees in our town were broken, and several
were even uprooted. Lots of tree damage everywhere,
although not nearly as much as we usually see during a
winter storm. We were fortunate, I guess, because we
have a yard full of big old oaks and pecans and didn't
lose a single limb - not even any large branches.

My sister-in-law lives where Blue Butterfly lives and
they apparently got the worst of all of us up in this
area. I talked to her this morning and she said they
are going to be without power until Tuesday also. She
had a houseful of evacuees (her husband's family all
live down along the coast), and it sounded pretty
chaotic when I talked to her.

My mother-in-law and my husband wound up being in
charge of the ARC shelter and they started running
into problems with folks wanting to leave and go home
first thing yesterday morning after it was clear the
hurricane had missed Houston. They didn't understand
that DPS had roads closed not too far south of here
and that there was no gas on the way either. Some of
them stayed after they were told about the situation,
but others left only to return a few hours later. I'm
particularly worried about my mother-in-law, who
suffers from fibromyalgia. She has been going for
pretty much three days solid now, and if you know
anything about fibro, you know that rest is of
critical importance to staying healthy with that
disease. I talked to her yesterday and she says she
is listening to her body, so hopefully she won't
crater after this is said and done. I really admire
her - she has a huge heart for people and this sort of
thing is right up her alley. She has a way of making
people feel better about their situation no matter
what it is.

Lucy
