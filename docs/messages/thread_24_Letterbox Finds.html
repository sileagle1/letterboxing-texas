From: droopwv (harland@pnx.com) | Date: 2004-02-01 21:38:45 UTC
Hello everyone. Took advantage of the fair weather here in southeast
Texas to search for a few letterboxes.
Found Silver Eagles Anahuac Anhinga Microbox without trouble. It is
in great shape and has been unvisited since he placed it (except for
me finding it today). Also located Baby Bears Coastal Night Light
without trouble. Was in great shape also and I was the first to
stamp since the letterbox was placed. Looked for Janis Joplin
Microbox in Port Arthur but had no luck. All in all a pretty good 4
hours of letterboxing. Drove about 200 miles to get to those 3 boxes
but it is kind of a circle back to my house.
If you have never been to Anahuac you would not believe that there
is a place there. I had never been there and was amazed that there
was such a community. Saw some nice birds but it was too chilly for
alligators. If you like coastal wetlands you will definately enjoy
this trip. I do plan on going back when the gators will be sunning.
Happy Boxing
DrooP
PS: made reservations to camp at Bastrop Park. Will be there for the
gathering.
