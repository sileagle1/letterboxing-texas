From: tko1235 (TKO1235@rocketmail.com) | Date: 2011-08-14 15:40:35 UTC
Yes, we put our pathtag in every letterbox we've hidden as a First Finders prize with a certificate. I also drop them in some of the letterboxes I've found when I find the letterbox to be out of the ordinary (awesome stamp, great adventure, etc). I don't feel they are greatly known about in the world of letterboxing because many people never logged them in and those that did said they we're neat, but weren't sure what to do with them.

FYI - If you ever find a pathtag. Log it in and send the owner a message of where you found it. They'd appreciate it. Then, you can keep it as a souvenier (my recommendation), trade it, or drop it in another letterbox or a geocache if you want.

-Team K9
