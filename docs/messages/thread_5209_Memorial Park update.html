Memorial Park update

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2013-05-12 13:56:49 UTC-05:00

I’d like to thank everyone who came out and braved the weather and mud yesterday.  I couldn’t do much about the mud, but the weather certainly cooperated!  We had a great time, made new friends and saved some boxes which lost their homes.  Several had to be repaired and moved to new homes, and sadly there were some classics we couldn’t find.  I suspect we checked on less than half of the boxes so there is still a lot of work to do. 

 

I’ve posted the highlights of the day on my blog: http://anopenspacelife.blogspot.com/2013/05/memorial-park-maintenance.html, and uploaded the pictures on the event page on AQ.  I’ll be posting a detailed report of all the boxes we checked later today and correcting the map for general use.  Thanks again for all your help.

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2013-05-12 19:55:52 UTC-05:00

Here is the list of boxes checked Saturday and their status (with some help from Wry Me).  If we moved a box I’ll send the new directions to the owners shortly.

 

Red/Blue/Purple trail area

Kayak Girl (NLW) – found with box open and full of water -- no stamp in sight so the box was removed

Nada Clue (Boots Tex) – fabulous as ever and we loved the new location!

NFL Series – Houston Texans (Gators88) – No box found, trees around it cut down. 

Cola Wars series (Wills World) – Choice B&C are there (maybe wet?), The home for Choice A is destroyed but MB found it in a bush by accident.  The box was damaged.  Planted in new location with a new box and logbook.

Cola Wars: Choice D (Open Space) – Ok

Rain Deer (Gators88) – Location has been cleared, box lost

Bones (LSQ) – Found the location with brick but the box is missing

Risk: Horseman Card – Found box, current location was at a dead tree near a cleared area so we decided to move it to a live tree.  Plastic jar is bent but dry.

The Patchwork Girl of Oz (LSQ)-- was buried in and amongst an acre of debris from the recent tree removals. The lid was creased, the box cracked, the logbook soaked and slimy and felt sopping. The stamp, however, is wonderful. You would never believe where this box was found, with barely a corner poking out of the broken limbs, in a clearing with no landmarks from the clues visible. Still singing a victory dance. MB is rehabilitating the logbook.

Cowardly lion of Oz (Boots Tex) – Alive and well

Jabberwocky (Open Space/Dreamwalker) – OK

The Writing’s on the wall (dewberry)– Found the writing but not the wall

 

Green Trail

Oz: The Gatekeeper (dewberry) – missing, stump is mostly gone and area behind it is completely cleared out

The games closet – Mousetrap – stamp missing although it’s hiding spot looked undisturbed

Washington State Flower (TeamKing) – alive and well (location for this on the map needs to be updated)

Bingo and Bingo piece (Baby Bear) – we couldn’t find Bingo, but it could be under a fallen tree or lots of debris along the log.  A shovel would help.  Bingo Piece is there but the clues don’t make much sense anymore (and the tree is dead – just the trunk is left)

MOM (AstroD) – Alive and damp.  We cleaned it up and donated a new bag.  It’s stump is slowly meeting it’s demise and overgrowth is partially hiding the stump. 

Irish Blessing – boxes are long gone

Season’s Greetings (dewberry) – replanted in it’s original location (although we are concerned that the spot gets too much attention). Dewberry plans to update clues on AQ.

Blue Marlin 06 – missing, probably washed away by Ike (based on comments I received about that area)

Toadally Hoppy – couldn’t find landmarks, but we didn’t check out the bottom loop of the green trail, so there is still hope

 

Puzzle Mystery boxes (not on the main list I shared Saturday)

Puzzle Queen – Alive and well

GG’s box – I couldn’t find the landmarks, but I think I started at the wrong place.

Surrender Dorothy – I checked this one last week.  It’s alive and well but in a slightly different spot (dewberry knows where it is J).  It’s original hiding place is destroyed.  One tab on the L&L is broken.
