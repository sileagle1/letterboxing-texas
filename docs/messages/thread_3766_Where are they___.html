From: Dave (DavHow@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-09-18 00:59:36 UTC
Hi guys -
Cold Front and I are still around and doing fine. I proposed on
Valentine's Day (yes, with a letterbox), and we're going to be married
in an old adobe church in the Terlingua ghost town next August 23rd.
I changed my major from Education (science teacher) to Geology (an
actual science) in hopes of getting a higher paying job when I finally
graduate, but geology classes are MUCH tougher than education courses,
so I keep my head in the books (and the science labs) most of the
time, and we're staying close to Alpine in an effort to save some
money. I have 3 lab sciences this semester, and work at the library,
and in the technical services department fixing computers, and Cold
Front has 3 part-time jobs, so we don't see each other much; much less
have time for letterboxing, but we still count it among our favorite
hobbies. We each carved a stamp for a box that Astro D put together
for Lionsmane (it was his 1000 find, so you may have seen his name in
a logbook even if you don't know him). I think it's called "road trip"
and it's out near Taos NM. We also managed to find 2 or 3 stamps while
we were in Vegas for my nieces wedding last month. My best friend is
getting married in April (Austin), and my nephew is getting married 2
weeks before we do next August (Houston) so we may get a couple
chances for letterboxing while traveling to and from all these weddings.
Anyways, just checking in. I hope all is well with those I know, and a
quick hello to those I don't. I'll try not to let another year pass
before checking in again.

Warm Front
