Great Circus comes to Houston

From: (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2009-07-09 07:56:24 UTC-05:00
Hello, Everyone!  A really fabulous one-ring circus from Mexico will be playing Houston, July 10 - August 02.  It is El Circo Hermanos Vazquez -- the Vazquez Brothers Circus, at www.circovazquez.com.  They'll be at I-45 and Airline.  We went to see them twice in Austin, and were blown away.  Pay to sit in the front row.  The experience is fabulous.  On the second night, I brought photos to owner Jesus Vazquez (he's the ringmaster for the second half) that I had taken of the show in Houston in 1996.  He was so happy!  The show has grown so much -- it has top performers from all over the globe.  The show is all in Spanish, but is fast-paced, wonderful, surprising, and thoroughly enjoyable.  I obviously can't recommend it enough!  -- Bell Motel
