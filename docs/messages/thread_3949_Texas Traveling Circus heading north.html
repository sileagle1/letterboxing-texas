Texas Traveling Circus heading north

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2009-02-09 02:17:52 UTC
If anyone has been looking for the Texas Traveling Circus, look no
more: the whole group showed up on our doorstep this morning. That
clown, LB Bozo, is apparently the ringleader, at least until the
ringmaster shows up. He said they decided that the woods wasn't a good
place to set up the bigtop, so they pulled up stakes and were heading
up north when he smelled cinnamon rolls. He asked if they might bunk
here at the Red Barn for a spell, so we said that would be okay, as
long as they behaved themselves. At least there aren't many of them,
just the Bozo, a bear, a monkey, a dog, a bearded lady and the
Juggler. I was afraid when they showed up it might be that bus of
hooligans that have been terrorizing the countryside recently,
masquerading as pirates. Thankfully, that bunch is someone else's
problem now. Anyway, don't go looking for the circus; they're bunked
out in the Red Barn (except for the bear, who's hibernating in the
woods). So LB Bozo can stop griping now; he'll be bundled up in
handmade quilts every night and eating cinnamon rolls every morning, at
least for the next month or so when they are scheduled to perform up in
Houston County. He said y'all were all invited to the show.
