Sunday morning. I was GOING to go shoot the match this morning. But
it is raining. I have lots of experience shooting in the rain - 20 years of
military you know. And I have lots of experience cleaning gear after laying
in the mud while shooting in the rain. Nothing like the smell of
damp LBE!

I hear thunder rolling in the east over the mountains. The El Paso
radar shows me there is a cell right over NE and the Ft. Bliss rifle range
now. Not a lot of rain, but enough to show on radar. Enough to make the
ground wet. Enough to make mud.

Just talked to a shooter living near the range. It is raining
pretty good over there, and he says the clouds seem to be stalled overhead.
Not a good sign.

Ya know what? I don't have to do that anymore. And nobody can make
me. I got nothing to prove to nobody. My gear is all nice and clean, and
clean its gunna stay. My rifle is dry and nicely oiled. Dry and nicely
oiled its gunna stay. After a hike yesterday, my legs are a bit shaky
anyway.

Maybe next month. We need the rain out here.

Looks like a good day to decide on a stamp for Hudspeth County. I
can have it done and in my hands by Wednesday. Letterbox COULD be hidden by
Saturday! We will see.

Y-all enjoy your day.
