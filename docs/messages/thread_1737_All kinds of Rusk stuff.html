From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-12 15:57:41 UTC-08:00
I believe campsites are all booked, but it seems like
someone was willing to share their site. DrooP maybe?

Catfish (Big Bertha's) doesn't have a street address
per se (well, I'm sure they do, but it's probably a
rural route). It's about six miles further down
Highway 84 from Texas State Railroad Park toward
Palestine, in the town of Maydelle. You could google
Maydelle. In Maydelle there are about three
businesses on the main road (Highway 84), all on the
right if you are going toward Palestine from Rusk.
Big Bertha's is the middle one. And she has a sign
out that says - you guessed it - Big Bertha's.
Apparently she is known for her Big Bertha Breakfast
too. It is really truly a little country store with
wooden walkways outside and a swinging wooden screen
door and wooden floors inside that aren't level when
you walk, but it is excellent catfish. And if you
don't like catfish, they do have a menu of just good
country cooking.
