Hello yall! My name is Mandeigh(pronounced Mandy). I recently heard
about letterboxing from a fellow homeschooler and thought it sounded

some potential hiding spots this weekend. Nice to meet you all! :)

Hi Mandeigh and Family,
 
This is a great hobby/sport for the whole family (4 legged ones if you got those too). Maybe you can be the trailblazer in the Abilene area, that'd be something, wouldn't it? You might be interested in Postal letterboxing too. That's where you send and receive boxes in the mail. I'm fixin' to send out my first one. I'm late, but it's finished, whew. How scary. I can't wait to start receiving them. You can look in Atlasquest to find info on postals.
