Well - I hang out with a bunch of folks here.  We are (in no particular order) ham radio operators, gun owners, dog owners, Letterboxers, Geocachers, jeep owners, hikers, etc etc etc.....  not all are all of these, but all are some combination.  We usually gather up at one house every Friday night for a pot-luck dinner and discussion of one or more (usually MANY more) of the subjects above.
 
You would be most welcome to join the group for your time here.  Who knows - you might just leave with a study guide for ham radio or something.
 
One thought - this Friday we are going to a dog show in Sierra Vista AZ, so we will not be here.  Still - if you find yourself running around Saturday, go to the Franklin Mountain State Park on the west side of mountains on Trans-Mountain rd.  Got a few letterboxes and several geocaches there.
 
Looking forward to meeting you.
