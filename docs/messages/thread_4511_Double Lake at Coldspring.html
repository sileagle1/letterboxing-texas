Double Lake at Coldspring

From: Bonnie's Email Inbox (bonnielind1@hotmail.com) | Date: 2010-04-15 23:07:30 UTC-05:00

After Boots Tex’s grand report on the availability of all of the letterboxes at Double Lake, I couldn’t resist.  Last week I went to visit the area for a couple of days.  The first day I was there, I was treated to letterboxing with the letterbox masters themselves, Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter.  Thank you both so much for showing me such a wonderful time and driving me all around the area!  Your hospitality is the greatest!  The next day, I spent from sun-up until sun-down letterboxing at Double Lake.  This beautiful park has so many wonderful boxes.  There are a number of different trails, so if you go—and I highly recommend it—you may want to review your clues for starting points.  The wildflowers were beautiful, the Dogwoods were in full bloom and the temperature was perfect.  Most of all, I’d like to thank all the folks who planted all of the treasures for us to find.  The trip was well worth the drive, which is good because I have to go back.  There are still a lot of boxes I didn’t have time to find!

 

Happy Letterboxing!

Mosaic Butterfly
