Today I checked on 4 of my boxes and one of Silver Eagle's (one that
is in the same park as 2 of mine)

The following boxes are alive and well:

Texas Welcome--some slight changes made to the clues due to some
trees being cut down. Print new clues if you will be looking for
this one.

Louisiana Welcome--some water in box and log book was slightly damp--
was this way when I was there several months ago too, and since it
wasn't any worse I re-bagged it and put it back. This box has a
tendency to get buried fairly deep--so if you go looking for it you
may have to look deeper than you would expect.

Secret Garden--alive and well--dry and intact

Myrtle Tyrtle--alive and well. Log book was full. Replaced with new
one.

Silver Eagles Spindletop was alive and well.

I released "keep on truckin HH" today.

Happy hunting,
