I just wanted to let Baby Bear know that we found "Park Angel"
yesterday, and we also saw some of the most enormous dragonflies
ever. We are heading to camp in Glen Rose this weekend, and are
planning several letterbox stops along the way. Any suggestions for
places are good for kids? I am not familiar at all with the route
past Washington on the Brazos. (We are travelling from Houston.)
Thanks! Mother of Four

Sounds like a fun weekend in a fun park. Be sure to let me
know the status of my Dinosaur Valley box in Dinosaur Valley SP.
As far as a recommended route, I would drive around the Brenham
area to see the wildflowers and if you have time, going to Lake
Somerville for the 2 letterboxes there. Then head to Independence
for another letterbox and visit the Anique Rose Emporium. Next, head
to Washington-On-Brazos SP for the 3 boxes there and more
bluebonnets. Continue north on Hwy 6 to College Station for 2
boxes there, then Messina Hof winery in Bryan for letterbox and
good wine tasting. Continue on Hwy 6 to Waco for several boxes
and enjoy the Texas Ranger Museum and/or the Suspension Bridge.
You could then head to Lake Whitney SP to find the box there
before arriving at Glen Rose.
On the way back to Houston, you could head east to Confederate
Reunion Grounds SP and Fort Parker SP for the boxes there. Then
south on I45 to Huntsville, where you can visit Sam Houston's
house and statue, plus 2 letterboxes. Finally back to Houston,
exhausted. Have fun!

Silver Eagle
