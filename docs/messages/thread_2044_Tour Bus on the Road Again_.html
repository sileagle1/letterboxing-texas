Tour Bus Trail Report:
After a close call with a prairie fire in North Richland Hills, the tour bus
stopped for awhile in Keller, then detoured to Weatherford for its next
passenger: Oliver Loving, Dean of Texas Trail Drivers, whose grave and historic
marker are in the Greenwood Cemetery (which is also where you'll find the Tour
Bus).
To get there: go to Weatherford to the Court House (you can't miss
that!) and go north from the courthouse to Front Street. Turn right on Front
Street and enter the cemetery under the old iron Greenwood Cemetery sign.
Follow the road to the left around the curve, stopping at Oliver Loving's grave
to read the historic marker and check out the "Badge of Friendship" letterbox
placed there by the 4 Fitzs. Continue on along that drive as it curves to an
intersection where you'll go left a short way, then right a short distance.
Watch for the large Corn Littlefield monument and two large cedar trees near
the road, with overgrown juniper shrubs and Johnson grass between them.
