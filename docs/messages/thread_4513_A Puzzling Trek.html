Just a note on my "A Puzzling Trek" series in Mansfield, TX... 

I know that at least some people have been working on solving my puzzles to go find the series.  The first box in the series (the communicator badge) is missing as of today's maintenance trip.  The remainder of the boxes are still in place, and in good shape.  If you solve the puzzles, when you get to the park the clues will still lead you to the remainder of the boxes.  Also, poison oak, waist-high grass and thorny vine overgrowth were all discovered during the maintenance trip, so use caution when traipsing towards the boxes.

Clue listing on LbNA has been updated to reflect maintenance trip and I left a comment on AQ about it.  The first box has been retired because I really don't feel like recarving it.  :-D
