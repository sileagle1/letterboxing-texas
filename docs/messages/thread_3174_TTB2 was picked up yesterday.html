Bloomin Gramma Jo, Bird Dog and Everyone else…

 

My bad.  I picked up TTB2, it was late Saturday and I didn’t look inside other than to make sure it wasn’t full of dirt or water until late Sunday.  I was unsure of the protocol (which I would have known had I taken the time to look at the log book right away) so I emailed CanCan , the last finder of TTB2 to ask her what I was supposed to do next.  She answered right away but by then I had gone to bed.  She was kind enough to announce to the list early the next morning (like 6 am) just so this sort of thing wouldn’t happen. Thanks again and thanks for all of you being patient with me and others new to this obsession/hobby.
