Candy Land Gathering

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-12-30 21:25:33 UTC-06:00
Now that I have had the chance to wash the ink off my hands (and my kids!) I wanted to say what a great time I had playing Candy Land today!  I remember the first time I met any other letterboxers at the Terrors on Telge mini gathering, how nervous and intimidated I was to meet strangers that I knew only through little boxes hidden in the woods.  Today as people arrived the kids were saying, "Look, Silver Eagle and Baby Bear are here!  Hey, it's Runs for Chocolate!  Hi Blue Butterfly!" and I thought how much fun it was to see these strangers that have now become familiar faces.  I don't know what I enjoyed more; seeing how much fun the kids were having, the kind compliment dewberry and I received from the infamous GG, or hearing a newer letterboxer say, "Oh, Silver Eagle, the Father of Texas letterboxing.  And I guess Baby Bear must be the Mother of Texas letterboxing!"  Thanks to everyone who came, we missed those that weren't there, and I can't wait until the next time we all get together!  Mother of Five

Hi everyone!
We had a great time at the Candyland gathering also. The boys
were thrilled with the stamps! We logged in the Pinata Box and even
had time for the boys to spend a little time on the playground.
Due to a late arrival back home, I left the backpacks in the van.
When I loaded the boys up to go to church this morning, they both
wanted to look at their logbooks. They compared stamps and one of
thier favorites in Runsforchocolate's Sweet Carolina stamp. They just
had to show Daddy! Thanks to all who worked so hard so we could have a
fun day. What a way to end the year!
It was great to see everyone and hope you all have a safe and
Happy New Year!
