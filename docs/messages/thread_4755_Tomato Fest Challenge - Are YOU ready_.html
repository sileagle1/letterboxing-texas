Tomato Fest Challenge - Are YOU ready?

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-03-23 04:00:47 UTC-05:00
If'n you is or if'n you aint, it matters not 'cause it's fast approaching!

This event is NOT for the faint-hearted, the shy, the shrinking violet. Nooooo...my goodness, NO! You're gonna have to bring it, and when you leave they're gonna have to know you left it on their doorstep!


I'll begin posting details in the next day or two, but I wanted to alert this group to look out for them.

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1749

Here's a taste:

1) Most important: This will NOT be a walk-up event - you will need to sign up in advance because of the need for advance planning and logistics.
2) To best experience this event, you will need to make up teams of 6 or fewer (or 7, for Rodgers 7). Individuals can do it, but your experience will be better as teams. I will need to know in advance who will be on which teams, although team changes, adds, and deletes can be made up to midnight the night before.
3) Each team will need a team name - a TOMATO-Y team name.
4) Each team will need a boxing buddy - a TOMATO-Y boxing buddy. A photogenic TOMATO-Y boxing buddy. Of a size sufficient to be seen in photos.
5) Each team will need a cover story - a reason for being at the Tomato Fest - ideally, a TOMATO-Y cover story. It can in no way make mention of letterboxing. This is a STEALTH event!
6) Each team will need a digital camera - a phone cam will be fine as long as it takes good pics.

Did I mention there is a competitive aspect this year? Creativity counts, so make #3, #4, and #5 GOOD!

--
Barefoot Lucy
