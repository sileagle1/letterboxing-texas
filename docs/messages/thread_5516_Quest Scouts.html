Quest Scouts

From: (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2015-09-12 22:10:48 UTC-07:00

Since we have met some of you at the TALE events, I know many of you are the creative, adventurous sort who enjoy having fun and learning new things.  I thought some of you might therefore be interested in Quest Scouts.  Martini Man & I and several other letterboxers that we know have been participating in Quest Scouts over the summer. 


If you want to get a quick introduction to it, a Quest Scouts Bingo game is starting on Monday SEP 14.  All you need to do is enter your name & email address and then you'll then receive an email with a link to your Bingo card. Even if you aren't interested in "badges", you can still enjoy the activities.

 

Here is some additional info & a link to register for the game:

 

How to Play Micro Quest BINGO:

Seasoned Quest Scouts know that micro quests are fun, quick objectives designed to help you to step outside of your everyday routine. Like our larger objectives, Micro Quests will challenge you to do a range of activities, from playing a game, to watching a video, to creating art and much more. 

-Each day we will release a new micro quest that will usually take you less than 15 minutes to complete. 

-Each micro quest will only be available for 2 days. 

-Micro quests will correspond to a BINGO square. Complete the micro quest and mark off its corresponding square on your BINGO board.

-Mark off 5 spaces in a row diagonally, horizontally or vertically to complete Micro Quest BINGO and earn your badge.
