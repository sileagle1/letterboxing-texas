Houston HH Hostel

From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2004-12-07 19:49:15 UTC-06:00
This weekend I will be placing Texas' 2nd HH Hostel. It's called "No
Problem Houston Hitchhiker Hostel". I have decided on Memorial Park as
the best central location as we obviously want out of state/town
visitors to pick up and drop off as easily as possible. I have gotten
numerous people to send HHs for this, but instead of waiting for all of
them to get here, I am going to get it started and then drop them off
as they come. So, check it out for new 'finds' whether coming or going
out of the city and keep those travellers moving.

Just a side note. . .if you read about the Monster Mash they had up in
Connecticut in October (I know we had a Texan or two send some monster
stamps on for that). . .anyway, about 2 of those monsters are being
turned in HHs and they will be starting their travels out our hostel!
Yea!

Hopefully this will keep some of our HHs moving about instead of stuck
in backpacks or at home because of the large number of microboxes we
have in Houston.

Will post the clue/direction on LBNA on Saturday or Sunday after
placing, so check it out.

Enjoy!

TeamKing

Re: [letterboxingtexas] Re: Houston HH Hostel

From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2004-12-07 21:22:49 UTC-06:00
There must always be one HH in the box at all times. So, if you find
only one in the box, then you must leave one in it's place if you want
to take it. If you find multiple ones, then you can take whatever you
want as long as at least one stays in the box. If you want to just go
and stamp in but not take any, that's fine. I would just ask that you
move some along like every other time or something so it doesn't get
taken advantage of. . .

I think that's it. :)
