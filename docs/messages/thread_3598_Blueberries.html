Blueberries

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-06-01 03:16:14 UTC
Since I had to travel north to Humble today for my daughter's soccer
tournament, we decided to go pick blueberries between games. I have
been going to Moorhead's Blueberry Farm for over 6 years and though
kind of far from Houston, it is worth the trip. Right now their
biggest blueberries are ripe for picking, and as further incentive I
placed a box nearby called TX Blueberry with clues posted on LBNA. I
recommend going early, especially on weekends, since it gets very
crowded and hot. Blueberries are only $1.50/lb and you can eat all you
want while picking. Enjoy!
