Lock-n-locks around the world!

From: Lore Guilmartin (loreguilmartin@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-08-20 02:50:06 UTC-07:00
I had to share this with y'all because who else would find it funny?

I moved to Doha, Qatar, a few weeks ago, and am starting to find my way around. Going into a few different grocery stores I've found one commonality: A whole WALL of lock-n-lock boxes in every shape and size! It's letterboxing nirvana!

Sadly there's only one box hidden in the entire country, a couple miles from me. I haven't tried to find it yet but will soon. Then figure I'll go on a letterbox planting blitz and try to spread the word in the geocaching community, to get people involved. I'm working at a university that specializes in art and design, so maybe I can convince people there of the artistic outlet that's letterboxing?

I guess this means that I'll appreciate your help if any of my Texan boxes need maintenance in the next few years! I'm sure planning to be back someday, but not for the next couple of years.

Let me know if you'd like to visit Qatar. ;-)

Lore
