5th Annual TX LB Event Venue

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-09-17 15:07:57 UTC
With all the TX LB Gatherings coming up, I realized I need to decide
on a venue for our Event next March and book it. I originally
planned to have it at McKinney Falls SP in Austin, but since they
have strict restrictions on boxes and our State Parks have not
established a letterboxing policy yet, I'm looking at other places.
I really like the Lower Colorado River Authority (LCRA) parks, and
there are a couple with everything we need (e.g. covered pavilion,
camping, trails). Also, they seem to not only allow geocaching, but
encourage it, since they advertise it as an activity under the
Scouting tab of their website. I assume letterboxing would be
welcome as well, and I have already tried to open up a dialogue with
them about it.

At this point I'm leaning toward South Shore Park on Lake Bastrop.
It has 38 RV campsites, 18 cabins, a large covered pavilion, and 3
miles of trails. Austin is not far away and it is very close to
Bastrop SP, so you could camp and/or letterbox there as well. This
makes it a sentimental favorite for me since our first TX LB Event
was held there.

As far as the date, I'm planning on Saturday, March 1, 2008, assuming
I can book the pavilion that day. Let me know what you think, and if
most like this idea I will book later this week. Thanks.

LCRA Website:
http://www.lcra.org

South Shore Park:
http://www.lcra.org/parks/developed_parks/southshore.html

Geocaching Activity:
http://www.lcra.org/newsletter/currents/july06/geocaching.html
