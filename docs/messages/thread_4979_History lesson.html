It's been awhile since I've gone back and read some of the earlier entries on the Texas talk list, but I agree, it is a fun history lesson! I do remember those days when we'd all check our computers daily in anticipation of a new box being planted, then change our plans for the next day so that we could drive 60 miles or more to go find the "new box". Each box could be a catalyst for days of discussion on the list. I'm thrilled that there are now so many boxes and boxers in Texas, but those days were exciting in the growth of Texas letterboxing! I was living in Connecticut when the first event took place, and I couldn't wait to get back home to Texas to join the fun. I'm definitely looking forward to TALE10.
