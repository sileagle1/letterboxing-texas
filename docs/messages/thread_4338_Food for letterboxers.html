I'm spring-cleaning right now (won't tell you if I'm late for last spring or early for the coming one) and I came across recipes I collected from the JEN gathering primarily, but also a couple of others along the way so far. I thought I would share it with y'all, and invite y'all to send along any other recipes you might want to see included, particularly gathering type recipes. If you send something along and you want me to include your sig stamp, scan it in and send it along and I'll use it too.

I've got others I've found along the way, not to mention ones from my Tomato Fest/Pampered Chef gathering. I'm looking for those and I'll post them as I get to them.

http://eatinglikeletterboxers.blogspot.com

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-01-02 11:19:30 UTC-06:00

River Rats are NOT for the faint-hearted or tender stomach. They are Texas to the core - what you wind up with is a jalapeno pepper stuffed with chicken and cheese and wrapped in a strip of bacon.

We have a close friend who married a biker chick (only wedding I've ever been to that had the word a** spoken by the preacher in the ceremony), so we found ourselves running with a biker club for a little while. Gotta say - they were fine folks who welcomed us with open arms even though we had a lowly Kawasaki. Those were fond memories and they are all good friends to this day, even though we rarely see them because our friends moved away before she got sick and passed away. Anyway, when they get together, they partied hard, ate like kings, and drank like fish. I'm not much of a partier anymore, my midsection can't afford to eat as well as they do, and I proved not to be able to keep up with them on the drinking front. River Rats came from our experiences during that time of our lives.

Recipe is on the blog:

http://eatinginglikeletterboxers.blogspot.com
