From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-11-09 17:37:39 UTC-07:00
I want to share this with you-all.  Today was our first outing.  We visited a retirement home where the pups were greeted warmly.  Then we visited with a few rangers and shared Parker stories.  They miss him too.  It was a little painful for me, but I imagine there is no jealousy in heaven, so I hope Parker was smiling down on us.  Other than missing Parker, I had a great time.  The tramway is at 5600 feet.  The pups learned elevator, tram car, altitude and stairs today.  Right now they are zonked out in their beds.  Slideshow will follow shortly.
 
 
 
The training has begun.  With luck, a year from now River will hike up to the tram deck.
