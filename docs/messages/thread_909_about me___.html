From: The Carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-06-12 21:41:17 UTC-05:00
Hey you all...I'm Mother of Five.  We started boxing about 2 years ago, heard about it through an article in Family Fun magazine.  The thing I like best about letterboxing is finding all these cool places that I never would have known about if it weren't for letterboxing...the strangest of all being a giant replica of stonehenge in a field near Kerville.  Only in Texas.  I am a Texan by choice, came here from Michigan about 13 years ago, and I never plan to leave.  I taught bilingual second and third grade here in Houston for 8 years, but started being a stay at home mom when my second child was born.  I am now mother to five little Texans ranging in age from 9 to 4 months.  My oldest son is having a lot of fun this summer planning and planting boxes of the things 9 year old boys love best...Star Wars, Harry Potter...I'm sure he'll have lots more ideas.  When I look at how many boxes there are in Houston alone compared to when we started, and all the different types of creative clues, logbooks, and box themes that are out there I think about Silver Eagle and Baby Bear...how exciting it must be for them.  Sort of like watching your baby let go of the couch and stand on their own...then before you know it they are running away at full speed!  I love to open my e mail each day and see what my "boxing friends" have to say.  This is such a great list to be a part of.
