Big week

From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-04-22 18:49:14 UTC-05:00

We are presently up to our elbows in moving boxes for our move to McKinney, however, before moving my computer, I felt a need to list all my recent finds. I was surprised to find that I had passed the "Five Hundred Finds" mark last week. BUT... even better than that, I beat Gryzzled Gryphon to a box this morning to be the First Finder on Stargazer's new Texas State Symbols Series: Armadillo box. A letterboxing "Excellent Adventure" with Viewfinder to the Hill Country last weekend, going over 500 finds, a Metroplex Event yesterday and First Finder today!!! What could be better for a letterboxer?
 
Puddle Splasher

I started letterboxing July 4th, 2004, but I was living in Connecticut at the time..... "where you trip over letterboxes while mowing the lawn"! However, it can be difficult to letterbox up there during the winter. I actually had a hand shovel in my backpack for those times I would get cabin fever and just have to take a hike in the snow. I remember one March I just had to go find a letterbox on the Appalachian Trail and ended up scooting on my derriere down a steep path because it was so icy I couldn't stand up. When I got to the box, it was under about 2 feet of snow and I had to dig at the right spot along a rock fence. I did get that box though, and of course it was one of my most memorable.
