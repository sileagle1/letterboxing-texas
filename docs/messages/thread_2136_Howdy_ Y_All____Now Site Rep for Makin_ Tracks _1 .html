Malia,
 
I found letterboxing three years ago (to the weekend as a matter of fact!), and only began geocaching around the first of the year, with thanks, by the way, to Mike who helped me figure out what kind of GPS and helped me navigate the different style of website that geocaching.com is.
 
I have to say, I'm a much better letterboxer than I am geocacher.  I like both, but I come up empty-handed too much for my own taste in GC'ing.  I'm sure I'll get better at it as time goes by...
 
I must say one of the coolest finds I've had on that front involved looking at a tree from a different angle to find the cache, then practically hanging out of it to reach the cache (and anyone who knows me will HAVE to laugh at the vision of that sight!).  Another had a hide so natural-looking, I all but banged my head against it repeatedly in frustration over not being able to find it.
