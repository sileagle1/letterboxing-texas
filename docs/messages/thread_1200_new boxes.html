I just planted and posted my first 2 boxes - Lions Park and Tiny
Butterfly.

I'm not sure if I marked them correctly on the LBNA website. I put
Lions Park as 2 boxes, since Tiny Butterfly is also in this park and
the clues are written to flow together. I thought it might be more
enticing if one knows that there are 2 boxes in 1 area. But then I
posted Tiny Butterfly so I could have the name listed as a separate
box. So it looks like there might be 3 boxes instead of 2.

Should I have not put the clues for Tiny Butterfly on the one with
Lions Park, and just put "see clues for Tiny Butterfly which is in
the same area"... and marked Lions Park as 1 box???

Also, I noticed that some clues include a difficulty level,
distance, and time, and some don't. Did this use to be an option
when you post clues and isn't anymore?

Thank you
and please e-mail me if you find them.

Great work placing two boxes!  I wish I were closer to where you are!  About your question: If I were you I would go into the edit function of the clues and perhaps list something like “Lions Park Duo” and indicate there are 2 boxes, then list them each by their individual names under the “Duo” name.  About the difficulty level:  I don’t ever remember  the LbNA site having a fill-in-the-blank feature about difficulty, but I try to put in a line or two at the beginning of my clues so the reader knows what he/she is or isn’t getting themselves into.
