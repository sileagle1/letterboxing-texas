TX Clue Page Fixed

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-02-20 17:31:01 UTC
Ever since I planted the first boxes in Nueces County
they have been showing up in the wrong region of Texas
(Eastern instead of Gulf Coast). After months of badgering
from me, Wes (the LBNA Webmaster) just informed me that it
has been fixed. With all the new Texas letterboxers out
there I did not want anyone getting confused looking for
boxes so that is why I pushed hard to get this fixed. Of
course, there are a lot of counties with no boxes in them
yet that could show up wrong in the future, so please
check to make sure your boxes are in the correct region
after you post the clues, especially if it is in a new
county. If you find any mistakes on the Texas Clue page
now or in the future, please notify me so I can send
one message to Wes. Thanks, and keep the boxes coming!

Silver Eagle
