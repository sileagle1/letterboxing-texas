Went boxing in Nebraska on Thanksgiving and Black Friday.  Also boxed in OK on the way up from TX.  I learned very important lessons.  Moisture and freezing temperatures  and letterboxes are not good combinations.  I had to break ice off pouches in OK in order to use the stamps.  One log was preserved (maybe) in a block of ice but I was able to pry off the stamp to use it.  I did plant a box in NE to pay back the ones I found.  Having just started boxing, I've realized we really have it good in Texas.
