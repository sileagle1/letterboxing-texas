From: busybeesfamily (brandi@busybeesfamily.com) | Date: 2006-02-02 22:53:02 UTC
Hey Boots Tex,

You can't image how glad I am to hear that someone...ANYONE...in
Midland is letterboxing!

My family is from Midland and I grew up there. Last summer, with my
husband working overseas, I took my kiddos to Midland for an
extended vacation with Nana and Papa. We toured Midland like we
were "tourists" and I created several letterboxes to leave in
Midland. Knowing of no letterboxers in Midland, I contacted several
West Texas geocachers to see if they would "adopt and maintain" my
letterboxes, but never really got much commitment from them.

Would you brother-in-law be interested in adopting and maintaining
boxes if I planted them? I should be traveling to that area
sometime in April, and would be DELIGHTED to plant these boxes if I
could know they would be well-tended.

Thanks, Batty b
