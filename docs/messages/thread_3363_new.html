I am a 71 year old great grannie to 4 and soon to be 6 and live
in Central Tx. My husband retired from the military after 25
years and is disabled with Muscular Dystrophy. I heard about
letter boxing from Grammy Pat first and then when ZTurtle
started doing it and heard of the fun, finally tried it and
now believe I am hooked. Went with JHamilton yesterday and
had a good time and also got in some exercise with it. Met
the Belton Girls on the walk also. I am planning on going to
Illinois this summer and have already looked up some in those
areas. Now to find time for housework too?
Jackie
