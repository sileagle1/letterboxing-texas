Railroad Monopoly Game

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-01-22 01:10:25 UTC
A local letterboxer (and reader of this list) suggested that I make a
stamp for frequent finders of our letterboxers. I have sort of done
that, but with a twist, because I want to highlight the upcoming
gathering.

I have carved 4 stamps--logos for B&O, Reading Railroad, Pennsylvania
Railroad & one short line (the South Shore line, which is not the
short line that the monopoly game was based on because I couldn't find
a logo for that one). I am going to hide them in the next couple of
weeks. If you find all of them and bring your stamped page with all
four stamps to the gathering & show one of the Gramatrick Gang, you
will be eligible for a special personal traveler available March
17-19th only.

Now, I'm going to hide these around the greater Houston, because well,
that's where I am. I'd love to think of a way that others in
different parts of the state could play along too. Any thoughts?

One idea is that I can retire them just before the gathering and hide
them around Rusk. I'd love some other suggestions if someone can
think of them. I don't mind carving multiples of the stamps (all but
one is pretty simple), but it seems darned impossible to get stamps
where everyone is.

Thoughts, suggestions and comments are appreciated.

Dewberry
