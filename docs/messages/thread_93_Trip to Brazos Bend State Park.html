Trip to Brazos Bend State Park

From: Birds of a Feather Family (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-05 06:02:28 UTC-07:00
The Birds of a Feather Family had a busy weekend with
Cub Scouts, soccer and band competition but we were
able to look for a couple of letterboxes.

We found Baby Bear's Star Gazer and Silver Eagle's
Brazos Bend letterboxes.

Both boxes had recent visitors in March. The Brazos
Bend stamp has some slight damage - Part of the letter
O is missing.

We enjoyed the trails at the park and recommend that
you take the time for a trip to this park.
