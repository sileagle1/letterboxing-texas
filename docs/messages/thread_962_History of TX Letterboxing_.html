I have some exciting news, guys!

After all the recent discussion of the history of Texas letterboxing, Barefoot Lucy and I have been trying to figure out how to contact Julie, the original Texas letterboxer here in the DFW area. Luckily, Julie is very well known in artist’s circles, so we were able to come up with a current email address. This morning I found an email from her in my mailbox – I was thrilled!

Julie has expressed enthusiasm to get together with us for a “letterbox fest”, and equally exciting – has said she may go ahead and place a few boxes!

I’m hoping she will join our talklist, so you all can get to know her – and in the meantime, Metroboxers, we need to figure out a time & place to get together with Julie ( and possibly a couple of the other friends who letterboxed with her). I had told her that we are tossing around ideas for mini-meets or gatherings some time this late summer/fall.

Perhaps a “Second Semi-Annual Letterboxing Congress and Muffaletta Fest". At Crescent City Caf???
