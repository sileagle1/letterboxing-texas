wildflowers, waterfalls, and letterboxes last weekend!

From: tx2chic (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-04-05 18:34:57 UTC

My husband and I were in Austin and Johnson City area last weekend for
a meeting, but of course took advantage of the chance to enjoy the
wildflowers, walk in the woods, and find LBs! We discovered Oakwood
Cemetery in Austin (what a great place just off I35 in the middle of
the city, but so quiet, huge old trees and ancient tombstones, and a
beatiful stamp! Clever hiding place, too, Gingercat) Saturday we hiked
around in Pedernales Falls SP and found these LBs: Pedernales Possum
(cool stamp, Silver Eagle!), Ziggy Gifts (tricky to follow the trail
and clues, but we were rewarded by being First Finder! My first time
ever to be FF! Thanks, Baby Bear - I love the gold sticker!); and Shrek
(easy find, especially after BB's tricky trail - thanks, MollyGrue).
Then we made a detour to LBJ SP at Stonewall for another of BB's LB's,
Scooby-Doo, where we also saw about 18 deer in the park, before driving
back through the hills and wildflowers (really beautiful near Marble
Falls around Inks Lake, where we saw more deer) and on toward Ft. Worth
and Keller. So many other LBs to find along or near that route, but no
more time this trip. Next drive.... Viewfinder (Benny, tx2chic).
