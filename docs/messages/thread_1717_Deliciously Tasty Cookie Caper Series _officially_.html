Deliciously Tasty Cookie Caper Series "officially" retired!

From: Brandi (brandi@busybeesfamily.com) | Date: 2006-03-01 14:36:48 UTC
Hi everyone,

I just wanted to let you all know that the Cookie Caper Series is
now officially retired. All of the boxes have now been pulled.

"Thanks" to all those who expressed interest in the series! It was
a lot of fun for me to get to meet boxers from all over the state
that I most likely would not have run into on the trails in my neck
of the woods. I think this experience has also made me a better
boxer by forcing me to sit down and carve (even when I thought I
couldn't), write clues (especially those cryptic ones) and plant
boxes in good (ie. safe) hiding places.

For those of you who missed the series this year...never fear! I do
plan on re-hiding the Series next year with our newly
introduced "lemon cookie of some kind" AND our newly retired "Lemon
Pastry Cremes" boxes. Plus the Hitchhiker will be "all new" and
most probably the bonus box, too!

If you are interested in seeing the clues pages from this year, here
is the link:

http://www.busybeesfamily.com/CookieCaperHardToGuessPage.html

Thanks again, Batty B (of the BusyBeesFamily)

PS I entered the Cookie Caper Series onto both LbNA and Atlasquest
for those of you who like to keep track of your finds that way. I
think I may have messed up Lucy Locket's "online logbook" when I
deleted an entry I had made earlier and replaced it with a newer,
more correct entry. Sorry! :-(
