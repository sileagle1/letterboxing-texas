Re: TALE clues?

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-02-21 01:26:37 UTC
You need to print out clues yourself for existing boxes that you want to find. A lot of folks will place boxes in the park Friday and Saturday and won't be able to post them on the Internet, so they will write the clues by hand and they will be laying out on a table for you to copy. Last year, we brought a copy machine and we plan to do that again this year. We'll set it up somewhere near an electric outlet and you can copy the hand-written clues on the copier. It's much easier than writing them all down by hand. By the way, there are other boxes nearby, in Luling and Gonzales, so you might want to print clues for those as well as the ones in the park.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
