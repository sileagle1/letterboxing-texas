Many of y'all know that I am a big one for eating out on the Friday night prior to TALE.  This year I think it would be better to get together in the park instead.  Here are my thoughts on why. 

I had my eye on what I thought would be the perfect place for us to meet for dinner.  It is a fantastic Catfish place---the Catfish Parlor on Ben White.  I was thrilled to see that they even had a separate room that would be perfect for us.  HOWEVER, they don't take reservations for a Friday night during Lent.  Silly me, what was I thinking.  It would be great to eat at an Austin icon, but I am concerned that all the fun places are going to be packed. 

I would also like to mention that we had dinner in the park at the October event, and I thought it was great fun.  But I wasn't the one grilling the hotdogs.  Funny, she just voted to go out to eat.

How about if we plan to picnic in the park unless we have bad weather, and then just happen to coincidentally all show up at the Catfish Parlor?
