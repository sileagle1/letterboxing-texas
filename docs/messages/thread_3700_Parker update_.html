Parker update.

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-08-08 21:08:01 UTC-06:00
 
Most of you know about my hiking companion.  A few of you have had the opportunity to meet him.  Someone on the list asked about him - so I thought I would toss out an update.

 Parker is shocking the vet - by still living. 
 
We asked for a refill on a prescription  last week .   The vet was SHOCKED that  Parker was still alive.  Moni  (my wife) has done a wonderful job of  slowing or halting the  bone cancer.  So good in fact that the vet asked her (Moni) if she (vet) runs into a hopeless case of bone cancer if she (vet) can give her (Moni's) contact info to the owners.  Did that make any sense???
 
Parker still demands his food.  He demands attention.  And gets it!  Today Moni was yelling at him.  She caught him chasing a mouse across the yard!
 
Our first goal was to get him to his 8th birthday , June 1st .  The second goal was to make it past the Oklahoma trip, July 4th weekend.  Both accomplished.  The next goal is to keep him around until James gets his mid-tour leave in Oct.  The vet thinks we are asking a lot.  But this dog has always amazed everyone with his ability to beat the odds.  He was dead twice and almost a third time before he was 6 months old.  That is why he never got sold.
 
He is doing quite well.
 
Here is a photo of us at a puppy training class last week.  Parker doesn't train any more.  But he enjoys the environment.  So we take him.  You can plainly see the bulging lump on his left fore-leg near the orange pillow.  The puppies graduated, and each got a little bag of cookies.  Parker is munching on one - he got his own bag as "BEST SUPPORTING DOG" in the class.
 
 
I am hiking tomorrow , updating a frail track-log for the state park mapping program.  Wish y-all could join me.   I will need to sneak out of the house - lest Parker catch me and demand to go along. 

Mike - KD9KC.
El Paso, Tx.

Parker update.

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-10-08 18:21:40 UTC-06:00

Well - I am shaken. Some things fell into place today.

Those of you who know me, or have had the honor to meet Parker - you know our
situation. Many of you have heard the Parker story, and how he came to be my
dog. And most of you know he is dying of cancer. He should have been gone
last May.

Here is Parker' page.
http://www.vonbonehenge.com/parker.html

I guess you all know my son James is home for a visit from Iraq. He has 12 days
left, then back to the war.

My wife has been raising a batch of puppies. They are cute. All puppies are.
Three are sold. Two are staying. I don't know why - I don't get involved in
the dog showing stuff much. I have my dog, that is all I want or need. Except
for the cancer taking him from me too soon. Then again, 10 years from now would
be too soon.

Here are the puppies. Notice the one with his tongue hanging out? His name is
River. He is all white - except for the black you see in the photo. He sorta
likes me. And strangely enough, Parker likes him too. Usually Parker doesn't
like the impetuous pups! BTW - Daisy is the black pup. She is a real fireball
already!
http://www.vonbonehenge.com/newlitterpage.htm

When I got home today, a message was waiting for me in my E-mail. This lady has
a gift. She also has Great Danes. She has known my wife for years, and knows
all of our dogs.

The message.

====================================================

Mike,

Last night I had one of my dreams. River knew all along he was staying and going
to be your next great dog! He is waiting for you.

Parker knows he must leave but he wanted to make sure he saw James and that you
accepted River first. He just wants to know that you will be ok without him?

Parker's time is just about here but not until James is gone.

My dream mainly revolved around River. He is one super extradinary dog....I
didn't say puppy because he is an old soul. Mike there is something very special
about you that brings these wonderful old souls to you.

Now Daisy is another story. LOL Have fun with her Monika! She is a very young
soul and going to be your real challenge, but you like a challenge dog. LOL She
popped into my dream for a fleeting moment like a whirlwind.

Mike, I hope this gives you some peace with the pain you are going to be facing
in the near future. Rest assure River is there to help you through it. He was
sent to you by your spirit guides.

Vickie
