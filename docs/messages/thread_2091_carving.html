Welcome, Janet!  You didn't waste any time at all getting started, the ink must not even have been dry on the Family Fun article!
 
Don't worry about carving, letterboxing is about a whole lot more than a pretty stamp!  And it gets easier the more you do it.  My first stamps were so awful, I am actually glad many of them went missing.  It is one of those things that if you worry about being perfect you will never start, and if you never try planting a box you miss out on a part of letterboxing that is almost as addictive as finding boxes.  The carving tutorial by Scout Dogs on Atlas Quest is really a great place to start.  Feel free to ask any questions here.  Welcome to Texas Letterboxing!  -Mother of Five
