For those of you who are planting your own boxes in Burroughs Park
for the gathering (regardless of temporary or permanent), please be
sure to bring a copy of your clue(s) to the pavilion. We'll have an
area to post boxes not included in our clue list handout, so we'd
like to have a copy there available for anyone who missed your listing.

Also, if you plan on planting your box really last minute or on
Saturday (meaning it won't make it on LBNA or AQ in time for people
to bring), you can write up your clue by hand and post it in the same
place as well.

Thanks!

Clues to *Jack Aubrey: H.M.S. Surprise*_

This stamp was carved by Mama Fox of The Little Foxes in North Carolina,
and sent to JOY of TeamTexUS especially for this event. This box will be
pulled after the event, so be sure to hunt it now and send a big Texas
howdy to The Little Foxes.

From the Pavillion, walk on the paved trail past the restrooms and
volleyball courts, to the trail map. Walk up the trail 64 footfalls.
Take the grassy trail on your left for 24 footfalls. Look to your left
for a dead tree trunk. The box is at the base of the tree, covered with
bark and sticks. Please re-cover well.
