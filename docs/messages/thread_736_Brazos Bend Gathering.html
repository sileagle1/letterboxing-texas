Brazos Bend Gathering

From: Semalee (Semalee@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-03-23 19:02:16 UTC-06:00
Hi all!
First I want to thank everyone for being so generous and friendly!!  We had a blast this weekend!!  Big thanks especially to all who planned the event especially the Celtic Lions, Silver Eagle and Baby Bear!  Thank you Jennifer of TeamKing for letting me know about this gathering and this wonderful group of people!!  Boxdn I meant to get a T-shirt from you, but ended up leaving without doing so.  Is it possible to mail you a check and have you mail me one?  I can't remember, how much were they?  I may want 2!!  I thoroughly enjoyed the clue game!!  We weren't going to play at first, cause we didn't carve a stamp, but we were talked in to it and boy was I glad!!  We didn't come out in even 10th place, but it was such fun anyway!!  I have to share, though.  We didn't find box #2, and as we were walking to find box #3 my youngest son says to me, "mom, we suck at this game"...  I just laughed and reminded him it is all about the journey, not the destination and that even though he was right, we did "suck" we were having fun, and that was all that mattered LOL!! 
 
I have to admit, I wasn't totally sure about coming, since I really didn't know anyone.  I was afraid we would feel like outcasts, but figured if that happened we could always leave and find the 40 letterboxes that were placed in the park, but that was not the case at all!  Everyone was so friendly, and we felt right at home almost immediately!!  We are so blessed by this group.
 
BabyBear, my boys were so excited to finally see you in person after finding so many of your boxes.  It seems like we "see" you on the trail all the time, and while we were in Colorado last summer we were so excited to find one of your boxes up there!!  They were absolutely marveled by this mysterious box placer who actually turned out to be quite a nice guy!!
 
Anyway, I think next year we will stay a while, and hopefully camp a few nights.  Although, from the stories, I am glad we didn't camp Saturday night!!
 
It was just a great time, and we are so grateful for the experience!!
 
Hope to see you all on the trail soon!
Semalee of MAGS
