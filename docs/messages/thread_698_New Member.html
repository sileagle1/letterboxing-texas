It looks like these gatherings happen about once a year so if I
don't make it over I'll see you next year.

The important thing is, get boxing, the rest will
follow!

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-06-19 20:27:00 UTC-05:00

Hey there, Michelle! Welcome to lone star letterboxing!

 

Michelle may be a newbie, but what a great addition to our sport – she found one of our CelticLion boxes down in Galveston and was kind enough (and responsible enough!) to take the time to let us know! We really appreciate it!

 

As far as “temporary” trail names – I think anything goes. My middle son has a family nickname which he started to use as a trail name, but it was already being used by a very prolific and wonderful Houston boxer, so he quickly chose another: Friar Jon. He just could not come up with a stamp image to carve for that name that pleased him, so he chose his current name “The Gentleman” and carved a very interesting stamp! ( But anyone finding our early boxes may think we have 6 rather than 3 sons!!!)

 

So it’s up to you – one of the most fantastic thing about this sport/hobby/activity is that you can pretty much mold it to suit your family’s needs.

 

And I’m very impressed that you’re already planning on placing boxes! Let us know if we can help-

 

CelticLions
