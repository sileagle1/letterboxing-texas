RE: [letterboxingtexas] Re: DFW Mini-Meet

From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-10-11 09:06:25 UTC-05:00
No rules - just whatever those participating want to do. It can be as easy as meeting at a park for personal exchanges, or making reservations at a casual restaurant for dinner or like the JEN Gathering last October when we met at a campsite at Lake Bardwell for a potluck and a few hidden boxes. Barefoot Lucy had a campsite, Gryzzled Gryphon "sneaked" in overnight and hid a mystery box and about 20 letterboxers were able to get together and meet, share stories, exchange personal stamps and hunt for the new boxes hidden in the area. It was very laid back and fun - especially because we were all trying to solve GG's very cryptic clues to his letterbox!
PS
