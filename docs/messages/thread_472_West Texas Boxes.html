Hello everyone,
I was in El Paso the week before Thanksgiving. Wanted to look for
the oldest Texas boxes out there from 2000. One in Franklin
Mountains and 2 in Hueco Tanks. As luck would have it I didn't get
to look for any of them. The weather did not co-operate at all. The
2 days I had available to letterbox had winds of 30 miles per hour
and heavy rain and cold temperatures.
However, I did get to stop at Davis Mountain State Park on the way
home and find 2 boxes and plant one of my own. Clues will be posted
soon for the one I planted.
I went to El Paso to visit my brother who is being sent to Iraq.
Please pray for our military men and women.
I will be going to Georgia on the 10th of Dec. Looking forward to
finding some boxes there.
