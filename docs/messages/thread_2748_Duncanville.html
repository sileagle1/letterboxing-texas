Don't overlook clues for nearby boxes that are actually in Dallas, not Duncanville, but are in that vicinity since Dallas sort of wraps around parts of Duncanville.  I'm thinking of my 2 boxes in Cedar Hill Nature Preserve, which is just a very short distance west of  those parks west of Duncanville, and of Silver Eagle and Baby Bear's two boxes in Cedar Hill State Park, which is just immediately west of the Nature Preserve.  And you might also check to see if PuddleSplasher's Wren Box is still in the Nature Preserve, although it has been reported missing (could be there, who knows, or maybe she'll have replaced it before your trip this way).  Actually, we're hoping you'll add a couple of your boxes to this area while you're up this way, too, of course!
