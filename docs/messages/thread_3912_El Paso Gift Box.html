From: Cancan (cancan1959@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-17 23:36:02 UTC-06:00

Dear Fellow Boxers in West Texas ;)

I blew into town on business and left you a small token of my appreciation of all the boxes you have planted in your home area. 

I hope this will tied you over until I can return for pleasure and not only hunt for YOUR boxes, but bring a few more.  My desire on this trip was to take the tram last night and eat a late lunch in Fabens today.  That quickly turned to disappointment when things did not go my way.  I arrived in downtown last night and had a business meeting at the Museum of History this morning. Of course the meeting went way later and my return flight was at 5:30pm. So all I could do was leave a remnant of my whirlwind trip...because when I shamelessly begged for more Hill Country boxes...I listened too.

Kind Regards,
Cancan
