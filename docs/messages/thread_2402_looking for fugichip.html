Lufkin Area Boxes (was Re: [letterboxingtexas] looking for fugichip)

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-20 14:43:55 UTC-06:00
Shelley,
 
If you are driving that far, you may also want to look up a bit further toward southern Nacogdoches and the SFA Experimental Forest for a few more boxes to find (and more still if you go on in to Nacogdoches).  There are several there in the forest and it really isn't that far from Lufkin.  About 45 minutes north of Lufkin is Rusk, where there alone are over 30 boxes (and we're having a small gathering there on January 20th).  
 
In fact, you can basically box your way up Highway 59, stopping in Livingston for several, detouring over to Cold Spring/San Jacinto National Forest for several more, stopping off at Diboll for one and hitting Lufkin for a few.  You can stay on Highway 59 to Nacogdoches and on to Henderson, or you can take Highway 69 from Lufkin on up to Rusk and then Jacksonville.  All these towns (except Diboll, which only has one, but it's not hard to get to) have a good number of boxes!
 
And if you ever decide to make that trip, holler at me!
