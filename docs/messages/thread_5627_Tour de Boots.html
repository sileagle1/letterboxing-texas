Tour de Boots

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2018-09-19 20:54:16 UTC


I just returned from a backpacking trip with my son in Colorado and since I was driving to meet him there I decided to find some old boxes placed by Boots Tex over 10 years ago.  I am ashamed it has taken me this long to look for them, but better late than never.  I found 4 out of 6 boxes and besides getting great stamps I learned many interesting things about Comanches, the Texas State Photographer renowned for his wildlife pictures, the Seedless Watermelon Capital of Texas, and a horned toad sealed in the cornerstone of the courthouse in Eastland for 31 years that was still alive when uncovered.  I even went to the courthouse to see it in its little coffin!  Even though these boxes were off the beaten path, they are worth the effort and I highly recommend them.  As an extra incentive, I placed several boxes along the way to lure you to that part of TX, including 4 more Famous Trees of TX and another in my TX Baseball series.


Silver Eagle
