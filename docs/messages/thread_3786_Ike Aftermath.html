Glad to hear you are ok, and I agree with you about the heavy heart,
but also the pride in the people of Houston and surrounding areas.
My in-laws live on Tiki Island and their house has major damage, but
is still there. I went with them yesterday to help get some things
and pile the debris on the edge of the property. The stairs were
gone so I had to climb up my extension ladder that we brought. They
are not able to climb the ladder so it was a challenge for me to find
and get the correct items they were requesting, especially clothes
for my Mother in-law (no, not those shoes, the other black ones).
The destruction on the island was depressing, so I can only imagine
what it is like on Galveston and other areas harder hit. I'm sure
they will be staying with us for at least a month until their house
is habitable again.

Another interesting thing I noticed after Ike was that when I finally
got power after 5 days and had a chance to check email, I had twice
as many people asking how Silver Eagle was than the "real" me.
Instead of assuming my alter ego has more friends than me, I choose
to rationalize that the letterboxing community is just a great group!
Take care everyone.

Silver Eagle
