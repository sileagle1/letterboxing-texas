From: (harland@pnx.com) | Date: 2005-01-06 21:14:32 UTC-06:00
My experience last year was that the campsites were first-come-first-choice. That is the way Texas parks do it. Most of the letterboxers ended up really close together since we all went for sites around the shelter where we were to meet for the gathering. So, I would think that if you have a choice get a site near the shelter. Makes it easier to get your stuff to the shelter that morning too. And---then we can walk from your tent, after coffee meeting, to the shelter---an added bonus! lol
