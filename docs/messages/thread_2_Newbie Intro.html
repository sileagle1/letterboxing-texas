Newbie Intro

From: Susan (ssmith043@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-18 20:49:32 UTC
Hi, my name is Susan Tashiro. I am very new to letterboxing. I've
been reading about geocaching and chanced upon
www.letterboxing.org I saw that there are quite a few letter boxes
in South Texas...and a bunch around where I live, in Beeville. I
saw that one was here in Beeville, so I went out this morning and
found it...my very first. I really wasn't prepared...didn't have a
rubber stamp or inkpad with me. I'll have to go back and stamp it.
I can't wait to go check out those at Goliad and the other
surrounding places. This is going to be so much fun!

Are any of you close to me? Wanda, I saw you name on the box here.
Are you in Beeville?

Since there are so few of us on here and it seems that you all
already know each other, would it be a problem for you all to
introduce yourself to me?

I sure wish it wasn't so windy. I'd go to Goliad right now.

Susan

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-01-19 20:22:56 UTC
Welcome to Letterboxing, Susan! Always good to have another
Texas letterboxer since there are so few of us. I have been
letterboxing for about 1 1/2 years and have been busy
placing boxes hoping for this hobby to catch on here in Texas.
There were less than 10 boxes in Texas when I started so we
have come a long way! In fact, I think we are ready for
our first Event, so keep watching the main Talklist for
my announcement about it.

Silver Eagle
