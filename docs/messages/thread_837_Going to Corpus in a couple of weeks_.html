From: monkeytoes108 (cruschhaupt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-13 08:07:19 UTC
The family and I are finally getting way for a few days. Illness
and car wrecks have kept us homebound for far too long. We decided
to go to Corpus in a couple of weeks and of course, letterboxing
will be done! It looks like most of the boxes in Corpus are actualy
missing, but there are quite a few in the surrounding area. There
seems to be good groupings in a few spots, but I know we'll only be
able to get to one, maybe two of them. Our options are:

Aransas Pass Wildlife Refuge-Karankawa, Hurricane Claudette, mustang
(missing),Hog Lake Whooper, Aransas Whooper.

Goose Island State Park- Texas Bonefish, Big Tree Letterbox, Rest In
Peace #3, Missing Key #1.

Rob & Bessie Welder Park- Hiker's maze, and the Looping Lassos Series
(5 boxes, 2 possibly missing)

Thoughts? Comments? Personal expierences? Have any of the missing
boxes been replaced? Special requests? I will have 20 month old
girl and 6 month old fetus tagging along.

Thanks a bunch.
MonkeyToes

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-05-13 13:27:08 UTC
Though I love Aransas and Goose Island parks, I think they
are better visited in the winter when the Whooping Cranes
are in residence and mosquitoes are gone. I would concentrate
more on Corpus Christi area, especially the beach at North
Padre Island or Mustang Island. There may not be as many
boxes, but it will be more relaxing.
Sorry my 2 boxes in Corpus area are missing (Corpus Porpoise
and Texas Turtle), I just have not been down that way yet.
I have them ready to replace, so if you want to replace
them for me just email me with your address. You would be
guaranteed 2 finds! Hope you have fun.

Silver Eagle
