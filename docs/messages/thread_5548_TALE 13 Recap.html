TALE 13 Recap

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2016-04-04 09:26:22 UTC-07:00

Another TALE has come and gone and even though the park trails were limited (plus muddy) and it got a little cold at night, I think it was a success.  We had several new TX letterboxers attend along with some from 4 other states (California, Arizona, Wisconsin & Louisiana).  Even Ghost Of IRA Boxer made an "appearance".  The weather was great during the day and everyone took advantage by finding some of the many boxes.  Blue Butterfly showed her determination by getting muddy in the process and almost losing her boots.  As a consolation she won a free mud bath.  The campfire Saturday night was fun and I really enjoyed sharing stories with those that were there. 

Since the event started on April Fool's Day, it was appropriate that a prank was played, even if it was on me.  I'm sure somebody will post pictures for those with strong stomachs.  Speaking of pictures, I have created an album called TALE13 in the Photos section of this Yahoo Group and posted several of my own pictures.  Feel free to add any you have.    

https://groups.yahoo.com/neo/groups/LETTERBOXINGTEXAS/photos/albums/1568885036

Thanks to everyone who attended; without y'all it would not have been possible.  Looking forward to TALE 14!

Silver Eagle
