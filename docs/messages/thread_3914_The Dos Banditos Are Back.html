The Dos Banditos Are Back

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2009-01-20 03:49:24 UTC
Baby Bear and I made it back late Sunday from a very successful trip.
I hope to post clues and a trip report in the next few days, but in
summary: we placed boxes in all 8 remaining virgin counties in South
Region, the 1 remaining virgin county (Kinney) in Central Region, and
even managed to put one in virgin Schleicher county of West Region. We
also placed several other boxes in other counties along the way, for a
total of over 40 boxes between us. We had fun but are exhausted!

It looks like I missed some fireworks on the Big List while I was
gone. I should know better than to post a comment right before leaving
on a trip. Glad it did not last too long, and I really appreciate all
the nice comments and support I received from my letterboxing friends.
Actually letterboxing is way more fun than debating it!

Oh, and welcome home James!

Silver Eagle
