Joy, I just realized who you are by your stamp -- we loved that Texans stamp, especially my husband thought that was really cool, and I love your name -- I am not good at coming up with cute stuff like that -- we did see your stamps.  Did you know that Magnolia crossroads is at the same cementary as time flies???  You could of done that one also, a little tricky, but a fun one to do.  When we were at Howard Hughes one, there were a lot of people coming to see his gravesite.  I kept thinking they were letterboxers also, but they weren't - the looked and then left.  I bet 4 people came up during that time.    ANITA
