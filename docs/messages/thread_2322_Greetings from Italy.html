Greetings from Italy

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2006-11-26 08:03:36 UTC
On Thanksgiving day, we found the first letterbox planted in Italy,
called "A Little Bit of Austin in Italy", and we were first finders, so
we were the first to ever find a letterbox in Italy. We think that's
pretty cool. It's hidden in an abandoned castle on top of a mountain
looking down on a small village far below in a valley, a town called
Gaiole in Chianti, which is the Tuscany region. It was only about 20
minutes from the farm where we stayed and had Thanksgiving dinner with
out daughter and her family. The box was placed by the Terrels of
Austin. We'll be back home next weekend and maybe I can post some
pictures of the castle. Ciao!

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
