I thought my colorful chameleon came back. . .

From: janet clark (jayceeinbedford@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-07 21:20:01 UTC-07:00
I received an AQ mail yesterday saying that a family had found "A Color of His Own". I was so excited, I started a reply with questions and comments about it.
 
This was my first carving to be used by other people. I had only carved my sig stamp prior to carving this cute little chameleon. He was carved for The Great Book Adventure in Houston in October 06.
 
Before sending my reply to this family, I decided to look at their found date. It was for back then, before he came up missing.
 
I was so bummed out. But, for a second there, I knew how y'all felt after being told that a box was missing and then all of a sudden it came back. It's a wonderful feeling.
 
Janet, Blooming Flowers
