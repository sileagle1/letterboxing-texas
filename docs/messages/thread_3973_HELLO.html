Hello all. We are new to the site. I 1st heard about letterboxing a
few years ago, right before we moved to TX from AZ. I lost the article
& could not remember what it was called, until recently one of my
search guesses brought me to it.
I searched through the LB North America & Atlasquest websites & found
a few in our area. We live in Lake Jackson, S. of Houston. I know my
husband & I with our 4 daughters are going to have a great time looking
for the boxes. They love having scaventure hunts on Easter & their
birthdays.
Does anyone know of any boxes around here that I might not have found
on the web site? We're also making a trip to the Brazos Bend Sate Park
in May for a family reunion, we hope to fid some of the many boxes
there. We also make a trip to AZ every summer to vist family. I found
several around Mesa & Apache Jct.
Hopefully we can gather some good hunting tips from here & plant a
few boxes of our own. I'm sure my girls will enjoy these adventures for
years to come.

Thanks everyone,
The Taylors
