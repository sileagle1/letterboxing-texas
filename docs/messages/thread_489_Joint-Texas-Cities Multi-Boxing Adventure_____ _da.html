Joint-Texas-Cities Multi-Boxing Adventure!!!!! (day 1)

From: Hawk (omkarmashanti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-08 18:07:07 UTC-08:00
Krohm (Ft. Worth) and I (Houston) boxed today in the
Houston area. We didn't get near the numbers that
Silver Eagle and Baby Bear got when they went to the
DFW area, but we're happy with the day. We found the
following boxes:

Tribute to England Series #4a (alive and well)
Lone Star Gal (alive and well)
Hershey's Kiss (alive but wet)
Hot Air Fun (alive and well with cardinals perched on
it)
Eyes of Texas (alive and well)
Ode to Soccer (alive and well)
Tribute to England Series #8 (alive and well with
great libations imbibed)
Crimson Roar (alive and well)
Strings Attached (alive and well)
Out on a Limb (MISSING: washed away with the last
storm)
Java Joy (alive and well)
The Heights of Quaint (alive and well)
Little Brown Bird (alive and well, but a bit lonely:
come on y'all!!!!)
Impossible Triangle (alive and well)

We plan to get out and about tomorrow to find even
more boxes.

What a great way to celebrate Corazon's birthday!!!!

;)

Hawk
(krohm lurking over my shoulder)
