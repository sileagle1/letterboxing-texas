From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-22 07:47:29 UTC-05:00
I was there just last week and Jim is right, the gauge-type carving tools are near the models - if you are facing the back of the store, they are midway down one aisle in that area and on the left.  (They have two or three aisles of models)  It's very easy to miss them - it's a small area at eye level!  If you prefer to try exactos, they are midway on the right, but not on the same aisle (I don't think).  They are on the same aisle as pine racing cars, which are at the back end of the aisle on the right.
