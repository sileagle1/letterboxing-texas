I have just listed the clues for the first five boxes in what promises to be a spectacular display of Texas Talent! (See Mythical Creatures, the Beginning on LBNA and AQ.) I listed each carver in the text of the clues, if someone knows a better way to do it, please let me know.  My plan is to continue the series with 5 more boxes in each of the next two sections of Terry Hershey Park, so when all are in place it can either be done in three parts, or for those with more stamina (ahem, Runsforchocolate) all at once.  Hike lengths are listed from the car and back to the parking.
