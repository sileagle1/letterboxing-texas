At the Library Event, June 19

From: Maureen Callahan (got2tri@hotmail.com) | Date: 2014-06-13 16:13:54 UTC-05:00
Next week is the "At the Library" letterboxing event.  It will take place at the East Parker County Library in Aledo, TX on Thursday, June 19.  I know, it's on a Thursday...but some of you may not have to work on Thursdays or some of you may have vacation time you need to take or some of you lucky people may be retired.  If you're available, please think about coming.  There are a few boxes hidden around and in the library, and yes, Virginia, there is even an event stamp!  Plus, if you haven't made it up to this area lately, there have been quite a few boxes hidden over the last year.  Yes, letterboxing is alive and doing well in North Texas.  It's a hip, hopping, happening kind of place.  Here's the event:  http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?eventId=3212      Thanks to PhotoPam and kiddos for hosting this one.  Hope to see you there.
