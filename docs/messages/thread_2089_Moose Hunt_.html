Moose Hunt!

From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2006-08-20 19:25:42 UTC-04:00
I've been waiting and waiting for cooler weather to make that 3-mile round trip hike to find all of PuddleSplasher's Moose Series, but no, this was the day!  First we found Rather Be Paddlin' (and watching those out on the lake doing that, I'd definitely rather have been paddlin'!).  Then we trekked the north shore of Lake Grapevine, extending the hike another half-mile because PS told me (it's really level for the next little bit, and just a short walk to the Nature Center Trail, so we might as well, while we're already here.  Never believe her, by the way.  It was up and down and up and down and NOT a short walk, but still, very scenic, and thankfully most of that trail is actually in the shade (a blessing!) and there were occasional sort-of breezes off the lake, plus the occasional friendly swipe of wet dog passing by after a short swim to cool off (wish it had been me!).  Forgot the part in her clues about taking fruit or trailmix bars, but did have plenty of water, so it wasn't really so bad.  And I did finally get to find all of those Moose boxes.  This is a great series, the clues have funny backstories and the stamps as always with PS are really little works of art, so it was a good morning.  And here's another good part:  maybe I lost 5 lbs on that trail, who knows!  I'm not checking, I'm just believing it must be so.  If you haven't made that hike, go prepared, but go.  It's worth it, even if you do decide to wait for cooler weather.  (And she did replace the missing #4 box on this hike today).
