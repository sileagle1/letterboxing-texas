New boxer's Adventure

From: Baby Bear (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-08 18:51:55 UTC
Below is a write up sent to me from a guy I play softball with,
while finding my "Fire in the Hole" box. He and his family went out
this weekend to give Letterboxing a try. Here is what happened.....

Message:
So....we ventured out on a Saturday afternoon for a fun-filled
family
'treasure hunt.' Little did we know we would have to be explaining
our
actions to the Constable for over 30 minutes!!!
.
Needless to say, our first adventure is one we will not soon forget.
.
We went to the park, me, wife, 2 little girls - baseball bat in hand
(to knock away anything that may be living around the box). It
seems
that some of the neighbors thought that looked a little strange and
decided to make a phone call.
Well, being our first time, it took us a little while to get the
directions right and actually find the box. So, as we're sitting on
the path
stamping our pads and talking about it, we see the cop drive up, and
just stay in his car by the exit to the park. We take 10-15 minutes
to
complete the stamping, put the box back in place, and make our way
to
the exit.
As we make our way out, he gets out of his car - "Everything ok out
there." (I think he senses we are harmless since we have 2 little
girls
in tow, and the bat was plastic). We explain ourselves and what we
are
doing out there, letterbox, stamps, etc... It all seems to go over
his
head as he just looks at us dumbfounded. He then started rambling
on
about wishing they'd put bathrooms out there, glad they put the
fence up
'cause all those dumb kids with their ATV's were tearing up the
park,
yadda, yadda, yadda...
.
Anyway, thought you would find it funny that the first one we try,
we
almost get arrested!
