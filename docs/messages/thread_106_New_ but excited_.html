From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-04-16 21:07:12 UTC
Welcome to Texas Letterboxing! Don't feel bad that you
live in an area without many letterboxes, there are many
others in the same boat. Maybe you can eventually place
a few of your own to help populate the area.
I'm curious when you found my Texas Star box and what shape
it was in since I have not heard from it in a while and
like to keep up with the status of my boxes.
Bob Woodward Park box is not only missing but abandoned
since the owner no longer participates. I am trying to
get the box moved off of the Texas Clue Page and put in
the letterbox basement (where abandoned box clues go) so
others won't make wasted trips.
As far as Spider's Lair & Halloween, I just visited them
in March and they were doing fine. They are both in
Arcadia Trail Park and don't require GPS or geocaching
abilities. Just follow the clues and you will find both.
There is another box that was placed in your area recently
called the Stampbook Box that I have not looked for yet
so you have at least 3 to look for.
The best place to find clues & info. for boxes is at:

http://www.letterboxing.org/

There are other letterboxing information and talklist sites, too.
I have many of the links on my webpage at:

http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/

Good luck and have fun!

Silver Eagle
