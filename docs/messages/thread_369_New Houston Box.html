New Houston Box

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-11-27 22:38:40 UTC-06:00
We planted a new box today, "Austin ain't got nothin' on us now!" in downtown Houston.  No offense, all you Austinites out there, I think Austin is a perfectly wonderful place.  But in case you Houstonians haven't heard, we have our own colony of Mexican Freetail bats living in downtown Houston, and watching them emerge is a pretty spectacular sight!  Here is a link if you would like to read more about them http://www.chron.com/disp/story.mpl/life/3460622.html.  We have seen them before, and went again tonight, even in this windy, cold weather there were lots of bats to be seen!
 
By the way, Silver Eagle, just reading everything you did in such a short amount of time made me tired!  Glad to hear you had such a great trip.  When I first came to Texas I was obsessed with seeing a javelina, and Choke Canyon was the first place I ever saw one, they walked right through our campsite!
 
Anyway, have fun!  -Mother of Five
