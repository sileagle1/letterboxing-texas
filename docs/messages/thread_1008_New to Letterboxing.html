From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-09-06 11:21:11 UTC-05:00
Welcome fivetxhikers! Letterboxing is such a great activity! Sometimes when you can't find a box, they actually are missing (dragged away by a curious animal or perhaps not rehidden well by the last finder, and thus discovered and thrown away). However, sometimes they are right where the placer said they would be but you may have misinterpreted a clue - that happens to me a lot! That's part of the fun of letterboxing. It's helpful when you attempt to find a box (whether you find it or not) to contact the placer and let them know what your experience was. That way, if it is missing, they can check on it and either list it as missing on the clue sheet, or maybe give you another clue to help you find it! Anyway, I hope your family enjoys letterboxing!
Puddle Splasher
