Woke up this fine morning in our campsite to find a frightened cajun character named Junior huddled in the corner of Lightning's extra sleeping bag.

It took some convincing, but Junior is weary of Zydeco Joe's single-minded obsession with our tour bus and with Pancho Juan's persistent pursuit and ominous presence.

We devised a daring plan to snatch the bus right from beneath their noses.

We sneaked up on the two of them, who appeared to have fallen asleep while staring each other down - the bus at their feet.

While Junior made a huge racket, Lightning slipped in and grabbede the bus, tossing it out to Fearless Fox. I backed up Junior to improvise a story if needed.

Now the bus is hidden safely away, at least for the moment. Anyone interested in claiming it had better hurry.

It can be found at the state park in the town known for its chicken and sausage.
Go to the site where the flag flies free over the 403. Sorry for being so cryptic, but we are trying to keep the bus a step ahead of Zydeco Joe and Pancho Juan.
