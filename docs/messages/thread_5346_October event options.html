From: (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2014-03-05 08:15:26 UTC-08:00

Several people at TALE expressed an interest in camping together for the next event, so I need your feedback.  I'm looking at Lake Somerville state park for the event in October.  I'm currently looking at the weekend of 24th-26th. There are two good options for the facility -
At Birch Creek (the northern park) there is a group rec hall (with kitchen) surrounded by campsites (14 pull-thru sites, and 6 tent sites) the tent sites are water only, but we would have the rec hall all of Friday and Saturday night so there should be plenty of power for food and charging electronics. We would be in an isolated area and surrounded by a .5 mile loop trail (perfect for night boxing!).  This is my first choice, but it's expensive - so we really need 7 or 8 people to commit to camping at $20 per night to make it work.
At Nails Creek there is an enclosed group pavilion (AC, heat, power, water, but no kitchen).  This is my second choice if we don't have enough campers.  If you want to camp together I'll reserve several sites and get them all together early Friday morning. 

So who is interested in camping for the event? (FYI - I'll post this on AQ for those who don't follow the yahoo group)
