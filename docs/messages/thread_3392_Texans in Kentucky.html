Texans in Kentucky.  See, now, that just sounds like fun.  Y'all should just pick a Saturday this fall and come on up.  We could do a Texas Chili Cook Off (Giant Armadillos Optional) kind of event.  Dust off the crock pots, have several chilis made by several participants.  Hang out a large piece of paper and have people stamp which one they liked most, if we don't go for a folded paper voting.  I'd plan on telling everyone that there's a stamp at the bottom of the pot...that should get them spooning up bowls of chili.  I have all kinds of crazy visions in my head over this transplanted Texas Chili Cookoff possibilities.  
 
Not that anyone should think that all the events I host are food related, I do plan on having an all Beatles one later this summer.  Wow us into silence if you can turn your sig stamp into a Beatles type reference.  Just need to pick a date for that one too.
 
Let's hope that you guys do make it up this way some time soon!
 
MoWizLiz
