i could try to explain why i've been unavailable for so long, but it
is much faster to just apologize profusely for the delay in publishing
the following:

turn East onto Beltline from 35E (in Carrollton).  after passing under
the back-to-back traffic lights (the intersections with Broadway and
Denton), take the third left turn lane opportunity onto Erie.  turn
right onto Walnut.  park in the smaller parking lot on your right that
is right in front of a small lawn.  park between the two trees closest
to the parking lot.  walk in the direction you parked (South, i
believe) until you come to the other side of a bit of brush on your
left.  there is a fallen log, and there might still be a red rug
leading to the tour bus, which is under the log.

i planted this three weeks ago before i left the land of unfettered
access to the internet, so i again find myself giving uncertain clues

Sorry you had to crawl around to find it - it's so big that finding the right place to conceal it was tricky, although I didn't expect it to have to wait long for a finder!  That's a pretty exciting find for a new letterboxer - it must have 50 passengers by now!  If you aren't quite sure what to do with it, you might email Teamking on this list since she created it.  The idea is, add a passenger and stamp the logbook and get it back "on the road" soon, and notify the letterboxingtexas@yahoogroups.com of its new location.  You can see why having it carries a responsibility - all those important Texas personages!  There was a scare last spring when it had apparently disappeared for awhile, and we were all nervous that it had been lost!
