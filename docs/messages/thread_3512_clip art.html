I like google images myself.  Go to Google, then look across the top for Images and click.  Then type what you are looking for and it will usually come up with pages and pages.  For myself, I don't care much for sites that are specifically clip art because they seem like the generate a lot of pop-ups and have lots of moofah that gets in the way of finding what I want, or want me to download software, or ....ad nauseam.
