Spontaneous Exchange!!!!!

From: Hawk (omkarmashanti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-17 14:14:51 UTC-08:00
I just had the most awesome experience!!! I ran into
another letterboxing group "on the trail". Actually,
we were all in the parking lot, but we were all in
pursuit of boxes at Memorial Park Arboretum and Nature
Center. I was returning to the car from finding "Bear
Hunting" and placing a box of my own (watch for clues
on letterboxing.org for "Koala-ty Time Together").
Another vehicle pulled in next to my car. The door
opened, and the driver had two journal-type books and
a piece of paper which was immediately recognized as
"clues". I think I caught Dewberry a bit off guard,
but after introductions, we had a great time
exchanging stamps: me, Dewberry, Patrick, and Graham
(great seasonal personal stamp, Graham!).

What a wonderful surprise!!!!! I'm still giddy with
excitement!
