Re: [letterboxingtexas] Denton Trip this weekend

From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2006-08-14 22:18:26 UTC-04:00
I haven't been up to Bob Jones for awhile, but as far as I know all is available there - Bat is actually on an equestrian trail near the lakeshore (just have that flashlight if you try to do it in the dark - who knows what you might step on out there!).  Roadrunner is also on an equestrian/dirt trail up in the park, but not far off a paved trail.  Neither is a long hike from your car, so both should be doable about any time.  If you were thinking of finding those two in the cemeteries in Southlake, it's possible that Lonesome Dove is locked at night.  Labyrinth is in a cemetery that has no real fence around most of it, so it's open, but they're doing some construction there on the old chapel and I don't know what that might mean if you were sneaking around there in the dark!  Better to try those in daylight if possible. If you get that far into Southlake, you might as well come to Grapevine or Keller and find some of those - you can certainly find the Bear Creek series in the dark, or the Grapevine Heritage Park boxes, unless you run into teenagers or something scary like that!  Knowing your stories, though, Lucy, you may have to give me a call to come bail you out of a local lockup!
