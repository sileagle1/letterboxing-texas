Strangest thing happened over the weekend - I got a tattered and bedraggled letter back in the mail.  I couldn't read all of the "sent to" address block, except to see that it was my own handwriting, and I could make out my return address, and inside was a patch order I sent out quite some time ago.  I could read a little of it, but I couldn't match it against anything I still had in my in-box and I haven't heard from anyone saying "HEY!  I placed an order and haven't received it!", so at the moment I'm not sure who this is supposed to go to. 
 
If you placed a patch order from me and you haven't yet received it, would you email me with your address and the number of patches you were expecting?  If the information you send me matches what I have available, I'll send it along to you!

No, don't say that. I just went to the post office today and mailed a package that was a couple of weeks late as it is.

When I was driving to there, I was planning my day (remember, I get up no earlier than 3pm). I pictured walking into the post office, mailing my package and walking out. WRONG. I was there at least a half hour. I had to pull out all my patience skills. These people were mailing 5-10 packages each. I had one little letterbox (really 2 in one envelope because I'm smarter than these other people). What could they possibly be mailing in so many packages. Don't they know that the post office is for postals and Netflix? The absolute nerve of some people. And then, to show up at the same time as me. We won't even begin that rant and rave.

When did the world stop revolving around me? Why wasn't I informed of this catastrophe?
