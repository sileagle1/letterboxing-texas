There is a cafe in Maydelle, which is about 7 miles
from the State Park, and they serve all-you-can-eat
out-of-this-world catfish on the weekends. They don't
have a huge amount of seating, maybe 10 tables of 4,
but I think they would be thrilled to serve us (if we
let 'em know we're all coming)! It's a laid-back
little country store kind of place, a great way to
start a laid-back little country gathering.

For myself, I plan on taking off the Friday of the
gathering, so if anyone needs/wants me to check them
in at the campsite so they can get a good site, I'll
be glad to do that if you've paid in advance (of
course, that means you also have to tell me what your
definition of "good" is).
