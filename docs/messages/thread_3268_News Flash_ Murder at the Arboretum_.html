News Flash: Murder at the Arboretum!

From: thetexians (thetexians@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-01-04 14:35:46 UTC
A body has been discovered on a popular hiking trail at an arboretum
nearbu. Police have identified the victim is one Ira Boxer, age
unknown, of Houston. Little is known of Mr. Boxer, and attempts to
locate next of kin have failed. When found, Mr. Boxer was wearing
only red boxer shorts decorated with white hearts. In his hand he
held a rubber stamp, the image of which was the same red shorts with
white hearts and the letters "IRA". In addition, a log book
imprinted with many stamped images was lying in the dirt nearby.
The coroner, based on clues found at the scene, has ruled the death
a homicide.
With certain information contained in the logbook, as well
as clues found at the scene, several suspects have been identified.
They are:

Sterling Seagull
Kid Kodiak
Suzy Shoeless
Rubus Trivialis
Jogsfer Jellybeans

For more information on this tragic story, click on the
following link:

http://bootstex.blogspot.com/2007/12/murder-at-arboretum.html
