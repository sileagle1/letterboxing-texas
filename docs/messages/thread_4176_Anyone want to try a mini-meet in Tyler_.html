Anyone want to try a mini-meet in Tyler?

From: Heather (heathercarnes@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2009-07-09 03:07:48 UTC
I thought it might be fun to have a back-to-school meet for the teacher letterboxers and a good time for everybody else. Our church has a recreation/nature facility (called the ROC) located just south of the Loop 49 and Highway 69 intersection in Tyler. I have reserved a pavillion for us on Saturday, August 8 from 8 a.m. - 2 p.m. The pavillion has a charcoal grill and electrical outlets (fans!). The ROC has a 2+ acre stocked pond (come join us - we'll do a little fishing early in the morning), baseball & soccer fields, frisbee golf course, a one-mile paved walking trail, and nature trails throughout the woods (although I'm told that one trail has a lot of poison ivy right now and I'm not sure it will be cleared by August). After the event is over, you can hang around town and locate (some of the) hundreds of boxes within a 30 mile radius. Lunch will be a potluck. I'll keep everyone updated as we get closer to the event.

I'll have to get the address/directions to those who sign up. Try as I might, everything I use for the location puts this even on Rock Road (it is NOT!). *shrug, smile*

I've posted the event on AtlasQuest. Let me know if you have any questions. I'd love to meet all of you. Hope to see you soon.

Heather
