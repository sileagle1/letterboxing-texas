From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-15 23:19:15 UTC-05:00
LOL...no.  You did fine.  I picked yours up already and put them in the book.
 
Some folks like to wait until the day of to put their clues out and they either bring copies with them or put a copy out for folks to write down.  That way the log entries start on the gathering day.
 
For myself, if I get a chance to plant a box in advance, I'd rather go ahead and put it out on the internet so I don't have to worry about copies.  When I go to a gathering, I get a bad case of sensory overload.  I wind up with stuff spread from one end of the group to the other, and juggling logbooks, personal travelers, hitchhikers, and a variety of other things.  If I have to juggle extra pieces of paper - it's just too much so I just don't! 
 
So - as with just about everything else in letterboxing, the choice is yours how you do it.
