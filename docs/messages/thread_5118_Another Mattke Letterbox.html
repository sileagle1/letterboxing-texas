Another Mattke Letterbox

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2013-01-02 04:28:03 UTC
I didn't think the Box Of Ants box was by the Mattke family but while I was looking through my old notes I found clues to another letterbox placed by the Mattke family during the first TX letterbox event held at Bastrop SP. They gave me a slip of paper with the clues hand-written on it so Baby Bear and I could look for it after the Event. They intended to post the clues on LBNA but never did, so I doubt anyone else ever got to look for the box. The letterbox is called TCM and I have scanned the original note and placed it in the Files section of this List. Not sure if the box is still there but the location is worth going to (it is not in Bastrop SP). After looking at the clues, see if you can figure out what TCM stands for and what is significant about its location.

Silver Eagle
