From: lennire (lennire@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-20 14:55:21 UTC
Yesterday, we intended to go letterboxing.

The clues didn't really tell us much, but we were in the right place.
We looked and found no box.

However...
The place led us to this little creek. The water was low, slow and
clear and there were little fish swimming. And it was hot. Yes, I took
my shoes off and wadded. It felt SO good. And it felt so much like a
summer activity! It was just pretty.

That's what I like about letterboxing. It can bring you to some very
interesting places. And honestly, I'm not disappointed that we didn't
find the box. I had fun!

The only thing that I dislike is that it's hot. And when I get hot, my
face turns BEET RED.
