I'm Tx dragonfly. I chose the name because I like dragonflys
and I am transplanted to TX (a little over 10 years ago). I LB in
Cleveland (Ohio) every time I go to visit family and thought the tx
part added to who I was now. I've been letterboxing for 2 years. I
recently moved to Smith county from Bell. This is a much more box
rich area and we've enjoyed exploring the area through hunting.
My favorite thing about LBing is seeing places that I never
would have otherwise. There was a small but cute residential park
(even has a little trail and a creek) near where I lived for 25+
years that I never knew was there until I found a clue leading to
it. Other than that, I love everything about it - I like nature and
to hike and be outside anyway, I like the stamps, the logbooks, the
challenge... And this obsession allows me to hike in the parks with
a few family members and friends who would not otherwise hike. As
long as I don't scare them off from hunting with me by being too
obsessed... I've been known to say - "ok you stay here and rest and
I'll run the rest of the way to find "just one more", bring it back,
then take it back to rehide it". (So what if we haven't eaten in 7
hours??? And I'm the one that gets hungry 1st and usually gets a
headache and nauseous (and cranky) when I haven't eaten...). Except
I don't get cranky when I'm letterboxing. :)
