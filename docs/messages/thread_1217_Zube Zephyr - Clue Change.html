Zube Zephyr - Clue Change

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-08-21 03:11:47 UTC
I took the family to Zube Park today for the free train
ride and to give my very first placed letterbox some well
deserved maintenance. It had recently been saved from the
creek by an observant letterboxer and I wanted to get it
up higher to avoid future floods. I gave it a new camo
box and lashed it to a tree with a bungee cord in the
same area it was before. Hopefully the cord won't be
visible in the winter when the foliage dies. I updated
the clues since some landmarks had changed in the 3 years
it has been out there, so reprint them before you go. The
logbook it still damp but I did not want to replace it yet
since it is fun for others to see who has visited in the past.
By the way, they have added more track and the train ride is
even longer than before and now goes through the woods near my
box. More people know about the free train rides offered on the
third Saturday of each month (except winter), so it was
crowded. But it's still a fun way to spend a Saturday afternoon,
especially if you wait until cooler weather.

Silver Eagle
