From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-07 13:54:20 UTC-07:00
Unfortunately, since I don't have the excuse of
needing to get outside and get some exercise, I have
to face the fact that I'm going to have to work on a
big scout project I've neglected this week. I enjoy
it too, but it's a monster project and I really had
hoped that it would have finished itself if I left it
alone for a little while - it didn't! Sooo...since
the rain started, I've set some peas to simmer, had a
glass of wine, started a stew, cleaned the kitchen,
had a glass of wine, started laundry...and yet still,
the project isn't finished...and now I'm typing email
and having a glass of wine!

Lightning scares me quite a lot too - I used to
referee soccer and was responsible for calling off or
postponing games if the weather deteriorated. We
played in extreme heat and cold, rain, sleet, and
snow, but let a bolt of lightning be seen in the sky
and I cleared the field. Of course, I always got a
kick out of the coaches who would say "Hurry! Hurry!
Everybody get off the field and come sit on the
bleachers til it blows over!" To which I said, "Uh,
Coach, do you mean the METAL bleachers???"

Lucy
