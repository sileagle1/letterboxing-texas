Just wanted to tell Sea Dove and family that their Texas Atlas is a
great tool! This weekend I was in a small town that didn't have any
boxes. So much for that. But then I thought I would check out the Atlas
and see if there were any in some the surrounding areas and I hit pay
dirt! Wonderful. Would not have known they were there as I wasn't
familiar with the area. So many small towns in Texas and if they are
outside of your 'stomping ground' you could easy miss some on your way
from one place to another.

Thanks Birds of a Feather!
