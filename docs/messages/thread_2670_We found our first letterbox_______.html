We found our first letterbox!!!!!!!

From: Mandeigh (TiMandi2@aol.com) | Date: 2007-03-25 23:11:59 UTC
Friday afternoon my husband called and said he would be home early from
work. We decided that the kids and I would meet him at the Abilene
State Park and we would hunt for Silver Eagle's letterbox as well as
the other one placed by Caleb.
When we got there the main gate was closed so we did the self pay thing
and went on in to find the park almost completely empty, which was
perfect! We followed our clues and found the microbox. It was soooooo
exciting to find it! We looked all over the log book at all the other
people who had been there and found a spot to put our stamp and little
comments. The log is almost full and was a little moist but we had no
problem writing on it or stamping. The look in the kid's eyes when they
found that box was priceless! My husband and were pretty exhilerated
too! lol lol lol Afterwards we set off to find the other box SHOTB by
Caleb. We had no luck finding it due to the fact that the clues were
gone. We looked as best as we could in the general area but no luck. We
were not disapointed though, we found one and now we can officially say
we have been letterboxing! We can't wait to go again and to also plant
our own boxes!

I am so happy my box was still there for you to find, especially
since you live in a letterbox-poor area. My experience has been that
a person isn't really hooked on letterboxing until they actually find
one, so hopefully this applies to you. You will find that placing
boxes will draw others to you, so give it a try and have fun!

Silver Eagle
