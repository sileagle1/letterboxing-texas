Annoucing two new letterboxes in Victoria.

The "Riverside Duck Pond" and "The Lost Moai" letterboxes were
placed Thursday in scenic Riverside Park. Along with the "Box Kite"
letterbox, there are now three boxes for your enjoyment in Victoria,
just in time for CalicoRose's trip.

Clues are at:
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?

and
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=9127&boxname=The_Lost_Moai

-MonkeyToes.
