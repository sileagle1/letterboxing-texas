LBs on the road last week

From: tx2chic (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-07-18 19:22:58 UTC
Last week my husband and I made a quick road trip from Keller to KY,
and managed a rest stop on I40 in Arkansas to find Wanda&Pete's
LB, "Adding a Splash of Color." Nice little roadside treat! Hurricane
Dennis literally put a damper on most of my letterbox plans, but on the
way home we did manage three little hikes in the Ozarks, finding
TxLuvBug's "B is for Branson" at a very scenic overlook NW of Branson,
and then two nice woodsy walks at Roaring River State Park to
find "Springhouse Trail" and "Devil's Kitchen" planted by Skipper.
Lucky for us, the weather was sort of cool and the rains stopped long
enough for us to make these hikes! Others will have to wait for
another time, due to the weather and enough twisty mountain road
driving for two days. I did manage to carve a new little HH
stamp, "Chick Tracks," and drop it off in one of those Ozarks spots to
begin its travels, though. Maybe it will make it around to some of
those LBs I couldn't get to this time! -- Viewfinder of tx2chic.
