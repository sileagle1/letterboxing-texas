Double Lake box report

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-05-25 22:10:19 UTC
We went out to check the boxes around Double Lake today and did some
maintenance work. LSQ's box, "Shoofly", which had been missing
(consumed in a controlled burn) has been replaced, but moved to a new
spot on the Lone Star Trail near Double Lake. It's a replacement, not
a new box. My box, "Picoides Borealis", which was pulled due to road
work, tree cutting and burning, has been placed in a new location,
also on the LST near Double Lake. In that same area, Will's World's
box "Holy Anole" and Baby Bear's box "The Joker" are alive and well.
In the park, Baby Bear's box "Know Your Swimming Hole" is also good,
as is Princess Aurora's "Rainbow Butterfly", El Lobo's "It Is What It
Is" and my box, "Double Lake". Will's World's box, "A Night at Double
Lake" is confirmed missing. LSQ's box "Flying Geese" was also
missing, so we made a new, identical stamp and replaced the box in the
same location. It should not be considered a new box. The park is
filling up for the holiday weekend and is always busy on weekends.
Monday-Thursday, it is deserted.

Boots Tex
