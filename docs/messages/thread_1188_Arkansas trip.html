Arkansas trip

From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-14 17:52:10 UTC
Hey all!
I just got back from camping and fishing in Arkansas and planted
three boxes there. There are hardly any boxes in that state so I
decided to do my part to spread letterboxing cheer! Unfortunately,
I didn't have time to find any boxes as the only ones we passed were
in Little Rock and we were in a hurry to get up to Bull Shoals State
Park to set up our tent before it was midnight! That is a beautiful
part of the country. If you haven't been up there it is great.
Also it has great trout fishing! We also took a side trip down to
Blanchard Springs Caverns near Mountain View and all I can say
is "WOW"! I had been to Natural Bridge Caverns near San Antonio but
these blew my mind!
We also had another run-in with ticks! I apologize in advance to
anyone hunting my Lunker Trout Box. I didn't realize it was tick
heaven at that park until an hour after I planted the box and saw
moving freckles! I think I am going to buy stock in Deep Woods
Off! LOL.
I hope I can make the barnraising in Oct.! It sounds fun. I can't
commit to it yet until it gets closer to the date. In my line of
work, I often don't know when I'll be working until one or two weeks
beforehand.
-Kantexan

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-08-15 12:54:34 UTC
Baby Bear and I took my father to Bull Shoals several years
ago (before letterboxing) and had a blast. We had never
caught trout before and it was exciting. We stayed at
a resort called Gaston's and went out in a boat with a
guide. He helped us catch many fish, cleaned them for us,
then the restaurant cooked some for our dinner. It was
expensive but worth it!
We also did a one day canoe trip on the Buffalo River out
of Ponca that I highly recommend.
You are right about not many boxes in Arkansas and part
of the reason is that their State Parks have strict rules
about boxes and geocaches. I found out the hard way when
my only box in that state (Devil's Den in Devil's Den SP)
was confiscated. I hope to go back next year and try
again. There are just too many great places in that State.
Glad you had a good time and I look forward to finding your
boxes if I go.

Silver Eagle
