I imagine that for many of us, a Silver Eagle or Baby Bear box was the
first that we found. For us, it was Baby Bear's Houston Texans box,
found almost exactly a year ago (I can't even remember what stamp we
used to stamp in). We didn't really know anything; there was a HH in
the box and we had no idea what it was, so we left it. The next one
was also Baby Bear's, Star Buck, at the beginning of a road trip to
Colorado in July where our enchantment really began.

Now, it seems that where ever we road trip in Texas, there's a SE or
BB box to be found--in some great places we might not otherwise visit.

So a big thanks from us!
