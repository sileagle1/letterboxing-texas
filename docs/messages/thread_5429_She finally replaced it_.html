Back some years ago, you don't know how I tried to get people interested.  I co-located my letterboxes with Geocaches, I got stamps and log books for people, I held letterboxing meetings, I ordered a few postals and we would sit in the Starbucks and stamp up.

 

Well, it never took off.  Finding someone local might be next to impossible.

 

Then again, I know there are boxes out there that have been abandoned, and are still in use.  I guess there isn't anything against that. 

 

I have given away or deleted about half of my geocaches.  And just a few weeks ago I was cleaning out the emergency kit in the dog show van.  I found an unplanted letterbox, and two lock-n-lock boxes with everything but the stamp for maintaining boxes found in bad shape.

 

OK - 3 votes for leave them out, and so far none for pick them up.  Thanks for the input.
