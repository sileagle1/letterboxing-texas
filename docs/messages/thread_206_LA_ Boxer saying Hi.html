From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-07-07 00:14:27 UTC
Glad you found us and looking forward to finding more of your
boxes (I found one while in New Orleans). For those that don't
know, BOXDN placed the first box in Louisiana way back in 2000,
and has been waiting patiently for the rest of us to catch up.
I live about 100 miles from Beaumont, but I have found or placed
most of the boxes in that area. Droop lives in that area and
might give more help, but I will take a stab at it.
First of all, you can use our Texas LB Atlas, developed by Sea
Dove, to graphically display most Texas Letterboxes on a map to
see how close various boxes are. The link is:

http://birdsofafeather.netfirms.com/cgi-bin/lbatlas.cgi?

The boxes I suggest are:

Lake Charles, LA - Mudbug
Vinton, LA - Louisiana Welcome
Beaumont, TX - Spindletop, Secret Garden & Myrtle Tyrtle
Port Arthur, TX - Janis Joplin, Dowling
Sabine Pass, TX - Sea Rim Gator
Orange, TX - Texas Welcome
Vidor, TX - Roo Dreamer, Tall Pine

Hope this helps and good luck!
By the way, if any Texas Letterboxers want to join the LbLa/Miss
Talklist, go to:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbLaMiss/

Silver Eagle
