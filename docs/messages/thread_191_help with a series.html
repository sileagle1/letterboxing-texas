Perhaps the series could be called something like Texas
Trivia, and each box would be the next number in the series
(e.g. Texas Trivia #1, #2, etc). That way each box would
have the name of the series in its name. The only other
way would be to post one clue for Texas Trivia Series containing
X number of boxes and keep adding to the clue as more boxes
are added. You would be the owner of the clue and have to
update it as people send you clues for boxes they hide.
