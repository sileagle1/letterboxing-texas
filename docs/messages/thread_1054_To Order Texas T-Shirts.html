To Order Texas T-Shirts

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-07-15 09:48:13 UTC-05:00
If you would like to order T-shirts, please include the following
information in an email to us at:
celticlions@comcast.net

1. Your trailname
2. Your "real name"!
3. Your shipping address
4. The number & sizes of shirts
5. The color (Blue or Green)

We MetroBoxers are talking about a gathering or mini-gathering this fall,
but nothing is beyond "chatting" stage yet. If we are able to host
something, then of course everyone is welcome to pick up shirts at that
event. However, when you are estimating your cost, it would be better to
assume you may need to pay shipping! Checks payable to me will be due only
when I can confirm to you the exact cost, which will happen when we have 20
or so shirts ordered.

Thanks!
Ruby T.

From: JOY (TeamTexUS@houston.rr.com) | Date: 2005-07-15 14:31:03 UTC-05:00
Yes, I have discovered that most people are so wrapped up in what they're doing, that you could be green with purple spots and  no one would notice.
 
In N.C. this summer, I got a box that was in a very, very busy bakery/cafe that is also an official bookcrossing zone. The first time I went on Sunday morning, but is was so crowded, I didn't even try. I went back on Tuesday morning around 10:30, figuring the rush would be over, which it was, but it was still pretty busy, and all the tables were full. Finally got a table, and started hunting to box. Found it, stamped in, and returned it, and no one paid the slightest attention!!!!! I even had a brief conversation with the girls at the next table in the middle of stamping in and they never looked curious or asked what I was doing.  
 
JOY
