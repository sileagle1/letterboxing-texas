From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-04-01 14:46:30 UTC
A few people at the Event asked me about the Iridium Flare I saw while
at the McDonald Observatory star party and it occurred to me that
others might be interested in how to see it, along with other
satellites. You can go to the website called Heavens Above, configure
your observing site, then choose which object you would like
predictions for. In addition to Iridium Flares, it has many other
objects, including the International Space Station, and other
astronomical information. Now you have something to watch for while
doing your nighttime letterboxing!

http://www.heavens-above.com

Silver Eagle
