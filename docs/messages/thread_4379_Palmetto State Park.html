Palmetto State Park

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-02-15 04:09:51 UTC
With TALE7 right around the corner, I was checking out the park on the website and noticed that, on the trail map, south of Park Road 11 and the Palmetto Trail, there is a large open area with a note "New Trails Pending". When we there last, I believe it was in October, those new trails were open, but the new maps were not available. You might ask at the office when you check in if they have new maps showing the new trails. You shouldn't ask them anything about letterboxing, however, as it could put the existing letterboxes at risk. The site states that there are 3 miles of trails, but that has been significantly increased, perhaps even doubled.

Boots Tex

Re: Palmetto State Park

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-02-15 16:30:49 UTC
I think all the existing boxes are on the Palmetto Trail, which is the main feature of the park. It's a natural wetlands that's kept wet by the ram pump that was built by the CCC in the thirties and is the most ecological sensitive part of the park. The new trails are really nice, wide trails that wind through a mostly hardwood forest area and very pretty, with lots of good places to hide letterboxes.

Boots Tex
