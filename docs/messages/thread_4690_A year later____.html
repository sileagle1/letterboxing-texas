A year later....

From: Lighthouse Luvin Momma (pyle.of.adventurers@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-12-29 22:37:39 UTC-06:00
So a few days ago was our 1 year anniversary of letterboxing and what a year it has been. We have has so many adventures as a family and been brought together in so many more ways. We havent done much the past few months because life has gotten in the way but letterboxing still remains a favorite activity of all of us. My 10 year old had to do a report on her favorite hobby and of course, she chose letterboxing. She took one of the stamps she carved herself and we made a box for it and she wrote her report from info she read off the 2 sites, and a copy off the clues to her Psalms 56:3 letterbox. (Another of her carves) A couple of the kids and her teacher came to her afterwards with many more questions than she could answer so she pointed them to the websites. We are looking forward to the TALE in March, this one is close enough that we should be able to attend on Saturday. I want to thank you all for the help and kind words youve given us over the past year. We are really looking forward to the next year of boxing!
