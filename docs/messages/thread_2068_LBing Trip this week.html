We're taking a little trip this week, too. We'll be in Cherokee
County to check out General Sherman which was reported missing and
maybe replace it, maybe find some of the new boxes there, then on to
Tyler, back to Corsicana, Waxahachie and Bardwell, Hill County and
maybe Freestone, Limestone, Leon and Brazos on the way home. If
anyone would like for us to check a box for you, let us know tonight
(Sunday). We'll check this board for messages in the morning before
we leave. We may have to invest in a travel trailer if y'all keep on
putting boxes out there.
