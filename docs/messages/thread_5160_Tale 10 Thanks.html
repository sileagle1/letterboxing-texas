Tale 10 Thanks

From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2013-03-04 10:50:56 UTC-06:00
Many, many thanks to Silver Eagle and Baby Bear for hosting another fantastically fun event.  This year, I spent less time being frantic that I hadn’t met everybody or found everything, and more time just savoring the moment and the people I was with.  The park is beautiful, and the Rec Hall was quite lovely in its old age.  I loved that the marshmallow-roasting could happen inside the building.  A HUGE thank you to whoever donated the Tiny World Terrarium to the auction.  I have wanted one ever since I first saw them, and I LOVE, LOVE, LOVE owning one now.  You have made me a very happy person.  If I counted correctly, I found 67 letterboxes, not counting event stamps or cooties (although I understand that there were at least 67 cooties, too) – wow!  Thanks to all the carvers and planters.  My one oops is that I forgot to get a CD from Baby Bear.  I’d love to make arrangements to have one mailed to me somehow.  I can’t wait to see you all again at the next event!  -- Bell Motel

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2013-03-05 20:45:55 UTC
All the kudos are well-deserved. Thanks to the TALE Brothers for another memorable event. We appreciate all the advance work that has to be done to make it a success, from the planning, which must start immediately after one TALE ends, to the outlay of money to pay for reserving the hall to buying prizes for the raffle to who knows what that most of us don't even think about. The Tale of TALES presentation alone must take days if not weeks to put together and the CD was a wonderful gift. Thanks for doing that, Baby Bear. I'm sure Silver Eagle is already at work planning the next one. I can't wait to about the theme so I can get an early start on carving this time. We had to leave early because LSQ was coming down with a bad cold and didn't feel like getting out on the trails, so we're planning another trip when the bluebonnets are in bloom to find the rest of the boxes.

Boots Tex
