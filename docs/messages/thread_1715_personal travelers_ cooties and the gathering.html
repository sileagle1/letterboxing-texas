personal travelers, cooties and the gathering

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-02-28 19:27:45 UTC
Wanted to make sure everyone planning on coming to the gathering
understands about stamp exchanges & assorted other kinds of stapps.

When you get there, you will be able to put your logbook out on a
table. People can come through and stamp their stamp in your logbook.
That counts as an exchange.

Event boxes may also be set out on a table. They are available at an
event only. BabyBear is carving the official event stamp but there
may be other event boxes. Stamp the event box's image in your logbook
and put your sig stamp in the event stamp's logbook.

Personal Travelers are a little different. They started because many
people wanted to keep their sig stamps private (that has sort of
changed), but still be able to exchange an image. A Personal Traveler
is carried on a person at all times. Many people ask that you give
them the answer to a mystery, perform an act or say something silly in
order to get their personal traveler. You can find clues for pts
sometimes on LBNA or in back archives of this list. The great thing
is that if you have registered as an attendee of the gathering on
Atlasquest, your personal traveler automatically shows up on the list
of PTs (assuming you've registered it on AQ of course). For instance,
I have a new one. To get it, you have to tell me the name of Alice's
cat. My boys will probably both have new PTs, but I've been too busy
carving RR stamps for the bingo game to do those yet. I will post the
clues here.

Cooties are like HHs, except they are given from person to person.
They are lots of fun for kids (and kid-at-heart) people. The kids (or
adult) will try to surreptiously plant a cootie on your person or in
your backpack. it's a stamp, and you can stamp it in your logbook and
then try to pass it on to the next person. Some cooties have
logbooks, some don't.

Finally: I have the following people playing bingo: Silver Eagle,
Baby Bear, Celtic Lions, Batty B (BusyBees Family), Runs for
Chocolate, Team King, Lucy Locket, Blue Butterfly, Barefoot Lucy,
Viewfinder, my kids, Corazon, Mother of Five. If you want to play,
let me know by tomorrow. I still have a bunch of work to do to set it
all up & get the boxes organized.

Okay, I think that's enough for now! Whew!

Looking forward to seeing you all,

Dewberry
