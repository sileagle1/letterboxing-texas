From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2013-02-28 16:57:46 UTC
The mornings are very cool, 36-40 degrees, a little windy and highs in low 60's. Saturday is forecast to be 57 degrees, so come prepared for cool weather. Also, I wouldn't drive on Park Road 1C out of Bastrop SP. We tried to drive there from Buescher and when we got close to Bastrop, there was a tree down across the road so we had to back up, and as we were backing, another tree fell across the road behind us. I managed to turn around and drove off the road to get around it.
