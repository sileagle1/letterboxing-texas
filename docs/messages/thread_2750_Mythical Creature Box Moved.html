If you are planning to look for the second part of the Mythical Creatures series, please take note of the change in clues.  I had to move the fifth box for a bit.  I was walking on Sunday and decided to just take a quick peek at the boxes, when to my surprise a mama bird flew out at me and scared me half to death.  I checked on the box (which is hidden in the crook of a multi trunked tree) and saw not only the box, but a nest filled with eggs (I'm pretty sure it was a nuthatch) DIRECTLY below the box.  So I decided to move it for a bit, I don't want anything to happen to those little eggs.  Thanks to dewberry who came along while I moved the box just in case I was attacked by mama bird (I wasn't).  Oh, and guess how many eggs she had?  Yep, 5.  Guess I won't be the only "Mother of Five" for much longer!  MO5
