I think there's enough demonstrated interest to go ahead and start the
group, so we've done so. Sandibox has graciously agreed to be a
co-moderator. I'm still working on setting up the site, but I can
verify that the message boards work! There are a handful of us in
there discussing the Candyland Gathering. Feel free to come join us.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/gulfcoastletterboxers/

This list is an experiment. We'll see how much it is used and allow
it to run its natural course, whatever that may be. Letterboxing has
changed a lot in this area, and we have a whole slew of boxers with
varying levels of activity. I hope to reach out to some folks who
currently don't participate on this list, but we'll see if there's
interest. My real hope is that in some way this sublist serves to
strengthen the larger list. For many, it seems more comfortable to
join a smaller group, and then once you get to it, perhaps join in the
state-wide list.

So, we'll see how it goes. . .
