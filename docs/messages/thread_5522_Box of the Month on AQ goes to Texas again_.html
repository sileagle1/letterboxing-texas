From: (candj1969@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-10-26 07:08:50 UTC-07:00

Congratulations, Open Space!  Texas seems to be on a roll as far as Atlas Quest's the Box of the Week is concerned!  We boxed for a few days last week in Kerrville-Schreiner Park in Kerrville and there are a lot of great letterboxes there.  Did you know that there are now three Open Space boxes in that park that have won the Box of the Week award?  I'm sure that's some kind of record!  Sadly, I have to report that the 3rd box in "The Cursed Treasure Series" has been converted into a geocache.  We didn't get the pleasure of stamping in because both the stamp and the logbook have been taken and geocache trinkets have been added.  There are still a lot of coins that were a part of the original letterbox "treasure".  At first, I thought it might be a real geocache and continued looking for the letterbox, but after finding a few more of Open Spaces boxes, I noticed that they all had a small number on the back, as did the box we found, so there's no doubt that it was her box. We looked around trying to find the geocache, but never did.  There's apparently one nearby called "Maverick's Cache" but we couldn't find it.  I left the box there and placed a note inside pointing out the fact that it was supposed to be a letterbox and not a geocache.
