Another exciting adventure

From: tigger32167 (tigger32167@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-06-26 22:40:36 UTC
Well i have already told the group about getting to pet an 8 week
old white tiger, and an alligator. well today i had another
interesting adventure (not once in a life time though) i went to
the tx state railroad park in palestine to find chancelier_de_lbu
boxes. i actually went first to look for barefoot lucy's box in the
same park that had been reported as missing. as i was searching for
that box, i stumbled across box #3 of the chancelier's box series
and proceeded down the trail to pick up 4,5,6. i then went back to
find box 1 and when i finally got to the tree that i was sure it was
and looked in the hole that it was in, i realized that chancelier
had placed a guard on this box...a copperhead...he was all curled up
and quite content. well i had my navigator walking with me, and so
i pulled him closer to me and proceeded to start tossing a couple of
sticks toward the guard to try to get thim to move on...this did not
work. i realized that you can stand over the hole and look down at
it, so i then found a long branch that i figured i would use to
caress him to leave his station...well it worked, however instead of
moving on, he just moved further into the hole. so he stood his
post and i moved on to box #2. i found box #2 and then proceeded to
wolf lake park (which i had a very difficult time finding) and
finally found those two boxes. I then decided to go back to the
railroad park and get that box one way or another. i happened to
have my grabber in the back of my car (the long stick that has a
handle to grab things with) so i got it out, and the snake was gone
and using the grabber I GOT THE BOX..what an interesting
experience...but jim please make sure that you warn people that you
have a guard for this box...LOL
