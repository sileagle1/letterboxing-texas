New boxes around Gonzales

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-03-08 04:41:42 UTC
If anyone happens to be driving along I-10 east of San Antonio, you
might like to know that a few new boxes have been planted in the Luling-
Gonzales area. They are "The Curse of the Courthouse Clock" at the
Guadalupe County Safe Rest Area (eastbound), "Palmetto - Quilt Block
Series" and "Ram I Am" at Palmetto State Park and "Sarah DeWitt's
Flag", "The Hat Makers", and "The Old Eighteen" in Gonzales and "The
Shot Box" near the community of Cost, south of Gonzales.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
