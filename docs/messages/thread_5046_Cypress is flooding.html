Bears can swim!!!! Have not had to yet, but it is close. We are between Little Cypress Creek and Cypress Creek, and both are way out of their banks. All roads outside my neighborhood are flooded, but we are still dry (at least if no more rain comes). Went by Telge Park last night on way home and it is all underwater (worse than any time I have ever seen), so probably a total loss on the 2nd more LB populated park in Texas. Once we dry out I will try to get out there and do an inventory. Thanks for checking on us, hopefully no bad news to report in coming days.
