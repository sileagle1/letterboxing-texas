I just wanted to tell everyone who was thinking about doing the
series to do it while they are still there! The series is doable for
anyone and tons of fun. Most of the boxes are pretty close to each
other, and even the furthest box (in Austin)is North Austin and not
very far from the others. Even if you don't get the chance to do all
the boxes for some reason, each one is a good find. Georgetown has
some great parks - I'm envious.
While doing the series, we also found Winter and Pocket Wocket.
