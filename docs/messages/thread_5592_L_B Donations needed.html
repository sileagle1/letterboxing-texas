L&B Donations needed

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2017-03-08 08:19:33 UTC-06:00

The Live and Breathe planning team needs your help! We are collecting donations of western/cowboy themed items to decorate for the event.  We especially want old boots!  These will NOT BE RETURNED.  We know it’s early, but since many of you are coming to TALE, it’s a convenient time to bring what you have.  If you have other items (even larger stuff) we can use them.  NLW and Wry Me are coordinating decorations so please contact them if you have questions.

 

We also need donations for the raffle.  We are looking for anything letterboxing themed – logbooks, carving material, inks, markers, patches, lock n lock containers, etc. 

 

Thank you!

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2017-03-12 21:31:09 UTC-05:00

We don’t have too much to announce yet, but here are a few things we can share…

 

The date will be finalized in October, and I expect to open up registration late this year.   We will open trackers for box donations in the summer/fall and have several themes we plan to ask for.  If you want to plant a series which are not on the tracker, please contact me, Mosaic Butterfly, or PI Joe so we can plan for it.  So far we have roughly 100 boxes planned, and I expect that number to grow!  We will also need lots of help as we get closer to the event – especially at the event. 

 

If there is something specific you want to do, please let me know.  Otherwise we can chat more at TALE.
