Siterep - Cypress Aug 2nd

From: tigger32167 (tigger32167@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-07 01:47:15 UTC
Started out the day at 6 am from The Woodlands, and along with my
navigator today, JR, we headed to Cypress. Our first stop was AD
Dyess Park where we picked up Snow Dog and The Quill Pen. We then
went to the Coles Crossing Community Center and saved a turtle. To
get to the trail, you walk past a pond which is next to a busy
street. There is a sidewalk between the pond and the street and
there was a turtle between the sidewalk and the busy street. JR and
I started pushing the turtle back to the pond. This was an
interesting endeavor since I had JR's leash in one hand, the clue in
the other and my lb kit over my shoulder. We got the turtle to the
edge of the pond and when it fell in he flipped over and landed on
his back. Now I am trying to keep JR out of the pond and trying to
get the turtle turned over as it is drifting away from me. I
finally stepped in the pond with one foot and managed to turn the
turtle over. I am sure that it was a hilarious sight to watch. We
then looked for Gas Chamber and could not locate it. We then
made it to Telge Park and located Park Angel, Legend of Sleepy
Hollow, Jordan's Birthday LB, and A True Goblin - Terrors of Telge.
After finding these four it started pouring rain, so we headed back
to the car. Within about 10 minutes the rain subsided, so we went to
a Harris County Pocket Park and picked up the Fire in the Hold box.
After another hard rain, we decided to head back to my parents for
the day.
JB Kokopelli
