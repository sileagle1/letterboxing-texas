I'll take one in pink. I look good in pink  Janet.  

Taurus 617 revolver in titanium.  .357 Magnum.  It comes in 4 colors.  You can load it with hot .38s if you can't handle the .357 magnum loads.

 Seriously, I'm thinking of taking a class, then I can get my license, then I can go shopping.  

The class is really more to teach you how to be aware and AVOID problems rather than shoot your way out.  But it is comforting that if you are totally dumb and stupid and do get surprised, you have an equal or better chance of getting out of it.

 I work in a dangerous neighborhood, on the third shift. Scary, very scary.  

A few things.  Walk to your car smartly, quickly.  Make eye contact with people you see.  Get someone to walk with you, then drive them back to the door.  Keep your arms empty and free.  Keep your key ring in the palm of your strong hand, keys sticking out between the fingers.  If attacked, punch the keys in their eyes.  Roll under a car to escape.  One other hint.

PEPPER FOAM.  Mace brand pepper foam.  NOT SPRAY - FOAM.  It shoots out in a heavy stream - doesn't blow back at you in the wind.  It sticks like shaving cream, it expands behind glasses.  It marks the attacker with a UV-purple dye.

In Texas pepper foam is considered simple assault.  If you would be justified in hitting someone, the same justification applies to pepper foam.

 For now, pray for me. Janet 

I would rather see you get training.
