You know, in the grand scheme of things it doesn't matter what we carry or what we know if we aren't watchful.  Someone with enough determination to hurt another person is very likely in the advantageous position because where my goal might be finding a box, their goal might be finding a victim, so while I'm focusing on the clues, they may be focusing on when I'm in my weakest position. 
 
Being aware of your surroundings and the people (or lack thereof) around you doesn't mean you have to be fearful too, but being watchful means you might avoid putting yourself in a position of becoming a victim of either human or animal or even vegetative (darn PI) attackers. 
 
And the beauty of being watchful?  It's this very state that also opens your eyes up to the beauty of the world around you, that lets you see the unusual, that allows the ordinary to become extraordinary.
