Hello from Team Agnew

From: kp4267 (katie@proff.us) | Date: 2008-07-15 04:12:18 UTC
Mother of Five suggested we join this group and we are glad she let us
in on the secret. My husband and I started letterboxing in late June
of this year. We quickly became engrossed in the sport/hobby and have
been out every weekend since we became aware. My sister (in Denton)
stumbled upon the LBNA site a year or so ago, and thought it would be
great once her little ones were just a bit older. She recently
revisited the site, to help her oldest son, a tiger cub, earn a badge
for use of a compass. When she posted her great adventure on a family
blog, we were all intrigued. Team Agnew was formed quickly thereafter
to attempt to find a box or two. After our first day out, we were
hooked. Two Tall Texan boxes were all we needed to jump start our new
obsession. Since, we have found that it is a great way for two busy
professionals (and newly weds to boot) to spend time together without
distractions, get some great exercise, and satisfy that inner child
with countless treasure hunts.

Hopefully in the next few months, we will put our own little treasures
out into the world and you will enjoy them as much as we have enjoyed
your boxes.
