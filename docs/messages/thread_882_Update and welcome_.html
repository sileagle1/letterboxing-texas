From: monkeytoes108 (cruschhaupt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-05 11:23:23 UTC
I am so behind! First off, welcome to all the new people. We're
very glad to have you, but would be gladder if you placed boxes in
the Victoria area. I'll gladly find them and take care of them for
you.

Secondly, a box update. It's not good news. I went to the Rob and
Bessie Welder Park in Sinton and found 3 out of 6 boxes.

Hikers Maze- Found but wet. Excellent hide!
Looping Lassos series:
#1- Not found :( I'm sure I had the right tree, but I didn't see
any box. The vines were pretty thick so I could have over looked it.
#2- Found but wet. Great detail in such a miniscule stamp! It was
kind of exposed, so I put some debris over it.
#3-Found, but only slightly wet. The logbook was dry, but the
little purple pieces of paper were washed out. Whatever was written
on them is long gone.
#4 and #5- Missing. I'm abslutely possitive I had the right trees,
but all I found was a lizard and a beer bottle.

Sorry guys. I wish I had had the time to dry out your boxes, but I
was only passing through.

-MonkeyToes
