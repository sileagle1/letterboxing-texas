State Of TX Letterboxing

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-09-24 00:07:17 UTC
Since I recently celebrated my second anniversary of finding
my first letterbox (8/31/02), I found myself reflecting on
the state of letterboxing now vs. then. Here is a summary
of some of the statistics:

2 years ago now
----------- --------
# of North American Letterboxes: < 2000 > 11,000
# of TX Letterboxes: < 10 > 300
# of Letterboxes in Houston area: 0 > 50
# of TX Letterboxers: < 5 > 100
# of TX Events: 0 1

As you can see we have come a long way in 2 years! Gone
are the days when Baby Bear and I took turns finding each
other's boxes, hoping for somebody else to join the fun.
Well, our patience has been rewarded and I thank all of you.
Here's to another year of prosperity!

Silver Eagle
