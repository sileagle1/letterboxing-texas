Yellow Trail Update

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-09-23 16:40:44 UTC
It's a little less than two weeks until the cyclone lifts us off to OZ.

Preparations are in full-swing. We have about 50 people coming, lots
of new faces to look forward to meeting.

We'll be in the back section of the picnic loop. When you turn off of
Memorial onto Picnic Ln, go left and through the gates (the gates off
Memorial where the sign says Memorial Park Picnic Area are never
actually open). This puts you on a one way loop. Towards the back
you should see some restrooms. We'll be in one of those picnic niches
(look for the green balloons) back there. Can't say exactly which
one. If you miss us, circle back around.

If you're planting a box that day, bring a copy of your clue to put on
the bulletin board for others to copy down.

The tables are pretty ick, so I'd definitely bring a picnic blanket or
a table cloth, & some portable chairs. Hoppers has offered to bring
home made rootbeer, but other than that it's a BYO-picnic event.

There's lots of room for the kids to play, and we'll have Jack
Pumpkinhead's Pumpkin Patch so that they can letterbox to their
hearts' content.

Afterwards, anyone who wishes can congregate at Texadelphia for food &
refreshment.

I've gotten a little carried away with the carving of OZ characters,
so there'll be plenty of boxes. Help me! I need to stop!

Anything else?

dewberry
