From: Amber (Cooper) Lujan (ladida@rocketmail.com) | Date: 2007-05-07 22:04:49 UTC
On Saturday my friend and I went looking for this box at Brackenridge
Park. We found the other one in the area, and went on to look for
this one. We found a tree with black paint on it, next to a tree with
green paint on it. We found the trail. We found the fallen logs. We
found the 5 foot stump. There was no box there.
Then, we ran into the Nelson clan! We saw them at the location where
we found the previous box. We interrupted them and introduced
ourselves and then searched for "Go Spurs Go" together, as we thought
maybe they'd see something we didn't and help us find it. They
followed the clues and came to the same spot we'd been before, no box.
They dug around with a stick and turned over a few logs, but no box.
