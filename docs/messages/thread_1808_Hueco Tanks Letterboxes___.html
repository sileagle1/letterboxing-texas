Sometimes results are slow in coming....  but I believe in the long run it is better to work with the rangers than to use the stealth method.  At least I have had good luck this way.  I figure that our hides will all be discovered sometime.  Having the blessings of the rangers gets help in keeping it hidden and promotes good-will rather than the opposite when they ask "What is this stuff, litter or something?"  And I think this is going to be an example of what working together can produce.  I am certain the head ranger will work with us in any way possible to not only recover the letterboxes, but to allow them to stay if possible.  From the descriptions, it sounds like one Letterbox can stay, and the other one is in a closed area.  As we discussed, I have a place near the entrance to Hueco Tanks Road where I can hide one if it must be moved.
