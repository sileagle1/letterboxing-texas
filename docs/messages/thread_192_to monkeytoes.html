I'll jump in and answer, in case monkeytoes doesn't check in today.

A PLB is a postal letterbox. It travels by mail and recipients sign
up for it by sending their address to the maker. The maker adds
names and addresses to the list of recipients which goes out with the
box. The first recipient stamps in just like a regular LB, then
forwards it on either to a recipient they pick at random, or to the
next on the list, or in whatever manner the maker has requested.

Lucy
