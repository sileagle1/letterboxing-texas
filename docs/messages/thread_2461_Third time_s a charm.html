OK, I have replaced my Spring Branch Secret box for the third time today.  Why not just let it go?  Because this is one of my all time favorite spots in Houston.  If you decide to go find the box, take the time to go through the museum.  I guarantee you won't be sorry.  And for those of you who have already found the box, it is a brand new image, and a brand new location.  I have been going to this museum on rainy or hot days for 10 years (actually 16, if you count the time I went there on field trips with my classes) and I never tire of it.  I hope you like it as much as I do.  MO5
