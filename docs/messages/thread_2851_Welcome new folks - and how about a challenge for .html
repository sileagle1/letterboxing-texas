I'm with WOW MOM on this one: 
Best advice for letterboxers:  
1a.    Recover Well (and reseal those plastic bags.  Even the best boxes sometimes get damp inside and the logbook is the main thing to protect!)  "Older" boxers -- yes, that's me in every respect, thank you very much, Lucy-- usually carry along a few extra plastic bags in their letterboxing gear to replace torn or damaged bags.  Very thoughtful and helpful.
1b.  (see, it's only one piece of advice, but has two parts):  Exercise stealth in public places - don't give away the location or existence of a letterbox to casual observers who might then find and destroy a letterbox out of curiousity because they saw you find or replace it.  Be careful.
Thanks my 2-cents worth.
