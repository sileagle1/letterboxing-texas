hi all,

thanks for your kind thoughts about my first lost box.
i knew you'd understand, and i knew the Others
wouldn't (non-letterboxers, i mean).....i have not
even bothered to mention it elsewhere! : )

as far as help finding it, thanks for that, too, and
here are some responses to your comments.

actually, Treasure had been relocated, kinda-sorta,
and it was not at the scout hut anymore, so no need to
look there. i tried to look there, just in case, but
there were cars all around on that day. i'm going to
ask my mom to check things out again, since she lives
closer, and she can go in the early mornings when
there will probably be fewer people at the park.

as far as what happened, it looked like there has
either been a huge storm, or they are beginning to
clear the area for construction, or something, because
lots of trees were down and in particular there was a
big huge pile of brush down over/around/on the place
where the box should have been. i did try for several
minutes to look around in the wider general area, but
it was pretty piled with stuff and getting dark, so i
did not stay quite as long as i might have.

and thanks for the "it might turn up later" thoughts.
i had that thought, immediately, since i have heard
several of you mention that situation about your own
boxes, here on the list, so my disappointment was
immediately cushioned with hope.

i did like having one at the scout hut and so i do
intend to put it back there (not done yet, though).

thanks for your kind thoughts

ashby
