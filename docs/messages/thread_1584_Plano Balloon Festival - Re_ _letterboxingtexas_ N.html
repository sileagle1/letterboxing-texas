*****Plano Balloon Festival was in disarray when we
found it in November. SJZ/Elmo and I found half the
box and the log, and we searched around the area for
the stamp and the other half of the box. Knowing rain
was emminent and knowing that raindear, the placer,
was from out-of-state, we took the already damp
logbook with us to dry out and return to raindear.
When I contacted him, he said he had gotten a report
just the week before that the box was in a similar
condition, except that all the pieces were located and
put back together and rehidden. It was also
interesting that a GC'er had found and signed in to
the box just the day before and it was apparently
intact. If you searched around, I'm sure you found
the GC in the ammo can in that area - that cache was
at the other end of the same tree the box was in.

Raindear would, I think, like to replace the box, but
would like to find someone locally to adopt it. He
asked if I would, but I am more likely to get to the
North Pole than I am to get to Dallar, so I told him I
couldn't do it properly. Any takers?
