Great Book Adventrure After-Event: Chicken Fried Chatter!

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-09-14 12:30:13 UTC
Thanks to everyone for their suggestions for a pre- or post- event
party for the Great Book Adventure. Since so many of you are coming
from out of town and are pressed for time on Friday, we have opted for
a Saturday night dinner. Goodson's is a Tomball institution
particularly known for its chicken fried steak, chicken fried chicken
and its country vegetables. After a day of hard boxing, you've earned
a treat!

This event should give everyone the opportunity to socialize, tell war
stories and exchange with other boxers that you may have missed at the
gathering itself. We're going to have more than 50 boxes to find
during the gathering (number is rapidly increasing!). There will be
some boxes that will be available Saturday only and some that will be
available just that weekend. You will be able to box as much or as
little as you like during the actual event and socialize as much as
you like as well.

We're still working on the day's structure. If TeamKing and I could
just get over our respective pneumonia and bronchitis, we might be
able to get out there and do a site plan!

Anyway, it would really help us if you would rsvp if you plan to come
to chicken-fried chatter. Goodson's doesn't take reservations, but
they'll save us a space (we're very unclear on the difference) if we
tell them how many are coming.

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=226

Thanks to Mother of Five for helping coordinate the after-party!
