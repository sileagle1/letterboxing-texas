Texas Tour Bus On the Road Again

From: janetsredbarn (janetsredbarn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-06 23:22:26 UTC
As we take the Texas Tour Bus to its next stop, I can hear the
words to that old song, "On the Road Again". Who's singing that,
George Strait? Oh, no, that's Willie Nelson, he's been on the bus
for a while, and here he goes again. George is one of the new
guys. He got off the stagecoach in Salado and hopped aboard the
bus, along with a few other new passengers and a wayward hitchhiker.
We started out this morning on a mission to get the bus on its
way, "Under a Blue Clear Sky". "Lead On", I said, but don't
get "Carried Away", and don't "Run", just "Go On". We've got to
find a good hiding place for "The Big One". "Nobody in His Right
Mind Would've Left Her," I Tell BT, "We Really Shouldn't Be Doing
This". Oh, "Right or Wrong", she'll be all right, was his
reply. "True". Let's just go this "Round About Way" and I bet
we'll find that "Ace in the Hole" we're looking for. How are we
going to remember the directions? "Write This Down" he said. We
found just the spot and got the bus in its place. I looked down at
it and said, "It Ain't Cool to be Crazy About You". As we were
driving away, I said "I'd Like To Have That One Back". BT's only
reply was, "Easy Come, Easy Go".

The bus stopped this time at the Mercer Arboretum and Botanic
Gardens. Take I-45 to FM 1960 and head east. Turn left on Aldine
Westfield and go to the stop light with park entrances on both sides
of the road. Turn right into the parking lot and park by the
visitors center. Get a map or use the map board near the entrance
and find your way to "East Oxbow Loop" shown on the map. You will
pass the bamboo section and the Tea House. Once you find the trail
post for "Littl Cypress TrailBig Cypress TrailPrimitive Trail,
turn right and count 40 steps down Little Cypress Trail. Look for a
10 foot tall tree stump on the right. At the bottom of that tree,
behind a log and covered with bark, you will find the Texas Tour
Bus. Take care of it and when you send her on, "She'll Leave You
With a Smile".

LSQ
