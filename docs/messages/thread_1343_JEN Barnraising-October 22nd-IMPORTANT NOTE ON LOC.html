Okay, I've given up on a park in Waxahachie. Dealing
with the Waxahachie Parks Department has been
frustrating and painfully slow and tedious. They
can't seem to answer any questions right off the top
of their heads and they don't return phone calls,
emails, or carrier pigeons in even a remotely timely
manner, so I give up on them. I realized I was really
stressing out over this gathering solely because of
the location thing, so I knew it was time to find a
new solution. Here it is, and if it causes anyone
heartburn, let me know and we'll figure out how we can
make it work for you (I really hope it doesn't kink up
anyone's plans though!).

I've decided to move the gathering itself to Bardwell
Lake, and specifically to Waxahachie Creek Park. It's
about 20 miles south of Waxahachie, and now not quite
central to the three counties (it only changes the
maximum distance East to West by about 5 or 6 miles,
so it's close enough I think), and Dallas residents
will have to drive further by about 20 miles-sorry
about that! I've rented an extra campsite next to my
own, so that we will have the two campsites to use for
the gathering (they have a group pavilion, but it's
for 200 people - figured the 30 or so of us would
rattle around in it!). I've posted the link for
Bardwell before, but here it is again:

http://www.swf-wc.usace.army.mil/bardwell/main.htm

My scouter friends tell me it's pretty nice, so I feel
pretty sure it is.

Here's what I have so far:

RSVP's:
Here's the link to who has RSVP'd. Check the list to
see if your name is there, otherwise, please go ahead
and give me a tentative RSVP now, even if you aren't
sure!

http://www.geocities.com/barefootlucy/Attendees

PARTICIPATING, BUT NOT ATTENDING:
If you aren't attending, but you want to send a box,
now's the time to go ahead and do it. Email me
offlist to get my snail mail address. Same if you
want to send recipe cards.

RECIPE EXCHANGE:
It's looking like 30 or so folks as it stands now. If
you want to participate in the recipe exchange, plan
to prepare 30 cards with your recipe and your personal
stamp. If you want to send recipe cards and have me
collect them and send them back to you, email me and
I'll send you my snail mail address.

I'm going to send specific contact information and
location information to those who have RSVP'd
off-list. That will probably go out tomorrow evening.

Any questions, shoot!
