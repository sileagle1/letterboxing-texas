Chicken Tortilla Soup

From: (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-03-09 08:45:08 UTC-07:00

For those that wanted, here it is:

In large pot add:

1 rotisserie chicken (pull all meat off in bite size pieces)

1/2 bag frozen corn

8 cups chicken broth

2 cans of Rotel

1 can black beans

1 cup chopped celery (or can do 1/2 celery and 1/2 bell peppers)

3/4 cup cilantro

salt/pepper/garlic powder to taste


bring to boil, then add

1 cup chopped green onions


then cut off fire and let sit for 5 minutes.  Serve

**** can serve with sour cream, guacamole, and chees if want.
