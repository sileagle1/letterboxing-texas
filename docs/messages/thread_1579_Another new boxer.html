Another new boxer

From: janetsredbarn (janetsredbarn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-04 04:15:59 UTC
I guess I should introduce myself as well. I've been reading the
messages with interest for a few weeks. I am Boots Tex and my wife is
Lone Star Quilter. We live in Coldspring. Never heard of
letterboxing until my brother-in-law visited Thanksgiving from
Midland. There aren't many boxes out there, so he took us around this
area to show us what it's like and we were hooked. We've gone on two
hunting trips since then and have found 45 boxes and 3 hitch hikers.
We've seen many new places, even in our immediate area, that we would
never have found without the incentive of finding a new letterbox.
What a great hobby!
