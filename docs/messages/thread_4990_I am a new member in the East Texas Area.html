I am a new member in the East Texas Area

From: Ann Grimes (grimesae@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-03-25 15:36:00 UTC-07:00
 
My trail name is Echo, this is my Girl Scout camp name.
 
I moved to the area about 7 years ago after spending 24 years in Odessa, Texas.
I graduated from A&M in College Station and I teach High School in a small school. I have 2 grown child and one likes to letterbox with me. I am a Girl Scout volunteer and I like to travel.
 
I began letterboxing after a Girl Scout event where letterboxing was an activity. So for the last 3 years I have been hunting and I recently planted 3 boxes publicly and 18 on a GS camp in West Texas. I have plans to create and hide more boxes in this area and where I travel this year.
 
I also am interested in getting to know letterboxers in my area and hopefully get some new ideas and a better understanding of differtent aspects.
 
I hope to meet some of you soon.
 
Echo
