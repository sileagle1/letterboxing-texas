Re: Texas Tour Bus 2 - New Clues - Update

From: Dave (DavHow@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-06-04 09:40:17 UTC
Imagine my surprise when I opened that box (arts & crafts rose), and
there is a note addressed to me! I certainly wasn't expecting that. In
fact, I put that box back without stamping in, then went and grabbed
the bus and put it in Cold Front's car before coming back to get the
original box (and one other). Then when I'm almost back to the car the
second time, a stranger comes out of the woods and asks if I'm Warm
Front... it caught me off guard.
Thanks Dewberry, that was a very cool surprise. The bus doubled my
stamps for the trip since I only had time to get 6 other boxes(only 4
for Cold Front, who is very upset that she missed out on the bus
adventure).

We got back to Alpine about 9pm last night, and I went straight to bed.
It's now just after 4am and I have to go do my Trig homework before my
8am class... ugh! I just wanted to post about the bus. I'll log finds
and visit the boards this afternoon.

As for the bus, I'm going to order some PZcut today (I typically use
pink pearl erasers :) but I guess I can try to do something bigger for
the bus. We're going to try and carve our additions this weekend, and
then re-plant the bus the following weekend. I'm thinking we might take
it to Kent, TX. It's almost exactly between Midland and El Paso, it's
only an hour and a hald from here, and it's located in a county with no
current letterboxes, so maybe we'll fill one in while we're there :)

WF
