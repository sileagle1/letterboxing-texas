Amusing Emails

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-07-29 17:24:43 UTC
Over the years I have received many amusing emails related to my
letterboxes, and I thought it would be fun to share a few:

A woman who inherited chairs supposedly belonging to Jane Long found
my clue page for the Mother Of TX Letterbox and wanted to know if I
was related.

An author writing a book about a friend of Will Rogers found my clue
page for the Cherokee Cowboy LB and asked if he could buy the picture
I used on the web page.

A fan of the Big Bopper found my clue page for the Big Bopper LB and
asked if I had any T-shirts for sale.

And the latest,
A man in Missouri found my clue page for the Pecos Cantaloupe
Letterbox and asked if I could ship him some.

I'll bet y'all have a few, too!
