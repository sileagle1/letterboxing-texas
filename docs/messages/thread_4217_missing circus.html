From: AnnaBee (annabee44@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-08-22 10:42:19 UTC-04:00
Letterboxing in Texas

I have the Circus.

 

It arrived in the Austin area some time ago, but almost immediately there was trouble with INS and the CDC.  Apparently some Texas politicos were grandstanding on the whole Border Security thing, some stimulus funds found their way into a couple state agencies and the next thing you know, they were coming after the Circus.  It seems some of the occupants were here illegally.  Pfft.  T Don Hutto offered free accommodations in Taylor, but thankfully we were able to avert that disaster.  No one here in Austin wanted to get mixed up in that trouble, so the Circus changed hands a couple times and finally was brought to me, since I am a lawyer.  Things are straightened out now – more or less -  and I expect it will be on its way soon.  It wouldn’t be a bad idea to keep any future locations a closely guarded secret.  I am unconvinced that the government is through nettling our circus friends. 

 

Word got out that the Circus was in Austin, and don’t you know, that OTHER circus showed up… what a mess…. Especially for the elephants…. They are very sensitive you know…..For now, I can assure everyone that the Circus and all its occupants are safe and sound and somewhere in South Central Texas.  Look for them again soon.

 

AnnaBee
