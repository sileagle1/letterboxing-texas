Update on Clear Lake area letterboxes

From: Birds of a Feather Family (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-28 21:27:47 UTC-08:00
I was able to go letterboxing on Friday and when to the Clear Lake area.
 
Found the following boxes in good condition:
  • Gramatrick's Labyrinth
  • Krohm's Aduceus-Cay
Found the following boxes in need of maintenance:
  • Hawk's STS 51-L - box had water in it. Double bagged log and replaced bag for stamp.
  • Baby Bear's Neptune - box was scattered on the ground - looked like bite marks on the bags. Log book was severely damaged but the stamp was ok. I salvaged the pages which had stamp images or writing.  I added a spiral for a log and rebagged everything after a run to a near by dollar store.
  • Silver Eagle's Space City Shuttle - stamp is cracking in half.  Reported it to Silver Eagle. 
I also place a new microbox called Silver Snoopy.
 
I have also been adding recent boxes to the Texas Letterboxing Atlas. 
 
Thanks to the following LBers for granting permission for listing their boxes: Lucy Locket, Frye Team, Desert Warrior, strawberry6703 and Big Red (Red Berry), runsforchocolate, Buckeyes in Texas, Astro D, TeamTexUS, vader, Lightnin Bug, stargazer, Thot, p ?uest, Delivery Girl, mater et regisfilia, and GS1760.
