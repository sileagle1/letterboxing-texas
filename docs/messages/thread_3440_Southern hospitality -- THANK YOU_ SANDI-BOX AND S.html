Southern hospitality -- THANK YOU, SANDI-BOX AND SOCCER-CHICK!!!!!

From: Lisa Lazar (lazar.bauer@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-03-23 17:34:40 UTC
I spent the most wonderful day with Sandi Box and Soccer Chick
yesterday. I had been at a theater conference in Houston, and had not
stray more than five blocks from the convention center. I hadn't
gotten a real taste of Houston (although the conference was a blast).

These two fine ladies gave me a wonderful Houston experience, with a
FAB-U-LOUS meal at the Breakfast Klub (you just don't see grits like
that in California), and a wonderful day at Memorial Park.

You guys may not know this, but I lived in Hartford Connecticut, right
before letterboxing took off in that Fabled Mecca. I always laugh at
the jokes about how you have to be careful while driving in Hartford.
if you leave your windows rolled down, letterboxes will throw
themselves into your car!

Well, Connecticut has nothing on Texas!

We were just finishing stamping in, when I said, "Hey, what's that?"
Without even trying, I found a letterbox that was peeping out of its
hiding spot.

Don't worry -- we snuggled it back into place, and tucked it in very
carefully.

How many places can one find a letterbox by ACCIDENT!!??!!

Combine that with a great sighting of a "life" bird, and the glorious
display of blue bonnets, and it was a perfect day.

Thanks, y'all!

Lisascenic
