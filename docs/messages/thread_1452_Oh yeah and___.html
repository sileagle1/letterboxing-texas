Actually, you can cut a stamp in half if you want to fit one in a container that would otherwise be too small. This is especially fun with urban boxes. I finally did one recently. You carve the stamp the way you ordinarily would, back it with a good tape and then cut it down to the tape. Sometimes with the urban boxes, you really get tired of doing little stamps and want to do something a little bigger.
