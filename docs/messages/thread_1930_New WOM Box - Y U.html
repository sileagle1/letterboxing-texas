New WOM Box - Y U

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-16 21:12:52 UTC
I have listed a new WOM box called Y U on AtlasQuest for logging
purposed. This is a letterbox with a twist:

I will distribute the clues to one local boxer (already chosen), who
after finding the box, will then be able to give them to ONE and ONLY
ONE letterboxer (or a group that lbs together). The new recipient
will be able to pass the clues along to ONE new person and so on.

When the clues are passed, you must tell the person Y U and you should
email me to tell me who you've given the clues too. I'll keep track
of who has gotten the clues and where they are at any given time.

If the clues come back to you a second time, you can pass them on to
someone else without refinding the box. If you don't know who to send
them to, you can pass them back to me and I will pass them on to someone.

You are also free to rewrite the clues if you so desire. Right now
they are straight-forward, but you may change one word or all of them.
The box should stay put.

Kind of like telephone, but with letterbox clues.
