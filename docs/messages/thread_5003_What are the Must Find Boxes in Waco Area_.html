From: (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-04-20 15:51:28 UTC

Several years ago, I thoroughly boxed Waco. They were all great boxes and I really enjoyed getting to know the city itself while I boxed (I enjoyed the first weekend there so much, I went back the next weekend). Last summer I went back to go boxing and took my grandson, Trail Whale. As I started planning the trip, I realized that virtually every letterbox I had gotten way back when was no longer there and there was a whole new crop of them! I'll post my sitrep that I sent to Boots, who had advised me on that trip before I went, but I just want to say - I am so eager to get back that way! For one thing, I want to take D'vir to look at Baylor (yes, we're to the point of college-shopping with him), but for another - I just can't get enough of Waco.

But off the subject altogether, since when does a Jeep need shoes? I would've thought a Jeep would be tough enough that it would laugh at the idea of shoes!
