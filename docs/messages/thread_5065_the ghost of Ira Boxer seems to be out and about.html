Hi all-
The Great Wuga Wuga and I were headed up to Denver this past weekend and we stopped at a brand new Gryzzled Gryphon/ Frost Maiden letterbox in Raton, NM. I was sure we'd be first finders so was dismayed to see someone had beat us to it! And who was it but the ghost of Ira Boxer! There was the tell tale sign of his - the boxer shorts with hearts - along with a veiled threat to GG - he said he had just missed him, but he was hot on his trail! I didn't see him sign in to any other CO boxes so I think he might have gone back to TX. I'd keep an eye out and tell Frost Maiden to watch your back, GG....
-Astro D
