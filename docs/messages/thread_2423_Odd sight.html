Odd sight

From: beneficus_rex (kevan.beijan@verizon.net) | Date: 2006-12-29 00:49:32 UTC
I had quite an encounter today. I was at the Denton Dog Park with my
dog "Belle" when an odd thing happened: I saw a bus pull into the
parking lot. I thought it must be a whole club of dog lovers coming to
enjoy a cool public space that has been set aside for puppy-kind.
However, the bus didn't stop in the parking lot like I'd expected. It
proceeded south to the actual park and turned right along the north
fenceline. It bounced over two large rocks and continued to the
northwest corner of the park. From there it turned to 200* and came to
a stop within a bramble on the west side of a tall tree a short
distance away. You'd never guess who came to play -- Hank the Cow Dog
himself (along with a variety of fantastic Texas true-bloods)! When I
had to leave, they were still there in the leaves and sticks. They may
be there still. If you hurry, maybe you could meet up with Hank and
the gang before they get away!
