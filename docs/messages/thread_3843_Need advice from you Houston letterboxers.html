From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-11-08 15:43:46 UTC-06:00
I would split them up into two piles, one for the boxes that start at the mountain bike trails, and another for the boxes at the arboretum.  I think that would be the easiest way.  You won't be able to find them all in two days (heck, I live here and there are still a few I haven't found) but you should be able to hit 100 boxes.  While hunting note that this area lost a lot of trees during Ike, so landmarks could be different from the clues.  Hope this helps, MO5
