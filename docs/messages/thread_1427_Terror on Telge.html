From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2005-10-30 13:26:26 UTC-06:00
Thanks everyone for coming out to enjoy Telge Park! If I counted right, we are up to 14 boxes in this park now. Many of these will be pulled by the end of November, so for those of you who haven't been there yet, make sure to take note of that date.

Great to meet the new people. We're glad you are joining in this fun 'obsession'. :) This gathering was just a mini meet and greet sort of thing for locals. The larger Texas gathering in the Spring should have some more events and activities in case you were wondering.

Thanks to the 'old-timers' (I think that's probably anyone who been letterboxing for a year or more in Texas?) for coming out also. Always nice to meet up and chat - and a special thanks to all of you who participated in planting a box in the park for everyone to enjoy.

Happy Halloween!

TeamKing
