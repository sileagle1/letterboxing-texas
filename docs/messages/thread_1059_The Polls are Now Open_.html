The Polls are Now Open!

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-07-15 22:48:33 UTC
Lots of great ideas for our Box Exchange. I have created a poll so
that you can select your favorite idea for the Texas Box Exchange.
These are ideas that were discussed here or sent to me via email. The
polls are open until July 29.

All members of the list are eligible to vote. You do not have to be
planning on carving or planting a box to vote. You will sign up for
that separately.

I hope everyone gets in the spirit and has fun with this. I will
organize the exchange, conduct the lottery, etc.

If I've left off a great idea, email me and I'll add it really quickly
so we don't lose any votes.

Thanks for your enthusiasm!

Dewberry
