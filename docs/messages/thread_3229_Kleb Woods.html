From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-12-23 14:35:04 UTC
Yesterday, Baby Bear and I finally made it to Kleb Woods Nature
Preserve to find all the Christmas-themed boxes and place some of our
own. It is a great little park and the weather was beautiful, plus we
had the place all to ourselves. Or so we thought. Turns out Blue
Butterfly and Lucy Lockett were placing boxes there yesterday and we
saw Sandibox had stamped into a few before us, too. Too bad we never
saw anyone or we could have swapped clues. Oh, well, it gives me an
excuse to go back again. If it wasn't so far away I would go more
often, boxes or not. What a great idea to make it the "Christmas
Letterbox Park". Everyone have a great Christmas and stay safe!

Silver Eagle

We also had a good time yesterday at Kleb. The weather started off
cloudy, but it turned into a beautiful day. I took a couple of brand-

Sorry for the delay since we had posted the "place holder" on AQ to
let people know it was coming. Then we vacillated a bit since we
didn't want to duplicate the effort that SandiBox put in. However,
perhaps God was making plans for Baby Jesus to turn up one way or
another!

We deleted the "place holder" on AQ since it was out of date and
listed a new plant since it was placed today.

It is not near any boxes we KNOW of in Kleb Woods park, but when we
saw this spot, we thought, "there must be a box here already". We
looked quickly but didn't spot another box. Disclaimer: We were in a
hurry -- life's full of stuff and it was getting dark.

So, if it turns out we've encroached on someone else's plant, just let
us know and we'll move it. We were just happy to be able to get it
planted in time for Christmas Day.

Merry Christmas!!

Key Note Sneakers
P.S.: SandiBox, we are saddened by your loss and hope that your Baby
Jesus turns up soon. We're glad we could help on short notice!
