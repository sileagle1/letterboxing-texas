Hi! We're new to letterboxing and this group

From: jeb78746 (joybaldwin@austin.rr.com) | Date: 2006-06-07 21:41:44 UTC
I recently read about letterboxing in the magazine Family Fun. My
kids, both 5, love treasure hunts, so I thought it would be the
perfect activity for us this summer. We're in Austin, and we've mainly
been working on letterboxes in the area, although we did manage a few
when we were in East TX recently. So far, the kids' favorite location
was Oakwood Cemetery. The hiding spot was very Scooby Doo, and we had
a thoughtful morning reading grave markers.

This week my kids are in Houston with their Aunt. I told her about
letterboxing, and her response was "I'm all for anything that involves
rubber stamps." This is a woman who has an entire room in her house
filled with rubber stamps. She has thousands of them!!! Anyway, it'll
be interesting to me to see what sort of found stamps and stories they
come home with.

We're looking forward to planting a few boxes ourselves later this
summer. We have a lot of ideas, but we haven't exactly mastered
carving stamps yet.

Thanks.
joy

Welcome to the list Joy!!

 

This is a VERY friendly list, so feel free to ask questions!!  We discovered letterboxing about 3 years ago and it has become a major fixture in our lives!!  We especially like to take it traveling with us and we just collect stamps from the locations instead of souveniers!  My log book has evolved into a log book/ scrap book hybrid and I usually journal while letterboxing.  I think it will be a great treat for my kids/ grandkids when I am gone.  Anyway, hope to see you on the trail some time!
