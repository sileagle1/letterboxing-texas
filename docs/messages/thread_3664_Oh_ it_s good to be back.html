Oh, it's good to be back

From: janet clark (jayceeinbedford@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-21 17:29:54 UTC-07:00

Can y'all remind me why we wear tennis shoes not flip flops, always take water and carry a small umbrella when boxing. Cuz I done forgot them all.

I went to get two very simple, close to the home, practically drive by boxes. How hard can it be. I don't need water, I don't need my backpack. I'll just grab my book, stamp, markers and go. Yeah, that's a bad plan.

Within a few minutes on the cement trail I was dehydrated. When I got to the spot for the box, it was 500 degrees outside (without the heat index). And, the fire ants. OMG, they are evil.

I'm trying to sit on this rock and look like I'm writing my memoirs and these little pests (G rating) are trying to eat me alive. I must've looked like a lunatic shaking my legs and slapping at my feet. All while trying to not let the baggies blow away and hide the box from the people running by me.

Yeah, I'm dying just sitting there and these people are running, in 500 degree weather. One lady had a long sleeve shirt and pants on. One family (whole bunch of crazy people) were wearing black shirts. Sheesh, I can't even imagine.

So, I have been able to log a find, find a gorgeous park not 3 miles from my house, tell LSS (who sat in the hot van) about a new fishing spot and find a cool play spot for Cassie girl.

Letterboxing
Not just a hobby
It's an adventure.

Janet
Blooming Flowers

Okay, I have a public service announcement.
 
This comes from my good friend, She Who Volunteers Me for Everything.  She currently lives in a very trying situation as her unnice, yet infirm, father-in-law has come to live with them.  She often vents to me by email and she has coined a nice little phrase that saves all the trouble of having to think of nice clean words (or new not-so-clean words) to type AND it leaves me open to use my own creativity.  Here it is:  EOC.  Simple as that.  EOC.  Expletive of Choice.
 
So you see, Janet, rather than working so hard at staying G-rated, you could have said these "EOC little EOC's."  And all of us could have applied our creativity to come up with something that seems appropriately disturbing - like venom-fanged little monsters, or - whatever...PLUS - banging out those three keys over and over again can relieve a LOT of stress!
