Since no one's replied, I'll give it a shot. You probably shouldn't go
out letterboxing alone. But if you can find someone to go with you
(sorry I'm not living in San Antonio or I'd volunteer), without a car
your best bet would be to print out some clues that look interesting.
Then mapquest them to see where they're at and once in San Antonio, get
some VIA city bus route maps and figure out which ones you can get to
by bus. I think there's a bus that goes up Broadway from downtown and
you could walk to Barney's Toilet Seat Museum for the box there.
Meeting Barney would be a really treat; he's such a neat, sweet old
guy. Most of the boxes that I've read about are in various city
parks. I don't remember seeing any that are near the Alamo. That area
is always too active with people that I wouldn't place a box there!

Hope this helps.
