Hey boxers,

I admit, I haven't been too active now that it's wet, messy and cold. Haven't boxed
since October. I did want to let you know that a friend of mine has opened a rubber
stamp shop in Salado, Texas of all places. His store in Houston closed and he decided
to move it there of all places! You would enjoy the visit. I just came back myself. It's
about 30 minutes North of Austin right off Main Street next to Horsefeathers. More
driving info is on www.stampsalado.com.

Mark says he'll be getting stamp carving supplies in any day now. You will also enjoy
looking at all of his personal stamp art. He is amazing! He has carved several found in
the store and is great at teaching, giving tips, etc. One day, maybe I'll get him in the
LB world after he gets his store off the ground.

Tell him we said hello!
