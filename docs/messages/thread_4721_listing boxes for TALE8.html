From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2011-02-26 02:49:50 UTC
I like to list ours ahead of time, even though they aren't planted yet. That way, I can print them out with the story and write the clues on the clue sheet after hiding the box. We will bring a copy machine so that everyone can make copies of their hand-written clues. Others can then pick up a copy of the clues to find the boxes at the event instead of making another trip.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-02-25 20:51:10 UTC-06:00
Listing after the event isn't usually the mode of handling (although you can do it if you wish). Some folks list them ahead of time (see Gryzzled Gryphon's boxes and BootsTex' and LSQ's boxes). Other folks wait 'til they get there and write the clues out and then folks hand-copy them. As a side note, last year I found that I could take pictures of the clues with my phone so that I didn't have to copy them. That worked out pretty well.

There will certainly be a central location for clues and everyone sort of knows to check that table for any new clues.

That help?

Lucy
