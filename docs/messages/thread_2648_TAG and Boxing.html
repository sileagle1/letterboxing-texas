We had a great time at the event also. I was shocked to see boxers
from Missouri,LA,& Kansas.Of course it is always a treat to get to box
with Texas LB'ers! It was fun chatting with the Atoy Adventurers and
meeting Lucy on the trail. It was a Looonngg walk, but the company on
the trail made it go by faster.
If you venture north to Little Rock, Lucy Locket and I found a
neat little series that is worth the stop also, A Lesson in Piccosso
by Nancy Drew. It is on the campus of UA Little Rock.Clever way to do
a stamp.
Hopefully next year, we will get to meet more boxers for
Arkansas.
