From: Hawk (omkarmashanti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-20 20:31:00 UTC-08:00
I hope y'all got home OK. That storm looked pretty
nasty from Houston.

Just wanted to let everyone know that this is one fine
group of folks! Thanks for all the work everyone put
in to make it happen: Silver Eagle, Baby Bear, the
Celtic Lions, Boxdn, and everyone else that made it
wonderful by carving stamps, placing boxes, driving
long (and short) distances and making the time to
attend, finding boxes, making food, telling great
stories, sharing travelers and personal stamps,
reserving areas, being creative, designing CLUE
booklets, finding prizes, etc., etc., etc. It takes a
LOT of work by a lot of people to make something like
this happen, and it was just FANTASTIC FUN!!!!

I am so glad I got to meet people that I have chased
on the trail and heard from through email. It's always
nice to put a name with a face. I am still looking
through my books of new stamps and marveling at the
quality, quantity, and people behind the stamps. All
y'all are good people!

Hawk
(jumping down from my perch and crawling back into bed
with a nasty cold)
