Well - we have had no graffiti in the area.  We have them barking in the background of our answering machine.  We have NEVER worried about a home burglary.  And we walk the streets in safety.  My handgun will probably never be needed either.
 
Everybody runs when they see a Great Dane.  Some run away from it, and some run TO it.  But everybody runs.
 
We get tired of hearing "Where is its saddle".  But the best one was this.
 
A guy is driving by.  He slows and rolls down the window.
 
Guy in the car: "WOW!  Is that a horse?".
 
Me:  "No.  It's a COW."
 
Guy in the car: "A COW?  Really?".
 
Me: "Yeah - a cow.  Come here and milk him."
 
He lost his smile, rolled up the window, and drove off in a huff.  The neighbor was grinning.
