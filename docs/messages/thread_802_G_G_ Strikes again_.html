G.G. Strikes again!

From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-16 05:13:38 UTC

Hey all,
I checked on my Granbury Legends #2-John Wilkes Booth? tonight and
guess who had already stamped in? The mysterious wanderer-Gryzzled
Gryphon! I planted it Monday and he got to it Wed.!
My son, The Pikachu Ponderer, joked that maybe he follows boxers
around stealthily, just waiting for one to plant, and then,
instantaneously-poof! There he is!
BTW, The reason I was in Granbury tonight was because I took all the
little Samurai Stampers to the Brazos Drive-In. If you haven't been,
and would like to have a mind-blowingly retro experience, go down
there and catch one of their Friday or Saturday double features! What
a blast! We loaded up the pickup with lawnchairs in the back, got our
popcorn and skittles and just enjoyed the whole ambience. I believe
there are only 9 Drive-Ins left in TX now. It felt like 1971 all over
again!
Also, thanks for all the help with the cammo tape search!
-Kantexan
