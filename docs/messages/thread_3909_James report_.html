James report.

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2009-01-17 10:21:41 UTC-07:00

James just called. From Bangor, Maine. He will be at Ft. Hood within the next
6 hours. Baring an aircraft accident, he is as safe as can be. I told him - no
detours to the Hudson River.

Moni and I thank you-all for your thoughts and prayers over the last 18 months
or so. It helped.

After nearly 8 years of service, nearly 4 of that in combat, James will be
coming back to live in El Paso for good. He has been offered a job as an
electrical Apprentice. He will be the foreman of a crew on days, and go to
school 3 nights a week. They told him they need his leadership - they will
teach him the electrical part. It is a great opportunity. Proof yet again that
the military can lead to other things.

Many of you have E-mailed me directly. Thank you for your thoughts. James
again reaffirmed he will be attending the annual cactus meeting in Tucson. We
will see many of you there.

And best of all - I gain a hiking partner who can hike me into the ground. I am
looking forward to the challenge! I may get below 190 pounds yet!!!

================================================================

If any of you are as proud of our soldiers as I am, and wish you could do more
to show it.... Consider adopting a soldier. A letter now and then. A few books
and magazines. Candy. The USPS has a flat-rate box. Under $9 with no weight
limit. Do you know what you can send to Iraq or Afghanistan for $9?

It means so much to them to know someone back home appreciates what they are
doing. And it is so little for us compared to what they do.

=================================================================

Y-all enjoy your weekend......
