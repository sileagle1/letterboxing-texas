Out on a limb

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-11-04 23:22:54 UTC-06:00
Lightning and I visited my mother in Nacogdoches today and did a
little letterboxing, then came home and went with Fearless Fox to a
folk festival not too far from home. What a nice evening!

One of the groups was promoting a three day songwriters' workshop in
January that they are hosting, and they happened to be my personal
favorite group on the lineup. Fearless Fox, knowing me too well,
pushed and prodded until I signed up for it. I can hardly believe I'm
going to do this - I wrote several songs in my teen years, and (as
y'all know) have enjoyed writing casually for quite a long time, but
I've resisted taking it more seriously because I already have at least
two major passions and I know that if I turn myself loose to it,
writing will become such a passion too.

I can barely breathe when I think about jumping in to this - signing
my name was QUITE a commitment!
--
Barefoot Lucy
