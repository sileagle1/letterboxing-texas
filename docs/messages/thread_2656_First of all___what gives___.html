From: wagtime2007 (WagTime2007@tx.rr.com) | Date: 2007-03-21 02:50:06 UTC
I'll go first!
I'm JBKokopelli's cousin. Last fall she started telling the family
about this new hobby she'd learned about and we all looked at her like
she'd lost her mind. You're gonna go WHERE, looking for WHAT? HUH?
It made no sense. Little by little we started to understand and I went
with her on a couple of hunts. Then I figured "If I'm going to tag
along, I might as well be keeping my own log, so now here I am. In
fact, we just got back from a road trip where I planted my first 4
boxes and she did something like 30. We also found a lot along the way.

As an FYI, a couple of months ago I was in the tire store waiting on my
car and flipped through a "Family Fun" or "Family First" magazine.
There was a 6-page article about LB. By that time I understood the
concept, but it was great publicity!

Happy hunting,
WagTime
