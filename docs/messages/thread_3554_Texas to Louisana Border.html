From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-05-15 15:41:58 UTC-05:00
Good luck with the flood and your boxes, Frogkisses. Last spring, a series of four of my boxes were completed underwater for a long period of time due to flooding at Lake Grapevine. I had used Lock n Lock boxes and good freezer weight baggies and had a substantial rock on top of each of the boxes to keep them from washing away. Due to good, conscientious letterboxers, the boxes had all been resealed and the rocks placed back on top before the flood hit. All four of my boxes survived in great condition for the two months it took before the waters subsided enough to go check on them. I was totally surprised. They were covered in dried mud, but they were fine inside! Hopefully your boxes will survive too!
Puddle Splasher
