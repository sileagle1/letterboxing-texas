Hey "Metroboxers" :)
I placed 2 new boxes in Fort Worth today-Maneki Neko and The
Slumbering Family Tree. Both are easy and family friendly but in
popular Fort Worth destinations so bring a "novel, camera, or sketch
pad" to look "busy" so as not to reveal the boxes. You might also
consider going during a weekday or during rainy weather when others
are not as likely to be around. Today was perfect! I also planted
a new hitchhiker today-Baby Neko. Have fun!
