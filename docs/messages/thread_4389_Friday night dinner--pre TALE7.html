From: Bonnie's Email Inbox (bonnielind1@hotmail.com) | Date: 2010-02-23 10:24:43 UTC-06:00

Barefoot Lucy and I have made a few calls and here is our report:

 

Bottom line up top: I think our best bets are Gonzales Food Market in Gonzales or Pizza Hut in Luling. What is your preference?

 

The rest of the report:

- We couldn’t find a church, VFW, or American legion that had anything going on.

- City Market: closed at 6pm, cash only

- Blake’s: table service wait staff, nicer place, extensive menu--burgers are around $6.  They also have salads, steaks (for example lb prime rib $13.49), fried shrimp $9, catfish $9, chicken breast $8, frog legs $10, children’s menu $4, etc. They are open until 9, probably crowded on a Friday night, one big room with SMOKE (their smoking section is one side of the room, but the smoke doesn’t stay there.)  This is a deal breaker for me.  I was there about 5 o’clock on Monday, there weren’t many folks there and it was already smoky. http://www.blakesluling.com/

- Gonzales Food Market: counter service--thus faster and less confusing for people to show up at will, not as nice as Blake’s—but very hometown. The lady I spoke with said we can order until 7:00 and then stay a while.  They have a party room we can reserve if we have 15 or more people (which I think we will).  Capacity is 50.  She rattled the menu off to me over the phone and the prices range from $6-$8 for a selection of meat and 2 sides, with a $4 kids menu. http://www.gonzalesfoodmarket.com/

- Pizza Hut, Luling: Seating for 75+, open until midnight, how can you go wrong with pizza?

 

Please let me have your thoughts on this and I’ll set up a tracker for folks to sign up.

Mosaic Butterfly
