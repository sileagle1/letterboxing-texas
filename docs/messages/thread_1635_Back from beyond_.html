From: CelticLions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-26 12:07:19 UTC-06:00
Good morning, guys!

Rather than write a 5-page epic of all the reasons I've been away from
letterboxing for the last couple of months, I will attempt a very short
list:

1. Youngest son is a HS senior( those of you have been there may relate!;
for the last month we've been organizing & preparing for Solar Car Team
retreat in Big Bend and Senior Class ski trip in Colorado; one apparently
involves javelinas and the other requires gators ;-))
2. 1 wedding (my very dear younger sister & her high school sweetheart after
many decades, other marriages and a couple of oceans intervening)
3. 1 engagement (yippee! Those of you at Brazos last year may remember our
oldest son; an engagement letterbox near the proposal site at White Rock
Lake will follow! Bride-to-be's parents do not live in Dallas, so I'm
thrilled to be included in planning!)
4. 2 more surgeries & more yucky stuff: some of you know I've been battling
cancer for a few years, and I go back & forth between wanting to keep that
info private, and wanting to help broadcast the fact that people now can
LIVE strong with the disease
....but it occasionally really interferes with my letterboxing ;-)!!!

So there you have it! No excuses - just reasons! I'm looking forward to Rusk
& will be answering as many (long overdue!) emails from you all as soon as
possible!

Cheers!
Ruby Tuesday

PS - I promise to write to those of you working on the Scarlet Pimpernel
asap - have to figure out how to explain something without using my
hands!!!(Almost impossible for me!)
