Okay, most of y'all know how I feel about heat! I'm
not a huge fan of it at all. Yet it is so
unbelievably hot today - I think it's hotter than it

deep-freezing, I think my refrigerator is going out

family, we are going to be in campsite 41 as I recall.
Campsites 33 to 42 all seem to be pretty close to the
showers and restrooms if that's what's important to
you, and all are within view of the lake.
