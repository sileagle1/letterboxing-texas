All,
 
Quick update on the atlas.  It now is showing 177 letterboxes from Silver Eagle, Baby Bear, Busy Bee, DrooP, mother of four, Barefoot Lucy, Cristy, Brett and Penny, Calico Rose, and Birds of a Feather family.
 
I have also enhanced the Search function so that it will check the search words against the letterbox name and the placer.  This will allow you to retrieve a list of letterboxes placed by a specific placer.  Also the answer set returned now has the format of placer: letterbox.
 
Right now the placer is based upon the id that was used to register the clues on the letterboxing.org site.
 
Question for Wanda:  Do you want your boxes to be listed under Wanda or Busy Bee?
 
Thank you,
