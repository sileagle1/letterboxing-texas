From: Shane Odom (pathmaker@wildmail.com) | Date: 2005-12-23 05:14:27 UTC
Hello all,
Papa Woodwoses here of MD, (Native Texan) I just wanted to pass on here
that our, The Woodwoses, Flower Mound series are missing. We come back
to TX for the holidays every year and planted this series in public
parks that my family was involved in creating. I have recieved a number
of delightful emails from folks about our series and read comments here
about them. We checked all three yesterday and found 1 of 3. The Be
Happy/Tiger Trail box was located and pulled, but the Flower Mound Mini
and the Gerault Park boxes are definetly missing. I just have to say,
that I've been boxing for awhile, and have lost a few boxes that I have
planted, but losing these is really a bummer. I was really excited to
read their logs and revamp them. So that is the boxing life, but I
can't help but let it get me down. So we will be recreating the two
missing ones, (and hopefully finding better hiding spots) and
replanting all three. Additionally we hope to leave ya'll some extra
boxes in the area to find when we head north..(and one HH we brought
with us...ssshhhh....) so thanks to all for the Texas boxing (I keep
hoping more would show up in the Lewisville/Flower Mound/Grapevine
area...)and glad to contribute, watch for coming boxes, Happy Holidays,
Much Peace and Happy Trails,
The Woodwoses
