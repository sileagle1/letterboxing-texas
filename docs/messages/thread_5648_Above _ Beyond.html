Above & Beyond

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2019-04-12 14:45:47 UTC
I recently received an email from a letterboxer in Florida about her adventures trying to get my Hangman Game 76 box. Here is her story:

"We got to the area and couldn't find
multiple trees with the rock in the middle.  At first
we thought the rock was gone, until we looked in the middle
of a large group of trees that had fallen over (roots
included).  When the group of trees fell, the letterbox
and the rock were wedged in the middle of the trees, all of
which were at least 3 inches in diameter.  So the rock
refused to budge.  We tried to budge it for a while
(even bent the tip of my EMS hiking pole doing it). 
Then we tried to chisel out the box.  Unfortunately,
there wasn't enough dirt near the box to chisel it
out.  Also, when I was trying to budge the box, I
accidentally poked a hole in the top of the box.  At
this point, we decided that the box was never getting
out.  So we decided to try to break the box. 
After spending about 30 minutes trying to free the box, and
then breaking the box, we were able to get two fingers into
the box to carefully extract the logbook and stamp".


She went back a few days later and placed the contents in a new box nearby and sent new clues. Now that is going above & beyond!  You can see a picture of the old box she sent me here:


https://drive.google.com/file/d/1GJdIFM0ujHluO7G-GF95hogR14W8ebrA/view?usp=sharing
