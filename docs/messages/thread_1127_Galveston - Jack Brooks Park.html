Galveston - Jack Brooks Park

From: The Frye's (frye@wt.net) | Date: 2005-07-29 08:39:39 UTC-05:00
We were able to get many boxes this past week in Galveston. (Of course I
got home to find out that 2 new ones were placed in areas that we were at-
bummer)

All I have to say though is to bring bottles and bottles of bug spray. I
have never seen so many mosquitoes in all of my life and I grew up in
Texas. The birding parks were really bad but at A&M they were the biggest,
baddest bugs I have ever seen. They were flying thru the bug spray to
bite us - some even made it thru the spray. Once we had the box, we ran
back to the car with a swarm behind us. We dove into the car and slammed
the door - it looked like thousands of them were trying to get in our car
window. It was amazing BUT of course we would do it again for all the cool
boxes we found - ha ha!!

Has anyone been to Jack Brooks park lately? We tried to find 2 there with
no luck but I was also thinking that my brain may have had too much sun
already this week.
