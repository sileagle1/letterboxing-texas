Just an idea

From: Tara Burton (mselly13@juno.com) | Date: 2008-10-25 18:39:59 UTC
I had to travel to Oklahoma last week to visit an ill relative.
While there, I stopped at the local WalMart to pick up some post cards
to send to my son who is in basic training and to send to my 4 yr old
daughter at home.
I found some interesting ones and got some ideas of places to later
plant letterboxes.
So, my idea to everyone is this, don't forget to look at post cards
not only in your local area but while traveling...they can be great
inspirations for planting boxes.
Have fun boxing y'all.

See you on the trail.
