I am not very controlling when it comes to my boxes.  I would love it if a herd of Girl or Boy Scouts found one of my boxes.  I would be honored if one of my boxes gave a bunch of kids some joy.  Although, I guess I do think kids should be taught to respect other people's property, including letterboxes.  I would hope the adult in charge would want to make sure the box is hidden well, whether the adult is supervising scouts or his or her own children.  -- Bell Motel
