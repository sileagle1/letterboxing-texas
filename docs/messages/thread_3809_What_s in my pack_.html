What's in my pack.

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-10-12 22:47:03 UTC-06:00


Several weeks ago the topic was WHAT IS IN YOUR PACK. As I am gearing up for a
month of hikes in the Celebration of our Mountains month
http://www.celebrationofourmountains.org/ I had to go through my pack. And here
it is. Of course, LETTERBOXING and GEOCACHING stuff too.

BTW - I believe in OVERKILL! ALSO - nearly everything electronic uses a
common AA battery. So I can remove a battery from say - the camera, to power
the strobe if it goes weak. Got to think of stuff like this too.

Here we go.


Operational - Mission essential Equipment.

1 Garmin GPS-MAP60CSx GPS Receiver.
1 Sony DSC-P73 Digital Camera.
3 Packs Spare AA Batteries.
1 Icom IC-T81 handheld with 2 Battery Packs.
1 Icom HM-75 Remote Control Microphone.
1 Comet SRH-999 Gain Antenna.
1 Pair Lightweight Binoculars.
1 Pair Comfortable Work Gloves.

Special Equipment.

Wallet - ID - Money/credit card.
Pistol (lightweight Titanium .357 Magnum).
Ammo Reload (7-shot speed-loader).
12 plastic shopping bags for trash/dog poop.

Useful Accessories.

1 Waterproof Notebook.
1 Ink Pen.
1 Sharpie Marker.
2 Mechanical Pencils.
1 Tube Lip Balm.
1 Small Bottle Hand Sanitizer.
4 Lightweight Carabineers.
4 ea Figure-9 hooks with 15ft 550 Cord.
1 Light Weight Canopy.

Emergency Equipment.

1 Emergency Data Card (MY info).
1 Emergency Info Card (Phone #s of rangers etc).
1 Desert Survival Card.
1 Emergency Beacon Strobe.
1 Emergency Signal Mirror.
1 Emergency Whistle.
1 Gerber Multi-tool.
1 Compact Emergency Blanket.
1 pr Eye Glasses (if needed - for removing cactus thorns).

First Aid Equipment. (Small Kit).

5 Bandage - 7/8 x 3" Standard.
5 Bandage - 5/8 x 1-1/2" Small.
5 Bandage - 2 x 3" Large Patch.
5 Bandage - 1-3/4 x 2" Fingertip.
5 Bandage - 1-3/4 x 3" Large Fingertip.
5 Antiseptic wipes.
5 Neomycin Antibiotic Ointment.
2 Vials StingKill bee sting insect bite swabs.
50 Tablets 200mg Ibuprofen.

First Aid Equipment. (Large Kit).
(In addition to the small kit - not always along).

1 Cravat
2 Field dressings
1 Eppi-injector
1 btl No-Doze
1 btl Nytol
1 btl Alcohol
1 pk Pepcid A/C
1 pk Tylenol
1 btl First Aid Cream
1 btl Betadine
1 pk Gauze
1 rl Tape
2 sml pk Toilet Paper
2 btls Water Purifier
1 pr Rubber Gloves
5 Finger Bandages
10 Moist Towelettes
1 Hemostat
1 pr Scissors
5 ea 4x4 Gauze Pads
1 Eye Dressing Kit
1 Head Dressing
1 ea CPR Micro-Shield
1 pk Kleenex
1 btl Pain Killer/Antacid

Sometimes along.

Canvas water dish for the dog.
Extra water for the dog.
Extra food for the dog.




Heh heh heh - - - - comments???
