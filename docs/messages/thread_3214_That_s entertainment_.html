From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-12-05 22:54:14 UTC
Hey All!
I am still up here in Arlington, actively boxing, but hadn't posted
since forever on this site,nor had I checked the posts in awhile.
I'm so glad I finally got around to checking it!

The Miata stories totally cracked me up and made me think of the time
I visited Teller City in Colorado. my Grandfather told me about a
ghost town up near Poudre Canyon. Well, when I was in my 20's I
finally drove my Chrysler Cirrus up there and totally bottomed it out
on all the huge rocks and ruts in the old mountain road! I kept
thinking, it's going to be worth it, it's going to be worth it!
After 2 1/2 hours of that I finally arrived in Teller City expecting
to see almost a Hollywood movie set of saloons, horse troughs and
tumbleweeds! Uhhhh, there were a few old timbers still lying on the
ground in a rough rectangular shape. :( Now I would completely
appreciate it and be fascinated by an old silver mining town
abandoned so quickly that it was said dishes were still on the
tables. (I also would now HAVE to plant a box there. Hmmmm, I have a
pickup truck now........) However, in my naive youth, I just kept
thinking "I ruined my car for this?"!, "A bunch of logs on the
ground!"

BTW, is anybody itching to see National Treasure II? I may have to
go back down to Granbury and spruce up my John Wilkes Booth box,
based on the previews!';)

-Kantexan
