From: CelticLions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-09-01 19:21:17 UTC-05:00


Have you ever felt as though you're wading through molassess;-)???

I picked up the T-shirts Monday and as soon as I began packing
envelopes, immediately realized the numbers were wrong. For some reason
known only to the gods of Murphy's Law, the Youth Small Texas
Letterboxing T-shirts were sent to Oklahoma...I'm not even going to try
to repeat the story I was told.
So...for those of you who *did not* order those small T-shirts, your
orders went out today.
To those of you with little kids waiting on those shirts, I'm so sorry -
the silk screen company here in Dallas is going to try to get them back
here by tomorrow afternoon & I'll mail them out immediately ( if the
Post Office is still open!) or Friday.

Ready for the good news? I thought I was experienced in getting T-shirts
made, but I have learned so much that I think if we ever want to order
more, this whole process could be MUCH faster and easier!

NOTE: Three people have not sent in their payment for the shirts. I've
nagged a couple of times, and it's been about a month since I first
requested payment.
No hard feelings - I understand how life happens sometimes. But I'm sure
you guys understand that I don't want to carry the expense, either. Any
shirts unpaid for by Saturday, September 4th will be put back up for
sale and made available to newcomers.

Hope you enjoy wearing your new Lone Star Letterboxing threads this
weekend!
