From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-09-26 14:20:48 UTC
Glad to see most of you are safe, as my family is, but I
will feel better once I hear from Droop. We did not
evacuate but had a houseful of family. We got hardly
any rain and minimal wind, so glad we stayed out of the
traffic. Glad we dodged the bullet, but sorry for East
TX. Lucy, you probably got more of the storm than I did!
I'm also glad Mike took my advice and joined this group.
I sent an email to the NM boxer on this list to try and
get them aware of each other. I think we now have a
letterboxer in every region of TX!
Riding out Rita ended up being fun for my kids since it
was not that bad and family was over. I was worried how
they would deal with it since I still have vivid memories
of the last big hurricane to hit Houston area, Carla in 1961
when I was 6 years old. I'm so glad we avoided that trauma!
Good luck to everyone getting back to their normal lives and
back to letterboxing when time allows, and thanks for being
such a great, supportive group.

Silver Eagle

I am really glad to hear from you! I felt like y'all

change...
