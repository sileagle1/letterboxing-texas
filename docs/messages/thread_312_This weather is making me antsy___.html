This weather is making me antsy!!!

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-10 10:44:26 UTC-07:00
Sometime about the end of July, I fell into a real
funk about the summer heat (I hate heat! Hate it!)
and didn't have any desire to go traipse through brush
and woods to find boxes. I had satisfied myself
during the first part of the summer with carving
stamps and making boxes, but eventually that's just
not as much fun if you never take the boxes anywhere
and leave them. I was to the point that I considered
stacking them in my car, so I could at least feel like
I was ready to go!

Fortunately, we got preoccupied with scout stuff and
were out of town for an extended period of time with
that, so that when I hit home again a week ago, I felt
like I had finally survived the summertime and there
was hope yet for nice weather!

Well, this weekend, I think I'm really, really gonna
get out there and take some boxes out! I'm thinking
they'll be somewhere north and east of Tyler, a real
dry area for boxes. I can't wait to get outside for
awhile!

Lucy
