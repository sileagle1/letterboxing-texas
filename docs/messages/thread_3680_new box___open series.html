OMG, that sounds so great. I have one I might add. It's just the spot to plant will be so hard, unless I enlist the help of the person that owns the house.

Before I moved over here to Watauga, I lived in Bedford. To avoid traffic from Ft Worth to home in the mornings, I would go the 'backroads'. This backroad took me by a house on the corner. During the heat of the summer, I would see this man painting his fence. He would sit in a folding chair and slowly paint his fence.

Then, all of a sudden, he wasn't there anymore. I'm afraid of why. I almost cried when I realized he probably couldn't finish for one reason or another.

The painting is mostly old car scenes. It was great. Anyone can paint their fence white, but he was creating an art piece right there for all to see.

I think I'm going to drive by there today and see what's changed in the last year. I hope to see more painting.

Janet
