What do I like about Texas?
 
I like the fact that as hard as I might try, I'll probably never see every inch of the whole state and I'll for sure never know everything about it.  And that there is such a huge array in scenery and culture and history that I'll never get tired of TRYING to see and learn everything.
 
But you know - I think what I love most is the fact that no matter where I go, I never know if right around the next corner will be some breathtaking view.  I've lived all but about 10 of my 43 years in Northeast Texas, and I still never tire of seeing the brilliant green landscapes with multitudes of other colors laid against the green, all so vivid they hurt my eyes when I look at them.
