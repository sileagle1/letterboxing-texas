Aside from the topic of my poor store ordered stamps - and for those of you who are more technically inclined (like me) instead of artistically inclined, tigerdirect.com is closing out the magellan roadmate 700 for $229.99.  This is a $1200 GPS unit.  YEAH - no joke!

NOTE:  This is NOT a hiking unit.  This is primairly for your car.  That said, you could do a lot of planning and routing with it to find all the parking places for your letterboxing outing.  It would make the traveling between boxes easy.  This one TALKS to you - "TURN RIGHT IN 500 FEET".  It can be used portable - but it is rather LARGE for that!  The screen is made to be used in a car, not in the hand.

Now - I dislike magellan because they are owned by thales, and thales is french, and the french are putting our soldiers at greater risk.  But that said, it is STILL a good deal.  Make your own decision.
