From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-03-21 21:20:24 UTC
I'm doing great! After I got out of the hospital on Monday afternoon, we came home to my house and I stayed home on Tuesday. Blue Butterfly went home late Tuesday afternoon and I went back to work on Wednesday.

My sixth graders were glad to see me back. They were glad that I was ok and they all wanted to see where the snake had bitten me. For the most part, the girls looked and declared, "eeeeww!" while the boys' reaction was more like, "cool!". I had one boy give me a hug "for not dying".

Things are pretty much back to normal. I still have a tiny bit of swelling and soreness, but that's it! I even went letterboxing this week while I was on spring break.

Do you remember me saying that I told the students and teachers at my school that I was bitten while picking up fire wood? Well, here was one conversation with one of my boys:

Boy: Well, did you learn anything?
Me: Uuhh....I like being in school better than being in the hospital?
Boy: No, You have to KICK the fire wood BEFORE you pick any up!
Me: Oh! Of course! You are right about that!!

Once again, thanks to EVERYONE who helped out and who sent my mom and I encouraging emails and messages. You will never know how much it meant to us.

Gratefully,
Lucy Locket
