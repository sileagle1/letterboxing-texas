From: Darcie (boughtonoutdoor@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-27 20:03:38 UTC
I usually get the messages from the group in a digest, but today I
decided to check what was going on. THE TOUR BUS IS IN MY
NEIGHBORHOOD!! So out we went. As we pulled up to the parking area
there was a man and his todler son leaving and I was sure we missed the
bus because just before he loaded up he put a small camo cooler type
box in the back of his truck. We decided to just check to make sure.
After they left we walked down the trail and there is was. YEA!

We just got home and it has started raining. Not something we see
around here lately. These passengers have been very lucky lately to
miss the fire and now the rain! All looks good and dry - we can't wait
to see who all is on the bus.

I guess I have to learn this carving thing, since I have yet to do
one. Looks like my first will be on the bus!! I will have questions
when I get to that point and know that you all will give me some
guidance! I'm looking forward to getting to bus moving soon!
