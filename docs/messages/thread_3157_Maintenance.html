From: Puddle Splasher (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-11-03 02:01:49 UTC
I got a couple of reports yesterday from finders of my Halloween
Treats boxes (in Ft Worth) that there were wasps or yellow jackets
buzzing around the vicinity of the first box. Not wanting anyone to get
stung, I drove over there today (70 miles) armed with my wasp spray and
wearing NO perfume! I thoroughly checked the bush the box is hidden in
didn't find any wasps or other stinging type creatures or a nest, but I
did go ahead and saturate the area surrounding the box with wasp
repellent just to make sure. Anyway, since a couple of people did
encounter some yesterday, I will post a warning on the clues just in
case.
I also got a report that the finders of my Delaney Vineyards box
in Grapevine accidentally dropped the box down inside the tree
yesterday and couldn't reach it. I didn't have my long BBQ tongs with
me today to retrieve it, so I'll have to plan another trip over there
soon to take care of that. I do appreciate them emailing me and letting
me know, however.
Puddle Splasher
