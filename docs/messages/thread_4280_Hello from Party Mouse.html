Hello all,

I just joined and wanted to say "Hi" to all. My trail name is Party Mouse, and already I recognize a few of you. I live near Austin with my wife and 2 kids (daughter 15, son 13). I work in IT, but am always ready to get outdoors. Letterboxing gives me another great reason to do so.

We just started letterboxing this past summer. We "discovered" the hobby just before we took a trip with extended family to Durango, Colorado. Found a few local boxes to whet our appetites, then found several along the way and while in Durango. What a great splash of flavor it added to our vacation! It took us to places we would have never seen otherwise.

We haven't placed any boxes yet, but fully intend to. Will let you all know when the time comes. Glad to be a member of this group and looking forward to learning more, hearing some good stories, and meeting some great people.
