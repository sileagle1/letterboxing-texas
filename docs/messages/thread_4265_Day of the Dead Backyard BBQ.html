Day of the Dead Backyard BBQ

From: (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2009-10-23 13:12:57 UTC-05:00
Bell Motel and Whiz Gee would like to invite every letterboxer in Austin (and any visiting Austin) to our house for a Backyard BBQ on the afternoon of November 1 -- the Day of the Dead, from 4:00pm to 8:00pm. It will be a good time to catch up with old letterboxing friends, and meet new ones.  Please sign up on AtlasQuest.  Please email me, and I will send you our address.

I plan to plant a few boxes in the green space behind our house.  We also live very near Great Hills Park.  If the weather is bad, we will move inside and I will plant the boxes around my living room!  We look forward to seeing everybody!
