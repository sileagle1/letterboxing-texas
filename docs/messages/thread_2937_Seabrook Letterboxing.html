Seabrook Letterboxing

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-07-18 22:22:52 UTC
The kids and I dodged the rain today and managed to find a few of the
many boxes in Seabrook. We found: A Literary Quest (both boxes),
Dragonology, and Its All Part Of Letterboxing (7 boxes), all by the
Hoppers. As a thankyou, I placed a box for them (and ya'll) to find
called Seabrook Seagulls and just posted the clues. I actually found a
park that did not already have a box in it, and was appropriate for
viewing seagulls. It did not have great hiding places, but hopefully
the box will survive since it is a micro. Anyone planning a trip to
Seabrook area will now have to allow a full day to get all the boxes,
but be sure to allow enough time to eat at Tookies. It has
some "different" hamburgers that are really good, and some of the best
onion rings around. Enjoy!

Silver Eagle
