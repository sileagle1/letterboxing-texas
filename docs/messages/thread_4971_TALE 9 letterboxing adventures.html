TALE 9 letterboxing adventures

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2012-03-04 20:54:07 UTC-06:00

We finally arrived home after an event filled weekend!  We boxed, ate, and talked to old friends and new, mislead hapless strangers, filled up logbooks with stamps, and found all sorts of WILD creatures.  We dodged gators, vultures, squirrels,  and the occasional bird watcher and park volunteer to find all those wonderful carves – and they were wonderful indeed!  We ran into letterboxes on the trail, at campsites, and places outside the park.  We stuffed ourselves on delicious food and enjoyed the company of others just as crazy as us.   We even rescued a few misplaced stamps J  We had a great time, and despite having BOTH our vehicles break down today we made it home safe and sound.  So here is a round of thanks for everyone who made this a great weekend….

 

To Baby Bear and Silver Eagle for once again organizing a great event

To everyone who planted boxes for the event….Wow! 

To everyone who found my boxes J

To everyone who went boxing with us

To those who gave us clues to boxes we missed, didn’t have, or couldn’t find (you know who you are)

To the mysterious and sneaky people for things I can’t discuss

To Wry Me for driving me and my stuff home after the 2nd car broke down and had to be towed. 

To my husband for putting up with this madness and doing the dishes!

 

Now I just need someone to log all my finds!  I got them all J
