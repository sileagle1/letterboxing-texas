From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-06-23 13:53:59 UTC-05:00
I've been giving some thought to our missing Texas Tour Bus One. (Sounds kind of like Air Force One, doesn't it?) Anyway, I digress. If the family that was given the TTB-1 is new to boxing and isn't actually on the Texas list and has no idea what they are supposed to do with the box, maybe we can contact them through LBNA or AQ. Perhaps they are checking those sites to get their letterbox clues and are looking for more boxes in the Austin area. Perhaps we can post clues for a bogus box named "The Missing Bus" located in the Austin area where they were given the box. However, instead of writing clues for finding a box, we can post a message to them explaining what the bus is and ask them to contact someone (perhaps Gingercat?) about getting the bus back on the road?.... Maybe impress upon them the historical value of the bus and the importance it holds to Texas letterboxers?
 
Any thoughts?
Puddle Splasher
