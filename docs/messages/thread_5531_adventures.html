my latest adventure was to Independence specifically to find the box Boots dedicated to Sam Houston, Jr. No only is the box the usual Boots quality, but the Independence location is steeped in a variety of history---the cemetery, the campuses, a historic home of Sam Houston, etc.

On a previous LB excursion there, an eager Houston resident snagged me and gave me a tour of the spring house he was constructiing on the creek where Houston had been baptized. (It was his current weekend project.) Still not satisfied, he took me to his mother's house and showed me the corncrib that Houston had built. Furthermore, at his insistence I got on my hands and knees and peeked under his mother's house to see beams Houston had marked.

He never learned why I was in the area.......but I do meet the nicest people.
