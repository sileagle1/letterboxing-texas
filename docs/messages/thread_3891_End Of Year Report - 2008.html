From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-12-31 22:16:51 UTC-06:00
Okay - I majored in art in college so sometimes my math is a bit creative (nice word for incorrect). But if I understand the comparison of this year's number to last, we've added 828 letterboxes in Texas (listed on LBNA) this past year. If that's correct, and my math isn't too far off, that means about 1/3 of our total number of letterboxes in Texas were planted within the past year! That's kind of amazing. That must be why I have a stack of clues about 3" thick of boxes I am hoping to find in the near future. I remember a time, not that long ago, that I had found every letterbox within 50 miles of the DFW area. Now I could letterbox for weeks and before beginning to catch up! How exciting!
Happy New Year, Texas Letterboxers!
Puddle Splasher
