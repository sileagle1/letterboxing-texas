From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-11-24 19:09:29 UTC
Just in time for your Thanksgiving pleasure, I planted a short series
at Memorial Park called the Writing's on the Wall. This was my first
time to get to Memorial Park since Ike, and the number of downed trees
was quite incredible.

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=98864

Perky P had emailed me about my Orange Your Glad box, and the tree
which was hiding it was a casualty of the hurricane. I knew about
where in the tree it was, so I tried several different approaches to
try and reach it. Luckily, after about 15 minutes and multiple
strategies, I found it. The logbooks were soaked, which makes me sad,
as it had been one of my most-found boxes in the 3 1/2 years it had
been out there. But, I brought it home to do maintenance and I plan
on finding a new spot and replacing it.

And, Perky P? No mosquitoes this morning. Seriously. I walked back
there without spray and left without a single bite.
