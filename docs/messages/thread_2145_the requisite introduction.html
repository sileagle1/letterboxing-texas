Well, I guess my intention was to "lurk" on this list and just gain info & news about letterboxing...but I'm a follow-the-rules guy and the moderators asked for an introduction.  My wife & I heard about letterboxing separately (I had forgotten to mention it in my excitement, but when she did I knew all about it).  We're only a couple weeks in, but we've found a half-dozen or so boxes in Houston & are already planning a couple boxes of our own.  Still need to work on our stamps--we started with store bought, but are planning more personal ones.  We're taking our 1 year old along for the ride and of course creating a logbook for her.  Occasionally our 7 yr old nephew joins in the fun.  We're hooked!
Peace to you all,
