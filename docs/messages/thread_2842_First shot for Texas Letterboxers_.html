From: Mike - 2007 (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2007-06-20 03:15:26 UTC

Good evening all.

        Just last week the discussion was hydration systems and Camel-Bak.  Well - due to Father's Day my Camel (Bak) stable has been increased by one new Camelbak.  I have too many now - one has got to go.  So I thought I would "offer" it up to those of you who were interested in a Camel-Bak.

        Note - this isn't one of the light-weight civilian models.  This is the Military version, tough and rugged.  It is 3-color desert camouflage colored.  It is in great shape - no rips or tears or worn spots.  I did have the local shoe shop sew two attachment points - one on each side of the upper cargo pouch - for hanging my radio gear etc.   It is a neat job - not a hack job (thats why I paid him and didn't try it myself).  The specs say 100 OZ/3L, but if you hold it right as you fill it - it gets nearly a full gallon.  The drinking tube is insulated too - water stays cooler than the clear civilian one.  BTW - the letterboxing kits for both me and the wife fits easily in the upper pouch with room to spare.  It has MOLE straps for hanging more gear/pouches on it.  The little elastic ears on the bottom allow you to roll up the waist straps and tuck them in so they do not dangle.  CIV version don't have that either.

SPECS:

Camelbak Military MULE, 100 oz./3.0L
Capacity:

Water: 100 oz. (3.0L)

Cargo: 540 Cu. In.  (8L)

CamelBak's MULE is still the most efficient and compact way to carry your water and field essentials. You will appreciate our Independent Suspension Harness during fast-paced, on-the-run missions - it stabilizes the load as you move. And, the Air Director™ back panel keeps you cool as you push onward.

Available in 4 colors: black, desert camo, woodland camo and army universal camo.

Features:

*Use D-ring attachment points to place unit anywhere
*Includes HydroLink Modular Connection System with positive shut-off HydroLock
*Includes Omega Reservoir fill and clean easier than ever
*Independent Suspension Harness keeps load stable as you move
*Tough, abrasion resistant 1000 Denier Cordura exterior
*Integrated MOLLE/PAL (MOdular Lightweight Load-carrying Equipment) attachment system
*Available in 4 colors: THIS ONE is desert camo.
*Secure storage pockets organize essentials, such as MRE's
*Dimensions:  18.5" L x 10" W x 5" H
*Volume:  Cargo Only // 540cu in - Including Water // 720cu in

*Weight:  Empty Reservoir //1.8lbs - Filled Reservoir //8.1lbs.

These have an MSRP of $112.  I have seen them on special for $85.  I want $40 for it, plus $5 shipping for the lower 48.  If you like, I will put a new unused bladder in it for an additional $10.  If no one is interested it goes to my buddy's surplus store.  Photos available on request.
