From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-04-01 22:18:16 UTC
The McBoxing on AQ is well planned and executed, and the reverse trail
names on LBNA is cute, but the best Letterboxing April Fools I ever saw
was back in 2004. The animosity between geocachers and letterboxers
was particularly high during that time, so imagine the surprise and
outrage when on April 1, 2004, letterboxers who clicked on LBNA, and
geocachers who clicked on their site, were all sent to a new site
called Boxcaching. It proclaimed that both hobbies had merged into
this new one and too bad if you didn't like it. The flames were out of
control and really amusing to peruse, including some from the webmaster
of Geocaching.Com, and I highly recommend that you look at the archives
of the main Talklist.

elgaE revliS
