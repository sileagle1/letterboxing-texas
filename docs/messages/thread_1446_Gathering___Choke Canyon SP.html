From: Susan Tashiro (stashiro@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2005-11-06 19:45:42 UTC-06:00
Choke Canyon State Park (Calliham Unit) gets my vote.   There are a couple of boxes in the park and another few in the area.  Choke Canyon has great fishing and boating.  Not as many hiking trails as some of the park,  but it does have it's share.  There is primitive camping as well as cabins and RV camping sites  (some of these are my former front yard about 25 years ago, before the park).  There is the "Kitchen" that is a fantastic group picnic area.  It has an actual kitchen with stove/oven and refrigerator.  If we decide to go for Choke, I'll get my act in gear and plant more there.  Busy Bee, you up for a challenge of planting boxes at Choke/Three Rivers?
 
I also think that Goliad SP would be a good place.  It is a beautiful park.  http://www.tpwd.state.tx.us/spdest/findadest/parks/goliad_and_mission_espiritu_santo/
 
CalicoRose

From: Wanda (wsthm@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-11-07 04:09:33 UTC
Yes, I'm up for it, and would vote for either Choke Canyon or Goliad
State Park. Actually there is one LB at Choke Canyone that I don't
think ever made it to the database. My DIL planted it on a birding
trail, but the lb.org database was all messed up for a few days when
she was trying to post the clues. I don't think she ever got back to
it. The trail she hid it on, has various kinds/styles of birdhouses
in the trees all along the trail....including HUGE ones that I
assume are for owls. There is at least one geocache there too -
maybe more, I don't know.

As for Goliad, I absolutely LOVE the history of that area. There are
several state park areas...Mission La Bahia with a museum attached
and interesting ruins on the grounds....Presidio La Bahia with more
historical structures....and the new archeleogical excavation-in-
progress of Mission Rosario. There is also the historical town
square and all those huge oak trees, including the old hanging tree.
The area figured quite largely in the 'Ox-Cart War', too. Fannin
Battlefield is nearby.

Adding my 2 cents to Calico Rose's...
Wanda/Busy Bee
