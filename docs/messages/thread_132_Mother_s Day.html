Okay guys and gals, I got the neatest Mother’s Day gift of all.  SunSnake’s class at school made booklets with different types of poetry, pictures and a letter.  Let me quote part of the letter she wrote to me:

 

“I think you’re a better letterboxer than Silver Eagle and a better carver than Monkeytoes.”

 

Now I’m sure that this tribute would have bewildered lots of moms. (SunSnake’s teacher asked her “Are you sure your mom knows these people?”) However, I’m certain that letterboxing moms, especially Texas letterboxing moms will agree that it’s about the highest compliment a mom could get.

 

Happy Mother’s Day All!

 

Corazon
