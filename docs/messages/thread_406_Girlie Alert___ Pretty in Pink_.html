From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-10-15 17:39:26 UTC-05:00
We have 3 letterboxes sitting on the kitchen table ready to plant - a series
of "Pretty-in-Pink"! My oldest son's girlfriend (Flicker, if any of you have
seen her stamp) remarked the other day that she would really like to see a
stamp that looked good in pink. We absolutely love her, so of course we did
our best to make it happen ;-).
Please picture my 16 year old son wincing slightly as he tried to come up
with cute PINK things to carve.
(And beware, all you manly letterboxers out there - if you find these boxes,
you might catch cooties from their sheer PINKness...;-))

Ruby T.
