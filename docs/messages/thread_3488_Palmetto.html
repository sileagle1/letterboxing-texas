From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-04-13 13:39:18 UTC
Another mystery: Lone Star Quilter's letterbox at Palmetto State Park
called "Palmetto - Quilt Block Series" has somehow moved. We got a
message from a non-letterboxer (I assume) who said they found the box
accidentally and replaced it where they found it. Then, when they went
into LbNA to check it out, realized it was in the wrong place. They
graciously told me where they left it. We have posted the new location
on the clue sheets with a request to move it back to where it was
originally placed. The new location is in a pile of rocks surrounding
a vertical overflow pipe for the CCC pump that feeds the wetlands in
the park. When we were there, the pipe was flowing freely, leading me
to think that the letterbox will be in a shower of water most of the
time. If anyone happens by that way, you might want to reprint the
clues. Thanks a bunch.

Boots Tex
