Polls Closed - Festivals the winner

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-08-07 03:01:26 UTC
Hey all,

I am on vacation, and as we stumbled into a wifi-ready motel tonight,
I am getting caught up on some business.

It stormed out to an early lead, and maintained that wire to wire.
So, I present to you the theme for the great Texas Box exchange:
Festivals.

In my admittedly humble opinion, wacky ones are much more fun to work
with, but the topic will be open to all ideas. I am constantly amazed
by the group energy and creativity so I can't wait to see what
everyone comes up with.

When I get back to Houston in a couple of days, I'll set up the
database. You will be able to sign up with your festival theme choice
and your hometown. I will do a draw that will be somewhat rigged to
create geographic diversity and then will come up with who you should
mail your box to. A few reminders of specifics.

1. target mail date is september 15.
2. the carver is responsible for creating all aspects of the
to-be-planted box--stamp, logbook and container.
3. the recipient will find a great hiding place and do all the
maintenance on the box and the clues.

Other than that let's all have fun. The primary driver behind the
exchange is to allow those from different corners and areas of the
state to see the creativity going on in other parts. I for one am
very excited that there might be a few boxes from Corazon, Miss Moon,
etc. in closer geographic proximity (although we'll see how the draw
goes).

Thanks again!

Dewberry, from somewhere on I-10.
