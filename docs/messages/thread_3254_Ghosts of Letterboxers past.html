My extended family and I packed up the bikes and a picnic and went to spend the day in Burroughs Park today.  I was thinking of all of you as I chased Naomi through the pavilion...remembering what fun we had the day of the gathering there.  It was as if it was full of a bunch of happy, inky ghosts.
 
Oh, and Lucy...it wasn't me hollering at the kids in the woods the other day (unless I was hollering so loud here in Houston that you heard me all the way to Palestine, which is entirely possible.)  I was jealous reading of your day there, over Thanksgiving break we took the kids to ride the state railroad, with great plans of finding a governor in Palestine and then stopping by Davey Dogwood park on the way home.  We got as far as Water from the Rocks, when the rain and then the stomach flu started at almost exactly the same time.  Imagine driving all the way home with a carload of sick kids (and a sick mom) it wasn't pretty.  In any case, we had to cut that trip short, but it is one I definitely am keeping in mind for a future roadtrip.  MO5
