Texas Vacation Report

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2005-11-27 06:51:09 UTC
Howdy to the Texans!

Well, we are now back in Wisconsin and none too happy to find SNOW in
the yard! In contrast, we really enjoyed the pleasant weather over
the past week while we were in Texas sunny & warm (almost hot)
every day except this morning. The rain today caused us to skip two
boxes we hoped to try for, but we can't complain about only one
morning of rain.

During our 8 days, we managed to do most of the things Silver Eagle
and others suggested many state parks (Enchanted Rock and Lost
Maples were our favorites, as was Hamilton Pool Preserve), a few
wineries, apple ice cream, some Tex-Mex (Old Spanish Trail in Bandera
was our favorite) and 3 different BBQ places (Smitty's in Lockhart
was our favorite).

Over the course of 8 days we attempted 84 boxes. However 17 eluded
us either because we suspect they are missing OR because we were
unable to decipher the clues well enough to find the right location.
Nonetheless, we were very happy to add 67 new stamps to our logbooks,
plus 2 hitchhikers. We'll be sending reports to the individual
placers to report on the status of each box (we added a few new
logbooks, and even a new box to replace one that was severely
cracked, and also duct-taped a few stamps that were broken). We
appreciated the hand-carved stamps. A highlight of the trip was that
my mom (Tabby Tales) reached her F100 milestone and we reached our
P50 and F1100 milestones.

During our vacation we placed 2 new boxes for those of you that enjoy
a hike. I'll post the clues in the next few days.

Thanks again to everyone that gave us some advice and we will proudly
add the Texas LB patch to our pack!

Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man
