Sunday Sun day...Keller & Grapevine boxes

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-05-23 19:35:25 UTC-05:00
They say that only mad dogs and Englishmen go out in the noonday sun...well,
we proved them right!

Stillwater, Vandal and I have been longing to go check out TX2Chic's boxes
in the Keller area - and each time Viewfinder planted a new one, it became
harder to resist!
So of course we picked a day when it was over 100 degrees, and waited until
noon to start...;-)
We've never looked around Keller - beautiful city and the box locations were
delightful. Clues were perfect, stamps & logbooks were very cute, and we had
a wonderful time....despite feeling as though we were trudging thru a sauna!

One funny note - in one of the logbooks I spotted a really neat stamp I'd
never seen before - and pointed it out to Stillwater. It reminded me a bit
of TeamKing's clever stamp. When I returned home I had an email from that
out-of-town boxer who had also located a couple of our CelticLions boxes.

It was especially exciting to hunt Tx2chic's boxes, because I can remember
back when I first found their stamp in one of our boxes & I'm always HOPING
that people who hunt our boxes will turn around and plant some for us to
hunt...well, they certainly did!

Thanks for a wonderful day!
