Local authorities have just confirmed that the rowdy escapees from
Rusk State Hospital have made contact with a family member to bring
them some vittles. Sources say that the family member was seen going
south on Hwy 69 and then turning west on Hwy 21. The family member
then turned towards the Neches Bluff Overlook and proceeded to the
trail head. At this point the family member almost lost sight of them
down the trail. However just as he was about to take a right at a
fork in the trail, he started hearing music and voices coming from the
left. So he turned left and rolled down a large hill and saw the
group to his left hiding near a fallen tree. The tree was big enough
that they could all hide underneath it with a little help from a small
tree next to it.
