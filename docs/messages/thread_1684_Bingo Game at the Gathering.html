Bingo Game at the Gathering

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-02-10 22:52:05 UTC
Wow! Three more great stamps arrived today from out-of-state boxers.

So far, I have received lovely donations from

SewSoBizzy
PostMystress
Dragonrider
So. Hill Hendersons
Speedsquare
Dixie Kin
Safari Man

with other stamps on the way from other fabulous boxers. A big thanks
to those who have donated stamps & more.

If you are going to play Bingo, please let me know by March 1st, so
that I make enough cards. I am still working out the rules (thanks to
Lock Wench for sharing her ideas), but it will work similarly to this:

You will receive a card with 25 squares on it. Instead of numbers,
there will be box names in the squares. As you box in Rusk SP and
around town, you can fill in the squares with the stamps from the
boxes. The first one to bring back five in a row wins a prize! The
one with the most squares wins a prize! The one with the most rows
filled wins a prize! Heck, you may all win a prize (this part is a
little fuzzy). Look for sneaky clues and easy clues. What would be
the fun if we didn't Gramatrick up at least a few of them? Yes,
TeamKing, that means that there will be at least a few puzzle clues,
but you should be able to still get BINGO without those boxes.

To register to play:

either send an email to my gmail account or to me at atlasquest. You
must also tell me something that not everyone will know about you. It
can be something like where you were born, your unusual profession,
that there were 20 people in your high school class, the fact that
you've been to the south pole, how your trail name found you, etc.

Now, clearly, there are some with closer relationships with others. I
am not asking for your deepest family secrets, but rather something
kind of fun that isn't general knowledge and that you don't mind if
others discover about you.

If you plan on planting a box that should be included in the BINGO
game, let me know that too. I need the name of the box to make up the
cards.

I probably forgot something, but that's it for now.

Dewberry
