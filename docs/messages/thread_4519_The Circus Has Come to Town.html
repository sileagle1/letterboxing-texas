Well after the Girl Scout cookie fiasco, the circus performers laid low for a short time and then headed south to the Tomato Capital of the World where they had heard that a circus fight had taken place.  They decided that this was the place for them to stir up a little trouble.  LB Bozo had secretly heard about a group near this place that had started up the East Texas Society of Save the Baby Bears and he just couldnt pass up the chance to start some trouble with that group.  When they got there, they found that there was already a circus in town, so they did go and recruit a couple of the members to join with the  Traveling Circus.  After the "good" circus left town they wanted to go check out the Mud Creek ATV park, but the owners said they were too rowdy for their park.  So instead they contacted The Rose and the Tomato Farmer for directions to their place, which just happened to be behind the Mud Creek Park.  The Rose made them a little area to stay in and gave them a black bucket "cave" to hide in if they needed them.  So if they are not in the pasture practicing their routines, then they are probably sleeping under the bucket.  Good luck with these characters, they are a handful.  (by the way, the owners of this property do know that the circus is here, so don't worry)

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-04-23 10:19:06 UTC-05:00
Well. I hated to see that poor bunch of circus folks being left in some pasture in this horrible rain. I can't believe you left them that way, or I couldn't...but I'm starting to see why you did it. Somehow seeing wet droopy clowns givesa bit of twisted pleasure to anyone who has suffered with them for even an hour.
Anyway, I got up this morning and saw the message and quickly devised a way to not have to be at work right at 8 am. We won't go into that. I jumped in my nice clean mini-van (back to that in a minute) and set out for the area where you dumped - I mean hid - the Circus.
It was no problem getting there since I had found that box before and the black bucket stuck out likea sore thumb to anyone looking for it, but looked perfect normal to anyone passing by.
What was odd when I got there? All the cows were gathered about the bucket, looking a bit wild-eyed. As I got out of my (no longer clean) van, two cows put their feet on the bucket, as if to point me directly to the bus. When they realized I was indeed going toward the bucket, the relief in their eyes was very real, and one of them flipped the bucket over with her foot, clearly revealing the Circus. I grabbed the Circus and headed back to my car to what seemed like the distinct sound of laughing cows. Yes, I swear they were laughing. I got in my mud-caked van and as I looked in the rearview mirror, I swear they were smiling at me and I think I heard one or two call "thank you, thank you" to me. I looked ahead to steer myself back in the direction I had come from, and when I looked in my rear view mirror again, I saw that the clowns had escaped and the others were clawing their way out of the box. The clowns were plastered against my back window, laughing and pointing at someone behind me - it WAS PuddleSplasher! The bad thing - Puddle Splasher was laughing and pointing back. Even as I drove back into town, I had the distinct idea that I hadn't necessarily "won"...
