Mother of 5 , I know what you mean about the laundry and kids. It's
also like when you get one room of the house clean, move to the
next, return to original room and find out your kids have made their

it weren't for the Zen moments I have whilst letterboxing I would
slowly lose my sanity with the unending housework! On laundry day I
often find myself muttering Brad Pitt's famous line from A River
Runs Through It. I believe he said, "I am haunted by laundry". Or
was it water? Heheheheheh! ;)
