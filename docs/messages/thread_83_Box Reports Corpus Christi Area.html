Hi, I had a busy weekend boxing. My family thinks I am crazier than
ever. Just wanted to let you know about some boxes.

Super Frogger: CUTE! but a little damp

Corpus Porpiose: Missing, found the rocks, no box.

Coral Snake: Found it, didn't get bit.

Texas Turtle: Found, however... email me for more info

Music Man: Found, aquired 100 new thorn scratches, thanks.

Light Catcher: Found, ran into a gathering of people in the woods,
it looked like a wedding perhaps, or a fairy circle? People with
ribbons and flowers in their hair and bubbles and confetti and
sparkles... A very interesting experience with them looking at me
like, What are you doing here! They each carried a wand, or a
walking stick festooned with ribbons, and they looked at my plain
stick, used for poking in holes before removing boxes, like it had
forgotten to get dressed before going out today. It was quite a
surreal experience, but I promise I did not dream it. By the time I
got back to the parking lot they had all changed and gotten into
very normal vehicles and headed back to the real world. Had to be
very discreet recovering the box.
