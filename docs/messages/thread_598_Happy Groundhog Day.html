Happy Groundhog Day

From: Hawk (omkarmashanti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-03 11:58:35 UTC-08:00
I was able to spend my Groundhog Day doing some
letterboxing! My goal was to find Groundhog Day 2 on
Groundhog Day, and I met my goal!

I also checked the No Problem Houston HitchHiker
Hostel, and it had several HHs in it. The lid was not
sealed, and had shifted off the top a bit, so the box
was filled with water. It was a strong reminder that
we all need to be very sure to seal up boxes well
before replacing them. None of the HHs were wet (all
were sealed in baggies), and I was able to dry out the
box and the baggies fairly well.

I also found Brown Mouse and The Lost Rope Swing. Both
were in excellent condition.

Though it was a cold, wet, miserable day, I was glad
to get out and do some 'boxing.

Hawk
