Inspired by Tx2chic’s awesome Lonesome Dove letterbox & chatting with Viewfinder over coffee, we’re carving a Pioneer Cemetery box to make the trip to find Shawnee Trail at that park more worthwhile! I always believed the founder of Dallas, John Neeley Bryan was buried there, but it turns out there’s only a marker for him: his actual grave is an unmarked plot in Austin…now doesn’t letterboxing lead you on weird & wonderful trails?

 It is also going to be the area for our Pennsylvania buddy Raindear’s Pegasus box – I’ve been trying for weeks to find a spot in Dallas where you can actually stand on a sidewalk and see an unobstructed view of the giant red revolving winged horse so near & dear to Dallasites’ hearts - yesterday I suddenly looked up and saw the perfect view – so keep watching the LbNA list for clues.
