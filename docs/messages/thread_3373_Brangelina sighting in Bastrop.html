Greetings from Bastrop. We're staying at the state park until tomaking

happens to be going by and finds it, I would appreciate your letting me
know. It's a hickory stick with several state park medallians on it.
Today should be warmer, but breezy. We have a couple more boxes to
hide, going back to Austin today. We're in camping space 76 if you're
here tonight and will be moving over to South Shore tomorrow. See you
all soon. I didn't know the park has wi-fi, but the library's open til
9:00 tonight, closes at 6:00 on Friday.
