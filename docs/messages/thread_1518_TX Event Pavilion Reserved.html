From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-12-07 00:23:56 UTC
I was able to reserve Group Pavilion #3 in Rusk Unit of
Rusk/Palestine SP for Saturday, 03/18/06, so we are set.
I will set up a webpage with information and post it to
LBNA & AQ when I get a chance, but we probably need to
agree on a theme and name for the Event first. I was thinking
of calling it the TX Boxcar LB Event, with a train theme,
unless ya'll have other ideas. I guess if we get several
ideas we can vote on it, or maybe the Letterboxers in
NE region should decide.
Also, I reserved a campsite for Friday & Saturday, 03/17-
03/18/06 and I recommend you do the same before too long
if you plan to camp in the park.

Silver Eagle
