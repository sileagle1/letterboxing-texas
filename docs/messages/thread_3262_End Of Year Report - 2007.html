As the year comes to an end, it is time again for my annual end of
year report, which is just a fun comparison of growth trends in
letterboxing. As you can see, growth in TX and USA were comparable
to previous years, so letterboxing appears to be doing just fine.
Also, since this was our first year with individual counties on the
LBNA TX Clue Page, I included the number of newly populated TX
counties with letterboxes. Not only did we populate way more than I
expected, we even completely filled two regions (Northeast &
Southeast), so great job everyone. Here's hoping things stay great
in 2008!

2007
Newly populated TX Counties - 38
TX LB Talk Group - 303 members
USA LB Talk Group - 3542 members
TX Letterboxes on LBNA - 1816
Total Letterboxes on LBNA - 37,337
