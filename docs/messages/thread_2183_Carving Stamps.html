my apologies for being slow to get around to it, but i
would also like to say hi to the new boxers who have
joined the list recently, and also encourage you to be
bold and fearless when it comes to carving stamps.
just give it a try -- it is not nearly as hard as it
seems like it will be, when you read the tutorials,
nor as hard as it seems like it will be, when you are
looking at the stamps made by experienced carvers.
even i, who am admittedly not "arty", have managed to
make satisfactory progress in carving skill, in just a
few boxes, and now i just contentedly look foward to
continuing to make progress over time. it is not a
art contest with jurors, after all, it is supposed to
be fun! and it IS fun! don't miss out on the fun of
it, by waiting to be perfect on the first try. jump
on that learning curve and get started! : )

ashby
