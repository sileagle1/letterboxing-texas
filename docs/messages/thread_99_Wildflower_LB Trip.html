Wildflower/LB Trip

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-04-11 16:29:04 UTC
My family and I spent all day yesterday up in Washington
county looking at wildflowers and doing some letterboxing.
If you haven't seen the flowers yet this year you should
get out soon since they are the best in years! We followed
the Bluebonnet Trail, which you can see at:

http://www.brenhamtexas.com/Default.htm

At Washington-On-The-Brazos SP I checked on my Texas Flag
box and found Droop's WOTB LB alive and well. On the way
we stopped at Zube Park and found Baby Bear's Lady Justice
box doing fine and checked on my box there. We then went
to Telge Park and found Park Angel and Puppy Love. The Park
Angel needs a new logbook soon and Puppy Love clues need to be
changed to show Harris county instead of Houston county
because they currently show up in the Eastern Region of Texas.
And finally I tried to find Hershey's Kiss LB with no
success. It is a nice park so I hope the box gets replaced
if it is indeed missing.
It was a great day and we are so happy we beat the rain home.
Happy Easter!
