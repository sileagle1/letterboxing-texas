My dream box is one I can find! As we travel a lot we usually avoid
the boxes with really tricky clues. Just finding the park when you
are not a local can be a big challenge. Also the boxes that require a
lot of research are out of the question when we are on the road. Last
year we were in Seattle and gave up on finding most as it seemed to
the clues were geared to locals who know the area geographically and
historically. Thankfully the TX boxes are not so elusive!
Guitarzan loves to solve the tricky ones but it is all a matter of
time. A few of my boxes at home have difficult clues, written mainly
for local boxers just to make it more fun. But I also have a second
set of clues for visitors and invite them to contact me for the easy
clue if they wish. We get so few boxers up north that I'll even roll
out the red carpet if it will help! Norasta
