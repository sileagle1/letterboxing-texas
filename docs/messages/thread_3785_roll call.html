From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-09-19 21:43:48 UTC-05:00
We are here and safe, albeit with a heavy heart for all those who suffered and continue to suffer because of Ike.  Now I have been through a hurricane, I say give me a good old blizzard any old day.  That being said, I saw again during this storm why I would never want to live any place else on earth than right here in Houston.  The way people have come together to help each other is amazing.  My heart breaks when I see what has happened to Galveston and most especially Bolivar, which I consider to be the most peaceful spot on earth.  My neighbors have a woman who has spent her entire life on Bolivar staying with them right now, she has lost her home, her business, the ability to visit her parents' graves, everything.  I continue to feel guilty every time I flush the toilet or turn on the lights in my house.  The devastation this storm has left behind is unbelievable, and I think we are just beginning to see the effects.
 
A big thank you to Lucy Lockett for checking on us, it made me so happy to see your text message!  If I had any idea how to use my phone (or any battery left) I would have answered you right away.  I hope you are all safe and sound right now.  I am so thankful to be a part of such a kind group of people.  -MO5 (plus every kid in the neighborhood who can't play video games and has joined in the whiffle ball game in my front yard!)
