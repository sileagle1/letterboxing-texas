From: Jenifer Barbee (jaydawnlove@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-10-04 18:43:55 UTC-07:00
Yes! All of the above. You can hide a box before the event if you have time to go check out the park in advance. You can hide a box the day of and have hand written clues to let others copy from so they can find your box that day. Or you can just come to box already existing boxes and the new ones just placed!.
At an Event you get to meet other letterboxers like yourself and do an exchange and try and get peoples Personal Travelers. Cooties and Event boxes float around at the gathering. We eat and talk and catch up. Slowly people group up and go boxing. (You don't have to go with a group it just always seems to happen...)
At dark Silver Eagle and Baby Bear have a campfire and more boxes get passed around. Jokes are told and memories made.

From: babybearlb2002 (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-10-05 03:32:58 UTC
Silver Eagle and I have discussed, and decided that we will NOT approach the park about permits for letterboxes before the event. So, if you want to permit your box please feel free to do so, but also please do not mention the event in conjunction with this. A solution (and probably what I will do) if you are not going to permit is to place your box for the event, then remove it Sunday and place somewhere else. It is up to each of you to decide how you want to handle your own box. My guess is that once a few boxes are permitted, then park will check website and find out there are more and either remove or maybe contact you to permit.

More details on event will come at beginning of the year. Can't wait!!
