From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-06-11 20:41:13 UTC
We were at McKinney Falls SP the first week of April and it was
beautful, covered with bluebonnets. Because of recent rains, the
two falls were spectacular. But, because the creek was up, the
Homestead Trail was closed. You have to walk across the creek to
get there and the water was too swift. Nice park.
