From: Rebecca Leath (pixiegirl621@yahoo.com) | Date: 2017-01-25 16:10:47 UTC
Hey Y'all!!
I am relatively new to the letterboxing game. Collected some last April, then didn't get out looking for any more until recently, and now I am hooked!  I tend to search for boxes with a sidekick and we share a signature stamp with the trailname Texas Wanderers.  We are from Tyler, but with Family in places like Austin and Dallas, plus enjoyment of driving, have us trying to find letterboxes all over!  We discovered letterboxing when rakoonz invited us to tag along to last years TALE event.  It was a bit of a start in the deep end, but it was also tons of fun!! I found it was a very welcoming community of folks who were excited to share their love for this activity with a pair of complete clue-less newbies! As The Sidekick has special needs we tend to go for boxes that are accessible for someone with less mobility, but if I hear of a box that seems too good to pass up, it goes on the list to be found while he's in school :D 
Hope to met some of y'all while on the trail, either in Tyler, or further afield!

Texas Wanderers
