From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-08-21 09:01:08 UTC-05:00
The fish hatchery is a really nice park/museum-ish place.  The entry fee is for the museum, but it's worth the price and depending on the weather, might be a good opportunity to get in out of the sun for a bit.  They have some pretty cool fish, all taken out of Texas rivers and lakes.  They have a huge school of monstrous catfish that you can feed among other things.  They also have a fishing lake near the picnic area where you can fish to your heart's content for free, if you release, or you can pay a little and keep them.  We've spent a lot of time talking about respecting our environment over the years of this list, and this is one facility where you will walk away knowing quite a lot more about the ecology of Texas waterways.  All of that - AND we're having a gathering there so you'll have plenty of great company!
