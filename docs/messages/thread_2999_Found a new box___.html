Found a new box...

From: Vicki Stecher (stecher@runbox.com) | Date: 2007-08-09 22:58:49 UTC-05:00
 Well my sons and I went on a successful letterbox hunt. It was League of Their Own #3 in Richardson. We had a great deal of fun finding it. Unfortunately, when we found it, it was soaking wet. The box and baggies had leaked, but the little book was in good enough condition to hold together. We set it in the sun next to me on the sidewalk while the kids played. Then I realized I had a complete flat, so we left it out longer until triple A showed up.
 
Unfortunately I didn't have any extra baggies with me, so I just dried it best I could and put it back into place. I did notify the person who posted about it...is that someone on this list, by chance? I haven't heard back. So if there is another hard rain it will get wet again. There were actually several small holes in the box.
 
It was fun to get a good find after 3 missing ones...my kids were thrilled.
 
Vicki
