Just found this email which I found interesting:


"My son stumbled upon your logbook when we were geocaching for the first time on 3/11/15. Since we had not heard of letterboxing, we wrote a message and returned the box to it's location (last stamp was 2013). I apologize if we broke protocol, but it peaked our interest and we were just trying to participate."


The box is called Little Oscar -Texas State Governor Series and was placed in Lake Tawokani State Park on November 30, 2012.  It only has one previous find, on June 13,  2013 by Gryzzled Gryphon.  They apparently found me through AQ.  Of course, I told them they did the right thing and little about letterboxing and thanked them for letting me know.  I thought it was lone gone.
