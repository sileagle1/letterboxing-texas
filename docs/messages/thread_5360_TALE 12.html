I know it is early, but I have booked the site for TALE 12 and posted the event on AQ.  I have also created a web page with details.  It will be at the Group Dining Hall at McKinney Falls SP in Austin on 3/7/15, and the theme will be "Don't Mess With TX".  Here are links:

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?eventId=3182
http://sileagle.webs.com/tale12.htm

It is getting harder to find a park we have not been to that has an enclosed Group facility, and since there are several parks with every thing else required (e.g. trails, camping) I may have to go back to reserving Group Picnic Pavilions after next year.  The main reason we started using enclosed facilities was because the weather could be too cold, so I was thinking about moving TALE to later in March or early April in the future.  The advantages would be:  access to other parks like Inks Lake, Mother Neff, etc; and less expense so no need for raffle.  What do y'all think?

I will remind everyone of this closer to the event, but this park has in the past been unfriendly to letterboxes.  Their main issue was boxes hidden in historic structures.  SO, please do NOT hide any boxes in any historic structure, including the 1 foot "wall" (stacked rocks) that crosses a few of the trails.    We should be okay other than that, or you can hide boxes for event and remove at end.  


If we do move to later and pavilion, would open up much more locations (we have researched a lot of places, and limited by indoor space available.  With outdoor pavilion, we have many state parks, and plus many regional parks could use.  I would vote to maybe alternate years, one year in indoor facility, and next year in outdoor facility.
