As the members of this list invariably have been kind and patient with
me, I feel it incumbent upon me to issue the following warning: As I
was visiting a friend near Clifton, I was viciously assaulted by a bus-

possibly evil clone) of the maniac who had earlier tried seven times
to run me down. Fortunately, my friend and I managed to subdue the
rabble and they are now at my house, doing yard work and repairing my
roof. However, I am uncertain as to how much longer I can restrain
these miscreants and am therefore giving you this advance warning. At
such time as they make their escape, I will (if I can) so alert you.
Best regards, Gryzzled Gryphon of Coryell County
