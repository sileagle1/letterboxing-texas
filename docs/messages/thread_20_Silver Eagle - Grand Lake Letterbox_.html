Hi Silver Eagle!

I noticed that you have a box near Grand Lake Lodge - Colorado.
Since there is much snow in the high country right now, do you think
that it will be accessible at this time? Wanted to check before we

much needed snow for our area though!

You'd be surprised at what you can find in the snow - ha! We found
four letterboxes in Deer Creek Canyon Park on Saturday. We hiked 5
1/2 miles in ice, snow and mud. That's progress for this Texas girl!

We are expected to road trip to Canyon, TX this weekend. To visit
Eoghan's mom - Eoghan is from Canyon. YES, I see that you have the
Palo Duro Mouse letterbox in Palo Duro Canyon. Trust me, we WILL
find it! I love that place! It's so great to add letterboxing to
road trips!

Look forward to your post!

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-01-28 22:16:03 UTC
I know that Grand Lake Lodge is closed for the season now
but you can park outside and walk in. Not sure how much
snow builds up around there since they probably don't
remove any while it's closed. If you can get to the tree
that the box is hidden under you may be able to dig it out.
I know somebody found it in November but not much snow then.
Glad that you will be going after my box in Palo Duro Canyon.
That is a great park and you will enjoy it more than I did
last summer when the temperature was 110! I did manage to
find Eoghan's box further down the trail from mine and it
was still doing well, but with few visits. Looking forward
to hearing how mine is doing since you may be its first
visitors.

Silver Eagle
