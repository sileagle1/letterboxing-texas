From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-11-02 18:13:22 UTC
A message from Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter: As some of you know,
we letterbox together as a team, almost invariably. We also have
family members who letterbox, such as our sons Wills World and Fish
Tree and our granddaughter Princess Aurora. Our daughter, Flash
Dreamer, even has a couple of letterboxes in Italy, where she lives.
We have been working on a new series of letterboxes that we hope to
introduce in the next couple of weeks, but we have done the research,
the writing, the stamps and logbooks, etc., as a family group.
Therefore, we have decided that we need to plant these boxes as a
family. Although we will continue to find boxes and plant boxes under
our individual trail names, when we plant boxes as a group, we will be
known as "The Texians". After all, we all have direct ancestors who
lived in the Republic of Texas, so we thought that name was
appropriate. Look for our new letterbox series coming soon.
