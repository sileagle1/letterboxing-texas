From: tejasangel1976 (mhcyclone66@verizon.net) | Date: 2008-09-07 14:27:30 UTC
Well, its been a little busy around here. After getting the bus, I
did a little Texas touring myself. My kiddos started school. And
yes we waited patiently for the reason to start that new $700.00
dollar Generator in storage. I guess it will stay in its nice
cardboard box forever. (Lets hope.)

Anyway the other day I took the bus passengers over to a local
Mexican restaurant for Fajitas and Margaritas. We sat down and
ordered. I then excused myself to the restroom. When I return to
the table and the bus was gone. I asked my non speaking English
waiter what happen? And this is what he said.

"El autobs ha dejado. Ningn fonocaptor de medianoche. Hay unos
pocos jvenes en el autobs. Ellos necesitan para encontrar algunos
otros jvenes. El autobs est en su manera al de Madre de Cinco."

Well, I guess I should feel bad their gone, but the they did get to
visit a few of mi amigos while in Crosby. And I just love they got
the chance to come over at all.

So to the bus passengers all I can say is "Adis amigos, Van con
Dios".

Thanks Yall, its been fun - Tejas Angel

Ps. I hope my translation is right, I barely passed Spanish 2 in high
school. By the way I believe the word Tejas was a Indian word, but
now is used as a Spanish word.:)
