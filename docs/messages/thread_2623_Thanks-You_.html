A big thank-you to Silver Eagle, Baby Bear and Clueless Collector!!
The fire was nice and warm, the people friendly,and the food was
great!! Especially those Oatmeal Raisin Cookies by Puddle Splasher!
It was nice to meet new people and hear their stories of how they
started LB'ng. The Trivia game was enjoyed by all, and we went away
with more knowledge about this intersted hobby of ours.
I did learn some other interesting facts:

1. If you are 6'9" tall you CAN turn over in a canoe!
2. One of our fellow boxers has a "Groupie" following.
3. If you give two boys a ball they will find a lot of ways to
entertain themselves.

4. Always check for bees/wasp/jellowjackets when retrieving a box by
Baby Bear.

We also had a nice day at WAshington on the Brazos. It's not very
often you go boxing and get to hang out with others who are
searching for what you are. Ginder Cat and Skye Bird made the day
even better!
