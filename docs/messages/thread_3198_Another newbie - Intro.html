From: Christine Auger Velez (christineauger@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-11-24 07:45:43 UTC-08:00

Hi there everyone!  I'm Chrispy, mama to Nutcracker and Mono, and wife to Avion.  My little ones are still very little, so we are restricted to easily-accessible letterboxes for now at least.  I come from a family of avid geocachers, but wanted to do something fun that didn't require any expensive equipment.  I'm trying to convince them that they've got it all wrong, and that letterboxing is much more interesting.  :)

We moved to the Ft. Worth area about a year ago, from Brooklyn NY.  I'm loving how letterboxing is getting us to explore new places, and am looking forward to finding more.  Right now I'm in WA visiting my folks, and was lucky enough to find a hitch-hiker letterbox here that I'll bring back to TX for one of you to find...

Thanks for letting us join this community - we're looking forward to many many many more letterboxes!

Chrispy
