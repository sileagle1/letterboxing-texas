From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2012-01-11 14:13:34 UTC
I've found that the online reservation system doesn't always work. You can usually get a site by calling the reservation center in Austin. The phone number is on the website. I've also found that, if you are trailer camping and only need a 30 amp site, that's the only choices you are offered. The best sites are those that have both 30 and 50 amp electricity, so always ask for the 50 amp site when you are making reservations so that when you get to the park, all sites are available to you. Try making your reservation by phone, you might be surprised.

Boots Tex
