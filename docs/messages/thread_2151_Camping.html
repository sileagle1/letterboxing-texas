From: JOY (TeamTexUS@houston.rr.com) | Date: 2006-09-16 08:15:29 UTC-05:00
Jennifer, your cake sounds yummy! I definitely am going to try it!

Here's my recipe: Put a stick of real butter in a 9x13 pan, and stick it
in the oven while you are preheating to 350. While the butter's melting,
mix 2 cups self-rising flour with 2 cups sugar. Add about 1 1/2 cups
mile and a good shot of vanilla. Pour the batter over the melted butter,
pushing the batter into the corners of the pan, but do not stir! Top
with fresh or frozen peach slices. Bake until toothpick almost comes
clean. Serve with whipped cream.

Of course you can use different fruit and flavor the batter with almond
or cinnamon, etc.

JOY
