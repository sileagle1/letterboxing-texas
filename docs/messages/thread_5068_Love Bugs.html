Love Bugs

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2012-09-07 20:58:48 UTC
East Texas is in the middle of the worst infestation of Love Bugs in years. They are harmless, but a huge nuisance. I was wondering how wide-spread they are in Texas. Has anyone else had a problem with them? They like to congregate in huge numbers over highways (the heat attracts them) and they can seriously mess up your windshield, clog up your radiators and pit the finish on your car if you don't wash them off within a few hours. They also swarm around the house and make their way inside. They die pretty quickly, but are immediately replaced with an even greater number.

Boots Tex
