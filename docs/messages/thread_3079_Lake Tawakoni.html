Hey All!,
I'm still here! I just haven't posted on this site for awhile. If
y'all haven't been out to Lake Tawakoni lately, you should catch the
massive spider web out there. I took the little Samurai Stampers with
me and it was pretty amazing! It was pretty windy that day, so I think
it's even more impressive when the wind hasn't knocked parts of it
down. From what I hear, though, the little arachnids repair it
everynight. Powerpuff Girl and I planted two boxes out there on
adjacent trails to the webs.

Also, Welcome, Welcome, Welcome! to all the new TX boxers! You have
found a great group of people! I have been boxing for 3 years now, and
still love it! I am in the D/FW area. Happy Boxing!
-Kantexan
