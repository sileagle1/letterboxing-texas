Lost letterbox replaced with new one

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2012-07-06 01:57:31 UTC
My box at the Walker County Rest Stop called Fantastic in Plastic went missing and today, I replaced it. Since the huge double-trunk pine tree that it was hiding behind was cut to the ground, I had to move the box slightly. Since I also carved a new stamp with a different image, I retired the original box. The replacement is called Fantastic in Plastic 2 and is to be considered a new box. I took the opportunity to place two other new boxes in Walker County. I'll post them soon.

Boots Tex
