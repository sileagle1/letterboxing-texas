October event

From: (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2016-05-07 11:17:18 UTC-05:00

I’ve finally scheduled the event in October.  For those who haven’t seen the event listing yet, you can find it here:

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=3672

 

I took the liberty of naming it TAFLE…Texas Annual FALL Letterboxing Event.  After 5 years it’s annual enough to deserve a name J  This will be the 6th year and we will be at Fairfield Lake State Park which is between Dallas and Houston, about 50 miles south of Corsicana.  I’ve never visited the park, but I have good recommendations from past visitors, and it has a dining hall!  I also wanted something a little closer to Dallas for all of our new boxers in that area.  Dates are October 21 and 22 (Friday and Saturday), and we have the dining hall both days. 

 

More details will be available closer to the date, but expect a pot luck meal on Saturday and some creative fun.  If you want to plan anything specific (meals, planting a special series, etc.) just let me know.  I’ll plan a trip to visit the park at some point before the event, so let me know if you have questions or things I can check out.
