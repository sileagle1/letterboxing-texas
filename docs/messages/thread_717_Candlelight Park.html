From: Hawk (omkarmashanti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-17 16:02:59 UTC-08:00
My very first box, Candlelight Park, has been
temporarily pulled. The box is fine, it's just that
the tree that it was hidden in now has a great big
orange "X" on it -- marking it for removal by the
City. I'm glad I was able to nab the box before the
tree came down.

We had a tornado go through this area on December 24,
2002, and it took the tops off of many trees. The tree
I hid the box in was one of these trees. A lady in the
park said that 56 trees were to be removed due to the
tornado damage. :(

I will be replacing this box in the same park, or
nearby, as soon as I find a suitable place and the
City of Houston is finished with all the removals.

Hawk
