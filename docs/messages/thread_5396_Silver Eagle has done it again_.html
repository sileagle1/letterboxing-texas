Silver Eagle has done it again!

From: Lou Catozzi (lou.catozzi@att.net) | Date: 2014-10-11 17:28:45 UTC-05:00
Silver Eagle has found another extensive trail system close to Austin,
this time basically in Austin! I was out on these new-to-me trails this
afternoon after a night and morning of rain and had a great time
tracking down five of his six new boxes in the area before running out
of time for the day.

I had no idea that Sunset Valley had this excellent trail system though
a series of connected greenbelts and nature preserves. It is hard to get
out of the hearing of traffic noise within the Austin metro but there
were places I visited today that seemed wild and peaceful. The various
signs warning of coyotes being active in the area helped with that feeling.

The trails were wet and very muddy in some places and a few of the
stream crossings were adventuresome but those problems should fade
quickly with a few days of dry weather. Thnx SE!

PI Joe

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2014-10-12 08:20:57 UTC-07:00

Glad you enjoyed the trails!  When I went everything was dry, but I wondered how it would be after rain.  I also didn't have any maps of the trails so was just "winging" it and exploring while I looked for places to hide boxes.  At the one area where I placed three boxes in three different Greenbelts linked together, I didn't want to backtrack to get back to my car so just headed north where I thought it would be.  The trail ended in a subdivision, so I walked north on various streets until I came to another entrance to the Greenbelt which eventually led me back to the beginning.  Therefore, the round trip distance to some of my boxes might be shortened if you can figure out how to do it.  I look forward to exploring further after y'all place more boxes there, especially with the weather getting cooler.  This is my favorite time of year.

I must say the signs warning about aggressive coyotes gave me pause, but I never saw any.  If you go with kids, just make sure you stay together just in case.

Silver Eagle
