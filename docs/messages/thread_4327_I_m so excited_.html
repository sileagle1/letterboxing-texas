From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-12-25 12:32:03 UTC-06:00

I didn't get anything specifically letterboxing-related, but I did get a very nice low profile blue-tooth speaker to go on my workbench in the garage where I go to carve and so forth. I can pair it with my phone and play music from it while I work. It's so much better than my previous setup, where I took some old computer speakers and had to position them for power and the connecting wire, meaning they were IN MY WAY! This is like going from a big console television to going to a flat panel.

As some of you know, we've had some pretty big things we've been contending with over the past year or so, the biggest being an elusive health problem that has been plaguing Fearless Fox. After numerous doctor's appointments, countless tests, a few procedures, referrals from one doc to another and one clinic to another, medications galore, many hypotheses, much research on the doctors' part and mine (every time they mentioned a specific possibility, I read about it until I knew as much as I could possibly know), and no real diagnosis, whatever has been going on seems to have resolved itself and he is his old self again. I suppose we could worry that "it" will come back, but they very effectively ruled out the things that are really scary and were focusing on long-term chronic type stuff when the symptoms quit. If it comes back, so be it, but today the best gift I received was to celebrate this Christmas with him making it through the holidays happy and healthy and at full power (for the last few Christmases, the stress and schedule of the holidays has resulted in him being home in bed on this day).

Merry Christmas!

Ordinarily, I wouldn't classify myself as a sentimental, sappy type person - although I do admire that quality in others, I seem to be lacking in that area.
