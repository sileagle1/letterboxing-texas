New member to the group- My introduction

From: remymom (remymom@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-29 14:23:41 UTC
Hey everyone!

I just joined the group last weekend, and thought I'd better step in
and say a quick hello! I'm also new to letterboxing... just found my
first box last week! It was one of Silver Eagle's many... and I hope
to find the time to hunt down many more.

Not much input to give on the discussion of TX regions... I live in
the panhandle and this area is pretty straight-forward. I do hunt for
boxes by city and by region... never did it by county name because I
might know my way well around TX, but not the counties. I think it
all matters the most to those who live in the edges where you might
be shifted from one region to another, so I think those should be
heard the most.

I'm a divorced mom to 2 boys, who are both still a bit young to enjoy
the long hikes, but I'm always looking for the shorter, easier
ones... especially for the summer, when the heat will make the longer
hikes even harder to do.

If I come up with any newbie questions, I will be sure to ask, but so
far all the online sites have explained things well. And as for
finding boxes... I think just plain experience will make things go
easier for me. I have had several hunts that have resulted in DNF...
but that just makes me more determined to go back and look again!

Happy hunting and hiking!

Bunnybee
