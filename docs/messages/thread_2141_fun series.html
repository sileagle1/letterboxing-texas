From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2006-09-06 11:12:39 UTC-05:00

Stargazer announced a new series this morning which will only be available temporarily. One of which was hidden in the same cemetery where I have my Tombs and Tees box so I had to detour by there on my way to workout this morning! It's been awhile since I've beat GG to a box, so that was additional incentive! So... as always.... great stamp by Stargazer! After locating the box, I carried it down to a little patio table to stamp in. Just as I was taking it back to it's hiding place, the "manager" of the cemetery was showing an extremely older gentleman some "available locations" and they went right up to the spot where it was hidden and stood there talking. I passed by them, carrying the camo box, and we nodded and spoke. I was hoping if they noticed what I was carrying, they would just think it was my camouflaged family Bible!  I took the box back to my Jeep and waited.... and waited.... and waited....   I thought I might have to take the box to the gym to exercise with me and then bring it back after, but I figured GG was probably in hot pursuit, so I had to use up some of my reserve patience (of which I have little) and continue to wait until the two gentlemen finally started to walk on down the path to the back of the cemetery. Finally I was able to slip back over there and rehid the box. I can't wait to go find the other two boxes in this fun series!
Puddle Splasher

I can just imagine you sitting there waiting for them to move on.  I'm not high on patience either!
 
How do you account for sitting in a parked car in a cemetery?  For quite a long time, the only way I could think of is to break into tears and blubber about "oh how I miss ..."  Unfortunately, while SJZ can break into very convincing tears on command, I can't!

I'm betting you weren't dressed in your camo workout gear, or you could've more easily passed off the box as your camo'd family Bible.
