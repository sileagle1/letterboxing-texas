From: DJ Hoppe (markd4good@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-06 22:56:17 UTC-07:00
I'm not sure why it would disappear either. We ride
our bikes to that park a lot, it is a busy park on
weekends, there are various day camps held there
throughout the year, but there is a bit of a
snake/alligator/bee problem (it borders armand bayou
nature preserve) and so parents don't encourage kids
to go poking around in the brush. So I think
accidental muggle discovery might be pretty rare. That
park does flood quite badly IF tide is in AND the
bayous are full of rainwater. I'm not sure of the
exact spot where that box is placed, but if its on the
bayou side, and its near the water, there's a good
chance it could get washed away, rare earth magnets or
not. I don't mind replanting and recarving if I had
some idea of the stamp you'd want placed, and given
the plant spot is on high ground. LMK.
