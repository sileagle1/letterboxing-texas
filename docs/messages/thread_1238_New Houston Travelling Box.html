New Houston Travelling Box

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-08-26 20:54:47 UTC-05:00
Ok you all, I'm getting ready to introduce a new travelling box (sort of like the tour bus) and I wanted to tell you a little about it.
 
You know the saying, "Nice place to visit, but I wouldn't want to live there", well, I read someplace (I can't remember where) that Houston is the opposite of that.  In order to really appreciate Houston's charms you have to be a long time resident (first of all, it takes a long time to drive to them!) and also because Houston is the sort of place that grows on you.  I love Houston, I feel like it is the home that I've been looking for my whole life.  In honor of this I have created a box called "Houston's Hidden Treasures".  The "rules" are this:  when you find it, grab it.  Take it home, decide where you are going to plant it next, add your stamp, and let us all know in the logbook why you chose to include your particular spot.  It doesn't matter if it is a park, a bar, a library, it just has to be in Houston (or less than about a half hour drive out of the city limits.)  Then post your clues on this list, and also e mail them to me off list.  I'm going to add it to the main LBNA sight as well, so that Houston boxers who don't participate on this talk list have a chance to look as well.
 
I hope this makes sense, thanks to Dewberry for answering all my questions, and sorry to all of you out there not lucky enough to live in this crazy town!  Clues to its first stop should be out this weekend.  -Mother of Five
