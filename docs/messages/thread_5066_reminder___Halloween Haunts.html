reminder...Halloween Haunts

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2012-09-02 14:42:22 UTC-05:00

Just a reminder for those of you who are undecided or have forgotten, the Halloween Haunts event is shaping up nicely and getting close.  Here is a sneak peak…

 

Clues for the haunted boxing challenge will be available Friday afternoon. 

For fun we are asking the boxers to make up a Halloween themed name and carve (or buy) a stamp representing you name.  You can also box as a team and work together on the clues.

Dinner Friday night

We will have over 30 boxes for you to find – including many with sneaky and creative clues and other surprises!  (plus whatever you bring!)

Saturday night will include a special Trick or Treat boxing series – kids can wear costumes!

 

So if any of this sounds like fun, please come join us in Kerrville next month!

 

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2399

 

 

I’m off to work on more sneaky goodness…my desk is covered in stamps, I just used up a full bottle of orange glitter spray paint, and I need to figure out how to make a coffin shaped log book…hmmmmm.
