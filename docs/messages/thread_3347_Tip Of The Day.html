Since Topic Of The Week has been a good way to stimulate discussion,
I thought it might be useful to also have a tip of the day. It could
be any tip pertaining to letterboxing that you feel others might
benefit from, especially newcomers. We may get more than one per day
at first, but try to space them out so we have time to digest and
discuss each. I'll start us off.

Many of you may not be aware of the features available in this
Talklist shown on the left side, so I want to point them out. First
of all you must log into the Talklist to see all of them, and some of
you that get posts via email might not ever do that. By the way, if
you don't want to receive posts via email, which is the default, you
can pick another option by clicking Edit Membership. So, looking on
the left I will point out some of the features:

Files - contains documents and other files submitted by members for
everyone to view.

Photos - albums with pictures submitted by members for everyone to
view

Links - contains links to websites submitted by members for everyone
to click on

Database - databases submitted by members for everyone to view and/or
update (like potluck info for TALE5)

Polls - Polls created by members to obtain information

Calendar - not used much, but could contain future notices

So try them out and see for yourself, you just might have fun while
learning a few things!

Silver Eagle
