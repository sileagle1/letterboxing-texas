I planted one here in Cherokee County, where quite a bit of debris fell, while the next shuttle was flying - called Flying Again.  The box is on the edge of what will be Lake Columbia.  When the lake is eventually filled, the box will likely be underwater, but I specifically placed it there as a remembrance of the Columbia crew and the box itself is a tribute to the next shuttle crew that flew.  When they begin filling the lake, I'll remove the box and replant it when the lake is finished.
 
I have one ready to plant when the currently planned mission flies as well.  It will go in another location in this area that was hard hit by debris.  I'll keep doing mission boxes as long as I feel like it.
 
Here's the link to my box:
