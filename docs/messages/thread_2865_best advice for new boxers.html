I have been thinking a lot about this question, trying to decide what my best advice would be.  Since we only get one *cough*dewberry*cough* I think I would say......(drumroll, please).....
Participate on the list.  Ask questions.  Take the opportunities offered through the list and gatherings to get to know other letterboxers.  I have made some wonderful friendships that started out with the commonality of letterboxing, and have now grown and become really rewarding friendships to me.  It is funny how you discover how many other things you might have in common with other letterboxers, and how much they have to share.  Now, back to my never ending pile of laundry.  MO5
