Divergent Series Box #2 Replaced/Relocated

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2014-05-28 12:34:21 UTC-07:00

After hearing that box #2 (Amity) of this series was already missing, I went out today and replaced it in a new but nearby location.  I have updated the clues so get them before you go.

With all the rain it was an interesting adventure trying to get to the box location.  The clues call for you to walk through a tunnel, but a creek runs through it and it was flooded today, making the tunnel a swimming proposition.  Instead I walked across Memorial road, through a muddy field and down where the box used to be.  You could do the same, but I recommend waiting until the waters recede.  Hopefully the new box will last longer in its new location.
