Bum-MER! First time I looked for Kantexan's Lake Chase series, I found
#1 and #3, but #2 was missing. First time I found Kantexan's mystery
box A Sunny Day, it had been vandalized. Since both had since been
replaced, today my Refinding and Stamping day. Found Lake Chase #1,
restamped, went on to find #2 -- not there, and not only am I sure I'd
followed those clues exactly, my husband (who always finds things I
can't find) searched with me and no luck. So we had to abandon that
series yet again. HOWEVER, we did find A Sunny Day in its new
location. This one is a sort of hard to find box even after you figure
out the mystery location, but the clues ARE exactly right. Next time,
though, I'll pay better attention to the warning about thorns and dress
appropriately. Open-toe sandals are NOT good footwear for letterboxing
if thorns are involved! Thanks, Kantexan, for rehiding it. AND, I'm
not giving up on the Lake Chase series. I really want to do that
complete lake picture!
