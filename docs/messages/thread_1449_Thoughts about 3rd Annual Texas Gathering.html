*****My thoughts exactly. It doesn't make sense for
Silver Eagle to foot the bill AND do the work - at the
past two gatherings, he's set out a cup for donations
and I think he's broken even each time, but everyone
needs to come prepared to kick in whatever they feel
they can afford or that they think is appropriate.

Silver Eagle is far too gracious to bring this up and
I'll bet he would not ever tell us what it really
costs him, but I think this is a good opportunity to
remind everyone that there are certain expenses that
go into putting a gathering together, and if we all
chip in a little, no one has to pay a lot.

Also, if he knows he can count on our donations, he
doesn't have to worry as much about keeping the
facility cost down. When you consider that we need a
facility that will accomodate a minimum of 50 (and
this is a bare minimum in my opinion - we had 50 at
the last gathering and we've grown by about 25% in the
past year) AND we really need electricity, AND it's
really best to have a covered area, we have a lot of
requirements withouth throwing on the cost ceiling as
well.

Now, that said, please understand that I in no way
support a per person charge because there may well be
some folks that will be straining their budgets just
to be there, much less to donate, and we certainly
wouldn't want them to exclude themselves because of an
expected donation. But if we each give what we can,
all will be well!
