Heh heh heh - go for it!
 
Being a ham operator, I was interested in SOTA - a European radio contest called Summits On The Air.  It isn't just any old Summit - they must be registered and qualified.  No one had done any work in the call-zone I am in yet.  So I teamed up with a guy in New Mexico and we decided to volunteer to start.
