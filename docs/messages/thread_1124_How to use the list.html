1. The website for the talk list is
groups.yahoo.com/group/letterboxingtexas
There you would find files, links, polls, pictures
maybe, and you can post directly to the list from
there. There is also a chat room there that we've
used at least once, and maybe we'll start a regular
chat one of these days.

2. To send an email to the group, the address is:
letterboxingtexas@yahoogroups.com

3. If you are having a problem with your email from
the list, you would email
letterboxingtexas-owner@yahoogroups.com
and that will reach both me and Silver Eagle, who is
also a moderator here (but he, lucky bird that he is,
has spent most of his summer away from his computer,
so you're stuck with me!)

As far as initiating an email to an individual, if you
reply to a message that came through the group, it
will go back to the group because that is how the
group is set. If you want it to go to an individual,
make sure to take the group address and put their
address in. If you can't figure out what their email
address is, post to the list asking them to email you.
