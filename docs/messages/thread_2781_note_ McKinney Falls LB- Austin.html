Just to let you know, "Chimney and Cornerstones" and "On onion creek"
are in each other's places. I mean clues for Onion creek lead you to
Chimney and cornerstones box and clues for Chimmney and cornerstone
lead you to onion creek box. I don't know if the clues were just
inputted wrong (they have different placers) or if someone accidentally
picked them up and replaced them wrong. Both were found, where the
other was supposed to be. Clues are clear and both boxes were well
hidden, but they are just switched. (Does this make sense?) I left as
found but if someone needs me to go and fix this, I can, I am close.
Thanks,

Thanks for letting us know. I checked the clues on LbNA and
