Happy Birthday, Texas PLB

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-07-15 11:18:43 UTC-05:00
We have so many new boxers on the list; I thought I'd just explain why I
keep wishing folks a happy birthday!!!

CelticLions created a "Happy Birthday, Texas!" Postal Letterbox, which has
been traveling around the state, with two quick trips OUT of state for one
displaced Texan and one honorary Texan.
This is a letterbox you don't have to find - it finds YOU!
If you would like to receive this box, please email me offlist at:
celticlions@comcast.net

Please include your birthdate (no need to add the year!) & mailing address.
If you sign up for this box, you MUST agree to the following:

1. To try your best to mail it to the next recipient on time for his/her
birthday (Please email me before sending it on - sometimes we have new
additions)

2. To send it with a Delivery Confirmation Tracking number & send that
number to me (NO EXCEPTIONS!!! This costs only an additional 50 cents and
helps me track it - it is now too precious to lose, since we have so many
cool stamp signatures in the logbook!)

3. To allow $6.00 or so in your budget to mail it! I'm sorry that it's not
lightweight, but I like to warn everyone ahead of time.

Once in a while, the box returns to us for maintenance & I've thoroughly
enjoyed seeing everybody's stamps & comments! It's especially fun to see the
signatures of folks who live in other far corners of this state.

Cheers!
Ruby T.
