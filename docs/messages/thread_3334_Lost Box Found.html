Lost Box Found

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-02-10 18:46:31 UTC
I got a report yesterday from Pathfinder, who found my box in
Georgetown called "To Hell and Back". This box hadn't been found in
over a year and has been attempted 3 times. I tagged it as "missing"
last March, I believe. She was looking for LSQ's box, "Square Dance"
and found mine in the same general area. Since she wasn't looking for
it, and didn't have the clues, she put it back where she found it.
When we're down that way for the TALE event, we plan to go by and try
to find it and rehide it. The clues will be updated at that time.
Thanks to Pathfinder, and lost box has been found.

Boots Tex
