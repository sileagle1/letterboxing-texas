The gathering in March is on March 3, at Lake Somerville.  Lake Somerville is a stone's throw from Washington-on-the-Brazos. 
 
WELL-
 
On March 2, 1836, the Texas Declaration of Independence was signed.  So we'll be in the area at the time of an important date in Texas history.
 
Does this give anyone ideas for gathering boxes???

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-01-27 13:07:34 UTC
Every year Washington-On-The-Brazos park has an Independence Day
Festival on the weekend closest to March 2. The festivities include
historical reenactments and craft demonstrations and is a lot of
fun. I highly recommend going before or after the Event if you have
never experienced it. There are a few letterboxes in the park, too.
The website is already on the Event webpage under "Other Nearby
Activities".

Silver Eagle
