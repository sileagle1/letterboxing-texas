Introduction - Open Space

From: borgfeldc (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2010-08-16 17:38:39 UTC
Hello, I'm Christine (Open Space). I'm from Alvin which is south of Houston, and I started letterboxing and geocaching with my husband and son about a month ago. Personally I prefer letterboxing, but the boys like the geocaching - especially the larger containers.

With the "lovely" summer weather and abundance of mosquitoes here lately we spent a couple of weekends in the Round Rock / Cedar Park area for our hunting. It's still hot, but avoiding the swarms of mosquitoes we had in July made it well worth the drive.

So we have gotten most of the letterboxes in Round Rock and Cedar Park now, ventured into Georgetown and had one trip to Austin. We have also picked up a few local boxes (Alvin, Webster, Pearland, Galveston). We also placed our first box in Round Rock this weekend.

I'm new to carving stamps, but I've spent many hours with photoshop - scrapbooking and designing brushes, so I love getting to keep images of all the stamps I find. (By the way, if anyone ever wants a digital image or brush made from a stamp just let me know.)

So, Hi to everyone and I hope to meet some of you in person one day.
