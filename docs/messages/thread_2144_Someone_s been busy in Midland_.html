Someone's been busy in Midland!

From: considerablewellness (sbilbrey@houston.rr.com) | Date: 2006-09-09 01:44:06 UTC
Ok, so I have been in Midland for a week now, and before I left I
printed clues to the ONE box in Midland and TWO boxes in Odessa along
with several boxes from nearby areas in case I got bored.. Well,
today was my first opportunity to get out and search because it has
been raining since I got here. So, we went out hunting... Found 1 of
3, but the meteor was closed, so we plan to try for that one again
tomorrow. So I get on the computer to log my find and I pull up this
area in Texas *in our new easy to understand divisions* and what do I
find????? Lone Star Quilter and Boots Tex have canvassed this
area!!! I am like, what is going on? I printed all the clues before
I left... Yep, that was the 30th... They went ABSOLUTELY CRAZY on
the 31st!!

So, I bet you can guess what I am getting up early to do tomorrow... :)

Semalee of MAGS
