From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2004-10-16 20:21:32 UTC-05:00
Another nice day in Houston (Go Astros!) although we had some ups and
downs in letterboxing. Confirmed that TWO boxes are missing: 'Tribute
to England #1' and 'Cat in the Box'. Will try to replace 'Tribute #1'
to same general location. But 'Cat in the Box' was not in a good place
so will re-do and re-place this box in a completely new area.

On the plus side, we launched a new 'series' today. Its called 'Trivial
Box Pursuit'. If you remember the Trivial Pursuit game, there are six
categories you solve to get a piece of pie to win the game. We are
placing six boxes in Memorial Park for each of the categories. Will try
to get the clues into LBNA tonight, tomorrow at the latest. Keep your
eye out for them.

Hope you enjoy!

teamking
