North TX Letterboxing event

From: (pamphotographer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2014-05-27 06:34:20 UTC-07:00

Hi friends!
We are hosting a letterboxing "class" at our local library to introduce the community to letterboxing on June 19 at 10:30 a.m.  We are also going to make an event out of it!  The Library is west of Fort Worth in Parker County. I am hoping that some experienced boxers can make it out to talk to the people that show up.  Plus, we'd like to meet other boxers, get new ideas and see what others are doing. 

The class is on a Thursday morning, though, and the idea was to have the event after the class so the attendees could meet and hear stories from other letterboxers. 
Would anyone be able to make it? 

I was also thinking that maybe extending the event to later that same day would get more people out who might have to work.  So having two event times: one for the class at 11 a.m., and one maybe around dinner time?  The library is open until 7 on Thursdays and since there will be boxes hidden in the library that day, we would need to be able to get in! Ha.

Just wanted to get a feel for the interest.
I have created an event page for this on Atlas Quest:  "At the Library"

Thanks!
