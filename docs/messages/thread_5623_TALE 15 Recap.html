TALE 15 Recap

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2018-03-20 12:47:50 UTC

It was great seeing everyone again and I was pleased to meet several new letterboxers.  I just hope the interference of an over-zealous park ranger didn't quash your letterboxing spirit.  Thanks to everyone who placed boxes, at their own considerable risk as it turned out, and I'm glad I was able to find most of them since I will not be returning to that park.  I'm also sorry that Wry Me went to the trouble of carving & placing night boxes that never got found.  This marks the first time I ever found boxes (while helping pick them up) but did not stamp in.  Thanks also to Mosaic Butterfly for organizing the delicious Pre-TALE dinner at Southside, and to Baby Bear for being my "partner in crime" and possible future cell mate.

I have posted some pictures on AQ and the Photos section of this Group, so feel free to add to them:

https://groups.yahoo.com/neo/groups/LETTERBOXINGTEXAS/photos/albums/1720457882

Hope to see y'all at L&B XX in October.

Silver Eagle

From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2018-03-20 13:05:08 UTC-05:00
Thank you, Silver Eagle and Baby Bear, for a fun, fabulous event!  
I did talk with the office staff at Brazos State Park on Sunday.  I learned that the Park Police Officer was called because some campers had complained to the office that people kept coming to their campsite and looking for something.  I apologized profusely, telling them that our hobby is about stealth, and not about annoying or worrying other campers.  Perhaps we can peacefully co-exist in Texas State Parks, as long as no one plants a box at their campsite in the future.  (I understand that it is a fun thing to do in other states).
The staff gave me the phone number of the person in charge of the park, to talk to about permitting a box, and I will share what I learn from that person.  I will also apologize to her for us annoying other campers until they had to call to complain.
-- Bell Motel
