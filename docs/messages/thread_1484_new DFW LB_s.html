as promised, and due to the intense pressure put on me by barefoot
lucy, i have finally planted my LB's! lucy, i could just feel you
watching over me as i went through the rest of the planting process.
maybe that's why i kept mumbling over and over to myself, 'barefoot
lucy commands, and i obey.' it's alright though, my doctor says the
twitching will go away with time. ;)

they're all on atlasquest, and i botched the posting job by only
putting two on letterboxing.org (which i'll be trying to fix here in a
minute).
total of 6 (i had to nix one). one is downtown denton, the other five
are ~10 miles due east of denton on highway 380. these are my first
babies, so bear with me. i would, however, love to get feedback, and
i do have thick enough skin to take critiques/criticism.

hope y'all enjoy

p ?uest
