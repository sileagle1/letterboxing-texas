On Wed, Dec 30, 2009 at 6:19 PM, LouC <lou.catozzi@att.net> wrote:

I am pleased to announce the start of Texas Treasure Hiking. This program, which started in North Carolina, has interested letterboxers keeping track of the mileage they hike while letterboxing (finding and planting) and earning points for miles hiked. You can get more information at:

http://www.treasurehikersusa.com/

http://www.atlasquest.com/about/wiki/browse.html?gCatId=14#q362

http://www.pathtags.com/

When any letterboxer has earned 25 points in Texas they will be eligible to purchase a specially designed commemorative PathTag for $3. The program is designed to be non-profit and revenue neutral. The $3 cost of the PathTag covers production, shipping, and handling.

There is a convenient Widget available on the AQ website to help you keep track of your miles.

If you have any questions please contact me at AQ or LbNA.

PI Joe
