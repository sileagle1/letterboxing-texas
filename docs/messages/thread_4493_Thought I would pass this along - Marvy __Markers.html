The nice thing about the Marvy website is that you can buy individual colors for $1.89 each. I go through red and blue really quickly, so buying them in onesies is really nice! Also, Marvy has color selections that are beyond what I've ever seen in either Hobby Lobby or Michael's - they have eight different 12-marker sets. There is some duplication between sets, but with 108 individual colors, there isn't much overlap!

The twelve marker sets are all $22.78, which makes the everyday price of $16.99 at Hobby Lobby look real good, and makes that 30% off this week even better! Still though, if you want any specialty sets, or individual markers, check out the Marvy site and use the discount available this month.

I don't think my link earlier went through correctly, but here's the jump directly to their brush markers:

I've never compared the prices of sets until Wagtime posted about her buy at Hobby Lobby and I started looking at the Marvy website. I'm very surprised they are that much more online than at the big box stores. I've really only ever bought my markers by the set - and waited for one of Hobby Lobby's good coupons.

I still think I could buy 4 red/4 blue for roughly the same price as a 12-set at HL and come out ahead. My problem would be that I would buy them and put them away, and then forget where I put them when it came time to pull out a new pair! I've done this with so many different things that I've now started a list of misplaced bargains, but I keep forgetting where the list is too, so I never know if I found something I was looking for, or just found something I hadn't looked for - yet.
