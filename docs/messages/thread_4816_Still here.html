Hey everyone,

I know you haven't heard from me in... oh, foreve... but things have been busy in our household. Figured it was time for an update. :)

I also accepted a new position at my company, so I've been busy learning the new job. Glad to say, it's going very well. Haven't had any time for letterboxing either, but don't count me out! I will be back on the trail soon!

Hope all the teams have a great time at the Tomato Festival! Sorry I didn't make it this year. Hopefully I'll see you all at the next gathering.

Hugs to all,
SandiBox
