Even though it is over 5 months away, I thought I would let you know that the 7th TX Annual LB Event (TALE) will be March 6, 2010, at Palmetto State Park. The theme this year will be Tex-Mex and you can find more details on my new web site:

http://sileagle.webs.com/tale7.htm

For those that don't know, Baby Bear and I host a Letterbox Event every year on the first Saturday in March, and every year it has been in a different place. It is getting harder to find places that can handle our growing numbers, but this one is plenty big, so please plan on coming. It will be potluck, so specify what you are bringing when you sign up on AQ:

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1446

We can discuss details when it gets closer, but put it on your calendars now.

Silver Eagle
