an Introduction

From: Meg Garrett (meg360@centurytel.net) | Date: 2005-05-23 16:48:59 UTC-05:00
Hello,
 
My name is Meg Garrett and I live in San Marcos, TX.
I letterbox under the name: Sunnyside Up and the image
of a wide, smiling sun, though my yahoo ID is: mr2lrn.
 
I came to letterboxing through orienteering.
I have been involved in sport orienteering since 1974
when I first learned about it in college. Years later,
in 1998, an orienteering friend showed me the
(now famous) letterboxing article in Smithsonian
maganzine. I thought it was interesting, but didn't
actually try letterboxing myself until 2002.
 
I grew up in middle Tennessee and moved to San Marcos
in 1999 to attend graduate school at Texas State.
I finished the Masters degree in 2003 (Geography), and now
I do a variety of things to make ends meet.
For example, I teach an online Geography
course for a state university in Alabama. I teach Geography
for a homeschool cooperative in Austin. I teach land navigation
for Texas Parks and Wildlife. I conduct workshops for classroom
teachers and orienteering events for private groups. From time to
time I even do corporate team-building events using navigation
and problem-solving. I even do treasure hunt birthday parties.
 
I consider myself fortunate to live in relative proximity to
SilverEagle, Baby Bear, and the many others who so generously
place box after box after box. Recently, I had the chance to
meet and lbox with Corazon who lived in a neighboring town.
It was so great to meet face to face. Corazon has been much
more dedicated and consistent in her lboxing than I, and I
learned so much of value about her approach to the hobby.
 
Family emergencies (sickness, death, etc.) have taken me out
of state during the last two gatherings, but I feel that I will
join you folks in the flesh eventually. Until then, I'll see you
on the net.
 
Meg/Sunnyside Up
