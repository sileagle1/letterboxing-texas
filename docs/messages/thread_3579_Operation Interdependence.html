Not exactly on the letterboxing topic, but something we have discussed
(thanks Mike) in the past. Anyone within the 550 KTSA (AM on the
radio dial) listening area might have heard about the Operation
Interdependence. Between Memorial Day and July 4, KTSA, along with
several local companies and organizations, will be taking collections
to send to our troops. If you are interested, visit the website (I
think KTSA.com) and see the list of items that are being collected. I
am going to be asking other people I know to join with our family in
donating so the impact will be bigger. Just wanted to let everyone
know in honor of the holiday. I guess you could say we could plant
boxes in honor of our military and SEND boxes in their honor as
well!! (there, I did get letterboxing in here afterall!! lol!!)
