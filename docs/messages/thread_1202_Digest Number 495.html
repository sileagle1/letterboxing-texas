Happy Birthday, Dewberry and CalicoRose, even though it's belated!  -- I just got home from about a week and half in the Canadian Rockies and Montana, and did manage to find a couple of letterboxes!  One was Matt the Rat's Bad Rock Canyon in Montana, between Columbia Falls and Hungry Horse - really neat stamp and great place.  The logbook had lots of cool stamps, including our own Silver Eagle and Baby Bear.   The other was in Banff - TopHatBox'R's Christmas Bow at Bow Falls, and I was FF!  Wow!  Going all the way from Texas to Canada to find a box placed by someone from Virgina, and being FF - what a thrill.  I think these may have been among the very few logbooks not yet signed by GG.  -- Viewfinder of tx2chic
