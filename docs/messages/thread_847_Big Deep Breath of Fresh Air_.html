Big Deep Breath of Fresh Air!

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-21 16:22:19 UTC-07:00
Hey all you fine Texas letterboxers!

Y'all may or may not have noticed I haven't been
around much this last month - I've had to take some
time away from home to tend to a family matter that
called for all my attention. I finally logged back on
last night to find I had over 700 message waiting for
me! OUCH! I've managed to cull it down to 99...

The one thing I noticed is that the details may
change, but the underlying current runs the same! It
looks like the big list is in an uproar about a Boston
Globe article? I quit reading those messages and just
deleted. Meanwhile, our little corner of the
letterboxing world has kept on as usual too - happily
planting and finding and cheering each other right
along. It sure is nice to come back to such a fine
group of folks!

I have managed to do a little letterboxing in the last
few weeks - actually, less finding or planting, than
just box maintenance. I've turned a new friend and
her family on to the hobby. I don't think they ever
fully realized we were finding boxes I had already
found before, so I sent them ahead with the clues and
I followed behind and enjoyed watching them discover
the box. They definitely have the fever!

I'll send some sitreps along after I catch my breath,
but all appears well box-wise up here in East Texas.
I specifically made it to Tyler State Park and Mission
Tejas, as well as Jim Hogg. And yes, our good friend
GG has made his way even deeper into the Pineywoods -
I found his stamp at Mission Tejas!

Gosh it's good to be back!
Lucy
