Boxing Report

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-03-26 23:56:50 UTC

We took a wildflower drive today to Lee/Washington Counties, both to
find a few boxes and hide some for others who might be doing the same.

We hid Dragonfly Dos at the Nails Creek Unit of Lake Somerville SP.

We found Wagon Ho and hid Flower Power at Old Baylor Park in
Independence (the wildflowers are beautiful here).

We found Washington on the Brazos, Texas Flag and Texas Birth at
Washington-on-the-Brazos SP. Texas Birth had been reported missing,
but it was right where it was supposed to be. Not only that but, it
(and the other boxes in the park) had had 3 visitors in the last week,
including Wanda and of course, our mysterious Grizzled Griffin, who we
missed by just one day. We hid The Power of the Pen in this same park.

Will try to post clues on LBNA and Atlas Quest later on tonight, when
I unscramble my notes. If you need them earlier, let me know.

Dewberry
