From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-12-10 23:24:40 UTC-06:00
I'm so excited I can hardly stand it - I have 11 days off at Christmas!!!  E-L-E-V-E-N.  I managed to be the first to ask off for the three days after Christmas, so I got it.  The people at work are tired of hearing about it.  My boss is tired of hearing about it.  Fearless Fox is tired of hearing about it.  And soon y'all may be tired of hearing about it!  About the only one who isn't tired of hearing about it is my faithful letterboxing compadre, Lightning!
 
A couple years ago I wound up with the week off between Christmas and New Year's and Lightning and I had a great time making day trips out to go letterboxing.  It was because of that week that we got to visit cool spots that are sort of in the folds of the map for us - there, but easily forgotten, like Freestone County where we found the Blind Lemon Jefferson - still a memorable box for me!
 
So - I'm planning at least a few such days this go around too.  I can't WAIT!!!
