BB
Thanks for the kind words. The more I carve and plant the better I
get and the more fun I have. Not including the exercise with the all
the walking to plant. I have been in the carving mood for the last
couple of weeks, but Hobby Lobby just can't keep up their supply of
the "pink stuff". I found out they only order 2 packages a week. I
have a special order for it, but my first order did not come in. I
hope this next order is here before my enthusiasm slows down. For now,
I am making the boxes and log books while I wait for the pink stuff.

Perky P,
 
No foul in being slow - take your time and enjoy it.  Letterboxing is a self-paced program!
 
As far as getting away without your clues - you have to get yourself a boxing pal.  Not one that goes with you, but one who knows letterboxing websites well enough to look something up for you when you call.  You haven't lived until you've gone down the trail with cell phone to ear, taking directions from someone by phone.  My dear friend, She Who Volunteers Me For Everything, is a non-boxer, but she very patiently looks boxes up for me from time to time and reads me the clues (but this is the same friend I called on my way to Henderson on Thanksgiving to ask her to go to my house and blow the candle out that I accidentally left burning).  I haven't yet called my mother for clues, but I'll bet she'd do it too (if I could actually catch her at home, which is sometimes a feat in itself)!
 
By the way, did you get your patches?
