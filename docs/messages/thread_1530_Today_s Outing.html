From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-17 21:16:11 UTC-08:00
Net box count for today's outing: 0. Of course, I
didn't try for any either...

I did, though, go out with Fearless Fox and Lightning
and hike a new part of I D Fairchild State Forest in
Maydelle (which is just a few miles from Rusk). The
forest has twelve miles of trail (that's the number I
got today - I've heard different counts). Hiking
there is a new experience each and every time. I had
in mind scoping out a place to plant a box, but I kept
getting distracted by the beautiful scenery. Oh well.
There will be at least one there by gathering time!

We followed that up with a trip to a small winery
there in Maydelle (little hiking, a little
tasting...of course!). It was quite an experience.
If you are looking for a serious winery, this one
isn't the place to go, but if you like quirky unusual
wines, you might want to bring your own barstool
because he only has two in his cozy little tasting
room! We used to go to the wineries in Temecula, CA,
when we lived in San Diego and we were accustomed to
being shown only four or five of the best wines of the
house - not in Maydelle! He proudly met us on the
front porch and rolled out every wine he made - and
opened up a fresh bag of Hershey's Kisses for
Lightning. Friendly guy, friendly wines - if you like
to try new wines and you plan to be at the gathering,
you might make a little time to visit the winery.
It's not terribly far from Rusk!
