Hello all,

Im finally getting around to clearing up mysteries in my logbook and recording my finds from the TALE 5 Event!  Here are some logbook mysteries that I havent solved: (1) A stamp the shape of Texas, outlined with barbwire, inside the shape of Texas are a diamond and a star and the words Nice TX Boxers +  groups.yahoo.com texasletterboxing  It is stamped half in red, half in blue (2) A wide-eyed cat, sitting, Im pretty sure this was an exchange and (3) A green gecko holding onto a branch (I think this was an exchange with one of Lucky Cats children). 

 

I was probably the only one who didnt post to this list about what a great time we had. We did. It was great seeing everyone and certainly wouldnt have been quite the success without the planning (and planting!) done beforehand.

 

If you missed the TALE 5 event or are just ready for another gathering, read on:

 

Lucky Cats is hosting Signs of Summer in San Antonio on May 3rd at OP Schnabel Park in NW San Antonio.  Heres the link:  http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=723  Yes, its short notice, and its quite a drive for some of you (not to mention that gasoline gets higher every day!)  But come if you canwell have a fun time!
