[letterboxingtexas] RSVP to 1st Texas event

From: Drew Mattke (drew@justdrew.net) | Date: 2004-02-02 20:25:04 UTC-06:00
Greetings, everyone!
 
We're the Mattke Family (who says you always have to have a cryptic or catchy handle?).
 
This is both an RSVP and a big hello to the group!
 
Can't tell you how glad we are that there is such growing support for letterboxing in the area. We were getting pretty tired of coming across boxes with no other stamps in them :-)
 
These days we're hardly ever the first to find and that's great by us. It's more fun to see how many folks have been there ahead of us so we can enjoy seeing others' stamps and log entries.
 
Looking forward to seeing you all at the event. We'll be staying just down the road in Buescher at the Cozy Circle site. We'll be there on Friday and will be staying through Sunday. Everyone's welcome to drop by and say Hi before or after the gathering. We'll be hanging out with our four (yes, we must be inSANE) dogs at our Terry trailer. We may have to come up with a distinctive marker so you'll recognize our site.
 
 
We'll bring some veggie goodies (suitable even for the meat-eaters among you) for the event.
 
 
For more on the Mattke Family, check the website, http://mattkefamily.com 
Just this weekend we placed a new letterbox NE of Austin and are finalizing the clues. We're working on a big series coming in the next month. Stay tuned!
We also plan to hide a "scary" one near the event. Clues will be available first to attendees only!
