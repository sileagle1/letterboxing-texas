Ok, I admit it, I stole the TTB2 again. That Bozo Bear made me so mad
with his insults that I planned to take the bus all the way to Mexico.
However, I only got as far as Sugar Land before I had a change of
heart. First of all, the bus was so crowded that there just wasn't
room for me or any new passengers. Also, I realized that I am a clown
first and hijacker second, so I decided to abandon the bus and join
forces with Baby Bear to buy a Big Top and start the Traveling Texas
Circus. I hope to garner your forgiveness by providing a home for all
things circus and ask that you help fill my Big Top with appropriate
entertainers. The TTB2 will continue to travel in TX, but will not
take on any new passengers, and when I last saw them Basking On The
Brazos, they seemed happy with that arrangement. So, if you know
anyone who wants to run away to join the circus, tell them to come join
me and let everyone on the TX Talklist know where to find me.
