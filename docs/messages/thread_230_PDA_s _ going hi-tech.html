I took us technology!
I got into Geocaching father's day its fairly tech by nature.

They have software for managing all their locations (such as GSAK) and
their clues.
I just put letterbox info into that program. I get approximate Latittudes
and Longitudes from the Texas Letterbox Atlas
and also load them into my GPS, or use a website that converts address to
lat/long. Then I cna load those into a program for my pocket PC to have
the clues handy and my GPS lets me know when I am driving near a letterbox!

When in a hurry, I do the basic copy/paste into a work doc and then just
pass that down to the pocket pc!

This can be as simple or complex as you like!
