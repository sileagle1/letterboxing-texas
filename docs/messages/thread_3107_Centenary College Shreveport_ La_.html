Centenary College Shreveport, La.

From: bluebutterflytexas (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-10-01 23:21:11 UTC
What an adventure we had at Centenary College was this week-end. PSGuru
and I decided to head East to The Red River Revel to check out the Arts
and Crafts and do a little letterboxing. The series The Boxalots have
on the campus were fun! I did get stopped by a security guard in a
vehicle asking if I needed help. He was very nice and helped me with
directions to the Jack London statue. There was a box located near this
statue, but it was missing. We searched and searched to no avail. We
decided to head to another building across campus to look for the
Centenary Swimming Box. As we approached the location, I was looking
hoping to see the spot where the box was suppose to be. I wasn"t
having much luck when PSGuru said "what about that box?" I looked and
there in a small tree was a letterbox just hanging on a bare limb! The
tree was close to the sidewalk,I grabbed it and was very surprised that
it was the Jack London Box! It was in plan sight and I was amazed that
it was still there. I stamped in the box and re-hide it where it was
suppose to be. I only stamped in 2 of the five boxes but, decided this
one was for the history books! For more info and a great history
lesson, read the clue for Forward, Forward Centenary Series. Thanks to
The Boxalots for planting this series.
