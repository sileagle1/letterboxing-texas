From: Cancan (cancan1959@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-04 13:58:34 UTC-05:00

Good Afternoon,

RE Road Rallies: Of the time we have posted for the event on AQ,  I hoped to set it up so about half is hunting on the road rally and half is spent at the "secret place" where we can gather, eat and exchange.  I prefer not to award points for time because I don't want to promote people racing around the city of Austin.  There will however need to be some time limits , so if someone gets lost, can't break the code for the secret venue etc.

I still have some doubts about having a road rally because they are not "kid friendly,"  Even though our kids are grown I don't want to alienate anyone, especially out-of-towners.  On the flip side I logged a query out into the AQ board and our group and have had a wonderful outpouring of ideas from LOTS of nice people around the country, several of whom have hosted a similar event and sent me all there information including clues!

It appears the Gunslinger Sweethearts need to meet again soon, unfortunately I am traveling on business starting Tues and will only return periodically until May 21, when (hopefully) I get to stay home for a good long while.

Kind Regards,
Cancan
