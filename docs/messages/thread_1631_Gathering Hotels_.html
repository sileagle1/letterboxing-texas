From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-26 10:00:58 UTC-08:00
The closest hotel is a Weston Inn, but it is also
centered between the state hospital, the prison, and
the county jail, so many of the guests at that hotel
are visiting folks confined to one of those three
places. You may or may not care about that, but I
thought it would be worth mentioning...As far as I
know, it's perfectly safe

There is a Southern Hotel in Rusk which I've not heard
anything about in quite awhile. It was very nice
awhile back...

In Jacksonville is a Holiday Inn Express (12 miles
away_, which my company has a group rate at and you
would be welcome to use. I'll do some checking to see
what that rate is and make sure it is still in effect.
Jacksonville also has a brand new Best Western.
There are three other hotels in town, but they are all
very worn, and two of those three are - ummmm - black
market business motels?

For anyone wanting to stay in hotels, I would be glad
to work on group rates.

Lucy

HOTELS: 

From what I can gather, no one is particularly
interested in providing group rates without knowing
numbers.  I used 10 rooms as an example and they
seemed to not bite on that.  Anyway, here are the
rates I've gotten for the local hotels and here are
also the phone numbers and distance from the park:

Weston Inn/Rusk - 3.4 miles from park - 903-683-8383 -
$73.00

Holiday Inn Express/Jacksonville - 13.8 Miles from
park - 903-589-8500 - $72.00 (you may have to tell
them you know they can give you this rate, but it is
available on Travelocity and they quoted it to me
verbally)

Best Western/Jacksonville - 14.1 miles from park -
903-586-0007- $68.93.  Same rate on hotels.com or
Travelocity. 

***************
The following are two other hotels in the area.  I
don't recommend staying at them, but if budget is a
really big issue, consider them.  They are run-down
and not very clean, but they are relatively safe.

Southern Motor Inn - 6.0 miles from park -
903-683-8383 - $45.00
  (this is mainly a weekly hotel)

Best Value Inn/Jacksonville - 16.0 miles from park
-903-586-9841 - $62.10

****************
I tell you about the following three hotels because
you do NOT want to stay there.  The two Jacksonville
ones are usually weekend trouble spots for the cops,
and I know by reputation that the Rusk one is the
same.  They are NOT safe!

Rusk Hotel
Tradewinds Hotel-Jacksonville
Pine Lodge-Jacksonville

****************

There are also some decent hotels in Palestine, but
the closest of them is 28 miles from the park and
roughly the same price as the three top hotels.

Lucy
