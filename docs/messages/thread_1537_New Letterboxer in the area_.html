You would be most welcome.  So far it is almost a 1-man effort out here.  I have 4 letterboxes out, and there are only 6 in the area.  I have found one and I am saving the last for a rainy day.
 
I have some ideas for two new boxes.  I will insert the artwork for the stamp I am working on.  I took a photo of the mountain in shadow, then added the white lines to accent the elephant shape.  If you fill in the white lines - you would see what the mountain actually looks like.
 
Come on out for a visit...  You will be able to get at least the 6 boxes - maybe more.
 
BTW - I spent one night sleeping in Vicksburg.  Right on the side of the road where I-20 crosses the river, in my van.  I had just been released from an assignment at Redstone Arsenal, and was on my way to join a desert warfare unit at Ft. Bliss on the way to Gulf War 1.  I needed a rest, so there it was.
 
Merry Christmas.
 
Mike.
