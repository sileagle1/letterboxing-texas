Silver Eagle & Baby Bear's 5th LB Anniversary

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-08-31 12:31:52 UTC
It's hard to believe it has been 5 years since Baby Bear and I found
our first letterbox, and even more amazing how much letterboxing has
grown in that time, especially in TX. I have been in a reflective mood
the past few days, so decided to write down a few memories of the "old
days". It was rather long, so instead of posting I put it in the Files
section for you to read at your leisure. I hope you find it enjoyable,
and I hope we continue to grow and have fun for another 5 years!

Silver Eagle

P.S. I plan to place an anniversary letterbox soon, where visitors
will record their anniversary dates and first boxes.


Re: [letterboxingtexas] Silver Eagle & Baby Bear's 5th LB Anniversary

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-08-31 08:29:00 UTC-05:00
Considering that the first several boxes we found were one of y'all's, I think it's a safe bet to say that we probably wouldn't STILL be letterboxing if it weren't for the two of you - so I thank you from the bottom of my heart!
 
Looking at the counts, it looks like there were maybe 10 boxes in the state when y'all got started?  And over the five years since there have been 1,983 listings in Texas (on LBNA, including archived boxes), and of those, around 330 belong to either you or Baby Bear.  That puts your contribution to about 16% of all Texas boxes.  WOW!
 
SO - that kind of growth makes predicting the future pretty difficult, but I DO have to wonder where letterboxing will be on y'all's 10th anniversary!
 
Thanks again,
Lucy
