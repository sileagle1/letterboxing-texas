All passengers are safely back on their infamous automobile. As they
traveled down Hwy 69 while "Lookin" for some "Love" they found a rest
stop with a wonderful view.

Take the trail off the left side past the covered picnic tables until
it T's. Go right on the trail past 2 markers. After the 2nd marker
walk 20 steps to the tree with the vines climbing up the trunk on your
right. If you get to the 3rd marker you have gone to far. Walk past
this tree to the leaning tree with the large split in the base. Look
for the bus's spare tire 19 steps past the leaning tree.
