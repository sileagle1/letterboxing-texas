From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-05-04 09:30:46 UTC-07:00

I have reserved the venue for our TX Annual LB Event and posted it on AQ.  Since it is our 13th Event and many people wanted me to move the event later when it wouldn't be so cold, I decided to have it start on Friday, April 1, 2016 with the theme "Fooling Around".  No, not that kind of foolishness, the kind that happens on April Fools' Day!  Also, since many expressed a desire to have an outdoor campfire, I reserved the Group Dining Hall at Stephen F. Austin State Park which has a group camping area outside for up to 60 tent campers and a place for a fire.  I reserved it for both Friday and Saturday so we can stay from 2:00 Friday to 11:00 am Sunday.  Instead of eating somewhere else Friday night, we can just eat at the campsite both nights.  Friday night you will be on your own for food, but Saturday lunch and dinner will be potluck as usual.  So cross your fingers for good weather and I hope to see y'all there!

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?eventId=3474

http://sileagle.webs.com/tale13.htm
