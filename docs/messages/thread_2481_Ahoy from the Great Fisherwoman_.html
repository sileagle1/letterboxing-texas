From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-01-06 17:15:04 UTC-06:00
Just got home from fishing with a few of my closest friends and their families.  We took the pack to the Athens Fresh Water Fishery for the tour, the dive show, and an afternoon of fishing for trout.  When I'm doing Cub Scout stuff, I try real hard not to be too girly, but I kept catching myself saying, as someone was reeling in another trout, "oooohhh....he's so pretty!"  Thankfully, I'm not squeamish about taking fish off the hook or baiting hooks and I can cast with the best of 'em, so I didn't totally ruin my credibility as a Cubmaster.  Wind-blistered faces, stinky hands, and muddy clothes, along with a catch of 25 trout and we are home.  I sure am thinking that's a park that deserves a letterbox, so I made friends with the curator and I think I'm gonna start working on my trout stamp to take back when I go back to Athens for my songwriters workshop in early February.
Fresh air - it does wonders for the soul!
