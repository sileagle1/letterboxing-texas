Both of them sell birds, fish, small mammals & reptiles. Petsmart
used to not sell any animals at all, but then they started about 10
years ago too. We probably don't want to get into the ethics of
this on this list.

I worked for an animal shelter for a while and they were both
relatively good corporate citizens--hosting adopt a shelter animal
fairs and helping us raise money, etc.

Now that Kroger and Target carry the specific kind of IAMS I like, I
never go to either.
