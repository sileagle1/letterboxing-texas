I was so excited and delighted to receive this message a short time ago regarding four of my boxes that were in the flood area south of Lake Grapevine. There is HOPE for more of our boxes around the lake. Mommytana and Pirate Bob made my day! Thanks!
Puddle Splasher
 
Subject: Artist Series Two Update!
Letterbox: Artist Series Two
Date Found: September 2, 2007

Chagall's Bonjour Paris: Found
Picasso's Petite Fleur: Found
Dali's Persistence of Time: Found
Munch's The Scream: Found

Message from Finder:

We took a chance since the flooding has gone down and went to find these boxes. They are all still there, the Dali box had fallen out of the tree, but we put it back where it belonged when we were done.

None of the logbooks or stamps got wet, but there was water in the Chagall box. I did not have the materials with me to dry it out, or replace the box/bags, but it might be a good idea to do it soon... although as I said the logbook and stamp are still okay (the hitchhiker that was left in the box was not in as good of shape... I guess it pays to use high quality plastic bags).

Mommytana and Pirate Bob
