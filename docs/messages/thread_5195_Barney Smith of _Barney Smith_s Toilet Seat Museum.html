Barney Smith of "Barney Smith's Toilet Seat Museum".

From: Mothers Love (cwenter1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-04-29 21:29:38 UTC
I've been following "Barney Smith's Toilet Seat Museum" through Geocaching and on a recent thread that a geocacher left he stated that "he wasn't sure how Barney's wife was doing as she was not expected to make it through the night. This was on April 8th. I do not know if she actually passed away that night or if at all but I know some of you have heard about Barney and his wonderful artistic talent. JB Kokopelli also has a letterbox toilet seat in Barney's museum which is how I first heard at this wonderful man and his museum. For those of you who haven't heard about it before it's located just outside of San Antonio. I was fortunate to go see Barney and his museum a couple of years ago while in the area to attend a bowling tournament. Barney is 92 now and very spry for his age and smart too. If you get an opportunity to go please do it soon as you never know how much longer he'll be around.

If anyone has heard for sure about Barney's wife can you please let us know.
