From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-05-02 06:46:24 UTC-05:00
The Big List is the national talk list, I guess the oldest LB talk list.  The address is:
 
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa
 
It's a very good group, but it isn't for the faint-hearted.  Seemingly innocent easy questions get that group riled up (like the question we've been talking about about what to do about the boxes that belong to boxers who have left).  It also isn't the place to ask questions like "What is a Hitchhiker?" and other common newbie questions.  To get those answered, go to newboxers:
 
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers
 
If you decide to join the Big List, I strongly suggest you watch and listen for awhile before participating.  I don't want to make it sound like a mean list, but there are personalities there with very strong opinions and the ability to express themselves.  I enjoy reading the Big List as long as it isn't flaming (and even sometimes when it is).
 
Lucy
