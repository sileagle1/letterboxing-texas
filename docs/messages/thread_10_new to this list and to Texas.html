Hello everyone!

Our family letterboxing name is the "happyhomeschoolers". We just
moved to north Houston in December. We have been boxing since March
of last year. A dear friend in Virginia introduced us to the hobby
and we have enjoyed it ever since! Our PFX count is P3F40X34E1.
I like to keep track of those things for fun. We've been to one
event back in VA and it was a blast. So I really hope we can attend
the 1st Texas event.

Among many things,what I enjoy most about letterboxing is going to
places that I never new exsisted.

We haven't been 'boxing in Texas yet (still going thru all those
moving boxes), but I'm getting the itch to go hiking again!

Looking forward to meeting some you on the trails!

happyhomeschoolers
