Duct tape vs. Spray paint

From: RachelS (prazehm878@yahoo.com) | Date: 2011-04-28 20:17:39 UTC
I am slowly getting my box ready to plant. In the field I have noticed lots of boxes covered with camo duct tape. For my lock-n-lock, it seems like it would be easier to spray paint it, than to duct tape it. Is there a valid reason for choosing duct tape over spray paint that I haven't figured out yet?

Elwood Fan


Re: Duct tape vs. Spray paint

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2011-04-29 01:09:45 UTC
Spray painting with Krylon Fusion is easier than camo taping in my opinion. I started out using camo tape because I did not know about a spray paint that would stick to plastic. Once I found it, I never went back. Some might prefer the tape or just not know about the paint and I guess it is possible the tape might last longer than the paint, but I would do what you like best.

Silver Eagle
