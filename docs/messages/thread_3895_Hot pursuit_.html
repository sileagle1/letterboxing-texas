Texas Tour Bus #2
left the road in a cloud of blue,
Lost among the loblolly pine,
No doubt smokin' and feelin' fine!

But Belle and Bloomin were in hot pursuit,
Having heard this group got away with the loot.
Down the trail and under loblolly bow
We found them hiding -- Holy Cow!!!

Tired and thirsty,
they crept to the road
Where Belle and BGJ were willing to load
this infamous crew, now turning feisty.

So off they all went in search of a new route,
with Cancan and Stonehunter now in hot pursuit.
All four joined forces to capture this bunch,
Round up this crew and take them home for lunch.

They're resting now, eating all in sight
at Belle's Motel, safe from their plight.

Who knows how long they'll rest and recoup?
Soon this bus will reappear with its recalcitrant troup.
