Well, I'm finally back from yet another vacation.
This one was over 2 weeks in Colorado and Wyoming
and we had a great time in COOL weather. I have
to admit that after a few nights camping in 38 degree
weather I was happy to be back in the TX heat. I
guess I really am just a Southern boy.

I did manage to find and place a few boxes along the
way and I must say there are some great boxes in
these states. Great locations and awesome stamps,
rivaling any I have seen. I was almost embarrassed
to leave my meager efforts, but hopefully the locals
won't care.

Speaking of locals, I did get to meet a few in Colorado
and they are typical of letterboxers I have met everywhere;
very nice and hospitable. I even met one on a trail
by pure luck! I had on my TX LB shirt, but it was under
a jacket so she only saw my LBNA patch on my fanny pack.
Good thing I put it there because it saves me from asking
if somebody is a letterboxer and getting weird looks.

I only got to find a few boxes in TX, but one I found
(Drifter in Waco by Rebel) had been visited by GG just 5
days previous and the log had a note for him/her to contact
the placer. I see from reading some of the posts while I
was gone that he/she did just that! Even if she/he decides
to remain elusive, at least we have a way to notify he/she
of events if Rebel doesn't mind being the go-between. I
would like to know GG's gender, though, and if he/she
carved their signature stamp. If so, GG should definitely
place some boxes because it is awesome.

I also see that Lucy has set a date for the Barn Raising
LB Gathering and I hope I can make it. Since it will be
so close to Dallas, maybe Ruby can either get Julie (first
TX letterboxer) to come or have a gathering with her in
Dallas the day before so we can double our pleasure.

And finally, I see several new people have joined the list,
so I say "Welcome to our family".

Silver Eagle
