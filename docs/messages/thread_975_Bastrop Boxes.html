Re: Bastrop Boxes

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-30 13:36:48 UTC
We were there in January, so someone has probably been there more
recently. But, there was a burned area that took out two of the
boxes: The Boots ARE made for walking (which we found the charred
remains of) and Houston Toad (which we couldn't find any trace of,
but the whole area around it was very burned).

We successfully found Cool Cat, Poison Ivy, Lost Loblolly, O-Knot
#1, Trail Rider, 10th Mountain Division, Skinny Pine (which I guess
has been reported missing, but I would look for it again, it had
gotten really pushed under the ledge when we were there--very cool
hiding place).

That was all the kids wanted to walk, so we didn't get to look for
any of the other O-Knots. I thought the orienteering course looked
like a lot of fun. Would love to go back and do that.
