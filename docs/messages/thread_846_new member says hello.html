From: Aisling DArt (eibhlinm@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-05-21 21:26:55 UTC
Hi,

I'm relatively new to Texas, but not to letterboxing.

People know me as Aisling D'Art (pronounced the Irish way, "ASH-ling")
and my art website is http://www.aisling.net/

I live in Katy, near Cinco Ranch, just outside Houston. I moved here
from NH about a year and a half ago, to be with the most wonderful man
in the world (he seems that way to me anyway).

I'm a full-time artist/author, working on three travel-related books
due in the next year or so: One is about New Orleans, one is about
Stratford-upon-Avon (England), and then there's one about Old Town
Spring (north of Houston) that's due in August 2006.

I carve my own letterboxing stamps (and teach rubber carving,
sometimes), but haven't planted any boxes in Texas yet.

In fact, I haven't gotten out for any letterboxing yet, and I'd better
do some soon because I'm not used to the heat here yet... and heat is
certainly on its way now!

Other than that, I have three grown children (NH, NY, FL) and my
mother and brother live in Mobile, AL. My hobbies include genealogy,
baking, and an eclectic mix of interests.

Because I'm working on books right now, I'll probably lurk on this
list, mostly. But, I hope to create and plant a few boxes this
summer, as well as get out and find some.

Thanks for inviting me to join this list!

Cheerfully,
Aisling (I usually just sign my nickname, "Ais")
