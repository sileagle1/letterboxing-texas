I just want to remind everyone that the TX Annual Letterbox Event is about 1 month away and to mention a new development. Lone Star Quilter has created a lap quilt based on this year's theme and is graciously donating it. The main reason for the raffle and auction is to raise money for next year's event at Bastrop, which will be more expensive than usual, but quite nice. Hope to see many of you on March 3!
