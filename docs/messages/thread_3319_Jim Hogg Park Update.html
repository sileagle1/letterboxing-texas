Jim Hogg Park Update

From: cwenter1 (cwenter1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-02-02 03:16:47 UTC
I was at Jim Hogg Park today (for the 3rd time) to find the last few
letterboxes that I ran out of time looking for on my 1st 2 visits and I
found out that the City of Rusk is doing some maintenance of the park.
They are temporarily closing the trails to cut down the dead trees in
the area. Some of the letterboxes are in the part of the park that's
being excalvated. See list below:

NOVEMBER HOLIDAY SERIES: JB Kokopelli
Take a Hike
Mickey Mouse Birthday
Veterans Day
Thanksgiving

I retrieved "Take a Hike" and "Mickey Mouse Birthday" from imminent
destruction and brought them home with me. I didn't have the clues for
this series with me so I was going from memory from when I found them a
couple weeks ago. I knew where "Veterans Day" was but I couldn't find
it and they were cutting trees down all around me so I thought it best
that I get out of there.

CUB SCOUT SERIES: Barefoot Lucy

If anyone else has boxes in this park. you may want to check on them
and any one hoping to go look for them should probably wait awhile
until the city is done cutting down the dead trees and the boxes have
been replanted.

JB: If you contact me we can make arrangements to get "Take a Hike"
and "Mickey Mouse Birthday" back to you.
