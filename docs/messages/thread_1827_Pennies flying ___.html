Trail Names - I think you hit the nail on the head.  Lena Is Who You Are.  If you don't feel that way about your trail name, don't use it. 
 
I picked up BarefootLucy back when Al Gore invented the internet, but I tried a lot of screen names first!  I just didn't like the way any of them fit, until my mother happened to refer back to our old running line between ourselves - "Get your shoes on Lucy - it's time to go".  That was a daily saying in our house.  I knew right away it was Me. 

And it is - I feel as comfortable being called Lucy as I do by my real name, and in some ways my trail name is more me than my real name is.  My real name is used to identify me in my roles of Mom, Wife, Employee, Volunteer, Friend etc (although I've noticed some of the folks that see those sides of me also call me Lucy at times!).  My trail/screen name strips away those roles and allows me to be who I consider myself to be. 

That said, I think you will eventually find a name for yourself, but you have to relax and quit looking, but continue to be observant about yourself. 
 
Lucy
