TX Wildflower LB Event Summary

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-03-05 19:18:28 UTC
The weather was chilly but sunny for our 4th Annual TX LB Event at
Lake Somerville SP, and we had about 30 letterboxers and a few LSS
attend. Baby Bear and Clueless Collector got a roaring fire started
in the fireplace and kept it going all day, and that, along with all
the chili, kept most people warm. And warm is the word I would use
to describe the feel of this Event. Everyone was friendly, excited
and just plain glad to be there as we talked with boxers from the
past and met new ones, too.

After eating, the LB Trivia game was played with Barefoot Lucy edging
out Gryzzled Gryphon for first place with 26 out of 35 answers
correct. Most found it fun to hear the stories behind the answers
even if they did not win.

Next it was time for Sandibox to give her demo on creative logbook
making, followed by Barfoot Lucy showing how to carve with a dremel.
Both demos were well received and highly appreciated.

The rest of the afternoon was spent looking for boxes in the park
until darkness forced us back to the pavilion for cleanup and more
talk. Many people were not camping and had to go home, but a few
stayed until after 9:00 telling stories and getting to know each
other better. This is one of my favorite things about Events, and I
am always grateful when some boxers stick around for it. Each time I
learn a little more about my fellow boxers (even GG) and it is fun,
too.

I would like to thank Baby Bear for helping me plan the Event,
getting the prizes for the Trivia contest, and everything else that
made it successful. I would also like to thank everyone who came
with food, boxes, or just a fun attitude. You are the best!

Silver Eagle
