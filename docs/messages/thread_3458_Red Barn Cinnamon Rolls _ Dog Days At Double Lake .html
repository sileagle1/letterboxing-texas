Red Barn Cinnamon Rolls & Dog Days At Double Lake Events

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-03-31 18:10:15 UTC
Two Events in one day, what more could you ask for! I didn't think I
would be able to make them, but luckily I pulled it off. The day
started early since I had a 90 minute drive and wanted to get a few
boxes along the LST before the first Event, so I was up at 5:30 and off
by 6:00. I met Baby Bear on the trail and then we headed to the Red
Barn Retreat for world famous cinnamon rolls by Boots Tex. They
definitely lived up to their reputation and I only wish I could have
eaten more (I did take one for the road). Boots & Lone Star Quilter
were great hosts and it was nice to see their Retreat. It was also
great to meet more of their family who happened to be in town.

Next it was on to Double Lake to find a few boxes and place mine before
the next Event started. It rained a little, but not enough to stop us,
and soon it was time for lunch. Everyone was already at the pavilion
eating hot dogs and it was great to see old friends again and meet a
few new ones. There were many boxes hidden around the pavilion, and
more boxes in the park to find, so no rest for the weary. The weather
turned nice and the Dogwoods were blooming, so it was a great afternoon
of boxing.

I had a great time and want to thank Dewberry, Boots Tex & Lone Star
Quilter for everything they did. What a nice way to spend a Sunday!
