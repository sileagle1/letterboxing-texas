Thanks to Lankin's Purple Monkeys

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-07-03 22:14:55 UTC-05:00
I haven't heard anything at all from my Buffalo boxes in a long time until earlier this week when Kristin of Lankin's Purple Monkeys emailed me to let me know they found two of the three boxes I have in Buffalo.
For whatever reason, those boxes get reported missing more often than average. Every time I've gone to check them, they've been in place so I have gotten to where I blow off missing reports on those boxes. However, in the last month or two I've been thinking I should probably just go ahead and mark them as missing since I haven't heard from them in forever and I my travels don't seem to take me that direction, so you can imagine how pleased I was to hear that two of them are indeed in place!
And in fact, according to Kristin, one of the containers was completely disintegrated so she replaced it with a sour cream container with apologies if it is destroyed by an animal, but that was all they had. And now, with this rain we're getting in East Texas, I'll take a sour cream container over no container at all.
Thanks so much, Lankin's Purple Monkeys, for finding my boxes and for taking care of the one = Y'all are awesome!
