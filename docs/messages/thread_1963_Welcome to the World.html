Fannie Flagg is one of my favorite authors, and one of my favorite books by her is "Welcome to the World, Baby Girl". I am working on a new letterbox which will be out soon with the same title. Three days ago, my daughter and son-in-law gave birth to a beautiful baby girl (my third grandchild, but my first granddaughter). So this box of course is inspired by little Bailey Nicole Purcel - all seven pounds, three ounces of her! She's NEVER in her entire life worn a pair of shoes, so I'm sure she'll follow in Barefoot Lucy's bare footsteps and be an excellent Texas letterboxer!
