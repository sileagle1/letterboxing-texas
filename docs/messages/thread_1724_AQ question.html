From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-03-28 04:20:20 UTC
You can't go back. But, it's easy to fix. Just go to LBNA, and click
Add a letterbox. Copy the URL to your clue on atlasquest.com (make
sure you are copying the actual AQ clue page, not just aq.com) into
the link section of the "add a letterbox" page that comes up on
letterboxing.org. If you check the little box that says link to this
automatically, it'll go straight from the page of clue listings to the
clue on AQ. Otherwise, it'll create an LBNA page for your box and
there will be a clickable link on it that will take you to atlasquest.

Clear?

You can also do it the other way and create a link on atlasquest if
you want to keep the primary clue on LBNA. All my older box clues are
on LBNA, most of my new ones are on Atlasquest.
