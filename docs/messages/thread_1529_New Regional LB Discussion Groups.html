From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-12-14 21:58:14 UTC
Not sure if you are aware, but Silent Doug has set up
2 new regional LB groups: Midwest (including Oklahoma &
Arkansas along with many other Heartland states) and
Southwest (including New Mexico & Arizona). The reason
I mention this is because those TX Letterboxers that
reside near one of the states in these groups might
benefit by joining. Maybe Mike in El Paso will find
a nearby Letterboxer in New Mexico who will start planting
a few near him. Or maybe someone in the Panhandle or
North TX will find a kindred spirit in Oklahoma. If
nothing else it will be fun to watch these Groups grow
as we have.
I have joined the Midwest Group since I have several boxes
there and have extended an invitation to them for our Event
since it won't be that far from Oklahoma & Arkansas. You
never know.
For the links to these Groups (and others), go to Silent
Doug's website of LB Links:

http://www.letterboxing.info/links/?id=75

Thanks S.D. for setting these up. You might be Silent, but
your actions speak louder than words!
