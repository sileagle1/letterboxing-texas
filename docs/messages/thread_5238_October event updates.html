October event updates

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2013-08-12 21:52:14 UTC-05:00

We have several new boxers in the group, so I’d like to start off by inviting everyone to join us for a letterboxing event at Lake Houston Park the weekend of Oct 18th to the 20th.  If you have never attended an event before, this is your chance to meet other letterboxers, exchange stamps, and find lots of fun boxes of various types.  We will have traditional boxes, stamp exchanges with other boxers, event boxes, and some fun twists and surprises. You are also welcome (and encouraged) to plant boxes of your own for others to find.  We will have a pot luck lunch Saturday, and pick a restaurant to meet up for dinner on Friday night.  It’s all very casual and kids are welcome too!

 

If you plan to come, please sign up on the Atlas Quest event page so we can have a good head count:

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2754

 

If you haven’t signed up yet, please be aware the tent campsites are all reserved.  I’ll try to post a list of nearby hotels soon.  For those who can come early, clue packages will be available on Friday around lunch time (exact time to be announced).  New boxes will not be listed before the event.

 

More details to come and I hope to see everyone there!
