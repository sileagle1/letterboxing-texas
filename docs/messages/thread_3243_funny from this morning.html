funny from this morning

From: Vicki Stecher (stecher@runbox.com) | Date: 2007-12-28 11:01:31 UTC-06:00
 I know I don't talk much, but I'm always reading the chatter from this list and enjoying it. I wanted to share this...
 
I was laying in bed this morning (I've been sick for several weeks; another drs. appt this afternoon) and my 6 year old son came in and crawled beside me, kind of flopping down on my husband's pillow. He said, "Mom, we have just *GOT* to go letterboxing. It has been sooooo long!" It was the cutest thing. I agreed and it turns out that he had found his letterboxing bag in the closet and was dying to use the stamp. So he ended up pretending he was letterboxing and stamping lots of sheets of paper with his older brother. Very cute and very funny.
 
We had tons of rain in the last few days. So if I can shake this bronchitis and the earth around here dried up, then we may be off and running thanks to him!
 
:)
Vicki
