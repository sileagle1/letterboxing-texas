Great weekend

From: carrascoruth2000 (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-04-19 03:43:57 UTC
It seems like it was a great weekend for Texas letterboxers! We went
to Brazos today with the intention of finding the new box and placing
one of our own, however an 8 year old with a severe itchy eye attack
made us cut our visit a little short. At least it gives me an excuse
for another trip to my favorite park of all time! We did see an
alligator that had to be about 13 feet long on the path at Elm Lake.
She was in the middle of the path with her head up...obviously
aggitated over something. We had first seen her moving from quite a
distance. Believe it or not we saw a man lead his scout troop right
past her (or him). The alligator hissed, but thankfully all the
scouts passed by safely. We watched (at a safe distance)for about 15
more minutes until it turned and went back into the water. Very
cool. (Except for the people who act like they are in an amusement
park instead of a state park). Anyhow, we did place a box in Houston
this weekend, in Hermann Park near the zoo. I'll try to post the
clues tomorrow.
