Who am I?

From: Lois Adams (lois_adams37@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-21 20:38:07 UTC-08:00

I am Lois. I live in Texas around Cedar Creek Lake. I don't live in a town or City but close to 7Points, Tx. I don't remember how I heard of letterboxing but it was early last year. I fully intended to jump right into it but circumstances prevented me from doing so at that time. This year I decided it was time for my husband and I to do something together as a hobby. He pretty much goes along with me on things even though he may not seem very interested at first. I think this will be a great way to have some quality time together as well as get out there and walk and see Texas. I am soon to be 70 and have lived in Texas all my life but I have not seen all of Texas yet. My trail name is loraad37. Look for me out there boxing. Lois
