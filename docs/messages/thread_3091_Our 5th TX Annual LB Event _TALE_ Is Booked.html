Our 5th TX Annual LB Event (TALE) Is Booked

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-09-18 16:09:01 UTC
Since the feedback has been so positive, I went ahead and booked the
pavilion at South Lake Park on Lake Bastrop for Saturday, 3/1/08. I
also booked a campsite and if you know you are camping you might want
to do the same. You have to reserve by calling the TX Parks & Wildlife
at 512-389-8900. They have 38 sites suitable for tents or RV. They
also have 18 cabins.

Baby Bear and I have decided to make the theme TX Icons, which is
basically anything that reminds you of TX. I thought about having a
Leap Day theme since the day before the Event is 2/29/08, but it was
too limiting. We will also have another LB game with prizes for the
winner.

Even though it is still a long way off, I wanted to make sure we had a
place and date to mark on our calendars. I will send more details as
time gets closer. I hope everyone can make it and that the weather
will be nice.

Silver Eagle
