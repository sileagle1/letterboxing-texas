Lost Marbles and Sweet Berry LB's

From: Lou Catozzi (txhipowr@austin.rr.com) | Date: 2004-07-25 00:27:29 UTC
Today I was out and about in the Hill Country letterboxing,
geocaching, and just generally wandering around. I checked on Lost
Marbles and Sweet Berry LB's just west of Marble Falls and both are
there but the log books are ruined. We are talking, like, reverted
back to wood pulp mush! I cleaned out the containers and put the
stamps back in and rehid them as described.

I wouldn't normally post a report like this but both of these caches
were reported to be missing so I thought you all might want to know
otherwise.

Lou AKA
TxHiPowr


Re: Lost Marbles and Sweet Berry LB's

From: Wanda (wsthm@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-07-25 03:13:21 UTC
Lou and I emailed about this earlier today. The strange thing is
that these boxes had been reported as missing twice and when Lou
found them they weren't hidden under the big rocks where I placed
them, but out in the open, more or less. I think maybe a non-
letterboxer accidently found one, then looked in the other similar
rock formation and found the other one too, took them, and just
recently (before a good rain) returned them after reading up on
letterboxing and realizing that they should be left there.
Anyway, I am glad they are back. :) Thanks again for drying them
out and replacing them in their correct possitions, Lou. Lou didn't
have any paper with him, and it will be October before I -possibly-
get up there again, so if anyone else is going to be searching for
these two micro-boxes before then, I'd appreciate it if you could
put some paper inside them to serve as temporary logbooks.
Wanda/Busy Bee
