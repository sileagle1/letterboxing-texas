I happened to be out in the Hill Country last week, around the Lost Maples, so I decided to check up on Lost Mable and that's when I saw it: a desperate note from some turkey saying "Help Me!". I didn't know what to make of it until I checked around and found a letterbox called "I.R.A. Turkey", placed by that scrawny little Baby Bear. He are a Turkey all right! Did you read what he said? It was not only pure fiction, it was outright plagerism! If I get my ghostly hands around his hairy neck, it'll take more than some East Texas Society to help him.
