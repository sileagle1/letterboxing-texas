One of the first letterboxes I found "SCC-Hole In The Ground" in Waxahachie, TX may be at risk. http://www.atlasquest.com/boxes/clue/index.html?gBoxId=11191
MagnaBlend is a factory in Waxahachie that burned to the ground several months ago. In the current location, clean-up is still in progress. They own several locations, and word is that they are now planning to move to the Superconductor property and consolidate all of their factories to this location. It was planted by CelticLions/Stillwater/Ruby Tuesday. I remember reading that Ruby Tuesday passed away some time ago. Should I rescue the box? Or is someone else now "responsible" for those plants?
