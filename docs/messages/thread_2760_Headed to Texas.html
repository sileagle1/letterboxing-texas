Re: Headed to Texas

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-05-10 12:05:18 UTC
Hey Kim, good to hear from you again!

One route you could try would be to go through Wichita Falls on the
way down, getting those boxes, then continuing to Possum Kingdom.
You might even help us out by placing a box or two in virgin counties
(e.g. Archer, Young, Jack, Stephens). On the way home, head east on
Hwy 180 and get the boxes near Mineral Wells and the one in
Weatherford. Continue into Ft. Worth (Tarrant County) where there
are tons of boxes. My Cat In the Hat box is in the Ft. Worth Nature
Center and there are several other boxes in this great place. Then
you could head north on Hwy 35 through Denton with many more box
opportunities. Have fun!

Silver Eagle
