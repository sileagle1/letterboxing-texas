We have just been to El Paso ourselves, by coincidence
at the same time as Sandibox, and I had the most fun I
have ever had with people I've never met, that I can
remember. We did try for an exchange, too, but it did
not work out this time, but it was still fun to know
that "somewhere out there" were other folks having the
same kind of fun. Letterboxing brought us to new
places we would not have known to visit otherwise, and
we had so much fun, and in general, I am still quite
delighted to have found this fun activity and fun
group. Thanks for everyone who plays the game, so we
can all have fun. : )

From: SandiBox (sandibox@msn.com) | Date: 2008-01-06 06:17:13 UTC
We just got home from our trip to El Paso a few hours ago. Before I
head off for some much needed sleep, I wanted to say "Thanks!" to Mike
for sharing his great letterboxes with us and for taking the time to
meet SoccerChik and I for an exchange.

For all of you who have been thinking about taking a trip to west
Texas, I strongly encourage you to do so. The quantity may not be
there (yet), but the quality is exceptional. I know we tease Mike a
lot about not carving his own stamps, but it is obvious that he puts a
lot of thought and care into the design of the custom-made stamps in
his letterboxes. And the locations are superb! We really got a
chuckle out of one particularly tricky hide... and made one park ranger
not just smile, but laugh out loud! I only wish we had had more time
to find more of his letterboxes.

Thanks again Mike!

SandiBox
