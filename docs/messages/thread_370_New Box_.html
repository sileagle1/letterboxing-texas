From: Rachel Stout (prazehm878@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-03-14 00:12:03 UTC
I am loving this spring weather! I went out boxing yesterday and found a couple of Frost Maiden's boxes-what great carves! I still have a lot to learn, but I am getting there!

Back in the winter I carved a stamp which is by far my favorite stamp-the Lions Paw. I figured it was about time to plant this one-and what better place than a college whose mascot is a lion?

If you get a chance to go boxing this week while it's warm and you're on Spring Break, and happen to be in Waxahachie (or just traveling down I-35 and need a quick break), I hope you get a chance to locate this one! I searched high and low for a good location on campus-they keep this place pristine-and I think I found a good location. Enjoy!
