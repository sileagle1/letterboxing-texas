Fearless Fox was scolding me earlier this evening for allowing my Norton Antivirus expire earlier this week.  Now he is the computer geek in the family and he gets all sorts of extra seats on the software he uses at work, so I think it should be HIS responsibility to keep my computer protected, but he somehow thinks I should be self-sufficient with respect to my computer.  He says I can't have it both ways - it seems he dredged up some ancient history and reminded me of our dance lessons when I felt that if we didn't at least take turns leading, we should flip for who leads.  I hate getting caught in my own words!
 
ANYWAY, as he was scolding me, he was also looking up a site to buy the download to get me covered again.  He asked me which package I would want and I told him I only needed the basic package, but he suggested we look at the features of the other packages.  We came to one that said something about protecting my computer from suspicious websites.  He commented that I visit so many weird sites that I should think about getting that feature.  Shocked, I asked him what weird sites I visit.  Without missing a beat, he said "Well - you go to all those letterboxing sites, to begin with!"
With one felling swoop, he called every last one of us weird!!!
