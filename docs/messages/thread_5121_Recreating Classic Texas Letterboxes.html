Recreating Classic Texas Letterboxes

From: Lou (lou.catozzi@att.net) | Date: 2013-01-06 14:46:26 UTC
It's been a week now and I haven't seen any negative replies to the idea of recreating some of the classic Texas letterboxes so I thought I'd continue the process and seek out volunteers for carving new stamps. In my mind, to be a recreation, the new stamp needs to at least be based on the old image. I have images in my older logbook of Gordian Knot, Wraith of the Woods (slightly blurry), all four O'Knots, and Gunslinger. There are probably other classics that others would like to see recreated as well. I'd be happy to send photocopies of any images I have out to people who want to help out if they don't have the image themselves.

With TALE10 just around the corner it would make some sense to start with the O'Knot series. From the records in LbNA it appears that only Silver Eagle, Baby Bear, and I have recorded finding all 4 boxes but others may have also done so. BTW - these original four stamps were commercially custom-made for Eoghan and are made up of a lot of fine lines.

Speak up, who has the images and who would like to try to recarve them?
Personally, I'd like to recarve Gunslinger and possibly Gordian's Knot.

PI Joe
