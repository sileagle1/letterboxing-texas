One Room Schoolhouses in Gillespie County

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-12-07 21:53:05 UTC-08:00

It was a beautiful day and I finally completed Baby Bear's series in the Fredericksburg area.  Even though a few boxes might be missing, it is worth going to all schools just for the chance to experience these icons of TX education.  While in Fredericksburg I talked with a lady at the Welcome Center who attended two of the schools and it was fascinating to hear her describe the experience.  I asked her how the teacher was able to teach multiple grades in one room (grades 1-8) and she said the teacher would teach each grade separately for a set time while the rest of the class did something else.  Those teachers must have been amazing to handle 8 different levels of instruction every day!  

I highly recommend doing this series, and if you go in December you can see the Hill Country Christmas Lighting Trail at night.  The lights in Johnson City and Marble Falls are the best.
