Hi fellow letterboxers!

My trail name is b minor. (I used to be Butterfly B of the busybeesfamily, but I outgrew that!)

Now that I am in middle school and am a Cadette Girl Scout, I am starting to work on my Silver Award. This project will take me most of 3 years to complete and will provide a community service and make me more of a leader in my community. For my project I have decided to create a Letterboxing Badge for Girl Scouts in my council and to permanently place boxes at one of our Girl Scout camps for them to find.

My first step in my project will be to teach letterboxing to a group of Girl Scouts at our Spring Campout. The theme of our campout is Alice in Wonderland and the campout will be the second weekend in April. For this project, I am hoping to plant some Alice in Wonderland themed boxes at the camp we will be staying at. I think it would be great for my fellow Girl Scouts to get to see letterboxes from different carvers, and so I'm asking my fellow Texas Letterboxers for help.

Please consider contributing to my project and sending a complete letterbox with stamp and journal, ready to hide. I am looking for different sizes of boxes and different styles of carvings. All boxes should be Alice in Wonderland themed, please.

I will return the boxes to you if you like, otherwise, I can plant them in my own community north of Austin. I will need to receive the boxes by March 26th, so that I can plant them at the camp and write/print clues.

If you are willing to help me with my Silver Award Project, please email me at b_minor@busybeesfamily.com. I will send you my mailing address.

Thank you in advance for your support. I am excited to teach responsible letterboxing to Girl Scouts and improve our reputation in the letterboxing community.

Yours in Girl Scouting, b minor
