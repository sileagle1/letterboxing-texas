Dear Texas LBers,
A lot has happened in TX LBing since I last passed through
Austin a year ago. It's great to see. Thanks to all for the status
reports during the year. I've completed all necessary
maintenance on my Austin boxes and updated the clues. Here's
the update:
1. Mt Bonnell: new box, new stamp, very fat logbook. To those
who left notes on slips of paper, thanks, but now there's a stamp
and logbook for you. And I found a much bigger rock to hold the
box in the crevice during the torrential rains. So if you were one of
the disappointed boxers, please revisit this one. The view is
great!
2. Barton Creek Greenbelt: new, very fat logbook.
3. 360 Bridge: "LB alert" note to geocachers (so they won't
mistakenly take the stamp).
4. Gunslinger: fatter logbook
If anyone needs me to check an Austin LB for them, let me know
and I'll see what I can do. Got a little more time here. The Brown
Knight
