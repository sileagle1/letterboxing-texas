Gathering Photos

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-03-10 23:07:06 UTC-06:00

Mosaic Butterfly mentioned it took her awhile to figure out how to upload photos, so I thought someone else may be having a hard time too. Here's what to do:

Go to the group's homepage:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterboxingtexas

Look down the lefthand column to Photos and click.

Find the album you want to add photos to, or you can create your own photo album by clicking Create Album on the righthand top side of the page.

If you aren't adding an album, click the album you want to add photos to. At the top left of the page, you will see Add Photo.

Select files on your computer, and upload.

Hope this makes sense!
