Camping at Rusk--or not

From: Connie Terry (connie@lavernia.net) | Date: 2006-01-26 20:18:02 UTC-06:00

I put in for a tent camping spot at Rusk yesterday and was turned down.  Navigator has an acquaintance near Reklaw (about 12 miles away from Rusk) who he has approached about overflow camping on his place, and lucky for us Dave is amenable to the idea.  I have never been to Dave’s ranch, but Navigator has.  Here is part of a page Navigator has written for you if you are in the camping mode but can’t get a spot at the state park:

 

Camping sites are plentiful for tents and trailers.  Motor homes and heavy trailers are confined to the high and hard ground near the large hanger.

Unfortunately there are no hook-ups available but water and electricity are available in the main picnic/camping area.

Generators are welcome, but with the reminder to be courteous to your neighbor.

The restroom facilities are limited to porta-johns and there are no showers available at this time.

 

Sounds like this is we will be.  Perhaps a little more primitive than the state park, but I think much more peaceful and secluded.

 

Email me if you want more info.

Corazon
