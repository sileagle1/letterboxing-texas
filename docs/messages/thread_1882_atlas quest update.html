atlas quest update

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-05-13 16:04:50 UTC
With the latest update that Ryan did last night, you can now list the
exact address for a park or an intersection as the starting point for
your boxes. Then, when you click, show nearby boxes, the actual
nearest boxes will appear, rather than all the boxes in Houston, say.
Or, you click the mapquest link and a map to the park appears rather
than a general map of Houston.

I love this, and I think it will make it so much easier for us to box
in Houston. I will be updating all of my current atlas-quest hosted
boxes over the next week to include this option (although not the
mystery ones, obviously). The ones hosted on LBNA, I will probably at
some point switch over to atlasquest as time allows. I will leave
them linked to LBNA, switch the clue-host to AQ.

If you want to see how it works, you can try my Llawnmower Llookout
box. I only had time to do one this morning before the usual Saturday
ballgame rush.

Dewberry
