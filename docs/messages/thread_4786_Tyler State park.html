From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-05-14 08:00:29 UTC-05:00
I would like to add to that that it isn't difficult to get permitted at TSP. You'll have to navigate the form intended for geocaching (I just marked through the word "geocaching" and put "letterboxing", and where it wanted coordinates, I put my written clues).

When I got permitted there, I was permitting two existing boxes and placing a third. They were fine with me emailing the forms on the two, and after I checked in with them to tell them I was planting a box, they were cool with me emailing them the form on the new box. They told me that if there was a problem, I would hear from them. I never did.

Fearless Fox and I are tentatively scheduled to help teach an Outdoor Family class hosted by TPWD there at Tyler State Park the weekend of June 4. They highlight geocaching during this program, but I'm hoping to be able to show a little letterboxing as well. If you plant a box at TSP and don't want it shown during this class, please let me know.
