Maintenance- foliage reminder

From: monkeytoes108 (cruschhaupt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-01-03 18:46:29 UTC

With the recent visitors to my boxes, I tought I'd go check on
them and see what new stamps the logbooks have aquired. I was very
surprised to discover that the lovely green canopy over the Lost
Moai is totally barren in the winter months. This provided total
coverage in the warm months for both box and boxer. It just serves
as a reminder that a box hidden well in June, may be out in the open
in December.
...and there was a extra stamp pad included in there so if
someone's missing a black inkpad, I've got it.

I also had a few mysterious log-ins on the Duck Pond box after
Silver Eagle's visit. They stamped the logbook with the box's own
stamp and wrote little words of wisdom in the margins. If you're
out there, thanks for the visit.

-MonkeyToes
