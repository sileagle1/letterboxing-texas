San Felipe Boxes - Back in action

From: babybearlb2002 (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-04-16 21:37:44 UTC
Due to contruction at San Felipe SHS (and Boy Scouts that found my box and turned into the Ranger) my box (Old 300) and Silver Eagle's box (Father of Texas) have been moved to a new location (clues are posted for mine, and he will update soon). This is my oldest surviving original letterbox. It was placed in 2002. The stamp was in 3 pieces when I got back in the mail from the state, but was able to glue it back together. Hopefully it will live on in its new home in the San Felipe De Austin Cemetery (that's right IRA, another cemetery - Maybe you and Oonie can go on your honeymoon there and enjoy some history).

Baby Bear
LBNA of Cypress, Texas
