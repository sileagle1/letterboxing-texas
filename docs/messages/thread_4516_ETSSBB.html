From: hellbrunn (hellbrunn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-04-17 21:31:09 UTC
In order to prevent the murder of Baby Bears for food, concerned letterboxers have come together to form the East Texas Society for Saving Baby Bears. It has come to our attention that a certain ghost has taken to murdering innocent Baby Bears, using them as an ingredient in Bear Stew, and selling the stew for profit. This is outrageous, egregious, and all together in bad taste. We sit in disbelief and horror that these types of acts can take place, with no consequences.

The charter members convened today in Kilgore, TX. Pictures of this historic event will soon be posted.
