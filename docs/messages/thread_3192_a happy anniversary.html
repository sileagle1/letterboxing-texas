a happy anniversary

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-11-21 15:52:48 UTC-06:00
4 years ago today the kids and I went out to Brazos Bend and found our very first letterboxes, a pair by Silver Eagle and Baby Bear.  So today on the first day of the long awaited break, with no worries of carpool lines or lunches to be packed or homework to be signed, we loaded up and went back to Brazos.  We found Soccerchick's box on the very same trail as those first boxes four years ago, how cool is that?  I love this park, so close to Houston, but when you are there you feel like you are a million miles away.  It remains one of my all time favorite spots on earth.
 
I never imagined when I first went out with those clues in my hand, unsure of exactly what it was I was doing, how much I would come to love this hobby.  Now we have passed the 500 find mark, one of these days we will hit the 100 plant mark, and I love it more than ever.  I love to see the world through the eyes of all the other planters.   I love a good clue, and appreciate the work that goes into making a stamp.  One of these days I may succeed in my quest to write a puzzle to make a certain someone crazy.  I look forward to seeing the posts here and finding out what you all are up to.  Thank you to all the great letterboxers here in Texas for all the fun you have given me with every one of your boxes.  And a special thank you to SE and BB for all their plants, and for being so encouraging towards us when we first started out.  You have all given a gift that is most appreciated.  MO5
