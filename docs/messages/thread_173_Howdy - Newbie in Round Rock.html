From: Lou Catozzi (txhipowr@austin.rr.com) | Date: 2004-06-14 19:20:25 UTC
I just joined the list a week or so back so I don't know if that
qualifies for lurking or not! I am TxHiPowr from Round Rock (just
north of Austin) and I've been boxing since last September although I
have visited a couple boxes earlier that were also geocaches. This
past March I dropped in on the 1st Texas LB Gathering at Bastrop SP
and met quite a few of you although don't ask me to connect names with
faces!
My family and I just purchased a travel trailer this Spring and we
went back to Bastrop over Memorial Day weekend where I introduced by
three sons (4,8, & 10) to LB'ing. They like the gee-whiz hi-tech part
of geocaching but they also like the treasure-map and clues part of
boxing as well.
We leave for a three-week trip to Colorado on Friday so we should
get to see quite a few new stamps and caches on this trip.
Now, if I could just find a round tuit so I could start carving and
placing stamps!

Lou AKA
TxHiPowr
