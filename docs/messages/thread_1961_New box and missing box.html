Re: New box and missing box

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-30 21:29:28 UTC
I thought that was such a great little hiding spot, too! But you
never know who's going to wander around, particularly in a busy park
like that. I hope you'll replace Fire Tower, though, so I can find it!

I've had about 10 go missing (or be discovered missing) in the last
couple of months. Most of these won't be replaced, except for
possibly Little Stamp Mystery . I'm not all that interested in
recarving images, so instead I'll try to put my efforts into putting
new boxes out there.

Sometimes it's hard to know what happens to the boxes--vandals,
mischievious kids, bad rehides?!? I personally have ongoing troubles
with raccoons. . .I believe the ones in Memorial Park can sniff out
boxes. They seem to like to find them and drag them out of their
hiding places.

When we go on our vacation at the end of next month, I'll have the
chance to check our box in Abita Springs, Louisiana. It survived
hurricane Katrina, although there was extensive damage to the park.
But somehow, the smaller tree it was hidden beneath survived and so
did the box.

DB
