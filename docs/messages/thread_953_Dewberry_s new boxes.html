Did you all tour the turtle facility?  Isn't it great?  We thought about putting a box there last summer, but as you say, it is very un-box-friendly.
 
We also placed a new box in Galveston today, and ours is a tribute as well, but not to anything as majestic as a sea turtle.  Our is a tribute to all things silly, and like you, I will post clues tomorrow.  I'm exhausted from carrying the toddlers back and forth across the most enormous mounds of seaweed I have ever seen.  There must have been at least 8 feet of it between the sand and the ocean all the way up and down the beach.  Good crab catching, though.
 
Looks like everyone better plan a trip to the Gulf for next weekend!  -Mother of Five
