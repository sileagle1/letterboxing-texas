Hey ya'll! Perky P here of The Rodgers 7! I miss my Texas Letterboxing family so much!! It's been slow going here in New Mexico. In my attempt to try to get the Letterboxing community here like it is in Texas, I decided to follow the great Silver Eagle and Baby Bear's lead and create The First Annual New Mexico Letterboxing Event. :) It's this next weekend coming up. I decided to tell ya'll about it just in case any of you would like to come. I talk about you so much to my New Mexico friends. I know they would LOVE to meet you! It's at Aguirre Springs Campground in Las Cruces. There is an event sign up on Atlas Quest. I know it's kinda far for ya'll, but just thought I'd let you know you are invited and I'd love to have you come. :)
