From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-12-05 16:18:23 UTC
There are all different sizes. I have one on my desk that is 2 inches
tall, 5.75 inches long and 4.25 inches wide. it is a great height for
a museum logbook and stamp. There is little wasted space, but it
could also hold a film container-sized HH.

I am replacing my little stamp mystery. The box I am using is also 2
inches high, 4 inches long and 3.25 inches wide. It is replacing an
altoids tin which I adored for its compact profile, but when I went to
check on it, had rusted so that it was too hard to open (dry on the
inside, though). I loved the low profile of the altoids-type tin, but
it just didn't work in the field. For this particular box, about 1.5
inches tall would be the perfect size. The other dimensions are perfect.
