From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-11-26 20:47:59 UTC-06:00
Since I'm not very good at doing mail order stuff, I order my carving
material for a year at a time. I wound up stuck with orange PZKut
last year, but this year when I saw that Stampeaz had white grade B
again, I ordered right away!

Waiting for me when I got home was my order - one whole year's worth
of white - WHITE - PZKut - the old consistency that I like so well.

There's nothing like walking by the kitchen table to see that stack of
block waiting!
