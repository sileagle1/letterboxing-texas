Brazos Bend State Park Update

From: (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2018-05-03 21:53:45 UTC

On Monday, I spent the day looking for all the letterboxes planted at Brazos Bend State Park since 2015.  Of the 31 I looked for, I found three.  Open Space had told me that she expected Hurricane Harvey to have swept them all away, and she was right.  Even my "Please Pet the Alligator" box was swept away, and it had been planted in a tree, at least 5 feet up from the ground.  I found two of Open Space's "Zombie Apocalypse" boxes, and "BFF - Pooh & Piglet", by Yertle.  Quite often I found a lovely white rock or two, right where the clue said they'd be, but no box in sight.  Open Space's two boxes had been washed out in the open, near where they had been planted, and I re-hid them.  Time to re-plant that park, perhaps.  -- Bell Motel
