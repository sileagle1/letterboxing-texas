Re: Event News?

From: (mjrekedal@gmail.com) | Date: 2014-10-28 06:48:09 UTC-07:00

You are right on the first count, Silver Eagle. We had so much fun and did soooo much boxing and trekking the triails, that we are pooped! My legs are still protesting. The event was a huge success, IMHO. Open Space did a stupendous job organizing, soliciting stamps from across the country, planting boxes and series two weeks prior to the event (in pouring rain, I might add), providing clue booklets, topping it off with several surprise (ok, sneaky) ways to obtain clues. Attendance was small as several had to cancel at last minute, but enthusiasm and interaction made up for the small numbers. Food was over the top delicious with many different contributions. And the weather was over the top perfect! Hats off to Open Space and her helpers--Wry Me, Mosaic Butterfly, Corazon, NLW. Great job!
