That's all I can stands; I can't stands no more!

From: Nick Bottom (gryzzgryph@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-01-02 18:04:49 UTC-08:00

    While walking my dogs and dodging tornados a few days ago, I was once again assaulted by that menace of the roadways who drives the Texas Tour Bus. This being the sixth time that I have had to take evasive action to avoid becoming yet another statistic due to this maniac, I determined to take care of the matter once and for all. To that end, I commandeered the Bus, and, having subdued the driver, transported the lot to my lair. In order to insure that the driver cannot find his way back too easily, I intend to take the Bus to a county where no letterbox has ever been planted -- a county that is not even contiguous to any other county in which a letterbox has ever been planted. I would have done so already, but Earl wanted me to wait until the Bowl games were over, and when the Tyler Rose talks, I listen. In any case, once I succeed in relocating the Bus, I will so advise you. And until it is found again, I can walk the trails without having to look over my shoulder. What? There's a SECOND Bus? Oh, dear.                                                                                                Best regards,                           Gryzzled Gryphon of Coryell County
