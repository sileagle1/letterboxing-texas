When we first found out about letterboxing, we went and checked the
letterboxing.org website and there were just a couple in the state of
Texas, but none anywhere near Houston. Since we had a new baby, I
just filed that away as something to do if I we ever made it to
Connecticut or Oregon...we surely didn't take the initiative and start
planting the way BB & SE did here. Several years later, we were
planning a vacation to Colorado and I remembered letterboxing, so I
checked the website to see if there were any there. Imagine my
surprise when I realized there were some letterboxes within a few
miles of my house! We went right out the next day and found our first.

Our first find was Houston Texans (since retired) by Baby Bear. Next
up were a number of the Indian Tribes by happy days as we made our way
through OK to Colorado. We realized right away how much fun we would
have finding new places and seeing new things. I didn't realize how
much fun it would be to carve, plant, and meet other letterboxers.
That all came later.
