Lost Live Oaks of Galveston

From: DeDe (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-11-01 16:09:21 UTC
During the Hurricane of 2008, the Live Oaks of Galveston were destroyed by the sea water. A group of home owners commissioned three Chainsaw Artist to turn these into sculptures. They are amazing, and I have posted a few pictures of them. You can get a brochure at the Visitors Center now located at Ashton Villa, 2328 Broadway. The brochure gives you the address' of all of the sculptures. It is well worth your time to check these out.  Just wanted to share how they have preserved these great trees and are sharing them with others.

Blue Butterfly
