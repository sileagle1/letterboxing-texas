From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-07-14 19:00:59 UTC-05:00
I am of the "less is more" school of thought on this one.  My favorite memories when I look through my logbook are one or two finds per outing, with lots of time for exploring.  Of course, this could have a lot to do with the fact that I would have a mutiny on my hands if I tried to push for finding more boxes each trip.  Usually discovering a quiet little place is reward enough.  That and the sonic milkshakes that must accompany every outing, that is.
 
That being said, I love a good series (hint, hint Boots...just in case you have something in mind!) so I can't really say that I never find more than a box or two at a time.  But if I were in an unfamiliar place, I would definitely look for just a box or two and enjoy the location the finder wanted to show me.  MO5
