From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2010-08-16 22:32:20 UTC-05:00
I just looked on AtlasQuest, under the "People" pull-down menu, choosing "Find Letterboxer", and typing in "Runs With Scissors".  That will only find you names that have been registered with AtlasQuest.  The "Runs With Scissors" on AQ does not have any info posted.
On the LBNA home page, you can click on "Members", and search for a name. You can also click on "Search for Boxes", and then search by "Placer".  I found a "Runs with Scissors" on that site, too, but no boxes placed by that person.
One of my other favorite trail names is "Sewsewbizzy".  I found an amazing carve by her called "Shenandoah Shadow", planted in Rusk,TX by dewberry.
