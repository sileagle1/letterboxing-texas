Locked car unlocked

From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2010-03-09 13:25:20 UTC-06:00
For those of you who know we locked our electronic keys (both of them) in Whiz Gee's new Corvette on Saturday night, we got back into it yesterday just fine.  Huge, huge hugs and thanks to Cancan, who drove us to the B&B in Luling Saturday night, drove back out to pick us up Sunday morning, waited while the locksmith failed to break in to the Corvette, and drove us all the way back to Austin to the hospital to visit Lucy Locket and Blue Butterfly.  And more thanks to Bloomin' Gramma Jo, who drove us from the hospital the rest of the way to our home.  Whiz Gee got up early Monday, and had a key made for the secret Corvette keyhole, and we drove back to Palmetto State Park.  We were expecting alarm noise when he inserted the key, but the car quietly let us back in.  The park staff graciously did not charge us any more fees.  We found the last four letterboxes that we couldn't get to before dark on Saturday, and are now home safe and sound, with all our cars and belongings.  Today Whiz Gee will activate OnStar!
We are blessed to belong to this wonderful family of friends, who do not hesitate to go out of their way to help each other, even when we do not know each other's names!  We had a wonderful time this weekend, and add our thanks to Silver Eagle and Baby Bear for hosting this great event!  -- Bell Motel
