The Madness Is Over

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2017-03-27 16:49:12 UTC

Another TALE has come and gone and it was fun seeing everyone again, plus meeting some new folks from out of state.  From Alabama we had ST-Ranger (not a stranger anymore) and Knotty Lady (if she was naughty I didn’t see it), from California there was Dark Zen (proud owner of a buffalo plaque) and Evil Cow Pie (loved the apron), and from Ohio there was Acorns4U (too bad you had to leave so soon).  I hope y’all had fun and come back again, especially for Live & Breathe XX in 2018.  We also had a birthday girl, Narnian, and I hope she enjoyed the party we put on just for her.

I was really pleased with how well the park had recovered from hurricane damage and we really lucked out on the weather, surviving a storm the night before the event.  I was a little worried when I saw all the trails closed the next morning, but by the time we starting boxing most were open again.  There were some reports of snake sightings but thankfully no bites.  The raffle went well, mainly because I didn’t get any “surprise” gifts this year, and I hope everyone had a good time even if they were embarrassed like Pink Cookie.  I have uploaded pictures for TALE 14 and the pre-TALE dinner event on AQ, so please add any you have.


Special thanks to Echo & Stiches for organizing the dinner at Catfish Cabin and the night boxing.  Thanks also to everyone for continuing to come and for helping out.  We really are like a big family and I look forward to these “annual reunions”.  See you next year, if not sooner!
