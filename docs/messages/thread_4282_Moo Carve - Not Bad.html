Moo Carve - Not Bad

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2009-11-09 03:00:32 UTC
I went ahead and bought a small sample (2.5"x2.5"x.75")of this new carving medium and tried it out tonight. With a 40% off coupon it was about $1.30 at Hobby Lobby. It carves about like Master Carve but might be a little more crumbly. It comes in bigger sizes and is thicker than Master Carve, but is cheaper. The Pink stuff is cheaper than both and is much thinner, so I still prefer it. Anyway, it might be worth a try if you can find it, especially if you plan to carve a cow :)
