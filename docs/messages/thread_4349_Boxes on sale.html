>Speaking of size, what is a good size for a logbook?

I use a relatively small logbook. It's about 4x5. I
didn't choose it because of the size but because it has
nice, thick paper that won't bleed through. An unexpected
"benefit" of a small logbook is that it fills up quickly.
I think I am on logbook #8, and that makes me feel
important!
