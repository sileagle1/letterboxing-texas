Just a Warning

From: Amy (aymztx@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-06-02 22:08:03 UTC
Yes I know it's been forever since I've been around, but I'm still alive and well! Unlike the majority of letterboxers, I tend to hibernate in the colder months and come out during the hotter ones!

Anyhow, I just thought I'd warn you that we are heading down to the Spring/the Woodlands area this weekend and plan to do some letterboxing and geocaching. I noticed the majority of the letterboxes in the area are at the Mercer Arboretum. Looks like there are enough there to warrant a visit.

If there are any other really good ones we can't miss there (or on the way down I-45 from DFW area) please let us know! I'm looking forward to getting some new stamps for my book.

aymztx
