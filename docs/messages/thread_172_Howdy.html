Hi, everyone. I'm new(ish) to the list and wanted to introduce
myself. I started Letterboxing back in April after the NPR story,
but had a long haitus where the weather wouldn't cooperate with my
days off and, when it did, my free time was spoken for.

I'm Gingercat and I live in Austin. I've gotten my husband, my
mother, my brother and his fiance interested in the hobby as well.
This past weekend, in honor of my brother's birthday, we went
letterboxing in Bastrop.
What a goldmine!
I just wanted to let everyone know that we found:
Trail Rider
Skinny Pine (with Hitch Hiker, Roarin' Roadster, I'll plant next
outing...Austin area?)
Cool Cat
Knots #1
Poison Ivy
Lost Loblolly
Houston Toad
Lone Star Letterboxers

We had a wonderful time and the weather, although a bit muggy, was
peachie.

Thanks for making Bastrop State Park such a letterbox wonderland,
everyone!
