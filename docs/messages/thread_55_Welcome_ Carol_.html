From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-22 09:28:06 UTC-08:00
Hi Carol,

I've been really busy this week so I haven't had a
chance to do more than the bare minimum on this list,
so I was glad to see you had joined up with Susan's
encouragement. We're really glad to see new folks
coming in to Texas letterboxing!

Have you had an opportunity to learn more about the
gathering? It's March 6th at Bastrop State Park.
Several of us are camping there for the weekend.
Silver Eagle has graciously organized it and is
bringing chili. BabyBear is carving the event stamp.
Everyone else is pitching in as they can. Hope you
can be there!

Take it easy and relax about figuring out what you'll
design for your own stamp. It'll come to you,
probably like one of those big "What took me so long
to think of that?" kind of moments! Although I've
carved my personal stamp and a couple of box stamps, I
go out to my workbench and just carve for practice and
in hopes of inspiration two or three times a week.
Right now I'm working on a non-letterboxing stamp as a
gift for my daughter's drill team director. We have a
few letterbox ideas, but getting the stamp idea is
giving me grief! I figure if I'm out there carving
and piddling around with the knives, something will
hit me.

Anyway, glad to have you!
Lucy
