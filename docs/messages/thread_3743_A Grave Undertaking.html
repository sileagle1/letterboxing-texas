Ok, so everyone knows about the City Cemetery Event that we had, well
all of the headstones in that cemetery have been transcribed and
typed. However, before I submit any of that list to the people who
need it (City Hall, Library, Cherokee County Historical Society, and
publish it on the Cemeteries of Cherokee County website) I really need
some help in proof-reading the typed list to catch my mistakes. So,
since so many people were willing to come and help transcribe, I am
reaching out again to ask people to come and help proof-read. So, A
Grave Undertaking Event (thanks to GG for the name of this event) has
been born. It will take place on Saturday, August 30th at 8 am till
whenever at the City Cemetery in Jacksonville. It is listed on AQ, so
please sign up. I hope to see you there...by the way, most of you
have heard of Wag Time, and some think she is simply a sock puppet,
since most of you have not met her...however, she is supposed to be at
this event (GG, this is mostly directed at you). Also, if GG shows
up, you can hear about his run-in with the local law enforcement.
