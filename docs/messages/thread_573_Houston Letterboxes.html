Just wanted to write a quick thank you to all the creators of Houston area boxes – Stillwater & I made a spur-of-the-moment trip down this past weekend and had a wonderful time! Thanks to Hawk, TeamKing, BabyBear, Silver Eagle & Tiger – it was such fun to find such a variety of boxes with wide-ranging types of clues and locations! We had hoped to cover the whole city, but due to a very late start Saturday, ended up with only 2 days instead of 3 to box – so we’ll have to make a return trip. On Monday, we spent the day at Mercer Arboretum, and thoroughly enjoyed ourselves in the chilly air and sunshine.

Can’t wait to get back down to Houston – maybe on our way to or from the Brazos Bend round-up in March!

Thanks again,                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            Ruby Tuesday
