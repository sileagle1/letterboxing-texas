Big Thanks to LSQ and BT

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-08-18 09:25:11 UTC-05:00

I would like to take a moment to publicly thank Lone Star Quilter and BootsTex for their efforts on their letterboxing jaunt through my area earlier this week.   In addition to finding quite a few boxes in a relatively few number of days, they also went above and beyond what they "had" to do in at least three cases:

 

1)       They called me to let me know my box had been exposed and emptied, but that they had repackaged and rehidden it.  They let me know so I could check on it, since apparently it is in a high traffic area and subject to easy discovery (and it was!)

2)       They found JBK's Rhett and Scarlett in trouble and attempted to dry out the logbook, then returned it to its box and added a new logbook. 

3)       They found my box, My Heart Belongs in Texas, with a hole in the container, although thankfully dry, and they replaced the container for me.

 

Their efforts demonstrate to all of us that we can get by with simply posting a status message for a placer, and that's good enough, but to be great boxers we do what we can to fix a problem when and where we find it!

 

Thanks BootsTex and Lone Star Quilter y'all are awesome and appreciated!

 

Lucy

 

P.S.  I have to confess that as I was packing my letterboxing backpack last night to go to Denton, I took out my extra containers thinking they would be too hot and too bulky to carry around and I had never had to replace an actual container before.   After getting the message this morning regarding My Heart Belongs in Texas, I slipped them back in.
