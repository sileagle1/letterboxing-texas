Hunt County has 8 new boxes!

From: Tara Burton (mselly13@juno.com) | Date: 2008-06-23 19:43:22 UTC
Hey y'all. Just wanted to let everyone know that Hunt County now has
8 new boxes!
We finally decided to try our hands at carving so be warned these are
the first carves of two idiots. And now there are actually some boxes
in this area.
We still have one more to plant but plan on planting it down in
Henderson County this coming weekend.
But looking forward to having these found!
Hope to see you on the trail.
They are all listed on both AQ and LBNA.
They are in Royse City, Quinlan and Greenville.
If you are coming to the area and want to contact us ahead of time, we
would be thrilled at meeting up with others.

The Duck Ducks
tara
