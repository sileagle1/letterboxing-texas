From: Bonnie Lind (bonnielind1@hotmail.com) | Date: 2013-01-28 18:30:50 UTC-06:00
While Silver Eagle was having his misadventures, I was in Bastrop and have some updates for you. I had dinner at the Texas Grill Restaurant and picked up a menu (attached). The offerings are vast and delicious. They offer breakfast all day long.  We have the back room reserved.  It can be partitioned off if we get too loud.  :-D

 

To get there from Buescher State Park, take Hwy 71 NW towards Bastrop. In about 10 miles you will come to a new giant Buckee’s (yeah, I know—cheap gas!) on your right.  Continue straight on Hwy 71 for about one more mile, crossing the mighty Colorado River and take the second exit: Hwy 150 East, Childers St, Hasler Blvd.  There is a Sonic on your right and the Texas Grill is next to the Sonic.

 

Please sign up if you can join us for dinner and delete your name if you change your plans.  We’d like to be able to give the restaurant a close count.  http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2658

 

Thanks,
Mosaic Butterfly
