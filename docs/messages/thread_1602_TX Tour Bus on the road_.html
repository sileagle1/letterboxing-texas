The Texas Tour Bus is back on the road again! 

 

Texas Tour Bus

Planted by Viewfinder and PuddleSplasher in Rockledge Park on the north shore of Lake Grapevine.

 

Clues:   

If you’ve found The Lost Moose and The White Sail, you know the way.

If not, those clues are on AQ and LBNA.

 

Find the Moose and then the White Sail.

Then continue a little farther on the trail.

 

When the bridge comes in sight,

Look for a trail going uphill on the right.

 

(You may see a red X marked there on a tree –

But we didn’t do it!  We’re both LNT!)

 

Don’t be a wuss – no need to cuss!

Just 25 steps uphill and you’re right at the bus!

-- On the right, behind that dead log, under some brush.

 

Take the bus back to the bench near the White Sail and rest,

Then retrace your steps – don’t miss the Moose on your way past. 

(We know you were racing to get the bus first!)

 

Difficulty:  moderate, about 20-30 minutes one-way to letterbox

Distance:  approximately 1 mile to box on dirt hike/bike trail through woods and along ledge above lakeshore. 

Caution:   be alert for bikes! Not stroller-friendly (trail is sandy and rocky in places).   You may want to wear a large backpack to carry the bus out once you’ve found it!

 

-- Viewfinder and PuddleSplasher
