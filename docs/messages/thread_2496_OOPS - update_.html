From: Mike - KD9KC (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2007-01-12 01:22:49 UTC

A week or so ago I mentioned that I was seeing magellan roadmate 700 GPS
units selling out cheap. Even thought it is a french company - I held my
nose and bought one. A big display at a reasonable price - I was dazzled by
that BIG display!

WHAT A P.O.S!!!!!

I have owned it about 24 hours now. It arrived - BROKE. Everything worked
- except one thing. It would not initialize and find satellites. Just no
way! I let it sit on my patio for an hour. No satellite lock. It sat on
my window sill overnight. No satellite lock.

Well - that can happen. OK. The killer was trying to deal with magellan
support.

On my fourth call to them I told them to close my trouble ticket. The lady
asked if I got it working. I said no - the store that sold it to me agreed
to let me send it back and trade it in - for a GARMIN! She was not amused.

Yeah - it will cost me more. But I will have something that will WORK!

If any of you were looking at the magellan deal, buyer beware!

For me - NEVER AGAIN.
