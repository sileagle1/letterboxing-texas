LBing adventures in the rain

From: tx2chic (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-05-29 01:48:50 UTC
It rained all day, but that didn't stop us from driving west of FW
toward Possum Kingdom to do a little letterboxing, hoping for a break
in the rain long enough to stamp&log. Lucky us! Rain stopped briefly
each time we reached a letterbox search site and the weather was nicely
cool for walking and exploring.
Found Celtic Lions' Tejas Caddo Cemetery (surprised a little field
mouse sitting next to the letterbox, or it surprised me!), enjoyed
exploring the interesting old cemetery.
Found Celtic Lions' Possum Kingdom, after a great little hike uphill
through the damp, good-smelling cedar woods (two great stamps of
course, carved by Cactus Licker). Saw a roadrunner, several deer, and
flock of wild turkeys in the area.
Detoured through Granbury - fidn't have Kantexan's LB clues with us -
big mistake! but had Baby Bear's clues for the Crockett Family at Acton
Cemetery, which we found in a clever hidey-place in another lovely old
cemetery. Now we'll have a good excuse for another pleasant drive in
the country to Granbury for those other two LB's we missed today.

Impossible Triangle -- MISSING

Looks like I'll be replacing boxes before I add more
new ones. When they're up and going again, I'll let
everyone know.
