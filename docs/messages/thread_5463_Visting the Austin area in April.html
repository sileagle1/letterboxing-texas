Good morning Texas letterboxers!!  I am not too familiar with Yahoo groups,so if this doesn't post correctly please let me know.  Crocker and I (Apple girl) are going to be visiting Austin April 11-21st.  We are avid letterboxers in Northern and Central New York.  We will be busy with family obligations on the weekends, but should have time during the week if anyone wants to have a mini-meet, stamp exchange with us.  We are staying with my nephew in the Southern end of Austin ( so he tells me), if that is helpful at thinking up a meeting place.  We have been studying Atlas Quest and have a good selection of boxes to look for in the Austin area ( we only expanded 15 miles in our search).  We like to box, shop, sightsee and try new restaurants  etc so we didn't pick any long hikes.  If you know any MUST DO boxes that are not too far outside that  area, I would appreciate a heads up.  We are also planning to plant a letterbox, so there will be at least one new one for  you to find.  


ps. we live in the "snow belt" area near Lake Ontario- currently 3 feet of snow on the ground and minus 7 degrees this morning.  I attached a picture of the igloo ( my house) so you will take pity on us!!   We can't wait to get to Austin!!
