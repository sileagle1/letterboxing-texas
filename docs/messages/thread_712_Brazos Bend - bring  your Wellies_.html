From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-03-17 09:07:40 UTC-06:00

There is at least one letterboxing team who will understand that subject line - but for everybody else;-):

 

The Gentleman & Tenderfoot called from Brazos last night to say it was absolutely great - but very muddy. As they were talking, the sun was setting over the water, and they raved about how different Brazos is from other Texas parks and how much they’re enjoying the wildlife, the scenery and so on. But they asked me to write a quick note to try to catch incoming letterboxers – apparently the trails are very, very wet and swampy.

Of course they could dry out a bit by Saturday – let’s cross our fingers!

 

(Not that wet trails will dampen our enthusiasm one bit!)

 

Ruby Tuesday
