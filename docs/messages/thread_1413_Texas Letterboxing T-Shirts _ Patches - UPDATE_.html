From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-10-26 11:39:05 UTC-05:00
Some of you received shirts/patches ages ago - you guys go ahead and delete
this email!!!

For the rest of you - I'm very tempted to just write a one-line
all-encompassing excuse for the shirts & patches taking so long to
send...something like "the dog ate my homework"!

And actually one of the reasons is very close - Vandal, in a fit of temper
when we left him home instead of taking him to San Francisco (he always
assumes he is missing a letterboxing adventure)actually did chew up the
exposed ends of all the extra-large Priority envelopes I'd purchased...which
I didn't discover until today.

BUT - that is just the latest in a long line of Murphy's Law reasons! The
T-shirts are still giving me headaches - as I was sorting sizes I realized
we were given wrong colors in wrong sizes. A couple of weeks later, we
finally had correct assortment.
THEN I noticed that a few shirts had tiny holes - one had an undone seam.
Each discovery has ended up in another phone call & a trip to Plano!
One shirt was apparently a different dye lot - I noticed it ages ago, but
forgot to follow up on it - the lovely recipients of that shirt have glossed
over that as if it were a bonus!
I have had two shirts on order for about 2 1/2 months - each time I call,
"they are going to be here by the end of the week".

I'll spare you guys the rest of the details - but admittedly part of the
problem has been our recent trips, because I haven't stayed on top of the
vendor each week.

FRYES, JOY, BALFOURS, REBEL - I'm mailing yours out today and I apologize
for the wait!

GRAMMYPAT & SEMALEE - I'm still waiting on one shirt each for you- I spoke
to the vendor again yesterday and was as forceful as I am capable of
being;-) - we'll see if it works! (If you would like me to send you a
partial order, please let me know)

Needless to say, I can't recommend that we use this company again- I'm sorry
because I like to give small businesses a chance, the owner is delightful,
and the shirts are very comfy and reasonably-priced. But this is becoming
waaaaay too time-consuming - I'd rather be boxing!

Ruby Tuesday
PS - The patches order from a totally different company was delivered in
good condition and practically overnight (I think it was 2 days)
