Checked missing spots? this morning and the box was still in its
hiding spot. Problem: since we last checked on them 10 days ago,
what was a nice little empty nook has been invaded by HUNDREDS of fire
ants--probably washed down there from the rains. Needless to say, our
dogs were feeling a little uncomfortable and were happy to be moved to
a new, currently ant-less residence not very far away.

Lesson, one we all keep learning over and over: amazing how quickly
hiding conditions can change.

We hid another mystery box this weekend, called The World's Smallest
Letterbox. Depending on how the placer lists mystery boxes, they may
or may not come up on the LBNA Texas regions. For this one, to see
the listing, you'd have to click Texas Mystery.
