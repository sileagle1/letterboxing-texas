From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-15 00:36:22 UTC
Hey All,
Wow, everytime I go out of town a bunch more new boxers pop up on
this talklist. That's fantastic! Do I smell new boxes in the
making?

I had a delightful time boxing in McAllister Park in San Antonio
last Friday. I found Silver Eagle's Lone Star Beer, Baby Bears
Peanuts, and Scoutdogs three boxes-Dancers, Cowboy, and Longhorn. I
also discovered that stinging nettles are no fun!!!!!! :0 You'd
think I would learn to wear pants instead of shorts and sandals out
boxing. It was so neat, though to find five boxes all in good shape
on the same trail.

Of course I also had to take the little Samurai Stampers to Sea
World and the cheesy tourist traps across the street from the
Alamo! I found it amusing that you could find the symbol of the
Shrine of Texas Liberty on a shot glass and a snowglobe! If Day
Crockett were alive
today............................................!?

On an unrelated side note I found a great bumper sticker the other
day that I promptly put on my car because I thought it so appropo to
LB. It was a quote from Tolkien:

"Not all who wander are lost"

Keep on Boxin'
-Kantexan
