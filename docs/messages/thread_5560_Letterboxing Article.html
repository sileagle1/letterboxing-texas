Letterboxing Article

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2016-05-19 06:21:37 UTC-07:00

Back in January I was contacted by a student at Truman University in Missouri to do an interview about letterboxing for a local magazine called Detours.  After realizing he was going to write the article whether I participated or not, I decided to do it.  The article has finally been published and it is not bad, even if there are several typos and some erroneous information.  He also asked for pictures, so I provided some that are included from a few of my trips.  He also insisted on using my real name, which I was reluctant to do, and it seems strange to see it in print.  Anyway, if you have some free time you can read it at:

http://tmn.truman.edu/detours/detours-magazine/

The letterboxing article is on pages 14-19, and there is a link to another side article at the end:

http://tmn.truman.edu/detours/2016/04/04/letterboxing-101/

 

Silver Eagle
