From: Ashby (ashby@ic.org) | Date: 2006-10-20 16:31:59 UTC-07:00
just wanted to share one more thought about the
training puppies and kids topic. i have actually seen
this book, and even read the whole book myself, called
The Dog Trainers Guide to Parenting (or some sort of
title like that, forgive me if I did not get it
exactly right) and guess what? it was a great little
book, with the main emphasis being on small positive
steps being made to shape behavior towards the
positive end goal, and with the importance of
consistency being stressed. it was funny and well
done, a book i'd actually recommend, and not at all
condescending or mean towards kids or parents.
Rather, the goal was helping people have another set
of tools they might find useful for helping their kids
turn out to be lovely people. really, there are a lot
of similarities. in fact, we used to call my son "the
puppy" because he was so much like having a puppy,
crawling on all fours and biting our ankles!
(probably why he has turned out the way he has!) : )
