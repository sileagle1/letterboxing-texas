Linda,

Many of us have non-letterboxing spouses. Ruby
Tuesday has possibly the best non-boxing spouse,
Stillwater, of all of us - he is very good-natured
about keeping up with her and indulgent in her whims
and he reaches tall boxes and even apparently wound up
in the ER recently with a dose of poison ivy after one
of their outings.

Ruby coined the phrase, LSS, for those non-boxing
spouses who smile in spite of all we ask them to do
and never complain about having to go along on our
ventures. LSS stands for Long-Suffering Spouse and
when I hear the term it give me warm fuzzies because
these are folks who could stay home and fuss about us
doing what we do, but they go along in spite of the
elements, the environment, and everything else.

My own LSS, Fearless Fox, is great too - although I
suspect he's actually come over to our side of the
fence. He hasn't yet popped up in the morning and
said "Let's go letterboxing", but I think the moment
may be at hand soon. But if not, that's okay, because
if I jump up and say it, he'll be gladly loading the
car before I know it!
