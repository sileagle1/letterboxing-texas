From: janet clark (jayceeinbedford@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-09-11 21:33:24 UTC-07:00
I was just telling my mom that I wanted to make a trip out there for the Fall (pumpkin patch). The only time I went to East Texas was the weekend after 4th of July (several years ago). We went to Gilmer for my DH's family reunion. I have never been so miserably hot in my entire life. I now what Hell is going to feel like. The only air conditioned place available was where all the older generations were hanging out. I used every excuse to go to the store that I could find.
 
I would love to see the country in better weather. I did love the drive there and home (the ac'd car helped). We stopped at several roadside stands on the way home.
 
I do hope y'all realize that my comment about tumbleweeds and cows was tongue in cheek though. I believe in the old saying, "If you can't say something nice, then don't say anything at all." I loved all the photos chosen and would love to see the ones that were submitted. I think photography is a beautiful art form and enjoy viewing other people's pictures. It's like looking through someone elses eyes and seeing in a completely different way than I might see for myself.
 
I'll probably end up buying all three calendars and hang one in the kitchen, one in the office and one if Cassie's or my room.
 
Janet, BF
