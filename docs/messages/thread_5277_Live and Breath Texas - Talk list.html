Ok...here is the event I listed as best as I remember it for your reading pleasure. Enjoy!

We Live to Breathe XX:Everything's Bigger in Texas

Date:Aug 21-28, 2015
Address: 211 Adult Center, Uncertain, TX

Admins(Hosts): Baby Bear has TX Pride, Silver Eagle Rules

Event Description:

Well, as you all know, Texas won the vote to host L&B XX. Now you might think, "That's a fer piece", but we know that even though it may be far away from you, you will make every effort to be there. Now, it is the summertime, and it is hot down here. So, we are thinking of rigging up a sprinkler system along the trails to help cool you off while you box. Just remember to bring your waterproof logbooks and non-water-soluble inkpads with you. If the sprinkler system doesn't work out, we will put inflatable kiddie pools at each letterbox so that you can lie in it while you log-in. And we will have a first-aid station in case anyone is sensitive to the heat, or allergic to snakes, or tumbleweeds.
The theme is Everything's Bigger in Texas -cuz it is! So, if you'd like to carve a stamp for the event, feel free to do so. The possibilities are endless - cuz everthing is bigger in Texas. Oh, and the rumors are true! We decided to do it for a whole week... cuz everything we do is also bigger in Texas. No measley three and a half day events here- that's just too easy (no offense to you MA folks). Some of the nightly events we are thinking of having include: a hoedown, a Texas Hold-'em competition, clown rodeo, road trip to Schlitterbahn, and a Willie Nelson karaoke contest. We are open to any suggestions you might have, so if you think of anything...feel free to contact us. No idea is too small. There are still more details to come. We hope to have the event details hammered out by the end of the year because, yes, we are that good....so we will keep you posted as things pop up. We look forward to the event and meeting everyone, new friends and old, cuz some of you we've howdied but we ain't shook yet.

ok-that's about all I could remember...it's pretty much what I wrote. I just wish you would've seen it originally...

fun facts about my fake event:
1. I had about 20 people signed up for it (inc open spaces and mark and sue pepe)
2. I created fantastical alter-egos for you two which still exist-so you can at least check those out. Baby bears I spent the most time on since he was the instigator in all this craziness...but silver eagle rules is not too shabby either. So, don't forget to check that out!
3. Didn't realize you all loved willie nelson so much!
