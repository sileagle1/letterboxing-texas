From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2011-06-18 20:19:41 UTC-05:00

Hey friends,

We are going to Alaska later this summer and I’m working on some stamps to plant there.  It crossed my mind that others might want to have a box there also.  I’d be more than happy to take any stamps or boxes you may want to donate.  Maybe we could do a series of boxes from Texas letterboxers if there is enough interest?  Anyway, let me know if anyone is interested and what ideas you may have.   Here is one idea…I was thinking of putting self addressed post cards in a box so visitors can mail me a card with their signature stamp on it. 

 

We are leaving at the end of July so you have 5 weeks or so.  We will be visiting Anchorage, Seward, and Juneau.  We will have a car and have plenty of time for boxing between the activities we have planned!
