Hospital box roundup

From: queenbeelovedx3 (queenbeelovedx3@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-20 16:25:59 UTC
I have it on my heart to organize a special letterbox series for the
patients at a major children's hospital in Fort Worth, Tx. My LBing
partner (the peas) and I have met with the events coordinator and she
is thrilled. She tells me that there is a lull in activities in the
summer months so the timing is right on. She also informs me that the
children have a really hard time adjusting once they are in
outpatient. Many are no longer able to participate in activities they
once enjoyed and the community at large doesn't understand the life
changing aspects of their experiences. Letterboxing could serve as a
link to the outside world. Something fun they were introduced to
during treatment and an activity they could continue with after they
go home whatever state/region that may be.

I plan on planting around 30 boxes within and around the hospital
some will even be carried by staff and will be accessed using secret
code words :) The staff will be teaching the children about the
letterboxes and handing out the clues. They might even hold some
craft days to create their signature stamps for the older kids and
there was some talk about possibly expanding this to a special summer
hospital camp. These boxes will NOT be open to the public and the
clues will NOT be posted outside of the hospital. I will post the
letterbox so the families can log their finds and contact the box
creator but NO clues will be given.

I am asking for anyone who would like to contribute boxes to sign the
Hospital RoundUp tracker on AQ. I need complete boxes. Stamp, logbook
(please be sure to include your name and the state you reside for the
children to see!) *small stamp pad* and a box or a couple of dollars
so I can purchase a box for your contribution. These boxes will be
larger than typical about sandwich size because sterile wipes must
be included. The stamps can be a bit larger for this reason if you
would like. Also, since most will not be in the wild there is no need
for LnL or even camo tape. I need them to be images that children are
familiar with. Cartoon characters, storybook characters, favorite
toys or treats I will need 30 different images, 10 for tiny wees, 10
for elementary age and 10 for older kids. I would like to have all
the boxes in my possession for planting in one month (by May 31)

If you are interested and would like to be a part of this special
letterboxing "event" please sign the tracker and send me a message
with your box idea -I will be moderating box submissions so as not to
have duplicates and I will be contacting the participants with
specific details.

P.S. I will be carving a special thank you stamp for everyone who
signs up to help. It will either be sent on an index card or will be
a single postal letterbox I haven't decided.

Thanks
Queen Beeloved
