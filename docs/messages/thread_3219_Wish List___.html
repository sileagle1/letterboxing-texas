Speaking of tracing paper - I found a really nifty device the other day at Hobby Lobby - it's a small plastic table (and I do mean small - something a little less than a sheet of paper in width and length) with a light underneath it to make tracing easier for - ahem - aging eyes.  Of course, right after I got it I lost my contact lens, so even that table didn't help with MY vision!  I finally got my new lenses yesterday and after I get through finding all the things I lost while I couldn't see, I'm gonna try it out!
 
Anyway, this is a cool little thing that enhances tracing paper, and it was all of $9.99 (which, ironically, I learned when I took it to the checkout and told the cashier I had looked all over it for a price and didn't find one, to which her teeny-bopper self looked at it and pointed to the corner and told me how much it was).
