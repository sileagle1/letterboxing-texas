Trip To East TX

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-09-27 01:52:23 UTC
I was in Lufkin this weekend visiting a friend and
managed to look for a few letterboxes on the way on
Friday, 9/24. I found Cleo Memorial, Tribute To
England #2 and Road Runner alive & well, but was unable
to find Lone Star Trail at Big Creek Scenic Area. I
also checked on my Big Creek Bug and Lufkin Lookout
boxes and they are doing well. I was amazed I was
able to accomplish all this on Friday since Ivan was
supposed to ruin the whole weekend. I did not hit rain
until almost in Lufkin, then it was gone that night.
I guess even a Tropical Storm can't stop a determined
letterboxer (or maybe I was just lucky). Thanks for
the boxes and for all the new ones placed while I was gone!

Silver Eagle
