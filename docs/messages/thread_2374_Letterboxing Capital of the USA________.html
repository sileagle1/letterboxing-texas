Letterboxing Capital of the USA!!!!!!!!

From: Baby Bear (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-11 14:43:00 UTC
Silver Eagle posted last week about the ongoing discussion on the
National talk list on "who is the letterboxing capital city". After
much debat, it looks like Houston, Texas is clearly the winner, and
that is not even counting the boxes I have not posted to AQ, or the
many metro boxes that all of us list by smaller town names that
technically could be "Houston" boxes. This prompted me to actually
post the the National list (it has been 3 years since I have done so),
which I am sure I will regret as usual. Anyway, EVERYONE on this list
should be proud of our accomlishment. From 10 boxes less than 5 years
ago in the whole state, to #1 city in the USA, (and I'm sure Dallas
would probably be in top 5 also), TEXANS stand a little taller once
again!!

Baby Bear
from Cypress (HOUSTON), Texas
