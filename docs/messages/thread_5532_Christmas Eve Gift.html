Christmas Eve Gift

From: (candj1969@yahoo.com) | Date: 2015-12-24 07:37:31 UTC-08:00

There's an old Irish tradition that has been practiced in LSQ's O'Neal family as long as anyone can remember.  It's called Christmas Eve Gift, and the idea is to be the first, on Christmas Eve, to say "Christmas Eve Gift" to anyone you meet or speak to.  So, if you get a phone call, you answer with "Christmas Eve Gift!", and each time you meet someone, you greet them with "Christmas Eve Gift!".  Traditionally, the recipient of the greeting has to give you a gift.  The gifting part has gone by the way with time, but the spirit of the tradition lives on, and there is a true competition to beat everyone else to the punch on that special day.  So, we extend to each and every one of our Texas letterboxing family, "Christmas Eve Gift!"  And a very Merry Christmas day.


Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
