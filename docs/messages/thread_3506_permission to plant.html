The old answer was that state facilities didn't require permission - the new answer is that it seems to depend on the site.  While Texas Parks & Wildlife has established a policy, it seems like some places are being very diligent in applying the policy and others aren't.  For my part, that sort of thing makes me really nervous - as in, so they aren't worrying today, but will they be gung-ho tomorrow? 
 
At this point, with respect to state facilities, I would ask permission.
