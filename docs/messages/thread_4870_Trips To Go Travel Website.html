Trips To Go Travel Website

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2011-09-03 00:38:04 UTC
My son had to create a website as a school project and decided to do one on travel. Since I have planned many trips over the years I agreed to provide some itineraries for him to use. The result is a website called Trips To Go that offers free detail trip plans for U.S. and Canada with fun activities, directions and links for more information. Since many of you like to travel I thought you might like to check it out and possibly use some of them. I don't include actual letterbox locations but many of the places mentioned do have letterboxes and you could search LBNA and/or AQ for them. There are only about 5 trips at this time since they take quite a bit of effort to produce, but if enough people like them I will continue to add more. Here is the link:

http://tripstogo.webs.com

Let me know what you think and happy trails!

Silver Eagle
