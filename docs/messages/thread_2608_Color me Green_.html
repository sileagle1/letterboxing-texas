From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-02-25 05:25:18 UTC
Two new letterboxes were placed yesterday in Grimes County, the first
two to be located there, so you can color Grimes County Green! The
boxes are "Sarah's Flag" by Lone Star Quilter and my "Fanthorp Inn".
Hey, y'all need to visit Fanthorp Inn in Anderson. It's an old
stagecoach inn that has been restored to the period of 1850 and they
have a beautiful stagecoach pulled by a pair of white Perheron draft
horses named Willie and Waylon, who live at Barrington Farm at
Washington-on-the-Brazos when they're not being used. Check it out.
Speaking of Washington-on-the-Brazos, we placed two boxes there
today. LSQ's box is called "The Dodson Flag" and mine is "The Last
President". We've been sort of into early Texas history lately. Next
friday is March 2, you know.

Boots Tex
