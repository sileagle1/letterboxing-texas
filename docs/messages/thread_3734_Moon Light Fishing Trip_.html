Moon Light Fishing Trip!

From: tejasangel1976 (mhcyclone66@verizon.net) | Date: 2008-08-17 07:43:23 UTC
Well guys,

My husband and I decided to eat a late dinner, and we both got a bad
case of the "I can't sleep". So I talked Mr. Angler aka Mr. Angel
into a moon light fishing trip. Now usually Mr. Angler prefers the
salt water of a bay, like around Port Bolivar. But tonight I had
somewhere else in mind. So we headed for the fresh water of the
Brazo. When we got to our destination, the moon light lit the
pathway just beautifully to the river. Walking down the path we
realized we had forgotten our poles. But we decided to carry on to
the river. An armadillo crossed our path , but having 4 armadillos
myself I knew what to do. It decided itself to move on. Almost to
the river , we were stopped again by a rowdy bunch of Natives.
At this point we gave up making it to the river bank. Anyway we
weren't able to catch any fish, but did manage to help a bus load
of Natives on their journey.

Been in around Galveston parts all week, now headed to the county of
Fayette for a week. Don't know if the Natives will get off here or
not. I will let yall know when I do.

I am cured of the "I can't sleep" and will talk will yall soon. -
Tejas Angel

PS. My favorite part about the TTB2, is you find it when you are
suppose to find it. Thanks SE!
