Cinnamon Rolls?  What cinnamon rolls?  Oh, you mean those secret recipe, sure I could tell you but then I'd have to kill you cinnamon rolls?  Welcome back to Texas!  If you can't stand the heat, you should have been here a couple of weeks ago, when it was 103 degrees.  LSQ and I went out and found most of Baby Bear's new boxes recently.  We would have been first finders on most of them had it not been for a certain deceased Boxer that must of have been right ahead of us!  The boxes are worth the trip.  You can learn a lot of interesting Texas history along the way.  We went for a walk this morning at Lake Houston Wilderness park and almost needed a jacket; the temperature was about 68.  Of course, that didn't last long, but may be a harbinger of good boxing weather to come.
