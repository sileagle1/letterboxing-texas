From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-12-16 23:58:10 UTC-06:00
Funny you ask - I was just sitting here plotting my 11 days of freedom from the working world.  I know I am neglecting to account for two days of holidays and two or three days of shopping, but still...I'm imagining I can go letterboxing every day of my 11!
 
It's nice to see gas prices down a bit - hopefully they will stay that way through the holidays.
 
I never get tired of looking at the listings and trying to decide how to get the most out of my letterboxing time.  Even last year it wasn't that difficult a thing to look at them and say "Well, I guess I'll go - here."  And before that, it was "well there's a box I haven't gotten yet!"
 
We always go to Henderson for Christmas lunch, so I'm hoping to plant a box or two over there while we're there.  Henderson is one of those odd towns that hasn't really been found by letterboxers - kind of like Tyler was until the last year or so when TxDragonfly moved there.  I planted one in Henderson two Christmases ago, and I think it might have recently gone missing.  Still though, two years is not too bad!  I want to plant one in honor of my mother-in-law, who tells the story of going to her parents' diner after school, and then going across the street to the cemetery and leaning up on the gravestones and reading. 
 
Personally, I like the cold - it is what motivates me to box.  However, having lived in CA, I can sympathize.  This is definitely NOT California cold!
