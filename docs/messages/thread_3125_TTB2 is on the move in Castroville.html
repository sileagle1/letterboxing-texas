We found Castroville Regional Park a few months ago while
letterboxing. We thought it would be a fun place to plant the tour
bus. Here is the info:

The park is located at:
816 Alsace Ave
Castroville, Texas


To find the Tour Bus:

Park you car in the "Pool Parking" lot near the hiking/nature trail
head.

Upon entering the trail you will see a directional sign pointing to
the different trails. Follow the "Cross Hill Trail."

Follow the path until you come to a second red bench. This one was
donated by the Sharp Family. You will go directly behind the bench
and there will be a tree with several small trunks, look near this
tree under several rocks and you will find the bus.

Before you head out grab the clues to the Little Guys series and you
can pick up those boxes as well.

Aside from the hiking trails, there is a playground, a swimming pool
(I think it may be closed now) , a camping area, and places to picnic.
It is a great place to spend the afternoon. While you do not need
money to hike, it is $5.oo for a picnic table.

So far the passengers on the bus all seem to be getting along. We
have added a couple who are eager to tour and see some more of the
great state.

Have Fun,
WooWooShoo and Squished Hiker
