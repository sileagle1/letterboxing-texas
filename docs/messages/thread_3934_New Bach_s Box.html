New Bach's Box

From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2009-01-29 12:24:08 UTC-05:00

Yesterday, I braved the ice in Dallas (OK, no ice by 4:00 p.m., and I had to be there anyway) to replace Bach's Box on the SMU campus with a new Bach's Box 2, with a new and improved stamp and logbook, in a slightly different location.  Those college campuses are always doing construction somewhere, but I ask you, why would they dig up perfectly good lampposts just to move them to the other side of the street!  And of course, when they removed the lampposts, they had to take out the bushes immediately behind them as well, and of course, Bach's Box was a goner after having survived this very unlikely location on campus for 3 years.  Oh well, that's the risk of letterboxing, and now there's a new box there in a slightly different location, still near enough to that beautiful Wave fountain/sculpture in front of the Meadows Museum to make you, me, and J.S. Bach all quite happy in the location.  As with the first Bach's Box, though, this one does require some very stealthy behavior in actually retrieving and replacing the box, though, to avoid being seen or putting the box at risk, so take care and enjoy.

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-01-29 12:46:46 UTC-06:00
Okay, I'm sorry, but I find humor in the lamppost situation.  My first thought was,
 
"Why did the lampposts cross the road?"
 
Following by the old boy scout skit:
 
Boy 1: Searches diligently all over the place for "something".  High and low he looks, over and under.
Boy 2 comes in:  "What are you looking for?"
Boy 1: "I lost my watch"
Boy 2: "I'll help you look."
Continue on, with 2, 3, 4 more boys coming in, asking the same question, getting the same answer, and offering to look too (They ARE boy scouts, after all).
Boy 6 comes in: "What are y'all looking for?"
Boy 1: "I lost my watch."
Boy 6: "Well where did you lose it?"
Boy 1 points several feet away and says, "Over there"
Boy 6: "Well why are you looking here then?"
Boy 1: "The light's better here."
 
Random.  Sorry.  Cool you replaced the box though.
