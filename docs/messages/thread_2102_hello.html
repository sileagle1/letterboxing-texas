From: Abbie Beck (abbiembeck@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2008-03-04 10:13:00 UTC-08:00
Hi!  We are Texas Trailmix.  We have been letterboxing for 2 or 3 weeks now.  My dh and I have 5 children - our oldest girl came up with the trailname and we all loved it.  We are great friends with the Rodgers 7 and lurked about their blog as they were detailing their fun letterboxing adventures and we couldn't wait to get started.  My dh actually was the first to print off clues to some letterboxes in a town nearby and get us going on this fun new hobby.
 
Because of our children, and the fact that I home school, we LOVE to letterbox in parks around our town.  Anytime school is done and it's a nice day, we usually look for some new places to go.  We love boxes with handmade stamps that coordinate somehow with the name or area, and we had the fun of finding our first hitchhiker over the weekend!! 
 
Well, gotta go, more boxes to be found!!
