From: bluebutterflytexas (bluebutterflytexas@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-01 00:38:49 UTC
Hope all of you have a safe and happy new year. As i reflected
today on the past year, my mind went to LB. I would like to thank all
of you for the great boxes we have found this year. A special thank-
you to Barefoot Lucy for all the great boxes in East Texas. Each box
we have found has been a treasure. When Stinky Monkey and Roarin' Lion
are with me, I always teach them the proper way to go about finding
and re-placing the boxes. I love old cemeteries, because of the
history there. I have learned a lot about different counties and
cities as we have made our way around Tx. and La. stamping in log
books.
Christmas Eve found Lucy Locket and I roaming around Tx. in
search of the Texas Tour Bus and others. What a wonderful way to spend
Christmas Eve! thanks Team King and all who contributed to the bus. It
is almost ready to go again.
Blue Butterfly :)
