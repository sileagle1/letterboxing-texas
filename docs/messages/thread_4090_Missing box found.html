Missing box found

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2009-05-18 03:13:17 UTC
My letterbox in Fredericksburg, called "Nimitz" was reported missing due to construction back in October. It was found yesterday by the WebWalkers, using the original clues, so it is back in business. They said that construction had been going on, and the area was pretty overgrown, but they were able to find the box alive and well. They placed a box nearby called "Lcheln", so if you are in Fredericksburg, look for both boxes. Thanks, WebWalkers, I appreciate the effort and the update.

Boots Tex
