Slight change... While designing a personal stamp, I was hit with
inspiration. I will no longer go by PathWalker, Call me
MonkeyToes!!!

BTW- my first stamp came out great. It was a lot easier than I
thought. I used a speedball knife (~$10.00) snd a pink SpeedCut
blank (~$5.00) I thought it might be too rubbery, but once I got
going it reacted very well to the knives. I urge anyone who has
been afraid to try making their own to give it a shot. Just go
slow. It can be very rewarding.

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-22 09:37:12 UTC-08:00
Welcome Carl/PathWalker/Monkeytoes!

I was surprised at one huge benefit we get from
letterboxing. Like your family, we love being
outdoors, so I figured letterboxing would just be an
extension of that appreciation. But letterboxing has
really honed our abilities to see detail. When you
are accustomed to living in a fast-paced world with
lots of stimulation, you lose that ability to see the
little things. I'm so thankful we found this activity
- it's given us back a whole little world we forget
exists sometimes!

Lucy
