From: Lou Catozzi (txhipowr@austin.rr.com) | Date: 2005-08-08 16:26:39 UTC
Hello. I haven't been active for a while and I have recently changed
my "signature" name so I thought I would re-introduce myself to the
list. My name is Lou Catozzi and I live in Round Rock, Texas, north of
Austin. I used to use the name "TxHiPowr" to refer to my other hobby
of high powered model rocketry but I have recently changed to "PI Joe"
for letterboxing and geocaching.

This past weekend I was in the Huntsville area caching and boxing and
just generally exploring the area. Friday afternoon and evening I was
at Huntsville State Park, Saturday I was hiking along the Lone Star
Hiking Trail network, and Sunday I was boxing and caching in the
Montgomery area.

Here are my boxing results:

Raven - Missing (I'm sure I found the right tree/stump)
Red Dragon - Missing (I'm sure I found the right stump)
Chop-Chop - Found
Steamboat Sam - Found
Star Buck - Found by stamp is broken in two - still usable.
Lone Star Hiker - Missing (I'm sure I found the right tree)
Ancient Sun Dancer - Found
Blue Northern - Found
Dragonfly - Not Found - I found the picnic tables but couldn't get any
further than that. Ran out of time.

PI Joe
