Some Good News

From: Lois -James (adamstrails@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-03-30 15:23:24 UTC
Friday I went to the library in 7 Points to check on our letterbook box
there. To my dismay, the library had re aranged all the books again. I
guess they do this every year at this time as it was this time of year
that I hid the first book and it went missing in 2 weeks. I then made a
volumn 2 and it has successfully stayed in the library until the yearly
rearangement. Back to good news. I went to George, the director, and
asked if he knew where they had put the letterbook. He said that he had
noticed they had put THEM in this office. He then went and got both
books and handed them to me. Some of the workers had stamped in the
first book in July 2007 but of course the clues would not have lead any
letterboxer to the book. I could not even find it. I rehid the Volumn 2
book and will update the clues. The Volumn 1, I will hide in a
different library. I was so upset when the first book went missing and
am really glad I have gotten it back and in great condition.
