I'm over on the western side -- about 45 minutes from Lake Michigan.
There are lots of boxes over here, but also lots on the east side as well.
Actually the UP has gotten a lot of action over the past couple of
summers. Michigan and Wisconsin just completed a friendly competition
to see which state could place boxes in all the that state's counties.
Michigan won, and that included the remote counties up north.
So, come to Michigan and letterbox. There's at least one in each
county .
