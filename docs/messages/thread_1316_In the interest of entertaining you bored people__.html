The four classes of fires ( or at least that I know them) are
 
Class A- Ordinary Combustibles ( paper, wood, trash)
Class B- Organic Solvents ( gas, oil, paint, acetone etc...)
Class C- Electrical ( wiring, static charges)
Class D- Active Metals ( sodium, potassium etc...)
 
 
Did you also know that fire hydrants can be one of four colors based on their levels of water pressure?
