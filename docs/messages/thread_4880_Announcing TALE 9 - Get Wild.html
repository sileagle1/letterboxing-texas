Announcing TALE 9 - Get Wild

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2011-09-13 17:03:01 UTC
The 9th TX Annual LB Event will be on 3/3/12 at Brazos Bend State Park near Needville, TX. I know it is a long way off, but for those who like to plan way ahead it has been set up on Atlas Quest:

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2249

The website with details can be found at:

http://sileagle.webs.com/tale9.htm

Even though it is almost 6 months away, I still was unable to reserve my first 2 choices and had to scramble to get this one. I also had to come up with a new theme to fit the new location, so I decided on one involving TX Wildlife since Brazos Bend has such diverse animals and plants. It was also the scene of our second TX LB Event where things got a little wild, what with crazy Louisiana letterboxers, an epic thunderstorm and BEAs. It was probably my most memorable Event for those reasons and the fact that I got to meet Wanda from Rhode Island along with Ruby Tuesday and her family Celtic Lions, who created the Wild West Clue game that we played. If this Event is half as fun it will be great!

To see some pictures from that 2nd TX LB Event, look in Photos:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterboxingtexas/photos/album/557522833/pic/list

For a report of the Event, see Message #1846 in this List:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterboxingtexas/message/1846

Unfortunately, the file that BOXDN put on this List describing his night activities is no longer there, but check out the messages around the date of the Event: 3/19/2005.

Silver Eagle
