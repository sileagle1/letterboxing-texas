Here's another circus character with Texas connections -- I just ran across a photo of  "Jack Earle, the Texas Giant".  He was born in Denver, CO but grew up on El Paso, TX.  His height is listed as anywhere between 7 1/2 feet and 8 1/2 feet -- probably the former.  Tumor on his pituitary gland.  He worked the Ringling Bros. and Barnum & Bailey (RBBB) sideshow for 14 years.  Googling him brings up several websites with info about his life.  -- Bell Motel
