From: Brandi (brandi@busybeesfamily.com) | Date: 2006-04-03 01:09:47 UTC
Hello all,

Just got back from camping with the Cub Scout Pack and while there,
managed to do a little boxing.

Found: Silver Eagle's Enchanted Eagle Microbox. Box was NOT in
correct location, but was visible to the naked eye as you approach the
correct location. We stamped in (there are about 4 pages left in the
logbook) and then replaced box in the correct location. The stamp is
beginning to crumble away on the edges, but otherwise, good image.
Last documented find was April (I think?) 2004. Maybe because box was
in wrong location?

Attempted: Eoghan's Enchanted Rock. Am fairly certain that I was in
right spot, but could not find box after more than two hours searching
while on two separate occasions.

Attempted: Baby Bear's Yogi. Again, am fairly certain that I was in
the right spot (esp. since that spot fit the clues for BOTH boxes),
but hate to report boxes missing unless it's so obvious that there is
absolutely NO WAY that I could be wrong.

Planted: Tiger Cubs Take Only Photos and Leave Only TRACKS! Will
post clues shortly.

The park was incredible...the bathroom facilities were the nicest I've
ever seen in a state park. Great camping sites, too! Enchanted Rock
is super cool and definitely worth the hike. Do go in the AM before
the sun gets too high or you will pay, however!

That's all for now...gotta go start unpacking and then repacking for
next weekend's camping trip!

Batty 'B' of the BusyBeeFamily
