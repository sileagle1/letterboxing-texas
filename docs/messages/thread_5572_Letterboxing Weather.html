Letterboxing Weather

From: (sileagle1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2016-09-29 19:19:09 UTC-07:00

Yesterday and today I took advantage of this great weather to find and place letterboxes in the Bee Cave/Lakeway area.  I am so impressed with all the parks and trails I have found and I think you will be, too.  Yesterday I went to Commons Ford Ranch Park, a park I had never heard of until I saw a letterbox was placed there back in 2011.  It is a great park along the Colorado River with a hike following a creek with waterfalls after a good rain.  I found the box, placed mine, then waded in the creek by a small waterfall.  Awesome!

Today I decided to tackle the Canyonlands Trails to find a box PI Joe placed back in 2012 and place two of my own.  This was another place I had never heard of and it was great!  I hid one box then continued on to Mt. Lakeway to place another one.  What a view!  Then I went back down to find the box that brought me there, but the trail was blocked by workers.  I managed to skirt around them and get on the trail anyway and found the box, which had a nice stamp in a good location.  When I started stamping in I couldn't believe it had only been found a few times, the last one being almost 4 years ago.  Hopefully my other boxes will draw more people to this great place, especially since the foliage is supposed to be beautiful in Fall.

To me, this is what makes letterboxing fun, nice hikes in great weather and exploring places you never would have heard of without our hobby.  So get out there and enjoy.

Silver Eagle
