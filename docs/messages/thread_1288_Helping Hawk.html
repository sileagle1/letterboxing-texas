Helping Hawk

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-09-09 14:57:41 UTC
This is off the letterboxing subject, but wanted to let you all know
that our Hawk could use a bit of help. Her household has grown
exponentially as she is now sheltering her cousin & several other
friends from NO who have been left temporarily (?) homeless, along
with their animals (although they are still trying to locate some
animals who had to be left behind). First she had to go through the
ordeal of trying to find them (they ended up in San Antonio), so I'm
sure you can imagine what this has been like. Just one story in many,
of course, but one that has affected someone we know.

They are doing their best to get along. However, if anyone wanted to
assist with gift cards to places like Kroger, Walmart, Target, PETCO,
or a gas station know that the human and animal companions sharing
Hawk's Aerie would be very appreciative.

Just email me off-list and I'll give you a mailing address.

Thanks!

Dewberry
