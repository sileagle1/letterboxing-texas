As a newbie of only a few months I got a chance to spend the beautiful
afternoon at Longhorn Caverns. I wish to thank Skyebird, Baby Bear,
TulipManiaFamiy, and Gingercat for spending the time to place boxes. I

didn't have anything with me but I did learn that I should, just for

aid and as I travel that road every few months will check them next
time I'm in the area.
