We just replanted a box in the Kaufman Memoral Park. The one there
went missing about a mth ago. We made a new box, logbook, and stamp
and hid it in a different place. We wanted it planted before memoral
day.

"Celebrate Our Military Day"

Here are the new clues:

This is a park that Kaufman built to commemorate our Military men and
women who died in service of out country. Kaufman is on Hwy 175 about
35 miles from Dallas, TX. When you get to Kaufman, turn south on Hwy
34. After you past Brookshire's grocery store on your left, get into
your left lane. Turn left at the light onto South 1388. On the right
side of the road, across the street from the Kaufman High School is
the Veteran's Memorial Park. After you have read the inscriptions and
meditated on our solders that gave their life for our country, you
will notice a tank sitting behind the memorial park. Go inspect the
tank. Go under the big gun to the far side of the tank. Look to the
top runner of the track. Count to 4. Look between 4th and 5th track
for the box. Look between the enter and outer tract. Hint: #4 track
is cracked.
