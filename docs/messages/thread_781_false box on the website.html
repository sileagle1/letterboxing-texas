From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-07 17:27:24 UTC

Hi all, I just wanted to put the alert out for TX boxers that I went to
search for the Cottonwood Creek box in Grand Prairie Monday, couldn't
find it, and emailed the placers. This is the reply I received today:

Message:
So sorry that you were unable to find the box. We would have been
surprised if you had since the box does not really exist. The clue is a
ruse intended to entrap someone else. Sorry that you were caught in our
dastardly web. We are removing the clues today so as to not trap other
unsuspecting boxers.


Ronnie n Winnie


This really annoyed me, so I wanted to let you guys know what they were
up to in case they posted any more boxes on the website.

I emailed the webmaster of the LBNA site to let them know.
-Kantexan
