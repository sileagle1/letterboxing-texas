It's up to you on camping - my family will be staying
at Bardwell Lake in Ennis. I would recommend camping
in the Waxahachie Creek Park to be more central to all
that will be going on, or High View is also open in
October and is also on the west side of the lake, but
further south. This is an Army Corps of Engineers
site, so it's a little different than state parks.
Here's the link:

http://155.84.70.101/bardwell/

You may also camp at Cleburne State Park, near
Cleburne (on the western edge of Johnson County)

http://www.tpwd.state.tx.us/spdest/findadest/parks/cleburne/

Lucy
