fun day in Greenville and Addison

From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-01 17:24:53 UTC

Hey all,
We had a fun (but rainy) day in Greenville yesterday finding all
four of Catpawz' boxes. If you live in the Metroplex, Greenville is
an easy 1 hour drive on I30. I wish the Cotton Museum was open, but
it is closed on Sundays and yesterday was the only day I could go.
Oh well, still worth the trip to see the neat Audie Murphy Statue
and the beautiful sculpture in Forest Park.
I walked through a web trying to get Greenville Sports Park and
thought I had escaped unharmed until I bent down to stamp in and a
HUGE garden spider fell out of my hair on a web onto the ground.
EEEWWWWWWW. The Pikachu Ponderer kept laughing at me the whole way
home because I kept flinching and twisting thinking there was still
a spider on me! Thanks Catpawz for the great boxes!
Ruby's Pretty in Pink was fun too. I was about to give up when
Pikachu Ponderer found it. He kept gloating about how he read the
clues better than I did! And he was right. Even though he didn't
stamp in I let him keep the First Finders Certificate!
Now I have to get started on some new boxes for the area.
-Kantexan
