Partial sitrep for Double Lake

From: Boots Tex (redbarn@eastex.net) | Date: 2010-03-18 22:53:59 UTC
I was out walking today at Double Lake in Coldspring and decided to check out some of the letterboxes there, by memory only since I brought no clues with me. I'll go back and check out the others next week. I looked for 17 boxes and found 16. Only Mr. Bobbles by Princess Aurora is missing. The ones I found in good condition were:

Dog Gone by Wills World
Dogwoods by Viewfinder (contains a HH)
Double Dog Dare by Silver Eagle
Know Your Swimming Hole by Baby Bear
A Doggie Delight by Boots Tex
Blue by Jake's Clues (contains a HH)
Buried Treasure at Double Lake by Wills World
Dogpatch USA by Boots Tex
Easter Egg Hunt by Lone Star Quilter (one of the eggs is missing, but we'll get it replaced next week)
Little Rabbit Fufu by Viewfinder
Mom, Guess What I Finally Caught by Baby Bear
Rainbow Butterfly by Princess Aurora
The Hound of the Baskervilles by The Ghost of IRA Boxer
The Hound's Tree by Lone Star Quilter
Airedale Terrier by JACKS (part of the Best of Breeds series by dewberry)(contains a HH)

If you have one there and I didn't list it, it's just because I didn't remember where it is. I'll check it next week. An interesting note: Probably 12 of the boxes I checked (even Lock & Locks) had water inside the box, maybe a tablespoon, but not a single one had a wet logbook, thanks to good, well-sealed, baggies. Some of them even still had little green plastic dog bones in them.

Boots Tex
