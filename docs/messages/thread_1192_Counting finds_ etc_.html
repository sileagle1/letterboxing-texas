From: Birds of a Feather Family (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-15 21:08:58 UTC-07:00
There is no "proper" way to track.  LBNA's FAQ and Silent Doug's website give some guidelines.  Quoting from one of Silent Doug's articles on PFX Counts: "There is a general consensus of how to manage your counts, but you're on the honor system, so feel free to modify your personal recordkeeping to fit your own desires. The overriding rule of thumb is that you shouldn't try to deceive anyone with your PFX count."
 
1) I think Silent Doug's website has a good definition:
http://www.letterboxing.info/glossary/term.php?term=exchange
So that would include Personal Stamps as well as Personal Travelers.
 
2) Catpawz is doing a Texas Passport Series. The actual series stamps I would count as Finds as they are hidden letterboxes.  The souvenir stamp I would not count as a find.
 
3) At this point, I have just been including Hitchhikers under my Finds.  I have been considering putting them into a separate category.  Whether to include hitchhikers in the F or use a separate count designated with an H or an HH is your choice.
 
Currently, I track the following categories: Placed, Found, Exchange, Event, Postal, Virtual, and Other for everything else.
