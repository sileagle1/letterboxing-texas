From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-09-06 14:28:29 UTC
Though I had trouble finding this one, too, I finally
was successful by crossing the creek back down the road
and then taking a trail up that follows the creek
higher up. Eventually you will get to the top of the
mountain and find the Endangered Species sign. I don't
want to give away any more information, but maybe
you can get the owner to supply some hints in private
email. Good luck!
