Depending on how much available time you have, you might find the
boxes in the Katy area, the ones at George Bush park in West Houston,
or perhaps some of the ones in the Sugar Land area.

I recommend using the Texas Letterbox Atlas on the Birds of A Feather
Letterboxing website. This will help you figure out what boxes are
closest to where you'll be. I have found this tool to be extremely
helpful when planning lbing road trips. A big thanks to the birds of
a feather for maintaining it!
