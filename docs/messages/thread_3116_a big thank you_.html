I had a great time at the OZ event yesterday, and wanted to say thanks to everyone who came.  I was so happy when SE and BB arrived, it wouldn't have been the same without them.  Thanks so much to everyone who ran around grabbing up boxes when the rain started, and a special thanks to Mr. Dewberry and Baby Bear for picking up the "Over the Rainbow" trail.  Daddy of Five didn't know I had snuck the bricks out of the flowerbed, so you all grabbing them from me saved me from having to explain how something else from the house had disappeared because of letterboxing.  It was great to see everyone, and I'm really looking forward to the event in March!  MO5

From: Puddle Splasher (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-10-07 23:48:20 UTC
We just got home from Houston. Wow, what fun! I loved every minute of
it. I couldn't tear myself away from the gathering yesterday to go find
boxes, but Dewberry and Mo5 took me out on the trails for some boxes
yesterday after we left El Cuca (or wherever we were)! Then my LSS and
I went out again this morning to find a few more boxes. I love the
trails at the Arboretum as well as the yellow trail in Memorial Park.
However, I think the boxes in Houston need to be marked "extreme boxes"
on AQ because of the humidity (at least this weekend). Thank you all so
much for such a fabulous event. The kids were all so fun and cute, and
it was just cool meeting some new boxers and seeing my old pals again!
It was a really great event! On the way home, I had the big Texas map
out plotting my trip to Bastrop next March.
Puddle Splasher
