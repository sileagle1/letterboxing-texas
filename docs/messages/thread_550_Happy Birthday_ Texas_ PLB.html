Happy Birthday, Texas! PLB

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-01-09 18:46:41 UTC-06:00

As I’m throwing red, white & blue confetti into the HAPPY BIRTHDAY, TEXAS! Postal Letterbox on my kitchen table, I realized that there may be some new Texas boxers who’d like to sign up to receive this box. It’s beginning to really be fun to thumb thru the logbook and read the entries!

For any new boxers out there, here’s the deal;-):

On a day as close to your birthday as possible, a large box will arrive in the mail. It may be slightly too large for some mailboxes, so it may require a trip to your local P.O. to pick it up. (My mail carrier kindly leaves them at my front door, but I know some folks aren’t as lucky) Inside will be a stamp and logbook, exactly like a regular Letterbox, but with a couple of extra items thrown in for fun! Stamp in exactly like a regular lb, and feel free to count it as a “Find” (some folks do and some don’t – it’s up to you).

The crucial part is that by signing up for this PLB, you are promising to mail it on to the next recipient as fast as you can, in time for his/her special day. PLEASE don’t sign up for this box if you don’t feel you can honor that commitment. In most cases, there is at least a week between dates. The other crucial part is that you agree to send this box by Priority Mail with a tracking confirmation number – the total mailing cost is around $5.00. (Many find it easiest to use the new USPS  “Click&Ship” feature online).

 

 If you’d like to add your name, please send me your snail mail address OFFLIST to “celticlions at comcast dot net”.

 

(We even accept honorary Texans, like our good buddy Boxdn in LA!)

 

Ruby T.
