Maintenance on Letterboxes

From: Lois -James (adamstrails@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-11-14 22:07:27 UTC
It was such a lovely day Thursday that James and I decided to do some
much needed letterbox maintenance. We went to "Hot & Cole" and all was
fine. Then we went to Mabank to "The Blue Daisy" and after some
searching, we found it and rehid it in a (maybe) better spot. The
workers of the park had done some clean up and also some trees had
rotted and fell so the clues need to be updated. Will do that as soon
as I write this. Next we went to Purtis Creek. We had some information
that some of our boxes were not in good shape. "Swimming up Stream" got
a new stamp and log book. "Lions, Tigers, and Frogs" also needed
attention. "The Great Outdoors" was in such bad shape that we brought
it home to repair. It will be back in action soon with new clues. Next
we went to "The Drifter". We replaced everything and put it back in the
same spot. Captian Zach's box was perfect and needed no maintenance. We
then went to "An Apple for the Teacher" and it needed no maintenance.
We went to "The Eyes of Texas and all was well. That is as far as we
got yesterday. I will update clues and have "The Great Outdoors" back
as soon as we can.
