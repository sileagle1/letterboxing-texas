I'm on vacation in Hill Country and managed to
find a few on the way, including the Tour Bus.
I stamped in but left it because I would not
be able to rehide for awhile and somebody said
they were coming for it on Tuesday. A word of
warning: a snake was coiled around the box when
I pulled it out of the tree! Scared me to death.
I was not able to get the box back up in the tree
since the snake was still there, so I piled sticks
over it to hide it. Just wanted to let you know.
Great stamps! I also placed a new box in Austin
today (Mellow Velo). I won't have internet access
for a while so everyone have a great July 4th.

P.S. I have been snakebit lately, but was almost
literally today.
