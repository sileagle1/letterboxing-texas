fall event approaching...bring a leaf!

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2016-09-22 14:03:53 UTC-05:00

As summer gives us it’s last gasp this week, hints of fall are in the air and you know what that means!  Letterboxing season is upon us.  So this is a not so gentle reminder that we have an awesome event coming up next month!  And with that in mind…

Here is you mission if you choose to accept it.  Carve a stamp of a leaf – simple and basic, elegant, realistic, stylized, or crazy elaborate.  Bring it with you and drop it off when you sign in.  No box or logbook required.  The leaves will be hidden in and around the dining hall in recycled containers with (yes you guessed it!) a leaf on them.   So now that you have your mission, get to it and make plans to come to TAFLE in October. 

Open Space

PS – If you want your leaf back, please pick it up Sunday morning after the event.  Let me know if you want to make a permanent plant of the remaining stamps somewhere and they are yours.
