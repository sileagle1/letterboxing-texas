From: Connie Terry (connie@lavernia.net) | Date: 2005-07-18 08:29:33 UTC-05:00

I think all of your other questions have been answered, and although I have never seen one, I am going to attempt to explain a cootie.

 

(To the best of my knowledge) a cootie is a letterbox that is placed on another letterboxer’s person, like in a jacket pocket, or in his/her possessions, perhaps in a backpack.  The person receiving the cootie may not know about it for some time, but when he/she does, the job is to stamp in and pass the cootie on to another unsuspecting letterboxer. 

 

Corazon
