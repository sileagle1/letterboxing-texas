Note from Grumpy Grinch

From: Bonnie Lind (bonnielind1@hotmail.com) | Date: 2015-02-14 20:02:41 UTC-06:00
For those who do not follow the AQ Texas board, Grumpy Grinch posted the following message.  I hope we can support them!
 
“We are looking forward to attending the TALE 12 event in Austin on March 7th. It won't be our first trip to Texas, but will be our first time in Austin. We are so eager to meet some of the Texas letterboxer, as well as boxing fanatics from other states.

In addition to our letterboxing passion, we are the happy stewards of a Little Free Library in our front yard, providing books to old and young alike. Gratefully, our Little Free Library is very well used, with books flying off its shelf. Many of the books get returned eventually, though most do not. We scour yard sales and used book stores to resupply our Library. This is where you come in. :-) As you prepare your boxes and potluck items for the gathering, will you also check your closets, bookshelves, and garages for books you no longer want? We will happily accept any books you care to share, especially books for children. Yes, we will carry them home to California with us and will put them out in the Little Free Library to advance a love of reading.

Frank and Susie”
