New to the Group

From: Darcie (boughtonoutdoor@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-08 05:06:49 UTC
Just wanted to introduce myself to the group. I am a new member of
Letterboxing Texas and new to letterboxing in general. I found out
about it from an acquaintance and was very curious. After our first
find in late May (planted by Puddle Splasher) we were hooked. Puddle
Splasher warned it would be addictive and informed me about this
group. Thanks. We (I have 2 young daughters that are always with me
and love it!) have since found about 14 letterboxes.

I look forward to learning more about the hobby!

Craf T Crew

Welcome, CrafTCrew!  I think I just saw your stamp in one of my boxes I was checking on last night.  I'll be looking for it in the others as I make it around to them, also.  Thanks especially for being careful to reseal the plastic bags tightly (soggy logbooks are no fun for finders!) and for carefully rehiding the boxes, covering them well so they'll not go missing!  I appreciate that very much and hope you and your family enjoy the adventures and discovery of many new and interesting places through letterboxing!

From: lori baker (loribaker2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-06 06:19:18 UTC-07:00
Hi, My name is Lori and I am from Arlington. I have
two children ages 7 & 11 who go with me letterboxing.
My husband goes occassionally but not to often. I
absolutely love this hobby because it gets us as a
family out of the house and into nature and also we
moved up here about a year ago and alot of the clues
thus far have had a little bit of a history lesson.
This may sound strange but the kids love it because
that means they are not sitting around the house
watching TV. "Blooming Flower" got me hooked on
letterboxing. Well I'm still new to all this (we
started 7/4/07) and still learning all the ropes so
forgive me if I ever ask anything stupid. Well I
worked the 7p-7a shift last night so I must wrap this
up.

Until next time,
Lori Baker
