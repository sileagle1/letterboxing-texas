From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-10-03 22:51:35 UTC-05:00
Check out JB Kokopelli's October Holiday Series.  She's been doing a holiday series each month in which she plants boxes in honor of different holidays in each month.  This month she changed the premise up a bit and planted all her October boxes in haunted locations and themed them on Halloween.  They are in the Rusk/Jacksonville area.  I've only found one of them so far, but it was a great location - Fearless Fox and I spent a good half hour perusing the location after we found the box.  I'm also going to try to get my Killough Monument replanted this weekend, and it falls in with one of JBK's October boxes.
 
I noticed there is another October Series which was planted here in Jacksonville today.  If you like to keep your series together in your logbook, be careful not to confuse the two different October Series, or if you go for both, realize there are two different planters. 

Lucy
