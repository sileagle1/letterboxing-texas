After staying in Kent for almost a week, the tour bus driver heard what he thought was "the call of the wild." It seemed to come from the east, so he headed that direction and got as far as the caprock and decided to stop again (it was, as they say, "a long haul"). Hopefully will be back on the road soon, but which way? North to the panhandle perhaps? Or east toward more settled parts. Due south? That way gets desolate again very quickly. Maybe southeast over a well worn path? When is the next full moon?
 
El Lobo
