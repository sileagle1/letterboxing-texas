Have you been visiting any letterboxes that were at least a half mile walk from that start of the clues? Keep track of the distances hiked for any round trip walk of at least a mile and when you reach certain milestones you can reward yourself for your efforts with a commemorative medallion. Specially designed PathTags are available for reaching 25, 50, and soon 100 mile goals.

The 25 & 50 Mile TTH PathTags are available now and the 100 Mile PathTags should be available in a couple weeks. You can check out the designs available at:
