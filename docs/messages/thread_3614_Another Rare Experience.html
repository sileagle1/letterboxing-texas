Another Rare Experience

From: tigger32167 (tigger32167@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-06-10 00:51:27 UTC
well, i already told all of you about my chance encounter with an 8
week old white siberian tiger and how i actually got to pet him. well
yesterday, my mom (CC Giraffe) and I went to Tennessee Colony to plant
some boxes and when we pulled up to the Beaver Pond at Gus Engeling
Wildlife Management Area, there was a boat on the pond which I thought
was unusual, and then when we got out of the truck we noticed that
they had captured about 6-7 turtles and had them in a cage. well we
went on and placed the 6 boxes i had for this trail and when we got
back the men were at their trucks and had a 4 foot long alligator
taped up and attached to a board on one of the trucks. we thought
this was odd and asked what they were doing (one of the trucks was a
texas parks and wildlife truck) come to find out, they were checking
the reptiles in the pond to see what they were eating. so being a
biology teacher i had to ask if i could possibly touch the alligator.
to my surprise they said yes. so not only was i able to touch a white
siberian tiger cub but then i got to pet a 4 foot long alligator. all
of this in a week's time...what a blast...i cant wait to see what i
get to pet next..
jb
