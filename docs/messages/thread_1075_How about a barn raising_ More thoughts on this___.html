>Here's the concept again: boxers converge on a

>eventuality that someone in that area will be
>interested. We take the time in the morning to do it,
>then we get together for a potluck lunch and exchanges
>and all that stuff that goes with letterboxing, then
>we head out to find each other's boxes before heading
>home.
>
>I'm looking toward October. Does anyone who thinks

>open date-wise, but I know that in the next month or
>so virtually every weekend will fill up with scout
>stuff, so I need to put a date on the calendar soon.
>
>I thought I would also try to start doing some
>research for parks and trails and sites of interest in
>the counties.
>
>Other thoughts about making this work?
