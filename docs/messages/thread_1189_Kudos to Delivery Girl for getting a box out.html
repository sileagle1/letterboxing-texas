Excellent. Very nice.

Yes, I noticed you had your first box posted and I
meant to take note of that in my update message about
the gathering - we are now up to one boxer, one box in
the three counties! Woohoo! If I remember, you have
another box or two out, but not posted yet, right?

I would certainly appreciate any insight you can give
us about the area - frankly, I've not ever spent any
time at all in your area, so that and the fact that it
is nestled among several high box areas made me decide
that's where the Barnraising had to be!

Are you aware of any other letterboxers in your area?
To my knowledge, you are it. I was that way for quite
a long time in East Texas, and Ruby and I were talking
about how her family were lone boxers in the Dallas
area even for awhile! Things do change...

Lucy
