From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-09-12 13:08:51 UTC-05:00

Thanks to all who have wished well for our family, and especially for SJZ. SJZ has always been my rebel child, the one that steps off on left when everyone else is stepping on right. When she was little, she never daydreamed about getting married or played dress-up wedding with her friends - she was out getting dirty and playing soccer. She hated dresses and hated dress-up events, so you can imagine why I really fully expected that either 1) she would never marry, or 2) she would get married and tell us later. I never really imagined myself as Mother of the Bride!

Her fiance has expressed an interest in letterboxing, much to my delight. I explained to him that letterboxing is NOT a summer activity in our house, but we would gladly take him on his first excursion when the weather begins to turn. I suggested he choose a trail name and let her carve him a stamp, so I guess that's where they are at right now. However, the other day, they were working on little fingerprint hearts to go on Save the Date cards. They had all my ink pads out and were experimenting with colors and finger combinations, so that when they held their hands up for me to see, I swore they had been letterboxing!

BarefootLucy
"Not about footwear, it's about philosophy"
