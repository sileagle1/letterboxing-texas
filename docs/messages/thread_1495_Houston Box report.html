From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-11-30 15:41:32 UTC
Have had a chance to do some boxing & maintenance work this week. So
far, I have these updates to report:

--Ghostly Lady of White Rock Lake--she's no longer haunting Telge
park, but may shortly pop up in the Dallas area. A huge thanks to
Celtic Lions for lending her to the Houston boxing community!

--Placed: Gleeful Grinch and i risked it all for bad haiku. Gleeful
Grinch will probably be pulled around February or so, because the park
where he's stashed is scheduled for improvements. Bad haiku may be a
little puzzling, but you know I can't resist (hoping to place some
more mysteries soon).

--Checked on some that I hadn't had reports on recently:
Emma/Franklin/Nurse/Spy, Missing Spots? and Time Flies are all doing
great and required no maintenance. That's the way we like 'em!
Behaving boxes rock!

I have a few more to place this week or next, including one that is
going to be a super special surprise box for Houston area letterboxers.

Here's to great weather! This is my favorite boxing season.
