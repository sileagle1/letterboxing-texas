Texas Tour Bus 2 Launched

From: TeamKing (teamkingus@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-12-27 16:17:38 UTC-06:00
With the retirement a few months ago of the Texas Tour Bus, the
response has been favorable to continue the tour. So, welcome the
Texas Tour Bus 2!

This box will work the same as the original bus. All finds should be
announced here. Once you have picked up the box, you should add a
stamp of a person or an animal (this is a new feature for this bus)
that pertains to Texas in some way. Then, you will hide the box in a
new location and list it here for the next person to find.

The bus should NOT be taken out of the state of Texas. Although
anyone else on this list who is visiting the state and would like to
participate can do so as long as you leave the box here before you go
back home.

You can also list your find on Atlas Quest if you like. This box will
only count as one find instead of the multiple finds like the last
one (an operations error).

The bus has left the depot and has arrived at its first location and
is waiting for its first passenger. Will it be you?!

Go to Mercer Arboretum in Humble. Find the playground area, then the
pavilion to the right of it. From the trailhead here, go left down
the trail. At the first intersection, go right. Walk along until you
see a 3ft stump about 5ft from left side of the trail. There is a
downed tree behind it. The bus is hiding out in the far end of the
tree away from the stump. (if you get to a T intersection and trail
sign with the bridge to the right, you passed it.) Also, there is a
snake on this trail further along from the bus, so stay alert.

Thank you for your continued support. We hope 2007 is another great
year for our tours.


TeamKing
Operations Director
Texas Tour Bus Lines
