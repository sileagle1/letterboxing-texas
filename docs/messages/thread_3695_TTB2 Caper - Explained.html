TTB2 Caper - Explained

From: lb_bozo (lb_bozo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-08-08 13:00:14 UTC
Ok, no more clowning around, here is the whole story as I understand
it.

It all started when Baby Bear posted his flame against The Ghost Of
IRA Boxer (TGOIB) and appointed himself President of the Anti-IRA
Boxer Fan Club. As you will remember, he warned TGOIB not to set
foot in Cypress, which was actually a trap to get his hands on it.

Soon after this, TGOIB sent an email to Silver Eagle & Barefoot Lucy
asking for advice on the TTB2 since it was getting full, and signed
off saying he didn't want to be anywhere near Cypress. SE said that
actually, it would be funny if TGOIB hid the box in Cypress to see
which fan club would find it first. BL then took it a step further
and said it would really be funny if TGOIB hid it in BB's own yard.
They all agreed and gleefully set about plotting their TTB2 caper.

TGOIB said he planned to add a stamp called The Ghost Of Davy
Crockett and it would tie in with BB since Davy killed a baby bear
when he was young. SE provided TGOIB directions to BB's house to
hide the box and called BB's wife, who works from home, to let her
know what was going on. TGOIB hid the box, and then posted his note
to the TX List, leaving out directions to BB's house to protect his
privacy. SE sent an email to BB telling him to look on the TX List,
and they all waited for the fun to begin.

After several hours SE got a phone call from BB's wife, who had left
their house on an errand, and she said BB got home from work but did
not seem to know anything about the TTB2. BB evidently did not get
the email before he left work, so SE called and left a message
telling BB to look at the TX List, then again waited for the fun to
begin.

Meanwhile, several people started posting on the TX List, including
one from Mommy Aulait about Cypress letterboxes that was totally
unrelated to the TTB2 caper, but suspicious none the less. Then
Mother Of Five posted that it would really be funny if Puddle
Splasher came down and stole the box right out of BB's yard. This
got SE to thinking that the TTB2 caper would REALLY be funny if he
stole the box before BB could get it out of his own yard and kicked
himself for not thinking of it sooner.

Since it would take over an hour to drive out there and BB was liable
to get the phone message and start looking for the box any minute, SE
did the next best thing. He called BB's wife to explain the new plan
and asked her to steal the box. Unfortunately, she was not returning
home for awhile, but not one to let a good joke die, she called a
neighbor and asked him to steal the box. She called SE back and told
him BB evidently had not gotten the box yet since the neighbor was
successful in stealing the box.

Not five minutes later, SE got a call from a very concerned BB saying
the TTB2 was not there. He was worried that TGOIB had hidden the box
at the wrong house. SE gently suggested that perhaps someone had
beat him to the box, and this changed BB from worried to mad at
himself for not looking at the posts earlier. He felt that his trap
to get the TTB2 had worked perfectly, though never expecting the box
to be in his own yard, only to be thwarted by some dirty
letterboxer. SE told him to not jump to conclusions yet, but keep
watching the TX List to see if anyone claimed the box.

Now the stage was set and all that was needed to complete the new
version of the TTB2 caper was a post from the box stealer. So I
posted my note about grabbing the box right under BB's nose and the
fireworks began.

I eventually gave BB back the box and BB's wife explained her part,
then SE explained the rest. At this point, the TTB2 is safely in
BB's paws, but I don't know how safe the conspirators are. I hope
you enjoyed the TTB2 Caper!

LB Bozo

On 8/8/08, lb_bozo <lb_bozo@yahoo.com> wrote:

Ok, no more clowning around, here is the whole story as I understand
it.

It all started when Baby Bear posted his flame against The Ghost Of
IRA Boxer (TGOIB) and appointed himself President of the Anti-IRA
Boxer Fan Club. As you will remember, he warned TGOIB not to set
foot in Cypress, which was actually a trap to get his hands on it.

Soon after this, TGOIB sent an email to Silver Eagle & Barefoot Lucy
asking for advice on the TTB2 since it was getting full, and signed
off saying he didn't want to be anywhere near Cypress. SE said that
actually, it would be funny if TGOIB hid the box in Cypress to see
which fan club would find it first. BL then took it a step further
and said it would really be funny if TGOIB hid it in BB's own yard.
They all agreed and gleefully set about plotting their TTB2 caper.

TGOIB said he planned to add a stamp called The Ghost Of Davy
Crockett and it would tie in with BB since Davy killed a baby bear
when he was young. SE provided TGOIB directions to BB's house to
hide the box and called BB's wife, who works from home, to let her
know what was going on. TGOIB hid the box, and then posted his note
to the TX List, leaving out directions to BB's house to protect his
privacy. SE sent an email to BB telling him to look on the TX List,
and they all waited for the fun to begin.

After several hours SE got a phone call from BB's wife, who had left
their house on an errand, and she said BB got home from work but did
not seem to know anything about the TTB2. BB evidently did not get
the email before he left work, so SE called and left a message
telling BB to look at the TX List, then again waited for the fun to
begin.

Meanwhile, several people started posting on the TX List, including
one from Mommy Aulait about Cypress letterboxes that was totally
unrelated to the TTB2 caper, but suspicious none the less. Then
Mother Of Five posted that it would really be funny if Puddle
Splasher came down and stole the box right out of BB's yard. This
got SE to thinking that the TTB2 caper would REALLY be funny if he
stole the box before BB could get it out of his own yard and kicked
himself for not thinking of it sooner.

Since it would take over an hour to drive out there and BB was liable
to get the phone message and start looking for the box any minute, SE
did the next best thing. He called BB's wife to explain the new plan
and asked her to steal the box. Unfortunately, she was not returning
home for awhile, but not one to let a good joke die, she called a
neighbor and asked him to steal the box. She called SE back and told
him BB evidently had not gotten the box yet since the neighbor was
successful in stealing the box.

Not five minutes later, SE got a call from a very concerned BB saying
the TTB2 was not there. He was worried that TGOIB had hidden the box
at the wrong house. SE gently suggested that perhaps someone had
beat him to the box, and this changed BB from worried to mad at
himself for not looking at the posts earlier. He felt that his trap
to get the TTB2 had worked perfectly, though never expecting the box
to be in his own yard, only to be thwarted by some dirty
letterboxer. SE told him to not jump to conclusions yet, but keep
watching the TX List to see if anyone claimed the box.

Now the stage was set and all that was needed to complete the new
version of the TTB2 caper was a post from the box stealer. So I
posted my note about grabbing the box right under BB's nose and the
fireworks began.

I eventually gave BB back the box and BB's wife explained her part,
then SE explained the rest. At this point, the TTB2 is safely in
BB's paws, but I don't know how safe the conspirators are. I hope
you enjoyed the TTB2 Caper!

LB Bozo
