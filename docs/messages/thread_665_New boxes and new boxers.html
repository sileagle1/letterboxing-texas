New boxes and new boxers

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-08 20:08:43 UTC-08:00
I'm sooo excited! I planted one new box in my town
(most of my boxes are out of town - don't ask me why)
shortly before the news article came out and it has
already been found by a new boxer. A couple of others
have looked for it and haven't yet been successful,
but I've given them a couple of clues and hopefully
they'll make their way to it. It's got kind of
cryptic clues.

More importantly, I was just on letterboxing.org
checking my clues and I found that just today a new
box was planted in town too! I grabbed my keys and my
logbook and stamp and was headed out the door when my
husband told me I surely could NOT be going to find
that box at 9:30 in the evening. Hmph. Maybe I'll
just wait until he's asleep and sneak over there and
snag it! I can literally WALK to it!

Hehe...maybe I'm not alone up here in northeast Texas
anymore!

Lucy
