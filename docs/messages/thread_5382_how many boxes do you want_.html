What a great opportunity for you and future visitors.  I recommend only 1 or 2 boxes per trail initially so that people like me that like to place boxes will have room to do that in the future.  I think 3 or 4 total boxes in the park would be enough to lure most letterboxers, but too many would limit future placements and people would only visit the one time to get them all.  Gradual placements over time would result in repeat visits, assuming the park is really nice.  Good luck and keep us apprised.
