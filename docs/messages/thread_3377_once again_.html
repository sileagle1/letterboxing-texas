From: JHamilton (horsefly@thegateway.net) | Date: 2008-02-28 23:36:06 UTC-06:00
So, I met Corazon on the trail in Bastrop last Saturday, then on Sunday Jackie and I ran into the Belton Gals at Confederate Park, and today on the Baylor campus in Waco... zturtle, Jackie and I meandered past a couple who were deep in though cyphering some code on paper.  We wondered if they were the couple from Santa Fe, NM who had stamped the log books at a cemetery just before us.  As previously instructed by Corazon, I approached them and asked which was was North, to which the gentleman pointed straight up.  It was lionsmane and AstroD and they were working on Travelin' Ts' Baylor Walking Tour.  We took pix and exchanged stamps.  They spent yesterday in Dallas and today in Waco, as they letterbox their way to Bastrop!  So, in the past week, I've met 7 fellow letterboxers on various trails. What a small world!
We had a great day today... although it was very windy!  Jackie's hubby printed off directions to lots of letterboxes for us... and we were bound and determined to find each and every one of 'em!  He probably won't help print out directions for us anymore, since it was way past supper time 'til we got home!LOL
Mim Z
