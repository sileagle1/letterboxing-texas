From: (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2012-04-05 16:34:10 UTC-04:00
Lucy, I don't think chaining boxes to trees would have been all that helpful (aside from the philosophical issues involved) -- did you see where those TREES ended up!  Any boxes chained to them might have ended up as debris in someone's house anyway.  Obviously, even chaining boxes to an 18-wheeler truck is no guarantee they won't fly through the air like Dorothy in a tornado.

It's likely some boxes will have gone missing, especially some of those in southeast Arlington parks off of Green Oaks Blvd or near Kelly-Elliott or Sublette Rds near I 20 and 287, such as some of those wonderful early Kantexan boxes, but most of those are probably long gone anyway.  We haven't heard from Kantexan in awhile.  Offhand, I don't know if any other box locations were particularly involved, possibly the Greenville boxes of CatsPaws, also possibly long gone and no longer active?
