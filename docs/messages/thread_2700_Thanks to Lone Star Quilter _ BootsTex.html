From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-04-07 21:39:29 UTC
A special thanks to these two letterboxers..

Our trip wasn't exactly what we'd planned. We weren't able to hike
today at Bastrop SP due to this odd, awful weather. And a nasty
stomach bug completely felled some of us. But, we were able to get out
and enjoy McKinney Roughs yesterday, only to find a surprise in Baby
Bear's Fox in Socks box...clues to a new box by BootsTex, left just for
us to find. The boys loved it! And when we got to BootsTex' box,
there were clues to another one by Lone Star Quilter! The boys were
thrilled again!

Thanks you two! You really made our afternoon...

dewberry
