Our Adventure!

From: Beach Bum TX (beachbumtx09@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-07-02 03:49:11 UTC

Hi all, I just wanted to give a shout out to the board about what an awesome day Pink Cookie and I had yesterday going on a little over 5 mile hike.  My Facebook friends in the group have heard this already but I think people use to post about their adventures on the board so I wanted to share on here too.  Frost Maiden and Gryzzled Gryphon's tag team efforts on The Enchanted Forest of Fantasy was well worth the 7 hours we were on the trail.  Beautiful stamps by Frost Maiden and the tricky clues kept us going to the end!

On top of finding wonderful carvings and enjoying the hike we also saw: a beetle that we thought at first was a snake poking it's head out... it was huge, I googled it and it was a Eastern Click Beetle over three inches long; bees; muggles; grasshoppers; muggles; lots of  birds; muggles; huge caterpillers; muggles; dive bombing butterflies; muggles; giant trees and to top it off toward the end of our hike we watched a beautiful Bobcat stroll down the trail in front of us!   Pink Cookie got one bee sting and I got two nice heel blisters as bonuses!  Oh did I mention the muggles... fun fun being so stealthy... they thought we were just some crazy girls going in the woods for the first time hehe "oh look.. oh look... it's a bird; it's a bug; it's a big tree!" 

So if your ever get the chance make sure you visit the Enchanted Forest it was a trip we won't forget!

Thanks again FM & GG for the wonderful day! 

Beach Bum TX & Pink Cookie
