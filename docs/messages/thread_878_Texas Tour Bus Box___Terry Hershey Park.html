On Jun 4, 2005, at 12:17 AM, The Carrascos wrote:

Hey you all...the Texas Tour Bus is finally "on the road again"! It is hidden in Terry Hershey park in Houston. To find it go south on Dairy Ashford from I-10. Just south of Memorial Drive there is parking for the park on the right hand side, the lot is marked by a blue precinct 3 sign. Go to the back of the lot and enter the trail through the green and white gates. Pass a trail sign on theleft hand side, and continue downhill on the asphalt trail passing between the white railings. You are on the Robin trail. Continue on the asphalt trail until you come to the second set of white side railings. When you get to these railings, enter the gravelly bike trail on the left. Pass by the white rail and continue down the bike trail for 145 paces until you arrive to a smallish bent tree in the middle of the trail (the water will be on your left). Facing this small tree, take the path to the right and go up and over a fairly steep but small hill.From the bottom of the hill count 6 paces. You should arrive at a fallen tree right next to the path on your left hand side. To your right you will see a faint path leading to a double trunked tree. Walk about 6 steps to this double trunked tree (the trunk is full of woodpecker holes). Face the tree, and go up a slight hill to the left of the tree about 5 paces. The Tour Bus Box is hidden in a clump of brush on your left, covered with sticks and weeds. (The box is a large Tupperware container covered in camouflage duct tape). Grab it and go...good luck! Don't forget to post on this list ASAP after you find it so that no one makes an unnecessary trip. It is a scary feeling hiding a box that really isn't yours, wait until you find it, you will see what I mean. Thanks to Teamking for this great idea! -Mother of Five

You should arrive at a fallen tree right next to the path on your left hand side.  To your right you will see a faint path leading to a double trunked tree.  Walk about 6 steps to this double trunked tree (the trunk is full of woodpecker holes).  Face the tree, and go up a slight hill to the left of the tree about 5 paces.  The Tour Bus Box is hidden in a clump of brush on your left, covered with sticks and weeds. (The box is a large Tupperware container covered in camouflage duct tape).  Grab it and go...good luck!  Don't forget to post on this list ASAP after you find it so that no one makes an unnecessary trip.  It is a scary feeling hiding a box that really isn't yours, wait until you find it, you will see what I mean.  Thanks to Teamking for this great idea!  -Mother of Five
