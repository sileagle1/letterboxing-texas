LB Report

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-03-21 19:50:27 UTC
I just got back from a ski trip in New Mexico and found a
little time to letterbox along the way. In Waco I found
Don't Mess With Texas but could not go to Cameron Park
to find Liberty Bell because of a bike race. Does anybody
know the status of Liberty Bell LB? I then went to Dallas
area and found Halloween and confirmed Spider's Lair is
still intact. I placed George Strait in Amarillo before
heading to Red River, NM to ski for 3 days. We headed south
through Taos where I placed Kit Carson and then to Santa Fe
where I found several boxes and placed Santa Fe Trail. We
then headed to Fort Sumner and the grave of Billy The Kid
where I placed Billy The Kid. Next stop was Lubbock where
I was unsuccessful finding That'll Be The Day but placed
Buddy Holly near his statue. Abilene SP was next where
I was first finder of Caleb and placed Buffalo Gap. The
final stops before getting home were San Gabriel in Georgetown
and Chisholm Trail in Round Rock. It was a fun trip but
I am glad to be home after all that driving. Looks like
several new Texas letterboxes have been placed while I was
gone. Good job!

Silver Eagle
