Goodbye all, it's been fun......

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2015-01-26 08:49:42 UTC-07:00

Well, I talked about it two years ago.  I gave all my Letterboxing patches away last year to be passed out at the gatherings.  It has been requested several times that I leave my boxes out there.  So I will.  There aren't very many anyway. 

 

My lifestyle has changed.  Two promotions at work, and while I make a lot more, I also work a lot more.  Looking forward to retirement somewhere in the next 10 years or so.  There isn't much letterboxing activity in this area, and we have slowed down the dog show circuit a lot.  My little dog Gray-Cee DID get an invitation to the Rally Nationals this year..... that competition is by invitation only.  But we will be in Germany visiting Moni's family then.

 

I just get too many E-mails every day, and while this group had been fun and is not a major player in the In-Box, it is time to go.  I need to down-size.

 

Maybe I will return some day.  When I cannot hike miles and climb thousands of feet anymore.  When I can't see the sights of my competition rifle and pistol anymore.  When we don't care about dog shows anymore.  And when my new Jeep isn't going off-road anymore, maybe I'll find time for letterboxing again.   I will probably unsubscribe tonight while I am on shift.

 

I wish you all - EVERY ONE - the greatest success in your future.  Take care, be SAFE.....   AND LEAVE THE DAMNED SNAKES ALONE!  <grin>.
