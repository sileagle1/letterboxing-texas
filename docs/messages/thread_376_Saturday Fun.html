Saturday Fun

From: corazon78121 (connie@lavernia.net) | Date: 2004-10-03 02:33:56 UTC

Navigator and I spent Saturday in Belton at a HamFest, which really
isn't much fun too unless you like looking at all those wires,
radios, microphones and other gizmos. When that party broke up, we
went to Miller Springs Nature Center to find Christy's two Indian
Symbols boxes. As she warned in her updated clues the bridge was
washed out... By the dates in the logbooks, we were the first ones
to stamp in since it had washed out. Me trying to crawl up the
slippery, muddy creek bank was NOT a pretty scene. But it got even
more fun when Navigator proposed a cross-country short cut back to
the parking area! It was a great hike, however, and we talked about
making it again. On the way back home we stopped in Salado and
found Silver Eagle's Salado College box. We never would have known
that structure was there without Silver Eagle's guidance. Then we
stopped at San Marcos for the Salamander box at Aquarena Springs.
This was another cool location, I hadn't been there for years. All
this makes me eager to sit down and do some carving to meet that
goal of one box per month until the gathering...
See you then!
Corazon
