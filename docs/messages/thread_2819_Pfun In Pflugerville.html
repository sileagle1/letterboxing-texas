Pfun In Pflugerville

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-06-10 22:31:16 UTC
Well, I just got back from yet another soccer tournament; this time in
Pflugerville (just north of Austin). Yesterday we had a few hours
between games, so talked the family into looking for the 3 boxes in
town. It worked out great since the boxes were in parks along a shady,
cool creek. We ended up getting lunch to go and spending a few hours
wading in the creek and relaxing in yet another great place
letterboxing has taken me to. I even found 2 HH in the boxes, so
managed to get 5 stamps AND keep the family happy. Nothing like a win-
win letterboxing day!

Silver Eagle
