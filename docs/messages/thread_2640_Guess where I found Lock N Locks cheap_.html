So, I was wasting time before 'having' to come to work. Baby Girl wanted some grapes, so I went to Albertson's and picked a bunch of grapes ($5.00 bunch, ripoff). And I was meandering through the store and noticed a whole end cap full of LnL's. You know how they have those specials of the week. Well, there special is LnL's. Okay, so I told you. For those of you in the DFW area, the store I went to is on the corner of 820 and Bridge St., the Woodhaven section of Fort Worth. Yep, that's the neighborhood I work in.
