New Letterbox Placed

From: janetsredbarn (janetsredbarn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-17 21:19:12 UTC
After finding 84 letterboxes since November, I decided it was time to
place one of my own. Besides, San Jacinto County needs more
letterboxes. There is only one active LB in the county, Silver
Eagle's Big Creek Bug. DrooP's Lone Star Trail has been missing for a
while, and Tall Texan's Cozy Campfire, while listed in San Jacinto
County, is in reality at Lake Livingston State Park, which is in Polk
County. My first box is called Double Lake and is located in the
Double Lake Recreation Area near Coldspring. There is also a small
gift for the First Finder, so y'all check it out. Lone Star Quilter
is planning some quilt block letterboxes and I have a few more ideas,
so San Jacinto County is getting into 'boxing!

Boots Tex

placed my first letterbox today. It's called Flying Geese Letterbox
and it's the first in a series of "Quilt Block" letterboxes I plan to

out. Boots Tex has a box in the same area and we are planning more
around Coldspring.
