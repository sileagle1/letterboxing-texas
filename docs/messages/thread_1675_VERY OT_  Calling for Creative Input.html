Y'all are the most creative people I know and I'm
desperate!

I'm working on a flyer right now for a Cub Scout event
I'm chairing in April. The theme of the event is
"Native American" and it's an overnight camping event.
Attendees are 1st-5th grade boys and their dads,
roughly 400 people.

I need a name for the event. Something catchy,
somewhat manly (not completely a requirement, but it
sure can't be feminine), and PC. Outdoorish. Last
event was a circus theme and we went with The Greatest
Show in Cub Scouts, and one before that was the 75th
anniversary of cub scouting and we went with Blast to
the Past.

I've thought about Welcome to My Village, but it
didn't fly in the test market (my husband and son).
Considering March in my Moccasins, but doesn't quite
do it for me. I also consider Potlatch, but the first
response is "What's a potlatch?" or "I have to bring a
dish?"

So please y'all get me off-center here!

TIA,
Lucy
