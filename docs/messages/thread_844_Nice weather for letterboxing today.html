Nice weather for letterboxing today

From: tx2chic (blhjrh@aol.com) | Date: 2005-05-20 20:51:44 UTC
Made a trip into Ft Worth to Burnett Plaza this morning (trying to beat
the lunch crowd in the plaza)to find Man With Briefcase. Actually, I'd
found it on Sunday, Mother's Day, but without my logbook or stamp, so I
went back to do it right! No one else was there, perfect! Even took
some really good pictures of the giant aluminum man, very impressive.
Kantexan, that is a great place for a letterbox! Then we went out to
Lake Benbrook SW of Ft. Worth to find the 4 Element-al letterboxes
there and found all 4 in good shape, although the stamp for AIR is
missing (but taking the suggestion of a previous stamper in that
logbook, I traced it from the cover). Those are great hiding places,
lots of fossils in the rocks along the lakeshore near FIRE, thick
wildflowers near WATER (so thick, in fact, that one is really tricky to
find right now. Have to really pay attention to the clues!).
