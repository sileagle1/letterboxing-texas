From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2013-05-01 19:47:17 UTC-05:00

I’m sorry it isn’t what you hoped for.  From what I’ve seen on the yahoo group in my short time here, events like this don’t usually draw many people.  Events devoted to letterboxing do much better.  I think the appeal of events is spending time boxing with other letterboxers, and having LB specific activities planned helps a lot.  Events where something else is the main attraction and letterboxing is a secondary activity don’t seem to have the same appeal. 

 

Barefoot Lucy usually has an event in early June at the Tomato Festival in Jacksonville, but the one time I went she kept us busy for most of the day - working to get the clues with a scavenger hunt, then boxing, then meeting up for an early dinner.  I enjoyed it,  but I wouldn’t have driven 5 hours to attend the tomato festival and get one event box.  I went for the letterboxing activities she planned and the dinner afterward.

 

Wry Me and I have also hosted meetings at a local library. We carve or make logbooks and mainly hang out and chat. These seem to draw just the locals (sometimes only 3 or 4 of us), but we have fun anyway!  So I hope you’ll make the most of it and have a great time.  Good luck!
