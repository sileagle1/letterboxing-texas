Well we just recovered from our trip to White Rock Lake to find
missing key #3. We came away empty handed, itching and sneezing.
After a warm soapy shower to ge the poison ivy off of us (we hope)
and a hearty snack I can reflect and help you know where we went
wrong. First of all, it is not clear which parking lot at the Big
Thicket we are talking about, there are at least 3 parking lots.
Secondly, we counted at least 5 different picnic tables with barbaque
pits. So we weren't exactly sure where to start. Then, Celtic may
not realize why they call this place "big thicket", walking into
waist deep thicket was not much fun, but we were
determined....sooooo....we "not so gleefully:" ran up to sets of 3
trees (there were many), thought we had something at one point, but
my arm was in far enough that I was scared of who might come out of
the tree to greet me....so we checked out about 4 sets of "3 trees"
and still came up empty handed. My eldest daughter who only went
with us so she didn't have to clean her room summed the adventure up
this way "Next time I'll clean my room", so you see, it wasn't a
total loss.

regards,

joann
