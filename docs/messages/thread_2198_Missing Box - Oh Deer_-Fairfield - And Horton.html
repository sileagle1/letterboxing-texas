Out of the four boxes we went looking for in Fairfield, we came up
short, including my CLICK AND CLACK box. It was a disappointing
trip to Fairfield as far as letterboxing was concerned. If Baby
Bear's TX TOUR was still in its place, it was heavily guarded by a
wasp nest, so we had to leave well-enough alone on that one.

We were successful, however, in stamping into BLIND LEMON JEFFERSON
and VAL VERDE CANNON. Both of these were in good shape.
