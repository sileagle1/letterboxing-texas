The Terrors of Telge
A Houston-area Informal Mini-Gathering
Saturday, October 29, 2005
3 pm to 5 pm
Telge Park


Watch out!

CYPRESS, TXOctober 29, 2005Our reporters are on the scene,
investigating rumors of a number of strange sights and sounds in the
woods of Telge Park. Word is that the woods might be full of
ghouls, goblins and other Halloween fiends and friends. Natives
have reported inky, ghostly images of Mr. Bones, a mummy and other
similarly frightening fellows.

Are you intrepid enough to withstand the Terrors of Telge? If so,
bring your compasses, log books, bug spray and snacks to Telge Park
on October 29, 2005. This is an unorganized semi-spontaneous mini-
gathering of Houston area letterboxers. For those that like, after
the mini gather, we will adjourn to La Hacienda Mexican Restaurant
up the road for more fun and fellowship. There will be plenty of
Halloween-themed letterboxes to find. To RSVP, visit Atlasquest.com.

Semi-organized by Mother of Five, TeamKing and Gramatrick. Feel
free to bring a box or hide one in advance. . .and let us know if
you can make it. As everyone's fall season is jam-packed, we are
going light on planned activities--just opportunities for boxing,
exchanging, chatting and fun.
