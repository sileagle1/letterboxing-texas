Telge Park - Halloween Central for Letterboxing

From: babybearlb2002 (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-10-25 19:02:21 UTC
I have added my 2009 addition to the park (Halloween 2009 - Boiled IRA) and posted the clues. While there, I then tried to find all the boxes in the park, and have the follwoing report. Some I found, some are definately gone, and some I just was not sure. The park had 2 big floods last year, and the hurricane also took its toll. But overall, I was happy with how many boxes are left. Here is a listing, with some notes.

Peek-a-Boo - Cound not figure out.
The Wicked Witch of NW Houston - Alive, but in the open, recovered it.
Howling at the Moon - Alive, but in the open, Returned to spot.
Now That's Scary - Alive and Well
State Bird: Texas - Alive and Well
Halloween Mistake - 2008 - Alive and Well
Baby Bear's Bane (Ha Ha) - Alive and Well
Terrors of Telge - Grim Reaper - RETIRED (gone)
The Great Pumpkin - Terrors of Telge - Box is Missing.
Halloween Friends - Alive and Well
Scrambled Web - could not find
Legend of Sleepy Hollow - Alive, but in the open, returned to spot
The Ox Problem - Could not find
CFBL - Could not find
The Ghost of John - Could not find the stump
Park Angel - Alive and Well (added new log book)
La Llorona - Could not figure out
A True Goblin - Found laying open on the ground near where should
have been. Log was ruined. I dried stamp, added new log book,
and put in new container, then returned to original spot.
Puppy Love - I did not go check , but I believe it is gone.
Vlad to Meet You - Pretty sure it is gone
Mary Shelly's Frankenstein - pretty sure it is gone
Telge Tarantula - Alive, but was on the ground. I re-attached it to
fighing line it was hanging on, and put in notch of tree.
The Telge Ghost of 2007 - I could not find it, but may still be
hanging there somewhere.
Stingy Jack - Alive and Well
A Box of Bones - Alive and Well
Ghost In the Graveyard (2) - Alive, but a tree fell on top of the
whole area. I found graveyard box in open, and returned it to
proper place with RIP rock back on top. I did see one hanging
ghost, but will take some work to get to it.
Jordan's Birthday - Could not find the rotted log?
I Salute You - Did not check on this one.
