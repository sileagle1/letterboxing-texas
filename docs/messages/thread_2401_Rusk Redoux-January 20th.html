Rusk Redoux-January 20th

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-19 12:16:12 UTC-06:00
Thanks to Gryzzled Gryphon for a nice catchy name for this event!
 
Work is well underway to get this event going.  The site is reserved, clue books are ready and waiting for box checking, which I hope to go do next week.  I understand from different placers that some of the boxes out there need to be either verified or have a little TLC, and that Celtic Lions' have retired their Cherokee Nation box.  If you were not ever able to get that stamp, you missed a great treat!
 
We've got a nice little group signed up for the event, with others who have said they'll be there.  I've had a couple of requests for a carving demo, so hopefully those who consider themselves to be good carvers, or who enjoy carving, or both, will bring tools and be willing to show how they carve. 
 
I'm trying to keep this event very casual - emphasis on box finding - but hope to have a couple of mixer type activities for those who want to stay close to the fire (and hopefully we'll be needing one!).
