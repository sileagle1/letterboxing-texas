From: Vicki Stecher (stecher@runbox.com) | Date: 2008-03-16 13:24:00 UTC-05:00
Okay, I'll do an intro...
 
I'm Ladybird Beetlebug and I have three boys, Sneaky Cat,8, Aslan,6, and Stinky Monkey,16 months. My husband goes with us on occasional letterboxing forays because he loves being outdoors and he loves hiking, etc. But he has not had a single flicker of interest in letterboxing until we came across Viewfinder's Ornette Coleman box. And then it was just a flicker, but hey! I'll take what I can get!
 
We live in almost East Texas, although that is stretching it a bit. We're between Dallas and Canton. We've only been letterboxing since last fall, but to hear my boys talk, you'd think we've been doing it forever. Stinky Monkey even has his own journal and will toddle after us, sit down when we are stamping, and hold his book in his lap and say, "Lah-dat! Lah-dat!" which alternately means, "That is mine", "Give it to me" or "Stamp in my book now, please."
 
We are planning to come to the Denton gathering. We actually lived in Denton for 10 years before moving back to the farm where I grew up. So when I heard the gathering was in Denton I was so excited...not only my old stomping grounds, but an excuse to see lots of old friends and drive past my favorite quilt shop (in Lewisville) to boot!
 
So that's us, in a nutshell!
LBBB
