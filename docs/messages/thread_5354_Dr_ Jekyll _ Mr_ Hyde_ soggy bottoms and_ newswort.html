Except offloading the damp camping gear piled in the back of my van.  Or maybe thinking you can grab just one more on the way home, "reversed engineered," during a lull in the rain.  Riiiiiiight. I can hear Open Space laughing from here.  Dammit!  We had a damp ride home!
