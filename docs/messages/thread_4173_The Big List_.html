Re: [letterboxingtexas] The Big List?

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-06-30 07:04:41 UTC-06:00

The Big List is the original letterboxing talk list. If you ever want to really get a feeling of the beginnings of letterboxing in the US, you should join that list and read the archives.

It has, unfortunately, spiraled into a cycle of flame wars that can be hard to stomach, but well worth it if you can read between the battles and learn the people. Quite often, clues are buried in the messages.

Many of the original letterboxers in the US continue to monitor or participate on the Big List.

That list isnt for the faint of heart, and newbies participating on it are not handled very kindly and that is the biggest reason why Mark Pepe began the Newboxers list, which has grown to be the largest letterboxing talk list.

If you care to join the Big List, the addy is here:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/letterbox-usa

Or if you want to join Newboxers:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newboxers
