From: janetsredbarn (janetsredbarn@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-21 15:27:24 UTC
I think y'all are reading something into this that isn't there. The
first time I placed a box, I left the "number of boxes" space empty
by mistake (I didn't know what I was doing). On the clue page it
showed the number of boxes as "-1". I went back in and edited the
clues to show 1 box in the space that I had left blank and then my
clue sheet properly showed 1 box placed. I tried one of my boxes
out just now by going into edit and deleting the number of boxes and
leaving the space blank. When I went back into my clue sheet, it
read -1 box. When I went back into edit, in the number of boxes
space it read "???", so I corrected it back to 1 box. This may be a
way of telling you that you forgot to enter the number of boxes.
Does that sound logical?
