Intro from NativTxn

From: Denise Vajdak (denise.vajdak@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-12-06 23:38:36 UTC-06:00

Howdy!

I just wanted to introduce myself. I am NativTxn (aka Denise) and I'm from the thriving metropolis of Snook, TX. For those of you not familiar with it, it is 12 miles southwest of Aggieland and is home to Slovacek Sausage and Chilifest.

I started Geocaching almost 3 years ago and one of my caching buddies introduced me to letterboxing along the way. I have found a few with her, but now I'm actively seeking them on my own. I like being able to look for landmarks better than keeping my nose in my GPS (smartphone).

I have liked rubber stamps since I was a kid and I'm now in my mid-forties and I still like them. I was into Stampin' Up for a while, but I am not the most artistic person. But you don't really HAVE to be to do this, do ya?

My first stamp I ever carved is the one in Chicken Fried Bacon. I think it turned out alright even though I got one of the N's backwards. I have carved 2 more since then and I just carved 2 small ones tonight that will become fleas.

I like all kinds of hide and seek, scavenger hunt type activities - geocaching (including OpenCaching.US and Terracaching.com), letterboxing and Munzees. My motto pretty much is "If you hide it, they will come". Most of my boxes are cross-posted to OCUS. It's not as heavily used as Geocaching.com, but the folks there are a lot more friendly and easier to work with.

When I'm not out in a park or cemetery or running around to my son's activities, I enjoy gardening, or yardening, and I prefer to use native plants. As my trail name implies, I am a native-born Texan. Thank the Lord for His favors!
