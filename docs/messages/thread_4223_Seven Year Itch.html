This coming weekend I am off for vacation to Glacier NP. We are flying in and out of Spokane, WA, the place 7 years ago I found my first letterbox with Silver Eagle at Finch Arboretum. I don't believe the Finch box is still there, but I am going to look, and hopefully I will finally be able stamp in to this box. Also, I will be planting a box in the park to memorialize our visit and begining into letterboxing ("7 Year Itch" box). All this after our visit to Glacier, so hopefully will not be eaten by a bear before getting to the letterbox!!
