We just wanted to thank you all for your hospitality and letting us
join in. We really enjoyed meeting ya'll and of course the boxing.
Everyone was so helpful, especially Barefoot Lucy with all her help
before the event with clues, suggestions and printouts. Her tribute
box was very thoughtful and obviously deserved.

All the kids were so well behaved and seemed to have a great time.
We commented on it on the way home. My daughter told me she made
several friends, although she didn't know any of their names.

On the down side we were silly enough to drive home today through
Dallas in the flash flood. It took us 5 1/2 hours to get from East
Dallas to Ardmore, enough said! We're exhausted but couldn't resist
taking a few min. to check our letterbox e-mails and seeing what
everyone said about the gathering.

Thanks again to everyone and remember you're welcome to cross the
river north and box anytime. Just let us know you're coming and
we'll be glad to help out.

Dewberry, my dad - Wes of HappyDays - loved the codes. The Oklahoma
boxers are in for a little more of a challenge I suspect from now on.

Thanks to Silver Eagle and Baby Bear for the chili and the warm
fire. Coming from a burn ban state it felt great!
