So, good bye now to her, and please make the introduction of my new and final name (to go along with the new and final stamp I mentioned earlier), Ashby.  Stay tuned for a new email to come later, that will reflect this name change. 
 
Looking forward to a long and happy time together and to getting to meet you all as time goes on.
 
Thanks!
