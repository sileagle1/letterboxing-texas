We spent the weekend camping and letterboxing in the hill country,
what a time we had! We stopped off in SA on our way home and looked
for COTTONWOOD, we found the spot where it should have been, but no
box to be found. Of course, it was so crowdwe that we couldn't
really look around too much, but I'm pretty sure it wasn't there. We
did find the stonehenge box in Hunt, if you are going to SA its not
too far away, its an amazing drive, and a VERY strange place. Only
in Texas. Have fun! -Mother of Four-
