I just want to publicly thank Lucy for the wonderful box that she put
out yesterday for Joan and I. Yesterday was the 4 month "anniversary"
of Joan's death, and I am sure that she would be so happy that she was
honored in such a way. The first box that Joan and I found was at the
footbridge, so placing the Friends are Friends Forever box at the
footbridge was perfect. Lucy, you are a wonderful friend. I thank
you from the bottom of my heart. Joan was a wonderful friend. The
last 4 months have been tremendously hard on me, and letterboxing has
kept me going. I do not post a lot of messages on the board, but I
feel as though the people in this group have become my friends, even
though I have yet to have the privilege of meeting most of you. This
hobby has also helped to bring me closer to my mom, who has gone on
several adventures with me, and my cousin who lives in grand prairie,
who I have managed to hook into the hobby. Again thank you to Lucy
for not only the Friends box, but also for being there for me, and
also to all of you for making the last few months more bearable.
