Palo Duro Canyon

From: remymom (remymom@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-06 13:31:09 UTC
Went on a letterbox hike yesterday to Palo Duro Canyon (TX panhandle)
and found Silver Eagle's Palo Duro Canyon Mouse box. :-) It was an
easy find, once I started looking from the right bench! Before that,
though- it was quite a hunt. I did go further down the trail to
Eoghan's lighthouse one, but it turns out to be a DNF because it was
getting dark and I didn't want to end up out there without a
flashlight! I will have to make another trip later on when it's not
as hot and I can get out there earlier! It's quite a hike (almost 6
miles round-trip)- and take your mountain bike if you have one- lots
of ups and downs on the trail.

Even though I grew up here, I still forget just how beautiful that
canyon is, especially at sunset. There are SO many places to hike in
this canyon- I've only barely begun to find boxes and caches out
there!

~Bunnybee in Amarillo
