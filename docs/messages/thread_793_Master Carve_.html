From: krohm (krohm-sub@charter.net) | Date: 2005-04-13 01:09:39 UTC-05:00
Re: [letterboxingtexas] Master Carve? The Michaels up here in Fort Worth carries it, but you have to know where to look.  It’s not ANYWHERE near the stamping supplies.  It’s in the very back of the store with the printmaking supplies.  I think most of the stuff we use was originally intended for carving linoleum and similar materials for making prints.  There’s a more specific name for this type of printmaking, but it escapes me at the moment.  Anyway, you might try asking for linoleum cutters and/or brayers or rollers.  The white stuff and the pink stuff are probably near there.  I had to do some describing before they figured out what I was looking for... and I think you have to find someone who is pretty knowledgeable about what’s in the store.
