Update on Palmetto State Park letterboxes

From: Bonnie Lind (bonnielind1@hotmail.com) | Date: 2013-11-03 16:33:45 UTC-06:00

Palmetto State Park added new trails and renamed trails since we were there for TALE 7.  One of the changes is that the Palmetto Trail is now the Palmetto Interpretive Trail and all the numbers were removed.  The trail that takes off between now missing numbers #8 and #9 has a name: Mesquite Flats Trail. 

 

With that in mind, I went to Palmetto State Park last weekend and checked on about half of the letterboxes that are currently listed.  I went back today hoping to check on the other half, but the park is closed due to flooding.  Sadly I won’t be able to go back for another month, so here is my current update:

 

Boxes still active as of 25 October and I sent suggested clue updates to owners:

Indian Stamp-pede by Bloomin Gramma Jo

Dream Symbolism—Time Keeper in Dreams by topcrop

Roasting Marshmallows by Baileys Bunch

La Placa Supremo by Dawns Glory

Freddy Fender (Music Box #14) by Viewfinder

Cowboy Meets Day of the Dead by Mosaic Butterfly

Biscuit Making Renegade by Shadow Boxer  (clues are current)

NEW!  Jurassic Palmetto Park, just listed today!  HIdden last week; I hope the floods didn’t wash them away.

 

Boxes missing (according to me—they may still be there, but I couldn’t find them):

Chayopin by buttercup:  (found the two small rocks, but no box)

Palmetto-Quilt Block Series by Lone Star Quilter (found strange looking tree and nearby oak, but no box)

Palmetto #2 by Baby Bear (looked behind every tree in the area)

Nacho Same-O Letterbox by Viewfinder  (found three trees, but the dead log was rotted away)

All Aboard! By Mazy Days (found tree with its “tongue stuck to frozen light pole,” but no box)

Salsa by Mosaic Butterfly (found bench, but no log)

Lydia Mendoza by Bell Motel (found rusty iron drain pipe, looked behind trees in 15 step radius, no box)

I’ll Get You, You Wascally Wenegade by Smitty

The Joker by TX Rebel

 

Boxes on my list that I did not have a chance to check:

Spanglish by SandiBox

Campfire Burned IRA by Baby Bear

The Tex-Mex by Boots Tex

Influences in Texas (3) by Beach Bum

Loteria (4) by Corazon

Swampy by SandiBox

Chilly Bean by Gryzzled Gryphon (I don’t have the clues)

 

If I missed listing your box and you would like for me to check on it, please let me know.

Mosaic Butterfly
