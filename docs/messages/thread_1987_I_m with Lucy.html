BL, you are not the only one who gets "sidetracked" while letterboxing.  Sometimes when I read how many boxes are found in such a short amount of time I am amazed.  It takes me too long to buckle everyone in their car seats and make sure they all have juice boxes and snacks, I guess.  Plus, no matter where we are, there are always rocks that need to be thrown in the water, or bugs to watch, or snakes to run away from (we had our own "close encounter" Monday, but that is a story for another day.)  But when we sit and look at our logbooks each stamp makes someone say, "Remember when we found that box....."  I think that is one of the best things about letterboxing, everyone enjoys and excels at a different aspect of it, and that is part of what makes it so fascinating.  MO5
