It’s funny how different folks can see things from different angles! Stillwater, Vandal & I walked over to the Keen Observer on Sunday afternoon and were so enthralled watching the alligators that I barely noticed any time spent in edging our way closer to the box. We thought it was a great spot and loved the stamp! It just might be my favorite TeamKing box yet.

But I was a little nervous about Vandal ending up as gator chow…;-)

I was the first finder for the Keen Observer box. TeamKing, in their infinite wisdom or due to a rather nasty sense of humor, take your pick, placed the box in a very high-traffic area and I spent a lot of time waiting for an opportunity to snag the box, and then a lot of time waiting for an opportunity to put it back, making this one of the most challenging boxes I've found. However, it was the first time I was first finder, so I felt well-rewarded for my patience!
