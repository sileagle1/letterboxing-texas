Texas/Louisiana Handshake Initiative coming to a close

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-05-29 22:40:44 UTC-05:00
I just wanted to remind everyone that the border initiative is coming to a close on the 31st of May.  We've had lots of new boxes planted in the area and several folks will be receiving a special Handshake LTC from me to commemorate their participation, and JBanana has generously offered a LTC to anyone who earns the Handshake LTC from me AND who has logged finds in Shreveport.  AND she has a further reward for anyone who earns the Handshake LTC and also plants a box in Shreveport.
 
To know the exact areas, go here:
 
http://tx-lahandshake.blogspot.com/
And don't forget to log your boxes in the database on the group...

--
Barefoot Lucy
"It's not about footwear, it's about philosophy"
