Update on the hoolie-hoolie monster

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-03-02 20:35:01 UTC-06:00
Well I thought I would let y'all know that Fearless Fox successfully identified the hoolie-hoolie monster he originally heard on the 4C's Trail a few weeks ago and that we kept an eye for when we went together to the 4C.  He was able to do this because he heard them again - last night - in South Shores! 
 
Are you curious?  Well - it was a wild turkey - and not the bottled variety, I might add!
 
The "experts" we asked all had thought that it sounded like turkeys, but they were puzzled because turkeys roost at night.  The best we can figure is that they were disturbed and on the move when they came upon Lightning's and Fearless Fox's camp and started the hoolie-hoolying!
He heard it distinctly again this weekend and we saw the proof for ourselves as we were leaving today - a turkey walking about in one of the empty campsites.  The odd little noise he was making was a much quieter hoolie-hoolie, but if there were several of them and they were disturbed, I can imagine it would be loud and daunting. They ARE odd creatures!
 
I think that Fearless Fox would have felt much more comfortable camping by himself after learning what a hoolie-hoolie is, except that Silver Eagle told us quite a story about having a bear to dinner last night...
