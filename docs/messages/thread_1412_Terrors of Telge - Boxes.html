Just a quick note on boxes placed in Telge Park for event.
1. Bring insect spray!
2. Many of the boxes I found Saturday were laying out in the open,
including my own. I have changed my clues a little (as now buried box
at base of the small tree).
3. This park will flood if we get heavy rain, so if box is permanent
you may want to make sure it is on high ground. Mr. Bones is in area
that is often a swamp in spring time.

Great Stamps by all. I had a great time running around the park
finding all your boxes. Thanks, and see y'all Saturday!!!
