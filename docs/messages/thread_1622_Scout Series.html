Scout Series

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-25 06:13:23 UTC-08:00
I finally got the clues for my Scout Series written
and posted. I hate that I procrastinate so badly in
writing up clues!

For those of you who don't remember what I was doing,
I placed a training series geared toward scouts so
that there would be some boxes out there for scout
groups to cut their teeth on before they approached
"real" boxes in the wild. Hopefully by the time they
finish my series, they will understand the points that
are important to letterboxers.

I've posted them on LBNA, but I've written them up on
a webpage to give some general information about
letterboxing. Scout leaders would have to email me
for the link so I can follow who is doing the series.
This is the link I would give to them. I'm giving
them to y'all now so you can go find the series if you
want when you come to the gathering or otherwise, but
also I'm hoping you'll review them for me and let me
know if you think there is anything I should add!

http://www.geocities.com/barefootlucy

Begin at the link that says "Clues for Scout Series"

Thanks!
Lucy

Hey Lucy,

Great idea for a Scout series! Being a letterboxer and a Scout
myself....I love the idea of teaching kids to letterbox responsibly!

And I thought your instructions were great. Lots of good safety
and "rules to live by" info. The only thing is that I couldn't read
the text very well on the page with the black and white textured
background. Awesome graphic for your series, but me and the kiddos
couldn't read the words on that one page.

If we are able to attend the gathering, my daughter wants to take
the time to do the series. Especially since her Cub Scout brother
wouldn't be able to be there with us! She thinks it'll be a great
bargaining tool...."if you'll clean my room, I'll stamp the Scout
series for you!?!"

Even if we're not able to make the gathering, we will definitely
make the trip to do the Series at a later date.
