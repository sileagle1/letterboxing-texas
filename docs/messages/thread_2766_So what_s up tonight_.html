From: William Watson (sldawgs@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-13 14:16:47 UTC
This is a cool web site. I use it to find some of my Texas
governors. I'm also a volunteer on the site. I get requests for
photographs of local headstones, so I go out and take pictures and
post them. By registering, you can edit local cemeteries, add
pictures and comments about your ancestors, etc. It's a great
resource for genealogists. And, as you have pointed out, you can
find some very interesting dead people (or reptiles) hiding out in
the most unexpected places.

Boots Tex
