NOT a Rant & Rave!

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-08-02 14:40:47 UTC-05:00
Since I was recently stomping around in my best imitation of a grinch,
complaining loudly about folks who don't replace letterboxes thoughtfully, I
figured I'd give equal time to singing the praises of some new letterboxers
in our area!
The Cummins Clan are terrific - I've found our boxes after they've stamped
in, and everything was replaced (and I think from their stamp, they have
their kids along - so obviously good role models, too!).
They have emailed us every time they found our boxes and let us know when
maintenance was needed, or just passed along a much-appreciated compliment.

They responded super fast when some out-of-state boxers, Nautilus & Culex
were looking for a special HH which had been left in one of our boxes.

Hats off to the Cummins Clan!

Please keep looking for our boxes!!!
RubyT.
