Great finds today!

From: Tara Burton (mselly13@juno.com) | Date: 2008-08-10 04:36:35 UTC
The Duck Ducks...Jeff and Tara had a great day out boxing today and
we wanted to share it with y'all.
The very first day we started this adventure we couldn't find a few
particular boxes by Catpawz2004. Since these are very close to us
we have gone back several times to look for them. We have even
taken our older son along thinking maybe we were doing something
wrong.
Well, Forest Park was fine. Found it, it just doesn't get much
visitors. But Oak Creek we could never find until today and we
finally did it. It was in bad shape. We cleaned it up, replaced
the box and moved to a more secure location out of danger. Since
catpawz2004 has not been heard from in awhile we have arranged to
adopt these two boxes for now. We consider it just babysitting for
awhile.
We also found a long lost HH today, which was a first for us. So we
will be setting it back free tomorrow, I can't wait.
There is one more box by catpawz that we have not been able to
locate and we will be going back to it again tomorrow. I am not
leaving that park until I have found that box!
So I just wanted y'all to know that Hunt County is really getting
busy with boxes. Head this way and you are sure to get some good
finds. Happy boxing!
