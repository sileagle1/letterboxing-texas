Latest letterboxing adventure

From: tonialmo (tonialmo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-10-12 00:56:51 UTC
One of our favorite bands, The Flatlanders, played Gruene Hall Friday
night. We stayed overnight in New Braunfels to letterbox Saturday
morning and got five with two MIA. While we were hanging out near
Gruene Hall waiting for the "coast to clear" and the sun to rise, we
came up with a codeword for a letterbox: "Molly's bracelet". I'm
curious, do other letterboxing teams have their own codewords?

On the downside, I have a box ready to plant in a town just off Hwy. 90
and I left it at home! I first put it in the bag with our changes of
clothes, then decided to put it in my Pooh (Winnie the Pooh) backpack
and must have got distracted during the transfer and left it sitting on
the kitchen table. That's what I get for trying to make decisions at
4:30 am! (BRC works in San Antonio and I went to work with him and
hung out all day Friday. He leaves the house in Uvalde at 5:15 am.)

He promises me that we will go out next weekend and make a special trip
to plant it.
