From: Gingercat (gingercat0217@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-05 07:54:01 UTC-08:00
Hi,
I'm from Austin and Sea Dove is right, The Austin TOWN
Butterfly is fairly close to the part of town you'll
be in. That same park has the Park & Hide in it.
*sigh* I've gone 3 times to look but have never found
either of them. I think it's just that there are so
many trails in that park (which constitute most of the
clues) and it's easy for me to confuse them.
The Austin Highlights 1 is actually in the Arboretum,
but I've heard that it's gone missing. It's an easy
one to search for though and Amy's icecream is nearby
as a consolation prize if it is gone. It was planted
near some fun life-sized granite cows which are a
must-see.
Another one that'll be close to you and quite a fun
hike is the 360 Bridge. Try that one if you have the
opportunity. Beautiful views!
A little farther away down the road on MoPac are a
couple in a nice little area called the Barton Creek
Greebelt. One is, naturally, the Barton Creek
Greenbelt and another is Hiking Pug.
There's one called Smith Falls that may be near those
two, but I haven't been to it yet.
Have fun in Austin! On weekends my letterboxing
sister-in-law works at a coffee shop near the
Arboretum called Trianon. Her name is Jill and she
might be able to help out with directions.
Gingercat
