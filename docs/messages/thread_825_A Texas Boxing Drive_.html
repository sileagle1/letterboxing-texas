A Texas Boxing Drive?

From: TeamKing (teamking@entouch.net) | Date: 2005-04-27 19:38:18 UTC-05:00
I'm seeing these drives they keep having up in the northeast and I'm
wondering why don't we have one down here? Well, obviously, it would
have to be held in one city (we're in that BIG state :)) But if I
understand them correctly, boxes are planted all along a route and they
all go out on a certain day and do the 'drive' - finding them.

So, anyone interested in doing this? I'll have to say it would be
around Houston for the first one. You would have to commit to creating
some boxes and planting them. But even if just a few people wanted to
do it, I would be game.

Just throwing it out there for discussion. . .

TeamKing
