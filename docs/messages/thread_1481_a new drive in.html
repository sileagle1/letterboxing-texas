One of my fondest memories of growing up in Brownwood is my best friend's mother taking all us kids to the drive-in. She would pile all of us in the car and away we would go. We always got out and went to sit in the metal chairs they had on a cement slab outside the concession stand. It was such magic to a little kid, being outside in the dark, the huge screen, the taste of the burgers we always got to eat.
