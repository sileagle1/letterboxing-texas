...and if you're on the west side of FW, there's a great zoo (on University Drive, south of I 30) and the Science Museum - great kids' museum, hands-on stuff, dinosaur digs, all that, on Montgomery, just across the parking lot from the WIll Rogers Coliseum, the Cowgirls Hall of Fame (also fun for kids - they can "ride" in a rodeo on video), and of course, the Kimbell and Amon Carter and Modern Art Museum -- if your kids like art, or for you if you do, and a great lawn for running and playing (and some of Kantexan's great LBs are there!) - and of course the Botanic Garden and Japanese Gardens (more Kantexan Letterboxes there, too!) and Log Cabin Village just across from the zoo on University, not far from TCU.  All great places to go with families!  (And University Village for some good shopping, tucked in between all those attractions, off University, south of I 30.)  Supposed to be a good airshow at Alliance this weekend, too - if the weather cooperates.  Or go to Cabela's Outfitters and look at the animal dioramas, just off I 35 at 170, close to Alliance Airport.  Enjoy your visit to FW, and hope you'll come back under better circumstances!  (or come on out toward Keller, Bedford, Colleyville,Southlake and Grapevine and find MY letterboxes!)  -- Viewfinder
