From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-27 17:26:12 UTC-07:00
Okay, I've gotten a good bit of feedback on dates and
I've been doing some research about the Johnson Ellis
Navarro (JEN) area, so here is the beginning of a
plan.

Date: 10/23/05
Base: Waxahachie

I'm sorry to those couple folks who said they couldn't
be there the 23rd - that just seems to be the date
when the fewest folks had conflicts.

Waxahachie is the dead center of the three counties.
I felt like that was important because of the nature
of what we'll be doing - we'll be scattering in the
morning to plant, then regathering around noon to EAT
and EXCHANGE, then going off to find.

I checked with my scouter friends who are more
familiar with that area than I am, and they tell me
there is no camping except RV camping in or around
Waxahachie. The closest camping is Bardwell Lake,
operated by the Army Corps of Engineers, about 20
miles away. My family will be camping there in all
likelihood. I understand it's nice camping (bear in
mind, that "nice" in scout terms, so that may not be
heavy on amenities, but they do compare it to State
Parks). Here's the website for the Bardwell camping
area closest to Waxahachie.

http://www.swf-wc.usace.army.mil/bardwell/main.htm

Right now, I'm trying to get as complete a list as I
can of towns in the three counties, and I will follow
that with some historical background and listings of
parks and trails, so that those who want to
participate can come armed with ideas and boxes. If
anyone wants to help with this (or any) part of the
project, let me know!

I've got my eye on a park in Waxahachie to set up as a
home base. I'm hoping we can have power so we can
have a computer to post clues to, to make clue
distribution easier and to make posting to LBNA
possible right away.

I am going to set up a webpage here shortly to post
updates to, and I'll post the information I have so
far there and then shoot the link out to y'all!

Lucy
