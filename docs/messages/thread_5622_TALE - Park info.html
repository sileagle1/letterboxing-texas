Be aware that many trails are closed in the park, so get current map or read on website about trails(alerts).  


Also, per website they are having 3 different guided hikes on Saturday, so be aware you may run into a large group with rangers.  One hike leaves from in front of dining hall we will be at, and looks like a good topic if anyone wants to join in.   Starts at 2pm - 3pm - and talks about the CCC.   Other hikes are at 10 - 11 and 1-2pm, both leaving from the playground.  


The lake is gone, and the road that use to circle park stops where the lake dam was, so you have to go around the park on right side (as entering). 


Don't forget to bring folding chairs just in case.   The dining hall has huge back patio, but no chairs on it.   Great news is the hall has air conditioner, which we may need since temps may go into 80's.    Looks like chances of rain, so bring you rain gear!   With warm weather comes snakes, so be aware and careful.


Look forward to seeing most of you Friday night at Southside BBQ!!!   You will love it!   Brisket is excellent.
