From: Hoppe, Dionicia Janne (markd4good@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-21 16:40:43 UTC-07:00
Hello letterboxing friends,
My trail name is hoppers of seabrook, I travel with
helter-skelter and we have 2 little ones, Moona is 11
and Dittr is 6. We are neophyte boxers, but we've
planted about 8 boxes and found about 25 now. Moona
and I learned about letterboxing at girl scout camp.
We are trying to put a letterbox in each of the parks
of seabrook because we think its a unique area and we
want other boxers to experience our local beauty. We
also get down to galveston fairly often and hope to
plant more down there for the boxing touristas that
come each summer.

Is Houston still letterboxing capital of NA? too cool,
good to be known for something besides obesity.

BTW, IKEA today had these great little packs of
unlined spiral bound notebooks, about 4" x 3" and they
are 99cents!!

Cheers all,
hoppers
