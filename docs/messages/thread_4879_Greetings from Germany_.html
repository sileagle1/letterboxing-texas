Greetings from Germany.

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2011-09-12 03:34:59 UTC-06:00

We are having an absolute BLAST here. The weather has been perfect, and
catching up with most of my ham radio friends has been wonderful. My wife's
family and us came together as if we have not been gone 26 years. Today I
toured the base where I was assigned. It was all the same, but all
different too. With the end of the cold ear and the falling of the wall,
the mission changed. There is no need for a Cavalry Troop to watch the
Fulda Gap for Russian tanks now.

I had forgotten how beautiful the forests here are. We took a short hike
this morning and it was wonderful. This weekend we are going to play radio
in what was formerly enemy territory, East Germany. No time for
letterboxing so far - but we did bring it along.

Hope you all are well.

Vy73 - Mike - KD9KC.
W5-SOTA association manager.
Vacationing in Northern Bavaria.
Rhone Mountains, Strahlungen,Germany.
