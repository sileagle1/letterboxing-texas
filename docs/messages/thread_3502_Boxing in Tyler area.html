From: cwenter1 (cwenter1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-20 22:47:05 UTC
Hi Bloomin Grandma Jo,

I am so sorry that I am going to miss you while you're visiting Tyler.
I am currently in California taking care of "MOM" business. I return
home to Tyler on Wednesday, April 23rd for 1 1/2 days and I leave again
on Friday, April 25th to Lubbock for a bowling tournament. I plan on
doing some letterboxing in the Lubbock area while I'm there.

As far as letterboxes in Tyler, there are 3 in Rose Rudman Park. "How
Hot is it East Texas?" (puzzle to solve first), "Ring the Bell", and a
new one by Blue Butterfly called "America's Rose Grower".

Also, JB Kokopelli's "September Holiday Series" in Whitehouse is very
nice and is about 5 miles from Tyler. "The Planetarium" and "Soccer
Rules" by JKC is good, and "Children's Park" is nice. There are many
more that you may want to check out on Atlas Quest.

Have fun and good hunting.

Mothers Love
