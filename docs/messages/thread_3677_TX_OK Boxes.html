Re: TX/OK Boxes

From: Tara Burton (mselly13@juno.com) | Date: 2008-08-01 14:24:40 UTC
We were up there for Mother's Day and planted Arbuckle SOS at Arbuckle
Wilderness. It can be gotten even if the park is closed already. You
would really make my husband's day if you got that one. It was his
first carve and first plant. Other than that we didn't have much luck
either. There are some closer to the OK/TX border. But also if you
look, there are only like 200 and something boxes in OK but over 1100
in TX. You could do some planting in the area.
I also know that of the few listed, one was listed as a letterbox but
when we found it, it was actually a geocache with no stamp. If you
send me a PM I'll be happy to tell you which one but I don't want to
out it in public. Good Luck. When are you going?
