Hello everyone,

I have decided that since our little group is growing and becoming
it's own organism, I'd make a stamp. Since Texas is so big and not
everyone would be able to visit a little box in Victoria, I decided
to make it a PLB. Everyone in the group is invited. If you'd like
to recieve it, please email me your address off list, or contact me
in the Texas/Oklahoma forum at www.lbworld.org

I don't plan on retiring it. Whenever we gain a new Texas
letterboxer, it is theirs for the asking, as long as it is returned
to me.

I won't have a cutoff date for the original list for a few days as I
have not made the stamp. Once it is complete I'll let everyone know
and give a date then.


-MonkeyToes.
