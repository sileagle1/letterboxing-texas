Hello Susan! I am just 11 miles south of you, in Skidmore. There are
3 letterboxes in Evergreen Cemetery between Beeville and Skidmore,
too... nearer to Skidmore.

Have you started carving your stamp yet? Or gotten a log book?

I have only been letterboxing since August of 2002 - not quite 6
months. Let's see...an intro... I am 51, married for 33 years, have
5 children and 5 grandchildren. I write a newspaper column -
Skidmore Sketches in the Beeville Bee-Picayune. My interests besides
my family and letterboxing are native plants, gardening, herbs,
nature, reading, history, paper arts, scarecrows and other 'yard
art', the internet, teaching religious education to mostly second
graders, and lots of other things.

I am hoping to get back to letterboxing as soon as this cold, windy
weather passes and my car is out of the shop.

Welcome to a great hobby!
