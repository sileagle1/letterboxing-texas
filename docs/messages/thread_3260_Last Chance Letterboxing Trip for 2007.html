Last Chance Letterboxing Trip for 2007

From: corazon78121 (connie@lavernia.net) | Date: 2008-01-01 02:55:53 UTC
This morning Navigator and I set out for a "last chance" trip for the
year. After replacing One Stoplight Town (pulled due to landscaping)
and Bear With Me (pulled due to excess moisture)we trekked to
Stockale to plant a new box "The Watermelon Patch". This box is so
new the clues aren't even posted yet.

Then the real fun began when we made it to Yorktown and were first
finders of "Setting Sun in Woods Cemetery". This is an excellent box
by a new placer, and I've invited her to join us here and introduce
herself.

Next we headed the truck to Goliad to find Blue Butterfly's Mission
Espiritu Santo. Despite what appear to be very clear,
straightforward clues, we didn't find it. I hope it's still there
and we just missed it. I can imagine what a beautiful stamp it would
be.

Well, it's time to post the clues for the box in Stockdale, then on
to my New Year's celebration. I'm going to drink a couple of wine
coolers and let the TV do all the driving.

Happy New Year to everyone here!
Corazon
