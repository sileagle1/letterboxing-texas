Ok, ok, I know this has been said before but I want to say it again. I
love children and they are great letterboxers. I have a little problem
with their Moms and Dads though. Children are Children and just
sometimes they are not as careful to replace the letterboxes and
recover them well. Either they don't know how or they don't understand
the point but Moms and Dads please suppervise them and check to see if
they have done it correctly before going on to the next box. Children
do not know unless we teach and show and sometimes show and teach
again. Let's make it fun for the children at the same time teaching
them. We all will enjoy letterboxing better.
