Curse You Vandals!:(

From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-22 01:24:21 UTC

Hey All,
The Pikachu Ponderer got a taste of the dark side of humanity
today. We went to check on his first box he had placed last August-
Green Oaks and the box was opened, the book ripped to pieces with
the pages strewn everywhere and the stamp was gone. Someone had
filled several pages with profanities. Luckily I got hold of the
pages first and shielded his tender eyes from them.
Our first letterboxing tradgedy. :(
I tried to assure him that most people weren't losers like that, but
it's hard to see your offspring experience that kind of
disappointment.
Thank God for the wonderful Texas boxers and all those from other
states who stamped his book and wrote really nice things about his
box. That really offset the bad part.
I guess it just reminds us to always make sure the box is where no
one but LBers will find it. It looked like a social trail had
formed to the box and beyond it.
We won't let it stop us, though. Letterboxing is still our favorite
hobby!
-Kantexan
