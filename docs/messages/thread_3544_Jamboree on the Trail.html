Jamboree on the Trail

From: JimGregEricRachelKeli Chancellor (chancelier_de_lbu@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-11 08:19:53 UTC-07:00

Well, Jamboree on the Trail is over and our Cub Scout Pack had a great time.  They did some letterboxing which was made to help them with their compass skills; fished; worked on a fancy knot, had a scavenger hunt geared toward tree and plant identification, cooked hot dogs over a campfire and made smores.  What else can you ask for?

 

The Jamboree on the Trail series of letterboxes are now open game for anyone to find.  I have placed them on LBNA and Atlas Quest.

 

I was thinking about the gathering next Saturday and noted that there are going to be about 40 letterboxes available within less than 10 miles of Palestine by the gathering time.  I hope this is enough to draw some more people to come. 

 

Jim
