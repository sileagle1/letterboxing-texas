From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-09-21 17:39:50 UTC
Fungus Woman is a long-distance friend of mine and TeamKing. Her dad
was a great, enthusiastic boxer. He also loved postal boxes, and was
eager to see the variety of stamps featured in them. Every now and
again, you would see his stamp in your PLB log, but usually he just
silently played the game.

He was a member of this list, and although he never posted, he avidly
read every message.

If you came to the Texas gathering, he had sent a bingo stamp carved
for the Event. It was the line drawing of a steam engine. I sent it
back to him, with the log, and he was thrilled to see everyone who
stamped in.

Although I never met him, he sounds like a great guy. I know he has a
terrific daughter, and that's a legacy in and of itself.

Dewberry
