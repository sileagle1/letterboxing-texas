From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-10-13 23:49:57 UTC-05:00
It took me awhile to realize that if I waited until I saw someone to find out if they had a PT, I was probably waiting too long!  There are a FEW cases where the PT is had for the asking, but many of them require answering a question of some sort.  Unless you are a Jeopardy champion, you likely won't know the answers!
 
I finally got smart and looked up all the PTs I could find on AQ and LBNA and printed off the ones for folks I thought I would like to meet or be likely to meet.  And then I discovered there are other places to look too!
 
The point of this is (for folks who have never been to a gathering) plan ahead for PTs.  And remember that there are more than just literary PTs out there.  A little legwork this weekend will pay off for you next weekend!
