My hubby, which I've often referred to as my LSS, informed me after this weekend that he's definetly NOT an LSS now. (I honestly think it was the excitement of the whole snake thing. I've decided to carve a Perky P stamp also. So we'll have those to exchange with everyone next time we meet again. :)
