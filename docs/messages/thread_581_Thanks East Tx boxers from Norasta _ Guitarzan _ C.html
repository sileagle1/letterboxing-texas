Hello East Texas Lbers! We had a great time finding boxes in January
something we usually can't do back here in Kingston, Ontario
Canada.
Here is a summary of what we found from Jan 11 to 19th:
All 5 boxes at the Mercer Arboretum! Yippee!! Highstepping HH as
well.
Tomball found Opossum, Funny Bunny, Bear Contry, Mr. LadyBug,
Frog's Life and The Hunter
Livingston State PK box is missing, its hidey hole has been
boarded up.
Huntsville State Pk Raven, Red Dragon -did not find either box
despite a thorough search. Clues were clear and we did find the
locations described, could someone be taking the boxes? Red Dragon
may be buried in the stump as it is really mushy and we didn't
want to knock it apart. We left a box nearby and will post some
cryptic clues soon.
Huntsville found Steambost Sam very cool place!
Bastrop State Park boy those trails are confusing! Lots of lost
souls wandering around so we didn't feel too bad. We found Cool
Cat and Trail Rider. Skinny Pine has vanished.
Martin Dies State PK found Can U Canoe, Day Hiker and Squirrelly
Camper. The 6 foot stump described in Squirrelly camper is now a 6
foot log! Took us awhile to figure this out.
Montgomery found Ancient Sun Dancer and Dragonfly. Did not
locate Lone Star Hiker, stump described is crumbling. Could the hiker
be in there?
Washington on the Brazos Found Texas Flag, Texas Birth. Texas
Flag box was filled with green slimey water, we dried it out as best
we could. Left box upside down so water could drain out. It will
likely need a new logbook. Couldn't find DrooP's Washington
on the
Brazos but we may not have found the right tree.
We also placed a box in Newton at Caney Creek Park and will post clues
soon.
Many Thanks for the wonderful time, we found 22 boxes and even took my
82 year old Mother- in- law on some of the hikes!
Happy Trails to you.....
Norasta, Guitarzan and Guitarzan's Mom
