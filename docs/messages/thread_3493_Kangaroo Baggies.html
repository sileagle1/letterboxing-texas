I saw the coolest plastic baggie in a postal I got the
other day. It had a big main pouch, then a smaller one
attached to the front of it. The owner of the box used
the smaller pouch for the stamp and the bigger one for
the logbook. I contacted the owner and she said they
were called kangaroo baggies and she found them on
ebay. However whenever I went to ebay the only thing
that came up when I typed in kangaroo baggies were
hospital type IV bags or burlap type bags. Nothing
like what I saw in this box.

Does anyone know what I'm talking about? If so, do you
know where I can find them? I would love to use them
for my boxes that I plant.
