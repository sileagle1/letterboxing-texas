Been a busy week. I have been meaning to send an E-mail to you but I just
got buried in stuff.

If the Camel-Baks didn't arrive today, they should be there tomorrow
(Saturday).

Lemme know how you like them, or if you have any questions.

For COLD-FRONT's Camel-Bak, study the straps carefully - looking for the
Velcro closures used to roll up the extra strap length. You may need to
lengthen the straps a bit, but from what you say it may be you need to
shorten them. Just get them adjusted, then roll the extra up and Velcro it
like you find it now.

I HATE straps dangling and catching on bushes etc.

Waiting to hear......
