From: rangefinder_lbntx (texastoryteller@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-11-17 22:23:13 UTC
Hello to everyone! A couple of months ago the letterboxing bug crept into my life and before I knew it I had been bitten hard. I live in Denton and go by the trail name of Rangefinder, so far the only one of my family to adopt an alternate moniker. I look forward to finding your boxes and to perhaps meeting you at events!

Oh, and am I correct in understanding that letterboxers who meet one another on the trail can identify themselves by asking "Which way is North?"

~Rangefinder
