From: Margaret Sanders (Classact527@nts-online.net) | Date: 2005-05-27 19:11:22 UTC-05:00
Hi everybody... I'm Margaret.  I've been married to the same wonderful guy for 37 years.  We have 3 grown children (2 are twins) and a step daughter.  We also have 7 grandkids ranging in age from 21-1.  My hubby recently retired and our oldest daughter got us into letterboxing.  It is great fun and we even get some exercise.  We recently went to Oklahoma City and found several letterboxes there as well as the ONE letterbox here in the Wichita Falls area.
 
Glad to be a part of the group!
 
Margaret
