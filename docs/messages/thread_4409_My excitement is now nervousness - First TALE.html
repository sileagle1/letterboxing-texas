Wow, nothing this scary happened to me before the first TALE, but I was nervous. This was back in 2004 when Baby Bear and I had decided to host the first TX LB Event (not counting the Dallas Gathering in 1998 among the few first TX letterboxers). When I started planning, there was no TX Talklist, so I had no idea how many letterboxers existed in TX or would be willing to come to an Event. The only way I could invite people was through the LBNA Talklist and via emails to the few letterboxers I had received messages from. So when we were driving up to Bastrop we thought it was quite possible we would be the only ones attending! As hosts, we were also worried about the weather, the facilities and how to make it fun if anyone did come. What a pleasant surprise when over 30 enthusiastic people showed up on a beautiful day and happily entertained themselves without any help from us. That is why I still don't plan too many activities (unless requested) at TALE because everyone seems to just enjoy meeting and talking with each other, then letterboxing. As a host, I still worry about some things, but letterboxers are great, and no matter what happens they always make the best of it. Looking forward to meeting new friends and visiting with existing ones!
