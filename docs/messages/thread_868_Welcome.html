From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-02-25 15:14:34 UTC
Welcome to the several new people on this List! I realize you might
be a little leery of meeting us in person, but if you want to attend
TALE5, I think you will have fun. There will be demos on carving and
discussions on the pros and cons of box placement, so it can be a
learning experience, too. There are already several people planning
on attending that have never been to a TX LB Event, so why not join
us? I will include links to it at the end of this post.

As far as carving quality goes, practice does help, and you will be
amazed how much better you will get. However, some people will be
better carvers than others no matter how much practice is involved, a
fact I have learned to live with. I am no artist and will never be
great at carving, but I carve well enough to please myself and I
enjoy doing it, so that is good enough for me.

Silver Eagle
