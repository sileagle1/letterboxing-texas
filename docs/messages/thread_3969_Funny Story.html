Funny Story

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2009-02-23 21:36:17 UTC
I just noticed that Baileys Bunch posted a box called Roasting
Marshmallows and it reminded me of something funny that happened during
my South Tx LB trip. When Baby Bear and I were in Palmetto SP, we
split up to each hide our boxes. I looked around until I finally found
a tree that looked appropriate and started attaching my box to the back
limb. I was just about to start writing the clues when I noticed a
suspicious pile of sticks at the base. After picking up a few sticks,
what do you think I found? Yep, a letterbox. And a box with no clues,
since I looked for it when I got home. Needless to say I moved my box,
but I had the pleasure of stamping into a box before it was even
posted. Oh, and I did call Baby Bear over to share.

What is even funnier is that just before our trip there was a big
discussion on the Big List about hiding boxes too close to other ones,
and here I almost hid one at the same tree!

Silver Eagle
