Still Rainin'

From: janet clark (jayceeinbedford@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-04 20:54:37 UTC-07:00
I didn't do any hunting today, but I was productive. I went to Hobby Lobby (they were closed). I always forget their hours. So, I scooted on over to Joann's (nothing interesting there). Then, I went to Michael's in Southlake, I wanted to see if they had anything besides speedy cut (they didn't). My oldest went with me. She bought a personal stamp (I haven't talked her into carving her own yet, give her time). Then, we asked the worker if there were any small "logbooks", he said he didn't know of any, but to check the sale bins. I had already done that, but looked some more and what do you know, hiding down in the address books were some note pads (binded) with blank white pages (hopefully not too thin of paper) and they were only 50 cents each. I bought a handful. I think they will do just fine. We'll test them out tomorrow before we plant one. So, I've got my box, tape, hand carved stamp and now the logbook. Now all that's left is to get brave and plant it with the clues.
 
I will post the results here, maybe even tomorrow. Wish me luck.
