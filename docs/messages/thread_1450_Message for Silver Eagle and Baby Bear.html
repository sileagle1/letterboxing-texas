Message for Silver Eagle and Baby Bear

From: remymom (remymom@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-07 19:32:31 UTC
Hey guys- I had the occasion to LB in the Kerrville area this past
weekend! What fun!!

Anyway, Silver Eagle- I found your 13th Floor Musicians box without
any trouble. The logbook though- was damp and unfortunately most of
the images have melted away into smears of rainbow colors. :-( I
guess any of you LBers who have been to this box- you might just
have to go back and stamp your image again. ;-) But otherwise, the
box is intact and doing fine.

Baby Bear- I found your Honest Abe box too- great stamp! The box was
just laying on the ground in plain sight by the tree- and the lid is
cracked (with a hole in it) but everything inside was completely dry
and fine. I put it back up 'in' the tree and off the ground when I
left it. And is your "Park Ranger" LB gone? I had the clues printed
out from the computer awhile back so I went to look for it on this
trip, but didn't find it. And now it seems that the clues aren't
online anymore anyway (If I'm not confusing your box with something
else I looked at)- so I assume maybe it's been MIA for awhile.

And Baby Bear- are you Angi too? I found the Stonehenge II box on my
trip, too. The baggie covering the whole thing needs to be replaced,
but the box itself and the contents are fine. I thought I had some
extra baggies with me to replace it, but all my ziplocks were the
smaller size. The location was a neat place. And the whole
Stonehenge thing was crazy! So many neat things I'd never see if it
weren't for LB (or geocaching) to take me out there.

Thanks for your boxes, guys!! They were all fun. :-)

~Bunnybee in Amarillo
