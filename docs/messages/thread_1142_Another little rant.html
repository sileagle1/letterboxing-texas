Another little rant

From: Lena Bernard (lennire@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-31 09:00:21 UTC-07:00
Okay, my hubby made a liar out of me. We went boxing
yesterday when I said we'd probably wait until the
weather cooles down.

We went to a relatively new box and had a nice(?) long
walk. When we got to the box, we were a little
disappointed. I know, I'm picky -already- about
quality carved stamps. We were tired after that long
hot walk. We felt it the walk would have been worth it
if the stamp were more artistic. I know I'll probably
get flack for this, but we were hot and tired so don't
be too harsh on me.

FW Botanical gardens... We went a few weeks ago to try
to find the one in the japanese gardens. I'm sure we
were looking in the right place, but the box was
missing. I just contacted baby bear thru the website.
But if someone here who is nearby and found the box
already ... wanna check it?

We also found, yesterday, the slumbering family tree.
I loved the find and the tree itself. So no compaints
on this one. We just love the botanical gardens. I'm
planning to plant one in another garden or another
area within the bg's the next time we go.

I decided to check on Polly's Paws as well. I wanted
to see who had come by and guess who ... GG (among the
others)! The box was there but it wasn't covered up
and it just looked like someone just tossed it into
it's place. I mean, I wasn't very close and the box
was pretty obvious. I replaced it UNDER my debris I
had previously collected. Other than that, the box was
still sealed pretty good. We just had a storm and
nothing was wet or wilted.

So I want to stress too, PLEASE recover very well!

My 2c.
