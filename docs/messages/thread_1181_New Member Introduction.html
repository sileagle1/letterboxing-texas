From: mollygruetx (mollygruetx@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-13 13:33:54 UTC
Hello:

This is Molly Grue. I've just joined the letterboxingtexas group, and
was asked to introduce myself.

I letterbox with my children, Spirit and Rain, ages 4 and 6. My
husband usually tags a long. We've been casually letterboxing for a
couple of years, which means we usually letterbox when we go camping
twice a year or when we happen to be near a letterbox.

I missed a couple of gatherings, but hope to make one soon.

I've had plans for hiding a couple letterboxes nearby (I live in the
country outside of Brenham) for about a year, but do not yet have
enough faith in my carving ability. I'm still working on it by
carving stamps for all my nieces and nephews to use in letterboxing.

This is a great hobby, as you well know, and even though we only do
it occasionally, we enjoy it immensely.
