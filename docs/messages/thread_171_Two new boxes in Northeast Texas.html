Two new boxes in Northeast Texas

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-13 15:34:53 UTC-07:00
The kids and I got out yesterday and planted a couple
of boxes we've been planning. It sure was hot, but we
had fun!

The first one was planted at Tiger Creek Wiildlife
refuge - a place north of Tyler that rescues big cats.
It's a really neat place and they do good work. I
hope y'all will try to go see it. I planted the stamp
so that you don't have to pay to go in and get it, but
you do have to go during their business hours, since
the stamp is inside their property.

The other stamp I planted is at Tyler State Park.
Both are now listed on LBNA.

Hope everyone else had a productive weekend too!
Lucy
