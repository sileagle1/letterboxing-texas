I ran across something that was of particular interest
to me the other day!

The town of Milford in Ellis County was established
when settlers moved to the area from Cherokee County,
where they had tired of the lawlessness, boozing, and
general exhibition of bad morals.

Now I live in Cherokee County and I found this to be
awfully funny! Our little county is now dry and only
in the last year has had a restaurant that even serves
alcohol. Further, we have churches on every street
corner, often back to back and side by side. Of
course, like most small communities we have our share
of scandalous behavior, but I wonder if we have more
or less of it than Milford does today.

I am sooo tempted to carve a little whiskey bottle to
place in Milford, but I'm afraid that if a local found
it without knowing that it is a part of my personal
little tongue-in-cheek joke, they would be offended,
so I guess I'll forego that box. Still though, don't
y'all think it would be funny for a modern-day
Cherokee Countian to "lose" a whiskey bottle in
Milford????

Lucy

I ran across something that was of particular interest
to me the other day!

The town of Milford in Ellis County was established
when settlers moved to the area from Cherokee County,
where they had tired of the lawlessness, boozing, and
general exhibition of bad morals. 

Now I live in Cherokee County and I found this to be
awfully funny!  Our little county is now dry and only
in the last year has had a restaurant that even serves
alcohol.  Further, we have churches on every street
corner, often back to back and side by side.  Of
course, like most small communities we have our share
of scandalous behavior, but I wonder if we have more
or less of it than Milford does today.

I am sooo tempted to carve a little whiskey bottle to
place in Milford, but I'm afraid that if a local found
it without knowing that it is a part of my personal
little tongue-in-cheek joke, they would be offended,
so I guess I'll forego that box.  Still though, don't
y'all think it would be funny for a modern-day
Cherokee Countian to "lose" a whiskey bottle in
Milford????

Lucy
