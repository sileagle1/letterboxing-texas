Mythical Creatures Series

From: the carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-02-09 14:13:36 UTC-06:00
I have received quite a few e mails this week asking questions about this series.  Since I have been busy getting ready for Kindergarten Nursery Rhyme day I haven't had a chance to get back to all of you, so here goes....
 
!.  My original plan was to hide the boxes in Sam Houston National forest.  Which seemed like a great idea, until I started thinking about checking on and repairing boxes.  So my new plan is to hide them in Terry Hershey park, a lovely park in West Houston that I walk 2 to 3 times a week.  It is a 15 mile loop (I don't walk the whole thing at once!) with plans for expansion.  There are several spots to park along the trail, so I will split the series into 3 parts, one part starting from each parking area.
 
2.  BOX/LOG SIZE:  Smaller is better...about 4x6 would be about the largest box I think could be safely hidden there.  I will list the boxes as "Mythical Creature Series Box 1, box 2, etc."  if you would like to include the name of your particular stamp on your log that is fine.
 
3.  DO YOU NEED TO SEND A BOX?  If you would like to send me a box ready to go, I won't complain.  If you would rather just send the log and the stamp, that is fine too.
 
4.  E mail me off list for my address when you are ready to send your box, remember we are trying to have the boxes ready to go by March 1st.  I hope this covers the questions you all have had, if I have forgotten something, please let me know!  Thanks to all who have offered to help with this project!  MO5
