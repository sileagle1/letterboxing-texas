While Garden Ridge does not carry carving medium or other tools for
the letterboxing sport they have lots of stamps and ink pads. Also
some suitable Rubbermaid containers are to be found.

If you are using or exploring microboxes with an ink pad...

"ink it" acid free, pigment based ink pads are on sale. Currently 25
cents.

The ink surface is 1 in. by 1 in. pad surface stands higher than
surrounding holder so it could mark bigger stamps than surface. They
have some colors not usually available. I like the Lime and teal
colors. Good color saturation. Total case size is: 1.25" X 1.25"
X .75" height

The Houston 7801 S. Main at Kirby Dr. store had about 100 of the
small size ones tonight. Because I don't get my employee discount at
Hobby Lobby, I'm pushing Garden Ridge (from the inside) to get
speedball carving items and the Pink and cream stuff, and
Mastercarve.

NOTE:
Avoid this area on Super Bowl Sunday - Because this is so close to
Reliant Park, Garden Ridge is leasing out the parking lot for Car Bus
and RV parking. They will have very limited parking for customers on
Sunday Feb 01, 2004.
