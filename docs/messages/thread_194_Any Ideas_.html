From: monkeytoes108 (cruschhaupt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-25 15:59:55 UTC
Mother of Four,
To get on the list, all you have to do is email me your mailing
address. The PLB will be mailed hopefully on July 9th, so I need it
before then. It might take a while to get to you since it is going
to other members also. Like Lucy said, just stamp in like a regular
letterbox, and mail it on to the next person on the list. You can
count it as a Found, or some people make a separate PLB category.
It's up to you.

As for your kit, it sounds like you have all the basics. The only
other thing you should add would be an ink pad. She can add any
personal extras later as she gets more experienced.

-MonkeyToes
