Texas Tour Bus 2 - New Clues

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-06-02 16:25:43 UTC
I left clues in the Arts & Crafts Rose for Warm Front, but the right
thing to do is post them here too so that others have the opportunity
to find it. In case someone wants to run out today and pick up that
bus first! If you do that, please remove the clues from Arts &
Crafts Rose, so Warm & Cold Front don't wander aimlessly through 11th
street park.

Location: 11th Street Park--11th & Shelterwood, Houston, TX
Directions: From I-10, take the TC Jester exit and head north. At
11th street, go left a few blocks to the park. If you turn onto
Shelterwood, you can find a nice shady spot.

Note that the signs about the park needing to be saved are being
taken down. Yeah! The park will remain an undeveloped preserve!
