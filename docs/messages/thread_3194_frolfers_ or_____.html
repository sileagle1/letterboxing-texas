OK - here we go.
 
There is only one cache within 1/4 mile, it is this one.
 
http://www.geocaching.com/seek/cache_details.aspx?guid=59064f94-1d27-42ee-bac7-83f62f38cf9f
 
It is a micro-cache.
 
To make the "degree" symbol....  º  as in 31º 12' 34.5"  is as easy as this.
 
Hold down the <ALT> key, and type 167 from your NUMBER keypad.  IT WILL NOT WORK if you use the numbers above the letter keys, it must be the number pad to the right.
 
ºººº - just that easy.
 
NOTE - you can get all kinds of graphics there, play with it some and see.
 
<ALT> 125 is }
<ALT> 150 is û
<ALT> 175 is »
<ALT> 200 is ╚
 
Here is a string from <ALT> 198 to <ALT> 210.
 
╞╟╚╔╩╦╠═╬╧╨╤╥
 
Y-all have fun.

RE: [letterboxingtexas] Re: frolfers, or.....

From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-11-23 16:55:33 UTC-06:00
We will be celebrating our fourth anniversary of the Texas Letterboxing list in four days. It's fun to go back and read how the list has evolved since that time. There have been some really fun, interesting and wonderful letterboxers who have contributed to the growth of letterboxing since its conception in Texas. Many of us have met at various events and friendships have developed. There have been many opportunities for our discussions to wander slightly away from the subject of letterboxing (ie: craving chocolate, families, and clutter... just to name a few topics). I would hate to think that some of our more fun, spirited discussions would no longer take place because they didn't pertain to letterboxing. However, having said that, I do think it's best to avoid the discussion of politically charged, religious and other controversial subjects on this list, because this list does represent many walks of life and many attitudes. For the most part, this is a very positive list. Let's keep it friendly.
Puddle Splasher

http://www.geocaching.com/seek/cache_details.aspx?guid=59064f94-1d27-42ee-bac7-83f62f38cf9f
 
It is a micro-cache.
 
To make the "degree" symbol....  º  as in 31º 12' 34.5"  is as easy as this.
 
Hold down the <ALT> key, and type 167 from your NUMBER keypad.  IT WILL NOT WORK if you use the numbers above the letter keys, it must be the number pad to the right.
 
ºººº - just that easy.
 
NOTE - you can get all kinds of graphics there, play with it some and see.
 
<ALT> 125 is }
<ALT> 150 is û
<ALT> 175 is »
<ALT> 200 is ╚
