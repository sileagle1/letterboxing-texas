From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-08-13 21:52:09 UTC-06:00
No - this place isn't about lunch.
 
Oh - you can have lunch there.  But this place is really all about dinner.  In a BIG way.
 
My suggestion - pack some PB&J and stay on the trail all day.  With a little planning and an early start, you could knock out every box here in one hard day.  Anyone really seriously considering that, you will need a pick-up because the hike will be end to end.  I will use our dog-show van and show you where to park, then give you a ride to the start.  You will knock out 4 letterboxes in an 10-mile hike over the mountain.  Then clean up a few easier ones, and on to dinner.
 
About 1 hour before sunset show up at the restaurant.  Grab a glass of your favorite adult beverage and sit on the veranda watching the sun go down.  Then go inside and order dinner.
 
Beg the hostess for a seat near the window.  After dark the cooks toss out the steak and rib trimmings, and the coyotes come in to feast.  You can see them - but they don't see you.
 
Then go get the last letterbox.  If you can still walk!  <grin>.
