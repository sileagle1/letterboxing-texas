Hello, my name is Puddle Splasher and I'm a tomatoholic. I'm in the ten step program and my sponsor is very helpful, but with tomatoes ripening in the fields, this is a very tough time of the year for me. I've already eaten two tomatoes this morning, but it was an experiment and I will share the results with you. A couple of days ago, I picked up some tomatoes from a farmers market in Dallas which had been trucked up to the area from south Texas. Yesterday, I returned home with a couple of bags of tomatoes from the Jacksonville Tomato Festival. I sliced a tomato from the Dallas market, as well as one from Jacksonville. Not even close! You want a good tomato? Head East! Those Jacksonville tomatoes taste like candy, they are so sweet and delicious. No wonder Jacksonville is the Tomato Capitol of the World. Oh well, back to rearranging the tomatoes in my basket.
Puddle Splasher
