From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-10 19:13:49 UTC-08:00
I mentioned previously that there is a very good
country cafe a few miles from the railroad park that
serves all-you-can-eat catfish on the weekends. Well,
I stopped by there this evening and ate again, and
talked a bit to the lady that owns it. She serves
until 9 p.m.

I would like to propose that those of us who want to
eat there find our way to Big Bertha's between 7:00
and 9:00 to hang out and visit. I figure that way
those who are a little later in arriving won't feel
like they are "too late" to go on down and have some
fish.

How does that sound? Could I get a headcount? If you
THINK you may go but aren't sure, let me know that
too. I thought it would be nice to let them know
there will be a bit of an influx - after all, the
RSVP's so far for the gathering are probably more than
Maydelle's population, so even if only 1/4 of us shows
up it'll be a crowd!

Lucy
