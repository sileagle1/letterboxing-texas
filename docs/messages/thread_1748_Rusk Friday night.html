From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-14 18:42:17 UTC-08:00
Yes. We are planning on going on down to Maydelle to
Big Bertha's for all-you-can-eat catfish (or other
homestyle cooking off the menu). We've planned to
assemble between 7 and 9 p.m., although Silver Eagle
and Baby Bear and their bunch will probably go even a
little earlier than that.

As far as how you get there. Assuming you are going
first to Rusk State Park, when you are leaving the
park, you will turn left and go about six miles or so
to the town of Maydelle. There are three businesses
on the main road (Highway 84) in Maydelle, and Big
Bertha's is the middle business.

Shall I count you in? (This is a very tentative count
- you won't be penalized if you don't show up).

Lucy
