First TX LB Event: Pictures

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-03-09 19:40:07 UTC
Well, I finally put the pictures I took at the Event on my web
page and you can see them along with a list of attendees at:

http://www.geocities.com/sileagle1/Events/FTLE/

Judging from some of the comments it looks like the Event
was enjoyed by all, and I want to thank everyone for helping
make it such a success. I really enjoyed meeting all of you
and sharing good food and conversation. As far as my chili
(the one in the big Crock Pot), it was just Wick Fowlers.
As for letterboxes, it looks like at least 4 new ones were
placed in Bastrop SP and 2 just outside the park. A funny
thing happened to Baby Bear and I when we went for the TCM
box. As we were stamping in a truck pulled into the parking
lot and a guy got out and asked what we were doing. We
thought quickly and said we were admiring the old gas station.
He brightened up and proceded to tell us all about it. It
turns out he is the owner and he ended up unlocking the door
and giving us a tour of the place!
Thanks again for making my weekend memorable and keep boxing!

Silver Eagle
