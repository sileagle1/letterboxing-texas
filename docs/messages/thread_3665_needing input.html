ok, i have an idea for an event, and would really like some input as
to what everyone thinks. the event would take place at the
jacksonville old city cemetery. recently, i was trying to locate a
grave for the whistler, fred lowery. if you have been to this
cemetery, you know that it is fairly large. i contacted the city
hall, and they told me that the graves are only listed by the name of
the individual who owns the deed to the plot, which might not be the
person buried there. needless to say, they had no listing for fred
lowery. so, my thought an event with the purpose of transcribing and
mapping the cemetery. we would have an event stamp, and could have a
poster with everyone who participates signature stamp and then present
the map, transcription and the poster to the city of jacksonville.
please let me know what you think.
jb
