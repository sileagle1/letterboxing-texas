From: Connie Terry (connie@lavernia.net) | Date: 2005-06-28 09:36:11 UTC-05:00

<"Blakley Dam" near Hot Springs had been found by Corazon not too long ago...is that "our" Corazon?  If so, could you tell me if it is one that can be done with kids?>

 

Wow!! I just felt like I got a hug when I read this note! 

 

Yes, it was me that reported that find.  And yes, it can be done with kids.  When you park your vehicle, you will go down some concrete steps towards the lake.  At the first landing, one person needs to crawl/climb under/over the railing and walk just a few feet from the steps.  You will never be out of sight from the steps. As long as your older ones can keep the little ones from continuing down the steps and going for a swim, it should be okey-dokey for you.  With two adults, everyone but the finder can stay in the vehicle—it will take you longer to unload and reload the kids than it will to find the box.  Also, know there is another box by the same placer just a few miles away. Due to time/darkness constraints, we did not attempt it.  

 

Good luck!

Corazon
