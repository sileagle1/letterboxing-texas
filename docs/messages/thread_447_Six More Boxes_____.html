Six More Boxes!!!!!

From: Hawk (omkarmashanti@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-13 18:00:26 UTC-08:00
Hey, Y'All: We've got 14% of the latest 100 boxes
right here in Texas!!!!

I just put out 6 more boxes today. They are my "Texans
to Avoid" series, and they are all in Jesse H. Jones
Park and Nature Center, located near Humble, TX (north
of FM 1960 between I-45 and US 59). Plan to spend at
least half a day in this park. There are great trails
and lots to see!

New boxes:

Scary Scorpio
Gator Gazer
The Hills are Alive
Big Bug
Bee Calm
Sstep Ssafely

Enjoy!

Hawk
