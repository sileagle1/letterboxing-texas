Another Crazy Day For Bear Boxes

From: Baby Bear (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-03-13 15:34:16 UTC
Well, I thought the story about my box in Tomball becoming a port-a-can
for someone was the wierdest thing that has happened to my boxes, but
yesterday I got a new winner. It seems my box placed in St. Marteen
was attempted by someone from a cruise ship. They searched the
location, but only found the zip lock bag. No box, no stamp, just the
bag, and some contents. They opened the bag to find a map, and some
pirate booty. It seems some pirate has taken my box, and left a map on
how to find it. The Letterboxer did not have time to do this, so for
now this will be an unsolved mistery. I guess pirates went after it
since it was named "2007 Texas Sailing Adventure". ARGH!!!
