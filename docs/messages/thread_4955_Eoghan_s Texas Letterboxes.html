Quite a while back I mentioned on this mailing list that I had the clues to Eoghan's Texas letterboxes and a couple people asked for them. I am embarrassed to say that I could not locate them in my files to pass them along. Well, I have located them again and now I have forgotten who asked for them. (do you see a pattern emerging here)

I have the clues for the following letterboxes:

Enchanted Rock - Placed on 11/26/2000 at Enchanted Rock SNA.

Mayfield Park - Placed 1/6/2001 in Austin.

Lighthouse - placed on 1/14/2001 in Palo Dura Canyon State Park.

O-Knots (4) - Placed 11/10/2001 in Bastrop State Park, but I have not been able to check all four since the fire. I do know that #4 has been lost to the flames.

O-Knots

Treasure Type: Letterbox Series
Placed on: 11/10/01
State: Texas
County: Bastrop
Nearest Town: Bastrop
Park: Bastrop State Park

Terrain Difficulty: 2.5 stars
Overall Difficulty: 2 stars

Clues:

To locate the four o-knots hidden in this park you will need to purchase ($3) an
orienteering map from the park headquarters when you enter the park. There are
two maps available; buy the one that shows the numbered locations of the
permanent control points. Each of these letterboxes contains a circular ('O')
Celtic knot stamp and is located near one of the permanent orienteering ('O')
control markers shown on the map. Each box is in the base of a tree trunk at the
bearing and distance from the control marker shown on the table below. Also
indicated is the type of tree or other clue that will help you locate each box.

Knot # Control # Bearing Distance Location

1 87 240 90' in base of dead pine tree

2 29 74 85' in base of small oak tree

3 71 180 50' in east end of rotting log

4 91 346 20' double-trunk oak at top of
ditch, beneath rocks

Additional notes: Bastrop State Park has an entry fee of $3 per person per day.
Those with a Texas Conservation Passport may enter for free.
