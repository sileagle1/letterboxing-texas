From: Maribeth Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-06-27 18:55:24 UTC-05:00
Today we drove by Wilson Creek in McKinney which flows behind our neighborhood. This is the creek where Baby Bear had his box hidden under a bridge. The water was above the bridge today, and streets were closed all around it. This is the same area I took my little hike in the lightning a couple of days ago. We are presently getting another huge storm. Whenever possible, I try to put a substantial rock on top of my boxes when I plant them, but they don't always get replaced, so it will be interesting to see what survives all this rain (which is in the forecast for the next seven days). I LOVE Texas - I truly do - it never ceases to amaze me how we can go from a terrible drought straight into floods and more rain than our swollen rivers can handle.
