From: JuneMcAllister (nfmoon@mindspring.com) | Date: 2005-07-31 14:12:20 UTC-04:00
With my urban boxes, it seems to always be location first, since I try to find interesting places that are nice and have places I can hide a box, or locations that a visitor to the city woudn't ordinarily go to, or that has some interesting facts about it. I have found out about places I never knew were here. It's great when someone says they would never have been to a place had they not looked for the box. One woman told me the friend she was with used to live a few blocks from one of my locations and never knew the neat little place was there. I love that. I guess generally speaking I have an idea first and then look for an image. But it can go the other way.
missmoon
