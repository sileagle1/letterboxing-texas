This is so cool – can you imagine a gathering of Texas Letterboxers all in our T-shirts???

 

 

You know, the last time I visited the T-shirt place I was very impressed with how polite and cheerful and helpful the young college kid at the desk was…turns out she was also a little flakey ;-)!

 

The last quote I was given for our T-shirts was $8.00 apiece – which I thought was really too good to be true. Turns out it IS too good to be true. The weathered pigment-dyed shirts are $9.00 up to size L, $11.00 for XL and XXL, and $12.00 for XXXL. (I’d been told all sizes cost the same) Because we stuck with 3 colours to silk-screen, we only had a $3.00 fee for the design (total, not per shirt), so that equals about 6 cents each, making the basic shirt $9.06.

 

The regular 8 % TX tax totaling $0.74 was added of course (NOTE: at some future date we might want to check out non-profit status for the Texas Letterboxing group). That brings the basic shirt cost up to $9.80.

I’ve looked around the internet & I think $3.50 will cover packaging plus postage. For each additional shirt, please add $1.00. (I’m basing this on buying a pack of 50 12 1/2x19 mailing envelopes at Office Max for $37.99 and estimating the postage at about $2.50). If this turns out to be wildly wrong, I’ll let you guys know.

 

We ended up with an order for 50 shirts, which totaled $570.23, and I have paid the 50% deposit, so I’m counting on you all to get those checks in to me ASAP!

 

The shirts will be ready to pick up on 8/20, and I’ll mail them out as soon as I receive your check. Please look for an email from me to each of you privately, giving you the total of your order, and my address for you to send your check.

 

And now we just need to arrange another gathering to show off our new TX LbNA finery!

 

Thanks!

Ruby Tuesday
