A couple of years ago, when moving, I got rid of everything that I hadn't used in the last year. My mother had a fit. I told her I was Feng Shui'ing. I was happy. When we moved into the new apartment I had empty cupboards. It was great. Well, Christmas came around and I didn't have the 'right' cake pan, so I had to buy some. Then, I needed a potato masher for something (other than mashing potatos of course), so I had to buy another one. Needless to say, my cupboards are full again.
 
I am moving again next month and DH said that we were going to only move the stuff we need (meaning the rest will go to a garage sale or Goodwill). I said, "Remember we tried that and ended up with the same ole stuff later."
 
And, I wonder, how did I end up with 4 boxes of pie crust? I use the frozen Mrs. Smith's, so how would that even end up at my house? I think my mother sends the stuff she doesn't want to my house so I have to store it. She can't throw anything away.
