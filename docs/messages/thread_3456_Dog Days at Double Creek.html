WOW...what a fun event..it was so great to finally be able to put
names and faces together..this is the first meet that i have been to
that is not in my region, and it will not be the last..thank you to
boots tex, lone star quilter for the wonderful cinnamon rolls and
great company...by the way, lucy, i did manage to get him to
sing "take me out to the ballpark" for his cane..sorry you missed it.
also thanks to dewberry (and of course boots and lone star quilter)
for such a fun event. i really enjoyed meeting everyone (i don't want
to mention names, because i am afraid of leaving someone out), and
thanks again to pug momma for helping me out with the log book (i
needed a new one and had forgotten to get one out of my car, and she
happened to have a spare one that she gave me). pug momma, sandibox
and soccerchik, thanks for letting me letterbox with you (and jr says
thanks to mica for the playing)..again thanks to all it was definitely
worth all the drive time today.
