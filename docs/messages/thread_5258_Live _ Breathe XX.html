From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2013-09-12 17:54:29 UTC-05:00

FYI…Choi posted this on the AQ forum for the Live & Breathe attendees.  It gives you an idea what to look for in a location:

 

I hope at least a few people are starting to think about where the next Live & Breathe should be. Der Mad Stamper owns the event name (as the first organizer) but is willing to lend it out to organizers that can carry on the tradition of a weekend summer camp experience.

If you want to bring L&B to your region in 2018, I would suggest the following in the next couple years:
1) find a summer camp with hiking trails that can handle 200+ people and let you book the entire camp for a weekend (doesn't have to be Labor Day),
2) gather a committee of about a dozen dedicated boxers with event organization experience and a range of talents at live within about an hour of the event location,
3) get the OK from Der Mad Stamper for your region to host the event
4) book the event on AQ!

One of the things that the original L&B event did, that I wish we had emulated, was use PayPal only for payment and do online registration.

From: (eee93@aol.com) | Date: 2013-09-14 13:31:33 UTC-07:00

 If y'all are serious about hosting the event here in Texas you might want to consider Wildcatter Ranch.  Wildcatter Ranch is located 90 miles northwest of Fort Worth and is set on 1,500 acres of North Texas Hill Country. It is about a 30 minute drive from the ranch to Possum Kingdom Lake which has crystal clear water,surrounded by beautiful cliffs and rolling hillsides. 

 

"cleo of 5" has four letterboxes in the State Park on the west side of the lake.

 

The ranch itself has hiking trails and so does Possum Kingdom Lake. A map of the lake trails is online at http://www.brazos.org/generalPdf/pkHikeBikeTrail.pdf  I contacted them just to see if they could handle 200 people.  Here is their reply.
 

Wildcatter Ranch has availability for up to 350 guest at an event. What type of event are you looking to host at Wildcatter? Are you looking for this fall or into 2014? 
 
I look forward to working with you and sending you over additional details. You are always welcome to reach me at 940-282-8588.

Thank you,

Group Sales Manager
