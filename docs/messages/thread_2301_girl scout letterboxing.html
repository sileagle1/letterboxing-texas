From: janet clark (jayceeinbedford@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-11-07 04:30:45 UTC-08:00
See previous post for what is a bus. But, to answer your questions on logging your finds:
 
You can go to www.letterboxing.org or to www.atlasquest.com to log your finds. There might be other sites too. If there are, I don't know about them. Some of the boxes are listed on both sites. There are really good tutorials on both sites on how to log in and search for your boxes.
 
I bet y'all had a blast. Kids love this hobby. It's hard for me to explain to my daughter (she's 6) how important it is to be discreet. I keep telling her that she's going to be kicked out of the club. It cracks my older daughter up when I say that, but it has no effect on the little one at all.
 
Have a great day,
Janet
