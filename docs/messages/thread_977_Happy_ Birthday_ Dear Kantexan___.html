So are finals over now??? Can you get back to letterboxing in earnest?
-- Yes, finals are done, grades posted, and new semester doesn't begin until mid-January, but other things just keep popping up!    I have several new boxes still in the planning or development stages, but I've also got letterbox maintenance issues - many of my logbooks are full and I'm busy making new ones and replacing those, and I've still got a few boxes that were pulled temporarily for one reason or another, still sitting in my laundry room that need to be replanted.  I'm really counting on the next few weeks giving us some nice dry weather!  Among others, I want to find your new ones in Denton, Lucy -- can't believe I haven't already done that! 
    Holiday wishes to everyone on this list, and thanks especially to Miss Chrispy who let me know she found one of my boxes just sitting out on top of some ivy in a very public place and carefully replanted it where it was actually supposed to be - which should be one of the safest locations ever!   Thanks to every finder who contacts a placer with an update or status report and who takes care to rehide and recover well, so others can continue to enjoy the search and be rewarded with a find, too!  And thanks to all of you who keep planting those new letterboxes for the rest of us to enjoy!
