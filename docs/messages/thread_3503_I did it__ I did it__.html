I did it!! I did it!!

From: Pam Rodgers (perky_p_99@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-18 18:47:27 UTC-07:00
I just planted my very first boxes!!! I planted 3 in
Harleton for the Longview/LA Initiative! We had SO
much fun!! I can't wait for someone to try to go find
them so I can know if the clues make sense. I have to
warn ya'll, these boxes aren't for those who are in
search for beautiful stamps. LOL It's for someone who
is in it for the adventure of the hunt. These were my
very first stamps that I've ever carved so they aren't
all that great. I hope to look back on them someday to
see that I've improved in my carving abilities. :)
It was kind of funny b/c I told my husband I felt like
I was leaving my babies out in the wild to face all
kind of dangers. (Weather, animals, vandalizers, etc.)
It made me kind of sad. LOL But not sad enough to not
do it again. I'm already planning my next plants in
Longview! I may actually like planting more than
hunting. Well, maybe not, but it's a close second!

Perky P of The Rodgers 7
