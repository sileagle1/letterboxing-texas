Gusher Gather -

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2010-04-17 20:12:08 UTC-05:00

I just want to thank all y'all that came out for the Gusher Gather today. It was a beautiful day with only hints of rain that didn't really come to pass until late in the day.

I was pleased to see that Lucy Locket and Blue Butterfly saw fit to form the ETSSBB and proud to be a charter member.

First Time gatherers Mega Family and Fruit Cup came from Tyler and jumped right in. As always, it's a great pleasure to meet boxers I haven't met before.

Dawn's Glory and her girls graced us with lots of cooties. Her girls also did a great job of keeping my grandbaby, Trail Whale, well entertained. By the time we got him home, he was all but asleep on his feet. I'm sure his mama and daddy are going to be most pleased to discover that he is now working to perfect a new way of barking. He also got his first logbook started today.

We realized last night as we were getting ready for the gathering that our family had four generations of letterboxers at the gathering - my mom, Nac Trail Cat, me, my son Draconis viridis, and my grandson, Trail Whale.

Several folks took advantage of the Texas Tour Bus' presence. The Circus box didn't make a showing since, according to JBK, they were too unruly to bring to such a nice park.
