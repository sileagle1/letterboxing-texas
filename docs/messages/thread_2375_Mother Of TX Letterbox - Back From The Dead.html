Mother Of TX Letterbox - Back From The Dead

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-12-11 16:16:51 UTC
After at least 2 reports that this box was missing (a different box was
found in its place), I dutifully changed its status to Missing on its
clue webpage several months ago. Imagine my surprise when I got an
email today from a new letterboxer saying she found it! I confirmed
that it is my box with my stamp by her description, so I am baffled as
to where this box has been hiding and what happened to the other box.
Since this box is located in a cemetery, I hope this hasn't become a
Ghost Box!

Luckily she did not see the Missing Status when she printed the clues,
or she would never have attempted it. I guess this points out the risk
of assuming a box is missing, even if it really is missing for a while.
Anyway, Mother Of TX is back for now.

Silver Eagle
