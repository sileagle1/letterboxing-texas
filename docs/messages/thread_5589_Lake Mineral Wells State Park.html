Just a heads up That Lake Mineral Wells state park is doing a prescribed burn this week, in case anyone was wanting to come out this way, or that might have a letterbox in the area of the burn.  I am thinking one of my GS boxes is out there and might not have made it...

From the website:

"For up-to-date closure information, please contact the park headquarters at 940-328-1171.

The State Park Fire Team is conducting a prescribed fire at 9 a.m. on Mon., Feb. 27. Some sections of the park may be closed, including camping loops, hiking trails, back country trail system and primitive camping area, day-use areas, and the rock climbing area. Smoke may be present in areas of the park. Fire operations are expected to end no later than Fri., March 3, 2017. "
