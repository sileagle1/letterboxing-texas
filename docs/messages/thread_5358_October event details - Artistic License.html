Some more information on the area for the event….I visited the park a couple of weeks ago to check it out and look for good box locations.  The area is laid out like a wagon wheel with the dining hall in the middle.  The pull thru sites are the spokes of the wheel and the tent area is off to one side.  We have a nice path down to the lake and the shore is very beach like.  There are picnic tables and fire pits outside the dining hall and throughout the area so we have a nice area to hang out in the evening. 

 

My only concern is that the tent area is small (well the flat part), and it slopes down to the lake quickly.  I think we can get about 5 tents in the flat part tent area (maybe more if we squeeze in).  The grassy area between the pull-thru spots is narrow in most cases, but I wrote down spot numbers which can support more than one tent.  I’ll mark these with signs or something as reserved for the tent campers.

 

Also there is lots of paved area but very few marked parking spots.  If you are not camping I’d suggest parking on the outside of the loop which circles pull-thru spots. 

 

PS – My bluebonnet box is missing but I’ll get it replaced before the event.
