Re: looking for lock-n-locks

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-09-02 13:03:00 UTC
If you're feeling brave, stop by the food stalls.

Here's a review of the one in Houston:

http://www.houstonpress.com/2007-08-02/dining/mom-s-hand-restaurant/

When I was first letterboxing, you couldn't get lnls anywhere. I had
a supplier, a very nice woman from Sacramento, who would send me
Korean knock-offs as long as I didn't tell anybody else (like it was
contraband). I had been to Vietnamese markets, Chinese markets--no
luck. One day I was driving on Gessner when I saw Komart and
realized the Ko stood for Korean. Heaven! I could get my own fix.

You can usually get the little small rectangular ones there, but
sometimes they have orange lids.

dewberry
