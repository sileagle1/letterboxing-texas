City Cemetery Event

From: tigger32167 (tigger32167@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-23 02:32:05 UTC
i just posted the city cemetery event for jacksonville for August 8-9
on atlas quest. for all of you boxers who live away from this area,
there are a lot of boxes to be found. as of right now, the event will
be friday from 6-dark and then saturday from 8-1. afterwards be sure
and go boxing in the area. there are also 3 boxes in this cemetery, a
haunted statue and there will be some surprise stamps that will appear
plus the event stamp. look forward to seeing you there.
jb


City Cemetery Event

From: tigger32167 (tigger32167@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-08-10 00:28:29 UTC
hey there, i just want to say "thanks" to all the people who drove so
far to attend this event to help me transcribe the cemetery. Gryzzled
Gryphon, Mother's Love, and Horse Rider braved both days of the event
and the Boxhounds actually came all the way from Corpus Christi to
help out today. The event was supposed to end today at 1, however,
due to the misreading of the times, we all ended up staying till
around 5:30 this afternoon. Four of us actually got there this
morning around 7:30-8. I also want to say thanks to my mom for
bringing us the sandwiches, cookies, chips, and drinks today. I think
we all needed it. It was great seeing "old" friends and meeting some
new ones. I am not sure how many headstones we transcribed in the two
days, but once i get that number i will surely post it. By the way,
if you have to talk to GG, be sure and ask him about his run-in with
the local police. again, thanks to all who came for making this such
a great event.

jb kokopelli
