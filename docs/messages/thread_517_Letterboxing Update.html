From: Birds of a Feather Family (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-29 22:57:33 UTC-08:00
All,
 
Updates to the Texas Letterboxing Atlas will be held as we are on vacation in the Rio Grand Valley.
 
We have gone looking for a few boxes on the way South:
 
Found MonkeyToes'  The Lost Moai and Riverside Duck Pond letterboxes.  The Lost Moai letterbox was laying in the open when we found it.   It may need to be hidden better as we were trying to be quiet as a lady was riding in the exercise ring on the other side of the fence.
 
Took the girls looking for Baby Bear's Coral Snake which my son and I had found back in January. The rock was on top of the wood chips but when we dug through we came up empty.
 
Hopefully, later this week we will try looking for some of WOWMOM's boxes here in the
Valley and place a new box or two.
