Announcing the Great Book Adventure!

Tomball. I have never stayed there, so I really have no idea about

out-of-town boxers (and we're hoping that we'll have some guests), we

Goodman's. If it's all in-town folk, we'll probably skip that part.

More details to follow. Contact either myself or TeamKing if you have
questions.

From: Eibhlin/Aisling (eibhlinm@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-06-09 05:22:24 UTC-05:00
Hi,

This sounds like fabulous fun! My husband and I will definitely be there!

This is a very timely announcement, too; we were just talking about
letterboxing, and wishing for someone to organize an event.

Meanwhile, after nearly a year of inactivity (repetitive stress
shoulder injury, mostly recovered now) I'm getting back to
letterboxing. Yaaaayyy!

I'm working on some new boxes, and carved two micro-box stamps last
night. It's funny, but it's harder for me to carve little stamps than
the big ones. I want to put too much into them... and simplifying is
a difficult concept for me. So, my new ones feel far "too simple" to
me, but hey, a stamp is a stamp!

Anyway, since both my husband and I worked in public libraries when we
were in school--and we're still voracious readers--the theme of this
event is perfect!

Thanks!

Cheerfully,
Aisling D'Art
http://www.aisling.net/letterboxing-blog/blog.htm
