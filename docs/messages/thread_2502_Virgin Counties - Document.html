Congratulations on completing your goal in one year and good idea to
keep a record of ongoing progress. I actually compiled a list of
counties without boxes back in December when I first saw the new map
of TX with individual colored counties. It is a word document that I
will upload to Files showing counties by region without boxes as of
last December (currently not colored on TX map) along with a list of
counties without boxes that will be removed as we place boxes in
them. Sea Dove has already placed a box in Wharton county, so I
removed it from the updated list. I know this isn't as nice as a
map, but it will be easier to update. So as you place boxes in
virgin counties, either update the file or let me know and I will do
it. I don't expect to populate every county by the end of the year,
especially the Panhandle Region, but it will be fun to see how far we
get. Let the planting begin!

Silver Eagle
