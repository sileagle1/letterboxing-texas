No, Calvin & Hobbes  aren't returning to the comics pages, at least not that I know of, but I have recarved my Calvin and Hobbes stamps that went missing from that too-busy park in Mansfield, and will be planting the new boxes in a totally different location soon (next week I hope!).  So for those of you who have been asking about them, watch for The Return of Calvin and Hobbes.  As before, you'll have to find Calvin for clues to his imaginary playmate/stuffed tiger, Hobbes.  The location is a place I've long wanted to plant a box, but just didn't have the proper inspiration for the stamps for this place until I realized it's perfect for these two!  I'm excited myself!
