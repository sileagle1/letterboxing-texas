From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2013-10-26 09:31:06 UTC-05:00

I’ve just finished listing all the boxes on LBNA.  The temporary boxes will be archived in about a week. 

 

More importantly perhaps, is that the clue content on AQ is now complete so you can get clues if you didn’t attend the event.  (Please let me know if I missed anything).  The temporary boxes on AQ are now listed as retired or unavailable, so if you haven’t logged them yet be sure to include that in your search criteria.

 

FYi…I only listed Letterboxer’s Logic as a puzzle.  Two others have puzzles but they are intended to be fun rather than challenging (and I don’t think anyone failed to get them).  I’ve noticed many people don’t seem to get puzzle boxes even when they are easy.  So I think the puzzle symbol keeps many people from even looking at the clues and trying. I plan to leave it off the easy puzzles in the future.  Letterboxer’s Logic isn’t an easy one!
