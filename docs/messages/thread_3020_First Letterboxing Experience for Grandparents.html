First Letterboxing Experience for Grandparents

From: lori baker (loribaker2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-16 17:02:19 UTC-07:00
My mom and dad live in a small east Texas town and
this whole week they have had my two children who love
letterboxing. My mom had agreed that while they (the
kids) were visiting she and my dad would take them
letterboxing in Kilgore. Neither of my parents had
even heard of Letterboxing until July when I started
doing this hobby. So today they get to the park and
they are following the clues. My mom thinks she is
going right and my dad thinks she is wrong so he
starts off with the kids in a different direction. A
couple of minutes later my mom hears my dad yelling at
the top of his lungs "RUN...RUN...RUN.." all my mom
sees is my dad running behind my children and he is
stripping off his shirt. So she takes off running.
When they get back to the car she discovered that he
had disturbed a wasp nest and he had been stung
SEVERAL times and my daughter had been stung once.
Both my parents have sworn that they will NEVER go
letterboxing again.

Don't worry everyone is fine and it probally shouldn't
be funny but I just thought I had to share the funny
adventure.
