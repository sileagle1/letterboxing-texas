I have come across an unlisted hitchhiker.  What's protocol?  Should I list it or just move it along to the next box?

Re: [letterboxingtexas] Unlisted Hitchhiker

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-05-28 21:11:47 UTC-05:00

Hi Kristie,

Did you check both letterboxing.org and atlasquest.com?

http://www.letterboxing.org/HHFind.php
http://www.atlasquest.com/search.html?gTypeId=9

Sometimes a box is listed on one but not the other.

Regardless, if the owner didn't list it, you shouldn't either. As a side note, If you are a premium member on AQ, you can list it for your own use.

Stamp the HH into your logbook, and your stamp into the HH's logbook, then when you drop it in another box, stamp it into the box's log and the box's stamp into the HH's log. It sounds confusing, but the thing to remember is that the HH log should reflect all the places it's been and all the people it's met, and it should leave it's mark amongst the people and places too.
