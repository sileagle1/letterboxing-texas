Greenville Treasure Hunt

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-11-14 09:44:01 UTC-06:00
It's been a long time since we were home on a weekend with a few
unschedueled hours...on Saturday we realized we had 4 1/2 hours, so we
headed for Greenville!
Catpawz2004 does an excellent job of leading a guided tour around their city
with very good directions from the first box through to the fourth. We truly
enjoyed a sampling of Greenville, with four very different kinds of
locations and four great stamps.
Our favorite location was Forest Park and our favorite stamp was a toss-up
between Forest Park and Greenville Sports Park. We had Vandal the Terrier
with us and his favorite was the Audie Murphy American Cotton Museum box,
but he wasn't sure why we insisted on covering it back up after he found
it...;-)

Thanks for a great Saturday afternoon!
