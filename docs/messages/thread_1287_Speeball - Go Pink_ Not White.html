I made the mistake of buying the same sheet of white
material over a year ago. It is thicker than the pink
stuff and cheaper since you get more (8.5" x 11"), but
it does not last. All of the stamps I carved from it
have eventually crumbled, broken or eroded away. Maybe
they have improved it and you will have better luck, but
I would just try one for a few months and see how it does
before doing too many.
I found it with the other Speedball products in the
Art supply area, not with the stamps.
