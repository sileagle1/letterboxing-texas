    I just wanted to take a few minutes this morning to wish everyone a Very Merry Christmas.  It is a special time of year that should be spent at home with your loved ones.  I hope everyone is afforded that opportunity, and everything good comes your way in this holiday season.
 
    Tonight we will take out the dogs and join with some friends at the Christmas Tree in the square downtown  We will just walk around enjoying the lights, and the people, and the season.  When we get home we will burn last years Christmas Tree in the fire place and listen to Christmas music on the radio.  This is something we do every year. 
 
    I also hope that sometime throughout this weekend you will stop and give a moment of thought to those family members who will not be coming home for Christmas.   They are out there, lonely and homesick.  But they know that by their sacrifice they are assuring that we at home can celebrate this holiday in safety and comfort.  All they ask for in return is to be appreciated.  To remember them.  A simple thank you.
