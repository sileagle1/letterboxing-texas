Welcome to a new Houston area letterboxer!  I do almost all of my letterboxing with my kids (ages 10, 8, 5, 3 and 1) in the Houston area.  Most all boxes around here are kid friendly, and many are in spectacular kid places (like Silver Eagle's Gordon Grouper, for example).  It just depends on what you are looking for (a long walk, a playground, a historical cemetery, etc) Houston has them all!  I would suggest looking at the boxes on LBNA and AQ, picking one or two close to home, and head out!  One tip I do have for going with kids is to use a group stamp to stamp into the boxes, with lots of individual stamps it can take a long time with lots of kids.  Also, I know kids LOVE to re hide boxes, and it is fine to let them help, as long as you are "checking their work" and making sure boxes are really well covered when you leave them.  If you have any specific questions about individual boxes or anything else, just feel free to ask!  Mother of Five
