From: Eibhlin/Aisling (eibhlinm@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-08-02 21:38:17 UTC-05:00
Hi,

Thank you to everyone who was at tonight's class in Katy! I had a
great time teaching, and I was SO impressed with the quality of art
that I saw... words, landscapes, classic symbols, wonderful designs...
it was SO much fun!

I'll try to get the PDF assembled in the next few days. I threw a LOT
of info at people, and barely had time to breeze through bookbinding,
but my goodness I was pleased with the good-looking stamps that I saw
tonight!

Thanks again, everyone. After I finish my upcoming book (about
haunted New Orleans), I'll try to teach another class.

This was really, really fun tonight!

Cheerfully,
Ais
