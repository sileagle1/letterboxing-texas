New box...Spring Branch, Houston

From: The Carrascos (a.carrasco@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-05-08 14:09:01 UTC-05:00
I just posted the clues to a new Houston box...Spring Branch Secret.  It is located at the Science center that is part of SBISD.  This is an especially great place to go with kids...one of my all time favorite places.  I found out about it when I taught in Spring Branch.  Teamking and Dewberry (and any other Houston boxers with little ones) don't miss this!  And you better hurry, because you know my boxes all seem to be doomed to short lives.  If it actually lasts long enough to be found please feel free to give any suggestions on what I could do to help it live a long, happy life.  Since it has been raining all afternoon I spent part of my Mother's Day re carving stamps for 3 boxes that have gone missing.  How sad.  Anyway, I hope you all like this spot as much as I do.  Happy Mothers Day to all!  - Mother of Five
