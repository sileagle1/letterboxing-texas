From: ckiris1989 (cnt120389@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-09-28 19:25:56 UTC
Hi all!

I've been "observing" this group for a while. I have been hiking
for years and when the article came out in Time Magazine, we began
to get "stuff" together for letterboxing.

I saw the discussion on the patches. I have three children and
since I love to hike I had been looking for a way to keep them
interested. Thus, patches. Most state parks have their own patch.
For every state park we visited AND hiked, I'd buy them a patch. I
then applied them to their hiking jackets. Now they have an award
for each of the trails "mom dragged" them down.

I love the idea of the letterboxing patch. It would be great to add
to their collection (and mine).

ckiris
