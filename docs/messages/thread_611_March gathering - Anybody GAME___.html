I’m so sorry you can’t make it this year – we will count on seeing you next year for the third gathering! But your letter struck me with an idea I’ve been toying with – I would love for us to play the game of “CLUE” at the Brazos gathering, as outlined by Scarab of the Doubtful Guests. I’m going to upload a copy of the rules to the file area of this group so you all can take a look at it. Basically, it’s the old game of Clue we all played as kids, but instead of cards, we would carve stamps to represent characters and weapons and locations. Instead of asking to see someone’s cards, you would ask for them to stamp in your journal. You would formulate a guess based on stamps which are missing, and check with the gamemaster.

 

Here’s the deal – everybody would need to bring at least one stamp. We’re going to need 22 stamps to play the classic game – 6 suspects, 6 weapons and 9 rooms( or locations).

 

I thought it might be fun if we gave it a very TEXAS spin – perhaps the same old weapons, but Texas characters such as Bonnie & Clyde? And perhaps Texas locations?

 

Anybody want to play? ( Miss Moon – do you want to carve a couple stamps for us? )

 

Ruby Tuesday

Of CelticLions
