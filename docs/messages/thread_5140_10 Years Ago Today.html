10 Years Ago Today

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2013-02-01 13:46:06 UTC
Hard to believe it has been 10 years since the Columbia Space Shuttle exploded over east TX while Baby Bear and I were on our first letterbox seeding trip in that same area. We didn't see or hear the explosion, but heard about it on the radio as we were driving west early that morning. It definitely put a damper on the rest of our trip, but at least the seeds worked. I know we hooked at least one east TX resident: Barefoot Lucy. Where have you gone, BL? We miss you.

Silver Eagle
