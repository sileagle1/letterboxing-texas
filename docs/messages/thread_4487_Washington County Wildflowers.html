Washington County Wildflowers

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2010-04-04 02:31:32 UTC
I just got back from a great day with the family looking at wildflowers in Washington County (Brenham, Chappell Hill, Washington-On-The-Brazos & Independence) and I highly recommend going. The flowers are extra good this year and will probably last at least 2 more weeks. I placed a new box in Independence and replaced the log and container of my Texas Flag box, and there are many other boxes in the area. You can see lots of flowers just by looking for the boxes, or use this link for ideas:

http://www.brenhamtexas.com/WildflowerWatch-Updates.htm
