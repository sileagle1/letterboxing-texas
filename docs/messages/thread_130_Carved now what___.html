From: Cristy (tryscience@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-05-07 02:57:47 UTC
Is the place so busy that it would be difficult for someone to find
the box unobserved and do the stamping? If not, and it's a nice
place you want others to "discover", then go for it. Can you make a
microbox or a smaller box in order to better conceal it? As for the
trash, visit your box often and take a trashbag with you each time.
We find that our favorite hiking trail accumulates trash even way
away from civilization, so we tuck shopping bags in our backpacks
and clear the trail on our way out. Most people are careless slobs,
just a fact of life.

Cristy
