What a terrific day to go for a short hike! 2 adults, a teen with his
rip stick (skateboard), 4 kids and a baby all bundled up.

Well we went to Lake Tawakoni today. Found a fairy working hard. A
beautiful stamp made by Lucy Locket. Then we went for a hike along
another nature trial. Read the clues to the kids about Shelob's
Lair. A couple of hoodies were thrown over heads, a couple of kids
said "no way, I'm not going down there". Well we made them go "down
there" and we made them go first. If the baby can go in the
stroller, so can the rest of them. Not that the baby had a choice in
the matter. :)

After walking for a few minutes the kids took off ahead of us.
Apparently the thought of spiders had disappeared from their little
brains. Thank goodness for winter, I didn't want to see them
either. Found the spider in its hiding spot. Thanks Whistler'
Mother.

Since we made it out without any problems, tomorrow we are going
searching for a mouse. I hear he is hiding in a pocket. I wonder if
the Alligators are sleeping now. Gus.here we come.
