Sweet Berry, Lost Marbles, Aly's Lb, Southern Magnolia

From: Susan (ssmith043@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-11 22:54:55 UTC
Monday I had the opportunity to do a bit of Lb'ing while in Marble
Falls. I found Sweet Berry and Lost Marbles. Both are alive and
well; well Lost Marbles was a little under the weather. The lid
wasn't on tight and the journal was a bit damp. I hope I got the lid
on there tighter. Both were hidden well before leaving.

Today, Wed., while visiting with my dad in Calliham, we went down
the road to Choke Canyon State Park and I have the honor of being
the FTF on both Aly's Letterbox and Southern Magnolia. Aly's is
alive and well. Southern Magnolia's canister had been chewed on by
what looked like mice and the lid was in shreds, but the stamp and
journal were still there. The journal is damp. I'll be back over
there next week to take my dad to the doctor and will try to find
another box to put the journal and stamp in if Baby Bear would like
for me to. Baby Bear, please advise privately. Both journal and
stamp were placed inside the ziplock and well hidden.

My 88 yr old father, went with me to find these 2 boxes today. He
kept shaking his head and giving me funny looks. Then he
said, "Why"? I explained it all to him and he gave me another funny
look, and said, "Don't ya'll have anything better to do?" So, I
likened it to hunting for deer and arrowheads and his response to
that, "If you say so." By the time we left the park, he was looking
for places to hide more boxes...lol.

Making memories with my dad,
Susan aka CalicoRose
