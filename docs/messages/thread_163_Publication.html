From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-06-07 15:24:35 UTC
I think as long as no specific letterbox locations are
mentioned it would be ok (in case Officials reading the
article decide to confiscate the box in their location).
You could tell the history and basic information about the
hobby and why it is fun for those of us that participate.
I was contacted recently by a reporter from Omaha, Nebraska
who wanted to do a letterboxing article. We never were
able to talk, but the article came out in the Omaha World-
Herald and was pretty good. Unfortunately the link to the
article no longer works or you could take a look. I say
go for it. Who knows, maybe it would generate more interest.
