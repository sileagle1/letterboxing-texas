From: Dave (DavHow@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-12-05 20:27:17 UTC
There are mirrors in the back corners of the "store", so you are
likely just seeing a reflection of the open space across the street.
I can assure you there are no doors on the back of the structure. The
front door doesn't work either, it's essentially just another window
into the display.
Astro D's first "Prada" box, was our first letterbox to find,
and we picked up the new one on the day after Thanksgiving. It's a
great stamp, and an interesting art piece.
