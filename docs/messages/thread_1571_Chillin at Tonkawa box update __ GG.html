Oh no! 

We were talking today on our way home from Nacogdoches
about first boxes planted by various people and
ourselves.  One of my all-time favorites was also one
of my first and it disappeared before it was ever even
found.

I had hoped to find your Pretty in Purple one of these
days - purple is my favorite color!

Have you considered contacting the park to see if they
know what happened to it?  We've seen a lot of strange
things lately, including the Frye Team's Box, Chillin'
at Tonkawa, which was displaced from its home but
found by Gryzzled Gryphon on the side of the road.

I don't remember if I shared this story with the list,
or just with Baby Bear, but he had received a report
that his box was missing at Caddoan Mounds.  The
structure where it was hidden had been completely
removed.  I had noticed a newspaper article about a
boy scout troop doing the work as part of one boy's
Eagle project, so I contacted the Scoutmaster to see
if he knew anything.  As it happened, he had not
participated in that particular Eagle project, so he
knew nothing and I forgot about it.  A few months
later, I was sitting on an Eagle Board of Review for a
boy in the same troop as the boy who had done the work
at Caddoan Mounds, so I asked him if he had worked on
the Caddo project.  He hadn't, but a lady from his
troop was in the room and heard my question and said
that she had been there (it was, in fact, her son's
Eagle project) and she had actually found the box.
She had set it aside to look at it later because she
was curious (and at this point in her story, I was
holding my breath), but when she went back to it, it
had been picked up by the truck hauling off debris and
was sent away.  Even though the story didn't end as we
had hoped it would, it was good to be able to tell
Baby Bear exactly what had happened with his box and
it was interesting to see how odd things happen. 

I decided then and there that I'm not going to just
walk away again if I have a box go missing - I'm going
to ask around and see if I can find someone who knows
something - you just never know!

Lucy
