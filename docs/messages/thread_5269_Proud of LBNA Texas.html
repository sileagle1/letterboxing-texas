From: Rachel Stout (prazehm878@yahoo.com) | Date: 2013-10-02 16:04:02 UTC
Every couple of months I find myself on a quick road trip-you know the kind-get straight to your destination to pick someone up/drop someone off, etc-which means I am never in town any significant length of time, but while there, I do try to get at least 1 letterbox. Some towns have tons of letterboxes, and others not so many.

I must say, though-I am proud to be a part of letterboxing in Texas! There are so many good quality plants to choose from here at home that I don't think I'll ever find them all.

So, Texas planters: thank you for your hard work!
