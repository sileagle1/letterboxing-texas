The Traveling Circus Is Traveling Again!

From: lb_bozo (lb_bozo@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-02-06 01:39:47 UTC
Well, whew folks! I finally escaped dewberry's garage. I mean, I
had such cabin fever, I didn't care where I was going at that point,
but come to find out that the circus train had been hijacked by some
sort of juggler. That fool said he wanted to get out and visit some
relatives. I didn't know what he was talking about, but apparently
he had won some sort of medal at the 2008 Summer Jester Olympics in
Beijing, China and he thought he was something special. He had heard
of these acrobatic athletes who practice the graceful art of Tae Kwon
Do (and won some medals themselves at the actual real summer
Olympics) - and he had heard that they were hanging out at Memorial
Park.

Well, so off we went to visit Team Lopez...and while we were there
chatting, I saw a coral snake. I know it was a coral because there
are lots of them at Memorial Park and it's diurnal, unlike its
harmless cousin the Louisiana snake. Well, I don't cotton to snakes,
even ones who look colorful enough to be in a circus, so I hightailed
it 12 more steps into the woods to hide until someone could come
rescue me. Behind the Lopez box, there's an enormous pine with vines
on the side. I am waiting desperately for someone to come get me.

LB Bozo
