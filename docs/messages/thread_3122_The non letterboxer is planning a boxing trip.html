Well, believe it or not, I plan to go get a box or two. My friends from work, including Lori of KMBaker, and I and our families are going to Possum Kingdom lake for the weekend.
 
It's taken us 3 weeks and two or more trips to the store and we are finally ready to go. Why does it take so much stuff to go stay at a campground for two nights. I believe it's as much stuff as we take for a week long adventure. Crazy.
 
But, I am so excited. Please pray for anything but rain. As it's been here in DFW lately, the nights are very cool and the days are lovely. That weather would be fine with me.
 
So, Friday and Saturday night we'll be sitting around a campfire making smores and drinking wine (dessert wine I'm sure).
 
It's only been 3 months since I've done any boxing out in the wild.
