pressured to make a favorable decision as to the next bus stop
location. there's barefoot lucy, who knows too well the pain of

daughter to the depths of denton in hopes of delivering the bus from
its current home in dallas. hmmm, then i feel the houston-ites
hovering over me, as if honing in on my house, hiding in the
hydrangeas outside my window.
maybe the clue for the next stop will read like this:
the box will be in (name of expensive restaurant) from (times
normally associated with dinner), being used as a booster seat by p ?
uest. to obtain box, buy a meal with enough yumminess to inspire
said letterboxer to prance around the restaurant, declaring the chef
a virtuoso. with the box thus unguarded, take it and run, trying
not to get stuck in the doorway with that enormous thing in your
arms!

teamking, there's still plenty of room for more stamps in there.
but how about a different touring vehicle, like the TX Tour Unicycle
or the TX Tour Radio Flyer Wagon? part of the fun of the bus is
getting those original stamps WITH the bus. besides, with multiple
touring boxes, more of our LBing population will have a better
chance of sharing in this great experience.

the awesome FF prize!
