From: samuraistampers (samuraistampers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-09 03:12:01 UTC

Hey all,
We had a great day of boxing today(and a Halloween ending,more about
that later)!
If you haven't gone to Dinosaur Valley State Park in Glen Rose -it
is well worth the drive. One of the ONLY places in the WORLD to see
dinosaur footprints! I had been there many times before but what
made it special today were the FOUR! boxes placed there by Baby
Bear, Mother of Four, Silver Eagle, and DrooP. A class was there
studying the prints so a lot of subterfuge was called for-especially
on Dinosaur Valley and Fred.
We also went to the Perry Homestead Box by Celtic Lions. Neat place
and beautiful handmade logbook. If you haven't found one of their
books yet, you are in for a real treat!
Now for the Halloween ending-We went to the Crockett Family box in
Acton. I don't know what I was expecting but it is in a real,
rather large cemetary! And we got there just at dusk with
thunderclouds moving in! Of course we didn't have a flashlight-so
needless to say that was the FASTEST STAMPING EVER!!!!!! Half of
our group stayed in the car while two of us searched. I don't know
which would be scarier-searching or waiting in the car but either
way I still don't think my heart has stopped pounding! Historical
importance aside-That will be my most memorable boxing experience
for the rest of my life! I guess it's kind of funny we did that in
October. You tell yourself you don't believe in ghosts, but try
that one at night when no one else is in the cemetary!
Thanks to Baby Bear for a chiiiiiilllllllllinnnnng box! All humor
aside-it is a great place and a neat stamp! :)
Kantexan and the Samurai Stampers.
