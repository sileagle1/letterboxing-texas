From: WagTime2007 (WagTime2007@tx.rr.com) | Date: 2009-03-21 15:03:01 UTC-05:00
Circle Tour 2009 is over. JBKokopelli and I got back in town last night
from our annual spring break "circle tour". This year's mission of
populating the panhandle with letterboxes is complete. We hunted and found
most of the existing panhandle boxes, and planted somewhere near 70 boxes
between us. One way or another, we left every single county in the region
with at least one letterbox. We also got to see some really cool (and
quirky) stuff. Details, clues, and travelogue to follow soon.

Silver Eagle, get ready to update the map again!

Wag Time

From: WagTime2007 (WagTime2007@tx.rr.com) | Date: 2009-03-22 15:24:26 UTC-05:00
To give credit where credit is due, JBK always does a mountain of research before we set out on these trips.  She finds out neat little bits of trivia about towns, works out routes, and pretty much plans it all out for us.  We had a binder of clues for existing boxes and a second binder of the things she'd printed off the internet about the towns we were going to plant boxes in.  She does all the driving and I do all the navigating and clue-reading, and as long as I remember to take my Dramamine, that system works out just great.
 
When we left DFW last Saturday morning, JBK had something like 48 boxes all set up and ready to go.  She then did an impromptu box (usually sitting in the car in a parking lot) each day of our trip and ended up with 54 or 55 boxes total.  The rest were mine.  There's no way I could even try to keep up with her!   The woman is a machine!
