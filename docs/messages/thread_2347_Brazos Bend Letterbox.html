Brazos Bend Letterbox

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2006-12-03 14:37:54 UTC
I was at Brazos Bend SP yesterday with my Boy Scout to enjoy the Old
Time Christmas celebration. There were people dressed in 1800's garb
showing old-time kid games and toys, and homemade dutch oven cobbler to
go with hot cider. It was fun and I barely had time to check on my box
before dark. It was laying out in the open, full of water, but at
least still there. This is my second placed box and first carved
stamp, so I'm glad the stamp is still there (even though it isn't
great, the first one you carve is special). I was not able to dry the
log very much, so I rebagged it and rehid the box. I will try to
replace the log next time I'm out there. I hope the log is readable
when it drys since there are many entries in the 4 years it has been
out there.

Silver Eagle
