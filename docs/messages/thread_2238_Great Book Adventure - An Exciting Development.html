From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-10-18 12:34:13 UTC
There may be a tiny misconstruing of terms.

If you plant it, it's not really an event box. Event boxes are out on
the table for all to find. You are eligible to receive them by virtue
of having come. They don't really count as finds, but they're still
cool. The Event Stamp for the GBA is humongous, and took me quite a
while to carve. There is also a smaller version if you only stamp on
notecards.

If you plant it, just for the day, it's a temporary box. There will
be lots of those.

This brings up a point I was just discussing with someone else. Many
of the boxes have been sent from afar. Some of the logs will be going
back to their owners. Please take the time to write a little note,
even if it's brief in each log. It means a lot to the people who took
the time to carve a stamp for us to enjoy.
