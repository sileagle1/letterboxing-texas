New Boxes in Texas

From: celticlions (celticlions@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-10-12 09:07:19 UTC-05:00




I get the biggest kick out of coming back to my computer after a weekend
away and finding out that we have even more NEW boxes around Texas! Thank
you, Hawk, Kantexan, Gingercat and a special thanks to the Cupcake Kid!
Our weekend was absolutely gorgeous - we went camping in Possum Kingdom
State park. My husband and sons had all camped there with Indian Guides and
Scouts and at the YMCA camp Grady Spruce, but I hadn't - it's a beautiful
area and well worth the trip. We loved all the delicate little deer
wandering around, and the scent of the cedar trees was wonderful. (And
Cactus Licker found several varieties of cactus to interest him!)
We placed one letterbox in Possum Kingdom (imaginatively titled "Possum
Kingdom";-))one in the Caddo Cemetery nearby and one in a truly fascinating
State Park about an hour further west: Fort Griffin...how could anyone
resist walking the grounds of the saloon where Wyatt Earp first met Doc
Holliday???
One thing that struck us forcibly all over again was how completely
different Texas is when you drive in any direction - we are so lucky to live
in a state that has such contrasts! A couple of weeks ago we did a tour of
East Texas pineywoods and a couple of months earlier we were down on the
Gulf Coast. And we're very much looking forward to alligator country in
March!
