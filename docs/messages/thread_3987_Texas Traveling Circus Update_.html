Texas Traveling Circus Update!

From: kristen (pacifickle@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-03-08 20:03:12 UTC
Mere minutes after TALE6 started, I saw a poster advertising tickets to
the Traveling Texas Circus. Before I could figure it out, I heard a
commotion and ran to the parking lot. To my surprise, the entire circus
was all there, hanging out in the bed of Boots Tex's DODGE truck- I knew
it was his from the license plate number. They were having a loud
argument- something about figuring out who's the real ringmaster and a
surprise performance in Austin. With Dee Pirate's help (I was a little
short to help them all out of the truck on my own), we wrangled all the
acts into the back of my Jetta. Luckily, they had a lot of experience
cramming many people into a small car. After a riotous drive home with
Dee Pirate, nrgkat and myself, the circus is laying low in my backyard.
They're groaning about the lack of cinnamon rolls- I think they've been
spoiled! I'll keep y'all posted on who the real ringmaster is and all
the details on the surprise Austin performance. I know we Austinites
have been dying for the TTC to come to town!

Thanks for all the fun at TALE6, everyone!
