We leave for Colorado on Friday. My plans are to attempt all the
virtuals caches in RMNP and all the boxs near the park. That would
include The Rockies, A Rocky Mountain Wedding, and Grand Lake. Also
possible in other parts of the state are Something Special for Eoghan,
Pike's Peak Cog Railway, Little Feet, Campus in the Sky, The Mystery
of the Lost Anasazi Pot (US Mystery), and Bighorn Sheep (CO Mystery).

limiting myself to caches and boxes that are no more than a mile from
pavement.
