From: Birds of a Feather Family (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-06 06:22:45 UTC-08:00
A quick update on boxes in the ER and Ann Taylor Park in Houston. 
 
I went out to check on my Runaway Circus series as the leopard had been reported as not in its hiding place.  I found it hiding in the leaves under the branch and I have replaced it back in its hiding spot.  The remaining 7 boxes were also in their hiding spots.
 
Dewberry's Timetraveler series boxes were in their spots as well as Runs for Chocolate's SEVEN box.
 
For those of you that were concerned about the reports of mosquitoes, spiders and poison ivy, now is a great time to visit this park.
 
I wanted to mention that Texas will soon reached the milestone of 1000 letterboxes placed.
 
Based upon my research for the Texas Letterboxing Atlas, I have found current and historical records for 991 letterboxes hidden in Texas. This number does not count hitchhikers or personal travelers.
 
Also welcome to all the new talklist members.
