From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2014-12-16 07:50:28 UTC-07:00

Of course!  BIER!  (Beer to you who do not read German).

 

Although I haven't seen a letter box in a few years, I am still very active outdoors.  Beside competitive shooting (I won the Garand Match in Nov) one of the events I participate in is called Summits On The Air, using small portable amateur radio equipment from atop high summits.  Even small radios are capable of worldwide communications when used atop a 7000+ foot stone tower.

 

Although Lucy has her ham license, I have not convinced her to come climb with me yet!  I keep hoping.

 

The summits are each graded by a point system, according to elevation.  The goal is 1000 points, to earn a Mountain Goat trophy.  I will earn my 1000th point in Germany.  I already know the day, and the summit.  As with many German summits, there is a gasthaus (restaurant) right near the summit.  Once I qualify the summit, I will hike the 2km back to the gasthaus where many of my German family and friends will be waiting, for a celebration of my Mountain Goat status.  There will be BIER that evening, along with good food and great friends.

 

I can't wait!  <grin>.

 

Mike - El Paso.
