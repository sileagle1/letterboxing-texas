Baby Bear,

Over the weekend I was camping with our Cub Scout pack
and I was working on putting together a box to plant
in the park were we were camping (Ratcliff Lake - to
be posted in the near future). Our den knows what
letterboxing is about, but for the rest of the boys it
was a whole new show. I wound up showing them my
personal logbook to look at the stamps I had found.

They think you make the coolest stamps of anyone they
saw in my book. I think the all around favorite was
the Hog stamp at Jim Hogg Park in Rusk! What really
surprised me was that they started seeing similarities
between the ones of yours that I had in my book, and
they could just about pick out who had carved them.
So I wanted to let you know that you are the hip fave
of the Under 12 scouting crowd!

Lucy
