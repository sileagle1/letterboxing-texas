From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2013-03-09 13:45:27 UTC-06:00
My circus “cousins” are bringing their show to the Long Center in Austin, April 12-14!  If you’d like to meet Nino from the Texas Traveling Circus, and of the hilarious Narrative Clue of TALE 8, please come to Austin to see the show.  The Bell Motel is available!
Their web site (built by Whiz Gee long ago) is www.zoppe.net.
The Long Center web site is www.thelongcenter.org.

ZOPPE ITALIAN FAMILY CIRCUS

April 12 - 14

Keep up with the Show!

  • Show Site

A 160-year-old tradition that began life near Venice, Italy, will come to life under a giant tent on the Long Center’s West Lawn! The Zoppe Family will take you to a world of acrobatics, equestrian showmanship, canine capers, clowns and lots of audience participation! Prepare to be enchanted! (Great for all ages.)
