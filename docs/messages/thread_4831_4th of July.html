From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2011-06-23 16:39:55 UTC-06:00
I will be in Germany. Leaving Monday - back in mid-July.

Did you know they have a 4th of July in Germany? They sure do - it is on
every calendar. Spelled 04 Juli. It just isn't a holiday except on the
military bases.

I do not think we are going to be doing any boxing over there. I have not
been successful in finding any information about letterboxes in Germany, and
we already have a plate full for the 2+ weeks we will be there. I do have 3
hikes planned though, and a lot of radio operating with my old friends.
Probably some hiking in conjunction with radio operating as that is popular
there.

If you would like to see a little bit about where we are going, watch this
web cam.
http://www.leder-weiss.de/lederweisswebcamschweinfurt.html

If you look at the right edge about half-way up, you will see a sidewalk
cafe. I will certainly be having lunch there one day. The building in the
top right with the spire is the City Hall.

There are 3 summits I will climb. The first is the Wasserkuppe. It is the
highest summit in the Rhone Mountains of northern Bavaria. Web Cam.
http://www.wasserkuppe.com/wetter.html

The second summit I climb will be the Kreutzberg. The reward for climbing
this one is the monk monastery at the top. The beer! The food is to die
for. http://www.kreuzbergbier.de/content/kb_webcam.php

The third summit has an old castle ruin and I just want to climb it to look
around.

Right now in Germany sunrise is about 0430, and sunset about 2100. I will
make use of every hour.

Hope you-all have a safe and happy 4th. I will not be thinking of you.
.
