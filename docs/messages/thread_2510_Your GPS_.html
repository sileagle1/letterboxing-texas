Yeah - the Legend is a good beginners GPS.  IIRC - it has 1MB of memory.  If we ever meet and I have my laptop with, I will offer to dump some maps in it for you.  It will be up to you to decide what maps.  <grin>
 
ExpertGPS is a mighty fine program.  It allows you to manage waypoints, track logs, and routes.  It will also make maps.  It will do TOPO maps ot Aerial Photo maps.  It will also let you scan in and use your own maps.  You might some time in the future decide you want National Geographic TOPO - at $100 a state.  But ExpertGPS does ALL states - does most of what NG-TOPO does, and costs only $60 or so.
