From: iraboxer (iraboxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-02-27 01:49:18 UTC
Well, they did it again. When I planted my "The Shot Box", a depiction of the Gonzales cannon, I didn't think they'd actually come and get it, but they did. So, I went back and replaced it and I'll be dadgummed if they didn't come and get it again! If any of y'all were planning on finding that box next week, don't bother, cause someone stole the stamp again and a good Samaritan, bless her HEART, picked up the box and logbook. I guess I'll have to put it somewhere else, since the Ghosts of the Mexican Army don't want it where I put it. Or maybe it wasn't them...now that I think about it, the last one to find it before it went missing the first time was that Cochorro de Oso! I wouldn't be surprised if he done it again.

The Ghost of IRA Boxer
President of the Cochorro de Oso Fan Club
