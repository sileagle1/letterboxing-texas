New Letterbox in Liberty County

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-01-27 00:40:47 UTC
Hey, fellow letterboxers: I would like to announce a new series of
letterboxes that I launched today. I call it the "Texas Governor
Series" and I planted the first one today in Liberty County. It's
called "Price of Liberty". I plan to plant the second one tomorrow,
so stay tuned. These boxes will not be in any order, like starting
with the first governor, but will be opportunistic. That is, I will
try to get the East Texas governors first, because they are closer to
where I live, and go from there. However, if I have the opportunity
to place one in another area, I'll take advantage of it. There have
been 46 different governors so far, so this is a long-term project.
The stamps will all be graphic portraits.

Boots Tex
