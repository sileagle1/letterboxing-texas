From: Pam Rodgers (perky_p_99@yahoo.com) | Date: 2010-03-03 06:17:35 UTC-08:00
You know how I sent out that email yesterday talking about how excited I was about the gathering and how I had been packing all day and getting ready for it?
Well, last night I had a dream about it. Everything that could go wrong did!! At one point I was sitting at the table doing exchanges, it was set up to where you stamped in the logbook and then passed it to the next person. Well, my pile was getting bigger and bigger and bigger! Everyone started getting mad at me b/c I was holding everything up. Then all of a sudden I was stamping into some boxes but got the stamps and logbooks all mixed up. I couldn't tell which one went to which box or where they belonged! So I ended up shoving them in my purse and running! LOL I ran to a bathroom b/c I had to use the facilities. However all the stalls were full with letterboxers stamping their sig stamps on the stall walls! I was getting nervous b/c it was taking everyone so long and I really had to go. I just knew it was them getting back at me for taking so long with the exchanges. Well then Blue Butterfly found out that I had shoved the boxes into my purse and
started yelling at me for stealing boxes. (Which is hilarious b/c if any of you have ever met Blue Butterfly there isn't a mean bone in her body. LOL) She demanded I give them back to her. When I went to reach for my purse I realized it had been stolen during the scary movie we had all went to see! When I told her my purse had been stolen she apologized for making me see that movie b/c it was so dumb. Then I woke up.

So now I'm nervous about getting behind during the exchanges, going letterboxing and getting the boxes all mixed up, having to go to the bathroom, someone asking me to see a movie with them, and getting my purse stolen. Just thought I'd share all of that with you so if you see me looking nervous you know what I'm thinking. LOL

Perky P

Hehe...  I don't think anyone could be more nervous than I was last summer when we went to our first event. Picture this now... I drove 3 hours to a town that I had never been to with the intention of meeting a bunch of people that I heard about on-line.  My friends thought I was crazy to take my daughter to a Tomato Festival to look for people I didn't know and tell them answers or ask them questions all for clues to a box. 
 
We were the two wide eyed girls walking around and around that festival with tomatoes on our hats trying to look cool while looking and trying to decipher Barefoot Lucy's clues.  We finally found Booth 83 and Maydelle QueenBee's Mom ands after sighing with relief and telling her the letterboxer's bible verse correctly we were pumped. 
 
Then we got really lucky and we were adopted by perfect strangers...lol Maydelle QueenBee & PrincessA !! lol I remember Pink Cookie whispering in my ear "Are we going to get in THEIR car??"  and me going against everything I had ever taught her about "Stranger Danger" saying back to her with a big smile "Why yes honey, they are Letterboxers too!!" and I said that without batting an eye!  Me not knowing the area at all it was a life saver for us...lol Pink Cookie and I had a great day of letterboxing with some wonderful gals!
 
And we might have been the last group to get to the restaurant to eat with everyone that afternoon.... but when we walked in the door it felt like a room full of family!  Everyone was so excited to meet us and we were so happy to put faces and trail names to owners of letterboxes that we had found on the trail and meet some of the names we had seen stamped in boxes too!  If this is your first event you will feel it too, just wait and see!
 
We are getting very excited to see our Letterboxing Friends and the meeting of new ones!
