From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-01-18 17:08:26 UTC
Hi, Mommyaulait,

According to LbNA, there are 36 letterboxes in Cypress, most of them
in Telge Park. I'm not recommending our boxes over any other, bou
are certainly welcome to look for our boxes there, called Stingy
Jack and Legend of Sleepy Hollow. I would encourage you to read the
clues and decide which box appeals to you in terms of subject,
location, walking distance, etc. Just follow the rules about being a
responsible letterboxer, making sure the box is sealed well and
rehidden and covered so that it can't accidentally be seen. Also,
it's been raining out there for a couple of days, so the trails will
be wet and muddy. Good luck.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
