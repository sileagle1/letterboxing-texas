Hello Texas Boxers!
This is Ponder from NC.  I have been boxing for a little over a year now........thanks to my big sister Wood6!  I've definitely been bitten by the boxing bug!  Here in NC most of us use Atlas Quest for all our boxing needs.  It seems that there are more listings there than on letterboxing.org. 
So, the reason I have come to your Texas board is because I will be in Houston next weekend (arriving 2/6pm and leaving 2/9) and I was hoping to do a little boxing or maybe even some exchanging.  Saturday I will be tied up most of the day in class.....(the real reason I'm coming)....On Sunday class is supposed to be over at about 2:30pm.  So, if anyone is interested, please let me know.  The only drawback is that I will not have transportation nor will I have a computer while I am there......so I'm hoping that I can make some plans before I leave NC on Friday. If anyone has any info about any boxes that are in the vicinity of the Holiday Inn Astrodome or any that I can get to using public transportation from there, I'd appreciate any input!
Thanks for letting me "barge" in on your board!
Looking forward to my visit!
Ponder
