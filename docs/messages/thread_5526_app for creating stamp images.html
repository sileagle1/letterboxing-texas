app for creating stamp images

From: Christine Borgfeld (christine@borgfeld.net) | Date: 2015-11-21 16:01:37 UTC-06:00

I posted this on AQ, but not everyone here reads the forums regularly (including me!)

 

I just learned that Adobe has a mobile app for making stamp-like images from your phone camera. You can see the world as a stamp on your screen! It's an easy way to get an image for carving from objects around you. It should work for portraits too. I'm driving the family (and pets) crazy by taking pictures of everything.

The app is Adobe Capture CC and I posted some images from it and a link to a tutorial on my sadly neglected blog:
http://www.open-space-designs.com/2015/11/21/see-the-world-as-a-stamp-with-adobe-app/

Getting images out the app is a bit of a hassle (Adobe is pushing their paid subscription services), but I posted instructions on how to get the best image from the free service.
