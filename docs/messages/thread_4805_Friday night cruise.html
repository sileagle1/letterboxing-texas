All who have responded with interest in a sunset cruise of Lake Jacksonville - we can seat 12 on the boat. If we can get it from one pier to the other, we'll be fine though, and we have time to figure that out!

Also, my in-laws (where the boat lives) have a great fishing area for anyone who might want to fish from the pier. A license is required and game wardens are active on our lake.
