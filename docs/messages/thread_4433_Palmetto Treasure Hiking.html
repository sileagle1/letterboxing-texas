Palmetto Treasure Hiking

From: Vicki Bell (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2010-03-10 14:06:20 UTC-06:00
On Monday, I asked at the Palmetto State Park office if they knew how long the new trails were.  I was told that someone was coming out to measure this week, and then signs would go up.  They believe that they have about 5 miles of trails total, and the the long back trail that skirts the perimeter is about 3 miles long.  In that case, I think I can safely say that I walked at least 3 miles on Saturday, trying to find every box in one day, and can safely apply 3 miles to my Texas Treasure Hiking count.  Does that sound right to everyone else?  -- Bell Motel
