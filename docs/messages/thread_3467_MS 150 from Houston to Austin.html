Not about boxing, but next weekend, my son Brian will be riding the
MS(Multiple Sclerosis)150 mile bike ride to Austin, TX. Pray for good
weather and safe ride, please.

He rides a Recumbent Bicycle that rests him back rather than on his
arms forward.

I think he will have his cousin's name written on his leg to be riding
for since you won't be able to see his shirt back where they normally
put it.

If you see a big,handsome, smiling transplanted Texan say Hi Brian for
me will you?

Granny Butterfly of SE OH

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2008-04-04 22:46:01 UTC
I did this ride many years ago and it was a lot of fun, as long as
the wind was at my back. The route goes through some very scenic
places, including Buescher & Bastrop State Parks, so it helps take
your mind off the pain. Not sure I could make it anymore; partly
because of my knees, but mainly because I would stop too much to
letterbox! Tell him good luck.

Silver Eagle
