The Terror of Telge

From: iraboxer (iraboxer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-10-13 16:19:05 UTC
What creepy crawly scary things
Exist in Telge Park,
That hide in logs and slimy bogs
And chunks of rotten bark,
Appearing ghostly and that mostly
Come out after dark?

There's Frankenstein and Dracula
And goblins with long green hair,
Grim reaper from Hell, haunted hotel;
They're all designed to scare,
But I'm a ghost, I turn bears to toast,
I'm the Bane of Baby Bear!

The Ghost of IRA Boxer

> > > > I'd like to share a quite amazing story (to me, at least), about one of my letterboxes, the one located in the small town of Benjamin, Knox County, Texas. I am a big fan of the work of Wyman Meinzer, who lives in Benjamin and is the official photographer for the state of Texas. In doing my usual research for our trip out to that part of the state last December, I discovered that nothing had ever happened in Knox County, Texas. Most of the county is taken up with large ranches and populated with javelina, wild turkey, coyotes and good upstanding citizens, but that's okay, because that's my kind of country. To get on with the story, I had a book of photographs by Mr. Meinzer which included a very nice picture of a howling coyote (not the letterboxing kind, mind you), so I made a box using his coyote as my stamp and I called it "Meinzer's Coyote". It had never been found until today when I got a very nice notice from Mrs. Wyman Meinzer as follows:
> > > >
> > > > Dear Boots Tex,
> > > > Wyman and I found the box with the stamp of a depiction of his, coyote, at the cemetery tonight. It was loads of fun. Wyman and I had a great time and we thank you for your kind words about Wyman's work. This was our first box to find, and will now search for others and even place some of our own.
> > > >
> > > > How cool is that? As most of you know, the usual subjects of my letterboxes are of folks who populated Texas in the distant past, usually of the deceased variety, and none of them have ever found one of my boxes! I'm hoping the Meinzers indeed do place some letterboxes. I'll try to beat Gryzzled Gryphon to them.
> > > >
> > > > Boots Tex
