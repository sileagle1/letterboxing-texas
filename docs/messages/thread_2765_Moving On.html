Moving On

From: TeamKing (teamkingus@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-12 17:29:08 UTC-05:00
Hi Everyone -

Just a note to let you know that we are moving. Yes, we're leaving
this big ole state of Texas and moving to England around August. I
have quite a few placed boxes - including the two Texas Tour Bus
boxes - which I am going to leave in place instead of pulling
everything. Any missing boxes will simply be retired, and if a box
comes up that needs maintenance (ie new logbook or such), perhaps I
will be able to ask for some assistance so we can keep it it
circulation. (:p)

I'll still be lurking around the list and getting some area boxes
before we leave. But I just wanted to tell everyone a big THANKS!
for placing all your boxes. There were hardly any boxers in Houston
when I started. In fact, I recall going after many FFs against Silver
Eagle, Baby Bear, and the missing Hawk. So few boxes popped up, that
we all descended like vultures when one did! LOL. I've had a great
time getting out and finding all of your boxes. SO many places I
never knew existed! And it's been really fun putting faces to the
names - especially from the gatherings. We're like this little tribe
out wondering around among the masses looking for things in the woods
and around buildings. Also, feel free to put a new box in any place
I have a retired box (hint hint Howard Hughes).

I'll try to place some boxes outside of London for you to find on
your way to Dartmoor. They'll be listed on AQ when it happens.

Carry on and I'll be in touch now and again!

TeamKing
