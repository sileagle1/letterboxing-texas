Great Day For Picking Blueberries

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2007-06-03 12:58:30 UTC
Yesterday I found myself north of Houston with my daughter at yet
another soccer tounament. For those of you that have never experienced
one, it usually consists of 2 or more games per day with much waiting
in between. As it turned out, our first game was at 7:30 (had to get
up at 5:30 to arrive by 6:45) but our second game was not until 2:30.
So, rather than go home and come back, I talked Running Rain into going
to Lake Houston Park to find a box and place one. Then we went to my
favorite place to pick blueberries, which is not too far west of
there. It is called Moorehead's and I will give the website at the
end. It was crowded, but we still managed to pick over 11 pounds in
about 1 hour (not including all the ones that went into our mouths
instead of our buckets). They only charge $1.50/lb and it is a great
family activity. I recommend calling first to make sure they are open
and that there are still plenty of berries. I also recommend going
early before it gets too hot and on a weekday if possible. Now I'm off
for more soccer and a family reunion. Enjoy!

http://www.moorheadsblueberryfarm.com/

Silver Eagle
