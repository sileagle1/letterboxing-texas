From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2005-09-02 21:44:07 UTC
Thanks to Puddlesplasher & Viewfinder this box, and
Baby Bear's Missing Bones box, have been saved from
confiscation. They moved both boxes today and sent
us the new clues. I have updated my clues, so be
sure to reprint them if going for this box. This was
really beyond the call of duty and I am very grateful!

P.S. I'll bet everyone on this list, including me, would
like to hear how it went today dealing with park personnel.

From: Curt Jagger (cjagger405@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-09-02 18:19:56 UTC-04:00
Believe it or not, the park personnel was extremely interested and helpful - even to the point to bringing out maps and showing us other possible places to locate the boxes! We hiked around the Caprock Trail twice, once to retreve the boxes and scout for good locations, then again to place the boxes and write (and double check) the clues. I used to enjoy hiking out there before I had moved to CT five years ago, so it was fun for me to get to go back and hike that area again. We actually spent two and a half hours out there, so by the time we left it was "good and hot"! That meant we had to hit a favorite restaurant of mine in the area for fried green tomatoes and iced tea for our reward! I surely LOVE being back home in TEXAS!
Puddle Splasher
