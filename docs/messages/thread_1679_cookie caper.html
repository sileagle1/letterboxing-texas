Well...since you asked! ;-)

So far, I've had both Texas Gal and TX Dragonfly complete the Cookie
Caper Series. These two nice ladies finished the series first,
although they were not the FF of all the boxes! Guess who? You got
it...the elusive GG. He actually completed the series the day after
Texas Gal and TX Dragonfly!!! He was though, to give him credit,
the FF on most of the boxes!

Texas Gal and TX Dragonfly are pretty new boxers, but I reckon
they're going to be a force to be reckoned with. They are rapidly
racking up F counts! Very nice ladies and I am very glad to have
met them.

As for GG, I was so tickled to meet him that I called my neighbors,
NorthStars family, to come over and meet him, too! All I can tell
you is that if you're able to track this elusive Gryphon
down...you're in for a treat! He is utterly charming and has some
great stories to tell! For my Thanks-A-Lots clues...he never even
deciphered the clue...just went to the park and thought "I've done
this enough...where would I hide a box?" and went straight to where
the box was hidden! Next time...I'm gonna have to think "outside of
the box"!

So far, the feedback on the Cookie Caper series has been good. I've
tweaked the clues a tad-bit (ie more North, than West), so make sure
you check again right before you set out for the series. My
cryptic clues are all "solvable". All boxes seem to be holding up
well, and all locations have been verified as "good" hiding spots.
Also, my contact info is correct as everyone who has completed the
series has been able to find me!

I want to invite everyone again to come to Central Texas to complete
the series. It was a lot of fun to create and I hope as much fun to
track down those boxes!!!

Looking forward to meeting, exchanging and giving away FREE Girl
Scout Cookies! Thanks, Batty B
