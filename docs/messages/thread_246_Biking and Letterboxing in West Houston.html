Biking and Letterboxing in West Houston

From: Birds of a Feather Family (birdsofafeather_lbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-08-01 12:59:46 UTC-07:00
The Birds of a Feather Family went different directions this weekend. 
 
The boys (Sea Dove and Liberty Eagle) went biking on 7/31/2004 at Terry Hersey Park and found the Hersey Kiss microbox. 
 
Nesting with this box we found the Franzsolo MM II hitchhiker. This fellow avian decided to fly along with us.  
 
This hitchhiker made a couple previous flights/stops at Hersey Park arriving with Armyswmr99 at the Blessing Box and taking a short flight with Hawk to the Hersey Kiss earlier on Saturday. 
 
We enjoyed the park and plan to make additional trips to the park for more biking and letterboxing.
 
Sea Dove
 
 
 
 


 
Sea Dove, Peacekeeper, Splish Splash, & Liberty Eagle
