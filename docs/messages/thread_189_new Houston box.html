I just wanted to let you all know that I just posted the clues to a
new Houston box called HAPPY BIRTHDAY BOX. I actually planted it as
a gift for my sister's birthday, and hadn't gotten around to posting
the clues yet. Then I got an e mail from a guy who had found it by
accident, and he wrote to ask what letterboxing was all about. He
said that he was going to try it out, that he had never heard of it
before and thought it sounded interesting.

We haven't been doing too much letterboxing latley because we have
had visitors from South America staying with us for the last month.
If you all think it is difficult trying to explain it to you friends
and family, all I have to say is try explaining it in a second
language! We hope to be getting out soon, we have one we are waiting
to plant in Brazos Bend. - Mother of Four
