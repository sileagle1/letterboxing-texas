12 x 12 fun for arty types

From: Ashby (ashby@ic.org) | Date: 2011-07-15 23:52:22 UTC-07:00
Here is a fun event that has been going on for the past few weeks, and is coming to a close today.

It is the annual SFA art department fundraiser, called the 12 x 12 show.

They give out boards that are 12 x 12 inches (wow, who would have guessed?)  and you can do whatever family-friendly art on them you want to.  Pieces are available for silent auction, starting at $250 and for $100 on the night of the show, whatever hasn't sold in auction.  At the very end of the show, they raffle off the last few (raffled tickets $1 each, I think?)

The closing "reception" is much more like a party and it's a fun time with fun people, all free of course (unless you want to buy some art) and snacks provided by Merci, so that's fabulous.

Hope you can come!  I'll toss my book in the car and if we run into each other, maybe we do a quick exchange, perhaps?  But even without that, it's a fun time and I encourage anyone who lives close enough to stop by.

It's 7-9 on Saturday night, and I'd recommend you arrive early for the most fun.

See you soon!
