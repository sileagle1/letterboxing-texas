From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2008-01-12 10:07:15 UTC-07:00
NOT PEPPER SPRAY!!!!!
 
First - this is a good choice, as far as it goes.  I carry something like it - I will use it rather that than my pistol.... if there is time.  Often attacks happen so swiftly there isn't time to select.
 
But not spray.  FOAM.  Yes - Pepper FOAM.
 
http://www.tbotech.com/pepperfoam.htm  The prices here are high, I have bought the smaller one for under $10 before.  Search the web.
 
Lessons - things learned from other people's problems.
 
Knives and stun guns work, but they are CONTACT weapons.  This means you must touch the bad guy (BG).  And that means the BG can touch YOU.  Not good!
 
Many of the pepper (Oleoresin Capsicum or OC) devices provide a good bit of protection and are non-lethal.  But the big plus is that they are STAND-OFF weapons.  There is no touch involved.
 
Pepper spray has a dis-advantage.  It can spray back.  It is a fine mist, almost a fog - like bug spray.  It can blow back on you.  And anything on you has two dis-advantages.  One - it can seriously lessen your eye-sight and breathing, and your escape.  Two - what gets on you did not get on your attacker.
 
Pepper foam shoots out in a stream.  Like long-range wasp killer.  It is a little harder to aim - thats true.  So you hold the button down and wave it in their direction.  Once it gets on the BG, it expands like shaving cream.  So even if the BG is wearing glasses, it will get behind the glasses.  Being thick, it forces them to halt the attack to wipe it away.  You cannot see through it.
 
Last, this brand marks the BG with an ultra-violet dye that can identify him to the police.
 
I strongly recommend you buy two.  Use one for practice.  Get you a man-shaped target and try shooting it first.  The time to learn is before you need to protect yourself.
 
Again, hope I am not scaring people.  I do live in another world than most people.  OTOH, the most passive guy I know was only one mugging away from asking me for lessons.  He now owns a gun - AND pepper foam.  The time to ask is before it happens.
 
BTW - In Texas OC components are considered simple assault.  If you are justified in punching someone to defend yourself - you are justified in using OC to defend yourself.
 
I am depressing myself - I think I will go for a hike!
