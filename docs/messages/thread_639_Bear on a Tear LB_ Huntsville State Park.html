Bear on a Tear LB, Huntsville State Park

From: Norasta (windorn@lycos.co.uk) | Date: 2005-03-01 18:55:07 UTC

Hello southern friends, before leaving Texas I planted a LB in
Huntsville SP where a couple of boxes had gone missing. The clues
are available by request only to bonafide LBNA folks, sent to them
via their trail name & the LBNA site. I had 4 requests quite quickly
from LBer's who
are listed, and I sent the clues to them. Friday I had a request for
clues from "Evosaurus" who I could not find listed on any LB site. I
asked
them to clarify and did not hear back. Today I got an email from
someone who said their grandaughter had found Bear On a Tear and had
brought it home. This box was well hidden off the trail, so an
accidental finding would be very unlikley. The grandmother (not a
LBer) asked me for the clues so she could put the box back....
Hmmmm?????. Has anyone been to look for this box? Is someone making
a collection of Huntsville boxes? Theives or well meaning folks?
What to do?
Norasta
