Last weekend Trip - Hill Country

From: Baby Bear (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-03-25 14:51:39 UTC
Love Bunny and I (Baby Bear) took off Friday for the Hill country.
We camped at Kerrville State Park and made a day trip Saturday to
multiple places, mainly wineries!!! Along the way we found and hid
some letterboxes. Here is the list and status.

Enchanted Eagle - Alive and well (had to be careful due to large
crowd)
Enchanted Rock - Alive and Well
Hid new box ("Yogi") at Enchanted Rock SNA
Hid new box ("Littel Phoexiz") at Fall Creek winery near Llano.
Found Lost Marble & Sweet Berry - both alive and well.
Hid new box ("Park Ranger") at Kerrville-Shreiner State Park.
Replaced Dino Tracks box at Natural Bridge Caverns.
Replaced Texas Jackalope at Lockhart State Park.
Had some great BBQ in Lockhart!!!

Great weekend!! Suggest you if you like wine you make the trip up
that way. We visited 7 wineries in one day saturday, and was worth
the effort.
