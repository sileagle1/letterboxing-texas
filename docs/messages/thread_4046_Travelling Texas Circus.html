Travelling Texas Circus

From: (vicki@bellmotel.com) | Date: 2009-04-11 20:22:08 UTC-05:00
The wake in Austin for Alberto Zoppe is over.  The Zoppe family members were so touched by the tributes from the Texas Travelling Circus that Alberto's sons, Tino and Nino, have joined the cast of the show.  And now, it is time to head down the road.  Perhaps, it is time to add some new animal acts to the show.  Pulling up stakes, the tent heads south and west, catching the faint trace of big cat smell in the air.  Following the scent as it gets stronger, the circus turns onto Circle Dr.  Of course!  There is a sign for a zoo!  As with old habit, they follow the arrows down and along Rawhide Trail.  Maybe some of these animals can be convinced to leave their life behind bars for a life of adventure on the road, with a family who loves them.  For the smell of the peanuts, the roar of the crowd!
But night is falling, and the gate to the zoo is closed.  The circus makes camp in the triangular patch of land in front of the gate.
