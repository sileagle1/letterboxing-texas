ahoy...Newbie to this board. i actually live in alaska. However all
my in-laws live in Texas and we visit frequently. We still own land
in Hooks and a lot of relatives are in Arkansas. I plan on doing what
i call "the little ole lady" tour. 6 of my husbands aunts are between
89 and 103 years old. My husband and I jump in the camper van, go
pick up the 89 year old who lives in Mississippi and then go visit the
other 5 "little ole ladies" who live between Miss. and Little Rock. I
figure there must be some letterboxes on the "tour" to be found. Who
knows maybe these little ole ladies know a good place to hide a
letterbox or two along the way.

If anyone has a good letterbox route from Chatawa, Ms thorugh
Shreveport and Texarkana and onto Conroy, AR and back to Texarkana and
then to Dallas Ft Worth...give me a shout.

If you ever make it up to Alaska we got some great Letterboxes here.

Having just returned from a trip into Northwestern Arkansas, I can tell you that there are small pockets of letterboxes in Arkansas, but mostly centered around Little Rock, Conway, and Hot Springs as far as the route you mention goes.  None in Texarkana, a few in Shreveport.  Lots in DFW, for which you may want to join the metroboxers talk list ( groups.yahoo.com/groups/metroboxers).
