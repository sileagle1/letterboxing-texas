Hi, y'all, and thanks for the invite! Like SilentDoug and a couple
others, we're not Texans but love to follow other regional
letterboxing lists. Each one takes on a flavor of its own, and
everyone knows a Texas board is bound to be FUN.

Although we don't live in the Great State, we do have an ongoing love
affair with Austin, visiting several times for the music scene and
the bats. My Dad's favorite "war story" from WWII didn't involve his
many missions in bombers over Europe, but rather an adventure he had
at Kelly Field outside of San Antonio. A navigator, he was in TX to
get training on a new bombsight when a hurricane threatened the base.
All the qualified aviators were ordered to fly the aircraft out of
the way of the storm, and his crew didn't have a specific assignment
on where to go. So they flew to my grandfather's ranch in California,
stayed a week entertaining the local girls, and were late getting
back to Kelly. He would get tears in his eyes telling that story.

For those that don't know us, we've been letterboxing since 1999 and
have a pretty high "PFX" count as a result. We're also involved with
the letterboxing.org website and wll be glad to help anyone with any
questions or problems.

Wish we could join you at the Gathering!
Jay and family in CT
