Double Lake update

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-10-03 16:09:32 UTC
I went over to Double Lake yesterday and walked around the lake to
check on letterboxes. There are quite a few trees down, but the
lakeshore trail has been cleared and the boxes near the lake are all
okay. I didn't venture out on the Lone Star Trail, but I intend to do
that soon. The trail is ankle deep in leaves, sticks, branches and
pine cones and no clearing has been done yet. I'll update in a few
days. On a different subject, my letterbox in Fredericksburg
called "Nimitz" has apparently been lost due the construction of a new
George Washington/Bush Building which resulted in the demolition of the
fence next to which the box was hidden and the erection of a new chain-
link construction fence. This occurred between Sept. 28, when AnnaBee
found the box and October 2, when patroln went to find it. Thanks to
patroln (California letterboxer) for letting me know. Has anyone been
to Mercer Arboretum since Hurricane Ike?

Boots Tex
