October 22nd Gathering - Head count please!

From: Barefoot Lucy (barefootlucy@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-15 19:18:43 UTC-07:00
Could I go ahead and start getting a headcount of who
plans to be at the gathering? This won't be an
etched-in-stone thing, just for the sake of making
sure the site is adequate.

I would also like to start getting a count of those
who want to send boxes, but won't be attending.

And don't forget the recipe exchange. Remember - you
will need to make up cards of your favorite recipe and
stamp it with your personal stamp. Make enough for
everyone who wants to exchange (including folks who
want to send their recipes on in even if they can't
attend).

October 22nd-Waxahachie. Tentatively set for
Getzendaner Park, but I'm still trying to get
confirmation from the City of Waxahachie on that.

I would suggest coming the night before and staying
close by and possibly stay on on Saturday night as
well. There is camping in Bardwell and at Cleburne
State Park, hotels in Waxahachie. My family will be
there both nights, and we'll camp at Bardwell.

The intent is to hit the ground hard Saturday morning
and get to planting, then gather up around Noon or 1
p.m. to eat and exchange, then go out and find all the
new stashes. I plan to have maps and material on hand
to look at to get planting ideas for those who want to
meet first thing Saturday morning, or if you already
know what you'll be doing, you can hit the trails
without meeting up.

Let me hear from you!
Lucy
