Letterbox report.

From: Mike Olbrisch (mike-2007@elp.rr.com) | Date: 2010-05-09 17:03:36 UTC-06:00
Moni wanted to do some hiking for mother's day. So we did the Ron Coleman trail
- the toughest trail in the Franklin Mountains.

Silver Eagle - I dropped by your Mogollon Mask letterbox - all is well.

Sandi Box - We all dropped in on your Franklin Mountains box. We sorta adopted
a wounded warrior and his family on the trail. He is recovering from one
surgery and getting ready for several more. He had two nice kids with him and
we used your box to teach them what letterboxing was. I think they will be back
- they were excited about it. Your box is still in fine shape too.

This is the trail Moni picked for Mother's Day.

http://www.localhikes.com/Hikes/RonColeman_2320.asp

Hope you-all had as good a day as we did - far far better than standing in line
at the IHOP for 3 hours.
