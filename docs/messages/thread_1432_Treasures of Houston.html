MO5, this is the first response I got from you, but my e-mail has been acting stupid all weekend.
 
Here are the clues:
 
Go to the Flower Man's House. Many years ago, The Flower Man was lost in an alcoholic haze. While recovering from alcohol poisoning in the hospital, he had a vision of a beautiful house, covered in flowers. He immediately decided to quit drinking and build his vision. Some 20 years later, he is still sober and still delighting visitors with his ever-changing folk art!
 
The house is at 2305 Frances. From Houston, take 59S toward Victoria. Just past MinuteMaid Park, take the McGowen/Tuam exit. Stay straight to go onto Hamilton. Go 3 blocks and turn left on Elgin. Go about 3 blocks and turn right on Bastrop. Then, go left on Frances and the house is on your left just a few doors down. 
 
Stand facing the house and look for a small piece of blue lattice fence. Look behind the concrete block leaning against the blue lattice fence.
 
Remember to observe all the usual precautions. This is a rough part of town- I would not go alone or at night.
