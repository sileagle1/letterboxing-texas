Hey Hawk, we are on spring break -- so I took the kids to Jesse Jones
and we went and found the Scary Scorpio box, which is in great
shape. The little village was not open, but we actually had more
fun, because we were the only ones out there, the kids had so much
fun. My son wanted to live there. lol Next to that big tree and
vine is a hole in the ground - a pretty good size one. and there

eaten corn cobs (I guess it got them from that corn cob shed in the
village) man I just wanted to get that box, do are stamps -- I was
kind of nervous about putting the box back. My 10 yo daughter was
with us -- matter of fact she was the one that found the tree, I
thought it was the tree right by the trail. ANYWAY, she put it back
and I went with her to make sure she covered it up and it got back in
the same place, but she was the brave one, mainly because she did not
notice the hole. Usually my husband is with us and he takes care of
that stuff.

We had fun and you stamp was great -- you do such a great job at
carving, remarkable.!!! THANKS ANITA
