Tawakoni State Park

From: Lois -James (adamstrails@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-06-15 00:32:34 UTC
We went camping and letterboxing this week end at Tawakoni State Park.
We looked for the 4 letterboxes there and only found one. I am pretty
sure that we were in the right places but no boxes. The one we found
was "Shelob's Lair" It was exactly where it was suppose to be. Fairy at
Work maybe there but the black berry vines were so thick that we were
not going to go through them. Maybe we will try again in the fall.
Kantexan's Arachnitopia and Arachnitopi'love could not be found. We
found the tree with the spiky branches but no box. We also found the
big rock and the small rock but no spiders in love. I will be
contacting Kantexan and hope they can shed more light on this. I am not
saying not to try to find these but I believe they are not there. Adams
