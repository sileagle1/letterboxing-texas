She will certainly be keeping us busy!  I’d also like to add that we have finished the Eoghan’s Knots series.  Mosaic Butterfly and I each carved two of the stamps.  It appears impossible to replace the original plants at this point since most of the orienteering markers are gone and maps are not available.  We plan to place the boxes in Buescher park as a tribute to the original series and they will be available by the event.  The stamps are the same images as the original series but slightly larger. 

 

I’ll be arriving Thursday night if anyone else is coming early.  Please let me know if anyone needs me to bring anything or help with any setup.  If anyone still wants to camp, you can pitch a tent next to our shelter.  (you will need to pay a fee for extra cars as we will have two).
