From: carie78504 (carierraine@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-30 04:33:30 UTC
I was in Charlotte, and they have two really nice nature preserves.
Both of them have several miles of clearly marked hiking trails, and
I really enjoyed myself, except for one thing. Here in Texas, I
kinda know what creatures to look out for, but hiking in an unknown
area was scary because I did not know what creatures could be there.
It all turned out fine. I came across a box turtle about the size of
half a shoe box just sitting in the middle of the trail, we spent
some quality time together. And a black snake slithering off the
foundation of an old stone house, and a deranged squirrel that ran
head long down the path straight at me, followed by another squirrel
that chased it up a tree right next to me. Man, those guys made the
most noise, I thought it was a huge animal.
So, long story short, No I do not know any good places in Ashville
(super big smile)

WOW
MOM

changed our name to the Boxhounds. We just found out about letterboxing
