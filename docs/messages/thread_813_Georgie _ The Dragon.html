Georgie & The Dragon

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-24 21:47:33 UTC

We planted a little series today, Georgie & The Dragon, in the
Montgomery County Nature Preserve, which is a beautiful greenway along
Spring Creek. No facilities, scant parking, just a trail that goes
along a pond and down to the creek. There are some rare plant
species, lots of butterflies, dragonflies and damselflies, and birds
galore. Oh, and at least one cottonmouth. On a beautiful day like
today, it made for a fun trip. I'm sure it's miserable in the summer.

The main trail is definitely stroller friendly up to the box, but the
main loop that continues on past it I wouldn't classify as stroller-
friendly. However, TeamKing might be able to manage it. . .

LBNA is acting nutty, so the clues are only on AtlasQuest right now.
Will add them to the lbna site later.

Dewberry & Batrick
