I NEED MORE DAYLIGHT HOURS!!!

From: cwenter1 (cwenter1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-12-31 03:48:01 UTC
Well, I planned out a day of letterboxing for last Friday, December
28th. I did my research on the letterboxes that I wanted to find in
Athens, Edom, Brownsboro, New York, and Tyler and I printed out the
clues for each of them. I had a total of 15 that I was going to find
before I returned home. I left my house at 8:30 and stopped at the
gas station to fill up the tank. I started with the new plant by
Fairy Dreamer at the Children's Park in Tyler, then off to Edom I
went for Fairy Dreamers next 2 new plants. (Thanks for the 1st Finder
certificates). Then I made my way to Brownsboro to one that I had
searched for before and couldn't find. Talking to my daughters after
my 1st attempt at locating this one they told me that it was there
because they had found it so I thought I'd give it another try with
no luck. I didn't want to waste too much time there so on to New York
I went and then to Athens. After finding several boxes in Athens the
sun was starting to go down and I knew that I would have to hurry
before it was totally gone. Well, I didn't quite finish before the
sunlight was gone. Of the 15 boxes that I set out to find there were
2 that I couldn't find, 10 that I did find, and 3 that I'll have to
look for another day. So....I can't wait for Daylight Savings Time
because I NEED FOR DAYLIGHT!!!

I guess I could have skipped the stop for lunch and the bathroom.
NOT!!

I still had a great time. :)
