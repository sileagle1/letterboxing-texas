From: monkeytoes108 (cruschhaupt@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-07 02:23:32 UTC
Well, we're back with a 4/4 success rate! We enjoyed a nice picnic
in the park and then it was on to the boxes. We saw dozens of
damselflies, butterflies, frogs, giant mutated grasshoppers, and
only a few mosquitos. The second bridge along the nature path is
closed for hurricane repairs so there is no longer a "loop". You
can still get to Busy Bee's boxes, though. When the trail splits
just go to the right, all the way around to the other side of the
bridge. Then you'll have to double back to where you started from.
It's not too far.

Here's the what and the how...

Silver Eagle's "Mexico Flag"
Busy Bee's "Echinacea", and "Twin Dragonflies"
Baby Bear's "Remember Goliad"

All boxes were fine and dry. Baby Bear's looked like it had been
wet at one time, but was okay today. I put the whole box on a
ziploc just in case.

I haven't been to the missions since I was a kid. Here it is some
15 years or more later and I still remember everything. Thanks for
the boxes and the remembrance.

-MonkeyToes.
