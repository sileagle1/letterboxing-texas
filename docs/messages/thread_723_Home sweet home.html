Lightning and I got home a short while ago - it was a
long trip home after a looonnnnggg night, but well
worth the drive!

We, or I should say our tent, suffered some
misadventure last night during the storm and we
decided to leave it to its woes and opted for the car
for a nice dry night. We got up bright and early and
hit the trails for a few boxes before we headed back
up to East Texas. Unbelievable how nice a morning we
woke up to after such a tempestuous night - and I
can't wait to hear the tales of those late night
letterboxers, bound to be a good story or two in
there.

We had a really good time yesterday and it was so good
to see everyone - those we met last year and those we
met for the first time yesterday - it seemed like old
home week from the first moment.

Thank you, thank you to Silver Eagle for putting the
event together, to Baby Bear for the classic event
stamp, and to both of them for great chili.

Thanks to Celtic Lions for the very cool game - you
guys really went above and beyond with it. And thanks
to all the carvers who made the game so rich with
their creativity.

Thanks to Steve (boxdn) for coffee and beignets - it
was a really nice way to start a great day. I meant
to get a t-shirt for Alex, so if you still have some,
I'd like to get one if you don't mind shipping it.
Just let me know...

I just love our group - it's just so darned much fun!

See y'all next year, if not on the trails...

BarefootLucy
