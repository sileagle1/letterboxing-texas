From: Rachel (rachelincolorado@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-14 04:35:33 UTC
Hello all!

As Eoghan said, all boxes are fine! I would also like to add that
we had a most excellent time during our road trip to Texas. We
camped in Abilene the first night, the weather was perfect.

Abilene SP, found both:

Buffalo Gap
SHOTB: CALEB

Arriving in Austin, found:
San Gabriel Park - found during the final four soccer tourney

Chisolm Trail Box - the path to cross over was blocked off due to
high water but we found another way to get in. The water was
rushing! I now know the story of Round Rock - a great one it is
too, I took some amazing photos and you can still see wagon wheel
trails! I'll add some photos to the 'photos' section soon!

Then it was time to pull out all the stops! The next day, we headed
for Bastrop, our favorite Texas SP! We managed to snag six boxes
before the rain started to pour! Bastrop is so beautiful when it's
wet and very green! We also were able to stamp in on the rock that
Eoghan proposed at - it was awesome!

Bastrop boxes stamped:

Lost Loblolly - where we also were the 'first to find'
Mr. Poppers Penguins "H.H." - it's now in Colorado
Poison Ivy
Trail Rider - Baby Bear, this logbook is full!
Skinny Pine
Park Pest

We wanted to find them all but it was time to go! The fun ended all
too soon! We will be back! Thanks to all of you for such wonderful
boxes! Might I add, Baby Bear, your carving is superb!

Take care all!

Cheers!
Pumpkin Princess
